A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.4.2014

Man Found Dead: Lalnuntluanga (36), a resident of Sairang Electric Veng, who went missing since 4th March, 2014 was found dead in a gorge below 5th IR Battalion Headquarters (around 150 ft. away from the road) in a decomposed state on Monday night. He was identified from his wrist watch and T-shirt, and his funeral was held yesterday.

 

* All the local daily newspaper highlighted the arrest of Pu K.Chhawnthuama @ Mizo Phantom and his aide K.Lalhruaitluanga, cameraman, who were arrested from Starline Hotel, Paltan Bazar, Guwahati on Monday night by Mizoram Police.
