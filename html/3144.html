SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/673

Dated: 2nd Sept., 2021

 

          Information received stated that today, i.e., 2.9.2021 @ 1:30 p.m., around
40 (forty) Assam Armed Police personnel physically assaulted, intimidated and wrongfully detained Lalnarammawia (42) S/o Biakmawia (L) of Kolasib Vengthar, Operator of Excavator (JCB) BR No. MZ-05 A-4464, while the latter was constructing road at Pu Lalngaisanga’s farm land Aitlang, Kolasib District, situated at around 4 kms south of Vairengte PHE Pump House. Upon receiving report about the incident,
Pu David J Byhnadaolai, Addl. SP, Kolasib and 20 (twenty) Mizoram Police rushed to the area, but Assam Police already left the area by the time they arrived.

 

          The victim Lalnarammawia stated that around 40 (forty) Assam Armed Police suddenly appeared at the road construction site this afternoon @ around 1:30 p.m. and damaged the front and side glass of the Excavator (JCB) and they forcefully pulled him out of the JCB and physically assaulted him. He was then blindfolded and dragged to the nearby river (Rongote Lui). They forcefully detained him for around 1 (one) hour and threatened to kill him if he continues construction of road at the area, claiming that the area falls within Assam territory. Assam Police also took away his mobile phone and JCB keys.

 

          As per input, the above road construction work was started on 30.8.2021 and is intended to connect Mizoram Police Duty Camp set up on 23.8.2021 at
Pu Lalhlimpuia of Vairengte’s farm land, at Aitlang, Kolasib District. At the time of the above incident, Pu Isaac C. Lalrempuia, SDO (C ) was around 100 mtrs away from the place of occurrence keeping watch on the said road construction works and not directly visible from the site of incident.

 

          Situation is under close watch.  

 

DGP (M)
