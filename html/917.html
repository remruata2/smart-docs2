REPORT ON INCIDENT ALONG INDO-MYANMAR BORDER

 

There was an encounter between Myanmar Army (MA) and rebel Arakan Army (AA) on 26.2.2018 @ 11:00 am at Kumprang Village on Myanmar side of the border close to Kakichhuah of Lawngtlai District. The State of Mizoram has recently witnessed cross border incidents like fleeing of 1750 Nos. of Myanmarese from villages like Varang, etc., under Paletwa township of Chin State, Myanmar into the different villages of Mizoram due to fierce clashes between Arakan Army (AA) militants and the Myanmar Army (MA).
