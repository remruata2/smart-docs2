**A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 3.5.2013**

**Police registered cases in the torching of Saikhumphai******: Champhai police registered a number of cases against people of Vaphai for torching houses of Saikhumphai village but no one has been arrested in connection with the case so far. Sources said that there is no untoward incident between people of Vaphai and Saikhumphai villages. Meanwhile, relief work has been done as much as possible. PHE Dept. has already fixed/mended the damaged water connection pipeline of Saikhumphai village.

 Former Legislators' Association of Mizoram (FLAM) also made a press release expressing sympathy for the people of Saikhumphai and condemning the action of Vaphai people.

**B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 3.5.2013**

**Land Owners alleged Government of suppression******: Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP) Land Owners' Association demands government to make notification for compensation and to assign collectors for it; and alleged government of suppressing land owners by using police force. 

**C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 3.5.2013**

**2 villages at loggerheads with one another** : Tuikuk (Bru) and Zakhai people were at loggerheads with one another at the south-end of Mizoram for an attempt to rape a woman. Leaders of Central YLA, Lawngtlai and Magistrate went to resolve the issue. According to Lawngtlai Mizoram Journalist Association (MJA) Secretary, Elvis Lalthangzuala, some men of Zakhai from Hmangbuchhuah village attempted to rape Tuikuk (Bru) woman of T.Dumzau village. Tuikuk (Bru) people caught the offenders and Zakhai people were angry at Tuikuk (Bru) people; people of the two villagers were at loggerheads with one another. Details about the incident could not be had.
