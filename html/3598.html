A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 09 .11.2022

871 persons fined for traffic rules violations: Since the implementation of Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 2019 from 1st September 2022 in Mizoram, 871 persons have been fined in Aizawl for various violations of traffic rules during the period between 1st - 31st October, 2022 which amounted to a total of Rs. 5,35,800. According to reports received by VANGLAINI from senior officers of Traffic police, violators of No-Parking area were the highest in numbers (207 vehicles) among the defaulters, wherein two wheelers occupied the highest spot in this category.

 

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.  09.11.2022

Rs. 20 crores for Health Care: Government of Mizoram has recently allotted Rs. 20 crores from Social Economic Development Programme (SEDP) funds to Mizoram Health Care Scheme (MSHCS). On 7th October 2022, Planning & Programme Implementation department under Government of Mizoram had made a proposal of releasing the said amount from SEDP funds to meet the require medical expenditure incurred by registered members of MSHCS. The MSHCS is intending to disburse these funds to their members who are having pending medical reimbursement bills next week if it could not be started within this week. Rs. 20 crores is expected to cover majority of medical bills submitted before September of 2021.
