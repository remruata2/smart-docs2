Sir,

 

                        With reference to your letter and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:-

 

Starred Question No. 259 :

 

a)       How many Arms & Ammunitions are seized by Mizoram Police Dept. within Mizoram during the year 2009-2013?

 

Ans:               Mizoram Police Department seized 239 Arms and 5504 Ammunitions within Mizoram during the years 2009-2013.

 

b)       If Arms and Ammunitions were seized, how many are kept and how many were disposed of?

 

Ans:               Whenever Arms and Ammunitions were seized, the concerned OC of Police Station registered a case and investigated. Since, the outcome of investigation has never been communicated to CID(SB) unit, it is suggested that either DIG(NR/SR) Crime Branch or Prosecution Branch, Aizawl Court may be asked.

 

c)       If they were disposed off, to whom with their names and addresses?

 

Ans:               Prosecution Branch, Aizawl Court may be asked.

 

 

SUPPLEMENTARY NOTES :

1.       How were the seized Arms and Ammunitions disposed of?  

Ans:                After all formalities and trial of the case had been completed, seized arms and ammunitions were disposed off by a Board under the chairmanship of District Magistrate.
