****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/312

Dated: 10th May, 2019.

 Information has been received that Lalhmingthanga Sanate (53) s/o V.L.Ramliana, President, HPC(D) (P) has been apprehended by 37th Assam Rifles last night, i.e. 09.05.2019 @ 9:18 p.m. with a .32 pistol with 2 (two) live ammunitions in his possession. Information received indicated that the Assam Rifles are intending to submit an FIR against Sanate at Lakhipur PS, Assam in connection with the murder of one Jubilee-a at Hmarkhawlien, Assam during March, 2019 and other charges including extortion, smuggling of arms etc.

 Information received has been indicating that Sanate’s faction are not more than 10 (ten) persons armed with 3 (three) AK-rifles, 2 HK pistols and 2 (two) SBBL guns. They are found not to have a proper area of operation with reports of occasional sightings in Hmarkhawlien, Jiribam and Vangai. However, there has also been report that they have been operating together with one Naga outfit called Zeliang Rong United Front in certain areas.

 Lalhmingthanga Sanate claimed to still stand firm on HPC(D)’s demand for ‘Hmar Autonomous District Council’.
