DRAFT

 

GROUND RULES FOR SUSPENSION OF OPERATION BASED ON PEACE TALK BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT OF MIZORAM AND THE HMAR PEOPLE CONVENTION (DEMOCRATIC)

 

 

 

             The Government of Mizoram and the Hmar People Convention (Democratic) agreed on this day, _______________________after exchange of views to abide by the following ground rules and modalities for effective peace process between the two parties.

 

OBJECTIVES:

 

(a)       The ground rules and the modalities are mutually agreed to with a view to ensure an effective peace process for dialogue in the common interest of finding solution to the existing grievances of Hmar People Convention (Democratic) in Mizoram.

 

(b)      To build confidence and trust in the common pursuit of finding permanent solution.

 

 

BASIC PRINCIPLE:

 

1.         The State Police will not carry out any offensive operations against the Hmar People Convention (Democratic). The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) on their part, shall not engage in any subversive activities which may jeopardize the peace process. To ensure free movement of Hmar People Convention (Democratic) personnel in and the adjoining states of Mizoram during the peace process, the State Government will make a request to the Central Government to honour this agreement. However, the Government of Mizoram reserves its right to continue operation against other militant groups who are not a party to this agreement.

 

2.         Government of Mizoram will also request Governments of Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura and Manipur to allow within their States, free movement to Hmar People Convention (Democratic) during the Suspension of Operation period and also issue appropriate direction to security agencies to stand down and cease offensive operations against the outfit during the round of peace talks, provided that the Hmar People Convention (Democratic) does not indulge in any acts detrimental to the peace process.

 

3.         A Joint Monitoring Group (JMG) with equal representation from the Hmar People Convention (Democratic) and the Government of Mizoram shall be set up to enforce an effective implementation of the agreed ground rules for peace talk.

 

4.         The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) in the interest of building peace, trust, confidence and goodwill, shall be allowed free movement without arms in Mizoram.

 

5.         The agreed period of Suspension of Operation between the Hmar People Convention (Democratic) and the Government of Mizoram shall be initially for a period of 6 months which may be extended by mutual consent.

 

6.         The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) shall immediately cease all subversive activities including collection of donations and other activities detrimental to the maintenance of law and order, and to desist from all forms of direct or indirect threats to any organizations or to any private individual.

 

7.         Personnel of Mizoram Police will refrain from any offensive operations against Hmar People Convention (Democratic). Measures to ensure security to the camps including patrolling for that purpose and movement of Force on Law & Order and administrative duties will not be restricted.

 

8.         Government of Mizoram will designate an officer to act as Liaison officer who in coordination with SP CID(SB) will arrange all logistics support including transport.

 

9.         The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) shall not directly or indirectly interfere in any Parliamentary, State Assembly and local election process which may deprive voters from exercising their franchise.

 

10.       Violation of any points of the agreed ground rules by the defaulting party in any of the agreed ground rules shall automatically amount to revocation of the ground rules.
