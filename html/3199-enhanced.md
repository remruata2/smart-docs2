A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 2.7.2013_

****17 FIR for fake seal**: 17 FIRs were lodged in connection with making of fake seals from the year 2008 till date under the jurisdiction of SP Aizawl (Aizawl Police Station–14, Bawngkawn Police Station–2, Vaivakawn Police Station–1), out of 17 cases, 13 cases were chargesheeted, 2 cases are under investigation and Final Report were submitted on 2 other cases.**

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 2.7.2013_

**No news of the abducted**: No news has been heard for almost a month about the three (3) border fencing labourers under Gautam Construction Company Ltd. abducted from Badalsuri near Rajiv Nagar on 5.6.2013, the abductors also remained silent for the time being.
