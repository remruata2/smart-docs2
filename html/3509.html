A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 31.7& 01.08.2022

Land dispute between LRS and EFCC:  For the proposed widening project of National Highway NH-06 and NH-306, which is in between Vairengte and Sairang town, works cannot be started yet due to the land dispute between Land Revenue & Settlement(LRS) Department and Environment, Forest & Climate Change (EFCC)Department.

            This project which is under Ministry of Road Transports & Highways (MoRTH) will be undertaken by National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL). According to officials of NHIDCL, acquisition of land cannot be done for the proposed four-lane widening works of these National Highway as both LRS and EFCC departments each claims the affected lands to be under the jurisdiction of their department. However, they expected the matter to be resolved soon as negotiations were already done with the consent of the Chief Minister, they added.

           

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 31.07  & 01.08. 2022

Golden Boy of India from Mizoram: In the ongoing Commonwealth Games 2022 held at Birmingham, England, Jeremy Lalrinnunga,Ainawn Veng, Aizawl bagged a Gold Medal for India in Mens 67 Kilogram category on Sunday i.e 31.7.2022. Jeremy Lalrinnunga smashed two Commonwealth Games Records by lifting a total of 300 kilograms with 140 kilograms in snatch and 160 kilograms in clean and jerk.

            President of India, Prime Minister of India, Union Sports Minister, Mizoram Chief Minister, Mizoram Sport Minister, other top officials of the country and state and several prominent citizens tweetted their congratulations in Tweetter.
