A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 27.1.2014

C.Lalfakzuala entered the house of Zemabawk North Local Council Chairman’s House with a Gun: On 25th January 2014 @ around 10:30 pm, C.Lalfakzuala h/o  R.Lalthiamsangi of Zemabawk North entered the house of Lalthanpuia, Chairman of Zemabawk North Local Council in rage with a gun. Luckily, some people managed to hold and stop C.Lalfakzuala before something bad happen.

            C.Lalfakzuala and his family were casted out of Tuikhuahtlang locality for selling abusive substances. So, they came to Zemabawk North and stay with his father-in-law. Zemabawk North Local Council could not allow C.Lalfakzuala and his family to settle down at Zemabawk North and C.Lalfakzuala was in a rage due to it. He was arrested by the Police and is now in Central Jail.

            NGOs of Zemabawk North made a joint Press Release informing that C.Lalfakzuala to never set foot at Zemabawk North again and requesting the Police and Government to take action according to the law.  

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 27.1.2014

Mamit Sub-Headquarters YMA Demand: The meeting of executive Committee of Mamit Sub-Headquarters YMA on 24th January 2014 decided that, they will organise social work to search for Deep Mandal who was kidnapped on 23rd November 2013 along with two Mizos by Bru armed gang, he was not released along with 2 Mizos. If Deep Mandal is not released within January 2014, Mamit Sub-Headquarters YMA intends to organise search operation to be started by 4-5 February 2014.
