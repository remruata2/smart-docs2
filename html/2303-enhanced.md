****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/51

Dated 22nd January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/48 Dt. 21.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – 6, MMV – 3, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 22.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV – 2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 7, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 22.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 9 (nine) vehicles (MMV – 9, LMV -Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 99 (ninety nine) vehicles (HMV-59, MMV – 1, LMV – 26, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab-8, Bus - 5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
