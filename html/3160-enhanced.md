_A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Vol-XXVII No.301 Dt.11.12.2013_

**Five foreigners were blacklisted:**Bureau of Immigration, Ministry of Home Affairs and Government of India had blacklisted five foreigners from USA who visited Mizoram on religious (church) function and stated that the five foreigners had violated VISA rules. It is also reported that the said foreigners were well known missionaries and entered Mizoram by using business visa and tourist visa.

_B Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Vol-XXVII No.301 Dt.11.12.2013_

 **New Government in Mizoram by tomorrow:**Pu Lal Thanhawla, President, MPCC while addressing the party workers at Congress Bhavan in yesterday said that new government of Mizoram would be formed by 12.12.2013 and asked all the elected congress MLA be humble in their deed and work.
