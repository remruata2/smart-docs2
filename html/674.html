                  With reference to the above subject, information has been received from SO(SB) Vairengte regarding the arrest of  Lalsangzuala S/o Dawrkaithanga(L) of Vairengte and his friends at Hmarkhawlian on 7.10.2015.

                 

                  On 1.10.2015, Vanchungnunga s/o Ngunawrha and Hranglinga s/o Suikama both from Farkawn, Champhai District asked Lalsangzuala of Bangla Veng, Vairengte to accompany them in their business trip. However, the kind of business run by Vanchungnunga and Hranglinga was not known by the family members of Lalsangzuala.

                 

                  After they stayed for about 1(one) week at Hmarkhawlian, they were arrested by CRPF (140 Bn.) alongwith their luggage/belongings for their involvement in illegal trafficking of explosives(100 nos. of detonators) and they are currently being detained at Silchar Jail since 12.10.2015. In this connection, Lakhipur PS Case No.231/15 u/s 3 Explosive Substances Act has been registered against them.
