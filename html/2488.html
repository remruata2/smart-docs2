MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/666

Dated 22nd  November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/665
Dt. 21.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

No vehicle entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 22.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

Shri Avinash Joshi, IAS, Principal Secretary, Forest Dept., Govt. of Assam accompanied by Shri. A.M Singh, IFS, PCCF & HOFF, Assam; Smt. Keerthi Jalli, DC, Cachar; Shri. Suman Mahapatra, IFS, CCF, SAC, Silchar; Shri. Akashdeep Baruah, IFS, CCF (T), LAZ and Shri Sunnydeo Choudhury, DFO, Cachar and other officials who intending to visit bomb blast site near Saihapui ‘V’ were stopped at the border by BSF and Mizoram Police on duty led by Pu Vanlalnghenga, Dy. CO, 3rd MAP today i.e., 22.11.2020 @ 11:30 a.m. informing them that without prior permission of DC, Kolasib and SP, Kolasib visit of the stated site is not allowed. After informing the matter to SP, Kolasib they were allowed to enter Mizoram and they went up to Buarchep Peng, which is around 1.2 km from the border and on their way back they glanced the bomb blast site from the road and then proceeded to Assam.

6(six) vehicles (Auto Rickshaw – 2, Tempo – 1, LMV-1, Bike - 2) entered Saihapui ‘V’ from Assam today i.e., 22.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

43 (forty three) vehicles (HMV-31, MMV-9, LMV-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
