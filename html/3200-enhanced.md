A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 3.7.2013_

**Assembly Session called**: Governor of Mizoram, Vakkom Purushothaman called for the 14th sitting of the 6th Mizoram Legislative Assembly to commence on 23.6.2013 @ 10:30AM, the notice was put out yesterday. Business Advisory Committee led by Speaker R.Romawia will plan the duration and the manner of the Assembly. This Assembly is thought to be the last session in the term of the present ruling party. Mizoram Annual Plan is expected to be put up in the session.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 3.7.2013_

**Intention to block road**: The request of VC and Joint YMA of Thingdawl to repair the dilapidated road in and around their village seemed to fall on deaf ears as no step has been taken by the authority to mend the road. As such is the case, NGO Co-ordination Committee was formed to proceed.

 Till today, no progress has been made to repair the road (NH-54) and estimate has not been accepted by the authority, said the NGO Co-ordination Committee. The Committee requests the government to accept the estimate and expects to witness progress in its demand, if that does not happen before the end of June, road block will be carried on to show their discontent.

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 3.7.2013_

**Pseudoephedrine seized from CID personnel**: 2,20,000 tablets of Pseudoephedrine were seized by Champhai Excise Staff yesterday @ 2:00PM, the alleged owners Lalbiakmawii(40) d/o R.Lianmawia, SI of Police posted at Champhai CID(SB) Post and motor driver, Robert Z.Tochhawng s/o Sangzuala, were detained. They both reside at Champhai Vengsang. The seized Pseudoephedrine is worth ` 16.5 lakh in the local market rate.

 Pseudoephedrine was carried from Aizawl to Champhai by using Maruti 800, it was then transited to Gypsy at the outskirt of Champhai by Lalbiakmawii and friend, said Excise. They were checked at the checkgate by Excise and seized the illegal substances. Lalbiakmawii and Robert Z.Tochhawng are detained by the Excise for interrogation.

D. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 3.7.2013_

**No rice supply from Bairabi to Lawngtlai**: Mizoram Truck owners Association and Mizoram Truck Drivers Association announced that they will not carry rice from Bairabi to Lawngtlai from today onwards because carrying contractor of rice from Bairabi to Lawngtlai, GB Chowdhury has not been paying them for some time now, and they will stop until they are paid.
