****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/336

Dated 30th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/333 Dt. 29.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****9 (nine) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-6, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 30.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-17, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 30.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
