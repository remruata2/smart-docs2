27.10.2014                  On 22.10.2014, Pu S.T. Khama (66 yrs) H/o Darziki, Electric Veng had submitted FIR to Aizawl Police Station in which he stated that on 17.10.2014 one unidentified person had contacted him through mobile number-8794695450 and demanded Rs.20 lakhs and threatened him with dire consequences unless the demand is fulfilled on or before 26.10.2014. As the unidentified person/caller had continuously threatening him, on 23.10.2014 the complainant Pu S.T. Khama had approached CID(SB) for help. The matter was immediately pursued by a team of CID(SB) and after careful analysis of intelligence input, the team laid a trap at KT Hotel, Zarkawt for three consecutive days and nights. Finally on 27.10.2014 at around 8:30 PM, the CID(SB) team managed to apprehend the main accused Jacob Lianhupa (40 yrs) S/o Nivunga (L), Aizawl Venglai  and his associate Vanlalliana (20 yrs) S/o Lalhnemi,Sangau. They had confessed their guilt before Police.

 

                                                                     The main accused Jacob Lianhupa is the Chief Organizer of ZNP Youth. In this connection, Aizawl Police Station Case No. 336/2014 dt.22.10.2014 u/s 506 IPC has already been registered and the two accused persons were instantly arrested and currently remanded into Police Custody at Aizawl Police Station.
