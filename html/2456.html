MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/715

Dated 14th December, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/712
Dt. 13.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          10 (ten) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-3, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 14.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 16 (Sixteen) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 16, LMV - 1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

          4 (four) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – Nil, LMV - 1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 14.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 5 (five) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 4, LMV - 1) transit their good at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Information received indicated that goods carrying vehicles (Pick Up, 207 etc.) are not allowed to enter Assam by SSB (CAPF) on duty (deployed by Assam). At the same time, in order to prevent the spread of Covid-19 the joint Village Council of Saihapui ‘V’ and Phainuam stop entry of goods carrying vehicles from Assam to Mizoram w.e.f., 11.12.2020.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

61 (sixty one) vehicles (HMV-18, MMV – 2, LMV – 41, Maxi Cab- Nil) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 14.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          133 (one hundred and thirty three) vehicles (HMV – 77, MMV – 25, LMV –15, LPG – 4, Maxi Cab – 1, Oil Tanker – 11, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 14.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
