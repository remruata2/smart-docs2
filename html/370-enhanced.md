****PEACE DIALOGUE****

Due to the initiative of SP CID(SB), 5 (five) rounds of Peace Dialogue between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) have so far been conducted within this year. The First round of Peace Dialogue was held on 10th August,2016 and the Second round of Peace Dialogue was held during 5th to 6th October,2016 where framework of agreement was work out. The Third round of Peace Dialogue was held on 16th December, 2016@ 11:00am at State Guest House, Aizawl as scheduled. During the talk, the following arms and ammunitions snatched away by HPC(D) from Mizoram Police were handed over to Govt. of Mizoram.

 The Fourth round of Peace Dialogue was held on 28th April, 2017@ 11:00am at State Guest House, Aizawl. The Fifth round of Peace Dialogue was held on 21st &amp; 22nd September, 2017 at State Guest House, Aizawl, Mizoram and the Peace Dialogue has shown positive direction. As per the schedule, the next round of Peace Dialogue would be held at Political Level tentatively before Christmas i.e., 25th December, 2017.

 Due to some avoidable circumstances, Political Level Peace Dialogue could not be held as per scheduled. Therefore, The Political Level Peace Talk between Mizoram State Government and HPC-D was held under a cordial environment at the State Guest House, Aizawl during 7th &amp; 8th March, 2018 and the Peace Talk ended with a logical conclusion today, i.e. 8.3.2018. The State Government and the HPC-D were represented by 10 (ten) members each. While the delegate of Mizoram Government was led by Pu Lalrinmawia Ralte, MLA and Political Adviser to Chief Minister, Pu L.T. Hmar, Working Chairman, HPC-D led the HPC-D delegates during the two-day Peace Dialogue.

_SP CID(SB) has been entrusted to arrange all the logistic support to the representatives of HPC(D) during the Peace Dialogue._
