****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/750

Dated 28th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

6 (six) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-2, LMV – 1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 28.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 10, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

2 (two) vehicles (HMV – 1, MMV – 1, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 28.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 8 (eight) vehicles (LMV-4, MMV–4) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 69 (sixty nine) vehicles (HMV-21, MMV – 3, LMV – 37, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 28.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam-Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOPis still remain halted till today i.e., 28.12.2020.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 114 (one hundred fourteen) vehicles (HMV – 49, MMV – 8, LMV –19, LPG – 4, Maxi Cab – 2, Oil Tanker – 32, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 28.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
