HPC (D) te annual fund khawn chungchang

Pu B. Lalthlengliana to ask-

Will the Hon’ble Minister for Home Department be pleased to state-

*HPC (D) ten an demand area chhunga Annual Fund an khawn avanga case Police Station hrang hrang 7 (pasarih) a siam kha engnge thlen a nih tawh ?

 

Answer :- HPC (D) ten Annual Fund an khawn chunghangah hian Police Station hrang hrang 7 (pasarih) nilovin 6 (paruk) ah case siam ani a. Tun thlenga hmalak tawh dan chu  separate sheet-ah rawn thil tel a ni.

 

Supplementary Answer:

  1. Hemi chungchang a mi mante hi Court phalna in  bail a chhuah an ni.
  2. Hetianga pawisa khawntute hi Mizoram pawna hmun khuar an nih avangin tun thlengin tumah man an la ni lova, a tul anga action la turin theihtawp chhuah zel a ni.

 

 

Yours faithfully,
