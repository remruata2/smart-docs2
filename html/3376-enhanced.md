A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 9.4.2014_

**Bandh Called off after 32:30 hrs. – Joint NGO Satisfied with the Outcome****:**NGO Co-ordination Committee called a total bandh for 72 hrs. from 5:00am of 7th April 2014 to show discontent at the casting of votes by Bru at Tripura Transit Camp on 1st – 3rd April 2014. NGO Co-ordination Committee demanded that Bru votes be cancelled. Bandh was called off at 1:30pm yesterday as NGO was satisfied with the agreement made between ECI and Mizoram government regarding the exercise of franchise by Bru in Mizoram.

B. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 9.4.2014_

**Heroin Seized****:**Kawlkulh YMA on duty of Total Bandh seized heroin in a vehicle coming from Champhai on Monday. The vehicle was said to carry a sick person and Kawlkulh YMA made them a pass slip to show to Keifang YMA. The vehicle remained at Kawlkulh for a long while even after they had the pass slip and that made Kawlkulh YMA duty suspicious. YMA duty checked the vehicle and seized 2 Hawngs of heroin, they bought at Champhai for ` 84,000/- and to be sold in Aizawl for ` 1,50,000/-. Four persons on board Lalfakzuala Pachuau (43) of Durtlang Mel 5, Lalfakzuala (42) of Ramhlun South, Lalthlamuana (50) of Ramhlun South and Jacob MS Dawngliana (35) of Kawlkulh village were apprehended by YMA duty and handed over to Police.
