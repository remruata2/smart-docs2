A. Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 20.3.2013

Pistol seized: A joint team of Bawngkawn PS Staff and Tuirial Branch YMA seized a local made pistol from Chinsianmuan (24) s/o Haumunga of Champhai Kanan on the night of 18th March. Chinsianmuan was arrested 2 years back for robbing different shops in Aizawl; he stacked up his stolen properties in a cave at the outskirt of Aizawl and lived there. He was arrested and detained in Central Jail for some time, after his release he was arrested again recently.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 20.3.2013

2 Taxis collided – One driver dead: Two taxis – MZ 01 H 6928 driven by Lianthanga (50) s/o Ranga (L) of Dinthar Veng and MZ 01 H 6525 driven by Vanlalhruaia (34) s/o Biakhranga of Hunthar Veng collided at Rangvamual on 18.3.2013 @ around 7:00PM, the two taxi drivers were apprehended by Traffic Police. One taxi driver Lianthanga died at 11:20PM in the hospital due to injuries he sustained from the accident.
