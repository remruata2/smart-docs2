  With reference to your letter and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:-

**Starred Question No. 607 :**

**a) How many kidnapped cases occurred in Mizoram during the year 2008 - 2013?**

**Ans:** There were 6 kidnapped cases in Mizoram during the year 2008-2013.

**SUPPLEMENTARY NOTES:**

**1. Who are the kidnappers?**

**Ans:** **i)**On 7.2.2008 @ 9:30AM,an armed group 7 (seven) suspected to be ULFB (United Liberation Front of Barak Valley), three of them in camouflage dress abducted one non-Mizo Thekadar namely, Jamil Uddin Laskar (35) s/o A.K Laskar (L) of Jamira, Hailakandi Dist., Assam P/A Saihapui while he was going to Damdiai river side from Saihapui Village.

****

**ii)**On 4.10.2008 a group of 4/5 men wearing green uniform, armed with a pistol kidnapped Kishore Rangia Chakma of Marpara, Headmaster Silsury Middle School while on his way to Silsury from Marpara.

****

**iii)**On 6.6.11, two ABCI employees were kidnapped/abducted by UDLF from ABCI camp near Meidum village in Kolasib district.

 **iv)**  On 25.3.2012 6(Six) ABCI workers/employees were kidnapped/abducted by National Liberation Front of Tripura(NLFT) from ABCI camp in between New Sachan and Mauzam, at Lunglei district.

 **v)**  On 25.11.2012, 3(Three) Bengali teak traders were abducted/kidnapped by a combined group of NLFT and BNCT (Borok National Council of Tripura) from Tuipuibari - II (Rajivnagar), Mamit District.

 **vi)**On the morning of 19.2.2013, 5 (Five) employees of Forest deptt. were abducted by NLFT/WNDLFM (Western National  Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from their camp, in between Tuipuibari and Damparengpui, Mamit Dist.

**2. What are the causes of the kidnappings and demands of the kidnappers?**

**Ans:** **i)**The abductors of****Jamil Uddin Laskar (35) s/o A.K Laskar (L) of Jamira, Hailakandi Dist., Assam P/A Saihapui on 7.2.2008 demanded Rs. 17 lakhs as ransom money. However, he was rescued by Joint Operation Team of Mizoram Police, Assam Police and CRPF on 17.2.2008.

****

**ii)**The abductors of****Kishore Rangia Chakma of Marpara, Headmaster Silsury Middle School on 4.10.2008 demanded Rs.4 lakhs as ransom money. However, he was rescued by Mizoram Police Operation Team on 6.10.2008 near 'Khawthlang Tuipui'.

 **iii)**  The abductors of ABCI workers on Dt.6.6.2011 demanded Rs.5 crores as ransom money. However, the 2 abductees were rescued by an operation team of Mizoram police on 16.6.2011.

**iv)** The abductors of ABCI workers on Dt.25.3.2012 demanded Rs.1.2 crores as ransom money.

 **v)** The abductors of three Bengali teak traders on Dt.25.11.2012 demanded Rs.30 Lakhs as ransom money.

 **vi)**  The abductors of Five Forest deptt. employees/workers on Dt.19.2.2013 demanded Rs.1 crore as ransom money.

**3. Did the demands of the abductors/kidnappers fulfilled?**

**Ans:** Apart from Rs.9 Lakhs given to the abductors of the three Bengali teak traders by their families, no ransom money/demand was given to any other kidnappers/abductors.
