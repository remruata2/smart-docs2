AMBUSH LAID BY HPC(D)

AT THE OUTSKIRT OF ZOKHAWTHIANG ON 28.3.2015

 

            On 28.3.2015 @ 8:50 Am, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance members such as Pu R.L. Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Pu Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Nl. Lalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) Insurgent group numbering around 12 cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village. Three (3) Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while five (5) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about one (1) hour and forty five (45) minutes. The MLAs were accompanied in this tour by Government officials such as Pu Zothansanga Ralte, Under Secretary, Pu Lalrinawma Sailo, Committee Officer, Pi Lalchuailovi, UDC, Pu Zohmingthanga, Peon and Drivers - R. Hmingsanga and Lalpekhlua. In this connection Sakawrdai Police Station Case No. 6/15 dt. 29.3.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120B/326 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act has been registered.

           

            Information has been received that laying of the ambush was carried out under the command of Malsawmkima Hrangchal who deserted the 1st Bn. MAP (on 11.7.2014) and joined the HPC(D) outfit. The HPC(D) cadres were carrying sophisticated arms like AK- 47 and G-3 rifles. The HPC(D) outfit targeted the pilot vehicle which went ahead of the other vehicles.

            The HPC(D) militants also snatched away the following Arms and Ammunition from the possession of Mizoram Police.

 

  1. 2 Nos. of AK-47 with several rounds of ammunitions.
  2. 2 Nos. of 9 mm Glock Pistol.
  3. 3 Nos. 9 mm Pistols.
  4. 1 No. of 0.38 Revolver.

 

            The HPC(D) cadres also fired upon the vehicles of 3 (three) MLsA causing partial damage.

 

            The injured persons were air-lifted from Sakawrdai at around 12:45 Pm and safely reached Aizawl at around 3:15 Pm and they were administered medical treatments at Civil Hospital, Aizawl on the same day.

           

            A funeral service for the 3 (three) martyrs of Mizoram Police was held on 29.3.2015 in front of 1st Bn MAP Commandant office, Aizawl. Chief Minister, Ministers, Parliamentary Secretaries, MLAs, Chief Secretary, DGP and senior officials paid their last respects by laying wreaths on the coffins of the three martyrs.

 

            Speaking during the funeral service, Pu Lal Thanhawla, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Mizoram strongly condemned the ambush and said that such kind of violence is a disgusting challenge. "We accept their challenge and we shall respond to it as per conveniene," he said. The Chief Minister also announced an ex-gratia of Rs. 7 lakhs to the next kin of the three slained Policemen. He also consoled the bereaved families by assuring jobs under Police Department. Pu R. Lalzirliana, Hon'ble Home Minister also said that the blood of the slained Policemen who had served the State will not go in vain.
