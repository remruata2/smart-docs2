CNA/CDF - Chinland Joint Defence Committee (CJDC) was formed on April 4, 2021 by the 17 (seventeen) factions of the Civil Defence Forces (CDF) in Chin State, Myanmar. In its initiative for the formation of Interim Government of Chinland, CJDC had decided to prepare a Constitutional Draft. In pursuance of CJDC decision, 'Future Chinland Constitutional Planning Workshop' was held during 7th - 8th September, 2023 at Central YMA Hall, Aizawl, Mizoram. The programme, which was presided over by Mr. Zing Cung, CNF Chairman, was attended by representatives from 16 (sixteen) factions and they had formed a Constitutional Drafting Committee comprising of 31 (thirty one) members. Mr. Ramkul Cung, CNF Assistant General Secretary, Mr. Tial Ling, MP and Salai Paul, CDF Paletwa were the Conveners of The Drafting Committee and the members consisted of 5 (five) representatives from CNF, 9 (nine) Members of Parliament, and 1 (one) representative each from CDF/PDF.

            The Final Draft of the Constitution, drafted by the Constitution Drafting Committee was approved by the Working Committee for Chinland Council at a Conference held at Camp Victoria between 27.11.2023 to 1.12.2023.             i)  Bangladesh Government - KNF dialogue:

            After a series of  online meeting, "First round of Physical Peace Talk" between Kuki-Chin National Front/Army (KCNA) and Government of Bangladesh (GoB) represented by the Peace Committee was held on 5th November, 2023 at Hmunlai village, near Ruma bazar, Bandarbun District, Bangladesh. Although no definite agreements were made, the meeting, agreed to maintained cease-fire between both the parties until the next peace talk. The KNF has submitted a draft of possible and proposed peace agreement with 6(six) point demands to the Prime Minister of Bangladesh through Peace Establishment Committee. The second round of bilateral physical meeting between the two parties is scheduled to be conducted on 05.03.2024 at Hmunlai Village, near Ruma Bazar, Bandarban District, Bangladesh.
