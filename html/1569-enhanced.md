 With reference to your letter no. cited above, I am sending herewith the required information as under:

****(a)** Details of bomb blast occurred in Mizoram State during the last three years and the current year are as under:**

1) On 17.9.2009@10:20pm, some unknown miscreants had exploded some explosive substances at NSUI office, Congress Bhawan, Aizawl and that the explosion damaged door, window panes and cracked the cement floor. Moreover, 73 taxi drivers were detained in police custody and released after recording their statements. Hence, Aizawl PS C/No.326/09 dt.18.9.09 u/s 436 IPC r/w 3 of ES Act was registered.

2) On 5.4.2010@9:30pm, two explosions took place within a span of three minutes inside the camp area of Pushpak Hqrs., Thuampui at the site of office complex and Supervisors' mess. In this connection, BKN PS C/No.161/10 dt.6.4.10 u/s 3(a) ES Act was registered.

3) On 13.3.2011@11:50pm, 33KV double power line located at Thuampui, Aizawl was attacked with explosion/bomb but fortunately caused no damage.

4) On 18.3.2011@10:45pm, 250KVA Transformer at Rangvamual was attacked with explosives believed to be gelatine. No major damage was caused except leakage of oil chamber. In this connection, Vaivakawn PS C/No.32/11 u/s 435 IPC r/w 3(a) ES Act was registered.

**(b)** The value of losses is not known and no persons killed nor injured on the aforesaid blasts.
