**Questions &Answers****:-**

1. **How did you join Bru underground/gang?**

****Ans:** In the year 2014 at the end f June while I was at my uncle's home at Darlak in Mamit, BDFM Vice President Lianphunga, his friend S/S. Pvt. Laipang of NLFT, husband of Sare-i and two other men came to Darlak, they were the guests of MP Raipha near our house, I didn't know the purpose of their visit but we got acquainted. Pu Lianphunga convinced me that instead of doing nothing I should join him to find a job with good pay and I did without my family's approval.**

 We left Darlak for West Phaileng in Sumo and we reached West Phulpui in Tipper from West Phaileng. After a night walking on foot, we reached Bangladesh the next day.

1. **What is your rank?**

**Ans:** I have no rank.

1. **Did you go through any training?**

**Ans:** No, I didn't have/get any training.

1. **Who are your leaders?**

**Ans:** I don’t know much except Lianphunga and Duhawma.

1. **What was your duty/responsibility and where was your post?**

**Ans:** As I am untrained, I was not issued any arms. I did menial work and carried the loads/belongings of my mates when we had operation/went out.

1. **What is the status of BDFM's Arms and Ammunition?**

**Ans:** I have no idea and I have not even seen them.

1. **Were you involved in kidnap incident or any operation?**

**Ans:** As I said, I was in Bru underground/gang for only three (3) weeks and I was never involved in such incident.

1. **Why did you flee from your camp?**

**Ans:** Our leaders said, "You cannot work properly so you can go home", then I just followed Flower and John to come back.

1. **How many of you were arrested and where?**

**Ans:** Five (5) of us were arrested at Silchar.

1. **Are you all from Bru underground/gang? Who are they?**

**Ans:** Yes, all of us from Bru underground/gang. And we are - Thlamuana, Endro Msha, John and Zorama. I got to know Zorama only from Silchar and he is an overground working for Bru underground/gang.

1. **Why were you following Thlamuana before being arrested this time?**

**Ans:** On 3rd July, 2014 Thlamuana asked my friends Flower and John to meet him at Hazachera saying that he would help to solve our case, and that with the help of HPC(D), we would be able to surrender. So, we met him at Hazachera and he took us around from there.
