MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/321

Dated 23rd April, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/320
Dt. 22.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          5 (five) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-2, LMV-0) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 23.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-17, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 23.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and
2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

76 (seventy six) vehicles (HMV-64, MMV-2, LMV-9, Maxi Cab-1) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 23.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Shri Debojit Mukherjee, DIG Southern Range, Silchar, Assam visited the disputed area at Assam-Mizoram border (Zophai area) today i.e., 23.4.2021 @ 3:32 p.m. and he was accompanied by Shri Pabindra Kumar Nath, SP, Hailakandi. Pu Timothy R. Lalhmangaiha MCS, BDO Bilkhawthlir received Assam Police officials at the site and Assam Police Officials informed him that they visited the site to take stock of the situation. They left the area @ 3:52 p.m. today’s evening.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

150 (one hundred fifty) vehicles (HMV-70, MMV-26, LMV-17,
LPG-4, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-25, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 23.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
