****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/25

Dated: 10th January, 2022

Information received stated that during 13.12.2021 to 16.12.2021 Demand Letters using HPC (D) official Letter Head demanding money (ranging from Rs. 30,000/- to Rs. 1,60,000/-) was served to non-Mizos, who are paddy field caretakers at Saiphai and Chemphai (Bilkhawthlir), Kolasib District. It was later found that HPC (D) Lalhmingthanga Sanate faction served the stated Demand Letters. In this connection, a case has been registered at Vairengte PS vide C/1/22 Dt 3.1.22 u/s 120B/384/511/34 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act and necessary investigation is being underway.

Upon enquiry, it was found that HPC (D) Lalhmingthanga Sanate faction reportedly served demand letter to non-Mizos for purchase of Arms and ammunitions to fight against Assam Police, who are presently deployed along the inter-state border in connection with recent inter-state border dispute. Input received indicated that their present cadres’ strength is about 25 (twenty five) with the below mentioned persons as their commanders:

1. SS Commander in chief Cum Commissioner RC. Singson @ Ramchanlova  @ RC. Sanga of Thenzawl, Serchhip District Mizoram
2. SS. Commander Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea of Saiphai, Kolasib District.

3. SS. Commander Lalhmangaihsanga @ Johna of Muallian, NC Hills.

Input received also stated that recently Lalhmingthanga Sanate held a meeting with RC Singson, at Hmarkhawlian, Cachar District, Assam, and they decided to take necessary action under the name of Mizoram Border Defence Army (MBDA) as they opined that it is not appropriate to take necessary action under the name of HPC (D) and also decided to make MBDA Letter Head. It is also reported that RC. Singson @ Ramchanlova @ RC. Sanga of Thenzawl SS Commander in chief Cum Commissioner stated that they intended not to take any action/activity against government of Mizoram and zo-ethnic residents of Mizoram. However, they intend to collect donation from non-Mizos presently staying within the state.
Information received further stated that Lalhmingthanga Sanate also contacted People Defence Force (PDF), Myanmar and informed his readiness to procure Arms and Ammunitions from National Socialist Council of Nagaland – Khaplang (NSCN- (K)) faction.

It is further reported that so far, HPC(D)/MBDA has no permanent Camp/Headquarters and presently used Hmarkhawlian as their temporary Headquarters and are presently stationed along Mizoram-Assam border. The family members of RC Singson (wife and children) are presently staying at Thenzawl, Serchhip District. 

It may be worth mentioning that attempt has been made to find out the exact location of HPC (D)/MBDA cadres without success so far and it may be necessary to conduct operation so as to apprehend the cadres after ascertaining their exact location.
