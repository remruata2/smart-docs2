Subject:          Installation of CCTV cameras.

 

Ref:                 No.PHQ/OPS-10/2012/9 dt.12.1.2012

 

 

Sir,

                        With reference to the above, I am to state that considering the rising trend of crime and criminal activities and in order to curb anti-social elements, criminals, loose characters, habitual offenders etc frequenting in the city, it has been rightly felt that installation of CCTV cameras at crime-prone areas would definitely supplement and aid police investigation, prevention of crime and criminals etc. As you are aware, the number of crimes took place proportionately and depending upon the density of population living in a particular area/locality. As such, most crimes took place in tally with the density of the people.

 

                        Considering the terrain, road condition and volume of traffic etc within Aizawl city, the following places are listed as strategic locations for the installation of CCTVs, keeping in mind that proper monitoring/control room be set-up for quick police action manned by adequate staffs with logistic support.

 

           

  1.  

Thuampui tri junction.
  1.  

Bawngkawn-Durtlang road junction.
  1.  

Bawngkawn-Chaltlang-Ramhlun road junction.
  1.  

Chaltlang dawrkawn.
  1.  

Chanmari-Chaltlang-Ramhlun road junction.
  1.  

Zarkawt traffic point.
  1.  

Dawrpui traffic point (near Millenium Centre).
  1.  

Israel Point bus stand.
  1.  

Bazar Bungkawn.
  1.  

Zodin Square.
  1.  

Treasury Square.
  1.  

Khatla traffic point.
  1.  

Sikulpuikawn traffic point.
  1.  

Kulikawn-Saikhamakawn-Tlangnuam junction.
  1.  

Vaivakawn junction.
  1.  

Power House bus stand.
  1.  

New Market areas covering Zion Street, market building etc.

 

                        Further, I am to state that other vulnerable/logistic locations outside Aizawl District for installation of CCTV's may be worked by concerned S'sP of the Districts.
