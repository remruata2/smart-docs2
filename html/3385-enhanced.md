A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 24.4.2014_

****Pseudoephedrine Seized at Post Office****:**12.400 kgs. of Pseudoephedrine was seized by Mizoram Police yesterday at Kulikawn Post Office. The package came from Delhi in the address of Zomuanpuia (29) s/o Remliana of Melriat. Zomuanpuia was arrested earlier on Tuesday by Police when he was about to collect Pseudoephedrine sent by post at Kulikawn post office.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 24.4.2014_

**Remsangi Confessed to the Police****:**Lalremsangi Fanai of Falkawn confessed to the Police that all the 310 kgs. of Pseudoephedrine seized by Police at Ngaizel on Thursday and Friday was indeed her own. She was taken out from Central Jail for interrogation, investigation is being carried on to uncover if more people are involved in the crime.
