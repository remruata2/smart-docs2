INTERROGATION STATEMENT OF

MAYENGBAM MAICHAN MEITEI

 

 

                        I was from Wabagai Mayal Leikai village, Manipur, having two brothers and one sisters. I studied upto Class  X  in Khianglam High School.

 

                        I enrolled myself in PREPAK in the year 1996 in the month of February as SS Private and I got promotion in the rank of Corporal in the year 2007. Our Headquarters is at Thingnungan Myanmar and established a training camp at Khamti which is also in Myanmar. Prepak was headed by K.Nongdrem Khomba under whom around 150 outfit enrolled and possession around 230 weapon of various type like AK- 47, M-16 M-23,RPG-7, 60-MM Rocket Launcher, Pistol,Grebade,M-79. We had undergone weapon training under Naga National Council instructors.

 

                        In the year 2007 PREPAK was splitted into four groups and I followed one of the factions led by Chinglemba which is name United Peoples Party of Kangleipak We, UPPK  comprised around 70-80 outfits of various rank having weapons of different types like AK-47,RPG-7 M-23,M-16, 60 MM Mortar, Pistol , I don’t know the exact amount of weapon we are possessing.

 

                        Aims and objective of UPPK is to achieve economic self sufficient  of Manipur which we turned it ‘ Green Revolution’ and to eradicate corruption in the state  of Manipur.

 

                        I and my friends M.Rogen Singh and Homeshor Singh were directed to proceed to Aizawl by our top leader Chinglemba to purchase a second hand vehicle Gypsy or Maruti from Aizawl to Guwahati for use of our group. While reaching river Tiau we were received by one driver and other unknown person by red colour Maruti (Zen) and carried us upto Aizawl. The unknown fellow could speak broken Manipuri language and we arrived Aizawl on 9.1.2009 @ 11:00 PM and lodged at K.T.Hotel which is arranged by one driver and his friend. While we are sleeping in the Hotel @ 12 midnight, we were arrested by plain clothes Police and brought to CID Office. 

 

                        This journey to  Aizawl is my first experience and my two friends also do not visit Aizawl before arrested by Police party they seized our articles like Mobile Handsets, Driving License, Passports and money Rs. 1,80,000/- (one lakh eighty thousand) appx. Which we carried for purchase of vehicle and expenditure of our journey.

 

                        On 23.3.1999 at around 3:30 PM our fighting group led by Mr. Chinglem Singh,SS Sgt. Major left Parbung, Churachandpur District and proceeded towards Vaitin village, Mizoram for purchasing medicine. We went on foot in the hilly tracts. At that time I hold one M-16 Rifle with 85 rounds. We crossed Lungthulian Hmar village and Sihpuikawn Hmar village (both in Churachandpur  Dist, Manipur) . On the same day at around 6 PM we crossed Manipur border and entered Mizoram. We halted the night in Mizoram jungle.

 

                        On the following day i.e. 24.3.1999 in the early morning we proceeded towards Vaitin village, Mizoram and reached there at around 4 PM.

 

                        In the meantime a team of Mizoram Police in one Gypsy rushed towards us. We ambushed the Police party and an encounter took place for about 4/5 minutes.

 

                        In that encounter two of my colleagues received bullet injuries. They are Mr Bodmao SS Cpl on his right chest and Mr. Lanchenba SS L/Cpl on his left wrist and 6(six) Police personnel who took part in the encounter were killed.

 

                        Six nos. of arms i.e. 3(three) AK-47, 1(one) SLR, 1(one) Carbine and 1(one) .38 Pistol were collected from the deceased Police.

 

                        After that we did not stay anymore at Vaitin. We returned back to Parbung.  We reached Parbung on the same day at around 3 PM.

 

                        Thus I was involved in the encounter with Mizoram Police party at Vaitin, Mizoram on 24th March 1999.
