A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.13.3.2013

Black flag: A black flag was hanged up in the office of N.Vanlaiphai SDO(C) yesterday to indicate the displeasure of Tuichangral Zirlai Pawl (TZP) at the government for the improper functioning of N.Vanlaiphai SDO(C) office since 1998. They are also displeased about not having a permanent SDO(C). A black flag was also hanged up at N.Vanlaiphai Hospital as it has no doctor since September last year.

 

 

B. Front page VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.13.3.2013

Pseudoephedrine seized: Taxation Department Staff seized 23,31,200 tablets of pseudoephedrine at the taxation checkgate at Vairengte yesterday in a truck bearing regd. No. NL-01 D-8415, they handed it over to Vairengte Police Station.

 

 

C. Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.13.3.2013

Best Traffic Police: Serchhip District SP Romil Baanya, IPS notifies that a committee was formed to choose the Best Traffic Staff in Serchhip District. Lalremtluanga, Addl. SP is its chairman, T.Rosiama, President, ZDU Sub-Hqrs, Serchhip and Kapliana Pachuau, President MJA, Serchhip District are the members in the Committee.
