Subject:   People March organised by AIFUCTO.

 

 

                  This is to inform you that on 19.03.2020 (Thursday), All India Federation of University & College Teachers Organization (AIFUCTO) are intending to hold People March from Mandi House to Jantar Mantar, New Delhi, under the leadership of Professor Arun Kumar, General Secretary, AIFUCTO in connection with their demands. Therefore, you are directed to collect the following information if any group/person from Mizoram is planning to attend the said People March:

 

1. No. of participants.

2. Mode of Transport to reach Delhi.

3. Name, Address and Telephone No. of the Leaders.

4. Place of stay in Delhi.

5. Any other information related to Law & Order.

6. Any political affiliation.

                 

                  The information so collected may be submitted on or before 18.3.2020 (Wednesday) for onward submission to Special Branch, Delhi.
