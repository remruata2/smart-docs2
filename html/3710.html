SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/099

       Dated: 9th February, 2021     

 

 

Information has been received that  road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) was started by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP on 24.12.2020 and it was halted the same day after Officer-in-Charge, Bairabi PS directed the workers not to continue their work. However, the road widening work was resumed today i.e., 9.2.2021 by engaging one excavator (JCB).

Today’s afternoon @ around 2:15 p.m., Pu H. Vanlalrova, Bairabi North VC Member and 2 (two) staff of P&E Department who conducted survey for erection of electric poles at the area videographed the road widening work undertaken by Assam. While they were doing their duty, they were physically assaulted by residents of Assam near the area and they then immediately retreat towards Mizoram side for their personal safety. Due to the physical assault, Pu H. Vanlalrova complained of suffering pain on his back.

Upon learning the above incident, personnel of Zophai BOP who conducted patrolling near the area immediately rushed to the area and SDPO, Kolasib; OC, Bairabi PS and Party also immediately arrived at the spot to control further escalation of tension. OC, Ramnathpur PS and staff, Assam also arrived at the spot to control the situation. Around 45 (forty five) youths of Assam carrying bamboo sticks gathered at the area and similarly around 30 (thirty) residents of Bairabi gathered at Mizoram side. Both parties pelted stone towards each other but no serious physical injury has been reported from either side. Announcement was made through PA system at Bairabi urging youths of Bairabi to proceed to the area and around 200 (two hundred) youths of Bairabi gathered at the area. However, they dispersed around 6:00 p.m. and in the meantime around 4 (four) farm huts occupied by Non-mizo paddy field workers located in between Zophai BOP and the said road widening site were set on fire by unknown miscreants. Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP Kolasib proceeded to the spot to take stock of the situation. 1(one) platoon each of CRPF and IR Battalion have been rushed to the spot to control the situation.

                        Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
