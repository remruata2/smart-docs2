**Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 13.5.2013**

**Saikhumphai aids stopped on the way**: The MZP(Mizo Zirlai Pawl) and MSU(Mizo Student Union) who were heading for Sakhumphai with two Mahindra pick-up vehicles full of essential commodities to be handed over to the homeless and starving Saikhumphai settlers were blocked at Dungtlang village by the Church leaders of Vaphai and Government officials.

 The people of Vaphai did not pay heed to the students activists prayer that they were not to help them rebuild the village, but just to give them their daily needs.

 Meanwhile the Mizoram Government is gearing up to challenge the Gauhati High Court order. LAD Minister Pc.Lalthanliana was quoted as saying that a decision in this regard has been taken and Government Advocate is studying the High Courts Order.
