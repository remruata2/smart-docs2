A. Front page of VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 22.6.2022

ACB investigation report awaited in skywalk and ropeway case ;  Tourism dept has no suspects:  The Lokayukta is awaiting the Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) investigation report in connection with a complaint on the construction of skywalk at  Sakawrhmuituai hill and ropeway between Chaltlang and Durtlang.  Meanwhile, the Tourism department the other complainant did not mention any specific suspect or company in their complaint.

            MNF party Vigilance department and Legal Board filed a complaint to the Lokayukta on 22nd November, 2022 which alleged presence of irregularities in the construction of the above stated skywalk and ropeway. Further, Mizoram Tourism department, the nodal department for construction of the said skywalk and ropeway, also submitted a complaint to the Lokayukta on 3rd February, 2022. The Tourism department, however, did not mention any suspected individuals/companies.

The Lokayukta, after conducting necessary scrutiny on the complaint of MNF Legal Board, decided that the case should be investigated by Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB).  In connection with a complaint of Tourism Department, the Lokayukta after receiving clarification from Tourism Department on their queries, it then sent to ACB for further investigation. In both the cases, the Lokayukta has yet to receive a report from ACB.

           

 

B. Page 2 of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.6.2022

AMC office announced its office as single use plastic free office:  Aizawl Municipal Corporation (AMC) Mayor Pu Lalrinenga Sailo on Wednesday declared the AMC office as a single use plastic free office. AMC also held a cleanliness drive the same day.

            AMC said that  based on Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016  the central government has ban the use of single use plastic as it could damage the environment. In this regard, AMC also issued an order on 1st August, 2019 which ban the use of plastic thinner than 50 microns.

            The order was amended by the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules, 2021, which prohibits the use, storage and sale of bags thinner than 75 microns.  Preparations are being made to ban plastic below120 microns in thickness from 1st December, 2022.

            After issuing ban on the use of bags below 50 microns, the AMC inspected the use of plastic bags below the permitted limit. So far, 5,540 Kgs of illegal plastic bags have been confiscated and violators of the law were punished under the Mizoram Municipalities Act, 2007.

 

C. Front Page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 22.6.2022

Copy Rights violators of Mizo movie apprehended:  As per the Police report, a pirated copy of the recently released Mizo movie -"ka U Liana" was circulated  several times through one social media platform i.e., Telegram by one Telegram user named 'Melvyn YT'. In pursuance of a complaint submitted by Runmawi App owners, Cyber Police Station, CID (Crime) apprehended Lalremsiama (22), Bethlehem Veng, Aizawl, who is alleged to be the main perpetrator behind this case and registered a case vide Cyber Crime PS Case No. 64/22 u/s 63 of Copy Right Act & Sec 72 of IT Act.

            In connection with the above, officers from the Cyber Police Station appealed the public to refrain from making and circulating pirated copy as it is violation of copy rights Act. The violators can be punished under IPC, IT Act , Copy right Act &Cinematograph Act, etc.
