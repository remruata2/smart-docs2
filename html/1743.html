1.

 

 

 

Hmar People’s Convention-Democrat     (HPC-D) 

 

Murder/Ambushed/War against State:

 

    (i)On Dt.2.9.2008, sixteen (16) security personnel of Mizoram First Indian Reserve Battalion (IR.Bn) were ambushed by HPC(D) outfit militants at the outskirt of Saipum village within Kolasib District. During a fierce encounter, the HPC (D) militants had killed four (4) IR Security personnel.

 

   (ii)On Dt.18.4.2006 Isaac Lalmalsawma Hmar S/o Prof. Laldena of Imphal camped Aizawl was murdered near, Zemabawk.

 

Extortion/Criminal Intimidation:

 

     (i)During 1.8.2009 to 30.9.2009, HPC(D)  raised  a sum of Rs 2, 49,400.00 through extortion notes from different villages of the  bordering Mizoram-Manipur area.

 

    (ii)On 22.2.2012 the State election commission of Mizoram, indefinitely postponed village council election in 15 villages following HPC-D's diktat to vote for the outfit-favoured candidates.

 

   (iii) On April, 2012 the HPC-D served a ‘dissolution order’ to all YMA branches in the HPC-D demand area in north and northeastern Mizoram.

 

   (iv)The HPC(D) militants had intimidated Village councils leaders of New Vervek, Sunhluchhip, Sailutar, Ratu, Daido, Skawrdai, Saiphai just prior to polling day of Mizoram Legislative Assembly Election-2013 intimidating them to vote for ZNP Official candidates.

 

   (v)On 20.11.2013, five (5) HPC (D) militants shot Pu.B.Zahunga, Campaigner of Congress Party at the outskirt of Khawlian village.

 

Blasting of Bomb(i.e Gelatine):

 

    (i)On Dt.30.1.2014 @ 10:55 am, gelatin was exploded from one vegetable shop at Treasury's Square market, Aizawl causing  severe damages to the shop and adjacent shops.

 

    (ii)On the night of 20.2.2014 at 11:45 pm, an explosive (i.e gelatin) was exploded at the entrance of New Market meat shop (old building),Aizawl.

 

    (iii)A powerful bomb (i.e gelatin) exploded near the residence of Pu Hmingchungnunga, Chairman, Sinlung Hills Development Council (SHDC), Bungkawn Vengthar on the morning of 22.3.2014 @ 3:50 AM damaging over 220 numbers of window panes/ventilation glasses.

 

    (iv)A powerful bomb (i.e gelatine) exploded in the morning of 22.7.2014 @2:35 am at Bungkawn Vengthar, Aizawl damaging some vehicles (including Mizoram Police Gypsy) and household properties, i.e. window panes, glasses, etc

 

    (v)On Dt.1.8.2014 at about 3:45 am, a powerful bomb (i.e gelatine) exploded near the residence of Pu Lt.Col (Rtd).ZS. Zuala, Hon'ble Parliamentary Secretary (Home, etc) at Mission Veng, Aizawl.

 

 

 

2.

 

Borok National Council of Tripura (BNCT) @ Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram (BNDLFM) @ Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM)

 

   (i)On 25.11.2012 a combined militant group of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Borok National Council of Tripura(BNCT) abducted 3(three) Bengali teak traders from Ragiv Nagar (Tuipuibari-II).

 

     (ii)On Dt.18.2.2013, 5(five) Environment &Forest Department, Government of Mizoram Muster Roll workers were abducted by joint operation team of NLFT and  Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram (BNDLFM).

 

   (iii) On Dt 5.6.2013 at around 10:00 AM, three (3) persons of Gautam Construction Company workers namely were abducted by a combined militants group of NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) and BNDLFM (Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from the village of Bodalsuri (4 Km from Rajiv Nagar), Mamit District.

 

    (iv)  On Dt.23.11.2013, one Deep Mandal and two Mizo drivers were abducted by a combined militants group of NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) and BNDLFM (Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from Old Chika Anti-poaching Camp area within Dampa Sanctuary, Mamit District.

 

    (v) On Dt.14.6.2014 at around 12:30 PM, a combined operation team of NLFT and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) abducted 5(five) persons near the tri-junction of Mizoram-Tripura-Bangladesh.

 

    (vi) On Dt.10.10.2014 a combined militant group of NLFT and BDFM abducted 11(eleven) construction workers near the tri-junction of Mizoram-Tripura-Bangladesh.

Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram( BLFM)

 

On Dt.30.1.2004, militants of BLFM kidnapped/abducted 3 (three) villagers of Tuipuibari, Mamit District while fishing in the Amsuri river near Tuipuibari and demanded Rs.10 lakhs as ransom.
