A. _Front page of VANGLAINI &AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 23.4.2013_

_A man killed his own mother with a big hoe._

 At around 4:30 Am Yesterday, Lawmsangzuala(34) of Tuirial killed his mother Lailianchhungi(67) by hitting her with a hoe on her head. Lailianchhungi died on the spot and Lawmsangzuala was handed over to Bawngkawn Police by leaders of Tuirial Community.

 Lawmsangzuala is a drunkard but went dry since Tuesday; leaders of Tuirial Community believed that he killed his mother in a state of deliriousness caused by his temporary abstinence from alcohol.

**Front page of AIZAWL POST &VANGLAINI**

_Langkaih bridge to be closed :_

 The meeting of Langkaih group YMA at Kanhmun decided that the Langkaih bridge between Tripura and Mizoram be closed down indefinitely from 5:00 Pm of 29th April 2013 for Bru wrongly accused Mizos to be involved in the death of Sibaram Reang and organised a strike in Damcherra.

 Langkaih group YMA plans to have a closing function of Langkaih bridge in the evening of 29th April 2013, and expects as many YMA members as possible to attend the function.
