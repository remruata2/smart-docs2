HPC(D) Cadres te MOS sign hnua an lo chhuah dan tur kawng leh an riahna CTI Sesawng inbuatsaihna report

 

HPC(D) Cadres mi sawmkua (90) bawr vel hi Dt.28.3.2018 atang khan Kani Phai (Tuiruang kam) ah an Deputy Commander-in-Chief Lalbiaknunga hovin  full kits leh thawmhnaw te nen an awm vek tawh a.

 

Dt.3.4.2017-ah lo chhuah inbeisei takin Mizoram Sawrkar thu leh hla ngaichangin inpeih takin an awm mek a. Zilthawchhuah (Tuiruang kam) ah rawn in chhhawpchhuak turin an inbeisei a, chumi tur chuan, Sawrkar lam atangin movement order an hmuh hun an ngaichang mek.

 

Vaitin - Zilthawchhuah kawng chungchang - He kawng hi truckable road ani a,14 Kms a thui niin  kawng a that loh avangin darkar khat leh a chanve ah thlen theih a ni. Tunah hian Bus kal theihna chi a ni lova, HPC(D) cadres te phurh nan hian 407 Truck in vawi li (4) vel Vaitin atangin tlan tir a ngai ang. Bus pali (4) in Vaitin-ah lo hmuah ni se. Helai kawng hi a that vak loh avangin 407 Truck full load tlan thei tur chuan repair a ngai hle a ni.

 

HPC(D) te lo chhuak hi Sakawrdai VCP Pu R.Lalfakzuala leh VC members te chuan demand area  khawpui ber an nih angin chaw  lo buatsaih an duh thu an sawi a, chaw ei khamah an awmna hmun tur CTI panin kal nise tha an tih thu an sawi a. Dt.2.4.2018 hian ei leh in buatsaih dan tur relin thutkhawm an tum.

 

CTI Sesawng inbuatsaihna - CTI-ah hian Barrack pahnih (2), viz. Darfeng Barrack leh Band Barrack te a awm a. Darfeng Barrack-ah hian mi sawmriat (80) vel an leng a, Band Barrack-ah hian mi sawmli (40) vel an leng thung. Electric wiring/fitting of lights, etc. erawh tih that a ngai.

 

Toilet chungchang - Darfeng Barrack ah hian toilet (Pit latrine) paruk a awm a, tha tawk tura ngaih a ni. Amaherawhchu, Band Barrack ah hian Toilet a awm lova, pali (4) tal siam chawp a ngai.

 

Tui - Tui hi a stock-na tha an nei lo va, amaherawhchu, Tank lorry tui phur Truck pakhat a awm a, hei hian mamawh tawk chu a thiar theih mai a rinawm a, chutih rual chuan water tank (Sintex) pahnih khat tui stock-na tur awm thei se.

 

Choka - Choka thingtuah lak hi an harsat thu Centre Commander, CTI atanga hriat ani a. Chuvangin, Gas thuk lian panga (5) leh domestic gas cylinder sawm (10) aia tlem lo dah ni se.

                                               

Bungbel - Bungbel hi duh ang tawk an neih daih loh thu leh Dekchi, thleng leh no engemawzat hawhsak theih nise an duhthu an sawi.

 

Samiana & Kanad - CTI in Samiana leh Kanad a neih te hi a chhe hlawm tawh hle a, programme leh function lian tham leh pawimawh bik atana hmanah chuan a rintlak tawh lo hle  a. PHQ, Aizawl atanga hawh/hman tur a ruahman ni se.

 

HPC(D) Cadres family - CTI Sesawng-ah hian HPC(D) cadres te chhungkaw hnih (2) bak  accomodate theihna hmun hma a awm lo.

 

CID(SB) - SB staff te tan lodging-ah harsatna a awm lo.

 

Cook - HPC(D) Cadres te eirawngbawl saktu tur chu CTI atangin ngaihtuah mai theih a ni.

 

Parade Ground - CTI-ah hian Laying of Arms Ceremony hmanna turin Parade Ground pakhat a awm a, hetah hian VIP Pavillion pakhat leh Pavillion dang pakhat a awm bawk a ni. Laying of Arms Ceremony atan uluk taka cheibawl erawh duhthusam a ni.

 

Volunteers Hall - He Hall hi a tul dan azirin riah nan pawh hman theih tur ani a, amaherawhchu, Toilet a awmlo thung.  

 

Electric Supply - Electric supply hi a tha pangngai hle a, fur hunlai te anih loh chuan current supply-ah harsatna a awm lutuk lo.

 

Zu - CTI-ah hian Wine Shop a awm lo. Sesawng leh Mualmam leh Tuikhurhlu ah te a awm thung a. Zu in chungchangah chuan control theih a beiseiawm.

Conclusion: HPC(D) Cadre mi 102 bawr vel CTI a dah tur hian a chunga ruahmanna hrang hrang siam te hi a rang lama tih hlawhtlin theih ni se a duhawm hle ang. Heng HPC(D) te hi CTI-ah hian rei tak dah chi anni lova, a chhan chu ei leh in mai bakah CTI a family awm te, training nei mek te leh a chhehvel a civil mi awm te nen inhmuhthiam lohna awm thei a ni.

 

Hei bakah hian nitin a sum senso hi hisap lawk aia tam senral theih a ni a, anmahni an inngaihtuah theih nan an pawisa dawn tur pawh a rang thei ang berin Sawrkar in ngaihtuahsak theih ni se. CTI atang hian senso a tlem zawk theih nan a hma thei ang bera chhuah tir turin ruahmanna fel fai siam lawk ni se. Rei deuh zawk awm tura ruahman anih chuan ei leh in leh tul dang atan senso tur fund pawh tam zawkin ruahman theih ni se.

 

Laying of Arms Ceremony neih ni atan hian VIP te leh invitees te thutna tur, sofa set leh chair, sound system leh a hmun hma fel fai taka buatsaih anih theih nan, a hmunhma neitu Commandant, CTI hnenah ngenna (instruction) pek mai ni se.
