****STATEMENT OF SI LALBIAKMAWII RENTHLEI****

****S.O CID(SB) CHAMPHAI POST****

 SO Champhai Post ah hian dt. 14.1.2011 atangin ka awm tan a, dt. 26.4.2013 khan Gauhati High Court Aizawl Bench thuchhuak avanga Vaphai mipui te reaction te advance a information collect turin ASI Lalthansiama leh C/20 Rokhuma te chu Vaphai ah kal turin ka tir a. Dt. 26.4.2013 khan Vaphai an pan thu leh mobile phone signal a that loh thu te an rawn sawi a. Tichuan dt. 27.4.2013 khan Champhai a rawn phei a report kimchang rawn pe a Hqrs. lama report leh nghal a ngaih avangin ka ko phei a. Dt. 28.4.2013 khan report kimchang ka hnenah min rawn pe in keiin Hqrs. lamah report ka dawn ang chu thlen leh chhawng turin Aizawl ah pute phalna angin ka kal a. Tichuan boruak liantham thleng mai tur awm source atang pawha kan hriat loh avangin ngaihtha takin kan awm laiin dt. 29.4.2013 khan Saikhumphai khua hal ani tih source te atanga ka hriat veleh ka naute pahnih ASI Lalthansiama leh C/20 Rokhuma te chu phone in Vaphai pan nghal turin ka tir nghal ani. Chutichuan kei pawh ngaihtha takin Aizawl lamah ka kal ta ani.

Q. Dt. 29.4.2013 a Saikhumphai hal ani tih hi khawia i awm laiin nge i hriat?

= Seling leh Aizawl inkara ka awm laiin source ten phone in min rawn hrilh a, tichuan ka naute pahnih pawh ka tir phei nghal ani.

Q. Eng ti turin nge Aizawl i rawn kal?

= Vaphai leh Saikhumphai chungchanga detail report pe tura rawn kal ka ni a. Hetihlai hian thil duhawmlo nasa zawk a lo thlen leh tak siah chuan pute thu angin dt. 30.4.2013 ah Aizawl chhuahsanin Champhai ah ka let leh nghal ani.
