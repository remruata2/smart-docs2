B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 12.7.2013

Daughter rapist arrested: A man called Matu Sanga of Phairuangkai village alleged of raping his own daughter age 14 was arrested yesterday by Lungsen Police. According to reports, the wife of the accused had been secretly suspicious and went back to the house from her way to their jhum farm; and found her husband trying to rape their daughter. The daughter confessed that her father often had sexual intercourse with her.

 

C. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 12.7.2013

Escaped arrestee re-arrested: In the morning of 10th July, in Myanmar area of Lianhna road, Laldenga Khenthang (47), who escaped from excise lock-up was re-arrested by Zokhawthar Police. Laldenga Khenthang was arrested by Excise & Narcotics, Aizawl on 8th July as 8 Hawng of Heroin was seized from his hands but he ran away. He was brought to Aizawl by Zokhawthar Police on the day he was re-arrested. Laldenga Khenthang already had a case of smuggling abusive substances.
