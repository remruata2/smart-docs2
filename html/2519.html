SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/437

Dated: 5th June, 2021      

 

          Information has been received that 2 (two) unoccupied farm huts located at approximately 1.1 km north of Zophai Mizoram Police BOP, Kolasib district, towards Assam side, were burnt down by some unknown miscreant(s) last evening, i.e., 4.6.2021 @ around 9:30 p.m. The said land is reportedly owned by Sangchhingpuii, d/o Thanhnuna Varte of Saitual and the farm huts were previously occupied by Ali Hussain (33), s/o Manik Uddin and Saidul Haque (35), s/o Zamir Ali, but left abandoned since border disputes occurred in February, 2021. No valuable properties other than the farm huts were damaged due to the fire.

          On receiving the above information, Inpr. Lalrinhlua, O/C Bairabi PS and team along with IR personnel of Zophai BOP immediately rushed to the spot today, i.e., 5.6.2021 @ 11:30 a.m. to take stock of the situation. Pu David J. Byhnadaolai, Addl. SP Kolasib also promptly visited the place of occurrence to do the needful. Further,
Pu Rohan Kumar Jha, IAS, DC; Pu Ramandeep Kaur, IPS, SP and Pu Chakraborty, APS, Addl. SP and 2 (two) other officials of Hailakandi district, Assam and around 20 (twenty) CRPF personnel also visited the spot to enquire into the matter. Subsequently, a meeting was held between officials of the 2 (two) states at Golachhorra Assam IR Post in which both sides agreed to extend cooperation and share any relevant information regarding the incident so as to detect the culprit(s). It was also affirmed in the meeting that status quo should continued to be maintained in the area and resolved to work together so as to prevent any further untoward incident.

          Bairabi PS Case No.11/2021 Dt.5.6.2021 u/s 436 IPC has been duly registered in this regard and necessary investigative steps are being undertaken.

          No further untoward incident or tension arises due to the above incident at the time of writing the report.

Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
