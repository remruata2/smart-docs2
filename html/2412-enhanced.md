****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/109

Dated 13th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/108 Dt. 12.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-7, LMV-6) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 13.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-15, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 13.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 1 (one) vehicle (MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Bairabi Joint VC and All NGOs meeting was held on 12.2.2021 @ 8:00 p.m. at Bairabi North VC House. Dr. H. Lalthlangliana, DC Kolasib chaired the meeting and 30 (thirty) members including officials viz., Pu Lalhuliana Fanai, DIG (NR), Pu J. Lalthafamkima, AIGP (Operations), Pu MS Dawngkima, CO 1st IR, Pu C. Vanlalvena, Addl. SP, Kolasib attended the meeting. The meeting reviewed the initiative taken on 12.2.2021 for construction of IR Temporary Camp at Ramri Lui. It was highlighted in the meeting that IR Temporary Camp construction was halted as Assam side also dismantled their newly constructed Camp at the area. The meeting further decided to wait state government initiative on the matter as the intended area for construction is not appropriate considering safety of the location from enemy attack. However, Joint VC Bairabi and all NGOs Bairabi still decided to construct IR Camp at the area, if necessary action is not taken by the state government.

Construction of road (around 300 meters in length) which will link Zophai BOP with Bairabi-Gharmura road was started today i.e., 13.2.2021. Pu C. Laldinpuia, the Contractor stated that he intends to complete the work soon.
Today's evening around 3:45 p.m. CCF, Cachar, DFO Hailakandi and Addl. DC, Hailakandi visited the disputed border area and they left the area after having short interaction with Pu C. Vanlalvena, Addl. SP Kolasib and Pu Gaston Vanlalhriatpuia, SDM, Kolasib who were present at the area. One section of IR and 6 (six) personnel of CRPF were deployed round the clock at the disputed area, Assam-Mizoram border to prevent any untoward incident. At the same time, Assam Police also made deployment at the area.

Bairabi Joint VC and All NGOs issued a statement today i.e., 13.2.2021 which stated that their intention to construct IR Temporary Camp today i.e., 13.2.2021 was kept in abeyance and decided to wait for the state government initiative in this regard. It further appealed everyone to wait for the statement made by Bairabi Joint YMA regarding border issues and urged not to take necessary action for their own.

52 (fifty two) vehicles (HMV-36, MMV-Nil, LMV-12, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 13.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
