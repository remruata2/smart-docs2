Ram leh hnam tana thawhpui duhawm tak,

            He lehkha hi ngaipawimawh a, ti dik ngei tuin kan ngen a che.

He hnam angin a hma kan lakna a mamawh tam tak i nei a. Heng kan mamawhte leh kan harstana phuhrukna turin hnam ta turin Rs. 20,000 khawtlangin in rawn pe ve turin kan rawn ngen a che.

            He ngena leh in hriattirna hi Dt. 21.12.2022 chhunga rawn ti dik ngei tura hriattir leh ngen in ni.
