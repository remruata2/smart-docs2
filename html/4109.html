NOTES ON LIA MEETING FOR 13.08.2024

I. Security scenario in Mizoram:

                        Security scenario in Mizoram is mostly peaceful. However, with the increase of influx of refugees into Mizoram due to the political condition in the neighbouring Myanmar and Bangladesh, some commercial activities are being exploited/used by these refugees to earn their living which is not welcome by the local residents of Mizoram.

                        (1) ZDU Gen. Hqrs. meeting conducted on 09.08.2024 discussed the issue of increasing number of non-indigeneous persons driving commercial vehicles recently in Mizoram.

                        (2) CYLA Lawngtlai in its statement reiterated that the association is against the illegal export of petroleum oil and fertilizers into Myanmar which are use to wage war against their brethren in Myanmar.

 

II. Present Law and Order Situation in Bangladesh:  The law and order situation has completely broken down and large scale vandalism goes on in Bangladesh.    

 

III. Conflict in Myanmar and its impact on Mizroam :

                                    (1) Import/Export of goods at Zokhawthar is strictly checked by Customs Department till date.

 

  IV.  Security Review: The 78th Independence Day Celebration of India:

         Public demonstration was held at Phuldungsei Village, Mmait District on Dt.09.08.2024 @ 08:00 am displaying placards urging the Govt. of Mizoram to revive the 10 deleted ration cards and members of their families due to the fact that the deleted members are indigineous of Mizoram. They demanded the State Govt. to address the issue before 14.08.2024 failing which they forewarn of boycotting the Celebration of Independence Day.

            In this regard, Supply Minister Pu B.Lalchhanzova addressed the situation and stated that the deleted family members will be given a manual ration card and will get their ration quotas manually till they get their normal ration cards which the Phuldungsei Jampui Village Council gratefully accepted. Hence, situation is normal and under close watch.
