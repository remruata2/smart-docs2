A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 8.1.2014_

**No news about missing persons****:** The whereabouts of two persons who have been missing recently is not known till last night and are being searched regularly.

 Robert Laltlanzova(30) of Hnahlan in Champhai District, an employee of Supply Department of Farkawn went missing on 5th January 2014 at about 4:00am at Farkawn. People of Farkawn and its neighbouring villages along with Hnahlan people had been looking for him and no news is heard till 7:00pm last night.

 No news is heard of Khamkhannanga(46) s/o Ngulkhansuma who had been missing since 20th December 2013 from the outskirt of Dulte. Police have detained one person in connection with the incident.
