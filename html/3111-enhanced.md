A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.8.2013_

**Picketing at Zawlnuam**: Zawlnuam NGO’s will continue to conduct picketing against government offices and closing of schools. Indefinite closure of High Schools and Higher Secondary institutions had been carried out at Zawlnuam to urge the government to post more lecturers, teachers and other employees at the Zawlnuam institutions, but as no good news is heard from the government, all NGO’s and all Village Council Joint meeting decided to continue picketing of government employees and closing of schools.

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.8.2013_

****LSA Total Bandh**: Lai Students’ Association General Headquarters Lawngtlai is calling a 24 hours total bandh in Lawngtlai town today in protest against the State administration’s failure to post Sociology and Geography teachers at Govt. Region High School, Lawngtlai. The students’ body conducted picketing in the town for 2 days during last week against government offices.**
