The arrested accused Floyd Ramfangzauva and the alleged owner of the weapons Lallaizauva are close relatives (cousins).

 

Ref.to Vaivakawn PS case no.223/'20 dt.5.9.20' u/s 25 (1AA)/27(1)(2) Arms Act....regarding seizure of 2 AK-47 rifles by Vvk ps, the arrested accused person Floyd Ramfangzauva(33) of Kanan veng Aizawl disclosed during interrogation by Police that the seized weapons belongs to one LALLAIZAUVA s/o Thangliana of Chhippui,Bangladesh.

 One of my Officers who personnally knew Lallaizauva from past encounters, managed to contact him through mobile phone yesterday ie 7/9/'20. The owner of the weapons Lallaizauva, who is presently staying in Chhippui village of Bangladesh confess that the 2 (two) AK 47 seized by Police belongs to him and he did entrusted Floyd Ramfangzauva to sent it to Bangladesh for further selling it to UPDF militant group.(United Peoples' Democratic Front = UPDF is Chakma insurgency faction group operating in Chittagong hills tracts of Bangladesh).He further stated that he purchased the 2 AK47 from one Enga (he don’t know his full name)of Tiddim, Myanmar during November last year..He took the weapons @Rs.3.5 lacs per piece (though he did not yet pay it) and intends to sell it to UPDF @ Rs.4.3 lacs.

 Lallaizauva used to stay at a rented house in Hunthar veng Aizawl till January this year and just before he left Aizawl for Bangladesh,he sent some of his packed belongings/household goods at the house of Floyd Ramfangzauva (which includes the weapons packed in a bag.).

The said Lallaizauva was earlier arrested on 25.3.2018 by W.Phaileng Police for carrying 2060 nos.of AK ammunitions (vide.W.Phaileng ps case no.6/'18 dt.26.3.2018 u/s 25 (1AA) Arms Act r/w 34 IPC. He is presently out on bail in this case.
