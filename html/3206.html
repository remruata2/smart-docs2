A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 11.7.2013

MSU intends to organise total bandh & MZP cancelled procession: Mizo Students’ Union (MSU), General Headquarters condemned Mizoram government for snatching the recruitment power of Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC)  of the Grade Pay of  ` 4,400/-  and below. MSU had pleaded Mizoram government to hand it back to MPSC many times but no to avail and now to urge the government MSU intends to organise total bandh in Aizawl on 30th July 2013.

            Meanwhile, Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) General Headquarters, the State's apex students’ organisation cancelled their impending procession tomorrow ie, 12.7.2013 for Mizoram government promised to fulfill the demands of MZP and said it will take any necessary steps for the improvement of education in Mizoram.

 

A. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 11.7.2013

ILS approved: Director General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) approved the use of Instrumental Landing System (ILS) at Lengpui Airport. ILS was examined by DGCA recently and approved its use on 9.7.2013 for the period of one year; the validity will expire on 8.7.2014. The use of ILS was resumed yesterday ie, 10.7.2013.
