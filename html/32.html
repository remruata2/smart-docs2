NOTE ON THE MURDER OF

 ZARZOKIMA (17) s/o LALNGILNEIA OF BUNGTHUAM AND THE AFTERMATH

 

            On 13.11.2009 @ 10:00 am Zarzokima (17) s/o Lalngilneia of Bungthuam was shot dead @ 1 km north of Bungthuam village by unidentified persons. The culprit left a note in which they claimed themselves as Bru Revolutionary Union (BRU) and that they did not want their repatriation without proper agreement and would fight the State Government for their rights. In this connection, Kanhmun Police Station case No. 23/09 Dt. 13.11.09 u/s 302 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a)/27(1) Arms Act had been registered and investigation is going on.

            The Superintendent of Police, Mamit District, SDPO Kawrthah and OC Kanhmun immediately rushed to the place of occurrence and camping. The State Government also directed all the nearby BOPs and Police Stations for prompt action in tracing out the miscreants.

            The DIG (NR) Pu L. Hrangnawn was sent to supervise Police deployment and to be stationed at Mamit District. Pu John Neihlaia, Commandant 3rd Bn. IR and Pu F. Lalhuliana, Commandant 4th Bn. IR had also reached there on 13.11.2009 and 15.11.2009 respectively to deal with law and order problems.

 

            Meanwhile, the DGP, Mizoram, Pu Lalrokhuma Pachuau, IPS had requested the DGP, Tripura for the safety of Mizo Community living in Zampui Range of Tripura. Pu Lalthafamkima, MPS, Dy. Commandant 1st Bn. MAP was also sent there to do liasoning between Mizoram and Tripura Police. The New Kawnpui BOP, located at the Mizoram-Tripura border is also strengthened with 1(one) platoon of 3rd Bn. IR.

 

            The Deputy Commissioner of Mamit District is also doing rehabilitation work for the victims and meeting of officials with the Chief Secretary regarding the incident was also held at 1300 hrs. on 15.11.2009.
