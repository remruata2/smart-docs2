Involvement in heinous crimes so far

:

1) On Dt 01.4.2003, BLFM kiddnapped Pu F. Lalfamkima @ Puia(30) s/o Biaksanga(L) of Thingsai P/A Maubawk driver of Sumo No.MZ-01(B)/4633 coming from Khantlang village while proceeding towards Tuipuibari and also extorted Rs.9200/- from two passengers. Released on 21.4.03 @ 1400 hrs. between Khantlang and Tuipuibari after 20 days. In this connection W.Phaileng PS C/No.7/03 dt.2.4.03 u/s 395/365 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(A) Arms Act.

 

2) On 30.1.04, BLFM led by Rothangliana(32) kidnapped 3 persons of Tuipuibari while fishing in the Amsuri river near Tuipuibari. Out of three, two were released the same day whereas Helia(21) s/o Birbahu (VCP Tuipuibari) is being still held hostage by BLFM. They demanded the ransom of Rs.10 lakhs. In this connection W.Phaileng PS C/No.4/04 dt. 1.2.2004 u/s 363/365/34 IPC r/w 24 (1-B) (A) Arms Acts has been registered.
