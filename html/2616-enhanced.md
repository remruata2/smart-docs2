****Investigation Report****

 Khupkhanawla received the consignment from Khaidala of Kawlpui (Myanmar) at Champhai on 16.9.2005 who set the price for the items at Rs. 3,50,000/- from which Khupkhanawla would received Rs.10,000/- as his cut/commission fee. He hid the consignment at his residence. He met potential customers, whose identity were not known, at Hotel Holiday Home on 17.9.2005(Saturday) and discussed about the price and delivery. Delivery was set for 19.9.2005. On that day, his residence was searched by police and the hidden consignment recovered before he could deliver the consignment.

 No information is known about neither Khaidala of Kawlpui (Myanmar) nor from where and how he acquired the arms and ammunitions.

 Nothing could be ascertained about the intended customers and the destination of the consignment.
