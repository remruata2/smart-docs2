ORIGIN OF THE MEMORANDUM:

                        The president of PTC said that the memorandum to the Prime Minister of India was submitted by them in pursuance of initiative taken earlier by the then Paite National Council.

 

 

 

SOURCE OF FUND:

                        The PTC do not have formal source of fund/income and is depending on contributions of a little more prosperous members of the organisation. The leaders said that they had never received any monetary assistance from any other organisation.

 

 

 

WELL WISHERS, SUPPORT IN AND OUTSIDE THE STATE:

                        On 5-8 April 2011, a conference organised by Paite Inpui was held at Sinzawl, Manipur. Thanglianpau, President of ZRA attended the same as Chief Guest. The president of PTC with other leaders attended the conference and submitted the demand of ADC as one of the agendas which was accepted. The conference resolved that demand for ADC by the PTC should be supported and rendered any possible help by different organisations of Paite in Manipur.

 

 

 

ROLE OF ZRA:

                        It is learnt that no monetary assistance has been rendered by ZRA but extended its moral support for creation of ADC and no agreement has been made between them to resort to violence/using arms for achieving the status of ADC.

 

 

YOUNG PAITE ASSOCIATION:

                        Formation of Young Paite Association has never been heard though some Paite organisations like Siam Sin Pawl Pi (Student body), Paite Cultural Group and Mizoram Paite Tribe Union are formed in Mizoram. However, Young Paite Association is formed in Manipur having numerous members and extended moral support for creation of ADC.
