A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 09.12.2022_

****Synod to strongly fight against corruption**: The 99th conference of Synod, the apex governing body of Mizoram Presbyterian Church is currently being held at Khatla Presbyterian Church, Aizawl. Yesterday, this assembly resolved to take stronger steps in the fight against corruption in Mizoram and left the matter in the hands of Synod Executive Committee (SEC) to take further and necessary actions. For the reconstruction of the moral and ethics of the state and the public, the assembly also agreed to put more efforts in monitoring the works of government servants and even right up to the state leaders. In this 99th Synod conference, there are 42 agendas in all out of which 21 have been discussed and will last till 11.12.2022 (Sunday).**

**B**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 09.12.2022_

****68 students of H/S and HSS in Mizoram addicted to Heroin:** Dr. K.Beichhua, Social Welfare Minister released a 'Survey Report on Drugs and Children in Mizoram 2022' which was prepared by Social Defence &amp; Rehabilitation Board under Social Welfare &amp; Tribal Affairs department. According to this report, there are 68 students studying HS &amp; HSS aged between 10-19 years in Mizoram, who are addicted to No.4 Heroin. During the preparation process of this report, an in depth studies were conducted on 10,746 students from 77 M/S, 33 H/S and 12 HSS of eight districts. The main purpose of this survey was to find out the vulnerability of teenage boys and girls from the menace of narcotic substances.**
