A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 9.1.2014_

**Safe release of abducted demanded again****:** A joint meeting of Bru NGO and Bru VC under the supervision of Bru Co-ordination Committee was held on 7th January 2014 at Damparengpui. The meeting demanded the abductors to release the victims safely, and a fasting prayer meeting is to be held on 13th January 2014 for the release of the abducted.

 Sub-Hqrs. of Mamit YMA made a press release stating that if the abducted persons are not released till 15th January 2014, it will not be a responsible if Mizo people decided to take the matter in their hands and deal with it.
