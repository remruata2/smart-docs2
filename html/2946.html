SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/405

Dated: 13th June, 2019.

 

 

            Today, i.e. 13.6.2019, Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) issued an appeal to all its branches to select volunteers (5-10 members from each branch) to carry out the final phase of drive on identification of illegal Chakma foreigners and to report themselves to CYLA before the end of 13.6.2019. The appeal reiterated the importance of their action for safeguarding the land and nation and requested all leaders and members of its branches to participate selflessly. The final drive will commence from 14.6.2019 and is expected to be completed by 15.6.2019.

            Earlier, on 30.5.2019, a team of CYLA led by Pu J. Sangthangpuia, President, numbering 12 (twelve) persons left Lawngtlai and carried out the first phase of drive against illegal Chakma foreigners in western belt of Lai Autonomous District Council (LADC).

            In this connection, it was also reported that 8 (eight) members representing Village Councils and Young Chakma Association (YCA) had submitted a joint application to the Deputy Commissioner, Lawngtlai District on 3.6.2019 with the following request :-

  1. Order investigation to find out the facts about the ongoing ‘Local area population census-2019’ being conducted by CYLA and fixed accountability for such illegal activities which vitiate communal harmony.
  2. Seize and destroy all the data gathered or collected through this illegal population census by NGO.
  3. Ensure such illegal census/head counting are not conducted anywhere in future and take strict actions against those who conduct such activities.

 

            As per latest information received CYLA had finished the said drive in as many as 25 (twenty five) villages and 6 (six) villages are still left.
