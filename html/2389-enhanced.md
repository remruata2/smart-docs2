****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/411

Dated 31st May, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/409 Dt. 28.05.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

5 (five) Assam Armed Police led by ASI and 15 (fifteen) civilians (Bru from Kutkuti Village, Assam) visited the paddy field of Pu John Halliday, located at the bank of Betchhora river/Sakeilui paddy field area, towards Mizoram side, in between Chuhvel and Saikhawthlir Village and around 1 km from Chuhvel Village on 30.5.2021 @ 10:00 a.m. During their visit, Assam Police and civilians from Assam informed Lalrindika, JCB Operator and Lalruatfela (employees of Pu John Halliday), who were present at the area that the area falls within Assam territory, and they had taken videograph of the area and left the area around 10:45 a.m. Information received stated that there had been no report of border dispute in and around the area before the said incident.

Information received also stated that Pu John Halliday bought the said paddy field from Pu Loncy, Evangelist Speaker in January, 2021, and since then he started cultivation and constructed a farm house at the area in February, 2021. In this connection, DC, Mamit and Addl SP, Mamit intended to visit the area tomorrow i.e., 1.6.2021.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****18 (eighteen) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-9, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 31.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

34 (thirty four) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-29, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 31.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
