**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 11.09.2013**

**Arrangement of traffic****:** The Addl. S.P, Aizawl Traffic police requested all travelers plying between Aizawl and Zanlawn to use the route only on emergency as several vehicles are presently stranded in these areas because of various landslides. Meanwhile, around 500 vehicles which were stuck between Kawnpui and Khamrang were able to resume their journey as the roads were cleared.

**Complaint on ACB****:**The Anti Corruption Bureau registered a case against P.C. Vanlalruata, a retired Supply Department Storekeeper for his manipulation of BPL/AAY funds which were worth Rs. 53,45,210/-.
