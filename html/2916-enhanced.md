****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/86

Dated: 9th February, 2019.

In continuation of our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/85, dated 8.2.2019, NGOCC Kolasib submitted FIR against Dr. Arun T, IAS, District Election Officer, Kolasib District today, i.e. 9.2.2019 @ 10:40 a.m at Kolasib Police Station. A small function was held by NGOCC before submitting the FIR at Kolasib Hmar Veng (Police Station junction) which was presided over by Pu C. Liankunga, Chairman and attended by approx. 40 (forty) members.

 Addressing the gathering, Pu C. Liankunga expressed his gratitude to the members and stated that the current leaders of NGOCC are chosen by the Almighty God to protect the State of Mizoram from outsiders and that the FIR was intended for the protection of Mizoram. Pu Thomas D. Lalengliana, Secretary also gave report on action taken by the NGOCC in its move to remove 217 (two hundred seventeen) Bru voters from the Electoral Roll and accused the DEO, Kolasib District of submitting false affidavit to the Hon’ble High Court which is the reason for submitting the FIR. Pu H. Laltanpuia, Advocate also addressed the gathering and stated that their intention behind the FIR is to ensure that voting rights are granted to only those who are legally qualified to vote and to strengthen Police in their investigation. He also expressed their willingness to help the Police as and when required.
 Subsequently, the FIR was submitted to Kolasib Police Station and the Officer in-Charge received the FIR and kept it for further enquiry.
