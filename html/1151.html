PRELIMINARY ASSESSMENT REPORT OF TAWI CONSTITUENCY

 

 

1.       SAITUAL: He khuaah hian voters 3860 vel an awm a, Congress party in voters a ngah ber a rin ani a, a dawtah MNF party. MNF ai hian INC hi vote 500-600 inkar velin a lead rih a rin ani.

 

 

2.       KEIFANG: Voters 2520 vel an awm a, hetah pawh hian INC in voters a ngah ber rin ani a, 2000 vel a neih rin ani a, abak zawng hi party dangin an insem anga, chung zingah chuan MNF in ngah ber in ZNP leh MPC party ten an dawt ang.

 

 

3.       RULCHAWM: Voters 760 vel an awm a, heta tang hian vote 400-500 inkar vel INC in a hmuh rin ani. Chuh anih chuan party dang chuan an hmu tam thei vak lo ani.

 

 

4.       RUALLUNG: Voters 580 vel an awm a, vote hmu tam ber tur a ngaih INC in vote 300-350 inkar vel a hmuh rin ani.

 

 

5.       MUALPHENG: Voters 530 vel awmin, MNF party in vote a hmuh san ber rin ani a, MNF hian votes 300 vel zet a neih rin ani a, a dawtah INC anih rin a ni.

 

 

6.       LENCHIM: Voters 199 an awm a, he khuaah hian INC leh MNF party an inzat tawk viau ni a hria ani a, a landan chuan INC hi vote 10 vela an lead rin ani.

 

 

7.       TAWIZO:  Voters 226 an awm a, ZNP tlem awm bak chu INC leh MNF an ni a, an inzat tawk viau a ngaih ani a, INC hi tlem a an lead deuh rin ani.

 

 

8.       MAITE: Voters 564 an awm a, he khuaah pawh hian INC leh MNF nilo chu an tam lo a, MNF leh INC te hi an in tiat tawk viau ni a hriat ani a, chutih rual rual chuan INC hi votes 20 vel bawra an lead zawk rin ani.

 

 

9.       TUIKHURHLU: He khua hi Tawi (ST) Constituency chhunga awm, Seling V/C  awpna hnuaia awm a ni a, In 48 awm in voters 117 vel an awm a, Political party ah chuan INC leh MNF ten unit an nei a. Tun dinhmuna a lan dan chuan Congress aiin MNF party in voters 10-20 velin an ngah zawk nia hriat a ni a, he khuaah hian Congress party unit hruaitute leh memberte inkarah a ruka inngeihlohna a awm nia hriat a ni bawk a. Hetiang a nih avang hian Congress party hian hma a sawn thei lo niin hriat a ni bawk.

 

10.     SELING: He khua hi Tawi (ST) constituency huam chhunga awm niin in 500 vel awmin voters 1670 vel an awm bawk a. V/C ah chuan MNF-3, Congress-2 niin MNF V/C awplai a ni. Tun dinhmuna a landan chuan party lian pahnih MNF leh Congress bak party dang chu in 10 vel bak an awmlo nia hriat a ni a, Tin, party lian pahnihte enrolment neih dan te ngun taka sawipui a nih hnuah leh mi hrang hrang kawm kualna atanga a lan danin MNF party aiin INC party hian 100-150 velin voters an ngah rin a ni. Amaherawh chu MNF atang hian candidate an neih that leh that lohin dinhmun erawh a ti danglam thei tura rin a ni.

 

11.     THINGSUL TLANGNUAM: He khua hi Tawi(ST) constituency chhunga awm niin in 250 vel a awm a, voters 920 vel an awm bawk.MNF leh Congress ni lo party dang chu 20-30 vel bak an awmlo nia rin a ni.INC leh MNF party te hi MLA election lo awm turah pawh inbei ber tura ngaih an ni. Chuti anih chuan party lian pahnih te party enrolment ve ve zir chianna atang leh mipui mimir hrim hrim kawmna atanga alan danin INC hi MNF aiin voters 100 velin an san zawk rin a ni.

 

12. THINGSULTHLIAH: He khua hi Tawi(ST) constituency huam chhunga awm niin in 830 vel a awm a, voters 2500 vel an awm. Party hrang hrangte enrolment leh party hruaitute leh mipui (neutral) te kawmna atanga a lan danin INC leh MNF party ni lo party dang hi voters 50 vel bak an awmlo nia rin a ni a, he khuaah hian V/C chu MNF ni mahse MLA election lo awm turah erawh chuan tun dinhmunah voters 300 velin MNF aiin INC in an ngah zawk rin a ni, a chhan  chu party dang engemawzatin INC an zawm then leh tak vang niin a lang. Anmahni Congress party hotute erawh chuan voter 700 vel laia lead turah an inngai thung a, amaherawhchu party hruaituten campaign han thlak tak tak ta se a boruak a zirin an inthlauhna tur hi sangin tlahniam thut ve thei reng chu a ni tho mai.

 

 

CONCLUSION: Assessment lak a nih lai hian candidate tur te hriat thawi ni mahse chiang taka puanna a la awm loh avang hian mipui mimir hrim hrim chuan an la ngaihven vak loh avangin politics boruak a la dai viau a, chutih lai erawh chuan party a Block leh Unit hruaitute erawh chuan an ngaihven in an buaipui viau tawh thung a, candidate turte han puangin campaign han bei tak tak ta se chuan a boruak hi danglam their reng tho chu niin a lang. Tin, Seling, Tlangnuam leh Thingsulthliah tlangdung hi chuan Saitual in district anihna anga function ve tura sorkar an nawrna chungchangah hi chuan thu lo neih ve vak hi tul an ti lemlo niin a lang a, helai tlangdung hi chuan Saitual district a awm ai chuan Aizawl district a awm hi an duhin kal vel pawh a awlsam zawk an tih avangin Saitual in district chungchanga an hmalakna hi chuan a nghawng ve vak loh rin a ni.
