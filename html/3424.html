A. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST, VANGLAINI & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 28.1.2014

Police Outpost at Chikha: On Saturday, Home Minister Pu R.Lalzirliana visited the proposed site for Police Outpost at Chikha in Mamit District in Western Mizoram which is vulnerable to kidnapping. A platoon of 5th IR Bn. has been deployed there since 29th November, 2013 after the 23/11 kidnapping. Chikha falls under Dampa Tiger Reserve that borders Bangladesh.

            Home Minister was accompanied by Lt. Col. ZS Zuala, Parliamentary Secretary, V.Lalthakima, DIG(Hqrs.), Neihchungnunga, AIG-II, Rodingliana Chawngthu, SP Mamit, C.Lalruaia, Dy. Commandant, 5th IR Bn., Laltlanhlua, Field Director of Dampa Tiger Reserve and other officials.

             

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.1.2014

Precious Stones seized by Police: Suspected Precious stones worth Rs. 37 lakh was seized by Mizoram Police from the possession of Khupmanga (39) at Chhinga Veng yeaterday. Khupmanga who hailed from Tahan bought these precious stones for 78,000 Kyat in Myanmar and intends to sell it for ` 37 lakh in Mizoram.
