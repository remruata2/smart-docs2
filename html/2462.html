MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/742

Dated 22nd December, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/739
Dt. 21.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          Due to non-availability of Test Kit at Screening Centre, Kanhmun, entry of vehicle in the state is not allowed for today i.e., 22.12.2020 and 4 (four) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 4, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          71(seventy one) vehicles (HMV-22, MMV – 1, LMV – 38, Maxi Cab-10) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          166 (one hundred sixty six) vehicles (HMV – 85, MMV – 34, LMV –12, LPG – 10, Maxi Cab – 3, Oil Tanker – 20, Bus - 2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 22.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
