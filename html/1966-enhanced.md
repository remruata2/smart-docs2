****ENQUIRY REPORT IN CONNECTION WITH TRAINING OF BRU CADRES UNDER PLA****

In pursuance of PHQ letter No. PHQ/OPS-97/2014/494 dt.18.6.2014 regarding training of Bru cadres(15) under PLA/Manipur based Underground(UG)outfit near Lungtak village under Tonjang Township, Chin State, Myanmar, discreet enquiry was conducted by gathering information from different sources and the findings are as follows :

During January, 2014 Bru cadres numbering of 25(twenty five) belonging to UDLA (United Democratic Liberation Army)/UDLF(B)-(United Democratic Liberation Front(Barak Valley)) Assam based Bru outfit proceeded to Myanmar and undergone training at UNLF(United National Liberation Front) training camp located at Tonjang Myanmar. The UNLF is the oldest Meitei(Manipuri) insurgent group in the State of Manipur and was formed on November 24, 1964 with an aim to establish an independent Socialist Manipur.

It is also learnt that out of the 25 cadres, 7(seven) of them returned to Assam during May, 2014 with one of their leaders namely S/S Commander Rajesh Chorkhy. On 4.5.2014 S/S Commander Rajesh Chorkhy was arrested by Assam Police with S/S Commander in Chief of Bru National Army Lalthazuala @Ornajoy Reang.

Information has also been received from leaders of UDLA/UDLA (B) that training was conducted as per the agreement signed between two militant outfit i.e UDLA &amp;UDLF. S/S Commander Kima(Bru name unknown) of Garo Tlang Village, Assam and S/S 2nd Commander Benson of Kutkutti Village, Assam were the two prominent cadres of UDLA joining the training course. The remaining 18(eighteen) trainees of UDLA had also completed their training and returned to Assam, Kutkutti area in the early part of this month i.e July, 2014. They entered Assam, Kutkutti area from Mynamar to Manipur, Vairengte(Mizoram)area , Cachar route. The name of these 18 (eighteen) trainees of UDLA are as follows:

Input received from leaders of ZRA (Zomi Revolutionary Army) and CNF (Chin National Front) also corroborated the training of the above UDLA cadres under the umbrella of UNLF (not PLA) inside Myanmar.

Fresh input indicated that on 23.7.2014 at 12:00 (midnight) an encounter took place between UDLA cadres and Indian Army at the outskirt of Rompur village, Karimganj District, Assam resulting into the death of two UDLA militants namely S/S Pvt Toimoi of Santipur and S/S Pvt. Hojimohan Reang of Moitilungma. The Indian Army also recovered one AK-47 and ammunition from the possession of UDLA cadres. One S/S Pvt Bodro Reang was also arrested by the Indian Army.
