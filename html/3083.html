A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST, VANGLAINI &  NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 10.4.2013

Training on Investigation: Anti-Corruption Bureau started a week-long training on ‘Anti-Corruption Law & Investigation of Cases’ at the Conference Hall of Administrative Training Institute (ATI)  for 31 Police officers of ACB office and other units yesterday ie, 9.4.2013. JS Emmanuel, DSP, a specialised trainer from CBI Academy, Ghaziabad, UP will impart training.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 10.4.2013

Burglar arrested in Serchhip: Around 10 shops - Drug store, Variety Store, Hardware Store and Shoe Store at Serchhip Market, Hospital road were burgled by breaking their locks on the night of 8.4.2013. Serchhip Police probed into the matter and arrested L.Lalremruata (Lalterema) of Serchhip Chanmari Veng, who was released from jail recently, and he also confessed to the crime. He stole 10 pairs of shoes, several strips of Digene from a drug store and ` 300/- from other shops which were all recovered by the Police.
