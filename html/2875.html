A. Front page of NEWSLINK newspaper.Dt.06.12.2012

 

Mizoram seeks Bangladesh help nap abductors: Mizoram Police have sought the Bangladesh's assistance in arresting militants who abducted three persons from Mizoram. The DIG of Mizoram Police has sent a request to the Bangladesh to launch a joint operation in border areas to nab these abductors who are believed to be members of BNCT and NLFT militant outfits.

    

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI/ZOZAM TIMES newspaper. Dt. 06.12.2012

 

Christmas with Christ: The Home Minister of Mizoram, Mr. R.Lalzirliana, on his speech to the people of his constituency during his tour of these areas, requested the population of Mizoram to celebrate Christmas festival with Christ while restraining themselves from crackers and fireworks.

Farewell to DIG: All India Service Association on 5th December bade a farewell to IPS officer Mr. Saizela at the Chief Secretary's Conference Hall at New Secretariat Complex.

 

 

 

D. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper. Dt. 06.12.2012

 

Court Hearing of Ex-Chief Minister: With the consent of Guwahati High Court, Court hearing of former Mizoram Chief Minister, Mr.Zoramthanga's case, in which he was accused of corruption, will be conducted on 29.1.2013. The Court Hearing of this case was delayed previously due to the inability of ACB in submitting supplementary chargesheet on time.
