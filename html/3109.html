A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 22.8.2013

Murderer’s brother arrested: Lalmuanpuia, brother of Lalremruata Ralte who brutally murdered his wife K.Zohmangaihi (49) d/o Ramfangzauva of Zarkawt, was arrested and sent to Central Jail in connection with the murder, report said.

            The involvement of Lalmuanpuia at the murder of K.Zohmangaihi has been hugely suspected. Aizawl Police Station was contacted for confirmation but as the case is under investigation much information could not be obtained. Lalmuanpuia, along with his son were taken for interrogation by the Police to probe deeper into the murder case. According to sources, Lalmuanpuia was fired from Police Department in the past.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 22.8.2013

Indefinite picketing to demand for teachers: Lai Students’ Association General Headquarters, Lawngtlai called for indefinite picketing and will stop Government employees from attending office to show disgruntlement at the Government for not appointing teachers for the posts of Sociology and Geography at Government Region Higher Secondary School, Lawngtlai.

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.8.2013

Total Bandh in Saiha District today: Mara Thyutlia Py (MTP) General Headquarters is organising a total bandh in Saiha District today from 5:00 AM to 6:00 PM to show disgruntlement over the transfer of two doctors out of Saiha District.
