A. _Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 13.3.2014_

**Strict Vigilance on Teachers :******In pursuance of the resolution passed by their Federal Body meeting at Champhai yesterday, Mizo Students Union intends to make an efforts to flush out government teachers who are employing their substitute instead of working themselves in their post. MSU believed that there are still numerous teachers who are still reluctant to stay in their post despite the orders of the government.

****Narcotic seized****:**At around 1:20 pm yesterday, District Special Branch under S.P Aizawl, seized substances which are suspected to be Pseudophedrine from the possession of Lalrelliana s/o Zalawmchuanga who is residing in the rented house of Laisawizika of Ramhlun South.
