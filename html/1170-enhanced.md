****BOMB BLAST AT LUNGLEI****

 On 31.5.2015 at about 1:50 A.M, a bomb exploded (suspected to be Gelatin) at the Main Gate of District Education Officer (DEO) Office Complex at Electric Veng, Lunglei,

damaging the following office properties i.e. windows/ventilation glasses and GI Sheets.

**Sl**

**No. Name of the building Detail damaged & Remark**

1.  DEO Office, Lunglei  a) Window/Ventilation glass - 6 nos.

2.  DPC( RMSA) Office  a) Window/Ventilation glass - 31 nos.

3.  DEO Office Canteen  a) Window/Ventilation glass - 14 nos.

 b) GI Sheet- 2 Nos (Partly damaged).

 Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. Pu Laldingngheta, SDPO, Lunglei and Officer-in-Charge of Lunglei Police Station had immediately rushed to the place of incidence. In this connection, **Lunglei Police Station Case No. 61/15 dt. 31.5.2015 u/s 427 IPC r/w 3(a) of Explosives Substances Act** has been registered and investigation is being conducted. Lunglei High Powered Committee (HPC) Vice-Chairman Pu S.Laldingliana, MLA had visited the place of incidence at 7:00 AM on this day.
