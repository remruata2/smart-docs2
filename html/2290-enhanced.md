****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/114

Dated 17th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/112 Dt. 15.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

38 (thirty eight) vehicles (HMV-15, MMV-15, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 17.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 4 (four) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-17, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 17.02.2021 @ 3:30pm. 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-7, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Forum Against Mizo Aggression (FAMA)called for an indefinite Rail blockade (Rail Roko Andolan) in between Jamira Rail Station, Hailakandi and Bairabi Rail Station w.e.f., 15.2.2021 @ 5:00 p.m. to forestall the movement of trains to Mizoram. The blockade continues today i.e., Dt. 17.2.2021 and around 40 (forty) members of FAMA participated. The group claimed that the protest will continue until there is a stable solution of inter-state border dispute between the two states.

61 (sixty one) vehicles (HMV-31, MMV-1, LMV-26, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 17.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

131 (one hundred thirty one) vehicles (HMV-43, MMV-30, LMV-27, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-6, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 17.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
