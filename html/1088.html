V.     PROTEST AGAINST INCLUSION OF CHAKMA STUDENTS IN MIZORAM         STATE QUOTA FOR MBBS AND BDS COURSE

 

         On 20.7.2017 @ 9:00 a.m, around 1,800 (one thousand eight hundred) student activists under the aegis of MZP General Headquarters gathered infront of the Office of Jt. Director, Higher & Technical Education (Technical Wing), Government of Mizoram at Chaltlang, Aizawl in protest against inclusion of Chakma students in Mizoram State quota for MBBS and BDS course. 4 (four) Chakma students who were qualified in NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)-2017 entered the Office building of Jt. Director, H&TE (Technical Wing) @ 9:20 a.m for Counselling Session under the escort of Mizoram Police. Consequence upon the entering of the office building by Chakma students, MZP activists tried to break the Police barricade to enter the Counselling venue resulting in a brief tussle between the agitating students and Police. However, Police managed to contain the situation and few Police personnel and students sustained minor injuries during the preventive action of Police. The injured persons were brought to Civil Hospital, Aizawl for medical treatment.

         Subsequently, an Emergency Meeting of MZP was held near Sericulture Office, Chaltlang and the meeting passed the following resolutions :-

  1. Counselling for filling up of seats in MBBS and BDS under Mizoram State quota be cancelled by Government of Mizoram.
  2.  State-wide Total Bandh be organised w.e.f. tomorrow, i.e. 21.7.2017 if Counselling is not cancelled.
  3. Demanded the removal of Pu R. Romawia, Hon’ble Minister, H&TE, etc. and Pu        B.D. Chakma, Hon’ble Minister, AH&Vety, etc. if Chakma students are not     withdrawn from the Counselling. 

         MZP General Headquarters had called off their agitation for the day @ 12:50 p.m. Altogether, 54 (fifty four) students have turned up for the Counselling session/class.

         Meanwhile, leaders of MZP General Headquarters, namely - Pu Lalsangzuala Ngente, President; Pu L. Ramdinliana Renthlei, General Secretary; Pu Lalnunmawia, Treasurer; Pu V.L Krosshnehzova, Adviser; Pu C. Lalrokhuma, Adviser; Pu Lalhruaitluanga Chawngte, Adviser and Pu Lalhmachhuana, President, NSUI met Pu R. Romawia, Hon'ble Minister, H&TE, etc. on the evening of 20.7.2017 in his office chamber. Leaders of MZP demanded the Hon'ble Minister to take initiative for the removal of Chakma from the list of Category-I of the Mizoram (Selection of Candidates for Higher Technical Courses) Rules and for removal of selected Chakma students from filling up of seats in MBBS and BDS under Mizoram State quota. The meeting decided to conduct fresh Counselling Session after confirmation of seat allotment by Medical Colleges is received. However, the meeting agreed to go ahead with the allotment of MBBS and BDS seats already made for the following Medical Colleges :-

i)       RIMS               -          7 seats

ii)     NEIGHRIMS -           3 seats

iii)    Dibrugarh       -          1 seat 

         Subsequently, MZP General Headquarters held an Executive Meeting at MZP Pisa Pui, Aizawl and after a long deliberation, the meeting agreed to cancel the proposed state-wide indefinite Total Bandh @ 8:50 p.m.
