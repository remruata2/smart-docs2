****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/711

Dated 12th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/705 Dt. 11.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-3, LMV-4) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 12.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (LMV - 2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 4, LMV - 7) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 12.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – Nil, LMV - 10) transit their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

60 (sixty) vehicles (HMV-22, MMV – 3, LMV – 29, Maxi Cab- 6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 12.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

141 (one hundred and forty one) vehicles (HMV – 60, MMV – 31, LMV –15, LPG – 10, Maxi Cab – 3, Oil Tanker – 20, Bus - 2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 12.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
