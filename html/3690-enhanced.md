A. _Front page of VANGLAIN newspaper Dt. 30.09.2022_

****Mizoram having the highest rate of Malaria**: Regional Review meeting organised by National Vector Borne Disease Control Programme (NVBDCP) and Health &amp; Family Welfare Department of 10 states was inaugurated by Mizoram Health Minister, Dr. R. Lalthangliana yesterday (29.9.2022) at Assembly Annexe Conference Hall, Aizawl. As per the records of this meeting, which will last for 3 days, Malaria cases were detected in Mizoram on 7,672 persons during the period between January- August, 2022. During the year 2021, 5637 cases of Malaria were also detected where 80 % of them were from Lunglei, lawngtlai, Siaha and Mamit districts. Hence, these four districts are included in the "Intensified Control Phase (High Endemic District), which is the highest in the category. All these factors propelled Mizoram state as the state with the highest rate of Malaria in all the states of India.**
