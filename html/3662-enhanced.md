**20.4.2022 :** Aizawl City Bus Owners' Association (ACBOA) held its 48th General Assembly at Synod Conference Centre, Aizawl 20.4.2022 @ 12:00 noon. Pu Zoliansanga, President, ACBOA presided the assembly and 63 (sixty three) members attended. Pu Lalrammawia, Director, Transport Department graced the function as Chief Guest. During the assembly, Nl. Vanlalsangkimi D/o R. Lalfaka (L) was felicitated for being the first and only lady Bus conductor in Aizawl.
