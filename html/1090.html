SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2016/50

Dated 21st January, 2016

 

Today i.e. 21.1.2016 around 1300 strong students took out a possession within Aizawl City under the aegis of Mizo Students Union (MSU) General Headquarters, Mizoram Blind Society (MBS), Aizawl City College Joint Students' Union (ACCJSU) in protest against extension of the re-employment period of Pi B.Sairengpuii as Commissioner, Persons with Disabilities (PwD) under Government of Mizoram. The students marched from Zarkawt Junction to the office of the Commissioner, Persons with Disabilities, Chaltlang, Aizawl.  Leaders of  MSU, MBS and ACCJSU delivered speeches during the rally and the  agitating students chanted slogans and carried placards against the Government of Mizoram and against Pi B.Sairengpuii. The demonstrators further staged a 'Sit-in demonstration' infront of the office of Commissioner, Persons with Disabilities  at Chaltlang, Aizawl demanding removal of
Pi B.Sairengpuii before 5:00 PM today. The procession and protest disrupted  movement of vehicles along the road leading to Chaltlang. At around 4:00 PM, the agitating students tried to break Police Barrickade at infront of the office of Commissioner, (PwD) and the Police were compelled to resort to Lathi charge and Tear Gas as the agitating students started attacking the Police personnel by pelting stone etc. Finally, at around 5:00 PM, all the agitating students withdrawn from the spot of demonstration i.e. infront of Office of Commissioner, PwD, Chaltlang and marched towards Vanapa Hall, Aizawl and they dispersed from Vanapa Hall premises at 6:20 PM. During the  clash between the two sides, the following 3 (three) students  sustained minor injuries and sent to  Civil Hospital, Aizawl and they were discharged after administering medical treatment :-

1)        Zosangliana (20 yrs), ITI Veng, Aizawl (broken small finger)

2)        R. Vanlalruatfela (20 yrs) Upper Republic (cut on palate)

3)        Peter Laltanpuia (20 yrs), Rawpuichhip (cut on head)

 

 

 

Meanwhile, 8 (eight) Police personnel i.e. 4(four) from 5th IR Bn., 3(three) from Aizawl DEF and 1(one) from 3rd Bn. MAP also sustained minor injuries while retaliating to the attack of agitating students. In this regard, Bawngkawn PS Case No 26/16 Dt. 21.1.2016 u/s 143/145/147/149/152/ 353/427 IPC has been registered.

The meeting of the leaders of Mizo Students Union (MSU), Mizoram Blind Society (MBS), Aizawl City College Joint Students' Union (ACCJSU) was held this evening @ 6:30 PM at MSU Office and the meeting was attended by around 70 members. The meeting decided to assemble infront of Vanapa Hall tomorrow i.e. 22.1.2016 at 9:00 AM and they are likely to stage the following agitations :-

1)        March towards Office of Commissioner, Persons with Disabilities,                              Chaltlang, Aizawl or Chief Minister’s Bungalow.

2)        Picketing  of Government Officials at Secretariat Complex, Aizawl.

Further, leaders of the students expressed their readiness to discuss about their demand (i.e. removal of Pi B. Sairengpuii from Commissioner, PwD) with representative of the Government of Mizoram at any time. However, student leaders also reiterated their strong determination to continue the agitation till their demand is fulfilled by the Government. Situation is kept under close watch.
