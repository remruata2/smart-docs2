CLARIFICATION OF THE ENQUIRY REPORT AGAINST

INSPECTOR R. LALHMACHHUANA OF 3RD IR BN.

 

  1. Why has the SP(SB) took over 9 (nine) months to reoly to the letter of PHQ cited above?

=                   On 21st June 2018, based on verbal instruction received from DIG(CID), an order to conduct discreet enquiry on Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana 3rd IR Bn. so as to ascertained if his assets are proportionate with his actual income as a Govt. Servant, was prepared and it was endorsed to Pu Francis Lallawmkima, the then Dy.SP(Hqrs) of CID(SB).

 

Later, on 29th June 2018, eight days after the order was issued, the letter from AIG-I No. CB/PHQ/MRP/GAS/08/291 dt. 29th June, 2018 was received by this office, a copy of which was also given to the said Dy.SP (Hqrs.).

 

The enquiry officer, while conducting this investigation, was also engaged in various aspect of works, which require a great deal of time and energy such as –

 

  1. Obtaining Bank account of Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana from a couple of bank which took lots of time in analyzing them.
  2. Attending departmental training programme and other confidential duties involving out station duties that lasted for weeks.
  3. The election process which require traveling to different parts of the state to assess the political situation, apart from normal duties in the office.

 

Because of the above mentioned reasons, the enquiry officer could not promptly conclude his investigation and it was already 18th February, 2019 when he finally submitted his findings.

 

The report submitted by Pu Francis Lallawmkima, Dy.SP(Hqrs.) was again summarized by Pi PC Liansiampuii, the then Dy.SP of CID(SB) who again completed it on 5th March, 2019. Based on this, the final report was prepared and forwarded to the higher authorities on 7th March, 2019.
