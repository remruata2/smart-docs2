A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.12.2022_

****Inauguration of Sewage Treatment Plant**: Yesterday, Pu Tawnluia, Dy. Chief Minister of Mizoram inaugurated the 10 MLD Sewage Treatment Plant constructed by SIPMIU which located on the banks of Chite stream below Bethlehem veng, Aizawl. This treatment plant, which was constructed from the year 2013 was undertaken with funds received from Asian Development bank (ADB) ( Rs. 26.76 crores) through North East Regional Capital Cities Development &amp; Implementation Programme (NERCCDIP). The plant is expected to accommodate sewage of 12,000 families within Aizawl city and are currently utilised by 9 Local Council areas where 4,326 families are given connections. In this programme, the Dy. Chief Minister was accompanied by Pu Robert Romawia Royte, Minister of State, three other MLAs, Aizawl Mayor and Deputy Mayor.**

**C**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.12.2022_

****'No Vehicle Zone in Aizawl':** As per the decision made by the meeting of Coordination Committee on Traffic on 9th December 2022, 'No Vehicle Zone' will be enforced within Aizawl City on 23rd,24th and 31st of December 2022. During these period, no vehicles will be allowed to ply on the main road within Aizawl city from Zodin square in the south to Chanmari square in the north from 10 am in the morning to 4 pm in the evening. The enforcement, however, will exempted medical, police duty and emergency issues. Parking of any vehicles and selling of any items along the roads within this zone will also be prohibited**
