A. Front page of  VANGLAINI /The aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 30.08.2022

Seizure of Meth : Champhai police on 28th August 2022 (Sunday) seized 10,100 tablets of illegal Methamphetamine (1.1 Kgs.) at Chalbawiha Junction, Khankawn police Check Gate from one vehicle (Scorpio) which is on its way from Champhai to Aizawl. The owner of the vehicle and the suspected owner of the contraband items, Vanlalringi(42) d/o Thangluaia, Champhai Zion veng was also arrested. The seized items are estimated to be worth Rs.330 lakhs in the international market.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 30.08.2022

Prohibitionary order issued by Deputy Commissioner naked : Aizawl district Deputy Commissioner, Dr. Lalhriatzuali Ralte on yesterday (29.8.2022) issued an order prohibiting the disposing of any garbage over the side of National highway (NH)-54. Violators of the order, which will be enforced for a period of two months, will be liable to be punished under Section  188 IPC . The order was issued to prevent the contamination of Tlawng river which flows along the side of NH-54 in between Sairang town and Khamrang village and which is the main source of drinking water for the public ,particularly the  residence of Aizawl city.
