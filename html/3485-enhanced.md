B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 27.3.2014_

****Mandal Reached Home****:**Deep Mandal, who was rescued by Mizoram Police CID(Special Branch) after being held captive for 119 days was met and welcomed by the community of his home land - Bankur. Mandal left Mizoram for Kolkata on Monday and was received by Bankur Community at Dum Dum Airport. He then left Kolkata for home on Tuesday; he was taken around the street of Bankur by the community as a celebration of his home coming.**

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST, & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 27.3.2014_

**Woman Body Identified****:**A woman ran over by a speeding truck on the night of 24.3.2014 at around 8:30pm between Sairang and Kawnpui was identified to be that of Lalduhawmi (40) d/o Rohmingliani of Thingdawl Vengthar. The corpse was handed over to her mother Rohmingliani and the funeral function was held yesterday at Thingdawl.
