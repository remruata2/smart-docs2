A. _Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 4.9.2013_

****Police Training****:** Police officer of Inspector and above rank in Serchhip District had training yesterday at SP office training Hall in Serchhip District. The training was on the duty and responsibility of Police in the election. Pu Romil Baaniya, IPS, SP Serchhip and Pu Lalbiaktluanga, MPS, Addl. SP gave lecture in this training.**

B. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 4.9.2013_

**Hunger Strike by Homeless People****:** Some families who were told to leave their houses in the neighbourhood of Mualkhang Gas Bottling Plant by Mualkhang Joint Action Committee, did not want to leave and stayed in the church even after JAC locked their houses on 30.8.2013. These families are reported to have a hunger strike from yesterday as reported to Vanglaini by Sairang Police Station duty.
