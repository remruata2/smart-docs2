Sir,

 With reference to your letter and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:-

**Starred Question No. 316 :**

**a) Does rebel exist within Mizoram?**

**Ans:** Existence of rebel within Mizoram is not known. The much talked about HPC(D) and BNA to be of Mizoram's rebel outfit have neither camps nor hideouts anywhere inside Mizoram.

**b) Does training camp formerly used by rebels within the city exist at present?**

**Ans:** No.

**SUPPLEMENTARY NOTES :**

**1. Where HPC(D) and BNA have Headquarters/Hideouts?**

**Ans:** HPC(D) has its Hqrs./Hideouts at Sibapurikhal (Ankachhuah), Manipur and Hmarkhawlien, Assam. The BNA does not have permanent camp anywhere.

**2. Where the rebel are undergoing training?**

**Ans:** HPC(D) has training camp at Chandhikhal jungle, below Khawbawn forest, Cachar district, Assam; 3 (three) hours journey on foot from Kangrengdor, Manipur.

 Some members of BNA undergone training under UPPK, an underground outfit of Manipur and it has no its own training camp.
