A. Front page of VANGLAINI /THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 19&20.6.2022

Heavy  rainfall causes minor damages:  Due to the heavy downpour on 18th June 2022, a collapsing of retaining wall swept away one Assam type house which was located at Tukhurhlu village . However, no human casualties was reported due to this incident.

            Meanwhile, HN-306 was also blocked by a massive landslide near Kawngthar Veng Presbyterian Church, Vairengte which was cleared for vehicles on the same evening. In another incident, the National Highway between Thiltlang village and Pangzawl town was also blocked by landslide at around 3 Kms. from Thiltlang village.

 

 

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 20.6.2022

Schools to be closed for two days:   As meteorological reports predicted heavy rainfall in the coming days, Government of Mizoram (School Education Department) issued a notification on 19.6.2022 for the closing of all schools - from Primary to Higher Secondary schools in Mizoram for two days i.e 20th and 21st June 2022 as a preventive measures.
