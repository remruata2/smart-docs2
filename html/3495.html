A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 12.5.2014

Murder at Hliappui: John Horo (70) of Jharkhand, a caretaker of a coffee plantation was badly bashed up at Hliappui at 6:30 am yesterday. He succumbed to his injury before reaching hospital.

          According to reports, the alleged killer Lalremsiama (24) of Hliappui went to the hut of John Horo asking for food and water to which John Horo replied in an insulting way. That made Lalremsiama lost his temper and slashed John Horo’s neck with a knife. John Horo did not die on the spot, he crawled up to the main road for help; he could not talk but he was able to point to the house of his killer.

          Lalremsiama’s family handed him over to Ngopa Police before the crime was spread in the community. Hliappui people ran after Lalremsiama but were stopped at Ngopa checkgate by Ngopa Police before anything untoward happened.

          John Horo was from Jharkhand and his relatives are not known. He had been staying at Hliappui for a long time and he was a Christian.  Post mortem was done at Ngopa Hospital and he was buried at Hliappui.
