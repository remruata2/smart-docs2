A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 2.5.2014

Dharmendra Kumar - New DGP: Union Home Minister announced yesterday that Dharmendra Kumar, IPS will be the new DGP of Mizoram.  He is an IPS of 1984 Batch and now holds the post of Special Commissioner of Delhi Police. He is directed to join his new post before 17th May 2014. AK Patnaik, Addl. DGP, an interim DGP of Mizoram will continue to hold Addl. DGP of Mizoram. MHA also announced that Zorammawia, IPS, and now an interim IGP is promoted to regular IGP.  

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 2.5.2014

Heroin Seized: Special Narcotic PS Staff of CID (Crime) and Aizawl PS Staff seized 43 grams of Heroin at Bungkawn Vengthar yesterday at 11:00 am and arrested the alleged owner Vannuampuii (27) of Falam, Myanmar and Lalbiaksanga (25) of Zokhawmawi, Myanmar. The seized Heroin worth ` 3,14,000/- in the market rate.
