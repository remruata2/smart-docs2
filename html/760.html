Sir,

 

                     With reference to your letter no. and subject cited above, I am sending herewith the assessment report regarding conduct of Bye-Election to Village Council in the HPC(D) demand areas for your kind information and necessary action.

 

                 4(four) Sector Officers of this unit viz. Vairengte, Kolasib, Sakawrdai and Saitual have conducted assessment by contacting the local leaders and political party leaders within their respective jurisdictions. All the SOs found no imminent threat from outside elements particularly from HPC(D) which could undermind the peaceful holding of Bye-Election within HPC(D) demand areas. It is also observed that the villagers support for early Bye-Election of the Village Council within their respective villages.

 

                 In view of the above assessment, it is observed that the present situation within HPC(D) demand areas remain relatively peaceful and no imminent threat to Bye-Election of Village Council has been perceived from any element outfit.
