****REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/579

Dated: 17th October, 2020

In continuation to our report Vide No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/576 Dt. 16.10.2020, Shri. Bijoy Malakar, MLA, Ratabari visited Pu John Zolawma Jhum hut, located at Mizoram-Assam border which was newly reconstructed after it was burnt down on 9.10.2020. The MLA was accompanied by Shri Ashish Nath, Chairman, Zilla Parishad, Karimganj, 2 (two) BJP Leaders, around 30 (thirty) other civilians and 25 (twenty five) Assam Police personnel. They reached Pu John Zolawma’s jhum at 3:15 p.m. today and they intend to proceed upto Tlawngte river. They were, however, not allowed to proceed to Tlawngte river by Mizoram Police force deployed in the area after the above incident and accordingly proceeded back for Zaite village, Assam at 3:40 p.m. The said MLA and party were received by SP Mamit and CO. 4th IR Bn.

Delivering a speech at Pu Zolawma’s jhum, Shri. Bijoy Malakar, MLA mentioned that if Mizoram Government fails to stop its illegal activities within Assam area, he will bring the matter to the notice of Chief Minister, Assam for blockade of all inter-state road connectivity between Assam and Mizoram.

Pu Zothantluanga, Aizawl West-I MLA visited Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border today i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 1:30 p.m. and handed over 10 (ten) silpauline sheets to Police on duty and left the area around 2:00 p.m. today.

As per further input, one Assam MP intended to visit Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border on Tuesday i.e., 20.10.2020.

 Situation is under close watch.
