**MUP Membership, Election criteria and eligibility:**

****MUP is an organisation for the elders (aged 60 above), wherein its members consists of all elders in Mizoram, irrespective of Caste, Race or Religion. As the organisation is set up for the main purpose of community service after their retirement, so this organisation does not make any discrimination on the grounds of political organisation or religious communities. Furthermore, as compared to other non-political organisation, MUP does not indulge in any campaign nor any malicious malpractice in their elections till date.

 As stated in their Constitution, on the year of their General Assembly, members of the Executive Committee nominates twelve (12) persons as official candidates and on the day of the election, five (5) more persons are nominated from the Floor. Then, along with the Official Candidates, altogether along with the five(5) nominated from the Floor, 17 persons are put up for election to the Gen. Hqrs Office Bearers, where six(6) persons are elected on a post by post basis. Each elected Office Bearers serves the elected post for not more than two (2) consecutive terms. Although not stated in their Constitution, the members of Gen. Hqrs. Office Bearers are generally elected from within Aizawl City.
