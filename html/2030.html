STATEMENT OF LALROKIMA (40) @ RINSANGA PA

S/O RAMSADUH (L) OF NORTH PHULPUI

 

          Ka hming leh ka nihna chu a chung ami ang khi a ni a. Eizawnna atan hian a tul tul thawkin thing te pawh zaiin kan chhungkua ka chawm thin a ni. Rinsanga Pa min tih nachhan chu ka fapa boral ta Rinsanga(L) vang a ni.

 

1. BNCT (Borok National Council of Tripura) leh NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) pawlah hian engtik atangin nge i tel?

Ans. :          Heng pawlte hi ka zawm chiah ka hre lo nachungin nikum November khan Vanlalliana'n Bangladesh-ah min hruai a, thlem deuh titih, vau deuh titih a min sawm avangin NLFT hi ka hlauh avangin ka pui a ni.

 

2. Vanlalliana chu tunge? Engtia in hria nge an nih?

Ans. :          Vanlaliana hi West Phulpuia khawsa ve thin anih avanga inhria kan ni. Nikum (2011) Krismas hma khan insawnin Lungleiah a pem a ni.

 

3. Vanlalliana hian Bangladesh-ah min hruai i ti a, engvangnge? engtik hun lai khan nge?

Ans. :          Bangladesh-a Vanlalliana'n min hruaina chhan ber ni a ka hriat chu an pawla telve tura min duh vang ni berin ka hria. Bangladesh-a min hruai hun hi nikum November 2011 vel kha niin ka hria, tin, Vanlalliana hian an pawl hming chu NLFT anih thu min hrilh bawk.  

 

4.Bangladesh in kal chuan eng kawng nge in zawh?

Ans. :          Phulpui khaw hlui lamah kan kal a (hei hi tuna W.Phulpui khaw chhung ani). Dhanpara an tih khuaah kan thleng a (Silsuri khaw thlen hma lawk), kan  kalna kawng hi Vanlalliana hian a hrechiang hle in ka hria (BSF te pawhin min hmu lo). Bangladesh tuipui kan thleng a, in 20/30 vel awm khua kan lut leh a, he khuaah hian Mizo pawh ka hmu nual ani. BDR (Bangladesh Rifles) te pawhin min hmu a, mahse civilian emaw min tih vang aniang engtinmah min ti lo. Vanlalliana hi heng lai vel khua ami te hian an hmelhriat hmelin a tlangnel hle ani.(Vanlalliana hmelhriatte hi zirtirtu an niin ka hria). He khua, Thangnang an tih kan thlen nan hian ni 2/3 vel chu kan thang tih ka hre chiang hle a ni. He khuaah hian Camouflage uniform ha mi 10 bawr vel ka hmu a, Pistol leh AK an tih ang chi pawh hi an keng nual bawk.Vanlalliana hian chung ho chu NLFT an nih thu leh amah pawh chu an hotu en riaua hriatna ka nei bawk a. Vanlalliana hian, 'Kan pawl ten enthlatu atan kan duh che a, pawisa pawh kan sem/pe ve thin ang che' min ti a, ka hlauh avangin 'Aw' ka ti ta mai a ni. BNCT hi an lam rik ka hre lo hrim hrim. An hotu pa ber anga lang, pa lian ngo lampang chu 'PHAIKRAU' anih thu leh Vanlallian ai pawhin a lal hmel zawk in ka hria.
