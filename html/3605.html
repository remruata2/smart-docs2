A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.22.11.2022

Sialhawk village requested government to grant wine license: Sialhawk pineapple growers have sold off Rs. 274 lakhs worth of pineapple till October of this year (2022). At the same time, leaders of Sialhawk village community requested Mizoram state government to grant them license  for manufacturing wine from their pineapple, recently. Their request, which was submitted to Excise & Narcotics Minister, however, has not been yet replied till today. The reason behind their desire to manufacture wine from pineapple is because of their expectation to earn more profit instead of selling their fresh products in the market. Speaking to VANGLAINI in this regard, Dr. K. Beichhua, Minister Excise & Narcotics, stated that the request of Sialhawk village has not been yet discussed and license has been granted by the government for manufacturing of wines from grapes only at the moment.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 22.11.2022

Seizure of firearms: A joint team of personnel from 8th Assam Rifles and Custom Preventive Force of Champhai yesterday (21.11.2022) recovered two India made rifles, ammunitions, grenade and walkie talkie at around Farlui-Tiau area in the Indo Myanmar border areas. The seized items were kept in the custody of Champhai Custom department. Meanwhile, on the same day, Mizoram police in collaboration with Assam Rifles seized .22 cal. pistol from one person at Armed Veng South, Aizawl along with 30 rounds of its ammunitions.

 

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 22.11.2022

Union Minister in Aizawl : Pu Ashwini Kumar Choubey, Union Minister for Environment, Forest and Climate change (EFCC) and Consumer Affairs, Food and Public distribution arrived at Aizawl yesterday (22.11.2022) for a two days visit to Mizoram and was received at Lengpui Airport by Pu K.Lalrinthanga, Mizoram FCSCA Minister. The union Minister will attend the ceremony for the handing over of appointment letters to newly recruited personnel of 2nd Assam Rifles today and visit EFCC and FCSCA offices of Mizoram government. He will also meet Mizoram Chief Minister tomorrow (23.11.2022) at  Chief Minister's Bungalow before leaving Mizoram.
