REPORT ON ALLEGED SETTING UP OF CAMP(S) BY ARAKAN
ARMY (AA) INSIDE MIZORAM
Information has been received from Sister Intelligence Agency that the AA (Arakan
Army) is setting up camps at Kakichhuah areas, along the Indo-Myanmar border on Mizoram
soil. In this regard, an enquiry was conducted to ascertain the stated input.
Pu Vanlalmawia of Hruitezawl Village was engaged and he personally visited
Kakichhuah and its surrounding areas on 19.12.2019 & 20.12.2019. He contacted several
reliable persons residing in the areas and as per his report there is no evidence to suggest the
existence of AA camp inside Mizoram.
Pu Laltlanchhuaha, VCP, Kakichhuah was contacted through telephone and he stated
that there is no AA Camp in Kakichhuah or its surrounding areas, such as, Sabualtlang. He,
however, pointed out that there were 2-3 AA camps at Kakichhuah areas during 2018 and the
AA Cadres were also frequently being sighted during 2018. He reported that those camps
were vacated during the concluding period of 2018 due to frequent clashes between AA and
Myanmar Army and due to heightened presence of Indian security forces in the areas. The
Village Council leaders were informed to keep close vigil on possible presence of AA on
Indian soil.
Known AA leaders were also contacted and they reported that they have no camp
inside Mizoram and reiterated that they have no intention to establish camp(s) in Mizoram in
the foreseeable future. As per input, the nearest AA camp inside Myanmar is around 15 kms
from the Indo-Myanmar border.
