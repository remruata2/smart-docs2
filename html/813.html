RECENT INCIDENT ALONG INDO-MYANMAR BORDER – CLASHES BETWEEN MYANMAR ARMY AND ARAKAN ARMY

 

            On 3rd May 2018 at around 9:10 am to 10 am in the morning, there was a gun fight between cadres of Arakan Army led by SS Captain Dinja, numbering around 200-300 and Myanmar Army in between Kumbrang village and Longmasu on the bank of Kaladan River, 2 km north of Kon Pyin Village, around 13 kilometres from Mizoram border. According to information received, this gun fight resulted in the death of 4 Myanmar Army personnel (including two officers) and 6 to 8 Myanmar Army personnel were also injured.  There has been no casualty from Arakan Army side, report said. It is also reported that the Myanmar Army are preparing for another heavy operation against Arakan Army before rainy season.

           

            Apart from this, there has been no report of any other arms confrontation between the two parties till today.
