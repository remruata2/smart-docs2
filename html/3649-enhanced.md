****12.5.2022 :**All Mizoram Farmers' Union (AMFU) held its General Assembly on 12.5.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at MC Hall, Durtlang. Prof. Chawngkhuma Chawngthu, Sr. Vice President, AMFU led the assembly and around 110 (one hundred and ten) members attended. Delivering a speech at the function, Pu C. Lalrinsanga, Agriculture Minister &amp; Chief Guest highlighted the various initiatives being undertaken by the state government for the benefits of farmers within the state. He also highlighted various productive methods of farming and urged all members present to follow such practices. The assembly passed the following 2 (two) resolutions:**

 (i) Expressed gratitude to the state government for introducing SEDP. At  the same time, selection of beneficiaries should be carried out in a fair  and impartial manner.

 (ii) The state government may provide suitable site/place for setting up of  AMFU Office in Aizawl.

 Election of OB for 2022-2025 was also conducted and Pu Chhuanvawra of Rawpuichhip was elected as President, AMFU.

****24.6.2022 :** All Mizoram Farmers Union (AMFU) held its Executive Meeting on 24.6.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at AMFU Office, Treasury Square, Aizawl. Pu Chhuanvawra, President AMFU presided the meeting and 24 (twenty four) members attended. The meeting decided the following:**

 (i) Election of AMFU District, Sub-Division and Branch should be held at the earliest.

 (ii) Expressed their support to State government's SEDP which reached the farmers, and appealed all farmers to utilize the same beneficially.

 (iii) Necessary action should be taken with the state government on various difficulties faced by farmers.

 (iv) It requested the state government to arrange a convenient time for COVID-19 vaccination to farmers, who are staying at their farm land.

 (v) It requested the state government not to import vegetables, where such kinds of vegetables are harvested within the state.

 (vi) It requested the state government to address the difficulties faced by grape growers as they have an unsold stocks of 25 (twenty five) Fermented Tanks due to restriction imposed by the state government, in midst of grape harvesting season about to set in.
 A Press Release in this regard was issued today.

**25.7.2022 :** All Mizoram Farmers Union (AMFU) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 23.7.2022 stated that leaders of AMFU General Headquarters led by Pu Chhuanvawra, President AMFU visited Hnahlan Winery and Champhai Winery the same day and discussion was held on the problems being faced by grape growers. It also stated that leaders of Grape growers requested AMFU to take necessary action so as to allow free selling of wine made from their harvested grapes. It further stated that 1,14,000 litres of wine made from harvested grapes are currently lying at Champhai Winery and 30,000 litres at Hnahlan Winery. It also stated that grapes are being harvested and farmers are facing hardship due to prohibition of wine. Leaders of AMFU Champhai District Headquarters accompanied them during their visit of the stated wineries.
