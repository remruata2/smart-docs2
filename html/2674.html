PROFORMA FOR MILITANT ORGANISATIONS

 

  1.  

Name of organisation

 

:

Bru National Army (BNA)
  1.  

When formed

 

:

Though exact time of its formation is not known, they are the remnants of the then BLFM who refused to surrender at the time of the latter's mass surrender on 26.10.2006. They formerly call themselves as Bru Revolutionary Army(BRA), Bru Liberation Army(BLA) etc and its present outfit's name i.e. Bru National Army(BNA) is adopted from May 2012.

 
  1.  

Aims and objects

 

:

They seem to have no concrete aims and objectives and are more likely to be an armed gang rather than well organised militant group.
