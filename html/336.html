RESUMPTION OF PEACE TALK BETWEEN GOVERNMENT OF MIZORAM AND HPC (D)

 

Introduction : In the wake of the resumption of Peace Talk between Government of Mizoram and HPC (D) which is likely to take place in the near future. It is anticipated that the Peace Talk may be last for 5 (Five) Rounds and the venue for Peace Talk is proposed to be held at Tourist Lodge, Chaltlang, Aizawl, Mizoram. The First Round of Peace Talk is scheduled to be held on 10th, August, 2016. It is also proposed that 4 (four) delegates may represent Government of Mizoram and the counterpart, i.e. HPC (D) will reportedly detail 4 (four) delegates at the First Round of Peace Talk. The proposed delegates from Government of Mizoram are mentioned below:-

  1. Pu Lalbiakzama, Addl. Secy, Home, GOM
  2. Pu Lalhriatpuia, Jt. Secy.–II, Home, GOM
  3. Pu Rodingliana Chawngthu, SP CID (SB)
  4. Pu David H. Lalthangliana OSD, Home
