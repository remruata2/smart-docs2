**1. Sairang PS Case No.10/24 dt.12.03.2024 u/s 5(a) Explosives Substances Act.**

Seized items :  1. 28 cartons of true blast, scurry explosive (Each carton weighing 25 kgs, containing 9 x L1 and 1 x L2 type explosive tubes) and 4 cartons of Detonators cap (Each carton containing 250 detonator cap and each carton weighed 12 kgs).

Accused:  1. H.Lalmalsawma @ Sawmtea s/o H.Lalrinawma, Phunchawng

2. Lalrokima, Mission Veng

3. H. Lalnunmawia, Kolasib Vengthar, P/A Lengpui

Gist of the case :  During the early part of February 2024, H. Lalmalsawma was contacted through phone by one named Robert @ Alu-a who later was known to be the source for Assam Rifles. H. Lalmalsawma is a resident of Phunchawng, who often deals and engage in petty businesses including selling of loose gelatine sticks to earn their livelihood and sustenance.

 Robert had asked him to collect explosives worth Rs.3 lakhs destined for Manipur. However, he was given only Rs 50,000/- and told him to give the rest of the amount once the consignment is ready.

 On the morning of dt.11.02.2024 Robert called Malsawma and asked him to deliver the consignment on the evening****and threatened him that his family would pay the price to the extent of slicing their throat. Malsawma sought the help of his friend Lalroliana and even borrowed his money for purchasing the explosives. The two bought the explosives from H. Lalnunmawia, a hired employee to look after M/S JAB explosives, Go-down, Sairang.

Modus operation :  On being pressurised by Robert @ Alu-a the duo collected the explosives consignment and while they were about to hand it over to Robert. They were apprehended by Assam Rifles while letting off Robert. They were apprehended on the night of dt.11.02.2024 however without producing them instantly to the concerned Police Station they were kept in their custody and only produced them in the morning of dt.12.02.2024 at Sairang PS. The phone number of Robert was deleted by the Assam Rifles personnel from the contact list of the two arrested - H.Lalmalsawma and Lalrokima.

 Upon enquiring into the matter it was found that H.Lalnunmawia had misappropriated explosives items from JAB explosives go-down on several occasions and in this regard Pi Lucy Lalnunsangi, owner of JAB explosives submitted FIR and Sairang PS Case No.11/24****dt.12.03.2024 u/s 380 IPC was registered.

 Action taken : The phone numbers of H.Lalmalsawma (9612698109), Lalrokima (9862378533) and H. Lalnunmawia (6033343304) were obtained and Call Details Report (CDR) of their respective phone numbers were sought for analysis.
