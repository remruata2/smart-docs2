A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.4.2014

Fire Service Week: All India Fire Service Week is to be held from 14th to 20th April, 2014. Fire & Emergency Service Department is carrying out fire safety inspection visiting workshop, private hospital, etc. of cities in each district.

          Regarding a Fire Service Week, a number of pamphlets are distributed and poster painting competition is organised for High School and Middle School students in Aizawl.

          The first day of Fire Service Week ie, 14.4.2014 will be observed as 'Martyrs Day' to mourn those who died while on duty in fire service all over India.

          Awareness-cum-demonstration is to be held at Millennium Centre terrace during Fire Service Week and Evacuation Mock Drill is also planned to be done in some school.
