Threat perception assessment of AR Ground and Raj Bhavan and Protected Persons

 

 

          Assessment of threat perception in respect of AR Ground and Raj Bhavan premises which are the proposed venues for the Independence Day celebration, 2020 scheduled to be held on 15th August, 2020 by Mizoram State Government along with all the protected persons within the state is hereby furnish as follows.

 

          During the course of threat perception assessment, sources and contacts, links and informants from insurgent groups operating in the adjoining  interstate and international borders were contacted besides constantly monitoring their activities and movements. Despite the existence and presence of several insurgent groups having base and operating areas along the interstate and international border, it is learnt that, at this moment, none of these underground insurgents pose any threat to disrupt or thwart the scheduled celebration of the said National Day. It is also further learnt that threat perceived  against various protected persons of the state Government from insurgent groups is minimal at the moment. It may be noted that the impending issues undertaken by Social Organisations, Student Bodies, Civil Organization were also taken into account.

 

          It is concluded that, the threat perceived against various protected persons of the state Government as well as the venues for the proposed National Day from insurgent groups, NGOs, student bodies, civil society organizations, political rivalry or even from personal animosity is considered minimal at the moment.

 

 

                      Threat perception assessment outside Aizawl City

 

                      Presently, CID (SB) is having 15 sectors, spreading throughout Mizoram viz. Lunglei, Lawngtlai, Siaha, Serchhip, Champhai, Saitual, Kolasib, Vairengte, Mamit, West Phaileng, Bairabi, Chawngte, Sakawrdai, Tlabung and Zawlnuam. Threat perception assessment  was conducted by Sector Officers in their respective jurisdiction and it is perceived that (except Chawngte) there is no imminent threat to the forthcoming Independence Day celebration, 2020.

 

                      At the same time, information has been received that Joint Young Mizo Association, Chawngte, in its demand for immediate repair of dilapidated road condition between Chawngte to Rualalung, Lunglei District, intended to resort to picketing of Government Offices and Banks within Chawngte if no action is taken for its improvement on or before 10.8.2020. If no action is taken till 14.8.2020, they further intend to boycott Independence Day celebration, 2020 at Chawngte.
