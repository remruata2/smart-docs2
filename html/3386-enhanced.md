A. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 25.4.2014_

**Police Rescued Kidnapped/Abducted****:**Champhai Police rescued Rorelsanga (44) h/o Lalrinzami of Vairengte on 23.4.2014 at Champhai Vengthlang, he was kidnapped as they have dispute regarding money between them.

 According to Police report, Rorelsanga was kidnapped from Zuangtui, Aizawl on 21.4.2014 night by the sons of Lalramliana Sailo of Champhai – Joseph Lalthuamluaia and his brother Moses Lalchhuanawma; with their father's consent and kept Rorelsanga at the basement of their house at Champhai.

Joseph and Moses recently went to Silchar for business and were helped by Rorelsanga, they were robbed and lost certain amount of money. The brothers assumed that their robbing was pre-planned by Rorelsanga, so they threatened him with a pistol and kidnapped him and brought to Champhai.

Lalramliana Sailo, driver – VL Hmangaihzuala of Ngopa, and handyman – Tinsuanmanga of Hnahlan were arrested for their involvement in the kidnapping along with Remzamliani of Champhai Vengsang who allegedly lured Rorelsanga. A **.**22 pistol and an Indica car used for the kidnap were also seized.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 25.4.2014_

****Lungthulien Feels Insecure****:**1st Battalion MAP had operation at Lungthulien, Churachandpur District, Manipur bordering Mizoram on Tuesday regarding reports of HPC(D) hiding/stocking up arms and ammunitions there. Knowing that MAP were coming, HPC(D) cleared up before operation team’s arrival and MAP missed them and no arms and ammunitions was found. Regarding MAP Operation, HPC(D) feels that there are people who reported their activity and there was a report that HPC(D) were coming/moving towards Lungthulien after the operation party left and this made the villagers of Lungthulien insecure.**
