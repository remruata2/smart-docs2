****REPORT ON RECENT INCIDENT ALONG INDO-MYANMAR BORDER****

 Information has been received that there was an encounter between Myanmar Army (MA) and rebel Arakan Army (AA) on 26.2.2018 @ 11:00 am at Kumprang Village on Myanmar side of the border close to Kakichhuah of Lawngtlai District. Details about casualty are yet to be ascertained, and so far, there has been no report of fresh influx of refugees following the incident. Situation is closely monitored.
