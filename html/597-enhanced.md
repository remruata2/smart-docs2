****RECOVERY OF ARMS****

****DT. 31.8.2010****

 In a coordinated effort by CID(SB), SIB, 10th AR and Kolasib Police, an illegal arms racket was busted as the Kolasib Police acting on a tip of raided the house of one Steve Lalhruaichhuaka (21) S/o Thangneihkhuma Hmar of Kolasib Tumpui Veng (who is working in the Minor Irrigation Deptt. on contract basis) on the night of 31.8.2010 and seized the following firearms and arms licences.

1. .22 Pistol(country made) bearing markings - Walter USA, Sl.No. 52912 with licence No. 13983/Azl/PR/2006 purportedly issued in the name of R. Lalneihsanga S/o Rengchhawna of Republic Veng.

2. .22 Revolver(country made) bearing markings - Colt made in USA, Sl.No. 1107 with licence No. 11498/Azl/111/91 purportedly issued in the name of C. Lalsangluaia S/o Lalhlira of Dinthar, Aizawl.

3. .22 Ammunitions - 25 rounds.

4. Unregistered Arms Licences - 5 nos.

 KLB PS Case No. 70/10 dt. 1.9.10 u/s 25(1B)(a) Arms Act has been registered against Steve Lahruaichhuaka (21) S/o Thangneihkhuma Hmar of Kolasib Tumpui Veng. During preliminary interrogation the accused had revealed that he had purchased the two weapons from Pu Dailova of Republic Veng for Rs.30,000/- and from Pu Kapliana of Venghnuai for Rs.25,000/- respectively.

  The accused also revealed that he had purchased the arms licences from Pi Sainunpari, LDC working in Arms Branch of D.C, Aizawl office @ Rs.10,000/- per licence and that Pi Sainunpari had also helped him to obtain retainership licences which would facilitate easy disposal to the prospective buyers.

 Pi Sainunpari is presently on leave and was interviewed by a team of CID(SB), SIB and 10th AR at her residence at Ramhlun Veng. She admitted having sold a number of Arms licences to Steve Lalhruaichhuaka. She revealed that she had purchased the arms licences from the licensees at different prices depending on the type which she again sold to the prospective buyers after obtaining retainership licence for them by which she gets the commission.
