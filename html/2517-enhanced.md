****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/569

Dated 26th July, 2021

**(a)** Information received stated that today i.e., 26.7.2021 @ 11:30 a.m. around 200 (two hundred) Assam Police led by Shri. Anurag Agarwal, IGP, Assam arrived at Vairengte Auto Stand, near Vairengte (inside Mizoram territory) and tried to forcefully push back 1st Mizo IR Battalion Personnel, who are on duty at the area. Upon learning the above incident, Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib and party immediately rushed to the area and tried to have a dialogue with Assam Police, but the Assam Police refused and informed him that the area falls within Assam territory and they intend to construct camp at the area.

 Later, Assam Police, who heavily outnumbered Mizoram Police at the area forcefuly pushed back Mizoram Police and stationed themselves at Mizo IR Bn. camp. Civilians from Vairengte also arrived at the area, and a confrontation and scuffle took place between Assam Police and residents of Vairengte. Assam Police fired a number of Tear Gas and Smoke Bomb towards civilians of Vairengte and Mizoram Police and due to this, the following 7 (seven) persons sustained minor injuries:

1. Zonunsiama (25) of Venghlun, Vairengte
2. C. Vanlalfela (57) Venglai, Vairengte
3. Lalmuanpuia Colney , Venghlun, Vairengte
4. Laldampuia (27), IOC Veng, Vairengte
5. Michel Rosangliana (22), Zalenveng, Vairengte
6. Vanlalzawna (30) S/o Lalthlanliana, Kanan, Aizawl
7. Sailoliana (49) S/o Ngurkhuma of 2nd MAP, Lunglei.

 One Mizo couple, who were proceeding towards Mizoram by their swift vehicle were physically assaulted and attacked by unknown miscreants from Assam, and as a result of which, they sustained injury on their persons and their vehicle was severely damaged.

 Tension between the 2 (two) sides continued to escalate as Assam Police continue to forcefully occupy Mizo IR Bn Camp and this evening @ around 4:40 p.m. exchange of fire took place at the inter-state border as Assam Police first open fire towards Mizoram Police and civilians. The firing lasted for about 45 minutes and subsequently Assam Police abandoned the Camp and withdrew from the area and Mizoram police are still remaining at the area at the time of writing the report. As per unconfirmed input, there are reported casualties among Assam Police Personnel due to firing and there is one Hav. Lalbuatsaiha of 5th IR who sustained a minor injury of grazed gun shot wound on his arm among Mizoram Police. PC Lalmuansanga of Vairengte sustained bullet injury on his thigh and was evacuated to District Hospital, Kolasib.

 _Cont/-2..._

-2-

 After occurrence of the above incident, 1(one) Bolero and 1(one) Jeep used by Assam Police, which were stationed at the area were allegedly set ablaze by unknown miscreant(s) this evening after they were abandoned by Assam Police.

 In connection with the above incident, heavy non-Mizo vehicles proceeding towards Assam are blocked at Kawnpui under the aegis of MZP Headquarters Kawnpui w.e.f., this afternoon and so far 25 (twenty five) vehicles were blocked.

**(b)**As per reliable input, Assam made reinforcement of Police Force at Ronpur Police Outpost, Karimganj District today. The said reinforcement was allegedly made with an intention to engage illegal and violent activities inside Mizoram at Thinghlun area.

**(c)** Information received also stated that last evening i.e., Dt. 25.7.2021 @ 11:30, 8 (eight) unoccupied farm houses belonging to the following persons:

 (i) Pi Hlirtei of Vairengte Zalen Veng

 (ii) Pu Saizuala Sailo of Vairengte Field Veng

 (iii) Pu Kenneth Lalchhandama of Vairengte Field Veng

 (iv) Pu Hrangmana of Vairengte Venghlun

 (v) Pu Darthangvunga of Bangla Veng

 All located near Aitlang stream were allegedly set ablaze by unknown miscreant(s). In this connection, a case vide Vairengte PS C/No. 18 u/s 436 IPC Dt. 26.7.2021 has been registered and investigation is being underway.
