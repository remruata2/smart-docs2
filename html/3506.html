A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.5.2014

20,000 Gelatin Detonator Seized: The joined team of Special Narcotic PS Staff of CID (Crime) and 36th Assam Rifles seized 40 boxes of Gelatin detonators yesterday evening at around 4:40pm at Bungkawn Phuihnamchhe Veng. There were 20,000 detonators in the boxes and the value is around ` 4,40,000/-.

          The alleged owners Zabiaka (49) s/o Lianlunga of Falam, Myanmar and Thanga (37) s/o Liannawla of Kalemyo, Myanmar were arrested. They told the police that the detonators were handed to them by non-Mizos to be sent to Myanmar. These two men were charged under section 5 of Explosive Substance Act.

         

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.5.2014

Pseudoephedrine Seized: The number of Pseudoephedrine tablets seized by the police on 26th May at around 5:00pm was 21,25,100 and the market value at Aizawl is ` 63,75,300/-.

                   He seized Pseudoephedrine is of three kinds – 14,86,800 tablets of S-Kuf; 62,800 tablets of HL Cold and 5,75,500 tablets of Sucet, all these tablets have 60% of Pseudoephedrine in them. Pseudoephedrine was seized from LD Drug Store owned by Andrew Lalawmawia (31) s/o F.Laldika of Khatla.
