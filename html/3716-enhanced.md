****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/549

Dated 14th July, 2021

Information received stated that Assam intended to enter into Mizoram and take new initiative by setting up camp near Tuikhuahlian Bridge, which is located around 1 km towards Kanhmun village (which they claim as within their territory based on Google map) from Medili Bridge (inter-state border) on 15.7.2021 or 16.7.2021. In this connection, a huge contingent of Assam Police is presently stationed at Rangamati, Karimganj District, which is around 10 km from Medili Bridge. It is also reported that Brus and Muslim residing near the inter-state border intended to support Assam government’s initiative.

Further, 1 (one) Company of Assam Police is presently stationed at Gutguti village, Hailakandi District, which is located around 3 km from Saikhawthlir village and they are accompanied by forest officials to take initiative at Chuhvel border.

Information received further stated that one motorized boat boarded by 6 (six) Assam Police proceeded upstream at Tlawng river near Bairabi town today i.e., 14.7.2021 @ 8:50 a.m. and they proceeded towards Teirei river. Later, this morning @ 9:30 a.m., 2 (two) motorized boats boarded by 7 (seven) Assam Police and 8 (eight) Assam Police respectively also proceeded towards Teirei river. The two parties were reported to have proceeded to Gutguti BOP, Hailakandi District Assam.
Information received stated that a huge contingent of Assam Police, who are presently stationed at Lailapur, Cachar District intended to visit Pi Thangseii’s paddy field at Mautuilui zau, which is located around 4 km east of Vairengte. In this connection, 20 (twenty) personnel from 2nd IR Battalion headed by Addl. SP, Kolasib are presently deployed at the area.
Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IPS, DIG (NR) is presently stationed at Vairengte to monitor the present inter-state border situation.
