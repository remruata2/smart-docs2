****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/283

Dated: 24th April, 2019.

 Information has been received that Pi Vanlalchami, Senior Civil Judge-I had issued an order Memo No. 250/SR.CJ-I(A)/2019 dated 05.04.2019 for eviction of 11 families, who it considered are illegally occupying the land of Pi Saizami Sailo w/o Pu SainguraSailo (L) of Dawrpui Vengthar, in Lungbial veng, Zemabawk and demolition of their houses before the end of 25.04.2019. The Court entrusted Officer-in-charge, Bawngkawn PS to execute the Court Sentence and also instructed PWD to provide JCB excavator for the demolition purpose. OC, Bawngkawn PS has to submit action taken/compliance report by 30.04.2019. Copy of the Court Order was also given to the 11 families. The order was passed following a Court Case on the basis of the petition submitted by Pi Saizami Sailo w/o Pu Saingura Sailo (L) of Dawrpui Vengthar. There is also a report of 20 other families who are expected to be further evicted after the completion of the eviction of the above mentioned 11 families and demolition of their houses. There is also a Community Hall and a Church of Salvation Army within the disputed land.

 Information received indicated that the families within the disputed land were absolutely agitated by the Court Order. Lungbial Welfare Committee had a meeting last night, i.e. 23.04.2019 which was attended by around 15 persons under the chairmanship of Pu Biakchhawna. The meeting decided to take any course of action to prevent the eviction of these families. Zemabawk Local Council and Zemabawk Branch YMA also intended to champion the cause of these families of Lungbial. They will most likely gather near Home Mission School Junction to block any party that will come to execute the Court’s Order. They also claimed that they will resort to any sort of action, even to the extent of physical action, to prevent the eviction of these families.

 Information received also indicated that the youthful section of the affected families has been meeting secretly meeting till the writing of this report at the residence of Pu Lalruatliana, Lungbial deliberating what course of action they should be following in the event of any attempt to execute the Court’s Order by the Authorities.

 Situation under close watch.
