REPORT ON MYANMAR REFUGEE

No. MSB/CB/2021/

Dated 16th March, 2021

 

Information has been received that the below listed 9 (nine) persons who were initially suspected to be Myanmar boarded IndiGo flight 6E 2434 Lengpui to New Delhi flight upon production of necessary travel documents and departed from Lengpui Airport today i.e., 16.3.2021 @12:30 p.m. As per information received, they intended to stay at the residence of Pastor Huhauthang, Uttam Nagar, New Delhi.  

In this regard, Pastor Huhauthang, Uttam Nagar, New Delhi (Assembly of God Pastor) was contacted and he stated that these persons contacted him through Facebook and sought his help as their life are in danger due to military takeover in Myanmar and he informed them that if they can get to Delhi, he would find a way to get refugee status under the United Nations (UN).

Information received further indicated that these 9 (nine) persons entered into Mizoram in between 9.3.2021 and 12.3.2021 via Saikhumphai, Champhai District. They arrived at Aizawl yesterday i.e., 15.3.2021 and stayed at Pu Zamneihmang’s residence at Bungkawn, Aizawl.

It was later found that they used fake Adhaar Card for entering into Lengpui Airport after close scrutiny of photo copy of their Adhaar Cards.
