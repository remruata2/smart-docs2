ACTIVITIES:

 

1.         On 25.10.07, Pu Chawnghmingthanga, Headman of Chhippui village(Bangladesh) and son with 3 businessmen while crossing Hnahva village(Mizoram) were stopped at gun point by 2 Chakmas suspected to be Jono Songho Somiti(JSS Karma Faction) of Chakma insurgent groups operation inside Bangladesh and demanded Rs.30000/- from Pu Chawnghmingthanga and friends. They gave them Rs.1200/- to the Chakmas as it was all they had. The Chakmas while pointing at them with SBBL gun(modified) were not happy with such small amount of money and warned them to pay the demanded money in full after their return. The said Chakmas frequented Hnahva village and normally stayed at the house of one Chakma who had a big house having orange garden at Hnahva village.

 

2.         During the later part of September 2009, S/S Army Chief Ashist of Jono Songho Somiti(former Santi Bahini), S/S Maj. Nikhul Chakma and S/S Lt.Hitusi Chakma of the same outfit are learnt to have constructed their respective houses at Nunsuri, Tlabung Sub-Division of Lunglei District and they were frequently involved in collection of taxes near Avapui river of Mamit District.

 

3.         On 9.12.2009@ 10:00am, a group of 10(ten) JSS cadres(Chakma rebels of Bangladesh) armed with sophisticated weapons in full uniform had entered Silsury village in broad daylight. The commander of this group who is identified as Indra had reportedly told villagers that they were there to protect the people and were looking for dacoits operating in the area. The JSS are learnt to have established a temporary camp inside Indian Territory not far from Silsury after the recent offensive by Bangladesh Army.

 

4.         On 15.11.2010, one Robert Vanlalliana(23) S/o Zokhuma of Tumpui Veng, Kolasib presently engaged as an excavator operator by a private company under NBCC was assaulted by suspected JSS(Jono Songho Somiti), a Bangladesh based Chakma armed group near Hnahva village of Mamit District. The victim sustained some injuries and was handed over to V/C Hnahva by suspected JSS cadre. It was also reported that the JSS is having a hideout in Hnahva and Silsuri area of Mizoram side and frequently harassed Mizo community who used to pass through this area for petty business.

 

5.         On 4.11.2011@4:00pm, Pu Thangnghinglova@Vala of Dinthar Veng, Aizawl and Ramliana of Pukzing were reportedly confronted by two JSS volunteers and extorted Rs.500/- at gun point on the outskirt of Hnahva village(Mizoram side). It was further reported that the JSS insurgent outfit are having temporary camp on the bank of Aivapui river near Hnahva and occasionally collected taxes from the Mizo people passing through this area.

            Further information received also stated that two members of JSS are reportedly trying to purchase weapons and ammunition from Myanmarese arms smugglers and they are presently staying at Aizawl. Their whereabouts and possible sympathiser are being enquired. As per latest information received, they may proceed towards Champhai from Aizawl on 11.11.2011.
