A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 3.6.2013

Occupants of Sibinasora to depart: Occupants of Sibinasora will obey the notification of the community based groups of Lungsen to depart their village but they wish the deadline for them to be extended.

            Sibinasora is 5 Km away from Tuichawng Village. Tuichawng River ran through the middle of the village; one side is under Lungsen VC area with 30 houses while the other side has 11 houses which is under Zodin VC.

            This hamlet is said to be formed without the provision of the law and different groups of Lungsen such as MSU, MZP and VC notified the occupants to leave the place before the end of June, these groups requested the government to take action as they are afraid foreigners will settle themselves in this place and if anything untoward happens after the deadline, they will not be responsible.

            Meanwhile, Lunglei DC sent officials to inspect the place alleged to be formed by immigrants of Chakma without government’s permission.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 3.6.2013

Mizoram Police owned 200 Colt M4 Carbine: Mizoram Police bought 200 Colt M4 Carbine made by USA, one Colt M4 Carbine costs about Rs. 64,000/-. It was tested and examined first and bought afterwards. They will be distributed among Commando team, and 8 Rapid Action Force in MAP and IR Battalions. Mizoram Police is the second in India after Mumbai Police in acquiring Colt M4 Carbine.
