****ABDUCTION OF SANGLIANTHANGA, LALZAMLIANA & DEEP MANDAL****

 On Dt.**23.11.2013**, members of **National Liberation Front of Tripura(NLFT) and Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram(BNDLFM)** abducted three persons namely **(i) Deep Mandal S/o Nikhil Mandal, Indas,Bakura, West Bengal (ii) Sanglianthanga @ Maliana S/o VL Hmuaka, Mamit Charkawn, Taxi Driver (MZ-08-1308) (ii) Lalzamliana S/o Buanga (L) C/o K.Vanlalchhuanga, Zuangtui Muanna Veng, Aizawl (driver 207 Pickup-MZ-01G-5860)** from **Old Chika Anti-poaching Camp area within Dampa Sanctuary, Mamit District**. The three hostages were taken to the hideout of the militants in Bangladesh. The abductors had demanded Rs. 5 Crore ransom for the release of the hostages. In this connection, **West Phaileng****Police Station Case No.24/2013 Dt.25.11.2013 u/s 364 A/379/34 IPC R/w Sec 25(1A) Arms Act** has been registered.

SP CID (SB) at the same time formed a Special Team under the command of Pu. H.L.Thangzuala,MPS Addl SP CID(SB) to solve the case. The Team had contacted the NLFT leaders and ascertained that due to some clashes amongst the top leaders of NLFT, hostages negotiation could not be promptly expedited. However, all out effort were taken by the Special team. SP CID(SB) had engaged number of sources and sent to Bangladesh, Tripura to negotiate with the abductors. Some prominent Mizo leaders residing in Bangladesh were also contacted to take measure for the release of the hostages. Victim families were given all out necessary assistance.

 The team's sources from Bangladesh had contacted NLFT senior leaders on 2.1.2014. The NLFT officer informed that due to the upcoming Bangladesh General Election scheduled to be held on 5th January, 2014, the High Command members of NLFT are facing difficulties to make final decision for the early release of the abducted persons and further assured that the High Command members of NLFT would make decision soon after the election at Bangladesh was over.

 Sources sent to Dhaka also met Civil Wing leaders of NLFT on 2.1.2014 and they had discussion for the early release of the abducted persons and were pressing hard the NLFT Army Wing to honour the team's request after Bangladesh Election is over.

 One Lalduhawma @ Daniram Reang, S/S Gen. Secretary of Bru Development Liberation Front of Mizoram (BDLFM), who was strongly suspected to be team leader of the abduction party and Roton Reang were arrested on 30.12.2013 at Anando Bazar, North Tripura. CID(SB) Team under the command of Pu HL Thangzuala,Addl SP who were stationed in Kanchanpur area, conducted an intensive interrogation on the arrested persons.

  On 15.1.2014, a community search operation of the three hostages was launched in Mamit District area. A search party comprising of around 200 volunteers from Mamit headed towards West Phaileng as the initial persuasion by Mamit District authorities failed to convince the volunteers at the outskirt of Mamit town. The volunteers were however, stopped at West Phaileng by security forces and civil administrators of Mamit district. The search operation party from Mamit were dispersed @ 3:30 PM after SP Mamit and CID(SB) Rescue Operation Team briefed the public about the possible release of the hostages. The other search party led by MZP General Headquarters numbering around 45 volunteers was stopped near Dialdawk Bridge (in between Lengpui to Rawpuichhip road) by security forces and Magistrates deployed from Aizawl District. The volunteers (led by MZP) peacefully dispersed at 5:35 PM.

 On **20.1.2014**, two of the three persons abducted by National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) on 23.11.2013 were handed over to CID(SB) source/agent stationed at Lungtian,Bangladesh and safely arrived in Mizoram on 21.1.2014.

 The Central YMA, victimâ€™s families and other NGOs warmly received the released hostages at West Phaileng. After they were medically examined by the medical officer in West Phaileng, the main reception function was organized in Mamit in the evening around 8:00-10:00 pm. After the main reception function, the released hostages were taken to their respective residences. Unfortunately, the abductors still hold Shri Deep Mondal in their captivity.

 Due to the initiative taken by Mizoram Police, Deep mandal(25) S/o Nikhil Mandal, Indas, Bankura District, West Bengal was released by the abductors i.e. National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Development Front of Mizoram(BDFM) on **22.3.2014** after he was held captive inside Bangladesh for 119 days.

 Deep Mandal was received at Udalsuri village near Indo-Bangladesh border at 5:48pm on the same day i.e. 22.3.2014 by Special Team of Mizoram Police led by Pu HL. Thangzuala, Addl.SP, CID(Special Branch). The team escorted Deep Mandal from Udalsuri village to Mizoram and reached Aizawl on 23.3.2014@3:30pm safely. Deep Mandal was medically examined by Dr. Zirsangliana Chhangte, M.O West Phaileng PHC and his health condition was fine and stable. The release of Deep Mandal was finally materialised due to utmost efforts taken up by Mizoram Police especially CID(Special Branch) enduring an arduous negotiations with the abductors.

 Deep Mandal and his cousin brother Arnab Mandal left Mizoram for Kolkata on 24.3.2014 by flight @4:10pm from Lengpui Airport and reached his homeland Indas, Bankura District safely.
