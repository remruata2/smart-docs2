****UNITED LIBERATION FRONT OF BARAK VALLEY (ULFBV)****

1. On 17.2.2006, Mizoram Police arrested one ULFBV Vikramjoy@Lalngilneia(26) S/o Horida of Ruma, Cachar Zion, PS Katlicherra, Hailakandi District P/A Thinglian on their way from Phaisen/Buhchang area. The party recovered one local made gun with lead pellet from his possession.

2. On 7.2.2008 around 9:30am, a group of 7 suspected Bru militant out of them 3 were in army camouflage dress carrying one AK-47 rifle and some other small arms abducted one non-Mizo Thekadar namely Jamil Uddin Laskar(35) S/o Abdul Khalique Laskar(L) of Jamira, Hailakandi District, Assam P/A Saihapui while he was going to Damdiai river side from Saihapui village with his three labourers by his motorcycle bearing registration No.MZ-05/2669.

3. On 17.2.2008@8:00pm, the abducted person was rescued by join operation party of Mizoram Police led by SI. NK. Nath of Kolasib PS, Assam Police and CRPF from Kalapahar/Golachora area after he was abandoned by the militants who fled away and escaped on seeing the approaching operation party.

 In this regard, Kolasib PS C/No.13/2008 dt.8.2.2008 u/s365 IPC r/w 25(2A) Arms Act had been registered.

4. On 19.3.2008@ 5:00pm, 16 members of armed group suspected to be United Liberation Front of Barak Valley(ULFBV), an armed outfit of Cachar District, Assam visited 3 jhum huts near Chuhvel village occupied by Bru families. These armed militants extorted money, domestic fowls and 3 bags of dry chillies.

 These miscreants were armed with one AK-47 Rifle, two .22 Rifle, one Pistol and two country made guns. Two of them were identified and one country made gun was recovered from a house at Mizoram-Assam border. Police operation is still going on for arrest of the identified members of the outfit.

  Counter Operation of the extortion committed by United Liberation Front of Barak Valley(ULFBV), the Mizoram Police operation team raided one jhum hut belonging to Juraham@Jura s/o Komlaha of Gutguti and came to learn that 16 members of the said outfit had taken food and halted the night of 19.3.2008 in the jhum hut and left at around 8:00am of 20.3.2008. The said outfit divided themselves into two groups, 9 members with sophisticatedarms left for Riflemara and the 7 members with 2 country made guns headed towards Gutguti, Juraham could identify 2 members of the outfit namely Moinirai S/o Soren and Mukta Singh S/o Kosomoni both of Gutguti.
