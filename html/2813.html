Recommendation No.18

"e-shraddhanjali system to be introduced for paying digital homage to Police martyrs, on the lines of Defence Forces".

A circular was issued to all Branches of SP CID (SB) to access e-Shraddanjali through National Police Memorial website (https://nationalpolicememorial.gov.in/npm /eshradhanjali) and  pay digital homage to police matyrs on the line of Defence Forces.

 

Recommendation No. 36 & 45

36.       "States and Central agencies should enhance coordination on important security issues like narcotics, FICNs, arms smuggling, cybercrime, etc. for better results".

46.       "DGsP of States/ UTs should take assistance of specialized central agencies like NCB in cases of major seizures of narcotics. Similarly, in CT cases, States should share information with IB and NIA for coordinated CT action".

Whether common communication platform like SMAC is being effectively utilized for sharing information ?

Suggestion for better utilization of the platform

Highlight two prominent case studies where this platform was useful for the security issues like Narcotics, FICNs, Arms smuggling, Cybercrime, CT etc

SMAC system installed at the office of SP SB  is functioning round the clock and utilized for sharing and receiving relevant information.

SMAC system run through a connectivity of Leased Line provided by BSNL, however the connection is often interrupted many times and suggested that the Lease Line be taken from other Service Provider.

Till date no achievement or Seizure of Contraband items are made through the input pursue from this platform.

 

Recommendation No. 39 & 41

39.       "All agencies should work on better utilization of SMAC platform and make it more result oriented".

41.       "A common Communication Protocol should be developed to ensure better coordination and communication between agencies dealing with issues like coastal security, border management, North East and LWE. In this regard, an evaluation of existing coordination mechanism between agencies working in aforesaid areas is required to be carried out to ensure that communication protocol between agencies are well laid out and periodic review of the same is being held".

Whether States/UTs and CAPFs are utilizing SMAC platform for sharing information and coordination?

Suggestions to improve utility of SMAC platform for better coordination and communication

Whether any other Common Communication Protocol has been developed for better communication between agencies?

Concerning to SMAC systems at SP SB, whenever an input concerning to Mizoram is received follow action are taken to corroborate the input and feedback report are uploaded in the system.

Every Unit Head should emphasises in sharing intelligence and Incident report to SP SB to be uploaded in SMAC platform after scrupulous scrutiny.

No Common Communication Protocol has been developed between Agencies, however, depending upon the needs and  circumstances it has been communicated through mobile phone and whatsapps or FAX.

 

Recommendation No. 52 :-

"State should work on developing an inbuilt mechanism to prevent and counter radicalization in its initial stage".

Field Branch of CID(SB) closely monitored any form of radicalization within the State. Training/Briefing has also been conducted frequently to develop individual and group abilities to prevent and to counter radicalization in its initial stage.

No radicalization case has been encountered by this unit so far.

 

Recommendation No. 65 :-

"A dedicated infrastructural setup may be considered in the States for effective dissemination of counter-narratives to counter radicalization".

A dedicated infrastructural setup has not been yet established for effective dissemination of counter-narrative to counter radicalization. However, if any issue which may be considered having a relation with radicalization when encounter, will be disseminated to other units and agencies promptly so as to demise its counter measures.

 

 

Recommendation No.79:-

"States to have a centralized cyber patrolling infrastructure to store and analyse large volume of data in order to continue identifying and disrupting modules of radicalized entities".

            Graded Police response has not been yet adopted by the state to check radicalization.
