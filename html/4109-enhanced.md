  Security scenario in Mizoram is mostly peaceful. **IV. Security Review: The 78th Independence Day Celebration of India:**

 Public demonstration was held at Phuldungsei Village, Mmait District on Dt.09.08.2024 @ 08:00 am displaying placards urging the Govt. of Mizoram to revive the 10 deleted ration cards and members of their families due to the fact that the deleted members are indigineous of Mizoram. They demanded the State Govt. to address the issue before 14.08.2024 failing which they forewarn of boycotting the Celebration of Independence Day.

 In this regard, Supply Minister Pu B.Lalchhanzova addressed the situation and stated that the deleted family members will be given a manual ration card and will get their ration quotas manually till they get their normal ration cards which the Phuldungsei Jampui Village Council gratefully accepted. Hence, situation is normal and under close watch.
