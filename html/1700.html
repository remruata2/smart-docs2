             Apropos above,  I have the honour to submit the report on character and  antecedents of the  leaders (Interim  Body)   of  Phawngpui  UT  Demand  Committee  as below for  your  kind information  and  necessary  action.

 

1.          Rev. MZ.Lawmthang Sathing (78), S/o Mawnglinga(L), Lawngtlai  Bazar. His educational qualification is BD (Bachelor of Divinity). He  is  not  involved  in  other  activities  besides  being    the  President/Convener  of  Phawngpui  UT  Demand  Committee.

 

2.          Lalchunga  Chinzah (Ex- MLA, Ex- Chief  Executive Member  of  LADC) (82) S/o Lianhmunga (L) of  Council  Veng, Lawngtlai. His educational  qualification  is  Bachelor  of  Arts (Hons.) and  is  the  father  of  Lallura  Chinzah, who  is an Executive  Member  of   Lai  Autonomous  District  Council(LADC). He is presently an Adviser of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee. He belonged to LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran) church. He is at present   occasionally   involved in politics.

 

3.          Biakchhunga Chinzah (60) S/o Zaluaia of Lawngtlai Council Veng. He belonged to LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran) Church. He is also the   Secretary of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee.

 

4.          B.Lalhuma (56) S/o B.Sangchema (L) of Lawngtlai –III. His Educational Qualification is PU (Pre- University level). He  is  an  Ex- MDC( Member  of  District  Council)  of  LADC. He  belonged  to  LIKBK(Lairam  Isua  Krista  Baptist  Kohhran).He  is  still actively   involved  in  politics  but  does  not  belong  to  any  political  party  at  present.

 

5.          T.T Nohmung (67) S/o Sanaka(L) of  Lawngtlai  Council  Veng. His Educational Qualification is Bachelor of Arts. He is an Ex- MDC (Member of District Council) of LADC. 6.          A.T Hlawnchhing (65) S/o Hnekhara (L) of Electric Veng, Lawngtlai. His Educational Qualification is BA. He belonged to UPC (NEI).

 

7.          Rev. E.F Sangchem (72) S/o Hniarkunga (L) of Lawngtlai –IV. His Educational Qualification is BD (Bachelor of Divinity). He  is  a  retired  pastor  under  LIKBK(Lairam  Isua  Krista  Baptist  Kohhran).

 

8.          B.Malsawma (55)S/o Thanmanga (L) of Council  Veng, Lawngtlai. His Educational Qualification is Cl-IX. He  belonged  to LIKBK(Lairam  Isua  Krista  Baptist  Kohhran)  and  is  also  involved  in  politics.

 

9.          F.Sangkima (36) S/o Biaktluanga of Council Veng, Lawngtlai. His Educational Qualification is   upto Cl-IX. He belonged to LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran). He is also involved in politics.

 

10.        Taichunga (65) S/o Alduma(L) of  Rulkual  Village, Lawngtlai  District. Educational Qualification is PU. He  belonged  to  LIKBK(Lairam  Isua  Krista  Baptist  Kohhran).He is a  retired  Primary  School  Head  Teacher  and  is  occasionally  involved   in  politics  at   present.

 

11.        H.Remmawia (56) S/o Hranglina, Council Veng, Lawngtlai. President of Mizoram Peoples’ Conference Party,Lawngtlai. His Educational Qualification  is PU  and  he  is  an  Ex-  VCP  of  Council  Veng  Lawngtlai  and  is  still  actively  involved  in  politics  today  besides  running  business (Owned  a  shoe  store  at  Lawngtlai  Bazar).  He is also at present President of Merchant Association, Lawngtlai District. He belonged to BCM (Baptist Church of Mizoram).

 

12.        Biakchungnunga (55) S/o Mawnglinga (L) of Thingkah Village. His  wife  is   Hranghlunchhungi, the  present  General  secretary  of  Lai  Women  Association(LWA). He  is  involved  in   politics  but  does  not affiliate  himself  to  any  particular  political   party. 13.        N.Lalzova (42) S/o. N.Lalmuana of Lawngtlai –IV. He  is extensively  involved  in  NGOs  activities  as  well  as  in  politics.              As  mentioned  above  majority  of   the  members  of  Phawngpui  UT  Demand  Committee  belonged  to   Lairam  Isua  Krista  Baptist  Kohhran (LIKBK),  they  are   fully  involved  in   preparation  works  for   organization   of   LIKBK Assembly  which  is  slated  to  be  held  from  25.3.15  onwards  in  Lawngtlai  town. The  UT  Demand  Committee  have  not   launch   any  initiative  with  regards   to  achieving  their  goal. A  joint  meeting  is  expected   to  be  convened   by  Phawngpui  UT  Demand  Committee, comprising  members  from  Chakma  Autonomous  District  Council  and  Mara  Autonomous  District  Council  areas  in  the  near  future  in  which   they  will   elect   the  office  bearers  for   the  said  UT  Demand  Committee. Information  received  also  stated  that  the extent  of   the  demand  area    will  cover  the  jurisdiction  of  Lai  Autonomous  District  Council, Mara  Autonomous  District  Council  and  Chakma  Autonomous  District  Council.

 

             It  is  also  important  to  note   that   the  above  mentioned  persons who are  involved  in  politics   have  no  permanent  allegiance  to  any  political  party. They are all susceptible to changing their stance depending on the situation in the political scenarios of Mizoram. The situation is closely watched.
