ASSESSMENT  FOR  AMC ELECTION 2010

 

WARD NO. 1 & WARD NO. 11

 

WARD NO.1

 

            Ward No. 1-ah hian boruak inlumlet neuh neuh a awm rualin liantham taka party-a lut leh chhuak chu an awm lem lo ni in alang. Hetiang hi vawiin thleng a hmuhdan chu a ni -

 

1) Selesih-ah hian Congres + ZNP party an kang hret zel a, vote 50-60 vel bawr a an chak theih a rinawm.

 

2) Durtlang North- ah hian MNF + MPC party chuan dinhmun an sawhnghet thawkhat ni in alang a, vote 100-120 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

3) Durtlang- ah hian, party hmasawn an awm vak lo a, MNF + MPC party chu vote 250-300 vela an chak theih a rinawm.

 

4) Durtlang Leitan-ah hian MNF + MPC party- an kang hret hret ni in alang a vote 30-40 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

5) Zuangtui-ah hian boruak a inthlak vak lemlo a, MNF + MPC party dinhmun atha hret ni in alang a, vote  20-30 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

6) Muanna Veng-ah hian tualchhhungah chuan Congress + ZNP party an tha hle a vote tla 70%-75% vel an hui theih hmel. Muanna Veng V.C member Independence ticket-a thlan tlin chu karhmasa khan Congress party-ah a lut bawk. Hetih lai hian vote hlawm 70-80- vel awmna TNT Camp zuangtuiin an awn tur hi hriattheih ala ni lo a, an awn lam turte tan erawh chuan nasa taka hmasawnna tur ni in alang. Tun dinhmunah chuan MNF+MPC party-hi an awn duhhmel ber ni in a hriat. TNT lam hian MNF+MPC party-hi awn tura chhut a ni a, chuti chung chuan Congress + ZNP party hi vote 10-15 vel a chak turah ngaih a ni.

WARD NO. 11

 

            He ward a tun thlenga boruak inlumlet dan tlangpui chu hetiang hi

a ni e -

 

1) Luangmual-ah hian an party lian thawkho pahnihte hi intluktlang tawk viau turah ngaiha ni a, 50-50 vel an chan theih a rinawm laiin MNF+MPC candidate hi veng chhung mi a nihna angina a tling tawk tawk thei turah ngaih a ni.

 

2) Chawlhmun-ah hian boruak inthlak vak a awm lo a Congress + ZNP party-hi vote 80-90 vel a an chak rin a ni.

 

3) Tanhril ah hian Congress + ZNP party an kang hret hret zel ni in alang a, vote 250-300 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

4) Sakawrtuichhun-ah hian MNF+MPC party in chhunglamah  hma an sawn hle ni in alang a, MNF+MPC party-hi vote 80-90 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

5) Rangvamual-ah hian boruak inthlak a awm lem lo. Congress + ZNP party vote 70-80 vela an chak a rinawm.

 

6) Phunchawng-ah pawh hian tun thleng chuan Congress party dinhmun a tha zawk ni in a lang.

 

7) PTC Lungverh-ah hian vote 10-20 vela Congress-an chak rin a ni.

 

8) Tuivamit-ah hian boruak inthlak hretin Congress + ZNP party-in hma an sawn hret a, Congress + ZNP party-hian vote 30-40 vela an chak rin a ni.
