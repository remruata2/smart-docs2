****BRIEF UPDATES ON CALL RELATED INFORMATION (CRI)****

****IN C/W ABDUCTION of 3(THREE) CIVILIANS VIDE WEST PHAILENG P/S CASE NO.24/2013 Dt.25.11.2013 u/s 364 A/379/34 IPC R/w Sec 25(1A) Arms Act.****

Date and time of incident: - **23.11.2013 MORNING @ 7:00 AM – 7:30 AM.**

Place: Old Chikha, Mamit District.

**Dt. 04.12.2013**

 Unknown Abductors had first contacted Vijay, Supervisor, Telecom Network Services (TNS) at around 02:36pm using mobile phone no.8729867055 (Airtel N/E) and demanded Rs.5 crores ransom for the release of hostages.

The mobile phone used by the abducted was checked/verified and found that it was the one used by one of the hostages named Lalzamliana of Zuangtui, 207 pickup driver.

L.I. request accordingly sent to the concerned Service Provider i.e. Airtel N/E.

**Dt. 05.12.2013**

 At around 10:55am Vijay called the number which was answered by the Abductor ( identified through voice recognition as Lianphunga, S/S Comdr of BNDLFM) requesting him to give proper care and not to hurt the hostages. He also told them that he is trying to manage the demanded amount by consulting his superiors. Vijay also talked with Deep Mandal. While talking he also put the call through conference to his families in W. Bengal.

 The Abductors also contacted relatives of the Drivers and let the hostages talk with them.****

**Dt. 06.12.2013**

 Abductor (Lianphunga) told Vijay that he would not be available for some time and had given charge to his assistant Duhawma saying that any further negotiation should be made with him.

**Dt. 10.13.2013**

 Duhawma had made a querry to Vijay about the demanded ransom. However, Vijay had told Duhawma that the demand is too huge and may not be able to manage that amount.

**Dt. 13.12.2013**

  Abductors had made contact to relatives of Sanglianthanga alias Maliana, Taxi driver requesting contact number of various news correspondent.

**Dt. 16 .12.2013**

 Abductors had contacted relatives of Maliana asking them to provide contact numbers of various news correspondents.

**Dt. 19.12.2013**

****Abductor again contacted relatives of Maliana to ask numbers of news correspondent, however they had stated various news correspondent numbers are already sent to them through SMS.

 The relatives of the hostages also told abductors that they had tried every effort for their demand and decided to submit memorandum to the Chief Minister of Mizoram in this regard.

**Dt. 20.12.2013**

 Abductor had made brief contact to Vijay, however Vijay had stated that with much difficulty he had manage Rs 2 lakhs of which one lakhs from his own pocket and another one lakhs through Bank loan. And the Abductor had immediately switch-off the mobile.

**Dt. 22.12.2013**

****A request was made to Duhawma(Commander) by Biakmawia ( Bru NGO leader) to release the 2(two) mizo hostages before Christmas. However, no clear cut decision is made in this regard.

**Dt. 23.12.2013**

****Abductor again contacted Vijay who stated that he could hardly manage another 50 thousands by selling his motor cycle in addition to 2 lakhs which he could managed earlier. The abductor still stick their demand of Rs 5 crores. They had extended their deadline till 15 january 2014 to collect/manage the said amount.

**Dt. 26.12.2013**

******Wife of Maliana contacted the Kidnapper: The kidnapper state that they will not release the hostages alive if the ransom demand is not paid within the stipulated time.**

 Kidnappers also contacted Vijay who stated that he could hardly managed 3.5 lakhs till date, however the kidnapper still stands firm on the demand of Rs 5 crores and extended their deadline till 15 January 2014

**Dt. 02.01.2014**

****Lianzama’s Uncle T.Zathanga of kolasib contacted kidnapper requesting to release the hostages on the ground of their health. Kidnapper stated that they were unhappy about the step taken by government making arrangement with other outfit groups to force them to release the hostages.****

**Dt. 04.01.2014**

****Kidnappers had contacted T. Zathanga: Kidnapper blamed the government for not taking any action in their favour and intimated the relative not to consult the government to secure their release, but to arrange necessary amount from their own. However, there is a slight argument on their conversation as T.Zathanga was unhappy or not willing to arrange any amount. Conversation ended without making any decision.

 Kidnapper again contacted Vijay who stated that he could now arranged 4 lakhs, kidnappers were still unsatisfied with this amount and argued to him to managed more by consulting families of Deep Mandal. However, Vijay stated families of Deep Mandal may not be in a condition to lease out any money as they are poor and both his sister and mother are traumatised and were hospitalised.

**Dt. 10.01.2014**

 One of the hostage Maliana had contacted his relatives and stated that the kidnappers were not happy with the steps taken by the government as they had not contacted their commander till date who is in-charge of the kidnapping and as adverse action had been taken in negotiating with NLFT. Maliana had requested his relative to consult government for releasing aid in respect of the hostages relatives but to later transfer to the kidnappers so as it may appears that the government did not pay any money for their release.

 Kidnappers also contacted relatives of Maliana and make a query on what steps they had been taken. They had stated they also had contacted leaders of Bru community and may able to arrange some amount of money collected from village council leaders of Bru dominated villages. The kidnappers seem relied on this.

 Kidnappers also contacted Vijay who stated with all efforts he could now hardly manage to arrange 5 lakhs from all the sources he could collect with no other means. The kidnappers seems satisfied, but however stated that as the hostages will be release at one go he intimated him to consult relatives of other hostages.
