SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/755

Dated: 5th October, 2019.

 

            Shri. Amit Shah, Hon’ble Union Home Minister visited Aizawl today, i.e. 5.10.2019 and he inaugurated the North East Handloom & Handicraft Exhibition, 2019 at Govt. Mizo High School Field, Zarkawt, Aizawl. After the programme, the Hon’ble Union Home Minister held a meeting with Hon’ble Chief Minister, NGOCC and BJP, Mizoram Pradesh at Raj Bhavan, Aizawl.

 

            Leaders of NGOCC led by Pu Vanlalruata, Chairman and other members, viz.
Pu Lalhmachhuana, Gen. Secretary., CYMA; Pu L. Ramdinliana Renthlei, President, MZP;
Pu Lalhuapzauva, President, MUP; Pi Lalthlamuani, President, MHIP; Pu Vanlalliana Khiangte, President, MSU and Pu R. Lalngheta, Vice President, CYMA met the Hon’ble Union Home Minister and raised their demands and concern on the issue of CAB and Brus. Consequently, NGOCC issued a Press Release to this effect. In the Press Release, NGOCC stated that the Union Home Minister promised them to make exemption in the proposed CAB for Mizoram state and to incorporate ILP in the CAB as a proviso. The Union Home Minister also assured them to preview the draft CAB with NGOCC before laying it in the Parliament.

 

            The Union Home Minister guaranteed to appoint Interlocutor in order to solve Bru issues at the earliest. He further assured to take initiative for raising of 1 (one) CRPF Mizo Battalion for deployment at the state boundary.

 

            Information received indicated that the Hon’ble Union Home Minister highlighted 1971 as the cut-off year for Mizoram for enforcement of CAB while 2014 will be the cut-off year for other states.

 

            The visit of the Hon’ble Union Home Minister was received by Mizo Hnam Inkhawmpui Committee, PRISM and MPC by displaying placards condemning and opposing CAB at Treasury Square area. Around 70 (seventy) Mizo Hnam Inkhawmpui Committee members and 40 (forty) PRISM & MPC members participated.

 

            BJP, Mizoram Pradesh also submitted a Memorandum to the Hon’ble Union Home Minister highlighting the following points :-

  1. Constitution of a Boundary Commission.
  2. Establishment of Regional AIIMS in Mizoram.
  3. Upgradation of Lengpui Airport to international airport.
  4. Expediting implementation of Kaladan Hydro Power Project (700 MW).
  5. Road connectivity :
    1. Upgradation of NH connecting Aizawl to Silchar by 4 lane.
    2. Construction of road connectivity between Lawngtlai and Dhaka, Bangladesh.
    3. Completion of Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP).

The Memorandum, inter alia, implored the Union Home Minister to exclude/exempt Mizoram from CAB-16 and the 9th Batch repatriation must be the last phase of repatriation.

            The visit of the Hon’ble Union Home Minister went off smoothly without any untoward incident and the Union Home Minister departed from Lengpui Airport at 3:45 p.m today.
