****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/739

Dated 21st December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/737 Dt. 18.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 5 (five) vehicles (HMV –Nil, MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via., Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 21.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 18 (eighteen) vehicle (HMV – Nil, MMV – 15, LMV-3) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
