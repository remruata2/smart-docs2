SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/207

Dated: 25th March, 2019.

                       

 

            Information received indicated that on 22.03.2019 Land Owners’ Association Sub-Committee, Rulkual village, Lawngtlai District issued a stay order to RDS Project Ltd, one of the contractors of the KMMTTP at Lawngtlai District, to stop their construction works within the area of Rulkual village w.e.f 22.3.2019. The letter further stated that the stay order was imposed to show their discontentment over the continued pending of payment of land compensation till date. Further information received indicated that leaders of Lawngtlai District Land Owners’ Association (LDLOA) visited the agitators and requested them to stop their agitation by informing about the initiatives they had taken, but in vain. At present, the agitators allowed no vehicles belonging to the contractor, except water tankers and ration supply vehicles, to ply through their area.

            In the meantime, DC Lawngtlai District promulgated Sec 144 of CrPC on 20.03.2019 which prohibited any disturbance or blockage of the ongoing construction of the KMMTTP by any individual or organisation. Information received indicated that the revocation of the order is demanded by land owners.
