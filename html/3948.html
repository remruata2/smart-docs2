Instructions for regulation of Cross Border Movement of People of Border Area across the Indo-Myanmar Boarder (IMB) from the 43 entry/exit points

 

The cross border movement of people residing in the “Border Area” i.e., within 10 kms on each side of the Indo-Myanmar Border, will be regulated as under:

 

1. Movement of people from the 43 designated crossing points will toe granted to holders of “Border Pass” issued by authorised representative of Assam Rifles.
