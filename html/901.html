INPUT ON CLASHES BETWEEN MYANMAR ARMY AND ARAKAN ARMY

 

            Information has been received that clashes between Myanmar Army (Tatmadaw) and the Arakan Army (AA) have escalated in several places along Mizoram-Myanmar border (under Paletwa township in Chin State) during the first & second weeks of November, 2017.

 

            On 9.11.2017, about 80 (eighty) Myanmarese Army led by Maj. Ko Nai Nai attacked Arakan Army (AA) Temporary Camp located near Mizoram-Myanmar border (B.P No.10) inside Myanmar and on 10.11.2017, Myanmar Army troops led by Capt. Nyin Nyin Oo also attacked AA Camp near Varang village, Myanmar which is located near Mizoram-Myanmar border (B.P No.8). As a result of these attacks, it was reported that 9 (nine) Myanmar Army men died and 10 (ten) sustained injuries while 7 (seven) Arakan Army militants died and other 12 (twelve) were wounded. Three of the following wounded Arakan Army (AA) militants were admitted at Christian Hospital, Lawngtlai on 13.11.2017 :-

            ( i)       Thun Thuan (32 yrs) S/o Senta-u of Thayar, Arakan State

            (ii)       Cho Cho (27 yrs) S/o Umong of Pongne, Arakan State

            (iii)     Aung Aung (21 yrs) S/o Mia-U Leihniata, Arakan State

 

            One of the injured person, Thun Thuan was brought to Aizawl on the night of 13.11.2017. He was admitted at Civil Hospital, Aizawl and a criminal case vide Lawngtlai Police Station Case No. 70/17 of Dt.13/11/17 u/s 6(a) PP (EII) Rules, 1950 has been registered against all the three wounded AA militants.

 

            Meanwhile, as many as 380 (three hundred eighty) residents of Varang village (near B.P No.8) Myanmar have recently crossed over to Mizoram and taken refuge at the following villages of Lawngtlai District, Mizoram :-

(1) Laitlang village                            : No. of families - 40, No. of members - 150

(2) Hmawngbuchhuah village           : No. of families - 30, No. of members - 120

(3) Zochachhuah village                   : No. of families - 20, No. of members - 110
