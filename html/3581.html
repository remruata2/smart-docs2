A. Front page of  VANGLAINI/ THE AIZAWL POST newspapers 

Dt. 26.7.2022

MLA,CADC CEM and other leaders convicted: The Special Court (PC Act) Judge, Pu Vanlalenmawia, on 22th  July 2022, based on PC Act 1988, Section 13(1)(d) r/w Section 13 (2) convicted BD Chakma MLA, Buddha Lila Chakma, Chairman Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC), four (4) CADC Executive Members and seven (7) former CADC Executive Members in a Rs.137 Lakhs misappropriation of funds case.

          The thirteen persons were accused of manipulating the centrally sponsored development funds during the year 2013-2014  for their personal benefits i.e as salaries for the then CADC leaders. However, the accused managed to repaid all the amount before investigation of the case was completed. Nevertheless,  the court Judgement issued yesterday i.e 25.7.2022 convicted the thirteen accused with an imprisonment of one year and a fine of Rs 10,000 each as the funds were misappropriated beyond its intended purposes.

          All the accused were also granted bail as they intended to appeal against the Special Court Judgement to Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench.

Dt:  26.7.2022

Meeting on Army recruitment: A mass army recruitment process (Agniveer) is scheduled to be held during 4-9 July 2022 at Rajiv Gandhi Stadium, Aizawl. A meeting in this regard was held under the Chairmanship of Dr. Lalhriatzuali, Ralte,DC Aizawl yesterday i.e 25.7.2022 by senior government officials.
