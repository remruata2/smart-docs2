Sir,

                        With reference to your letter no. cited above, I am furnishing the required information so far available on record under this unit as follows:

 

(a)    Tun hnaiah Myanmar buaina avanga Lawngtlai District-a Refugee rawn lut engzatnge ni tawh?

Ans:                Tun hnaia Myanmar buaina avanga Lawngtlai District-a Refugee rawn lut zat chu an tam ber laiin mi 1750 (sangkhat zasarih sawmnga) an ni.

                        Raltlante hi boruak a muanawm deuh hnuah Myanmar lamah an kir ve leh zeuh zeuh a, dt.5.3.2018 thlenga Mizoram chhunga Myanmar raltlan la awm zat chu mi 557 (zanga sawmnga pasarih) an ni.                       

                       

 

(b)    Sorkarin hengho chhawmdawlna hi a ngaihtuah sak em?

Ans:                Sawrkar hian a theih ang angin chhawmdawlna pe in an mamawh a ngaihtuah sak a, an Riakbuk tur a siam sak a, ei leh in tur te a pe a, an hriselna endiktu tur Doctor te a tir a, tin anmahni vengtu tur security a dah bawk. Tin heng bakah hian Assam Rifles leh NGOs ten an mamawh ang te an theih ang angin an buaipui bawk a ni.

 

 

(c)     Myanmar depah rahpuah bomb avanga chesual a vanduaina tawk mi engzatnge?

Ans:                            Myanmar depah rahpuah bomb avanga chesual a vanduaina tawk mi 2 (pahnih) an awm a, 1 (pakhat) in hliam na tak a tuar a, 1 (pakhat) zawk chuan a nunna hlu tak a chan phah a ni.

 

 

d)      Rahpuah bomb engzatnge sut a nih tawh?

Ans:                            Rahpuah bomb hi 41(sawmli pakhat) sut/tihchhiat tawh a ni.
