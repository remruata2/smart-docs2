SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/58

Dated: 26th January, 2019.

 

 

Reference to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/57, Dt. 25.01.2019: The 70th Republic Day celebration, 2019 was held peacefully today, i.e. 26.1.2019 @ 8:00 a.m at AR Lammual. The Hon’ble Governor of Mizoram, Pu Kummanam Rajasekharan unfurled the National Flag at 8:00 a.m as scheduled. The ceremonial parade was concluded without any untoward incident despite the strong demonstration by the NGOCC in their boycott of the celebration in protest against the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, 2016. Dignitaries and Government officials who were listed to attend the function by the meeting between the Hon’ble Chief Minister and NGOCC attended the programme.

The protestors, estimated to be around 250, gathered outside the 3 (three) entries of AR Lammual preventing civilians, students and others not listed in the discussion between the Government and the NGOCC held yesterday, i.e. 25.12019 from entering the venue.

At other places in Mizoram, Republic Day celebration programmes were also carried out peacefully and no untoward report received.

 

DGP (M)
