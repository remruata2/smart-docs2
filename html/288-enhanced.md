**1. Pu K.Lianzuala, ex MLA and Chairman, MKVI Board :**

****

Pu K. Lianzuala is the incumbent Chairman of Mizoram Khadi Village Industry Board, whose function is to select beneficiaries of loans/funds under MKVI. In performance of his function, Pu K. Lianzuala said that he sometime faced disgruntled persons who are rejected from receiving loans/funds from MKVI Board. However, this cannot be treated as threat perception. Being the Chairman of MKVI Board and prominent politician, he have to travel different part of the state for official and political purposes, wherein he often took his PSO along with him. He also claimed that his designation, i.e. Chairman of MKVI Board is equivalent to Minister of State which entitled him a PSO.

On careful observation, it was found that Pu K. Lianzuala purpose of demanding PSO is not due to security point of view. Therefore, the assessment found that the requirement of PSO by Pu K. Lianzuala is minimal and out of the obligation of PSO and there is not imminent threat against Pu K. Lianzuala. 

**2. Pu Lalduhoma, President, ZNP :**

****Pu Lalduhoma, Ex MLA and President of ZNP has been provided one PSO since 2015 consequence upon his request due to the alleged intimidation received from undesirable elements. During the assessment, it was learnt that Pu Lalduhoma had no more receive threat from any person. However, his justification for allotment of PSO is that he often visited his farm at Sihhmui and Sihhmui area is prone to arms smugglers and insurgents as a hide-out (arrest of insurgents and seizure of arms were done in in the vicinity earlier). He also said that he have to attend party programmes in Aizawl and throughout Mizoram where there is possibility to face anti-social elements.

 After careful observation, the finding of the assessment is that as of now, there is no particular threat on the security of Pu Lalduhoma which necessitated him to provide PSO.

****4. Pu Liansangzuala, Special Judge, ND&PS:****

Pu Liansangzuala, the incumbent Special Judge of ND&amp;PS has been provided PSO in consideration of his nature of work, which includes conviction of Drugs dealers and smugglers who involved in cases under ND&amp;PS Act. Pu Liansangzuala had revealed that due to his nature of work, i.e. disposing of cases involving drugs dealers and traffickers, sometime he felt a sense of uneasiness and discomfort towards his safety and that of his family. Since cases under ND&amp;PS Act have been increasing, he is likely vulnerable towards conspiracy and attacks by drugs trafficking cartels.

Due to the above circumstances and the nature of his work and the possibility of a security breach and in order to enable him to pass fair judgment without fear, it is most suitable that he may be provided PSO.

**5. Pu Laldinpuia Tlau, CJM, Lawngtlai:**

Pu Laldinpuia Tlau is the CJM of Lawngtlai District and he is provided PSO as per his request. He stated that he is worried about his safety due to his nature of work which involves disposal of criminal cases and granting/rejecting of bail plea, etc. Pu Laldinpuia Tlau also claimed that he is posted in an area where community and locality feeling are deeply rooted in the minds of the people.

However, on careful assessment of threat perception, it was observed that Pu Laldinpuia Tlau does not receive any specific threat which can obstruct his normal duty. Therefore, due to absence of imminent threat against him, it was observed that Pu Laldinpuia Tlau does not require PSO.
