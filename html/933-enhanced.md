****CHRONOLOGY OF EVENTS IN C/W ABDUCTION AT RAJIVNAGAR****

****ON****5.6.2013 @ AROUND 10:00 AM****

****(Final Report)****

**Dt. 05.06.2013**

 On 5.6.2013 at around 10:00 AM, three (3) persons of Gautam Construction Company workers were kidnapped by militants suspected to be a combined group of NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) and BNDLFM (Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from the village of Bodalsuri (4Km from Rajiv Nagar), Mamit District. Sources said that the abductors are around 8 members.

Date and time of incident:- **05.06.2013@9-10pm**

Name of person abducted:-

1. Nizam Uddin(28) of Karimganj(Labour supervisot)
2. Mitun Bagani(18) of silchar (Labourer)
3. Dairgudon Chakma(30) @Bijoy of Rajiv nagar(Driver)

A team of CID(SB) led by Pu Lalrinpuia Varte DySP rushed to Tuipuibari on the same day @10:00pm and arrived at the P/O i.e. Tuipuibari II (Rajiv Nagar) the next day i.e. 06.06.2013 @ 07:00 am

**Dt. 06.06.2013**

 Sandeep Bhagaria, Project I/c, Gautam Construction Company had reported from Silchar that the kidnappers had contacted Sanjoy Chakma (Driver) of Tuipuibari, demanding ` 25 lakhs ransom. The Caller also instructed him to make further contact to mobile phone +88923057426635.

 Analysis of Sanjoy Chakma’s number indicated that the call was made from +923327765239 at 09:26am on 06.06.2013.

**Dt. 07.06.2013**

 The abductor using Nizam Uddin’s number (8731027322) had contacted Sandeep at 09:29am. The Caller who spoke in Bengali, later identified from his voice as S/S Lt. Parimol Debbarma had demanded ransom of ` 1 crore to be paid immediately and threatened to kill the hostages. The hostages were also made to talk with Sandeep in which the hostages reported of being torture and not being given proper food. 

**Dt. 09.06.2013**

 S/S Lt. Parimol Debbarma contacted Sandeep Bhagaria at 09:31 am urging him to pay the ransom i.e. ` 1 crore immediately. The Caller spoke in Bengali.

**Dt. 10.06.2013**

 Sandeep had received a call from a Vodafone number (8794876741) @05:42 am and 08:49 am. The Caller identified himself as ‘NLFT’ and demanded immediate payment of ` 1 crore. The Caller spoke in Hindi.

**Dt. 19.06.2013**

  A report has been received from Sandeep Bhagaria that Abductors called Nizam’s brother this morning @09.30am. Caller insisted to talk to the Director of the Company and hinted readiness to negotiate with ` 60 lakhs.

**Dt. 21.06.2013**

 Surveillance has been mounted on T. Malsawma by a team of CID (SB). Places visited and places of stay of the target recorded and photographs taken. The target left Aizawl on Dt. 22.06.2013 and returned to Refugee Camp.

**Dt. 25.06.2013**

****A team of CID (SB) has been sent this morning to locate Sanjoy Chakma who had returned home from Tripura.

**Dt. 26.06.2013**

** Sanjoy Chakma and Marisa both of Rajivnagar were picked up by SB Team and brought to Aizawl. Interrogation was done at CID (SB) Office. Sanjoy admitted having called back a missed call which he received on 06.06.2013 which was a Bangladesh SIM **923327765239** which was earlier thought to be a Pakistani number. Sanjoy corroborated earlier report that the abductors made their first ransom demand of ` 25 lakhs and also instructed him to make further contact to Mobile Number **88923057426635.**They were released on Dt. 28.06.2013 as their involvement in the case could not be established during the interrogation.**

**Dt. 26.06.2013**

 Abductors contacted Sandeep Bhagaria using another SIM 8974344839 (Airtel NE) in which they have reduced their ransom demand to ` 40 lakhs. Sandeep told them that he would convey the same to his Director. Sandeep stated that the Company is not in a position to pay such an amount. The caller spoke in Bengali.

**Dt. 27 & 28.06.2013**

 The abductors again contacted Sandeep Bhagaria, urging them to pay up the ransom immediately and threatened to kill the hostages. Sandeep Bhagaria stated that they were not in a position to pay such a huge amount. The caller this time spoke in English. There is no contact thereafter.

**Dt. 19.07.2013**

 According to input received, families of two(2) of the kidnapped persons of Gautam Construction Pvt. Ltd. namely (1) Nizam Uddin and (2) Mitum Bagani are negotiating with the NLFT for payment of ransom today at an undisclosed location at Kanchanpur, North Tripura through an unidentified middleman. The amount of ransom to be paid though not immediately known appeared to have been realized by families of the two Bengali captives after selling off their properties.

In the meantime, another input received indicated that Gautam Construction Pvt. Ltd. still refused to pay the abductors demand of ` 40 lakhs.

**Dt. 05.08.2013**

 Input received indicated that the NLFT has set a deadline till 07.08.2013 for payment of ` 40 lakhs ransom and threatened to kill the hostages if the ransom is not paid by Gautam Construction Private Ltd. within the given period. While maintaining its stand on ransom demand, the NLFT however, hinted on payment of ` 30 lakhs for now, and remaining ` 10 lakhs to be paid later.

 The negotiator for Gautam Construction Private Ltd. (name not known) had offered ` 15 lakhs to the kidnappers this morning @ 10:08am with a plea that the company is not in a position to pay more than their proposed amount. However, it is learnt that the negotiation has ended abruptly during their discussion as the NLFT leaders rejected lesser amount offered by the company and stick to their demands of ` 40 lakhs.

**Dt. 07.08.2013**

 Regarding the NLFT deadline for payment of ransom, unconfirmed information received stated that, the Parbatya Chattagram Jana Samhat Samiti (PCJSS), which is a faction of Chakma Santi Bahini Group, has sent a message to the abductors requesting them not to kill the hostages. It may be recalled that the NLFT had rejected the company's offer for payment of `15 lakhs refusing further negotiation.

**Dt. 25.08.2013**

 One Sanjoy Chakma Driver, Gautam Construction Company, Rajiv Nagar who was abducted by NLFT (Parimal faction) on 05.06.2013 from Rajiv Nagar was released on the night of 24.6.2013 from Udalsury village near Bangla-India border and reached BSF Camp, Rajiv Nagar safely on 25.6.2013 @ 11:00 am. Debriefing report from the freed hostage revealed that the other two hostages being held hostage are expected to be released in the next few days.

 Unconfirmed report received stated that the family of the hostages and Gautam Construction Company, after prolonged negotiation paid some amount of money as ransom for the safe release of the hostage.

**Dt. 25.08.2013**

 Unconfirmed information received stated that the abductors had conveyed message through JSS (Jana Songa Samiti) that the other two captives would be released on payment of Rs. 25 lakh. It is learnt that the abductors are sticking to their demand of Rs. 40 lakh out of which 15 had been paid demanding payment of the remaining Rs. 25 lakh.

**Dt. 10.11.2013**

Two abducted labours of Gautam Construction Company who were abducted on 5th June, 2013 near Indo-Bangladesh Border of Rajiv Nagar, Mamit District, Mizoram by National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) militants were released today. The two hostages namely Mr. Nizam Uddin and Mr. Mitun Bagani reached Border Security Force (BSF) Camp, Rajiv Nagar, Mizoram safely on 10.11.2013 at around 3:00 PM. Further report received stated that the family of the hostages after prolong negotiation paid Rs.3 lakhs and seven mobile handsets as a ransom for safe release of the hostages. The two captives were handed over to Mizoram Police by BSF personnel at West Phaileng Police Station on 11.11.2013.
