A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, MIZO AW, THE ZOZAM TIMES AND VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 12.6.2013

Condolence meeting held: Chief Minister Pu Lalthanhawla today expressed deep sympathy to the relatives of the 17 persons who died in Laipuitlang building collapse on 11.5.2013 during a special condolence meeting held at the site today. CYMA president T.Sangkunga who spoke on the occasion disclosed that the aids received by the CYMA for the victims have came to Rs.82.06 lakhs.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.6.2013

Around 27 insurgent camps inside Bangladesh opposite Dampa Tiger Reserve: Mizoram State Board for Wildlife held a meeting yesterday at CMO conference room presided over by Pu Lal Thanhawla, Chief Minister and the Board Chairman. It was mentioned during the meeting that there are around 27 nos. of insurgents camp inside Bangladesh opposite to the Dampa Tiger Reserve. This was mentioned by the BSF officials who further stated that they had made a proposal for creating seven new Border Out Post(BOP) and a patrolling road.

 

Note: All local newspapers subscribed by this unit have highlighted the Green Mizoram Day observed on 11.6.2013 in their headlines. The Green Mizoram Day was observed in all districts, Mizoram.
