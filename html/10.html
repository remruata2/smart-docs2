COLLECTION OF INTELLIGENCE

    Hei hi CID (SB) hnapui ber a ni tiin sawi ta ila dawt kan sawi awm love. Sorkar rorelna tha nei tur chuan CID (SB) hnathawktute hian mawhphurhna kan nei sang hle a, kan hna kan thawh that a kan efficient viau chuan sorkar rorelna tha kan siam thei a ni. Collection of intelligence hi a mozo tawngin a nihna tak sawifiah har deuh mahse a tum ber nia lang chu information (chanchin) Police emaw intelligence department tena kan hriattur tul lakkhawm ti ila a sual tampui lovang chu. Chutiang taka kan hna te thawk puitling thei tura tul tlangpui te chu:-

1. Attention (Rilru/Ngaihtuahna) pek:-

    Hei hi Police hna eng pawh CID hna a ni emaw, a nilo emaw hna thawhna a rilru/ngaihtuahna pek hi hna pawimawh hmasa ber a ni a. Kan thil tihtur leh mawhphurhna hlenchhuak tur chuan rilru ngaihtuahna pek a, phur taka hna kan thawh hian kan tum kan hlenchhuak thin a ni.

2. Efficiency/Sincerity:-

    Taihmakna leh tih takna tello chuan hna a thawh theih loh. Officer tha leh mi rintlak ni tur chuan mi sincere leh efficient mi a ni tur a ni a. Tin, a thil tih reng reng chu a la zam tur a ni lova, chik takin a hnathawh tur chu a thawk tur a ni. Hei hi Special Branch ten a kan tih dan tur, kan thil tihna reng reng lanchhuah tir tur a ni.

3. Integrity:-

    Hei hian dikna, rinawmna leh takna te pawh a kawk thei ang. Kan hnathawhna reng rengah rinawmna hmangin hna kan thawk tur ani a. Pawisa lam chauh ni lovin kan hun hmanah te, kan hnathawhna engkimah kan rinawm tur a ni. Kan duty/assignment kan neih reng rengin kan tih turte rinawm takin kan hlen chhuak tur a ni a, kan hotute hmuh phak lova kan awmin he kan hnathawhah hian danglamna kan nei tur a ni lova, eintlak leh dik takin kan thawk zawk zel tur a ni.

4. Engagement of contact:-

    Police hna reng reng hi Intelligence Department kan ni emaw mahni chauhin hna tha a thawh theih lova, midang/thildang puihna kan mamawh em em a ni. Chutiang tur chuan Public Support neih te alo ngai a, chutiang hlenchhuak tur chuan thian/hmelhriat tha siam tam a pawimawh. Material/documents atang ringawt hian kan thil hriat duhte kan hriat kim theih loh avangin mi contact kan neih that a ngaia milian leh mite ber thlengin thian/hmelhriat tha kan nei thei tur a ni. 
eg. :-     i)     Chanchinbu sem
    ii)     Lumet
    iii)     Dawrnghak
    iv)     Tuisem/Tuipu
    v)     Bawnghnute sem
    vi)     Driver
    vii)     Bazara thu
    viii)     Sumdawng/ Zinmi
    ix)     Loneitu leh
    x)     Ram vak mi leh a dangte.
    A chunga kan han tarlan takte hi kimlo hle mahse a tlangpui thuin hna hniam ang deuha ngaihte an ni a. Amaherawhchu kan hnathawhnaah hian hna sangtak tak thawk ho aiin an tangkai thei zawk a ni. A chhan chu misual, mi awm tha duhlote hian an ni ho hi an nel zawkin an hmu thei deuh zawk a ni.
5. Endurance:-

    Hei hian taihmakna te, tuarchhelna leh teirei peihnate pawh a kawk thei ang chu. Thahnemngaia thil hriat tuma taima tak leh tuarchhel taka kan hna kan thawh talh talh chuan kan hna thawh turah hlawhtlinna min pe thin a ni.

6. Correct and Timely Intelligence:-

    A hun taka information dik leh rintlak thuneitute hnena thlen hi kan tihtur pawimawh tak a ni. Chu chuan kan hmalak dan turah hun min siamsakin thil tihtur tul leh pawimawhte pawh min tihtir hman thin a ni. eg. :- Law & Order problem lo awm ta sela heng a hnuaia mite hi kan hriat ngei ngei tur a ni:-
    i)     Persons involved
    ii)     Group involved
    iii)     Issues involved
    iv)     Tampers
    v)     Leaders
    vi)    Differences
    vii)     Financial Support
    viii)     Their preparation
    ix)     Plans and
    x)     Routes etc.

7. Advance and Adequate Intelligence:-

    A chunga kan sawi tak nen hian danglamna lutuk a awm awm lova. A theih chin chinah chuan kan thil report tur (information) a hma thei ang berin kan nei thei tur a ni. Tin, heng hna hi CID (SB) hna tih tur diktak leh pawimawh tak ani a. Kan information dawn chu a kimchang tur a niin hmaih kan nei tur a ni lo. Thil ho leh te reuhte anga kan ngaih zawng zawng pawh hmaih nei hauh lovin kan hre tur a ni. Tin, kan hriat reng atana tha chu media-ho aiin engkim kan hre hmasain kan nei hmasa tur a ni. Hei hi CID me leh sa te chuan principle-ah kan nei tur a ni.

8. Un-biased mind:-

    Information kan ngaihchan laiin emaw assessment kan siam laiin kan rilruin tum dan bik, rindan leh duhdan bik kan nei tur a ni lova. Kan thil hmuh dan dik tak leh hriat dan dik tak kha kan thil report dan tur chu a ni ang a, awn lam neiin hna kan thawk tur a ni lo.Kan thil chhui dan leh hmuh dan khan a nihna tak a pawchhuak thei tur a ni. Report diklo emaw assessment diklo emaw hotute hnenah kan siam palh a nih chuan thil thalo lian tham zawk a siam thei tih hriat reng tur a ni.

9. Liason with neighbouring state CID /District/PS:-

    Kan thenawm state CID te nen inhmelhriatna tha leh thawhhona tha hian kan hnaah nasa takin min pui thei a ni. Tin, keimahni state chhungah pawh Unit danga kan thawhpuite, Police Stationa kanthawhpuite nena thawhhona tha kan neih hian kan hna nasa takin min tanpui thei.

10. Checking of Crime Records:-

    Crime Records hi a bikin Police Station-ah te pawh maintain a ni a. Tin, Unit dang CID (Crime) ah te pawh crime records te hi nei tura beisei an ni a, anmahni atang hian a khattawka an records te en a, chhinchhiah a, mahni pawhin update/refresh reng tur a ni. Chu chuan kan hnaah nasa takin min pui thei bawk.

WORKING RELATIONSHIP WITH OTHER AGENCIES

    Pawl dangte nena inkungkaihna tha neih tiin a sawi theih tho ang a, heta pawl dang han tih hian Intelligence Deptt. dang Police Deptt. hnuaia mi emaw Para-Military emaw Army etc. hnuaia mite pawh a huam tel thei ang chu. CID (SB) hna thawhnaah hian public relation (mipuite nena thawhhona) neih a pawimawh ang bawkin pawl dangte nen pawha inkungkaihna leh thawhhona tha neih a pawimawh a, chu chuan kan hna thawhah nasa zawkin min tanpui thei tih hriat reng tur a ni. Mahni chauha hnathawh that a harsat avangin keimahni theihna (capacity) atang pawh hian pawl dangte a theih chin china thian tha a siam athawhhona thaneih atul tak zet ani. Kan hriat theuh angin staff indaih lohna avangte, hmanrua (equipments) neih that theih loh avangte leh kawng hrang hrang avangte pawh a ni thei ang, hmun tinah awmin keimahni pual chauhvin thil kan hriat theih loh avangin pawl dangte hi mamawh chang kan nei thin a ni.


ENQUIRY

    Enquiry leh investigation hi thil thuhmun nilo mahse thil inhnai tak anih avangin a chang chuan han hman pawlh mai te pawh thil awm thei a ni a, thil inang chiah lo a ni tih erawh chu kan hre tur a ni. Thil chhui ngai emaw enfiah ngai emaw complain emaw allegation emaw a lo awmin enquiry tih a ngai fo thin a ni. A case a zirin kan tih turte a in anglo thei mai thei a, amaherawhchu a tlangpui thuah inquiry kan tiha kan hriattur tulte chu:-

1. Examination of Complainant/informant:-

    Complain emaw allegation emaw bawhzui ngai a awmin khawi atanga lo chhuak nge, tunge a complainant leh engvanga complaint/allegation lo awm nge tih hriat a pawimawh a, chumi atan chuan a complainant/informant kan exam laia kan hriat turte chu:-
    i)     Hlauh vang a ni em?
    ii)     Mi tirh vang a ni em?
    iii)     Hrem hlauh vang a ni em?
    iv)     Phuba lak duh vang a ni em?
    v)     Hlawkna um vang a ni em?
    vi)     Thikna vang a ni em?
    vii)     Khua leh tui tha nih duh vang a ni em?
    viii)     In thian that vangte a ni em?

    A chunga kan sawi bak pawh hi thil dang pawh a la awm thei anga, heng hi kan hriat a pawimawh a ni.

2. Management of informant/complainant:-

    A chunga kan han sawi tak engkim kan hriat hnuin kan informant te chu eng ang mi nge an nih an chanchin leh background zawng zawng kan hriat a pawimawha an eizawnnate, an thian thate, an pawisa lakluh dan, Police/CID te an tanpui dana an rilru sukthlek kan hre thei tur a ni a, tin, pawisa duh vangte lo nita sela engtianga pawisa pek tur nge, khawi hmuna inpek tur nge chik taka kan hriat chian a pawimawh, chutiang atang chuan a mizia, a nihnate thenkhat chu a hriat theih ang.

    Information mimal hnen atanga kan dawng a nih chuan kha kan informer kha kan thuhnuaiah kan dah thei tur a ni a, tin, kan duhna hmunah kan ko thei tur a nia chutih rual chuan keini enquiry officer pawhin kan kohna hmun chu a hmun kan thlang uluk tur ani a, a theih chin chinah chuan midangte hriat loh leh hmuh phak lohvah informant te kan ko thin tur a ni. Tin, information kan dawn chu a rintlak em tih te, a thusawi a thlak em tih leh a theih chi chiana kan cross check tur a ni.
3. Inspection of P.O:-

    Hei hi enquiry thenkhatah chuan P.O visit ngailo te pawh a awm thei ang a. Amaherawhchu P.O visit ngei a tul chang a awm thin a, P.O. awm chi a nih chuan a hmunah kala thil engkim chipchiar taka hriat tumin sulhnu chhui theih a awm leh awm loh te rinhlelh zawng trhil enge awm tih hmuh kan tum tur a ni.

4. Collection of physical evidence:-

    A chunga kan sawi tak ang khian a P.O. atangin emaw hmun dang atangin emaw mita kan hmuh theih leh kuta kan lak mai theih thil evidence a awm reng reng chuan chhinchhiah emaw, dahthat vek tur a ni. Amaherawhchu chutiang kan evidence neihte chu dan ang thlapin kan lain kan kawl tha tur a ni.

5. Consultation of crime records:-

    A tul dan a zirin Police Station-ah emaw Unit dang atangin emaw criminal reports en thin ni sela chu chuan kan enquiry tihnaah min pui thei ang.

6. Examination of witness/suspects/accused:-

    Section 156 CrPC in a phal angin Police Officer chuan cognizable case reng reng a chhui thei a, tin, witness emaw, suspect emaw accuse emaw pawh nisela under section 161 CrPC hmangin exam theih an ni. Chuvangin Police Officer chuan tupawh thilsual hmuhchhuahna turah chuan mi tup[awh thu a zawt thiang a ni.
    Verification :-
    
    CID (SB) hna pui chu kan hriat theuh angin intelligence collection hi a ni a. Amaherawhchu kan information hmuh leh hriat reng reng hi a rintlak em tih fiah (verified) thin tur a ni., chutiang kan hna te hlenchhuak thei tur chuan heng mite hi hriat a pawimawh :-
    
    (1) Spot verification :-

    Information kan dawn leh hriat reng reng chu a theih chin chinah chuan a hmunah ngei kalin kan enfiah a, thil a nihna leh dik ngei a ni tih kan finfiah thin tur a ni. Information hi rumours (tlangkam titi) atangin emaw, telephone atang mai maiin thu tlukna kan siam tur a ni lo. Thil ani ngei ani tih a hmun atangin kan finfiah hram thin tur a ni.

    (2) Contact of reliable persons :-
    
    Khaw tinah hian mi rintlak leh mi rintlak lo, mi pawhin an thusawi engahmah an ngaih loh chi mihring hi awm theih ani a. Chuvangin information kan dawn leh kan hriat te hi mi rintlak hnen atangin a theih chin chinah chuan hriat kan tum tur a ni.

    (3) Collection of Evidence :-
    
    Information kan dawn leh kan hriat reng reng hi Evidence (a nemnghettu) a theih chin chinah chuan a awm tur a ni. Kan thil hmuh leh hriatte a dik ngei a ni tih mihring emaw hmanraw dang eng pawh a awm thei apiang atangin kan finfiah hmasa thei tur a ni,

    (4) Examination of witnessess/crime records etc :-
    
    Police hnathawhna reng rengah informant emaw witness emaw tel lovin hna a thawh theih lova, chuvangin verification tih dawn pawhin witness atangin emaw crime records atangin emaw tam tak chu a dik ngei ani tih verified theih a ni. Tin witness kan exam reng rengin uluk tak leh chipchiar takin thil kan zawt thin tur a ni a, an background leh an tunhma nun zawng zawng te pawh kan hre tur a ni. Tin, crime records te chu Police Station-ah emaw Unit dang crime record maintain tute hnen atangin thil tam tak a hriat theih avangin kan hnathawhlai nen inkaihhnawihna a neih chuan heng crime records te pawh hi en thin tur a ni.

Tin, information kan dawn chu a rintlak em tih te, a thusawi a thlak em tih leh a theih chi chiana kan cross check tur a ni.
3. Inspection of P.O:-

    Hei hi enquiry thenkhatah chuan P.O visit ngailo te pawh a awm thei ang a. Amaherawhchu P.O visit ngeai a tul chang a awm thin a, P.O. awm chi a nih chuan a hmunah kala thil engkim chipchiar taka hriat tumin sulhnu chhui theih a awm leh awm loh te rinhlelh zawng trhil enge awm tih hmuh kan tum tur a ni.

4. Collection of physical evidence:-

    A chunga kan sawi tak ang khian a P.O. atangin emaw hmun dang atangin emaw mita kan hmuh theih leh kuta kan lak mai theih thil evidence a awm reng reng chuan chhinchhiah emaw, dahthat vek tur a ni. Amaherawhchu chutiang kan evidence neihte chu dan ang thlapin kan lain kan kawl tha tur a ni.

5. Consultation of crime records:-

    A tul dan a zirin Police Station-ah emaw Unit dang atangin emaw criminal reports en thin ni sela chu chuan kan enquiry tihnaah min pui thei ang.

6. Examination of witness/suspects/accused:-

    Section 156 CrPC in a phal angin Police Officer chuan cognizable case reng reng a chhui thei a, tin, witness emaw, suspect emaw accuse emaw pawh nisela under section 161 CrPC hmangin exam theih an ni. Chuvangin Police Officer chuan tupawh thilsual hmuhchhuahna turah chuan mi tup[awh thu a zawt thiang a ni.
    Verification :-
    
    CID (SB) hna pui chu kan hriat theuh angin intelligence collection hi a ni a. Amaherawhchu kan information hmuh leh hriat reng reng hi a rintlak em tih fiah (verified) thin tur a ni., chutiang kan hna te hlenchhuak thei tur chuan heng mite hi hriat a pawimawh :-
    
    (1) Spot verification :-

    Information kan dawn leh hriat reng reng chu a theih chin chinah chuan a hmunah ngei kalin kan enfiah a, thil a nihna leh dik ngei a ni tih kan finfiah thin tur a ni. Information hi rumours (tlangkam titi) atangin emaw, telephone atang mai maiin thu tlukna kan siam tur a ni lo. Thil ani ngei ani tih a hmun atangin kan finfiah hram thin tur a ni.

    (2) Contact of reliable persons :-
    
    Khaw tinah hian mi rintlak leh mi rintlak lo, mi pawhin an thusawi engahmah an ngaih loh chi mihring hi awm theih ani a. Chuvangin information kan dawn leh kan hriat te hi mi rintlak hnen atangin a theih chin chinah chuan hriat kan tum tur a ni.

    (3) Collection of Evidence :-
    
    Information kan dawn leh kan hriat reng reng hi Evidence (a nemnghettu) a theih chin chinah chuan a awm tur a ni. Kan thil hmuh leh hriatte a dik ngei a ni tih mihring emaw hmanraw dang eng pawh a awm thei apiang atangin kan finfiah hmasa thei tur a ni,

    (4) Examination of witnessess/crime records etc :-
    
    Police hnathawhna reng rengah informant emaw witness emaw tel lovin hna a thawh theih lova, chuvangin verification tih dawn pawhin witness atangin emaw crime records atangin emaw tam tak chu a dik ngei ani tih verified theih a ni. Tin witness kan exam reng rengin uluk tak leh chipchiar takin thil kan zawt thin tur a ni a, an background leh an tunhma nun zawng zawng te pawh kan hre tur a ni. Tin, crime records te chu Police Station-ah emaw Unit dang crime record maintain tute hnen atangin thil tam tak a hriat theih avangin kan hnathawhlai nen inkaihhnawihna a neih chuan heng crime records te pawh hi en thin tur a ni.

 

INTERROGATION:

        Thil thalo (sual) chhuina kawnga thil tisual nia puh, rinhlelh emaw thu hretu(witness) (an thil hriat sawi hreh tak) te hmai chhana thuzawh a, chu an thil hriat in thup tlat te awmze nei leh thiam tak a zawh chhuah kan tum a hi INTERROGATION chu a ni.

INTERVIEW:

        Mi, kan thil hriat duhte tha taka min hrilh duh tute hmai chhana kan kawmna hi INTERVIEW chu a ni.

INTERROGATIONIN A TUM TE: (Aims and Objectives of Interrogation)

1.     Kan thil hriat duh tak kan hriat theih nan.
2.     Kan information hriat ang ang te nemngheh a nih theih nan.
3.     Physical evidence kan neih theih nan.
4.     Thil kal tawh hnu chipchiar taka kan hriat nan.
5.     Thilsual tihna a in hnamhnawih dangte kan hriat theih nan.
6.     Thil sual tive lo te kan thliar theih nan
7.     Local links (tualchhung) leh Foreign (ramdang a a thurualpui) te hriat nan
8.     Sum leh paia tanpuitu an awm leh awm loh hriat nan
9.     Misual ten an thil chin than dan bik (modus operandi) hriat nan
10.     Pawl dang thurualpui an neih leh neih loh hriat nan (in case of terrorists)
11.     Thilsual tih nan eng ang hmanrua te nge an neih hriat nan
12.     An mahni duh ngeia inpuan tir theih nan.

ENVIRONMENT OF INTERROGATION:

1.     Hmun finarial tak ani tur a ni.
2.     Furniture (Bungrua) a tlem thei ang ber ani tur a ni.
3.     Kan thil zawha nen a hnai thei ang berah in thut chilh tur a ni.
4.     Kan in zawh lai nen kan inkarah thil dang engmah a awm tur a ni lo.
5.     Telephone, Fax, etc. a awm tur ani lo.
6.     Pawn lam atanga min be buai thei tu an awm tur ani lo.
7.     A ruk in voice recorder dah tur a ni.
8.     Venhimna thatak hnuaiah Interrogation neih tur a ni (in case of Terrorist).

TYPES OF INTERROGATION:

1.     Ordinary Interrogation
2.     Extra Ordinary Interrogation
3.     Joint Interrogation
QUALITIES OF INTERROGATION:

1.     Patience (dawh theihna)
2.     Confidence (In rin tawkna)
3.     Experience (tawn hriatna)
4.     Determination ( Tum ruhna)
5.     Knowledge (Hriatna)
6.     Histronic Quality
7.      High degree of comon sense
8.     High knowledge of psychology and humna behaviour
9.     Impartiality (Thlei bik neilo)
10.     Pleasant manner

PREPARATION FOR INTERROGATION:

        Interrogation a hlawhtlin nan chuan heng a hnuaia points te hi a tlangpui in hriat a pawimawh.

(A)     1. Interrogator chuan a theih chin chinah a thil zawh tur chinchang a hre tur a ni.

    e.g:-     i) Eng anga sualna nge a nih.
        ii) Loss (Hlauh) te a awm em
        iii) Hmun, a hun leh ni
        iv) Thil sual tihna hmun leh a vel te.
        v) Eng ang in nge thilsual chu a tih anih. i.e. hmanrua hman, luhna leh chhuahna.

    2. Interrogator chuan kan mi zawh a background hriat theih chin zawng zawng a hre tur a ni.

    e.g.:-     i) Age, Education, Marital status, financial and social circumstances and criminal record                if any.
        ii) Physical and mental condition as well addition to alcohol, drugs etc.
        iii) Hostile or co-operative.
        iv) Relationship to victim or crime scene
        v) Possible Motives
        vi) Home environment
        vii) Relegious or fraternal affiliation
        viii) Social attitude in general
        ix) Sexual interest

    3. Interrogator chuan a victim chanchin a hrechiang bawk tur a ni.

    e.g.:-     i) Nature of the harm and details therof.
        ii) Age, sex, marital status, family responsibilities (no. of dependants)
        iii) Social attitude
        iv) Financial and social circumstances
        v) Physical and mental characters.
        vi) Sexual interest etc.

(B)     Dress: Interrogator chuan plain clothes (civil dress) nalh tak leh smart takin a inchei tur a ni.

    Examination of complaint:
    Complainant kan exam dawn chuan a thil report chuchang background leh a thil sawi kha a dik ngei am tih kan hre tur a ni.

    Examination of rape victim:

    i) Rape victim chu Police hmeichhe Officer in exam thei ngei se a tha a ni.
    ii) A chhungte awm lai a exam a tha
    iii) Doctors ten a hliamna te an exam ang
    iv) Hmun fianrial-ah neih tur a ni.

    Interrogation of witness at the scene of crime:

    i) Naupangte hi thudik sawi turin an tha ber.
    ii) Scene of crime hi alo berah karkhat chhung chu enthlak (Surveillance) tur a ni.

    Different types of witness/suspects and their interrogation:
    
    Interrogation in a tumber chu information diktak leh thatak hmuh chhuah a ni a, chutianga information hre tur chuan witnesses chi hrang hrang interrogation an ti thin a, chung witnesses te chu chi hrang hrang an awm thei bawk.

    i) Interested Witness: Hetiang chi witness hian thil an sawi tha duh viau a, sawi that duhna chhan tam tak an nei thei a, amaherawh chu an thusawi zawng zawngte awih nghal vek tur ani lova, finfiah hmasak phawt tur a ni.

    ii) Disinterested Witness: Hengte hian thu an sawi tha duh lo thin a, a chhan chu hlauh tam tak an nei thin. e.g. HUn paih tam, pawisa sen thlawn thil thalo thleng thei an dawn vang leh accused/suspect khawngaih/lainat vang, hetiang witness ho hi a tangkai thei ber an nih avangin ngun taka interrogate tur an ni.

    iii) Simple type of Witness: Heng witness te hi fimkhur taka thil zawh tur an ni. Tihthaih/vau ni lova, dawt hrilh chi pawh an ni lova, direct takin zawhna zawt ila a sawi duh zawk.

    iv) Lying witnessess: Hetiang witness ho hian dawt an sawi thin a, thudik lo an sawina chhan hi hriat chhuah a tha a, hun rei tak dawh thei taka interrogate tur an ni a. Tin, an sawi duh zawng zawng sawitir veka, an mahni thusawi chuan khen let leh tur an ni.

    v) Hostile Witness: Hetiang witness hian accused lam an tan daih thei a, a chhan tamtak a awm thei ang. Hetiang witness ho hi koh (summon) dawn chuan information pe lawk lovin kohthut (surprise) tur an ni.Tin, an mahni in bul hnaiah emaw, thiante, chhungte emaw dan hremi (lawyers) ho bul hnaiah interrogate loh tur an ni.


    vi) Talkative Witnesses: Hetiang witness hian an hriat bak leh duh bak bak an sawi thin, a chhan tamtak a awm thei ang.

    e.g.     a) Khua leh tui thatak ni anga lan duh vang.
        b) An pian ken
        c) Publicity (in thi lar) duh vang
        d) Intelligent (thil hre tak) nih duh vang
        e) Duhsak/puhsak loh (bias) an neih vang
        f) Thudik tak hai lan anih loh nan

    vii) Reluctant Witnesses: Hetiang witness ho hi thil an sawi tha duh lova, chhan hrang hrang a nei thei.

    e.g.    a) Remchang lo /thalo riau a a hriat vang
        b) Hmun hrang hrang kal atan a harsat vang.
        c) Accused/Culprits kut tawrh hlauh vang
        d) Police rin tawk loh vang etc.

        Hetiang ho hi Court in a veng (Protect) thei ani tih hriattira, rin ngam tir an ngai.

    viii) Drumkard: Heng witness ho hi fuihpawrh theih tak an ni a, Interrogator chuan anmahni ah zawhna te phur taka chhang thei tura thiam taka enkawl an ngai, thil hreber leh tha berah an in ngaihna kha tihphur pah a hai chhuah theih a n ni.

    ix) Children Witnesses: Naupangte tamtak hi chhia leh tha pawh hre mang lo, an mahni leh midang tan pawh a thil pawi thei leh pawilo pawh la thliar thiam lo anih avangin an thil hmuh leh hriat ang ang te sawi phawng phawng chi anih avangin an thil sawi a dik duh hle. Chutih rual chuan naupang fing leh dawt sawi hreh lemlo te pawh an awm ve bawk. Hetiang an nih avang hian naupang thilo kan zawh reng rengin, dam tak leh a tlangnel thei ang bera thil zawh tur an ni, reitak zawh tur an ni lova, an mahni thil lo tawn tawh te zawh mai mai an tha.

    x) Female Witnesses: Hmeichhe ho hi a tlangpui thuin an hawk dak a, amaherawhchu an thilhriat te hi an vawng tha reng thei lo tlangpui a, chuvang chuan thil kan zawh reng reng in kan zawhna kha chiangkuang tak leh tha taka zawh an ngai thin. Tin, an zam na leh zahna awm thei lakah kan dah fihlim phawt a tha a ni.

    xi) Lunatic Witmess: Heng ho hi doctor thiam bik te rawn ang in Lucid internal-ah exam tur an ni a, tin, Doctor pawh an awm theih a tha.

    xii) Nervous Witnesses: Hetiang witness ho hi thil tak tak zawh hma in ti ti pui a tih tlangnel phawt tur an ni. Tin, an thil hriat te pawh tlem te te a zawh a, a khat tawka an information hriat te zawh zauh zauh chauh tur an ni. Chung an sawi zawh hunah chuan an thil hriat te pawh lakkhawm chauh tur an ni.

    TYPES OF SUSPEFCTS:

    1. Strong
    2. Weak
    1 Strong :     (a) Active suspects
            Heng suspects ho hi miaia sang leh thil ti thei nia in hria an ni a, mi hneh theihna leh mi rilru hneh thei tak an ni.

            (b) Passive Suspects: (Botherless) 

            Heng ho hi pawisak neilo misual tawrawt, interrogator te pawh engahmah ngai lo ang chi an ni.

    2. Weak     (a) Active suspects:
            Hetiang suspects ho hi in puang zung zung chi an mahni tih ni miah lo pawh an mahni tihah an sawi thin.
             (b) Passive Suspects:
            Thil tha taka sawina chang hre lo a tlangpui thuin an lu te an buk loh leh an thing ringawt thin.
