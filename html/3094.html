A. Front page of Vanglaini and The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 29.4.2013

 

MMTTP road construction resumed under Police Guard :  

 

                        Mizoram Government sent around 400 personnel of 2nd IR Bn.MAP of Lunglei to guard the workers working under Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project in constructing road between Lawngtlai and Zochachhuah. The construction was temporarily stopped due to the stay order given to the contractors - RDS and ARSS by Land Owners Association on 25th April 2013. Though construction work was resumed; it was not done as it should be.

 

                        Regarding the stay order of road construction, PWD EE(MMTTP Division-II) Lalthanzuala Ralte made a notice on 25th April 2013 informing the contractors to resume their work under Police guard and to submit daily report of their progress to them.

 

                         

B. Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 29.4.2013

 

FIR lodged :

 

                        YMA Sub-Headquarters, Champhai lodged an FIR at Champhai Police Station against unidentified offenders who threw the carcass of pig which allegedly died from 'Classical Swine fever'. The carcass was found on the cultivated rice field near Tlangsam Tuipui River. Tuipui River is the source of water supply for the people living in the vicinity where the carcass was dumped which polluted the same.
