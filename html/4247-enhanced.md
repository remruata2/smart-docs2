**Insurgent arrested (2019 till date) :**

Mizoram experiences low level insurgencies in the North Eastern region. However, Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) (the insurgent group from the northern parts) had signed a Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) on 2nd April 2018. However, the other faction of HPC(D) led by Lalhmingthanga Sanate have started resurfacing and are indulging in random act of extortion in the Assam- Mizoram bordering areas.
