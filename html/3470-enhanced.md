A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 3.3.2014_

**Mizoram police Police Seized 378 Gelatin Sticks****:**In the morning of Saturday at around 7:00, Mizoram Police seized 378 gelatin sticks, 90 detonators and 6 coils of fuse wire from the possession of Hrangthanpara Hrangchal s/o R.Darthuama of Sakawrdai.

 Hrangthanpara Hrangchal was on his way to Manipur in a Gypsy regd. No. MZ -01 E 4678; he was stopped and checked by Mizoram Police at the outskirt of Vaitin and seized 180 gelatin sticks, 90 detonators and 4 coils of fuse wire, said IPRO of Mizoram Police.

 Hrangthanpara was interrogated and his house at Sakawrdai Vengpui was searched and 198 more gelatin sticks and 2 more coils of fuse wire were seized. Charges were made against him under Explosive Substances Act 1908.

 Mizoram Police are of the opinion that Hrangthanpara intended to hand over these explosives to HPC(D) at Patpuihmun, Manipur.
