A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.6.2013

Intention of road blockage: As Saihapui K Village does not get power supply for a long time, the people of Saihapui K intends to block NH-54 if power supply is not received within the month of June.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.6.2013

ILS fit for use: Examination of ILS at Lengpui Airport was resumed yesterday morning and the calibration flight proved that ILS is fit for use. Director General of Civil Aviation’s (DGCA) approval is expected by the end of this week, said GAD Aviation Wing.  

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.6.2013

B.Sangkhumi arrested: Former MPSC Chairman and President MHIP Gen. Hqrs. B.Sangkhumi of Chaltlang was arrested by the police yesterday for spreading untruthful text message through SMS in her mobile phone, but she was released on bail.  Lalthanzari of Ramhlun Veng accused of spreading the same text message through SMS as B.Sangkhumi was also arrested but she was also released on bail. Court has given anticipatory bail to B.Sangkhumi and Lalthanzari on 21.6.2013.
