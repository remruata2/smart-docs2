                        A chunga mite khi ka particulars chu ani a, 2nd Oct, 1976 ah Taliamura, South Tripura ah ka piang a, ka pa hi Jamatia hnam ani a, ka nu hi Bru(Reang) hnam a ni. Mahse ka nu pawh hian By-marriage in Jamatia hnam ah a inlet tawh a, certificate te pawh a nei tawh.

Tripura ah ka school kalna ka sawn a. Hemi hnu 1992 atangin Class-IX leh X chu Shillong ah Providence H/S ah ka zir a, mi in luahin Happy Valley ah ka awm thin.

 

                        Lehkha ka zir chhung 1990 atang hian ka nu leh pate pawh Dosda, North Tripura ah an lo pem tawh a, Class-X ka zir zawh hma hian ka haw chho a, Dosda ah ka nu leh pa bulah ka awm a, leilet ah te ka chhungte ka tanpui thin.

 

                        1999 October velah nupui atan Choranti D/o Lawma of Bethani, Kolasib Dist. ka nei a. Naisingpara ah kum khat ka awmpui hnuin kan khua Dosda ah ka pempui leh a, ka nu leh pa bulah tun thlengin kan la awm.

 

                        2006 June/July vel ah Haflong ah keimahin DHD Nunisia faction ka va tlawhpawh a, thla khat vel ka awm hnuah an remtih vak loh avangin ka haw leh a, thla khat vel kan in a ka awm hnuah keima thu bawkin DHD hi ka va fin leh a, min lo pawm ve ta mai.

 

                        2007 ah chuan Havangazau, NC. Hills ah DHD hnuaiah training ka zawm a, hetah hian keimahin Bru hnam zing atangin Vanlalfela, Muikau leh Keiya te pawh an lo tel ve a, anni bakah hian Bru atangin cadres 14 vel kan nei ve a, Mizoram, Assam leh Tripura atangi lokal te an ni hlawm. Thla thum kan training hnu chuan kan naute/cadres ho chu kan haw tir leh vek hlawm a.

 

                        Hemi kan trainingna atan hian Dergang Valley, N. Tripura a Village Chairman (CPIM) te hnen atangin donation kan khawn a, Rs.30,000/- vel kan hmuh ang chu DHD hnenah kan pe a, min tanpui tura ngenin NLFT hovin Bru hnam min tih duhdah dan leh BNLF thlenga an nghaisak dan te kan hrilh a, an hnuaiah inhmuthiam takin kan training thei ta ani.

 

                        Kan training zawh hnu, kan naute/cadres ho kan hawtir hnu hian kei, Muikau, Keiya leh Vanlalfela te chu DHD Camp ah kan la awm zui ta zel a, hetihlai hian ZRA atanga tlanchhuak awmtha duhlo ho leh Assam ram chhung a Bru Gang te zinga mi Donoram te nen infinkhawmin, an zinga mi tho chuan Donoram chu an kaphlum a. Chumi hnuah ULFB pawl hming invuahin rei vaklo an khawsa a, 2008 ah Assam Chief Minister hnenah an in surrender a, mahse sorkar in a intiamna ang te a hlenchhuak lo nia hria in, Bru hnam ho bik chu an in re-group leh a, heng zingah hian Raj Muni a tel ve thin ani tih ka hria a, ka zawng chhuak ta a ni.

 

                        July 2008 vel atangin Raj-a nen chuan kan inkawp tan a, kan ngaihdan leh duhdan te kan inhrilh tawn a. Raj-a hnenah chuan Tripura South, West, North velah NLFT in Bru hnam an tihduhdah dan te hrilh in chhan an ngai nia ka hriat thu te ka hrilh a, hetatang hian ka ngaihdan chu min thlawp niin ka hria.

 

                        October 2008 vel atang chuan Raj-a hian recruitment a siam tan a, 2009 kumtir vel atangin mi 14 vel chu ZRA hnenah hruaiin, training te a neihpui a. Kan inhmu lo zui vang vang a. September 2009 ah Silchar ah kan inhmu a, arms an neih thu te min hrilh a, thukhat vuain kan awm zui ta ani.

 

                        Tichuan Raj-a chu Silchar/Assam ah a awm zui a, Muikau a hovin a naute silai tha kengin Tripura an pan a, keipawhin November 2009 khan Kalapahar ah ka fin a, Gurguthi leh Riflemara velah kan cham pui a, January 2010 ah Kaiskau leh khakchang area velah kan lut ta chauh a, helai hmunah hian Raj-a mi rawn tirh Thomas-a hnenah charge ka hlan a, ani hian Muikau leh a naute chu a kaihruai ve ta thung a ni.

 

                        Kei chu January thla tawp atangin February 2010 thleng kan khua Dosda ah ka hahchawl a, hemi hnu hian phone a inbe pawp in February 2010 khan Raj-a nen chuan Silchar ah kan inhmu leh a, kan chet lak dan tur leh South Tripura kan pan theih dan tur te kan sawidun a.
