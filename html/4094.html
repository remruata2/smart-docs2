REPORTS ON BRU ARMED GANG

 

 

            Termed previously as Bru Armed Gang and presently called themselves 'United Liberation Army of Bruland' (ULAB) was formed in 2003/2004 by Bru youths namely Rajmoni Reang, South Tripura; Thomas@Tonsiram Anando; Vanlalfela, Tuirum; Henry Lalmalsawma(37) S/o Kathihari Jamatia, Adibasi, N.Tripura. They are having cordial relationship with DHD and some of their leaders are with them (DHD) till date.

 

            On 17.1.2010, two of their cadres namely S/S Lt.Vanlalfela and S/S Sgt.Chusarai were arrested at Silchar and the following documents were seized from them.

 

            1) Two United Liberation Army seals and one Round seal.

 

            2) Receipt book of Bru Reunification Organisation meant for collection      of donation.

 

            3) Seals bearing inscriptions of Commander-in-Chief and Deputy     Commander.

 

 

            On 7.7.2011, they had discussion regarding changing of the group's name and had chosen United Liberation Army of Bruland from four proposed names and used the same for collection of donations etc.

 

            The ULAB used to abused innocent civilians living in Assam, Tripura and Mizoram and also made forced collection from Relief funds given to the refugees by Tripura Government fixing percentage of the amount.

 

            The ULAB had also indulged in felonious activities and some of them are:-

 

            1) They shot dead Zarzokima(17) S/o Lalngilneia, Bungthuam on     13.11.2003. Kanhmun PS Case No.23/2009 u/s 302/120B IPC r/w            25(1B)(a)/27 Arms Act had been registered.

 

            2) On 25.11.2010, two trucks carrying teak-logs were ambushed near          Medili Bridge and snatched Rs.2000/-, Teak Transit Permit and motor      documents from the drivers.

 

            3) On 18.10.2011, one Biloram(50) S/o Sugurmoni Reang, Medili,    Assam was brutally killed and also threatened other civilians.

 

            4) They had killed one Tiger-a, ex-BNLF cadre at Naisingpara in 2010.

 

            5) On 7.4.2012, they had served a demand letter to Zadingliana, Vaisam,     Tripura demanding Rs. 1 lakh and the latter gave them Rs.20,000/-.

 

 

            The ULAB, besides these, had damaged the vehicle of non-Mizo and also snatched gun from a civilian of Kanhmun village in the year 2010.

 

            List of ULAB cadres is appended. It seems that the ULAB are staying in different camps of refugees freely and collecting donations.
