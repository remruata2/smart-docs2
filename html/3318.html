A. Front page of VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 15.5.2013

Stolen Steel tubular poles recovered : Sairang Police arrested two men alleged of stealing and selling 171 steel tubular poles meant for Power and Electricity Department.

On 18th May,2013, the supplier of steel tubular poles to P&E, Satnam Global Infrastructure Project, reported to Sairang Police Station that 497 steel tubular poles, which they kept at NEC, Mualkhang were missing. Sairang Police investigated the case, arrested Ngurthanmawia of Bawngkawn, Durtlang road with his non-Mizo accomplice and recovered 171 steel tubular poles from them on Monday. Stolen Steel tubular poles they had sold to W.Phaileng and Marpara are expected to be recovered soon.

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 15.5.2013

Pseudoephedrine seized  : Special Narcotic Police Staff under SP, CID(Crime) seized 1,00,000 tablets of Pseudoephedrine at Zarkawt and Chaltlang Lily Veng, Aizawl yesterday and arrested the alleged owner Sams Uddin of Doidanpur, Cachar and Nur Jaman Barbuya of Natikhal, Silchar.
