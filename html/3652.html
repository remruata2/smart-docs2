(i)        Appeal the general public to inform PAMRA General Headquarters regarding
            any reliable information pertaining to dry areca nut smuggling.

(ii)       Involvement of Pu Rosangzuala, OSD to Chief Minister in dry areca nut      smuggling was not found as per enquiry conducted by them. 21.4.2022  : Peace Accord MNF Returnee's Association (PAMRA) General Headquarters held its Special Assembly on  21.4.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at PAMRA Office, Treasury Square, Aizawl. Pu B. Zorampara, President PAMRA, presided the assembly and 80 (eighty) members attended. The Special Assembly constituted Martyrs' Cell under PAMRA today and informed all Martyrs' families to report to the said cell. It also decided that authority over the Martyrs' Cemetery at Luangmual should be handed over to PAMRA due to concern for its better management in future as they are also a registered society under the State government. It further decided that General Assembly will be held on 2.8.2022 on the occasion of 'Remna Run Lawi Ni (date of occupation of Remna Run)'. They also passed the following resolutions:

            (i)        Financial Assistance should be distributed to Peace Accord Returnees by the State government before 30.6.2022, which is an anniversary of 'Remna Ni' (Peace Accord Day).

            (ii)       Necessary action will be taken as per the Action Plan prepared by the General Assembly if no favourable information in connection with grant of Housing
Assistance is received before 2.8.2022.
