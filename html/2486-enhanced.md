****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/664

Dated 20th November, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/661 Dt. 19.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-5) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 20.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 9(nine) vehicles transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

There is no restriction for movement of locals to attend their farm works since 18.11.2020. In the meantime, for security measures they are not encouraged to venture beyond their farm. Today i.e., 20.11.2020 @ 11:00 a.m. around 40 (forty) Non-Mizos who were engaged as caretakers of paddy field in Phainuam Village and its surrounding areas approached Commanders of BSF and SSB to allow entry of vehicles into Mizoram via Saihapui ‘V’ border to ferry their goods (betel nut and fish etc.) to Assam. After consulting DIG (SR), Assam and DC, Cachar entry/exit of vehicles will be allowed during 6:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. and register should be maintained mentioning Driving Licence, Voters ID, Vehicles No. and Name of the drivers.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

43(forty three) vehicles (HMV-19, MMV-2, LMV-22) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Pu Vanlalrova, Bairabi North VC resumed construction of his farm hut at his paddy field around 2(two) kms towards north form Zophai BOP and there is no report of complaint regarding his construction. Situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

****337(three hundred thirty seven) vehicles (HMV-150, MMV-97, LMV-31, Maxi Cab-21, Bus-4, LPG-9, Oil-25) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 8:00 a.m. to12:00 noon.
 As per Deputy Commissioner, Kolasib District order to open Covid-19 Checkgate on every Thursday in order to minimize the hardship faced by Villages of Serkhan – Bukpui Hill range, Covid-19 checkgate of Saiphai was opened for movement of vehicles carrying essential commodities into Mizoram on 19.11.2020. Medical Team, Saiphai VLTF and Police personnel (MRP+IR) were on duty at the checkgate. As reported by SP Kolasib, while the Medical screening and movement of vehicles were going on smoothly, one non-Mizo driver who failed to maintain social distancing and safety protocol was kicked at his calves by one IR personnel at around 2:30 p.m. and the non-Mizo driver instigated his fellow non-Mizo driver not to enter into Mizoram and at the same time around 100 (hundred) Civilians from Assam side gathered near Saiphai bridge due to the incident and they were dispersed by Assam Police personnel from the spot. However, movement of vehicles into Mizoram could not be resumed. Assam Forest Minister accompanied by DC Cachar, SP Cachar and OC Dholai PS arrived at the PO at around 5:00 p.m. and the Minister enquired about the nature of duty performed by Mizoram Police personnel at the check-post. He was informed that the check post is solely for Covid-19 screening and Police personnel are deployed to restrict movement of vehicles and people to prevent further spreading of Covid-19 in the state. The minister addressed the gathering and stated that there is no Covid-19 in Assam and DC, Cachar also informed the gathering that Assam side will also made a counter measure for restriction of vehicle/public movement made by Mizoram. Thereafter, the gathering peacefully dispersed from the Bridge.

 In this connection, 12(twelve) members of Saiphai VLTF performed duty at Kachasora Bridge, Assam-Mizoram boundary today i.e., 20.11.2020. No movement of vehicles across Mizoram-Assam border. Today is supposed to be the day of exporting freshly grown vegetables to Assam from Saiphai but they are not exporting vegetables to Assam due to the prevailing situation.

 Situation is under close watch.
