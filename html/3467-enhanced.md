**1.4.2022 :** Mizoram Ministerial Service Association (Assistant Unit) held its 16th General Conference on 1.4.2022 at Vaivakawn YMA Auditorium, Aizawl. Pu Lalnghinglova, President, MMSA presided the conference and 160 (one hundred sixty) members attended the programme.
****8.4.2022 :** Mizoram Ministerial Service Association (MMSA) held its General Conference on 8.4.2022 @10:30 a.m. at Horticulture Deptt. Conference Hall, Tuikual, Aizawl. Pu T.
