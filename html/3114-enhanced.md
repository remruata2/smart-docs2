B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 30.8.2013_

**31 families evacuated**: 31 families living in 26 buildings in Champhai Vengthlang IB Veng in Champhai were evacuated till date due to a large earth crack that endangered their houses. The houses will be dismantled from today by Champhai vengthlang community.

 The officials of DM&amp;R Department, H.Rochungnunga, Secy., Lalpeksanga, Director, and Lalsangluaia Dy. Director will visit this place and will reach Champhai today. Champhai DC, Lalthangpuia Sailo, Chairman, Champhai District Level Disaster Committee and his team recently visited the earth cracked place.
