****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/204

Dated 22nd March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/199 Dt. 19.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

27 (twenty seven) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-14, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 22.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

13 (thirteen) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-11, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 22.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

86 (eighty six) vehicles (HMV-53, MMV-2, LMV-24, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Ref. to our Report Vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/177 Dt. 12.3.2021 construction of Agriculture link road undertaken by Joint VC Bairabi in between Zophai BOP and Ramri Lui, Assam Mizoram border which started on 11.3.2021 was halted w.e.f., 20.3.2021 as per the request of SP, Hailakandi and the work was not resumed till today.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

107 (one hundred seven) vehicles (HMV-60, MMV-12, LMV-10, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-10, Oil Tanker-13, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 22.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
