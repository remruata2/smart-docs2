A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 01.12.2022

Home delivery of LPG to begin from today: Starting from today (1.12.2022) home delivery of LPG will be implemented in Aizawl and all the district headquarters of Mizoram. This new system came after Food Civil Supply & Consumer Affairs department Secretary issued a notification vide No.B.12013/4/2022 - FCSCA  Date 01.11.2022 which directed all LPG distributors to practice home delivery system from 01.12.2022 and it will which abolished the common delivery/distribution in different parts of the city and district headquarters. At the same time, although home delivery of LPG is to practice, adequate information and awareness programme in this connection has not been carried out among the public in Aizawl and other district headquarters , according to sources.

 

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 01.12.2022

Christmas blood donations:  Association for Blood Donation, MSACS and blood bank intended to organised voluntary blood donation programme to mark the Christmas festival and the inaugural function will be held at Aizawl Civil Hospital today. Under this programme, willing donors will be able to donate in any blood bank according to their convenience where refreshment will be provided to them. AVBD requested all the youth of Mizoram to participate in this programme and to voluntarily donate their blood.

 

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 01.12.2022

Sadbhav Mandav : Pu H.Lalzirliana, MLA Mamit constituency inaugurated Mini Sadbhav Mandav (Multipurpose Community Centre) at Bawngva village,mamit district. This community centre was constructed using Rs. 50 lakhs which are funds received from NITI Aayog. The MLA also handed over silpauline, tea cups, utensils, footballs, volleyballs, jerseys and other useful items to the leaders of Bawngva village community.
