Brief history  :

 

                        A chunga tarlan ang khi ka particular chu a ni. Kum 1974 March 24th ah Tinghmun khua ah ka piang a, he mi khua ah hian ka seilian a, zirna lam ah chuan Zohmun H/S ah Class IX thleng ka zir a ni. 12th January 1997-ah Lalhmingzovi of Khangbawr, Manipur nen kan innei in tunah hian fa pali (4) kan nei tawh. Ka nupui neih hma kum 1992 – 1994 inkar khan HPC ah lutin underground-ah ka tang ve a, sipai satliah ah awmin rank ka nei hman lemlo.  

                        Eizawnna lam ah chuan hna nghet nei lem lo in hna hrang hrang ka thawk kual thin a. Tunhma chuan Tinghmun atang Thingzai pu in Tuiruang lui ah ka tawlh thin a, heng thingzai te hi Lakhipur, Fulavtol leh Binakhandi- ah te ka pe thin. Thingzai tawlh hna engemaw chen ka thawh hnu hian kum 2002- khan kan khua Tinghmun-ah HPC ticket-in VC member atan thlan tlin ka ni a, tichuan politics lamah ka inhmang zui ta zel a ni. Tin, kum 2003 – 2007 khan VFDC (village forest development committee) Chairman- atan thlan tlin ka ni a, hemi hnu kum 2007 – 2009 (Pu Hmingchungnunga term hmasa) khan SHDC member ah ruat ka ni bawk. Tin, tunah hian Vice President of Tinghmun VC ka ni mek a, Ex-Hmar volunteer association President ka ni mek bawk.

Kum 2007 khan Sakawrdai-ah ram ka lei ve a, kum 2009 khan ka ram leiah chuan In ka sa a, hetah hian ka Nupui fanau te nen kan khawsa zui ta a ni. Sakawrdai-ah hian ka fate zawng zawng in School an kal avangin awmpui an ngai thin a, Tinghmun-ah hi chuan keimah deuh ngawtin ka awm thin a, tun kum 2012 atang Pu V. Lalawmpuia, Tinghmun H/S Teacher-in min rawn awmchilh a kan pahnih in kan khawsa ta zel a ni.

                                      

 

BRIEF  STORY (in conn. with the case) :

 

                        Tun tum a HPC(D) hnen a pek tur pawisa ka hnen atanga police te’n an man hi Rs. 3,94,250/- (Nuai thum, singkua, sangli, zahnih leh sawmnga) niin ka hria a. Ka kut atanga an man theih dan chanchin tlangpui chu hetiang hi a ni – Dt. 22.6.12 (Friday) @ 11-12 noon inkar khan Pu Dinsanga Sakawrdai VC member (ZNP ticket) chuan ka mobile phone-ah min rawn bia a, HPC(D) hnena pek tur pawisa an rawn thawn thu leh an mi tirh a rawn thlen tawh leh tawh loh min rawn zawt a, keichuan a lo la thlen loh thu ka lo hrilh a. Kan inbiak zawh atanga hun rei lote chhun dar 12 bawr vel ah chuan Remsiama of Sakawrdai Chhim Veng chu ka In ah lo kalin Pawisa khawnkhawm leh lehkha min rawn pe ta a, lehkha ah chuan ka hming min sign tir a, a copy pakhat chu a hawn let leh nghe nghe a ni.Remsiama a lo thlen hian Pawisa chu a chhiar thlap em tih ka lo zawt a, a ni chuan a chhiar thu a sawi avang leh a dikthlap a tih angin keichuan ka chhiar nawn ve ta chuang lo a, hetia ka hming ka sign zawh hian Remsiama pawh an khua Sakawrdai lam panin ka hming sign na lehkha copy kengin a haw ve leh ta nghal a ni. Pawisa ka dawn hnu hian Pu Chhanhima of HPC(D) hnen ah pawisa ka hnen a a awm tawh thu ka hrilh nghal a, pawisa inpek dan tur chungchang erawh kan sawi dun lo. Hemi hnu hian a tuk (Saturday) @ 7:00 AM vel ah Pu Chhanhima chuan min lo phone a, pawisa chu ‘Kangrengdawr’ khua- a keng thla a amah chu pe turin min rawn ngen a, ka ngam loh thu ka lo hrilh a. Tichuan, pawisa chu ka mirinzawng hmanga HPC(D) hnena thawn mai turin kan insiamrem ta a. Nimahsela pawisa pe tura tirh mai tur ka hriat bik si loh avangin pawisa chu ka kawl zui ta rih a, tichuan he mi ni vek zinglam dar 10:00 AM vel chuan police- te chu ka In ah lo kal in ka In chu check a, an beisei ang an hmuh mai loh avangin ka pawisa thuhrukna leh thil awmzia min zawt a, phat ngaihna ka hriatloh avangin HPC(D) ho hnena pek tura kan ruahman Rs. 3,94,250 chu ka phawrh a police- te chu ka pe ta a ni. Ka pawisa kawl leh lehkha pawimawh an man rual hian keipawh thu zawhfiah turin police te hian min la ve ta nghal a ni.

Q. HPC(D) donation khawn chungchang a I thil hriatchin leh inbiakna kaldan zawng zawng I hriatte enge?

 

Ans:    June thla laihawl lam khan Pu Dinliana VCP Sakawrdai nen kan titi a, a ni chuan All party leh HPC(D) te inbiak tumna a tlawh chhoh zel avangin HPC(D) demand pawh tih hlawhtlin mai angaih tur thu te a sawiin, pawisa an lo khawn ngah thei anih chuan HPC(D) hnena hlan dan kawng dappui turin min ngen a, VC-te chu in mamawh tawna intanpui tawn reng kan nihna laiah keipawh chuan ka puih tur thu in ka chhang a. Keipawhin Chhanhima of HPC(D) chu ka be pawp ve zui a, donation chungchanga hmalak an tum dan te ka zawt a, anni lam chuan inbiakna kaltluangin kaltluang lo se doanation chungchangah chuan hma an lakzui zel tur thu min hrilh chu khaw dang VC te pawh ka hrilh chhawng nual a ni. Hemi hnu hian Pu Dinliana nen hian phone ah kan inbe pawp leh a, Pu Dina chuan ‘mipui ah HPC(D) demand tihhlawhtlin nana emergency a pawisa an lakkhawm tur thu te, mipui mimir nitin khawsak a harsat avanga HPC(D) demand angzat pawisa an tlin theih an inrin loh thu te min rawn hrilh chu HPC(D) ka hrilh chhawng ve leh a ni. Thawm a reh vang vang hnu ah Dt. 22.6.2012 @ 7:00 AM vel khan Sakawrdai VC te chuan min rawn be pawp leh chauh a, pawisa pawh hemi ni vek hian ka dawng ta nghal a, a tuk lam a HPC(D) te nen inhlan kan tum chu kan inhlan hmain police te’n min man ta a ni. Donation pawisa tlingkhawm hi ka kuta hlanchhawn tur a ni tih HPC(D) lam hian min hriattir lo a, tin, keiin HPC(D) te ka hlanchhawn lehzel dan tur pawh sawi lawk a awm lo hrim hrim a ni.
