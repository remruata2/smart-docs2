****REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/586

Dated 20th October, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/582 Dt. 19.10.2020, today afternoon i.e., 20.10.2020 @ 2:30 p.m. Pu R. Lalzirliana, Minister visited Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Mizoram – Assam border and he was received by Dr. Lalrozama, IAS, DC, Mamit, Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit and Pu Lallianmawia, IPS, CO, 4th IR Battalion. During his visit, the minister stated that electric connection will be provided to Mizoram Police Camp at the area and handed over one no. of Cooler, one no. of Fridge, two nos. of Solar Lamps and Rs.5000 to Mizoram Police on duty. He also handed over Rs.10,000 to VCP, Thinghlun and Rs.5000 to MHIP Thinghlun and he left Thinghlun around 4:00 p.m. today. The Minister was accompanied by Pu Zothantluanga, MLA, Er. H. Lalzirliana, MLA and other government officials.

 VC members of Thinghlun Village held a short meeting at Pu Gospel Zoramchhana’s residence, Thinghlun today morning at 7:00 a.m. and Pu Lallianmawia, IPS, CO, 4th IR Battalion and Pu Zoramthara, Addl. SP, Mamit were also present. The meeting resolved that they will not oppose construction of Assam Police Camp at Assam side.

32 (thirty two) MZP members who are stationed at Thinghlun since 19.10.2020 left Thinghlun for Aizawl after having a short interaction with Pu R. Lalzirliana and they stated that they will not oppose construction of Assam Police Camp at Assam side. 30 (thirty) members of Zawlpui Branch YMA who assist border duty at Thinghlun will perform duty till late evening today.

 Further, no action has been taken for construction of Assam Police Camp near the area by Assam Officials today i.e., 20.10.2020.

 Situation is under close watch.
