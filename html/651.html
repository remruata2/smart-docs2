                                                                   ORDER

 

            Shri Amit Shah, Hon'ble Union Home Minister is likely to visit North Eastern Police Academy (NEPA), Meghalaya on 15.12.2019. In view of the ongoing protest against the contentious Citizenship Amendment Bill, especially in the North Eastern Region of the country, personnel of Field Branch, CID (SB) are herby instructed to monitor the movements and activities of leaders of NESO,MZP,MSU and other organizations of Mizoram who may disrupt this visit of the Hon'ble Union Home Minister and information of the same should be communicated to the undersigned by quickest possible means.
