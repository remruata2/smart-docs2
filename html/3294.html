A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.7.3.2013

House gutted in Lunglei : A newly built house belonging to C.Lalawmpuia at Chanmari-II in Lunglei was gutted by fire yesterday evening, ie, 6.3.2013. The house was not even occupied by them; some labourers of bakery used it for sleeping and keeping their materials. When the personnel of Fire Deptt. arrived, almost everything was already burnt. Police apprehended two suspected persons in connection with the incident.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.7.3.2013

Technical hitch of ATM : Due to technical hitch, ATM of SBI, Bawngkawn produced/released Rs. 2,000/-  amount in excess to the user than the amount of money they should have to withdraw between 12:00 noon to 01:00 PM yesterday, ie, 6.3.2013.

            SBI officials requested all the customers who took out excess amount to report and return it within 3 days; if not their names and account No. will be publicised in the newspapers and case will be filed against them to the police if they do not comply with their request.

 

C. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.7.3.2013

Bike lifter nabbed: Lalngaihzuala (25) s/o Dosanga and his partner Lalremsiama (19) s/o Lalthapuia both residents of Police Veng in Saitual were nabbed by Saitual police yesterday ie, 6.3.2013 as they were suspected in Bikes stealing. They admitted to the crime later.

            From their confession to the police, 4 bikes stolen by them from Saitual, Keifang and Aizawl were recovered by the police. They dismantled and modified all the bikes they had stolen so that they could not be identified by their owners. Lalngaihzuala had stolen more than 10 bikes before and was arrested for the crime and imprisoned for it. He sold some of his stolen bikes in Myanmar.

 

D. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.7.3.2013

Farmer charred to death: A farmer Engthanga (47) of Rawpui Village in Lunglei Dist., was burnt to death in a Jhum fire yesterday ie., 6.3.2013. Sources said Engthanga tried to flee from the wild fire that spread from the neighbouring forest but the fire caught him.
