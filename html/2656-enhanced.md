****ENQUIRY REPORT ON FAKE COVID-19 FREE CERTIFICATE****

(1) There has been indeed rumours regarding issuance of Fake Covid-19 Free Certificate at Assam near Mizoram-Assam border such as Patharkandi, Karimganj and Silchar with a fees ranging from Rs. 300 - 500. However, the used of such fake certificate for entry into Mizoram could not be ascertained as there has been no report of detection of Fake Covid-19 Free Certificate inside Mizoram.

(2) Bilal Uddin (30) of Patharkandi, Assam, alleged accused person for arranging such fake certificate resided with his brother Abdul Salam (42) at Zarkawt, Aizawl till March, 2020. Being a house painter by profession, he sometimes assisted his brother Abdul Salam in running RPS Maxi Cab Counter at Zarkawt, Aizawl.

(3) Abdul Salam operated Maxi Cab Counter business till March, 2020 and he returned back to his home town during March, 2020 due to the outbreak of Covid-19 pandemic. He has not yet returned to Aizawl till date and it may be due to reconstruction of his Maxi Cab Counter building at Zarkawt.

(4) Bilal Uddin and Abdul Salam both are fluent in Mizo dialect. On being enquired they both denied to have any involvement in arranging such Fake Covid-19 Free Certificate. Bilal even claimed that he have not come across any of such kind of Fake Covid-19 Free Certificate.

(5) Reports received from Mizoram Police personnel deployed at Vairengte and Bairabi border indicated that rumors regarding availability of such Fake Covid-19 Free Certificate was heard. At the border they only cross check whether Covid-19 Free Certificate possessed by the person tally with their name in their mPASS along with their ID Cards. It also mentioned that Covid-19 Free Certificate normally bear name of the Hospital, Official Seal, Signature of Examining Doctor and name of testee.
