 Raymond Lalbiakzama lodged an FIR at Bawngkawn Police Station on 14th May 2013; following the FIR Dy.SP Zoramsanga Ralte probed into the matter and submitted chargesheet to the Court on 23rd September, 2013.

D. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.9.2013_

**High Court Ordered Mizoram Government****:** Zofa Welfare Organisation had lodged an FIR against Assam as it laid a stone which appeared to be a boundary milestone in Vairengte rice field/farmland and for snatching teak from Bairabi more than 80 times. Zofa Welfare said that the Scheduled Caste And Scheduled Tribe (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989 is not practiced in Mizoram as such FIR they lodged was neglected. JAC was formed with other groups after the Mizoram did not take any initiative to their request to practice the act. JAC now made a complaint to National Commission for Scheduled Tribe and Gauhati High Court. High Court ordered the Mizoram Government to explain its reason for not using the act within 14 days on 24th September, 2013.
