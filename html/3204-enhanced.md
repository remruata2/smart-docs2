A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 9.7.2013_

**Mizoram Assembly Election in November**: Mizoram Assembly Election is to be held in the later dates of November tentatively along with election in Delhi, Madhya Pradesh and Rajasthan announced the Election Commission.

 Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) will be used in election of the four states including Mizoram. EVM to be used is not like the ones used in 2008 Election, a new kind of EVM will be used.

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 9.7.2013_

**Bru Repatriation**: Government officials meeting led by Chief Secretary Pi L.Tochhawng yesterday informed Mamit district authority to make plan and to be prepared to repatriate Bru refugees anytime.

 Reports from Home Department said that according to the ongoing plan as soon as free ration is received from Food Corporation of India, repatriation of Bru will be commenced. Mamit District authority will take care of security matter and other necessary actions.

**Murderer(s) must be found**: Zuangtui community demands that the Mizoram Police must find people responsible for the death of Lalnunmawia (22) s/o Sawichhunga at Falkland on 5th July, 2013, and if Police cannot find the culprit Zuangtui community will take steps accordingly.
