CENTRAL YOUNG MIZO ASSOCIATION (CYMA)

S.R. No.4 of 1977 under Indian Society Registration Act(XXI of 1860) on 14th May,1977

1. **Formation** : Young Mizo Association was established on 15.6.1935 originally as Young Lushai Association (YLA), which was later replaced with the “Young Mizo Association” in 1947. It was initiated by the Christian Missionaries who understood the need of cultural conservation of the Mizo tribe, who were under pressure of Political and social modernization
2. **Aims and Objectives** :

1. Good used of leisure
2. Development of the Mizo Society
3. Revere Christian ethics

1. **Type** : Young Mizo Association is a voluntary organization without any Political Party interference
2. **Motto** : “Help the needy”
3. **Structure of CYMA** :

1. **General Conference & Election** :

1. Office Bearers of CYMA are elected by the General Conference for a term of 2 years.
2. For a member to be elected as Office Bearers, he/she must be a resident of Mizoram capital Aizawl.
3. Same post as an Office Bearers cannot be held for more than 2 consecutive terms.
4. Guide lines for election :

1. Central Executive committee, according to the situation, may nominate not more than 5 persons.
2. Executive committee of each Sub-Headquarters may nominate not more than 3 persons.
3. Not more than 2 members may also be nominated by Group Executive Committee.
4. All the Branches within Aizawl City may nominate 1 candidate each.

This nomination is not necessarily to be a member from within their respective Branches.

1. These nominated persons should at least be a member of YMA for 5 years provided they have been a member as an Office Bearer in Sub-Headquarters/ Groups or Branches.
