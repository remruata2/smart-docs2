A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 28.6.2013

HPC against Darlawn demand for district: Lalrosiem’s led HPC Gen. Hqrs. made an announcement yesterday saying that they do not allow villages under HPC Demand Area to be included in the Darlawn’s demand for administrative district, they also do not allow Suangpuilawn region to be included in it.

Darlawn District Demand Committee already expressed on Press Conference that they had a talk with HPC(D) led by H.Zosangbera and not HPC who told that regarding demand for district there is no problem on the side of HPC(D).
