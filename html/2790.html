SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/143

Dated: 2nd March, 2022

 

 

          Ref. to our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/135 dated 25.2.2022, the bridge across Tlawngte river, situated in between Thinghlun, Mizoram and Kalagang, Assam, and around 1 km from Mizoram Police Camp at Thinghlun, Mamit District, Mizoram, which was burnt down on 24.2.2022 was repaired by members of Young Muslim Association, Kalagang, Assam on 26.2.2022 by using Bamboo and is now serviceable.
