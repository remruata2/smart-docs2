A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 19.10.2012

 

Discussion on Zawlnuam burglary case: Pu Zorammuana, DIG(NR) left for Tripura yesterday to have a DIG level discussion on Zawlnuam BDO office burglary case in which Rs.1,98,41,996/- was stolen.

 

Rs.90,75,600/- has been recovered (i.e. 45.72%) till now, most of which were recovered from Tripura State. Tripura police have recovered Rs.4,47,370/- which they handed over to Mizoram police.

 

It is reported that Mizoram police and Tripura police have a cordial co-ordination on this case.

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post, Tawrhbawm.

 

 

 

B. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper. Dt. 19.10.2012

 

All Party Joint Committee wish for Peace Talk: The All Party Joint Committee leaders from Sinlung Hills Development Council express their wish for a peace talk between Govt. of Mizoram and HPC(D). It is also reported if the peace talk could not be held, leaders of political parties, NGOs and Churches will have a meeting to discuss their future course of action.

 

 

C. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 19.10.2012

 

YMA to organise procession and bandh at Chawngte: V.Lalramlawma(46) S/o Laiawrha, Tuithiang Veng, Aizawl, Chowkidar at ICDS office, Chawngte died on 18.10.2012 at 3:30am yesterday at Civil Hospital, Lunglei resulting from the alleged assault by Shanti Bikash Chakma on 17.10.2012. The Chawngte Group YMA decided to organise a procession on 22.10.2012 from Chawngte 'P' to Kamalanagar SDO Civil office and a total bandh on 23.10.2012 from 6:00am in protest against this suspicious death.

 

The Chawngte Group YMA demanded that the accused Shanti Bikash Chakma should pay atleast Rs.5 lakhs to the family of deceased Lalramlawma as compensation. The Chawngte Group YMA and MZP Chawngte suspect that Shanti Bikash Chakma had an accomplice who should immediately be detected and arrested by the police.
