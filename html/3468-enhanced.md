**1.3.2022:** United Sports Association of Mizoram (USAM) a conglomerate of 32 (thirty two) sports associations in its Press Release issued on 1.3.2022 expressed disappointment over allotment of only Rs. 2422.12 lakhs to Sports &amp; Youth Services Department for the year 2022-2023, which is lesser than the previous budget. It also stated that Sports Budget of Rs. 5077.21 lakhs was laid in the year 2018-2019 and in the current Budget Session, only 0.17 percent out of the Total Budget i.e., Rs. 14,008 crores was laid. It considered decreasing of Sports Budget as neglect of the youths and expressed their readiness to take necessary action so as to increase Sports Budget.

****26.7.2022 :** Serchhip District Sport Committee (SDSC), Serchhip held its General Body Meeting on 26.7.2022 @ 2:00 p.m. at P&amp;E Rest House, Bukpui, Serchhip in connection with constitution of District Sports Committee under the chairmanship of respective Deputy Commissioner. The meeting after deliberate discussion strongly opposed the constitution of new District Sports Committee under the chairmanship of Deputy Commissioner. It also demanded the State government to take necessary action so that no change is brought into the system. It also decided that if no action is taken on their demand, sports associations under Serchhip District Sports Committee should not participate in any new initiative taken by the new District Sports Committee. A Press Release in this regard was issued on 26.7.2022.**
