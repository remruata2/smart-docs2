**Peace Dialogue :**

******Due to the initiative of SP CID(SB). 2 (two) rounds of Peace Dialogue between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) have so far been conducted within this year. The First round of Peace Dialogue was held on 10th August,2016 and the 2nd round of Peace Dialogue was held during 5th to 6th October,2016 where framework of agreement was work out. The third round of Peace Dialogue was held on 16.12.2016@ 11:00am at State Guest House, Aizawl as scheduled. During the talk, the following arms and ammunitions snatched away by HPC(D) from Mizoram Police were handed over to Govt. of Mizoram.**
