A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 05.08.2022_

****Conference of Senior Police Officers:**  Retired and servicing IPS and MPS officers of Mizoram on yesterday i.e 4.8.2022 held a programme of "Experience sharing: Learning from Elders" at Arya Mess, Aizawl. In this programme, which was graced by Mizoram DGP Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastava, senior retired police officers shared their valuable previous experiences with servicing police officers and offered encouragement and advise on policing works . The programme was conducted under the Chairmanship of Pu John Neihlaia, IGP (Hqrs.) with a vote of thanks delivered by Pu Lalhuliana fanai, DIG(CID).**

_B.Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 05.08.2022_

****High amount of rainfall during July:**According to the records of State Meteorological Centre under Directorate of Science and Technology, Mizoram received 460.3 mm of rainfall during the month of July, 2022. State Meteorological Centre started recording data of rainfall received from 1986 where the previous record for the highest was July, 2019 (539 mm) and July, 1997 (495 mm) which are followed by July of this year. Till the month of July this year, the total rainfall received by Mizoram is recorded as 1310.9 mm which may be compared with 1917.6 mm received during the whole year of 2021.**
