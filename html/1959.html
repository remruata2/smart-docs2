Subject :         Surrender of three militants of HNLC.

 

Sir,

                        In continuation to our telephonic conversations, this is to inform you that 3(three) militants of Hynniewtrep National Liberation Council (HNLC) namely (i) S/S Corporal Wiren Pohti S/o Riangdeng Lamin, Nongbarchrin,East Jaintia Hill (ii) S/S Private Treibor Tynhiang S/o Liding M.Lyngdoh, Myndo, West Khasi Hills (iii) S/S Private Sambormi Lyngdoh S/o B. Sutenga (L), Khleihriat West. East Jantia Hill had expressed their willingness to surrender before Meghalaya Police and  after receiving assurances from SIB, Aizawl  they  crossed Indo-Bangladesh Border on 12.12.2014 and reported to the undersigned (i.e. SP, CID(SB)) today i.e. 15.12.2014. under the guidance and escort of SIB Officers, Aizawl, Mizoram.

 

 It may be mentioned during the last week of November, 2014 Border Guards of Bangladesh (BGB) and Bangladesh Army had  destroyed  several hideouts of Indian Insurgent Groups like NLFT, HNLC and NDFB due to which the said three militants of HNLC also escaped from their camp i.e. Aru Sumer, near Bolsury village in the Chittagong Hills Tract of Bangladesh.

 

          I           In view of the above, it is requested that swift necessary actions may be taken from your end to receive the said 3(three) militants of HNLC from Aizawl, Mizoram. The said 3(three) militants of HNLC are presently in the custody of SIB Mizoram, Aizawl.

 

 

Yours faithfully,
