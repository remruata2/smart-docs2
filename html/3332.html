Traffic Police for Charity: Lunglei Traffic Police donated ` 5,000/- for charity to Bethel Camping Centre on Saturday ie, 31.8.2013. There are 121 patients in this Centre.

Habitual Robber Arrested: A joint team of YMA and Police arrested Lalchhuma @ Bakchehliana (51) of Selawn, Myanmar P/A Electric Veng, Lunglei who robbed YMA Information Centre, Lunglei Rahsi Veng on 27.8.2013. He is a habitual offender and had 4 cases in Lunglei Police Station alone, sources said.
