****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/221

Dated: 20th April, 2020.

 In continuation to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/218 dated 19.4.2020, the following NGOs and individuals visited New Kawnpui area of Mamit District in protest against physical assault of 2 (two) 5th IR Bn. personnel deployed on Covid-19 duty by Chakmas of Tripura on 18.4.2020 :-

1. MZP General Headquarters led by Pu B. Vanlaltana, President and 19 (nineteen) other members.
2. MSU General Headquarters led by Pu J. Lalmuanzuala, Vice-President and 4 (four) other members.
3. Dampa Group YMA led by PC Robuanga, President and 5 (five) other members.
4. 2 (two) Ex-OBs of CYMA viz., Pu R. Lalngheta and Pu Peter Chhakchhuak and 2 (two) others.

 The above persons were stopped en-route by BSF deployed at Tuipuibari for about 2 (two) hours in demand for Lockdown Movement Permission. They were allowed to further proceed to New Kawnpui after DC, Mamit issued the said permission at 3:00 p.m.

 They visited New Kawnpui BOP manned by 5th IR Bn. and a meeting between leaders of VC Zomuantlang, Taitesena Group YMA, Dampa Group YMA, Tripura Group YMA and Mizoram Police officials was held in which they conveyed in serious note their resentment and disappointment over the incident and asked SI (AB) Monu Kumar Chakma, Post Commander to personally inform the Chakmas residing across the border in New Kawnpui-I, Tripura the gravity of their action. They left New Kawnpui for Aizawl @ 8:05 p.m after having dinner at Mission Compound, Zomuantlang.

 As per information received, out of the 3 (three) accused persons arrested by Tripura Police on the night of 19.4.2020, 1 (one) accused person viz., Santi Lal Chakma (31) s/o Danya Ram Chakma of Kawnpui-I, Tripura escaped from Tripura Police custody today @ 11:00 a.m from PHC, Vanghmun where he was brought for medical examination.

_Cont/-2......_

-2-

 Taitesena Group YMA, Dampa Group YMA, MZP General Headquarters and MSU General Headquarters issued a Joint Press Release later today wherein they strongly condemned the assault of 2 (two) IR Bn. personnel by Chakmas on 18.4.2020. It demanded the authorities to immediately arrest all the perpetrators and expressed desire for better re-enforcement of guarding of inter-state boundary, especially Zomuantlang area. It also welcomed the transfer of SI (AB) Monu Kumar Chakma, Post Commander.

 Situation is under close watch.
