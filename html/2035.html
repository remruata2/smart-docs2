INTERROGATION STATEMENT  OF VANLALLIANA

 

 

                        I, Vanlalliana, S/o Saino Chondro (L) passed Class VIII from Saipullui village and did not continue further studies after completing HSLC. Later on, I got married and joined the MNF party where I rose up to become the unit secretary. I later on joined the RDP when it was formed in 1989 and became the Vice President. RJD later entered into an alliance with BJP. In 1994, I was elected V/C member at Sailupui. However, before the term of the V/C was over, I resigned from the V/C membership and migrated to Tuirum. While in Tuirum, I joined BNU. I was made Fin. Secretary and later became Gen. Secretary. The main objective of the party was to demand the status of district council for the Bru community. BNU later submitted a memorandum to the Govt. of India and Mizoram placing demand for the district council. However, there was a general feeling among the rank and file of the party that both the central and state Govt. did not give due cognizance and attention to our demand leading up to the formation of BNLF on 9.11.96 at Tuirum.

 

                        I was subsequently appointed the president of BNLF and started negotiations with NLFT while recruiting cadres for the outfit at the same time, that was the beginning of our movements as an underground organisation. In 1997, I visited Aizawl and held a meeting with our supporters/sympathizers at Hotel Ritz. I later returned to D/B and continued as the president of the outfit.

 

                        On 23.3.2001, I was arrested by Tripura Police while at Naisingpara refugee camp and later handed over to Mizoram Police. I was lodged at the central jail.

 

                        I was later released on bail from jail and subsequently joined BLFM which was in disarray. I reorganized and strengthen the outfit and became the president. I spurred the offer by Mizoram Govt. to lay down arms.

 

                        On 20.4.2006, I was again arrested in joint operation by A.R and CID(SB) and served time in central jail.
