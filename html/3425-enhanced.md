A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 29.1.2014_

****ACB Report****:** According to the report of Anti-Corruption Bureau, from the year 2011 till date, 134 cases were registered, among these 71 cases were charge sheeted. During the year 2012, Out of 66 cases registered - 41 cases were under Preliminary Enquiry (PE) and 25 cases were under Regular Case (RC), among these, 31 cases (PE-25 &amp; RC-6) were charge sheeted. During the year 2013, 68 cases were registered, out of which 26 cases were registered as Regular Case (RC) and 42 cases were registered as Preliminary Enquiry (PE), among these, 40 cases (PE-27 &amp; RC-13) were charge sheeted.**

 During the year 2011 to 31st December 2013, 71 cases were charge sheeted/disposed and forwarded/sent to Court.

 

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 29.1.2014_

****Police verify rumour****:** A rumour has spread recently that suspected Bru armed gangs were seen near Bunghmun Village in Lunglei District. Police investigated the matter and found that this report was just a rumour. There is one Police Station at Bunghmun and around 20 Police personnel of MRP and MAP are posted in this PS.**
