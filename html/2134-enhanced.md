****CENTRAL YOUNG LAI ASSOCIATION (CYLA) ON CHAKMA CENSUS****

 In pursuance of the agenda adopted by the Conference of CYLA held in 2018 at Thingkah, Lawngtlai district, the CYLA conducted foreigner drive within the western belt of Lawngtlai District in the month of May &amp; June, 2019.

**1st Phase:** Led by Pu J. Sangthangpuia, President and other members numbering 12 (twelve) persons commenced the drive on 30.5.2019 and visited the following villages:

i) Pandawnglui ii) Sakeilui One iii) Sakeilui Two iv) Karlui One

v) Jognasury vi)Sumsilui  vii) Sedailui  viii) Tuikhurlui

ix) Karlui Two x) Sedailui

  In this connection, 8 (eight) members representing Village Councils and Young Chakma Association (YCA) had submitted a joint application to the Deputy Commissioner, Lawngtlai District on 3.6.2019 with the following request :-

1. Order investigation to find out the facts about the ongoing ‘Local area population census-2019’ being conducted by CYLA and fixed accountability for such illegal activities which vitiate communal harmony.
2. Seize and destroy all the data gathered or collected through this illegal population census by NGO.
3. Ensure such illegal census/head counting are not conducted anywhere in future and take strict actions against those who conduct such activities.

**2nd Phase:**

Pu J. Sangthangpuia, President and other 30 (thirty) members left Lawngtlai on 14.6.2019 @ 9:00 a.m and conducted drive in Fangfarlui, Damlui, Pandawnglui, Karlui-I, Karlui-II, Jognasury, Nghalimlui and Vaseikai villages and returned to Lawngtlai on 17.6.2019 @ 2:45 a.m.

 Necessary security arrangement had been made by Lawngtlai Police.

 Information has been received that 2 (two) villages (one of which is Boliasora) are still left and CYLA is intending to conduct the drive at the earliest.
