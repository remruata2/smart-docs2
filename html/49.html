MIZO LAND TRAVELS  &   CARGO  SERVICE

Tun thleng hi chuan an customer ten  engmah buaina an la nei lo. Heng an pawisa pukte hi an service agency tihchangtlun na atan hmangin 17th Dec/08 pawh khan Bus (Passenger phur) pakhat lei in, heng an motor te thawhchhuah atang hian an pawisa puk interest leh capital te hi an pe chhuak thin ani. Dan ang taka mipui hnena pawisa puk thei tur in leh advertisement te siam tur chuan engmah thunei tu lam atangin permit an nei lo.

 

                        An pawisa pukte hi affidavits siamin, an puksak tam dan a zir in an assets neihte dahkham ang rengin affidavits ah hian an ziak lang thin. An motor khalh tute tih loh ah chuan counter leh bungraw thawn tur lo ziak fel tur hian mi pathum chhawrin thlakhat ah Rs.2500/- an hlawh tir a, tunah hian Pu Khawnghinga In Zarkawt office atan an luah ani.
