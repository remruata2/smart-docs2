MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/97

Dated 9th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/94
Dt. 8.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

26 (twenty six) vehicles (HMV -9, MMV -15, LMV - 2) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 9.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV- Nil, MMV-11, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 9.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-11, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP was resumed  today i.e., 9.2.2021 by engaging one excavator (JCB). Pu H. Vanlalrova, Bairabi North VC Member and 2 (two) staff of P&E Department who conducted survey for erection of electric poles at the area were physically assaulted by residents of Assam near the area today's afternoon around 2:15 p.m. and they then immediately retreat towards Mizoram side for their personal safety. Due to the physical assault, Pu H. Vanlalrova complained of suffering pain on his back.

Upon learning the above incident, personnel of Zophai BOP who conducted patrolling near the area immediately rushed to the area and SDPO, Kolasib; OC, Bairabi PS and Party also immediately arrived at the spot to control further escalation of tension. OC, Ramnathpur PS and staff, Assam also arrived at the spot to control the situation. Around 45 (forty five) youths of Assam carrying bamboo sticks gathered at the area and similarly around 100 (one hundred) residents of Bairabi gathered at Mizoram side. Both parties pelted stone towards each other but no serious physical injury has been reported from either side. Security personnel are deployed at the area to control further escalation of tension, and the crowd from either side dispersed around 6:00 p.m. 1(one) platoon each of CRPF and IR Battalion have been rushed to the spot to control the situation

85(eighty five) vehicles (HMV-38, MMV-2, LMV-35, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-10) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 9.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

176(one hundred seventy six) vehicles (HMV-72, MMV-31, LMV-46, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-7, Oil Tanker-13, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 9.02.2021during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
