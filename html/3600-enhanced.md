A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.11 .11.2022_

****All public transport vehicles to follow government rates - STA**: The State Transport Authority (STA) of Mizoram instructed all the owners /drivers of public transport vehicles to follow the prescribed rates laid down by the government, failure of which will be violations of the laws. In the instruction, STA highlighted the agreement of all the holders of plying permit of public transport vehicles to comply to the rates fixed by the government, the route allotted to them and not to carry any illegal items. The STA further requested the public to give a report to any Transport Department officers of the state if they encounter any owner/driver of a vehicle who violated these terms as it can lead to suspension and to the extent of cancellation of their plying permits.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.11 .11.2022_

**Salaries of Government servants :******Government of Mizoram started giving out salaries of government servants (for the month of October 2022 ) starting from last evening (10.11.2022). Apart from the presently serving personnel of Group A,B,C,&amp; D, pensions of retired government servants were also released. Salaries of muster roll and provisional employees of the government had already been released previously in the beginning of this month (November).

C. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 11.11.2022_

****Guidlines for Retired Pastor/Minister :******Synod Executive Committee (SEC), one of the highest governing body under Mizoram Presbyterian Church , who was having a meeting recently at JM Loyd Hall, Synod Office, Aizawl discussed about the guidelines for retired Pastors entering politics and decided to leave the matter as one of its agenda for the upcoming Synod Conference (the apex body) which is scheduled to held next month. The same committee also entrusted five members to devise a scheme for promotion of integration amongst church members as well as to have good cooperation with other Church denominations. This meeting of SEC was commenced on 8th November 2022 and will be concluded on 11th November 2022.**
