A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.9.2013

Police Submitted Chargesheet on Laipuitlang Disaster: Police have submitted its report regarding the collapse of PWD building, Laipuitlang in the wee hour of 11th May, 2013; the collapsed building and landslide swept down several buildings/houses below it and claimed many lives, the report held the being suspended PWD CE Vanlalduhsaka and Contractor Lalhmasawna of Thenzawl Kanan Veng who was under contract to dismantle PWD building responsible.

            Raymond Lalbiakzama lodged an FIR at Bawngkawn Police Station on 14th May 2013; following the FIR Dy.SP Zoramsanga Ralte probed into the matter and submitted chargesheet to the Court on 23rd September, 2013.

ACB to Appeal to High Court: Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) will appeal to Gauhati High Court Aizawl Bench regarding the discharge of the case of formerAH&Vety. Director Dr.C.Sangnghina by Special Court which could not accept the Prosecution Sanction ACB produced.

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.9.2013

IPS Officers transferred: Union Home Ministry transferred two Mizo IPS officers Pu Zorammawia and Pu V.Lalthakima to Mizoram working in Delhi Government. Commissioner of Police (Armed) in Delhi.

 

D. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.9.2013

High Court Ordered Mizoram Government: Zofa Welfare Organisation had lodged an FIR against Assam as it laid a stone which appeared to be a boundary milestone in Vairengte rice field/farmland and for snatching teak from Bairabi more than 80 times. Zofa Welfare said that the Scheduled Caste And Scheduled Tribe (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989 is not practiced in Mizoram as such FIR they lodged was neglected. JAC was formed with other groups after the Mizoram did not take any initiative to their request to practice the act. JAC now made a complaint to National Commission for Scheduled Tribe and Gauhati High Court. High Court ordered the Mizoram Government to explain its reason for not using the act within 14 days on 24th September, 2013.
