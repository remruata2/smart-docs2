A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 28.11.2022

Proposal for increase in professional tax: Under the Chairmanship of Union Finance Minister, Pi Nirmala Sitharaman, meeting on Pre-Budget (2023 -24) was held on 25th November 2022 at New Delhi by Finance Ministers of  various states. In this meeting, Mizoram state, which was represented by Pu Lalchamliana, Taxation Minister proposed the raising of professional tax of government servants which has been fixed at Rs.2500 per annum to a higher level so as to increase the revenue of the state. Apart from this, the continuation of Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment (SASCI) which were extensively utilised for development works was also proposed for the year 2023-24. Pu Lalchamliana further expressed his desire for the creation of Special Assistance to States for Revenue Expenditure (SASRE) like the SASCI, for states with deficit in revenue expenditure where the loans granted to the states are to be repaid after a span of 50 years without any interest. Mizoram Taxation Minister was accompanied by Pu Vanlalchhuanga, Mizoram Finance Commissioner.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 28.11.2022

178 units of blood donated by 6 voluntary organisations: On 26th November 2022, 178 units of blood were donated in different parts of Mizoram by various organisations. KTP, Muallungthu Pastoral area donated 41 units (30 males and 11 females) at ZMC blood bank. 24 male members of Hunthar veng KTP Branch and 20 members of Ramhlun Venglai KTP Branch donated 24 units at Synod Hospital, Aizawl and 30 units at Civil Hospital, Aizawl respectively to marked their raising day anniversary. At Lunglei Civil Hospital, Adventist Youth Federation of Lunglei North & South donated 45 units of blood (26 males and 19 females) while Zohnuai YMA branch members also donated 15 units of blood at the same hospital (14 males and 1 female). Meanwhile, at Champhai District Hospital, 33 units of blood were donated by members of KTP, Champhai Zotlang North Branch (33 males and 3 females).

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 28.11.2022

Cigarettes  seized:  Champhai police on 26th November 2022 (Saturday) seized 201 cartons of illegally imported cigarettes at Farluichhuah  which were transported using two trucks by T.Lalbiakhlua (31) s/o Vanlalnghaka (L) and Lalpapuia (33) s/o Lalrinchhana (L) who are both a residents of Champhai Ruantlang. The owners of the illegal seized items were booked under appropriate section of law under COTPA.
