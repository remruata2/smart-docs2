MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/493

Dated 29th June, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/491
Dt. 28.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

           20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-13, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 29.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-3, LMV-19) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

45 (forty five) vehicles (HMV-31, MMV-Nil, LMV-8, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

Reference to yesterday report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/491
Dt. 28.06.2021, today i.e., 29.6.2021 @ 7:00 a.m. around 120 (one hundred twenty) Assam Police led by Mr. Nirmal Gosh, Dy. SP (Border), Hailakandi District visited the farm land own by Pu Ramhluna of Vairengte Chhim Veng, located at around 5 km North-West of Vairengte, inside Mizoram. Around 40 (forty) civilians from Dolchera/Balichuri, Assam also came to the spot.  Subsequently, Ms. Ramandeep Kaur, SP, Hailakandi; Mr. Jutin Moidori, SDO(Civil), Hailakandi; Mr. Montaz Ali, DFO, Hailakandi; Mr. K. Barman, Circle Officer also arrived at the spot.

On receiving information about their presence, Pu Isaac Lalrempuia, SDO(Civil), Vairengte; Pu Ramtharnghaka, SDPO Vairengte, leaders of Vairengte Thalai Pawl, Social Reconstruction Enlightment (SORE), MZP and MSU along with 14(fourteen) Mizoram Police personnel, 7(seven) CRPF personnel and 30(thirty) civilians from Vairengte immediately proceeded to the area. Mizoram officials met Assam officials at around 12:30 p.m. today and on being asked about the purpose of their visit, Assam officials informed them that they are conducting normal patrolling and intended to return after having lunch. SDO (Civil), Vairengte informed his counterpart SDO (Civil), Hailakandi the need to discuss the matter and invited him to discuss the matter at his Office Chamber. Pu Lallawmawma, Addl DC, Kolasib and Pu David J Byhnadaolai, Addl SP, Kolasib also arrived at the spot today @ 2:00 p.m. A brief meeting was held between officials of the two states and agreed to hold DC level meeting in the coming week to resolve the matter.

Both parties are still present at the area at the time of writing the report and as per latest input, Assam Police is intending to set up makeshift camp near
Pu Ramhluna’s farm land.

It was later found out today that the farm hut of Pu Chhandama, Vairengte Field Veng, located at around 1(one) km towards Vairengte from Pu Ramhluna’s land and some betel nut plantation were allegedly destroyed by Assam Police/Civilians in between 27.6.2021 @4:30 p.m. and today.

132 (one hundred thirty two) vehicles (HMV-64, MMV-19, LMV-29, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-1, Oil Tanker-17, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-11, LMV-16) entered Saiphai from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

          Situation is under close watch.
