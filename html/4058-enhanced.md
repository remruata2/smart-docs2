****SPECIAL REPORT****

 On Date 05.02.2024 at around 4:00 pm, telephonic information was received from SI K. Lalrinpuia, OC Sangau that the 42nd Assam Rifles, Sangau Post had intercepted one Bolero B/R No. MZ-01B-6369 driven by Pu Siamthanga (50) s/o Chhanerha of Sentetfiang village, Lawngtlai District along with his wife, Pi Chimengi (40) near Pangkhua village, Lawngtlai District and that they had seized the following items from the said Bolero at around 10:00 am today:-

1. Cordex – 3750 metres
2. Detonator – 1800 nos.
3. PEK – 1200 nos. (6 box)

Upon a brief interrogation with Siamthanga, he said that he was approached by one Myanmarese national named Pasuia of Selen village, Myanmar, in the morning of 05.02.2024, requesting them to carry some few parcels to Pangkhua village and deliver it to Pu Hmunsanga of Pangkhua. He alleged that he was given Rs. 1000 by Pasuia, as transportation charge which he accepted without knowing the content of the parcel. On their way to Pangkhua, they were intercepted by the Assam Rifles and were apprehended at around 10:00 am. Pu Siamthanga also alleged that the parcel was loaded to their vehicle at Sentetfiang from the residence of Bawihleiuka at Sentetfiang. He also said that he believed Pasuia to have personally bought the seized items at Aizawl, transported through Sumo to Siaha, and from Siaha he must have transported them with another Sumo.

 Sangau PS staff then apprehended Hmunsanga of Pangkhua village at 06:30pm the same evening, to whom the parcel is said to be delivered. They also apprehended Suichung aka Pasuia s/o Hrangthanga along with onother Myanmarese national named TunTun (32) s/o Chhanawl both belonging to Salen village, Myanmar. Upon interrogation, TunTun said that he is a member of PDF, and brought their organisation money, around Rs. 2 lakhs from Myanmar and sought the help of Suichung to acquire explosives at Aizawl. They both went to Aizawl during mid-January 2024 from Sangau to Aizawl. They lodged at the residence of one person named Rivunga, of Tuikual Veng, Aizawl. They purchased the explosives from one person whom he did not know his name at Chawnpui locality, Aizawl, and transported them to their lodging places by hiring a taxi. On Dt. 3/2/2024, they transported the explosives with Din Din Sumo, driven by Bawihleiuka from Aizawl to Sangau and

then to Sentetfiang. Then in the morning of 05.02.2024, at around 7-8:00 am, they loaded the explosives onto the Bolero at Sentetfiang to carry them till Pangkhuavillage.

 Case is being registered at Sangau PS with PS C/No. 3/24 u/s 4 of Explosives Substances Act r/w 34 IPC &amp; 14 Foreigners Act.

 The following persons involved in the case were detained at Sangau PS after medical examination is conducted at South Vanlaiphai PHC as there is no medical officer at Sangau PHC:-

1. Pu Siamthanga (50) s/oChhanerha of Sentetfiang, Lawngtlai District,
2. Pi Chimengi (40) w/o PuSiamthangaof Sentetfiang, Lawngtlai District,
3. Pu Hmunsanga (50) s/o T. Manghrina of Pangkhua, Lawngtlai District,
4. Suichung(50) aka Pasuia s/o Hrangthanga of Salen village, Myanmar,
5. Tun Tun (32) s/o Chhanawlof Salen village, Myanmar,

Further investigation is being carried out.
