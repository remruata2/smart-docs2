****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/113

Dated: 25th February, 2020.

  As per input, Misael Lalremruatpuia (13) s/o Vanlalmawia of W.Lungdar, Mamit District was alleged to have been abducted by 3 (three) unidentified non-Mizos and proceeded southward in a Bolero (white colour) yesterday, i.e., 24.2.2020 @ 3-4 p.m. As per the alleged statement of Misaela, he managed to escape at Ramlailui, which is approximately 2 kms away from the village while his abductors were answering the call of nature. The news went viral in various social media platforms last night.

 In response to the above alleged incident, YMA of surrounding villages were immediately on high alert and look out notice disseminated and duty post set up in various villages. Police also immediately sprung into action and proceeded to W.Lungdar early this morning, i.e., 25.2.2020 to verify the above alleged incident.

 Subsequently, this morning, i.e., 25.2.2020 @ 9:00 a.m, residents of S.Sabual, which is located approximately 35 kms South of W.Lungdar, apprehended the below noted 3 (three) non-Mizos on suspicious ground :

1. Rahul Chaudhary (27) s/o Sunil Chaudhary, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh.

2. Raja Mansury (38) s/o Jamal Mansury, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh.

3. Situ Hussain (21) s/o Sunahar Majundar, Silchar.

 As a result of the above, a team of police led by O/C Kulikawn PS immediately proceeded to S.Sabual. At the same time, the 3 (three) apprehended non-Mizos were being brought to W.Lungdar and they were made to be identified by Misaela. On confirming that the 3(three) are his abductors, the crowd suddenly turned violent and despite the best effort of the Police and community leaders, they managed to inflict physical injuries upon the 3 (three) persons. However, the police somehow managed to rescue them from the angry mob and they were immediately brought to Aizawl. The mob then set on fire a Bolero (black colour) B/R/No. AS 11D-7533 purportedly belonging to the 3 (three) persons. The above incident took place at around 5:00 p.m and re-inforcement of police force, including 2 (two) platoons of Armed personnel, which were sent from Aizawl could not reach W.Lungdar at the time of the incident.

 The above 3 (three) persons were brought to Aizawl Civil Hospital and are under medical examination at the time of writing this report.

 In this connection, Kulikawn PS Case No. 0/2020 dt. 25.02.2020 u/s 363/34 IPC was registered due to the alleged kidnapping of Misaela on 24.02.2020. Also, W.Phaileng PS Case No. 3/2020 dt. 25.02.2020 u/s 120B/323/341/342/435 IPC was registered due to incident of physical assault, wrongful restraint, mischief by fire etc. of the above three (3) persons today.

 Situation under close watch.
