**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.13.2.2013:**

****744 foreigners came in Mizoram in 2012 – ‘Visitors/Tourist do no increase due to relaxation of PAP’****:** 744 foreigners comprising 480 male and 264 female were registered at Foreigner Registration office by CID(SB) last year i.e., 2012, which is more in number by 87 compared to 657 foreigners who came in 2011, most of the foreigners were American. As per the record of Foreigner Registration office, 138 Americans, 104 British and 78 Australians visited Mizoram in 2012. However, workers at Foreigner Registration office reported that there were certain foreigners who came to Mizoram but did not report/register themselves to the Foreigner Registration office.**

 In January this year, foreigners who came to Mizoram and registered at Foreigner Registration office were 71; 48 male and 23 female, most of them were American, followed by Australian and British.

 The purpose of their visit is mainly religious purposes, there are not much tourists, and some of them came due to government or company’s work, said Foreigner Registration office.

 Protected Area Permit (PAP) was temporarily relaxed from 1st Jan. 2011 but still Mizoram does not have much more visitors from foreign country, said Foreigner Registration office.
