A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.1.2014_

****Relatives of the abducted on hunger strike****:** Families and relatives of Lalzamliana, Kolasib who was kidnapped on 23 Nov 2013 by Bru armed gang gathered on the street of Kolasib DC office at 9:00am yesterday morning to start hunger strike.**

Lalzamliana’s relatives came with beddings and intend to remain on the street of Kolasib DC office until the abducted are safely released. They also demanded Mizoram government to act faster in rescuing the abducted. Some children came along with them.

Jitender Yadav, DC Kolasib visited and met the people on hunger strike and promised to bring the matter to the Home Department as they requested him. He also asked them to disperse and not go on with their hunger strike. Chief Secretary also requested them to do the same but they were still there when this news was written.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 21.1.2014_

**Many Brus fled to Tripura****:** Some Bru families in Tumpanglui and New Eden fled their villages on Sunday for Tripura as the consequence of three people abducted by Bru armed gang; the number of Brus who fled is not verified yet. According to heads of Relief Department of Tripura as reported by Tripura media 368 Bru families with their 2,423 members came to Tripura. But Mizoram police said that could not be true.****
