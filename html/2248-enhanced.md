****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/285

Dated 12th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/278 Dt. 09.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-19, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 12.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

25 (twenty five) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-22, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 12.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-7, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

89 (eighty nine) vehicles (HMV-55, MMV-1, LMV-26, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 12.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

105 (one hundred five) vehicles (HMV-52, MMV-5, LMV-27, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-22, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 12.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 VLTF of Saiphai issued restriction for crossing of state border due to recent surge in Covid-19. Situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
