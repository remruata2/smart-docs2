****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/759

Dated 30th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/754 Dt. 29.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

6 (six) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-1, LMV-1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 30.12.2020 @ 3:30 p.m. 3 (three) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 3, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV – 7, MMV – 6, LMV - 1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 30.12.2020 @ 3:30 p.m. 15 (fifteen) vehicles (HMV-1, LMV-1, MMV–13) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 51 (fifty one) vehicles (HMV-21, MMV – 24, LMV – 1, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 30.12.2020 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 30.12.2020.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 176 (one hundred seventy six) vehicles (HMV – 98, MMV – 21, LMV –24, LPG – 5, Maxi Cab – 2, Oil Tanker – 26, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 30.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
