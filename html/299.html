EXAMINATION OF CREDENTIALS OF V.MALSAWMDAWNGLIANA, H/NO.E-58, CHANMARI, V.NEIHKUNGA BUILDING

 

Mr. V Malsawmdawngliana is a reputed businessman who runs the Modicare Service centre, located at Zarkawt near KFC, as well as being Dalmia Cement Dealer and distributor of Nokia phones. The Modicare service centre is the main office of Modicare in Aizawl where new products are introduced and conference are held for the various consultants of Modicare. He being an experience businessman was introduced to Modicare by his friend from Nagaland who is also a Nokia distributor there in 2012. Since then he has been running the Modicare service centre as well as the warehouse for stocking all the products of Modicare at their office at Zarkawt. But from 2018 the volume of Modicare products expanded rapidly due to the massive increase in demand for the company's product in Mizoram and it became difficult to adequate both the service centre and the warehouse in one location, so the company decided it was necessary to find another partner to be in charge of stocking product and running a warehouse in Aizawl.

Therefore, in 2018, the company made an agreement with TVS Logistics, which is a third party logistics company to locate a suitable warehouse and to hire a necessary manpower and take up stocking of products and from June 2019, Mr. V Malsawmdawngliana was relieved of the responsibility of stocking the product. He now runs the Modicare service centre alone at his store at Zarkawt.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

EXAMINATION OF CREDENTIALS OF TVS LOGISTICS SERVICES, 1ST FLOOR, CHALTLANG, SAIRANG ROAD, OPPOSITE ZODIN PLASTIC INDUSTRY

 

Mr. Lalremsiama Pachuau is the operational manager for TVS Logistics Service Limited having their office at the house of Mr. Rinsanga, IAS Retd, Chanmari, Section1 . TVS Logistics was formerly known as Drive India Enterprises under TATA Company. It was sold by TATA company to TVS Logistics and the name was changed to TVS Logistics Services. The name has now again been changed to TVS Supply Chain Solution Ltd. It is a third party company here in Aizawl. The company has working partnership not only with Modicare but also with AVON, AMWAY and Wonder chef.

Negotiations between Modicare and TVS Logistics began in 2018 when the volume for demand of Modicare product increased significantly in Mizoram. Since then, Mr. Lalremsiama was intimated by the company to allocate a suitable warehouse in Aizawl which would meet the requirement of the company. After searching around for a year, a satisfactory location was found at Chatlang, Sairang Road opposite Zodin Plastic Industry. So, from June 2019, this location came to be used as the main warehouse for stocking all the Modicare products coming to Aizawl.

Presently there are around 32 Modicare dealer points in different parts of Mizoram. The owners of these dealer points play their order online and the products are delivered from the warehouse by TVS Logistics to their respective locations. The payments for these orders are also made online by the dealer points. The dealer points in Aizawl make their order online and either collects the products from the warehouse themselves or they have it delivered.

It is understood that TVS Logistics are only concerned with receiving and stocking of Modicare products in the warehouse and sending the products to the various dealer points as per the order that is received
