SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/381

Dated: 14th May, 2021     

 

All NGOs of Champhai held a meeting today, i.e., 14.5.2021 under the aegis of Sub-Headquarters YMA Champhai @ 11:00 a.m. at MJA Press Club, Bethel Veng, Champhai in connection with obstructions and difficulties faced by workers engaged in construction of NH-6 (Seling to Champhai) Double Lane at Khawzawl area. The meeting was chaired by Pu F. Lalrinmuana, President, SYMA Champhai and 42 (forty two) persons representing 16 (sixteen) NGOs, such as, SYMA, NGOCC, MZP, MJA, EXIM, etc. were present.

The meeting expressed their firm resolve to face and remove any difficulties and obstacles that occurred in the way of construction of NH-6 as per Road Alignment already approved by Central Government, MoRTH and Government of Mizoram. The meeting accordingly issued a Statement which highlighted the following resolutions :-

  1. People of eastern Mizoram will face any problems faced by road construction workers in support of the government.
  2. Will promptly respond to any difficulties faced by construction workers resulting in stoppage of work irrespective of time of occurrence.
  3. People of Champhai, especially the youths, should be in readiness at all times to respond to any situation that calls for action.
  4. It is the responsibility of people of Champhai district to support the decision of the government as road project has already been approved by the Central Government and State Government.
  5. Informed those who are blocking movement of workers and vehicles transporting machineries for road construction to cease their obstructive activities and to dismantle the Gate constructed for the above purpose before the end of 17.5.2021. In the event of failure to comply, people of eastern range will take necessary action as deemed fit and necessary.

 

In this connection, around 15 (fifteen) leaders of Champhai NGOCC proceeded to Khawzawl at 2:30 p.m. and lodged an FIR at Khawzawl PS in the name of Secretary Champhai District NGO Coordination Committee, requesting to take necessary legal action against person(s) who caused impediment to road construction work and to dismantle Gate built at Puilo junction (inside Kawlkulh town) before the end of 17.5.2021.

Tuipuiral Group YMA also issued a Statement yesterday, i.e., 13.5.2021 wherein it expressed their full support to steps taken by the government for carrying out smooth and speedy construction works of NH-6 (Seling to Champhai). It also expressed their desire that no action should be undertaken which affect the already laid out plan.

 

Recapping recent developments in this connection, Kawlkulh NGO Co-ordination Committee (NGOCC) held a meeting in connection with NH-6 road upgradation between Dulte to Champhai road (Kawlkulh By-Pass road) on 6.5.2021 @ 7:30 p.m. at Kawlkulh Town Hall. The meeting highlighted their opposition to the proposed NH-6 road up-gradation between Dulte to Champhai as it will By-pass Kawlkulh Village and decided to take necessary action so as to re-align the said road in order to pass through their Village. The meeting resolved the following :-

1. Gate should be constructed at Puilo road Junction, Kawlkulh in order to block movement of all workers/machineries of road construction Company w.e.f., 7.5.2021.

2. To bring their decision/demand to the notice of Transport Minister and authority of NHIDCL.

3. Fencing should be erected below Dulte Village where the proposed road started deviating from the existing road and entrusted NGOs Kawlkulh to take necessary action in this regard.

 

Pursuant to the above decision, a wooden gate was built at Puilo road Junction, Kawlkulh on 7.5.2021 in order to obstruct Heavy Vehicles from proceeding towards Puilo Village side.

 

In connection with the above, a meeting was convened under the chairmanship of DC, Khawzawl on 7.5.2021 @ 2:00 p.m. in which SP, Khawzawl and 8 (eight) VC members of Kawlkulh Village attended the meeting. During the meeting, VC members of Kawlkulh stated that their demand for proposed NH-6 road up-gradation (between Dulte to Champhai) to pass through their village/town was already brought to the notice of higher authorities viz., Chief Minister of Mizoram, Authorities of NHIDCL and PWD on several occasions, but the outcome of their demand is not known till date. However, Dhariwal Buildtech Pvt. Ltd. (Contractor of the proposed road) has recently started their construction work at Puilo and such being

the case, they intended to protest the said road construction work. The officials (DC, Khawzawl & SP, Khawzawl) requested them not to take further actions without knowing the final decision of the state Government or concern authority. They also requested VC, Kawlkulh to dismantle the newly constructed gate at Puilo junction.

 

On 11.5.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. another meeting was again convened by Pu Zothanmawia, DC Khawzawl and Pu PC Lalchunglura, SP Khawzawl, with leaders of  Kawlkulh VC, YMA, MHIP, MUP, Kawlkulh Thalai Pawl and VC and YMA of Puilo Village at Khawzawl DC Conference Hall. During the meeting, the officials requested leaders of Kawlkulh NGOs to dismantle temporary gate constructed at Puilo Junction so as to enable free passage of all vehicles and not to further obstruct construction activities of the said road. They also requested them to allow free passage of machineries (which will be engaged for road constructions work) presently en-route to Puilo Village.

 

The same evening i.e., 11.5.2021, a meeting was convened by  Kawlkulh NGO Coordination Committee(NGOCC) at Kawlkulh with leaders of Puilo VC and YMA. Leaders of Puilo VC and YMA requested leaders of Kawlkulh NGOCC to allow free passage of machinery and to dismantle temporary gate constructed at Puilo Junction. However, leaders of Kawlkulh NGOs declined their request.

 

However, on the same evening of 11.5.2021, heavy machinery of DBL Company arrived at Puilo Village without any obstruction. In this connection, OBs of Kawlkulh NGO Coordination Committee held a meeting on 12.5.2021 @ 7:30 p.m. and the meeting expressed regret and disappointment over dismantling of gate constructed at Puilo Junction on the evening of 11.5.2021 and decided to find out the person(s) responsible for the said act. The meeting also warned that if there is any re-occurrence of any act of defiance of Joint NGOCC decision or commencement of construction of by-pass road work in future, villages where DBL Company is stationed will be held responsible, and Kawlkulh and Dulte Village will take necessary action against them.

 

In opposition of the action taken by NGOCC Kawlkulh, NGO Co-ordination Committee (NGOCC), Champhai issued a Press Release on 12.5.2021 wherein it expressed regret and condemned the action of some
