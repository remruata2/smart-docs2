1. _Front Page of Vanglaini Daily Newspaper as on 09.09.2022_

**MHIP, requested those who appear naked on social media to repent:**

MHIP Ramlai Block Conference was held at Thenzawl on Thursday, in which Pi Ngurmawii Sailo, President, MHIP Gen. Hqrs Mizoram, attended the Conference and emphasize the importance and responsibilities of women in the society. On behalf of MHIP, she requested mizo women to refrain from posting naked pictures on social media. There are 15 MHIP branches under Ramlai Block.
