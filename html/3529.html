A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post /Second page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 31.08.2022

Fines for violations of Building Regulations increased: In a sensitization programme on Swatchh Bharat Mission (Urban) organised on Dt.30.8.2022 for Councillors of Aizawl Municipal Corporation (AMC), Pu Lalrinenga Sailo,  Aizawl Mayor announced the increased of Fines for violations of Building Regulations of AMC by tenfold of the existing amount. Previously, violators of the regulations whose buildings were constructed beyond the limits of their permits were often simply fined without revoking their permits. However, from now on, violators of AMC Regulations will be liable with a punishment of fines along with an imprisonment which is the maximum punishment, the Mayor added. This decision was approved by  the meeting of The Executive Council of AMC held on 29th August 2022.
