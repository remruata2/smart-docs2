****REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/580

Dated:18th October, 2020

** In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/579 Dt. 17.10.2020, Shri. Anbamuthan M.P, IAS, DC, Karimganj and Shri. Mayank Kumar, SP Karimganj visited Zaite Village, Assam today i.e., 18.10.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. and conducted site inspection to explore possibility of renovation of road between Zaite Village, Assam to Thinghlun, Mizoram (fair weather road) allegedly owing to visit of the area by Assam government officials in the coming days. During their visit, DC, Karimganj and SP, Karimganj expressed their intention to **deploy Assam Police at Segontillah Hill peak (Teak Tlang) near Zaite Village, Assam**from where Mizoram Police Camp was visible and instructed Zaite Village Headman to clear up the required land. Both DC and SP, Karimganj left Zaite Village around 4:00 p.m todayâ€™s evening. Further, today around 4:10 p.m. ASP (Border), Assam and a section of Armed and Unarmed Assam Police conducted foot patrolling at Assam-Mizoram border near Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Thinghlun and left the area around 4:25 p.m. Upon hearing news of their visit, around 300 (three hundred) members of Langkaih Group YMA and MZP assembled at Zaite Road, Thinghlun near newly established Mizoram Police Camp and they dispersed afterwards under peaceful atmosphere.**

Further, Langkaih Group YMA meeting held today i.e., 18.10.2020 @ 1:00 p.m. decided to deploy duty from their members to assist Mizoram Police at Thinghlun and Medili Bridge but no deployment has been made at the time of writing the report i.e., 18.10.2020 @ 7:30 p.m.

 9 (nine) leaders of MZP General Headquarters led by Pu Andrew F. Lalramnghaka, Treasurer proceeded to Thinghlun today i.e., 18.10.2020 @ 5:30 p.m.

 Situation is under close watch.
