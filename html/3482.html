A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 24.3.2014

Bomb Blast: On Saturday morning at around 3:50am, an explosive suspected to be gelatin was exploded at the verandah of Chhinlung Hills Development Council Chairman Hmingchungnunga’s House at Bungkawn Vengthar. The main door and several window panes were smashed under the impact. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. Police are investigating the case to find out the culprit.

 

* All the local daily newspaper highlighted the safe release of Deep Mandal who was kidnapped on 23.11.2013 by Bru armed gang.
