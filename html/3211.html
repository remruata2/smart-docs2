A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 18.7.2013

122 candidates for VC By-Election in 15 villages: Nomination papers for the VC by-election of 15 villages were checked yesterday. After withdrawal period, there are 122 candidates to contest for 57 seats.

As Hmar Welfare Committee’s request caused ruffle in some villages, State Election Commission extended time to file nomination in 5 villages; and 10 more nominations were filed in Aizawl District while 11 nominations were withdrawn. No changes were made in Kolasib District. So, 122 candidates will contest for 57 seats in 15 villages.

 

B. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 18.7.2013

Non-Mizo shops closed down in Lawngtlai: A truck driven by a non-Mizo crashed into a couple walking on the road side at 2:00 PM on 15th July at Lawngtlai. Regarding this Lai Students’ Association made an announcement that shops in Lawngtlai owned by non-Mizo be closed for today i.e, 18th July.

     The victims are from Lawngtlai College Veng and the husband RK Thankhuma sustained serious injuries, he was referred to Lunglei Civil Hospital where he died from his injuries. Sahab Uddin, the driver of the truck from Hailakandi District, Assam ran away but later surrendered himself to Lawngtlai Police on 17th July at around 9:00 AM. He said the mishap was caused by the failure of his truck’s brake. He is now detained in District Jail.
