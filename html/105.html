Brief note on PCJSS is as under:

 

Shanti Bahini (Peace Corps) is the name of armed wing of the Parbatya Chattagram Jana Samhati Samiti (PCJSS) meaning United People’s Party of the Chittagong Hill Tracts. It is considered an insurgent group in Bangladesh. The Shanti Bahini is made out of mostly member from the Chakma tribe. The Chittagong Hill Tracts Peace Accord was signed between Bangladesh Government and PCJSS on 2nd Dec. 1997.

 

The PCJSS accused Bangladesh Government that the peace accord agreement are yet to be implemented despite more than 20 years had lapsed since the signing of the Peace Accord. Hence, their armed wing is still in operation and some of their armed wings are in operation from Mizoram. Reliable information has also been received that the Shanti Bahini might attack Bangladesh army and Muslim civilians at any time in Bangladesh.
