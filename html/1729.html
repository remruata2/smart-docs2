i) Enforcement of internal security measures :

            During the lockdown period of 2020 and 2021 due to the pandemic covid-19,civil volunteers, designated by the government as Local Level Task Force(LLTF) in the city and Village Level Task Force (VLTF) in the rural areas have been successfully employed for the implementation of the curfew promulgation. The contribution of these LLTF/VLTF members is invaluable as most of the members  are derived from the locally elected councils and prominent voluntary organisations who are respected by the society. These same volunteer forces could be utilise  to a considerable extend, for assisting the police forces, who will be stretch to their limit in any war like situation which may arise with any foreign nations.

            The office of CID(SB) is currently maintaining list of persons on the basis of section-I of Chapter III of Union War Book which are reviewed after every six months in consultation with other sister intelligence agencies  operating in the state.

 

ii) Protection of  'Prohibited/Protected places and regulated areas :

            Owing to the lack of infrastructures in the state, Mizoram police are not heavily engaged in the securities of vital installations. Most of the responsibilities of the state police includes sensitive government offices, VIPs, residences and bungalows of  various dignitaries of the state.

 

iii) Duties and responsibilities in connection with civil defence :

            A bomb disposal squad of Mizoram police is operating under SP Security. This squad may be employed for defusing and disposing any un exploded bombs or shell whenever they may be discovered.

 

iv) Maintanance of Liaision with the army :

            Even during peace time, police-army joint operation are often conducted in the state in tackling unwanted elements, smugglers and the likes. While planning and discussions are conducted smoothly at the senior officers rank, one hitch which often surface in the actual ground level is the language barrier. Mizoram being a remote state is having a very low percentage of Hindi speaking persons and so is the case in the state police forces. Emphasis may be given to applicants who are fluent in Hindi while conducting recruitment.

v) Implementation of defence of India Rules and other notifications:

            No comments.

 

vi) Implementation of coastal security measures and treatment of emergency shipping:

            Mizoram is a landlocked state with no access to the coast line. Hence, this point may not be applicable for the state. Few of the navigable rivers are meant for small patrol boats only.

 

vii) Duties in connection with control of civil aerodromes and civil aircrafts:

            Apart from the only airport located at Lengpui which is under Department of civil Aviation of the state, there are no other civil aerodromes inside the state which are worth mentioning.

 

viii) Restriction on movements of aliens and other persons :

            The office of SP(SB) have been maintaining records/lists of all foreign nationals entering inside Mizoram. Not only this, it is mandatory for even the non tribal citizens of India to obtain Inner Line Permit from the state government before entering the state. These passes are thoroughly checked on all the entry/exit points of the state border while at the same time conducting frequent checking amongst the non-native persons (excluding government employees) staying in the state.

 

ix) Censorship measures :

            Today all individuals are connected to each other through a platform called social media, in other words, the internet. A branch of police dealing with crimes, fake rumours, deceptions, defamation etc. through the internet are functioning under SP(Crime).This branch, with up-gradation in equipment and manpower may be an instrument for censoring in the social media.
