****REPORT OF FIRE INCIDENT AT DC OFFICE, AIZAWL ON 17.12.2021****

 In the early morning of 17.12.2021 around 4:10 AM, a telephonic information was received at Aizawl Police Station that a fire broke out at the office complex of Deputy Commissioner, Aizawl Office (Pisapui) located at Treasury Square, Aizawl. The fire was firstly spotted by one Armed personnel deployed as Treasury Strong room duty. Upon receiving the information, Police party immediately rushed to the spot and Fire tenders were called to help. Serious efforts were made to put out the fire. However, since the fire had already spread beyond control on their arrival, all they could do was to try and contain further spreading to nearby buildings. Unfortunately, several office buildings inside the DC office Complex were destroyed. The fire was subsequently kept under control at around 8:30 AM.

1. The buildings which were burned down are :

- Main office building
- Election office building
- Record Room building
- One building occupied by Nazir, Planning, Arms, Judicial and ILP branches

1. The following Office Branches under DC Aizawl Office which were completely destroyed by the fire are as follows :

- Election Branch
- Judicial Branch
- Planning Branch
- Arms Branch
- ILP Branch
- Census Branch
- Establishment Branch
- Nazir Branch
- Stationery Branch

1. The pecuniary lost suffered as a result of this unfortunate incident could not be fully estimated as of now.
2. Valuable and important documents of various branches of DC, Aizawl Office were destroyed by the fire.
3. The cause of fire is being investigated and not yet established.

****(C.LALRUAIA)****
