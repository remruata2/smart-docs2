REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT

 

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/566

Dated:13thOctober,2020   

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/563 Dt. 12.10.2020 around 60 (sixty) volunteers from MZP Headquarters, Zawlnuam, Tuidam Sub-Headquarters, Kanhmun Sub-Headquarters and leaders of MZP General Headquarters who are remained at Zawlnuam after their visit of Thinghlun participated in the community work organized today i.e., 13.10.2020 for reconstruction of Pu John Zolawma’s Jhum Hut at Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border which was burnt down by Assam Police and Forest Department Officials on 9.10.2020.

Further, MZP General Headquarters issued a notice today i.e., 13.10.2020 which stated their intention to organize a community work at Thinghlun on 14.10.2020 (tomorrow) for reconstruction of Jhum Hut which was burnt down by Assam Officials. It also stated that those who intended to participate in the said community work must bring bedding and available house construction tools, and they will depart from MZP Office Treasury Square tomorrow morning i.e., 14.10.2020 @ 6:00 a.m. As per input around 50 (fifty) volunteers intended to participate in the said community work.

Today i.e., 13.10.2020 at around 4:00 p.m. around 14 (fourteen) Assam Police and Forest Department Officials came to Zaite Village, Assam by 2 (two) LMVs and conducted foot patrolling near Assam-Mizoram border but they did not make any verbal or physical contact with Mizoram Police currently stationed at the area, and they returned back to Assam around 5:00 p.m.

In connection with the incident, DC, Mamit and DC, Karimganj will hold a meeting tomorrow i.e., 14.10.2020 1:00 p.m. at BNRGSK Hall, Kanhmun, Mamit District. Further, Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IPS, DIG (NR) will proceed to Kanhmun tomorrow morning to take part in the said meeting.

            Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
