MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/112

Dated 15th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/110
Dt. 14.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :

28 (twenty eight) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-12, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 15.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 3 (three) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-8, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 15.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 9 (nine) vehicles (MMV-9, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/110
Dt. 14.02.2021 residents of Ramnathpur, Gharmura and Jalnacherra, Hailakandi District Assam organized a procession under the aegis of  Forum Against Mizo Aggression (FAMA) yesterday, i.e., 14.2.2021 @ 2:30 p.m. and around 500 (five hundred) volunteers participated. The procession started at Ramnathpur, Assam and they reached upto Mandir Tlang, near Bicherra Assam Police Camp, and dispersed the same evening around 6:00 p.m. without any untoward incident. Information received indicated that leaders of FAMA and leaders of other Muslim Associations and various Business Associations could not come into agreement regarding imposition of economic blockage. Officials of FAMA intended to hold talks with officials of Assam on the matter.

73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-31, MMV-2, LMV-31, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 15.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

 

 

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

146 (one hundred forty six) vehicles (HMV-57, MMV-36, LMV-25, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-6, Oil Tanker-19, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 15.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
