A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.2.2014_

**Suspected Aizawl Bomb Blaster Arrested; Two HPC(D) Cadres Surrendered to Government of Mizoram****:**Five (5) cadres of HPC(D) namely; 1) S/S Sergeant Major Anthony Lalwelcome (35) s/o Darrosanga of Ngampabung, 2) John Lalnunsanga (23) s/o Salsawma of Parvachawm, 3) Rualneilal (23) s/o Tlansiama of Lungthulian, 4) Ngamlianvel (30) s/o Zakungliana of Parvachawm, 5) Fimthanruma 930) s/o Fimzarlian of Thenzawl, all from Manipur were arrested on 19.2.2014 at around 3:30am by a joint operation team of Mizoram Police and 35th Assam Rifles at Parvachawm, Manipur. The 5 cadres were arrested in connection with the bomb blast at Treasury Square Bazar between Assembly House and Information &amp; Public Relations Directorate, Aizawl on 30.1.2014. The arrested HPC(D) cadres were handed over to Manipur Police as they were arrested inside Manipur.

 300 rounds of AK-47, 180 rounds of other weapons/guns, 3 hand grenades, 8 ammunitions box, 1 cap, 5 pair of shoes and letter head of HPC(D) were recovered from them.

 Meanwhile, two (2) HPC(D) cadres came from Manipur and surrendered to SP(CID), Mizoram recently.
