**Hmar People’s Convention (Democrats)**

1.The Hmar communities who settled in the North Eastern parts of Mizoram have been demanding a separate Autonomous District Council for more than 2 (two) decades by taking up arms.

2.A peace accord was signed way back in 1994 between Government of Mizoram and HPC, the Hmar militant group.

3. The HPC-D was formed in 1996 by remnants of the HPC who were disgruntled with the MoU signed between HPC and Govt. of Mizoram in 1994.

4.The HPC (D) resorted to criminal activities like kidnapping, collecting donations, extortion, blasting of bomb and threats in Sinlung Hills Development Council Areas.They even hampered the proper functioning of our Democracy by intimidating the Hmar community not to participate in the Village Council election of Mizoram-2013 and even intimidated voters during the Mizoram Legislative Assembly Election-2013.

 5.The HPC-D had split into two factions on 29.9.2011. One faction is led by the outset President Lalhmingthanga Sanate and the other faction led by H. Zosangbera. HPC-D H. Zosangbera faction is more active and reportedly now in possession of 34 nos. of arms of different types and 950 ammunitions.

 6.The Government of Mizoram and HPC –D (Zosangbera faction) held the first round of Peace talk on 11.11.2010 and subsequently on 31.1.2013,18.7.2013 and 14.8.2013.However, no breakthrough had so far been achieved.

 7. Steps are being taken to declare HPC-D (Zosangbera faction) as unlawful organization under Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act-1967.
