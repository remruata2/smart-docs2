A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Murderer(s) not yet arrested: Lunglei Police has not yet arrested any person involving in the cruel murder of 3 members of a family in their house at Tawipui North on 3rd September, 2013. Meanwhile, people of Tawipui vehemently condemned the murder and to show their condemnation they will have a procession on Friday ie., 6th September 2013 at Tawipui village.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Traffic Police notice: Aizawl Traffic SP made a notice that the area of Bawngkawn to Sairang road, Fishery veng, Chaltlang North around the old/unused quarry is not safe for people and vehicles as such no vehicle is allowed to be parked along the roadside of this area, and parking vehicles overnight is not allowed.

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Police wants Excise’s salary: Mizoram Police Subordinate Officers’ Association (MPSOA) requested the State Government to give Police constable the amount of salary taken by constable of Excise & Narcotics. MPSOA also said it will not file any case regarding their request to the Government to withdraw its decision of appointing Police Inspector as gazette status without MPSOA’s request. MPSOA said that it has full faith in the words of Home Minister regarding the issue.

 

D. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Attempted rapist arrested: Biakkama s/o Chhanlinga of Thlengang village a labour wood cutter working at Hlumte village was arrested by the police and sent to District Jail Lunglei as he was alleged of attempting to rape a girl at Hlumte village.

 

F. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Election Training: Chief Electoral office, Mizoram held a one day State level election training programme for sectoral magistrates and police officers at Synod Conference Centre for the fourth time as per the instructions of Election Commission of India. Around 180 designated sectoral magistrates and police officers attended the training programme. Jitendar Yadav, Asst. Commissioner Aizawl, Dr. Andrew H.Vanlaldika, Addl.DC Lunglei and James Lalnunmawia, SDO(S) Champhai gave lecture on this training.

 

E. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Stolen Maxi Cab accident: Tuipui D Darzo Maxi Cab service from Sangau to Aizawl was stolen on Tuesday night. This stolen Maxi Cab met with an accident between S.Vanlaiphai and Sangau. Lalengzuala (18) and Ramdinthara (19) of Tuipui ‘D’ alleged of the crime were arrested by YMA and brought to Hnahthial Police Station. The Maxi Cab is owned by Lalruatpuia of Cheural.

 

G. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.9.2013

Champhai to protest: Govt. Champhai College Students’ Union intends to organise procession in Champhai town on 6th September 2013 due to the shortage of faculty in their college.
