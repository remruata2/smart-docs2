ESTIMATED EXPENDITURE FOR THE AMRS LAYING CEREMONY OF HPC(D) AT CTI, SESAWNG:

In order to ensure a smooth conduct of the Arms Laying Ceremony of HPC (D) at CTI, Sesawng on 12th April 2018, a team of CID (SB) officers led by Pu Malsawma Royte conducted spot verification of CTI, Sesawng where the HPC (D) cadres numbering over 100 (one hundred) are to be accommodated for around 15 (fifteen) days. The reports of the inspecting team submitted to the Superintendent of Police, CID (SB) indicated that the following expenditures will have to be incurred by the State Government in connection with the Arms Laying Ceremony and during the course of their (HPC (D) Cadres) repatriation into a normal civil life.

  1. Strength /Numbers of HPC (D) cadres to be repatriated: 104 (one hundred and four).
  2. Transportation Cost (Zilthawchhuah to Vaitin):  Zilthawchhuah to Vaitin Road which is around 14 (fourteen) kms is in a bad condition and found not conducive for plying of Buses. Therefore, 407 Truck will have to be deployed for transportation of HPC (D) cadres and 407 Truck will have to be sent 4 times to Zilthawchhuah from Vaitin Village for the said purpose.
  3. CTI, Sesawng has 2 (two) Barracks, viz., Darfeng Barrack & Band Barrack.  Around 80 (eighty) persons can be accommodated at Darfeng Barrack and around 40 (forty) persons can be accommodated at Band Barrack.  Electric wiring/fitting is needed in Band Barrack.
  4. Darfeng Barrack has 6 (six) nos. of toilet (Pit Latrine) but Band Barrack has no toilet and is needed to make/construct at least 4 (four) nos. of new toilet.
  5. There is no good water tank to stock the water, 2 (two) water tank (Sintex) is needed to stock the water.
  6. 1 (one) Tank Lorry is available to collect water and then necessary POL is needed for this Vehicle.
  7. As information received from Centre Commander, CTI, due to scarcity of fire woods, not less than 5 (five) nos. of Commercial Gas Cylinder and 10 (ten) nos. of Domestic Gas Cylinder is needed for cooking at the Kitchen.
