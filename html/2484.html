MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/657

Dated 18th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/655
Dt. 17.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-3) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 18.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 16(sixteen) vehicles transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

Today i.e., 18.11.2020 locals from border villages were allowed to cross the border to attend their farm works. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

46(forty six) vehicles (HMV-23, LMV-23) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 18.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

Funeral of the dead body of Lalsiamthara (2) s/o C. Zothanmawia of Vairengte Venglai was held today i.e., 18.11.2020 @ 2:30 p.m. after conducting PME at Vairengte PHC. Vairengte Police Station registered a case vide C/No. 38/20 DT. 13.11.2020 u/s 361 IPC regarding his missing and there is no report of law and order situation.

218(two hundred eighteen) vehicles (HMV-94, MMV-68, LMV-34,
Bus-1, Maxi Cab- 4, LPG-4, Oil-13) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 18.11.2020 @ 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

          Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
