****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/48

Dated 21st January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/46 Dt. 20.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

6 (six) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 4, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 21.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 4 (four) vehicles (MMV – 4) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

8 (eight) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 6, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 21.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 4 (four) vehicles (MMV – 3, LMV -1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 78 (seventy eight) vehicles (HMV-41, MMV – 3, LMV – 29, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 21.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
