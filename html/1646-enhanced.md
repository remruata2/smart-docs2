Sir,

 With reference to your letter no. cited above, I am furnishing the required information so far available on record under this unit as follows:

**a) Mizoramah hel pawl an awm em? An awm chuan eng pawl te nge?**

**Ans:** Mizoram chhungah hian hel pawl an awm lo.

**b) Hel pawl te hian sorkarah demand an nei em? An neih chuan eng nge?**

**Ans:** Manipur-a inkulh Hmar hel, Hmar Peoples' Convention (Democratic) ( HPC(D)) te hian Mizoram hmar lam khaw thenkhatah Autonomous District Council din an rawn ngiat ve tawp a ni.

**c) Hel pawl thil demand hi sorkarin tihhlawhtlin a tum em?**

**Ans:**  Hmar Peoples' Convention (Democratic) ( HPC(D)) thil demand ber Autonomous District Council pek chu sawrkarin a tum lo.

**d) Kum 2012-2016 chhungin hel pawl avangin harsatna engte nge tawh anih? Mi engzatin nge hel ho kutah hian nunna chan?**
