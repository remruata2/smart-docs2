A. Front page of  VANGLAIN  newspaper  Dt. 07.10.2022

Mizoram the top in utilising PMJVK for Sports : Among all the states in India, Mizoram  occupied the top spot in having the highest number of sport projects under Pradhan mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK). 63 projects out of the 70 projects under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme PMJKV in Mizoram were sports projects, while the remaining 7 were -  3 common service centre projects and 4 hostel projects. For the year 2020-21,the central government had approved projects from 15 states under PMJKV where Arunachal Pradesh is the only other state to have sport projects under this scheme which are two in numbers. From the North Eastern States, Sikkim is having the highest number of projects which is 537 in total followed by Manipur with 81 approved projects. West Bengal, with 3,506 projects is the state with the highest in the whole of the country and  toilet, drinking water facilities and additional classroom are included in their approved projects.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.07.10.2022

ZMC Bye-Laws approved:  A meeting of the Governing Council of Zoram Medical College (ZMC) was held yesterday at Chief Minister's Office conference hall, Aizawl under the Chairmanship of Chief Minister, Pu Zoramthanga wherein they approved the draft guidelines for the college - ZMC bye-laws .The meeting also discussed topics relating to the restructuring of post in TB & Respiratory Medicine department, shortage of staff nurses, procurement of additional lands for consolidation of ZMC, way to offer Post Graduate facilities in the college and other relevant points. The meeting was attended by Dy. Chief Minister Pu Tawnluia,  Health Minister Dr. R.Lalthangliana, MLA Prof. F. Lalnunmawia, and other senior officials from Health department, Finance, DPAR, PHE, ZMC, IMA and Mizoram University.
