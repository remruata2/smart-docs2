SPECIAL REPORT

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/549

Dated 14th July, 2021

 

Information received stated that Assam intended to enter into Mizoram and take new initiative by setting up camp near Tuikhuahlian Bridge, which is located around 1 km towards Kanhmun village (which they claim as within their territory based on Google map) from Medili Bridge (inter-state border) on 15.7.2021 or 16.7.2021. In this connection, a huge contingent of Assam Police is presently stationed at Rangamati, Karimganj District, which is around 10 km from Medili Bridge. It is also reported that Brus and Muslims residing near the inter-state border intended to support Assam government’s initiative.

 

One section of 4th IR Battalion has been deployed at Medili Bridge, Kanhmun. Mizoram government started levelling work for Police Barrack at
Pu Sangkhuma’s farm land near Medili Bridge.

 

Further, 1 (one) Company of Assam Police is presently stationed at Gutguti village, Hailakandi District, which is located around 3 km from Saikhawthlir village and they are accompanied by forest officials to take initiative at Chuhvel border.

 

Information received stated that one motorized boat boarded by
6 (six) Assam Police proceeded upstream at Tlawng river near Bairabi town today i.e., 14.7.2021 @ 8:50 a.m. and they proceeded towards Teirei river. Later, this morning @ 9:30 a.m., 2 (two) motorized boats boarded by 7 (seven) Assam Police and 8 (eight) Assam Police respectively also proceeded towards Teirei river. The two parties were reported to have proceeded to Gutguti BOP, Hailakandi District Assam.

 

Today i.e., 14.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m., Shri. Pulok Biswas, Circle Officer & Executive Magistrate, Katlicherra, Hailakandi District, Assam; OC, Ramnathpur PS; Post-Commander, Guguti BOP and 20(twenty) Assam Armed Police; forest officials and 5(five) civilians from Assam came upto an entry point of Mizoram Police camp at Bildawp tlang, which is located around 2 km west of Pu John Halliday’s Farm Hut, Chuhvel. Pu Zoramthara, Addl. SP, Mamit; Pu Andy Lalfakzuala, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah; SDPO, Kawrthah and
1 (one) VC member of Chuhvel held a brief talk with them and Assam officials requested Mizoram government to withdraw forces from the disputed area and not to take any new initiative and maintain status quo at the area. They were not allowed to enter Mizoram Police camp and Assam officials left the area @ 3:45 p.m.

Cont/-2....

 

-2-

 

 

Around 50 (fifty) volunteers from Chuhvel village constructed Mizoram Police makeshift camp at Bildawp tlang, which is located around 2 km west of Pu John Halliday’s Farm Hut, Chuhvel yesterday i.e., 13.7.2021 and subsequently 1 (one) Company of 4th IR Battalion are deployed at the area. Mizoram started construction of road at around 2 km west of Pu John Halliday’s Farm Hut where Assam reportedly intended to construct road at the area w.e.f., 12.7.2021. However, the road construction work could not be resumed as excavator (JCB) engaged for the said purpose is out of order.

 

Information received stated that a  huge contingent of Assam Police, who are presently stationed at Lailapur, Cachar District intended to visit
Pi Thangseii’s paddy field at Mautuilui zau, which is located around 4 km east of Vairengte. In this connection, 31 (thirty one) personnel from 2nd IR Battalion headed by Addl. SP, Kolasib are presently deployed at the area.

 

Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IPS, DIG (NR) is presently stationed at Vairengte to monitor the present inter-state border situation. At present,
127 (one hundred twenty seven) personnel from 3rd MAP and PTS headed by
3 (three) Assistant Commandants are deployed at Pu Thangkhawliana’s farm land, Buarchep. 63 (sixty three) personnel of 1st IR Battalion headed byAssistant Commandant are deployed at Saihapui ‘V’ and 55 (fifty five) personnel of
2nd MAP headed by Assistant Commandant are deployed at Aitlang area.
