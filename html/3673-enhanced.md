A. _Front page of VANGLAINI/The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 07.09.2022_

****Rs. 152.41 crores spent for Electricity** : Replying an unstarred question of ZPM MLA ,Dr. Vanlalthlana, , Power and Electricity Minister, Pu R.Lalzirliana told the Assembly House on yesterday's session (6.9.2022) that Rs. 152.41 crores have been used by Mizoram for the purchased of electricity during the year 2022-2023 while Rs. 139.04 have been received from the sales of electricity. The Minister further stated that electricity have been purchased by the State from 15 places outside Mizoram and the average amount of funds utilised per month for electricity during 2021-2022 was Rs.33.33 crores while the revenue received from the consumers was Rs.22.37 crores.**

 Presently, there are 14 Hydel projects/ small Hydel projects in Mizoram which are under the state Government and 1 Central Sector which is Tuirial Hydro Electric Power.

B. _Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 07.09.2022_

****Closing Function of Police Camping 9 different places :** Closing functions for the various Detoxification &amp; Rehabilitation Camps organised by Mizoram Police were held at 9 different places on 5.9.2022. These camps, which were organised using funds received from National Fund for Control of Drug Abuse (NFCDA) lasted 45 days and were attended by 346 personnel. DGP Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastava at 3rd MAP, IGP (Hqrs.) Pu John Neihlaia at 1st MAP and IGP (Training) Pu Parwaiz Ahmed at PTS, Thenzawl were among the senior officers of Mizoram police who graced these functions as Chief Guests.**

 A statement issued by Mizoram police in this regard mentioned that the 45 days camping were utilised systematically for physical fitness and mental health awareness programme which greatly benefited the campers. The statement further appreciated the efforts of all the Resource Persons, the campers themselves and their families, and all others who gave their efforts for the success of these Detoxification &amp; Rehabilitation Camps.
