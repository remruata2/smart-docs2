****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/216

Dated 25th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/213 Dt. 24.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-10, MMV-16, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 25.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-7, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 25.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

81 (eighty one) vehicles (HMV-60, MMV-1, LMV-15, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 25.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

134 (one hundred thirty four) vehicles (HMV-49, MMV-41, LMV-15, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-15, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 25.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
