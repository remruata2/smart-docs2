MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/56

Dated 27th January, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/53
Dt. 25.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :

6 (six) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 2, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 27.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm.

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 7, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 27.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 6 (six) vehicles (MMV – 6, LMV -Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Nowadays is a raw broom harvesting season in Mizoram and the raw broom collected are transited at Saihapui ‘V’ border near BSF and SSB place of duty. SSB deployed by Assam continued strict enforcement of blockade of vehicles at the border w.e.f., 24.1.2021 as per direction of higher authority, but there is no report of any untoward incidents.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          95 (ninety five) vehicles (HMV-49, MMV – 2, LMV –36, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab-8, Bus - Nil) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 27.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

145 (one hundred forty five) vehicles (HMV – 64, MMV – 41, LMV –16, LPG – 1, Maxi Cab – 6, Oil Tanker – 17, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 27.01.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
