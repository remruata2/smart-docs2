A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 21.5.2013_

**Burma****Liquor seized**: 184 packets of BEDC (Burma alcohol) worth RS. 27,000/- were seized from two men from Vaphai by police duty at Saikhumphai yesterday. The alleged owners of BEDC are Rualthangpuia (26) s/o Rualnghina and Zoramparmawia (24) s/o Vawmlianthanga both from Vaphai village; they are now in the custody of Champhai Police Station.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 21.5.2013_

****Second batch of training for OC**: Mizoram police organised Second batch training on Preparation course for OC of Police Station at Aizawl SP office Conference Hall. The training was inaugurated by Alok Kumar Verma, DGP, Mizoram. 30 Inspectors and Sub-Inspectors attended the training.**

C. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 21.5.2013_

****Talk postponed**: The proposed talks between the state government and HPC(D) slated for the later part of this month has been postponed following a request from the underground outfit. HPC(D) is reportedly yet to complete its agenda (Charter of Demands) for the talks.**
