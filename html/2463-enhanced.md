****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/746

Dated 23rd December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/742 Dt. 22.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 13 (thirteen) vehicles (HMV-10, MMV-1, LMV – 2) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 23.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 7, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 4 (four) vehicles (HMV – 1, MMV – 3, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 23.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 8 (eight) vehicles (LMV-2, MMV–6) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 78 (seventy eight) vehicles (HMV-21, MMV – 4, LMV – 43, LPG-1,Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 23.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 125 (one hundred twenty five) vehicles (HMV – 61, MMV – 16, LMV –17, LPG – 10, Maxi Cab – 5, Oil Tanker – 16, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 23.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
