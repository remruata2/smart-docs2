A. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 13.3.2014

Strict Vigilance on Teachers : In pursuance of the resolution passed by their Federal Body meeting at Champhai yesterday, Mizo Students Union intends to make an efforts to flush out government teachers who are employing their substitute instead of working themselves in  their post. MSU believed that there are still numerous teachers who are still reluctant to stay in their post despite the orders of the government.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.3.2014

Narcotic seized: At around 1:20 pm yesterday, District Special Branch under S.P Aizawl, seized substances which are suspected to be Pseudophedrine from the possession of Lalrelliana s/o Zalawmchuanga who is residing in the rented house of  Laisawizika of Ramhlun South. The seized narcotics are estimated to be worth around Rs.23,40,000 in the local market of Aizawl.
