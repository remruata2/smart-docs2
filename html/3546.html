A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 23.12.2022

Bood donations: In the ongoing voluntary blood donation programme organised by Association for Voluntary Blood Donation(AVBD), Mizoram State AIDS Control Society and Blood Bank to mark Christmas festival during the month of December 2022, as many as 878 persons have donated their blood in Aizawl blood bank alone. AVBD statement stated the since the programme covers the whole month of December, willing donors can still contribute their precious blood and refreshment will be provided to all the voluntary blood donors during the programme.

 

 

B. Second page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 23.12.2022

2,425 vehicles fined during two months:  Since the implementation of Mizoram Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019 from 1st October 2022 to 30th November 2022, 2,425 traffic rules violators have been fined in Aizawl city and the total amount of funds received from them amounted to Rs.19,60,300.As per information received from officers of Aizawl Traffic Police, during the period between 1st - 31st October 2022, 1,286 defaulters were fined which reached Rs. 10,00,000 in total amount while Rs. 9,60,300 were received as fines from 1,139 traffic rules violators during 1st - 30th November 2022.

 

 

C. Second  page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 23.12.2022

These Ganjas were smuggled from Assam state and a criminal case was registered at Kanhmun Police Station. On the same day, a team of Special Narcotic Police Station recovered 37.94 grams of Heroin at Luangmual Vengthlang, Aizawl from the possession of Rodingpuii (25) Hnahthial Venglai. Zolawmsanga (22), Champhai Kanan Veng, who is suspected to be a co accused was also apprehended. These contrabands are estimated to be worth around Rs.8,00,000 in the international market.
