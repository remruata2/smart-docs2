No. MSB/CB/B-18(A)/ Dated Aizawl, the Apr.,2020.

 Minutes of meeting of SsP,CID(SB) with officers of the Unit on Dt. 7.4.2020 to discuss ways to collect intelligence regarding presence and activities of Muslim Fundamentalist, particularly Tablighi Jamaat, from gathering a stronghold in Mizoram and to monitor their influences is reproduced as below.

 After agenda wise discussion and deliberation, the following discussion were reached with action to be taken up by concern branches -

 1. A separate file should be opened on the subject if not yet done.

** **Action: Confidential Branch.****

 2. Appropriate training should be given to staff dealing with the subject.

 **Action: R.O/Field Branch.**

 3. Detailed profiles of the people in different Mosques within the state should  be maintained.

 **Action : Field Branch.**

 4. Search of Mosques suspected to entertain or harbor Tablighi Jamaat  members or sympathizers may be conducted by surprise as may be required.  Similarly search of such places occupied by persons suspected to be involved in such activities may also be done. It should be ensured that element of surprise is strictly maintained in all such operations.

 **Action : Field Branch.**

 5. Known sympathizers such as Dr.Jamal Hussain, Department of Mathematics  &amp; Computer Sciences of Mizoram University who had facilitated the visit of two groups of Tablighi Jamaat in February and March, 2020 should be closely watched and their activities monitored including technological surveillance by following all required formalities.

 **Action : Technical Branch.**

 6. Details of persons who have sponsored and supported and financed Tablighi  Tamaat activities in the state should be collected.

 **Action : Field Branch.**

 7. The list of persons who are members of Tablighi Jamaat who visited  Mizoram during February and March, 2020 in two groups with details (in three pages) is enclosed. The following additional information should be collected in respect of these groups.

 1) Purpose of visit as mentioned in the Temporary ILP/ILP issued to them.

 2) Their date of departure and point of departure from Mizoram.

 3) Full details of the persons who have come in contact with these  Tablighi Jamaat members who might have come under their influence.

**Action : Field/Technical Branch.**
