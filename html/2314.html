MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/035

Dated 14th January, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/22
Dt. 13.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV – 4, MMV – 15, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 14.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV- 3) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

          18 (eighteen) vehicle (HMV – 2, MMV – 16, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 14.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 6 (six) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 6) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-36, MMV – 4, LMV – 29, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 14.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          161 (one hundred sixty one) vehicles (HMV – 67, MMV – 46, LMV – 34,  LPG – 8, Maxi Cab – Nil, Oil Tanker – 5, Bus - 1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 14.1.2021 between 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
