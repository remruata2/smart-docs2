State where cow slaughter is banned

 

 

1.         Maharastra, Haryana, U.P., Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan, Punjab, Odisha, Puducherry, M.P. Karnataka, J&K, Himachal Pradesh, Gujarat, Delhi, Bihar and Andhra Pradesh.

 

2.         Bulls and Buffaloes are sold and eaten in most state even where cow slaughter is banned Karnataka eg. allows slaughter of bulls and buffaloes above 12 yrs.

 

3.         Some states like Haryana, Rajasthan, Punjab,J&K and Himachal Pradesh have more stringent laws that ban the slaughter of all.

 

4.         West Bengal permit slaughter of cows, bulls and buffaloes over the age of 14 but requires a fit for slaughter certificates.

 

5.         Daman & Diu and Goa permit slaughter of those cows which are old or silk or for medical purposes.

 

6.         Kerala is to be the India's most beef friendly state. There is no state lagislation banning cows slaughter and dishes like beef fry and beef chilli are available at roadside stall.

 

7.         State like Kerala, Manipur, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland allow slaughter of cattle with no certificate required.

 

8.         Despite the banned, India is only second to Brazil when it comes to exporting beef, but most of it is buffaloes meal. It is also the fifth largest producer.
