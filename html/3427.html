A. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 31.1.2014

Pseudoephedrine seized: On the night of Wednesday @ 8:00pm, District Special Branch under SP Aizawl seized 1,00,000 tablets of Pseudoephedrine near BSNL office, Aizawl from the possession of Khumakcham Maipak Singha (28) s/o Meino Babu Singha (Manipuri) who is residing at Venghlui, Aizawl. The local market rate of the seized Pseudoephedrine is more than Rs. 4 lakh.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 31.1.2014

CM’s FIR in the SMS Scandal: Regarding the FIR lodged by Chief Minister Lal Thanhawla on 11th December 2013 for the insulting SMS he received, the accused K.Chhawnthuama of Durtlang appeared in the court before Chief Judicial Magistrate Michael Lalrinsanga. As the Police have not submitted Chargesheet, the case could not move further. The court decided that the next hearing will be held on 24th February, 2014.

 

 

C. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 31.1.2014

Deep Mandal still Datained: Deep Mandal who was kidnapped from Dampa forest by Bru armed gang on 23rd November 2013 has not been released till today. He is alive and not tortured, said his abductors. The police are doing their best for the release of Deep Mandal and are still engaging the same agent who worked for them in the safe release of Sanglianthanga and Lalzamliana.
