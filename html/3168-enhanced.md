**A. Front page of VANGLAINI/Tawrh Bawm/ The Aizawl Post/ The Zozam Times newspapers Dt.01.02.2013**

**Peace Talk:** Representatives of HPC(D) and Mizoram Government held a peace talk at  Aizawl Guest House yesterday. The Government of Mizoram was represented by Home  Deptt. Jt. Secy. Pu Lalbiakzama, CID(SB) S.P Pu H.Ramthlengliana, Home Dy.Secy. Pu  Lalhriatpuia, Home OSD Pu David lalthangliana and CID (SB) Inspt. Laltanpuia Sailo  while Pu Lalbeisei, Pu Hrangneihkung, Pu Lalthalian and Pu Francis Songate were  present from the HPC(D) side. The dialogue resulted in an agreement from both sides to  have a suspension of operations for six months which could be extended for another six  months with a proposal for resumption of talks as soon as possible.

**B. Front page of The Aizawl Post / The Zozam/ VANGLAINI Times newspapers Dt.01.02.2013**

**Seven persons detained by police:**In the on going investigation conducted by the  police regarding the incident in Bethlehem Veng on the night of 30th January 2013  where several vehicles and a house were set ablaze , the Aizawl Police have detained  seven suspected persons .Expectations to track down the culprit is extremely high,  according to information from the police.
