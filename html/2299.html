MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/41

Dated 18th January, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/38
Dt. 15.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV – 9, MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 5 (five) vehicles (MMV – 5) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

9 (nine) vehicles (HMV – 4, MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm.7 (seven) vehicles (MMV – 7) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          91 (ninety one) vehicles (HMV-46, MMV – 1, LMV –34, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-10) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

162 (one sixty two) vehicles (HMV – 53, MMV – 34, LMV –38, LPG – 5, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 27, Bus - 1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 18.01.2021during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
