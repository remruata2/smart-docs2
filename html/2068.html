BRU REVOLUTIONARY UNION/ARMY (BRU/BRA)

 

 

1) On 13.11.2009@9:45Am, suspected Bru militants shot dead one Mizo named Zarzokima(17) S/o Lalngilneia of Bungthuam about 1 km. north of Bungthuam. He was shot thrice in the body and the suspected Bru militants left a note in which they claimed themselves as Bru Revolutionary Union, stating that they would continue their struggle till they achieve their demands.

 

2) On the morning of 31.12.2009, one Saad Uddin(50) S/o Arpin Ali of Bagaricherra, Karimganj Dist, Assam while proceeding towards Panchgram, Assam by his truck bearing registration No.AS25-A/4517 was stopped at Gobin bridge about 3 kms from Bungthuam by two unidentified persons who demanded money from him allegedly using Bru dialect and out of fear he handed over all his money i.e Rs. 60/-.

 

3) During the end of December 2009, some Bru undergrounds clad in civil dress and without arms collected donation from Chhimluang South and Rasdali villages, Kolasib Dist. at Rs.200/- per family. These Bru underground reportedly beaten up those who oppose their collection and also intended to collect donation from Meidum village and Dilzau village.

 

4) On 28.1.2010@ 1800 hrs One Monir Uddin(54) S/o Kalamai(L) of Kallarbagh, Hailakandhi Dist, Assam, a farm tenant of H. Sanghmingthanga at Zophai, Bairabi was abducted from his farm hut by suspected Brus numbering 11 who carried country made guns and also dao(knife) and allegedly took him to Katlicherra area, Assam and later released him on 1.2.2010 after beating him up as he could not pay ransom demand of Rs.300000/- and 30 mobile handsets.

 

5) On the night of 17.3.2010, PC.Gulgulia and his friend who were working for a private company constructing Mamit-Bairabi road were robbed by 8 unidentified persons suspected to be Brus carrying four local made guns and daos before reaching Suarhliap on their way to Pathiantlang from Bairabi. They were reportedly snatched  Rs.60000/- cash and jewelry worth around 35000/-. The robbers were reportedly conversing in Hindi and did not seriously injure the two victims.

 

6) On 1.4.2010@ 6:30pm, a Bru armed gang suspected to be Moikau and his party abducted Roton Joy@ Tiger of Naisingpara refugee camp and murdered him at the outskirt of Anonda village, Tripura. His dead body was recovered on 11.4.2011. No mark of bullet injury was found on his body while injuries caused by blunt weapons and sign of tortures could be seen from all over his body.

 

7) On 25.11.2010, suspected masked Bru militant group numbering about 7 equipped with weapons such as AK-47, lethode gun and carbine attacked 2 plying trucks laden with teak logs at Mizoram - Assam bordering near Medili bridge (Mizoram side) on their way to Dharmanagar. The militant fired at the two vehicles and punctured the front wheel of both the trucks and snatched away 3 nos. of mobile phones, documents and Rs.1000/- cash from the drivers. The trucks were driven by Narul Uddin and Mohammed Abdul Ahab both of Dharmanagar. The militant group handed over a note to the driver which contained two mobile phone numbers and to contact them later and verbally told them to pay regular road tax to them if they wanted to pass through their area safely.

 

8) On 26.11.2010@ around 7:00pm, the suspected Bru armed group assaulted 3 hunters near Medili(inside Assam) and snatched away SBBL gun with 2 nos. of ammunitions and their mobile phones. The hunters, however, were released with their guns.

 

9) On 4.4.2011@ 11:00am, Elvis Chorkhy, Gen. Secy of Bru Co-ordination Committee was contacted by Raj Muni, S/S Chief of Bru armed group through telephone and held him responsible for the arrest and detention of Henry Lalmalsawma, Dy.Chief of the group and further accused him of prolonging the detention period by influencing Home Minister of Mizoram and the judiciary. It was also learnt that the said Raj Muni threatened him to wipe out all his relatives including himself at refugee camp or in the very heart of Aizawl city unless Henry Lalmalsawma (who is in judicial custody at central jail, Tanhril) is released within 10 days. He even threatened to kill as many Mizos as possible to secure the release of Henry Lalmalsawma.

 

10) On 18.10.2011 at about 7-8:00Pm SS L/Cpl. Moilau and party killed one Beloram@Sadonipa(50) of Medili by chopping off his head at Medili village of Assam. The miscreants left a note stating that Bisoram Reang@ Didii Pa@Bura of Medili, who surrendered to SP Mamit on 26.8.2011 will face the same fate.

 

11) Unconfirmed information received indicated that a combined group of BLA and DHD(N) cadres numbering about 25 had entered Kaiskau Refugee Camp last week i.e. 2nd week of November 2011. This combined group, according to the information received are planning to start fund raising from ginger grower in Cachar Zion areas of Hailakandi District of Assam by extorting certain percentage out of the sale proceeds of ginger. The BLA further threatened to kill Ajit and may also target his family allegedly for helping the Army Operation Team recently in which 4 BLA cadres were killed.

 

12) On 22.11.2011, Bru Armed Gang(Tripura Group) led by Henry reacted to the refusal of their demand of Rs.10 lakhs from Mizo residents living in the Cachar Zion area of Assam by planning to abduct one Thanzuala and Panchayat Chairman of that area(name not known). As per their plan, they may kidnap the above two mentioned public leaders and would hold them up at Kaiskau(Refugee Camp) of Tripura to extort Rs.10 lakhs from the Mizos of Cachar Zion, B.Tlangnuam and Sena Veng. They also expressed their anger towards the residents of that area of both Mizos and Brus who welcomed their rival faction UDLA and YMA members to counter them and even intended to harass them by visiting these villagers.
