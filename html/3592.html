A. Front page of VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 24.6.2022

Rs.2,64,34,652 worth of narcotics destroyed by END :        Mizoram Excise and Narcotics Department destroyed around  Rs.2,64,34,652 worth of narcotic substances seized by them at Bethany Multipurpose Farm, Phunchawng.

          This disposal of drugs and other narcotics are part of the two weeks 'Drug Free Fortnight' (Nashe Se Aazaadi Pakhwada) programme and is dedicated for the upcoming International Day Against Drug Abuse & Illegal Trafficking which is to be observed on 26th June 2022. They destroyed narcotics were from seizure made in between 2014-2022 in 124 narcotic cases and approved for disposal by the Court.

 

 

 

B. Front Page  of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper  Dt. 24.6.2022

No positive result in exploration of oil: Under the ongoing process of exploration of oil in Mizoram, drilling had been done on six locations,all of them without success, so far. However, proposal has been made for further drilling at two other places.

          In a meeting held by Commerce& industries Minister , Dr.R.Lalthangliana with officers of different branches under Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas and other concerned department officials of Mizoram, discussion was made on the exploration process of Oil & Natural Gas and its related issues. The ongoing process is divided into three Blocks -Block No.AA-ONN-2001/2 which covers the northern parts of Mizoram, Block No.AA-ONN/2004/1,who conducted drilling in the middle parts of the state and Block No.AA-ONN-2004/2 for the southern parts of Mizoram.

          In all these explorations, only in Meidum under Block No.AA-ONN-2001/2 ,small amount of gas is detected and the estimated reserve of which is too little for further efforts.

 

 

C. Front Page  of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper  Dt. 24.6.2022

Ambulance donated : Dr. Sunil kumar, Chairman, All India Acupuncture Foundation, New Delhi handed over ambulance which was received under the initiative of Pu Devesh Chandra Shrivastava, Director General of Police,  Mizoram to Mizoram Police. This ambulance which is a TATA Winger 3448 WB, was flagged off by the DGP at PHQ complex, Aizawl. It can accommodate 7 passengers apart from a patient and it is also equipped with standard auto-loading stretcher, oxygen delivery system, Air conditioning system, PA System, siren, blinkers etc.
