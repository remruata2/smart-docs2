A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.14 .11.2022_

****Union Minister announced Rs. 1,00,000 crores for Highway constructions in NE**: Union Minister for Roads, Transport &amp; Highways, Shri Nitin Gadkari on 11th November 2022 announced that Central Government had allotted Rs. more than 1,00,000 crores for Highway constructions in the North Eastern States, out of which Rs. 6,664 crores will be utilised for highway constructions inside Mizoram state. The proposed Rs. 6,664 crores for Mizoram will be divided into four main projects where one of these projects - Aizawl by pass road will be constructed into a 2-lane highway and will involve drilling of 3 tunnels, the total length of which (tunnels)will be around 2.5 Kms. In this North Eastern states Highway construction project, Arunachal Pradesh is receiving the highest amount of funds (Rs. 44,000 crores) with Sikkim as the lowest (Rs.2,000 crores).**

B. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt.14 .11.2022_

****Talks to be resumed between Mizoram and Assam during 15-17 November:******Due to the visit of Mizoram by President of India during the first week of November 2022, Mizoram Government proposed 9-11 November 2022 as the time for talks between Mizoram and Assam regarding the disputed border issues of the two states. However, as Assam state was supposed to have Cabinet meeting during these period, the talks were scheduled to be held during 15-17 November 2022 as approved by both the states. According to reports received from Home Department, officials of the two states will have a meeting on 15th and 17th November, while talks between Minister level of both the states will be held on 17th November 2022 at Guwahati, Assam.**
