2. Total members –

            Total members from Gen. Hqrs, Headquarters, Sub- Hqrs. and Branches may be 5950 approximately. With members from different Affiliated colleges, the total numbers may reached (excluding members from Higher Secondary School and below) around 25000.

 

3. Criteria/eligibility for OB elections

            1) To be an Elected OBs, they must be a member of (station)  Gen. Hqrs.

            2) To be an Elected OBs, they must be a admitted in any institution.

            3) To be an Elected OBs, he/she must be a Graduate from any Recognised University

            4) To be an Elected OBs, he/she must not be a government servant.

            5) Only existing Executive Committee members may be nominated.

            6) They must not be affiliated to any political party.

 

 

4. MZP  Sources of income –

           

            1) Income from their Building/floor/house rent

                        Rs.30000/- approximately from building rentals.

 

            2) Other source of income per month/year.

                  

                    Annually, various Headquarters, Sub Headquarters and Branches contributed Rs.100, Rs.50 and Rs.20 respectively to MZP General Headquarters which can amounts to around Rs.4990/- in all.

                        Apart from this, the main sources of income of MZP Gen. Hqrs. are monthly donations received from non-Mizo traders, Suppliers, Courier Service and various Companies working inside Mizoram. This practice of collecting donations is also followed by Headquarters, Sub Headquarters, Branches etc. in their respective areas.

                        For organising big events, MZP General Headquarters often seeks assistance from concerned Minister in Charge which normally resulted in the form of sponsorship from suitable Government Department.

                        From the various sources of income, it can be roughly estimated that MZP General Headquarters can earn Rs.2,50,000 - Rs.3,00,000 per month.
