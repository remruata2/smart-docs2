COUNTER TERRORISM CONTINGENCY PLAN FOR LENGPUI AIRPORT, MIZORAM

Introduction:

              Lengpui Airport was constructed, owned and operated (with technical assistance from AAI) by the State Government and commissioned on 12th December, 1998. As of now, Lengpui Airport plays a vital role in providing the much needed  linkage between mainland India and neightbouring states.

          A terror attack on an airport is always something that gives a terrorist organisation maximum exposure and shows their alleged power. The Aviation Sector is always seen as an iconic and lucrative target for terrorist elements because the attention attracted by any kind of attack on airport premises, aircraft and airport facilities would be much more rather than an attack on any other target. Aviation security is always taken as a sovereign function for any country. Therefore, the threat level of Lengpui Airports is also high keeping in view the strategic location of the Airport.

 

 Security of Lengpui Airport:

 

             Security of Lengpui Airport has been looked after by Lengpui Airport Security staff who are under the control of the Superintendent of Police, Security and personnel of 225 Battalion CRPF. Based on the guidelines issued by the Bureau of Civil Aviation Security(BCAS), Standard Operational Procedure has been outlined for Security of Lengpui Airport.

 

Role of Airport Security Unit:          

 

             The overall responsibility of security of Lengpui Airport lies with the State Police(APSU) under the supervision and control of Superintendent of Police, Security. Access Control of the Terminal Building, frisking of passengers, pre-embarkation check, x-ray machine, Inner Line Permit checks and records, security of VIP/VVIP movement, CCTV etc are the main duty of Lengpui Airport Security Staff.

Role of 'E' Coy/225 Bn. CRPF:

 

             Guarding Terminal Building, Security Hold, VIP Gate, Departure, Arrival, Gallery, Apron, Outside Parking Area, Intelligence/LO, Dog Squad, Watchtower, Morcha around Runway during and after operational hours, ATC Tower, Access Control, Perimeter Patrolling, armed support and assisting civil police during operational hours of the Airport are the primary duties and responsibilities of Armed Police(CRPF).

 

Role of CID(SB)

(i)The main role of CID(SB) is to undertake periodical assessment  of the potential threat to the security of Lengpui Airport.

(ii) To collect information and gather intelligence on  all matter relating to the  security threat of Lengpui Airport.

(iii)To promptly disseminate real time intelligence/information to Aerodrome Committee/ Crisis Management Group at the time of  terror attack/incident.

Scenarios

 

I.           Attempt to hijack airplane by terrorist element

 

             Activities and tasks :

             The following set order of responsibilities shall be taken by Lengpui Airport Security - State Police and CRPF to detect, prevent and counter terrorist activities.

 

(i)         The ATC duty will inform the Airport Director about the occurrence.

(ii)        QRT will be activated and access control measures be taken by making the QRT of CRPF respond to the spot immediately and local police to provide back up and additional support.

(iii)       QRT of CRPF shall cordon and isolate the location of aircraft.

(iv)       Command of the operation shall be with Superintendent of Police, Security and Commandant, 225 Bn. CRPF.

(v)        Fire tender will be parked blocking the Runway and Airport Ambulance be kept alert and in ready position.

(vi)       The Airport Director will inform the members of the Aerodrome Committee about the occurrence and be assembled AHCR Room and sequence of action taken by reported.

(vii)      The Chairman of the Aerodrome Committee will take charge of the situation and negotiate with the hijackers.

(viii)    Superintendent of Police, Aizawl District shall be available with the operational commander for negotiation and tie-up with the liaison officers of all operation agencies of Lengpui Airport.

(ix)       Superintendent of Police, Wireless will tie-up with all operation agencies of Lengpui Airport with common frequency of radio for co-ordination.

(x)        Minimum collateral damage shall be ensured by the QRT.

(xi)       All sentry post of CRPF on the perimeter wall be alert and be in ready position to handle and react to the Commander of the operation.

 

 

 

II.         Terrorists Armed with Explosives Trying to enter the Terminal Building:

            

             (a)   Activities and Task of Security:

(i)           DSP Lengpui Airport will immediately inform Superintendent of Police, Security     and members of Aerodrome Committee about the incident.

(ii)          Lengpui Airport Security and CRPF will react in co-ordination as first responders.

(iii)        On receiving information, Superintendent of Police, Traffic along with           Superintendent of Police, Aizawl District will deploy Police at approach road for     checking of vehicles and screening of suspicious elements.

(iv)         The QRT of CRPF at Lengpui Airport shall immediately rush to the spot of the          incident and shall undertake immediate counter measures. The anti-Hijacking and anti- terrorist counter measure shall be initiated under the Officer Commanding,          Airport Security of CRPF.

(v)          The Local State Police of Sairang Police Station will immediately reach the spot       and shall take counter measures and shall join the counter-ops.

(vi)         The OC, Lengpui Airport Security of Mizoram Police or in his absence, his next       senior most subordinate shall remain outside the Terminal Building at the ATM        area to guide any additional Force arriving to conduct anti-terrorism counter       measures.

(vii)       The Lady Officers/SO of Lengpui Airport Security on duty shall be responsible for             segregating and escorting the affected civilian away from the area of conflict in   order to minimize casualties.

 

 

 

III.       Protection of cargo complex/baggage make up area:

            

(a) Operational Measures:

              (i) Access control over these areas will be the responsibility of Lengpui Airport Security and CRPF shall ensure that these areas are well guarded adequately. Unauthorised person including civilian staff of Lengpui Airport shall not be allowed in these areas immediately before and after the take off of all flights.

 

              (ii) Surveillance through CCTV shall be done by the Operator, CCTV Control Room.

            

(b) Fuel Depot: The Fuel Depot  located  inside  the  Airport must be well protected by the        ````Lengpui Airport Security and CRPF personnel.

 

(i) First contingency response will be done by the CRPF personnel and Lengpui Airport Security along with Fire Service present at Lengpui Airport.

 

(ii) Local Police through the Officer-in-charge of Sairang Police Station and Fire and Emergency Services shall respond immediately as per requirement of the situation.

 

 

 

IV.        Terrorist intruding into the airside of the Airport:

 

            (i)     The Company reinforcement of CRPF shall be rushed to the Airside at the Watch Towers/observation posts for perimeter protection.

            (ii)    The QRT-1 of the Company of CRPF at Lengpui Airport shall be stationed at strategic locations.

            (iii)  The terminal area shall be closed by Local State Police Security and no one shall be allowed to enter the premises.

            (iv)   The civilians and staff stranded inside the Terminal Building shall be escorted out of the Terminal Building by the State Police Security staff to a safe area outside Terminal area.

            (v)    CRPF QRT-2 shall reinforce the Apron area and shall ensure security and clearance from any negative element.

            (vi)   The officer at ATC shall divert all flights from landing in Lengpui Airport and shall also lock the ATC from inside to prevent further intrusion in the ATC area.
