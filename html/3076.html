A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 1.4.2013

Least Cases Registered Within 10 Years: Lunglei Police has only registered 5 cases in March, 2013 which is the least within 10 years, even the cases registered been minor/petty cases.

            Meanwhile, Romana of Lungsen Village, a prisoner of Lunglei District Jail, Sazaikawn who was treated in Civil Hospital for illness escaped from custody on 21st March, 2013 and has not been found till yesterday. He was arrested for stealing gas cylinder and almost completed his prison term.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 1.4.2013

Sent Home After Medical Check-up: After 38 days of detention, 5 persons abducted by militants suspected to be Bru armed gang were released and they were brought to their respective home safely.

            According to Police report, Lalliansanga (42) of Tahan, Myanmar who resides in Teirei Forest Veng, Lalsangmawia (37) of Bethlehem Veng, West Phaileng and Vanlalpeka of Zodin Veng, Damparengpui were sent home in vehicles arranged by Environment & Forest Department after their health condition were thoroughly examined by doctor.

 

C. Front page of The VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 1.4.2013

New Traffic Rules: Aizawl Traffic SP notifies that the road at Upper Republic locality from Pu J.Lalthangliana’s house to the southern gate of Raj Bhavan which was used as one way will be used as double way on Sunday and weekdays from 7:00PM to 7:00AM for LMV. The order is to be effective from today onwards.

 

D. Front page of The VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 1.4.2013

Road Accident: A bike driven by a juvenile run over Pi Darnghengi (72) w/o C.Kapliana, Lunglei on 30th March @ around 5:00PM near Tourist Lodge at Zotlang, Lunglei. Pi Darnghengi succumbed to her injury on the same night @ around 11:00PM at Christian Hospital Serkawn. Lunglei Police reported that the bike rider is a juvenile and charged him with Juvenile Act.
