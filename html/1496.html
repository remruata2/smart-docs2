Action taken report :-

 

4.4   Agenda Item No.4 : Human Trafficking in the North East.

 

                        (a)       Social medias are being monitored by staff of  CID(SB)  for any reliable and important intelligence output, including those which may amount to sexual related offences.

 

                        (b)       Personnel of this unit are well versed as a result of attending frequent training programs for dealing both drugs and human trafficking cases.

 

                        (c)       As per the instruction of Govt. of India, four Anti Human Trafficking    units (AHTU) has been establish in Aizawl District, Lunglei District, Champhai District and Kolasib District under Mizoram Police Department.

           

                        (d)       The accommodation and welfare of the rescued victims are being handled by the concerned District and Police  administration authorities.

 

 

 

 

4.5  Agenda Item No.5 : Indo Myanmar Border Issues :-

 

                        (a)       From the beginning of 2020, there has been no reports of cross border movement of Insurgents Groups across the Mizoram-Myanmar border areas.

 

                        (b)       As for informal trade across the border, the concerned districts and sector officers of CID (SB) posted in these areas have been sensitized and alerted to take precautionary measures in this regard.
