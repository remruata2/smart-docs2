Reference to our DSI dated 16.8.2019, Item No. B(a). Leaders of various Bru NGOs in Tripura Transit camps met Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister at his office chamber today, i.e. 20.8.2019 @ 2:00 p.m and discussed about their demands in connection with the Bru repatriation. The NGOs were represented by the following :-

                        1.         Mizoram Bru Displaced People Forum:

                                    i)         Pu A. Sawibunga, President

                                    ii)        Pu Bruno Msha, Gen. Secretary

                                    iii)       Pu Lalbiakthanga, President, MBDPF Kaiskau Branch.

            2.         Mizoram Bru Displaced People Coordination Committee:

                                    i)         Pu Laldingliana, President

                                    ii)        Pu Joarge Molshoy, Gen. Secretary

                                    iii)       Pu A. Lalramliana, Adviser

            3.         Mizoram Bru Indigenous Democratic Movement:

                                    i)         Pu Phillip Apeto, Gen. Secretary

                                    ii)        Pu Paritosh Chorky, Secretary i/c Relief. 

                                    The NGO leaders reiterated the various demands that they had already submitted to the Hon'ble Chief Minister and the Home Minister on 5.7.2019 and implored the Minister to implement their various demands. The Minister apprised the NGO leaders that the Government acknowledged their demands and stated that the State Government could do nothing beyond the Joint Monitoring Agreement, 2018. He further assured them to highlight their demands before the JMG meeting to be held on 6.9.2019.  

                                    The Minister further mentioned about the inconveniences of conduct of spot verification of the areas selected for the settlement of the repatriated Brus due to the financial implications but conveyed them that it does not preclude them to do so if they can do on their own. On the arrangement of the Minister, the NGO leaders met Pu Lalbiakzama, Home Secretary at his office chamber today itself. The Home Secretary also promised to raise their demands before the JMG Meeting.
