Subj :  Report on contribution of Special Police Officers.

Ref. :  No.CB/PHQ/251/SPO/2014/116 Dt.25th June 2025.

Sir

 Kindly reference to your letter no. cited above regarding the contribution of Special Police Officers, I am furnishing herewith the required information as follows.

 With the approval of Govt. of Mizoram, Home Department, 15 (fifteen) Special Police Officers have been engaged by CID (SB) till date for collection of real time and actionable intelligence input for the security and peace of the State of Mizoram.

 As of today, 3 SPOs are engaged from Bangladesh, 3 SPOs from Myanmar, 2 SPOs from Tripura, 4 SPOs from Assam and 3 SPOs from Manipur.

 These SPOs engaged in the bordering States and Countries have collected and shared a real-time intelligence inputs regarding activities of insurgents groups operating within their respective areas and the borders.

 With reference to the above mentioned subject, it may be inform that CID (SB) is actively monitoring the activities of insurgent groups operating in the neighboring States and across the borders to safeguard the security of the state and the country. Collection of real-time, reliable and actionable intelligence inputs requires engagements of dependable sources and feelers. Given the risks involved and the regrettable historical incidents of sources and feelers losing their lives, providing regular pay checks to trustworthy sources and feelers engaged as Special Police Officer (SPO) has greatly aided in inspiring and recruiting volunteers.

 Owing to their crucial and vital role played in safeguarding the State’s Security, CID (SB) presently engage 15 (fifteen) nos. of Special Police Officers on Muster Roll (MR) basis (with DA) in 3 (three) neighboring States and 2 (two) Countries as shown under:

 1. Bangladesh  – 3 SPOs

2. Myanmar  – 3 SPOs

3. Tripura  – 2 SPOs

4. Assam   – 4 SPOs

 5. Manipur  – 3 SPOs

  SPOs have proved their mettle and worth over the years in collection of real-time and actionable intelligence inputs. And, continued strengthening of intelligence network across the State’s border by way of appointment of trustworthy person from the concern locality of the area as SPO is considered crucial for maintaining the hard-earned peace of the state.

 For continued engagement of SPO, the Govt.’s OM No.A.11012/3/2010-P&amp;AR (ARW)/49 Dt. 28th June,2016 point no. 1 (ii), has required the concerned Department to submit proposal for extension of engagement of Muster Roll employees to the Government on or before 20th December each year.

 However, the proposal for continuous engagement of 15 (fifteen) Special Police Officers has been submitted late for the running period i.e.1.3.2025 – 28.2.2026 due to unintentional omission to submit the same and it may be mentioned that the 15 SPO’s are still engaged till date.

 In view of the above, it is therefore, prayed that necessary steps may be taken to obtain renewal permission for engagement of 15 (fifteen) nos. of Special Police Officers on MR Basis for another period 1.3.2025- 28.2.2026 at the following rate:

1. Daily Allowances  - Rs.240 per day.
2. Ration Allowances - Rs.550 per month.

Enclosed : 1. Copy of Engagement Cards of Special Officers

  2. Copy of Engagement Order

Yours faithfully

**(STEPHEN LALRINAWMA)**
