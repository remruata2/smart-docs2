SAIPHAI  AREA CHHUNGA HPCD/MBDA INCHHAL TE CHET DAN LEH POLICE TEN AN MAN DAN CHUNGCHANG

                        HPC(D) Founder President Lalhmingthanga Sanate hi Mizoram Sorkar leh HPC(D) ten kum 2018 a inremna (Peace Accord) Signed a nih pawh a lo chhuak ve duh lo tun thlenga kaihhruai tlem te nei a la awm a ni a, Hmarkhawlian Cachar, Assam ah a nupui fanau te nen tun thlengin an la awm a ni.

                        Kum 2021 December thla lai hawl khan a pawl HPC(D) a mi ni thin Army Chief hlui RC Singson @ Ramchanlova, a nupui fanau te nena Thenzawl, Mizoram a awm chu mobile hmanga be pawp in Assam leh Mizoram ramri chungchangah hmalakna tur a awm thu leh, chu hma la tur a tan chuan HPC(D) hming ni lo hming dang, "Mizoram Border Defence Army"(MBDA) a phuah tawh thu hrilh in a awm na Hmarkhawlian ah chhuk thla vat turin a hrilh ta a ni.

                        Lalhmingthanga Sanate in a kohna ang chuan RC Singson chu Dt.15.1.2021 a Sumo in Hmarkhawlian lam pan chuan a chhuk ta a, hemi ni vek hian Hmarkhawlian chu a thleng a Lalhmingthanga Sanate te In ah a awm ve nghal a, ni thum a awm a, chumi chhung chuan an hma lak dan tur te an sawi dun ta a ni.

                        Lalhmingthanga Sanate chuan Assam leh Mizoram ramri chungchang ah hma lak a tul thu Mizoram lama Action Committee te nen pawh an sawi ho thin thu a lo hrilh. (Action Committee te hi tu te nge hriat a ni lo). Chu mi a hma la tur chuan HPC(D) hminga hma lak erawh a remchang dawn lova, chuvang in "Mizoram Border Defence Army"(MBDA) hming in hma lak tur a ni dawn a, he pawl ah hian RC Singson @ Ramchanlova Singson chu ho tu ber Commissioner/Commander-in-chief a nih tur thu leh a hnuai ah Commander dang Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea chu Saiphai area Command tu a ni ang a, John Lalhmangaihsanga NC Hills lam mi chu Tuiruang dung Commander a nih tur thu leh volunteer dang pawh an awm thu a hrilh.

                        MBDA in hma a lak hmasak dan tur chu hetiang hi a ni ang :-

            (i)        Assam leh Mizoram ramri a duty Assam Police te hi kah phawt tur a ni        anga, Ramri hnaih a Vai(non-mizo) awm te hi tih thaih/vau an ni bawk ang.

            (ii)       Silai leh a mu tam zawk ngaihtuah phawt a tul ang.

            (iii)     Silai leh a mu te chu NSCN(IM) hnen a tang in lei tum tur a ni.

            (iv)      Silai leh a mu leina tur pawisa neih phawt a ngai a, chumi tur chuan             Saiphai leh a chhehvel a vai te hnenah pawisa (Donation) khawn phawt tur a ni.

                        Heng hma lak dan tur hrang hrang te an sawi chhung hian Lalhmingthanga Sanate chuan HPC(D) in Silai an kawl sa leh an neih mek chu an Cadre zinga pakhat Rorelsanga @ Rela of Lungthulian Manipur chu a va lak tir a, a ma chenna inah Rorela chuan rawn phur in a vai in AK-47=10 leh .32 Pistol=1(pakhat)  chu RC Singsona chu a entir ta a ni.

                        Tihtur leh sawi tur tul te an sawi hnu in RC Singsona chuan an Cadre zinga mi pahnih te hruai in Dt.18&19.12.2021 khan Hmarkhawlian a tang in Saiphai lam pan in an lo chho leh ta a, he tah hian Pistol .32 = 1 chu a rawn keng chho nghal a, Silai lian erawh a rawn keng lo.

                        Saiphai an thlen hmu hian a thian te pahnih Biaka leh Sisiama te chuan Hmarkhawlian pan in an kirsan leh ta a, a mah in ni 2/3 a cham hnu ah Sangtea (Lalngaisanga) chuan a rawn fin ve ta a ni. Sangtea nen chuan Donation khawn dan tur te an sawi dun a, Saiphai leh Saipum lam a vai (non-mizo) an mahni support tu Jamala leh Basira te nen chuan inhmukhawm in "Demand Letter" pek dan tur te an rel fel hnu ah, Saiphai leh Chemphai Area a vai, leilet hnathawk a awmchilh tu te hnen ah chuan 25/26 December 2021 vel khan Saiphai area lamah Demand letter chu an sem ta a, 23/24 December 2021 vel khan Chemphai area ah an sem hmasa phawt a ni.

                        Hetianga Donation demand letter an sem tih Police ten an hre chhuak tih an hriat veleh hian khawchhungah pawh awm tha ngam lovin ramhnuai ah leh Mauchar, Palsang khaw lamah te an biru kual a, a langsar lo ho, an zinga mi thenkhat te erawh chu khawchhungah an awm thung.

KHAIKHAWM NA (CONCLUSION)

                        He pawl " Mizoram Border Defence Army" (MBDA) hi pawl phuah chawp, Lalhmingthanga Sanate HPC(D) President in a vuah chawp, Cadres pawh engzat mah awm lo, Actives Cadres chu RC Sanga @ RC Singson a leh Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea te chauh hi an ni a, member/volunteer recruitment tih chu an tum mek a, Hmarkhawlian a tang in Lalhmingthanga Sanate hian a control ni in a langa, HPC(D) zinga mi Biaka leh Sisiama te chauh khi operation an neihna Saiphai area thleng ve te an ni a, a bak chu civil mi mizo leh vai an sawm chawp te an ni.

                        An thil tum ber a an sawi dan in, Silai leh a mu man tur tuak chhuah a, an indaih tawk an hmuh hun ah, Assam Police ramri a duty te chu kah an tum ni in an in sawi. Kum 2021 a  Saihapui 'V' leh Assam ram inpawhna Lei(Bridge) bula Bomb tih puah leh a puak lo (Missfired) an chhar te kha Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea leh a thian te tihpuah leh an kam a nih thu an mahni hian an sawi a, he tih lai hi ZDA(Zoram Defence Army) an lo invuah tawh thu an sawi bawk.

                        He pawl MBDA ah hian mi pangngai a chhiar tlak tumah an awm lova, an hmalak tum dan hi thil chenfakawm leh Mizoram tana thatpui na tur thil ruahman na mumal engmah a awm lova, pawisa hmuh theih nan chhuanlam ah, pawl hming hi Lalhmingthanga Sanate hian a hmang in a ma hmathat nan mai heng mi te hi a chhawr ni in a lang.
