A. _Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 27.8.2013_

**Police celebrate Departmental Sports Champion**: Mizoram Police celebrated its championship in the recently held 26th Inter Departmental Sports 2013 with Pu AK Patnaik, ADGP at PHQ Hall yesterday ie, 26.8.2013. Mizoram Police won 2 Gold medals, 1 Silver medal and 1 Bronze medal to be the champion. The function was presided over by Pu H.Ramthlengliana, SP, CID(SB). ADGP made a speech in this function and promised to reward all the medal winners.

B. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 27.8.2013_

****LSA Total Bandh called off**: A 24 hours total bandh at Lawngtlai organised by Lai Students’ Association General Headquarters yesterday ie, 26.8.2013 in protest against the State administration’s failure to post Sociology and Geography teachers at Govt. Region High School, Lawngtlai was called off @ 10:00 AM yesterday as the government promised to take initiatives to fulfill their demand.**

C. _Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 27.8.2013_

**Bandh at Khawzawl**: Khawzawl District Implementing Demand Committee intends to organise total bandh on 12th September 2013 at Khawzawl from 5:00 AM to 5:00 PM to urge the government to function Khawzawl as a District Headquarters which was declared on 1st September 2008.

D. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 27.8.2013_

**Picketing at Zawlnuam stopped**: MZP Zawlnuam continue picketing against government offices and closing of schools at Zawlnuam till yesterday ie 26.8.2013 to urge the government to post more lecturers, teachers and other employees at the Zawlnuam institutions. The meeting of MZP leaders held yesterday decided that the picketing of government employees should be stop from today ie 27.8.2013.
