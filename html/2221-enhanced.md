****CHIN REFUGEE RESIDING AT DELHI****

 There are around 3000 Chin (Burma Refugee) in Delhi, they were residing at Deklhi West, Janakpuri and Uttamnagar, the leaders of Chin refugee Committee are as follows :

Among the refugees, there are around 15 families of Myanmarese National (Kawl) and the rest are the clans of Chin and Khumi. The refugees are looked after by United High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR).

As reported by the leaders of Refugee committee, before the outbreak of Covid-19 and execution of Lockdown, the refugees have no problems at all, but, after the execution of Lockdown, the refugees faced many problems especially in essential commodities supply. Due to this problems, the following essential commodities items were distributed to the refugees by the UNHCR on 6.4.2020 :-

1. Buhfai - 15 kgs per family
2. Dal - 1 kg “  “
3. Tel - 1 litre “ “
4. Chini - 1 kg “ “
5. Soap (Dettol) - 20 nos. Rs. 5/- per piece per family

 As of now, they have only small quantity of rice to be distributed to the needy, and are shortage of money to be used by them, reported by the leaders. They are also likely to be reported to the Government of Mizoram about their problems.

 Fortunately, there are no positive case of COVID-19 at the moment.
