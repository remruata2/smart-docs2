SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/384

Dated: 11th June, 2019.

 

 

            Information has been received that Village Council, Zote, Champhai District is convening a public meeting tonight, i.e. 11.6.2019 @ 7:30 p.m at Community Hall, Zote to deliberate on the decision of Cabinet Meeting held on 10.6.2019 for shifting of District & Session Court, Champhai District from Zote area to CHANEM, Champhai. This public meeting was convened in pursuance of the decision of All NGOs Zote meeting held last night, i.e. 10.6.2019 wherein the decision of the Cabinet Meeting was vehemently opposed.
