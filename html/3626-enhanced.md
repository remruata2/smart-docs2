**6.6.2022 :** North East Students' Organisation (NESO) which comprises of the Khasi Students' Union (KSU), All Assam Students' Union (AASU), Naga Students' Federation (NSF), Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP), Twipra Students' Federation (TSF), All Manipur Students' Union (AMSU), Garo Students' Union (GSU) and All Arunachal Pradesh Students' Union (AAPSU) in its Press Release issued on 6.6.2022 expressed its deep shock over incident of bomb attack that took place on 5.6.2022 @ around 3:15 a.m. at the gate of Little Flower School, Sangaiporou, Imphal, Manipur. It strongly condemned such cowardly act and demanded the concerned authorities to take necessary legal actions against the perpetrator(s).

****23.11.2022 :** North East Students' Organisation (NESO) in its Press Release issued on 22.11.2022 stated that NESO is deeply perturbed by the incident which occurred on the same morning along the Meghalaya-Assam border in West Jaintia Hills District of Meghalaya and West Karbi Anglong District of Assam, which led to loss of 6 (six) precious lives and caused injuries to many people as a result of police firing and violent actions. It also stated that NESO is sending its condolences to all the bereaved families who met with such sudden and tragic loss. It further stated that NESO once again strongly urged upon all State Governments to be committed to solve the long pending problem once and for all and if necessary, the Central Government should be a party in these dialogues and NESO on its part will continue to encourage peoples to peoples contact and peoples to peoples relationship along the border areas. It also informed all the indigenous peoples that the said issue is not an ethnic issue but of border dispute in which the respective State Governments are duty bound to solve.**
