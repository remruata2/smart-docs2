**A. Front page of MIZO AW/ VANGLAINI/ The Zozam Times newspaper Dt.22.1.2013**

****Life imprisonment for murderer:** Constable Lalnunmawia of 2nd IR Bn. who was accused of murdering constable Lalfakmawia of the same battalion was convicted to life with rigorous imprisonment and a fine of Rs.5,000 by the District and session Judge, Pu T.Sangkunga. According to report, Lalnunmawia murdered the victim on the night of 9th January 2009 near the gate of 2nd Bn. IR Head Quarters, Khawzawl and the reason is believed to be jealously over his wife.**

**Repairing of roads**: The Aizawl Traffic S.P. make an announcement that due to the repairing of roads between Vaivakawn and Company Peng, the road between these areas will remain closed for traffic from 22-25 January from 7am in the morning to 7 pm in the evening.

**C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.22.1.2013**

****Inauguration of CCTNS Pilot sites by DGP**: The DGP of Mizoram, Mr.Alok Kumar Verma,IPS, inaugurated Crime and Criminal Tracking Network and Symstem (CCTNS) yesterday at Aizawl Police Station. This sites, which will assist the police for tracking and investigating criminals and crime is already installed in 25 places of Aizawl district. The whole of Mizoram is expected to be covered by this project by the month of July this year.**
