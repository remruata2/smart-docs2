MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/191

Dated 17th March, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/186
Dt. 16.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

25 (twenty five) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-14, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 17.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

36 (thirty six) vehicles (HMV-12, MMV-19, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 17.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-6, LMV-1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

71 (seventy one) vehicles (HMV-51, MMV-2, LMV-12, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 17.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

138 (one hundred thirty eight) vehicles (HMV-61, MMV-25, LMV-18, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-11, Oil Tanker-21, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 17.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
