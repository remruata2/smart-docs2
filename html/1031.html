Involvement in heinous crimes so far

:

1) On 25.10.2007, Pu Chawnghmingthanga Headman of Chhippui village (Bangladesh) and a son with 3 businessmen while crossing Hnahva village (Mizoram) were stopped at a gun point by 2 (two) Chakmas suspected to be Jana Sanghati Samiti of Chakma insurgent group operating inside Bangladesh and demanded Rs. 30,000/- from them. Pu Chawnghmingthanga and friends gave them Rs. 1,200/- to the Chakmas as it was all they had. The Chakmas while pointing at them with SBBL gun were not happy with such small amount of money and warned them to pay the demanded money in full after their return.

       Information received indicated that the said militants frequented Hnahva village and normally stayed at the house of one Chakma who had a big house having orange garden at Hnahva village.

 

2) On 4.11.2011 @ 4:00PM, Pu Thangnghinglova @ Vala of Dinthar veng, Aizawl and Ramliana of Pukzing were reportedly confronted by 2 (two) JSS volunteers and extorted Rs. 500/- at gun point on the outskirts of Hnahva village (Mizoram side). It was further reported that the JSS insurgent outfit are having temporary camp on the bank of Aivapui river near Hnahva and occasionally collected taxes from the Mizo people passing through the area.
