Point No. 11- Foreigner registration and tracking :

 

 * On 21st May 2025, Lengpui Airport Foreigner Cell came into function under FRO/SSP CID (SB). There are 2 (two) counters at the Airport – Arrival counter and Departure counter which are manned by 2 police personnel (MRP) in each counter.

 

*  Strength of Foreigner Cell (Lengpui Airport) : 1 (one) ASI, 1 (one) HC and 3 (three) Constables, Total – 5 (five). Due to shortage of staffs, no off days are enjoyed by the existing staffs as they have to perform their duties 7 days a week.

 

* At the departure counter i.e. inside the terminal main entrance, 2 (two) police personnels are stationed. However, the CISF personnels are not permitting our staffs to occupy the Helpdesk which is lying vacant inside the terminal main entrance.

 

* On 11th April 2025, all Hotels within Aizawl District have been issued an Advisory Note regarding compliance report of  Form C.

 

* On 7th Feb. 2025, all District FRO’s/District SP’s other than Aizawl have been issued regarding the compliance report of Form C.

 

* Foreigner arrival form is kept in all Entry-Exit points.

 

* As soon as the arrival of foreigners, the dedicated staff conducted all follow-up-action and formalities by contacting the foreigners and the Hotels in which they lodged.

 

* Guard duty for holding area is under the purview of SP Security and presently, 15 Security personnels are posted for the same.
