MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/471

Dated 22nd June, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/468
Dt. 21.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

           1(one) Platoon of 225th Battalion CRPF arrived Saikhawthlir BOP today i.e., 22.6.2021 @ 2:40 p.m. from Zophai, Kolasib District to reinforce the existing border duty at the area. Situation is normal.

  

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

           14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-7, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 22.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-4, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-12, MMV-2, LMV-5, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

116 (one hundred sixteen) vehicles (HMV-58, MMV-9, LMV-31, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-2, Oil Tanker-9, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-6, LMV-14) entered Saiphai from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
