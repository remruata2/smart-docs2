A. _Front page of VANGLAINI/TAWRHBAWM/MIZO ARSI newspapers Dt.28 & 29.7.2013_

**New Fire Station**: Last Saturday, the Home Minister along with members of Local Council of Kulikawn discussed proposal for a new Fire Station at Ngaizel and even visited the spot.

  The proposed new Fire Station site was first leased by the Local Council to AH&amp;Vety and Fisheries Department. However, as the need for the station increases, negotiation to hand over the said land to Fire Department was conducted between these concerned department and the Local Council members.

1. _Front page of MIZO ARSI newspaper Dt. 29.7.2013_

****PRISM lodge FIR against Serchhip DC Office**: PRISM lodge an FIR at ACB because of the withdrawal of funds from DC Office, Serchhip using fake seal. Some of the funds intended for development of the district were withdrawn using the name of R. Lalremsiama, Chairman, Thenzawl Welfare Committee. However, according to PRISM, the said R. Lalremsiama was not a Chairman in Thenzawl Welfare Committee and the seal used does not belonged to Thenzawl Welfare Committee. To enquire the foul play which were evident in this case, FIR was lodged by PRISM.**
