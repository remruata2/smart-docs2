**(i)** **Latest update of terrorist activities in Mizoram**:

 On 28.3.2015 @ 8:50 Am, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance members such as Pu R.L. Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Pu Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Nl. Lalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) Insurgent group numbering around 12 cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village. Three (3) Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while five (5) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about one (1) hour and forty five (45) minutes. The MLAs were accompanied in this tour by Government officials such as Pu Zothansanga Ralte, Under Secretary, Pu Lalrinawma Sailo, Committee Officer, Pi Lalchuailovi, UDC, Pu Zohmingthanga, Peon and Drivers - R. Hmingsanga and Lalpekhlua. In this connection Sakawrdai Police Station Case No. 6/15 dt. 29.3.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120B/326 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act has been registered.

 Information has been received that laying of the ambush was carried out under the command of Malsawmkima Hrangchal who deserted the 1st Bn. MAP (on 11.7.2014) and joined the HPC(D) outfit. The HPC(D) cadres were carrying sophisticated arms like AK- 47 and G-3 rifles. The HPC(D) outfit targeted the pilot vehicle which went ahead of the other vehicles.

**(ii)** C**ross-border terrorist linkage in Mizoram**:

 1) An armed group of 8-10 cadres of NLFT(BM) led by S/S Lt. Bikram Bahadur Jamatiya@ Bomthom were seen on 16th February 2015 at about 8:30am on Indian Border near Badalchuri Nallah opposite to BSF BOP Amchurimukh, Ex-181 Bn. BSF. Further, it is learnt that the group also have 2/3 unarmed cadres in civil dress with handbags and were moving along the border. The above mentioned 2/3 cadres in civil dress may intrude into Indian Territory to recce the security forces movement and to prepare a blueprint for next kidnapping inside Indian Territory.

 2) On 28th April, 2015, movement of 4 (four) cadres of National Liberation Front of Tripura/Biswa Mohan (NLFT/BM) in camouflage dress was noticed along the Indian Border inside Bangladesh territory opposite BSF BOP Amchurimukh PS-West Phaileng, Dist.-Mamit, Mizoram.

 3) In the morning of 14th may, 2015 a group of (6/7) NLFT/B militants led by S/S Sgt Santosh Debbarma @ Situng accompanied by S/S Cpl. Laxmi Bhum Halam, Sunil Tripura, Dagalam Jamatia, Sujit Debbarma and others wielding sophisticated arms was sighted lurking at 4 km from Chakma Basti area (near tri-junction of Tripura, Mizoram and Bangladesh), PS Bagaichari, dist-Rangamati, Bangladesh (opposite Khantlang BSF BOP, under PS-Vanghmun, dist-North Tripura). The group is waiting for an opportune moment to enter Tripura/Mizoram and may cause heinous crime like kidnapping in bordering areas of Khantlang (PS-Vanghmun), Bandanima (PS-Anandabazar) and Rajivnagar, PS-West Phaileng, Dist-Mamit, Mizoram and may also target security forces deployed there.
