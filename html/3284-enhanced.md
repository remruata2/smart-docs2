A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 20.6.2013_

****VC bye-election in HPC(D) demand area**: Mizoram State Election Commission is preparing for Village Council bye-election to be held within the month of July, 2013 in the HPC(D) demand areas, 15 Village Councils are to be elected in 13 villages of Aizawl district and 2 villages of Kolasib district.**

 These 15 villages faced law and order problems during the VC general election held on 23.2.2013 and election was cancelled; the VC term was extended due to the problems and as the term will end on 25.8.2013, Mizoram State Election Commission decided that VC Bye-election should be held within the month of July, 2013.

 The 13 villages of Aizawl district are:

 1. Zokhawthiang 2. North Tinghmun

 3. Mauchar 4. Zohmun

 5. Palsang 6. Sakawrdai

 7. Khawpuar 8. Vaitin

 9. North Khawdungsei 10. Thingsat

 11. New Vervek 12. Damdiai

 13. Lungsum

 The 2 villages of Kolasib district are:

 1. Saipum 2. Saiphai

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 20.6.2013_

****Minister collapsed in office due to overload of work**: Pu Zodintluanga, Minister, UD&amp;PA, etc. was taken ill while working in his office at around 3:30PM yesterday, he was treated by a team of doctors inside his office chamber, and was later brought to the Civil Hospital, Aizawl. He is out of danger now and the doctors said his collapse was due to tiredness and heavy load of work.**
