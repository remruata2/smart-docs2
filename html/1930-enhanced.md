**Annexure -I**

**Seizure of a large number of AK-47 rifles on 28th September, 2020:**

On 28th September, 2020 @ 10:00 p.m. BSF party led by SK Pillai, Second in Command, Office of Commandant, 90th Battalion BSF acting on specific input about delivery of Arms and Ammunition laid ambush at approximately 7(seven) kms towards West Phaileng at the outskirt of Phuldungsei, Mamit District. Two vehicles viz. Mahindra Thar B/R Mz-01 K-5262 and Mahindra Bolero Camper B/R MZ-01 L-4949 proceeding towards Phuldungsei were stopped by the ambush party and after a thorough search of the vehicles the following Arms and Ammunitions were seized from the vehicles:

1. 28 (twenty eight) Nos of 7.62 AKM
2. 1 (one) No. of 5.56 AKM,
3. 1 (one) No. of US Carbine .30
4. 5398 (five thousand three hundred ninety eight) Nos. of 7.62 mm Ammunitions
5. 2490 (two thousand four hundred ninety) Nos. of 5.56 mm Ammunitions
6. 6 (six) Nos of .30 Ammunitions
7. 26 (twenty six) Nos of AKM Magazines
8. 1(one) Nos of 7.62 mm AKM Belt Ammunition Box Type
9. 1 (one) No. of US Carbine .30 Magazine.

Three persons viz. (1) Lalhuapzauva (56) S/o Thangchhunga of Phunchawng (Driver of Mahindra Thar), (2) JH Liansanga (46) S/o Hrangtinkapa of Phunchawng (Tenant of Lalhuapzauva), (3) Vanlalruata (25) S/o Thangnghinga of Ramhlun South (Driver of Bolero Camper)were immediately apprehended. In this regard, FIR was submitted to Marpara Police Station and a case vide Marpara PS C/No. 1/2020 Dt. 29.9.2020 U/S 25(1A)/35 Arms Act r/w 120B IPC was registered and investigation is underway.

The following steps were taken during the course of investigation:

1. The three accused persons namely 1) Lalhuapzauva (57) S/o Thangchhunga of Phunchawng, Aizawl 2) Vanlalruata (25) S/o Thanghlira of Ramhlun South, Aizawl and 3) JH Liansanga (46) S/o Hrangtinkapa of Phunchawng, Aizawl were arrested on 29.9.2020 and brought to West Phaileng PS under proper escort. The arms and ammunitions as well as the vehicles used for transportation were seized and brought to West Phaileng PS for safe custody.
2. A requisition was sent to the SP, Aizawl to conduct house search in respect of the accused persons on 29.9.2020. House search was immediately conducted and reported that no arms and ammunitions were recovered from the residence of the accused persons.
3. The accused persons were produced before the Court of CJM on 30.9.2020 with a prayer for Police Remand for 72 hours. A joint interrogation of the accused persons was conducted the same day under the supervision of SP, Mamit.
4. The accused persons were interrogated in successions during the remand by the SIT.
5. One of the accused persons JH Liansanga of Phunchawng was forwarded to judicial custody on 02.10.2020 on medical reasons.
6. A requisition was sent to the SP, Aizawl to provide CCTV footage of Bawngkawn, Vaivakawn and Hunthar Peng respectively for a specified period for analysis and necessary examination.
7. A requisition was submitted to the SP, CID (SB) to provide CDRs in respect of the accused persons. The mobile phones of the accused persons were carefully examined to find any suspicious communication or other electronic records which could be used as leading recovery/evidence for the case investigation.
8. A prayer for 96 hrs Police Remand in respect of accused persons, Lalhuapzauva and Vanlalruata was again submitted to and allowed by the Court of CJM, Mamit on 03.10.2020. A thorough interrogation was conducted against the accused persons during the remand period.
9. The two accused persons 1) Lalhuapzauva (57) S/o Thangchhunga of Phunchawng, Aizawl and 2) Vanlalruata (25) S/o Thanghlira of Ramhlun South, Aizawl were forwarded to judicial custody on 06.10.2020.
10. Further interrogation was conducted on 07.10.2020 against the accused persons.
11. On 28.10.2020, the IO submitted prayer to CJM, Mamit to record confessional statement of accused persons. However, all the accused persons declined to give confessional statement before the court.
12. On 16.11.20, the seized arms and ammunitions were sent to FSL, Aizawl for examination.
13. The case investigation is pending for waiting FSL report

1. Source and destination of the weapons?

= In light of the investigation, the weapons are believed to have come from Myanmar and the date and time of arrival in Mizoram could not be ascertained as the arrested accused persons claimed that they are carriers only and does not have any idea regarding the source of weapons. The arrested accused persons believed that the seized weapons are intended to be delivered to Leaders of United Peoples' Democratic Front (UPDF), a Chakma Militant outfit based in Chittagong Hills tracts of Bangladesh as per verbal conversation between one of the absconder accused Bhaia(full particular not known) and the intended buyer.

1. Smuggling network involved?

= So far, involvement of proper smuggling network could not be ascertained as the arrested persons claimed that they are carriers only and accuses Pu Liana @ Zaneihliana of Tahan, Myanmar as the owner of weapons and the accused persons stated that he (Pu Liana of Tahan) delivered the weapons by one 207 vehicle (details of vehicle and driver not known)to the house of Pu Lalhuapzauva of Phunchawng, Aizawl on 26.9.2020. The accused persons along with Bhaia (absconder) proceeded to Phungdungsei to deliver the weapons on 28.9.2020. They stated that accused absconder Bhaia was the one who knows the intended buyer.

1. Whether the smuggling network is only smuggling arms or is it also a part of any other contraband smuggling nexus.

= In the light of the investigation, there is no suspicion regarding involvement of any other contraband smuggling nexus.

**Annexure –II**

**Seizure of huge quantity of Detonators at Zokhawthar on 8.10.2020**

On 8th October, 2020 @ 10:30 pm, ASI David Lalthapuia of Zokhawthar PS submitted a suo-moto FIR to the effect that on the same night at around 10:00 p.m. while he was on duty at Zokhawthar PS, Officer Commanding A coy, 8thAR, Zokhawthar produced 10 cases of Detonator (10 cases x 100 packets x 100 pieces= 1,00,000 pieces) value of Rs.20 lakhs alongwith Hmangaihzuala (41) s/o Rengkhuma of Zokhawthar, Lalchhuanmawia (42) s/o Phirthanga and Lalrinmawii(43) w/o Lalchhuanmawia all of Ruantlang Champhai who were suspected to be the owners of the seized detonators. After conducting thorough enquiry, the involvement of Lalrinmawii (43)w/o Lalchhuanmawia of Ruantlang was not found. Hence, she was set free from the liability of the instant case. He then submitted FIR to OC, Zokhawthar Police Station for registering a case. Hence, ZKTR- PS C/No. 11/20 Dt. 8.10.2020 U/S 5 (a) ES Act r/w 34 IPC was registered and duly investigated into.

During investigation, the case IO arrested the accused persons Hmangaihzuala(41)s/o Rengkhuma of Zokhawthar and Lalchhuanmawia (42) s/o Phirthanga of Ruantlang, Champhai and forwarded to CJM, District Court Champhai for judicial remand on 9.10.2020. Further investigation revealed that the main accused was Hmangaihzuala (41) s/o Rengkhumaof Zokhawthar and that he hired Lalchhuanmawia to transport the said detonators towards Myanmar side claiming that it was ‘Khaini (tobacco). Hence, the IO sent prayer to discharge co-accused Lalchhuanmawia(42) s/o Phirthanga of Ruantlang, Champhai to CJM, Court, Champhai as his actual involvement into the case was not found.

The sample of seized detonators was forwarded to Director, Forensic Science Laboratory, Aizawl for examination by expert. FSL report was received and it stated that the metallic tube are live Detonators (blasting caps) and are in working condition.

Prosecution Sanction u/s 5(a) ES Act against the accused Hmangaihzuala (41)s/o Riangkhuma of Zokhawthar was obtained from the District Magistrate, Champhai District, Champhai. After completion of investigation, case has been charge sheeted vide Charge Sheet No. 10/2020 dt. 18.11.2020.

1. Source and destination of the detonators?

= In the light of the investigation and as per the statement of the main accused Hmangaihzuala(41) s/o Riangkhuma of Zokhawthar, one Zungmanga of Tahan, Myanmar was the owner of the seized detonators and as per his instruction they ferried the detonators from New Champhai, Mizoram from unknown persons with an intention to deliver at Indo-Myanmar border, which will be arranged and informed by Zungmanga of Tahan. The seized detonators are intended to be delivered to Myanmar and the arrested accused person does not have any idea where and whom to deliver the detonators. It is believed that it may be used for road construction as demand is very high in Myamnar.

1. Smuggling network involved?

= So far, involvement of proper smuggling network could not be ascertained as the arrested persons claimed that they are carriers only.

1. Whether the smuggling network is only smuggling arms or is it also a part of any other contraband smuggling nexus.

=From the light of the investigation, there is no suspicion regarding involvement of any other contraband smuggling nexus.
