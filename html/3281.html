A. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 14.6.2013

Police seized 1296 bottles of IMFL: Vairengte Police seized 63 cases of alcohol/liquor (IMFL) ie, 756 bottles on 12th June 2013 near MAMCO Checkgate, Vairengte from truck JK-02 AA 3975. The driver Onkar Singh (32) s/o Joginder Singh told the police that one Mizo woman and a man of non-Mizo asked him to carry alcohol/liquor (IMFL) for ` 15,000/- and relented. On the night of 12th June, 2013, Kawnpui Police seized 540 bottles of alcohol/liquor (IMFL) from a truck TK-02 AF 6069 driven by Gurpreet Singh s/o Amrik Singh of Jammu. The driver said that one Mizo woman hired him to carry it for ` 1,000/- from Bagha.

 

B. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 14.6.2013

Police officers’ training: 3 days training on ‘Gender Sensitization & Investigation of Crime Against Women’ organized by DIG(Trg.) at ATI Training Hall started from 12.6.2013 will be over today ie, 14.6.2013. 6 Inspectors, 31 Sub-Inspectors and 17 Asst. Sub-Inspectors attended the training.
