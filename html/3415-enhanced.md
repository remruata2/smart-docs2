A. _Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 15.1.2014_

**Search Operation From Today / ‘It is hopeful that the abducted will be released’-Police****:** MZP and its supporting groups are to start a ‘social work to search for missing persons’ Lalzamliana, Sanglianthanga and Deep Mandal who were abducted from Dampa Forest on 23rd November 2013 and their families did not receive any fresh news about them till yesterday evening which prompted MZP and other groups to start their social work. Meanwhile, heads of Police Department said that in their talk with the abductors it was agreed that the abducted will be released by last night.

 Heads of Police Department had a meeting yesterday regarding the social work to search for missing persons to be organised by MZP. The meeting was attended by DGP-in-charge V.Lalthakima, DIG (Hqrs.), LR Dingliana, SP Aizawl, Lalbiakthanga, Commandant 1st Bn. MAP, Lalhuliana Fanai, Cammandant 3rd Bn. MAP, H.Ramthlengliana, SP CID(SB) and other Police officers.

 The police reported that representatives of Mizoram government and the abductors had a talk, and the abducted are to be released by last night according to their talk but at the same time Mizoram government representatives are not sure if the abductors would really do that.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 15.1.2014_

**A Man Found Dead****:** Hrangmana(55) of Tuipui ‘D’, Lunglei District was found dead in the ravine at the outskirt of his village near ‘Sailuta Vau’, 100 mtrs. away from the road. He had been missing from 13th January 2014 and his families and friends searched for him but in vain, and found his body in the ravine later at 12:00pm yesterday.

 After Hnahthial Police performed inquest, his body was sent to the hospital for postmortem. As injuries were found in his body he was thought to die in the hands of someone and the police are investigating the case.

C. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 15.1.2014_

**YMA to Send 11 Persons to Aizawl for Violation of ILP****:** Inner Line Permit (ILP) of non-Mizos living in Sakawrdai village were checked by Young Mizo Association (YMA), Hmar Students’ Association (HSA) and Village Council (VC) of Sakawrdai for the first time on 13th January 2014, 8 non-Mizos without ILP and 3 non-Mizos who misused ILP were nabbed. They were handed over to the Sakawrdai Police but the Police have a problem in transportation, so YMA is to send these arrested persons to Aizawl in a bus hired by YMA.
