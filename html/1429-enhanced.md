 With reference to your letter No. and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:

1. Many of the surrendered militants have stated leading a normal life. Mizoram State Government has been providing government jobs as far as possible to these surrendered militants.

1. According to the Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) signed between Government of Mizoram and Hmar Peoples Convention (Democratic) (HPC(D)), on 2nd April, 2018, Government of Mizoram is to take steps for relief/rehabilitation of all HPC(D) underground personnel hailing from Mizoram coming over ground and implement an ex-gratia/rehabilitation scheme.

1. 5 (five) rounds of Peace Dialogue was conducted between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) within the year 2016 and 2017. Finally, on 2nd April, 2018, Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) was signed between the 2 (two) parties followed by the Arms Laying Ceremony of HPC(D) on 13th April, 2018.

1. Not applicable in Mizoram.

1. As a rehabilitation package, Government of Mizoram distributed an amount of Rs. 3,00,000 each to personnel of HPC(D) coming over ground.

1. Considering the standard of their living, the rehabilitation package of Rs. 3,00,000 given to HPC(D) cadres coming over ground are considered to be sufficient to start a new life.

1. At present, there is no proposal in the State of Mizoram to raise any battalion consisting of surrendered militants.
