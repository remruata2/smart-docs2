A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 16.10.2012

 

"Nothing to say in VC election" - Sangbera

"Political party visit us in Jail" - Ropuia

HPC(D) Chairman H.Zosangbera said that they had nothing to say regarding HPC(D)'s threat not to file nomination in the VC election at 15 villages of northern side of Mizoram. Lalropuia, Army Chief, HPC(D) revealed that certain Mizoram political parties came to Central Jail to visit them.

     The three leaders of HPC(D) being detained in Central Jail by Mizoram Police were brought to District Court yesterday and responding to Vanglaini's question, Sangbera said that as they were prisoners they had nothing to say concerning HPC(D) not allowing to file any nomination in the VC election. He said they were in no position to say whether the act of their fellow HPC(D) members were good or bad.

     Lalropuia blamed Mizoram Govt. for their arrest. He said that Mizoram Govt. deceived them by telling them that an arrangement was made to have proper discussion. Instead, the Mizoram Govt. detained them and that now they were very displeased with Mizoram Govt. He also said that member of certain opposition parties and ruling party came over to Central Jail to see them but he was not sure if they were sent by Mizoram Govt. to pay them visit.

     Their lawyer said concerning the pleas of bail for the three HPC(D) leaders, the District Court allowed 21, High Court allowed 3 and another 6 are still pending.

     H.Zosangbera was caught on 17.7.2012 at Delhi by Mizoram Police and Delhi Police, Lalropuia and Lalbiaknunga were caught on 10.6.2012 at Silchar Airport by Mizoram Police.
