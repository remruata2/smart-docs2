****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/194

Dated 18th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/191 Dt. 17.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV-6, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 18.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-15, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 18.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

71 (seventy one) vehicles (HMV-50, MMV-1, LMV-15, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 18.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

145 (one hundred forty five) vehicles (HMV-63, MMV-37, LMV-20, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-16, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 18.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
