DSI 6th November, 2018-

(a) Reference to our DSI dated 5.11.2018, Item No. B(a). NGO Co-ordination Committee organised ‘Hnam Chhan Hnatlang,’ demanding removal of Shri S.B. Shashank, CEO, Mizoram today, i.e. 6.11.2018. The main event was held in front of CEO Office, Raj Bhavan North Gate, Aizawl @ 10:00 a.m and around 40,000 (forty thousand) members of NGOs within Aizawl City participated in this agitation. Pu R. Lalngheta, Vice President, CYMA presided over the meeting and representatives of CYMA, MUP, MHIP, MZP and MSU also delivered speeches. The agitators raised slogans and also displayed banners and placards showing demand for removal of CEO from Mizoram as well as their objection against casting of votes by Bru refugees in Refugee Camps. In support of this agitation, most of private schools, shops and petrol pumps, etc. remain closed. The agitators dispersed peacefully at 12:15 p.m.

 ‘Hnam Chhan Hnatlang’ was also organised at District capitals (except Siaha and Lawngtlai), Sub-Divisions and some villages in support of the initiative taken by NGO Co-ordination Committee as under:

 a) Kolasib District: DEO’s office was picketed and a crowd numbering around 1,500 partcipated. The District NGOCC submitted a Memorandum requesting DC, Kolasib to reject 100% of Bru appeal forms on the following grounds; i) Commitment of ECI was made in 2014 MP Election that Electoral Roll Revision will be held only inside Mizoram and Brus will cast their votes in their respective places in Mizoram in the next Assembly Election; and ii) New Bru voters from Transit camps were enrolled after official hearing date was over which was in contravention of Notification of ECI.

 b) Serchhip District: The agitation took place from 8:00 a.m. to 2:50 p.m. and around 700 people gathered at the junction leading to the DEO's office.

 c) Champhai District: DEO's office was picketed from 9:00 a.m. to 12:40 p.m. in which around 2,500 people participated. Government offices, shops and educational Institutions were also closed.

 d) Lunglei District: DEO's office was picketed from 7:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. by around 5,000 people. Government offices, educational Institutions and shops were also closed; and

 e) Mamit District : A Total Bandh was organised from 8:00 a.m. to 12:15 p.m. where Government offices, educational Institutions and shops remain closed during the Total Bandh and around 200 persons participated. DC office was re-opened after the closure of the Total Bandh.

 In Lawngtlai and Siaha Districts, eventhough the DEOs' offices were not picketed; the Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) and Mara Thyutlia Py (Young Mara Association) expressed their support of the initiative of the NGO Co-ordination Committee.

 The residents of Lengpui, Sihhmui, Phunchawng and Rangvamual also demonstrated their support of the NGOCC by lining up along the roadside in their respective localities donning 'Mizo Pawn Dum' during the movement of ECI Team from Lengpui Airport to Aizawl today.
