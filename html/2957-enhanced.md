****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/49

Dated: 23rd January, 2019.

In continuation of our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/46, Dt. 22.01.2019: Executive Committee meeting of the Ex-Hmar Volunteer Association (HPC) held today, i.e. 23.1.2019 at Sakawrdai had adopted a resolution criticising the Government of Mizoram for extending the interim period of Sinlung Hills Council (SHC) and expressed their disapproval of the newly appointed members of the Council describing it as a total disregard of the Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) signed between the State Government and the HPC(D). The Association demanded the State Government to respect the MoS and to rescind its appointment order immediately. In the meantime, the Ex-HVA (HPC) also appealed the appointed members to decline the appointment.
