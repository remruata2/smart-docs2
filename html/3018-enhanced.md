****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/770

Dated: 11th October, 2019.

An organisation named ‘HPC(D) General Headquarters Sinlung’, held its Council Meeting under the leadership of Lalkhama @ LK Hmar today, i.e. 11.10.2019 at their Office located at Churachandpur, Manipur and 14 (fourteen) members attended. The meeting discussed the upcoming SHC Election in Mizoram scheduled to be held on 5.11.2019 as well as their organisational set-up.

The meeting welcomed the steps taken by the Government of Mizoram to conduct the first SHC Election after the signing of Peace Accord between the Government of Mizoram and HPC in 1994. The meeting agreed to play their part for successful and peaceful conduct of the SHC Election and further resolved to issue a Press Release informing all the Hmar community members that HPC(D) Gen. Hqrs. Sinlung is against any attempt to disrupt the electoral process by any individual or organisation. The PR is expected to be issued tomorrow for circulation in Manipur and Mizoram newspapers.

The meeting also agreed to officially appoint their leaders at the earliest. At present, the following are the designated leaders of HPC(D) Gen. Hqrs. Sinlung :-

Commander-in-Chief  -  Lalkhama (LK Hmar) of Churachandpur

Chief Organiser  - Laldina of Mual Vaiphei.

Commander  -  Lalthangsiem of Vairengte (P/A Churachandpur)

Commander  -  Laldemlova (LD Hmar) of Churachandpur

Commander  -  Lalpeklawm (Peka) of Churachandpur

Commander - R. Lalbieklien of Vairengte (P/A Hmarkhawlian, Assam)

HPC(D) Gen. Hqrs. Sinlung was formed on 12.4.2019 at Churachandpur, Manipur with the main objective of taking up the cause of families of HPC(D) Martyrs who are yet to receive monetary assistance from the deduction of Rs. 80,000/- each made from 63 (sixty three) HPC(D) cadres, amounting to Rs. 50,40,000/- who came over-ground with the signing of the Peace Accord. They are demanding for payment of monetary assistance to families of HPC(D) Martyrs. As per input received, they have no intention to disturb Mizoram or raise fresh demand.

Situation is under close watch.
