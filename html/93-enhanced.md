****Statement of Malsawma Royte, Inspector of Police CID(SB)****

 Ka hming leh ka nihna chu a chunga ka ziah ang khi ani a, tunah hian CID(SB) Field Staff ah ka awm mek ani.

 Const. Lalremruata hian a absent hnu a office a alokal leh hnu pawh in engmah Field i/c ka nih ang pawh in harsatna a tawh chhan leh a absent chhan engmah mi hriattir na a neih ka hre lo.
