Aizawl District-ah hian Village Council 69 a awm a, Village Councils 8 te chu hripui COVID-19 leng mek avanga hripui inkai darh hlauhawm tak dinhmunah an awm avanga Sorkar-in Containment Area-a puan, Inkharkhip/Lockdown, Contact Tracing leh test result nghah mek a nih avangin an inthlan hun sawnsak an ni a. State Election Commission, Mizoram chuan heng khua te dinhmun thlir chung leh Health &amp; Family Welfare Department rawn chungin inthlan hun tur ruahmanna thar a siam a, ni 4.9.2020 hian heng khaw 8 (pariat) ah hian inthlanna tluang taka neih a ni. Heng Village Council 8 (pariat) te inthlanna atana mawhphurtu ber Deputy Commissioner &amp; District Election Officer, Superintendent of Police, Observer, Assistant District Election Officer, Sector Officer-te, Returning Officer-te, Presiding Officer-te, Polling Officer-te, Booth/Village Level Officer-te, Police-te inpekna avangin inthlanna tluang taka zawh fel a lo ni a, State Election Commission chuan an chungah lawm thu a sawi a ni. COVID-19 laka kan him theihna tura sul sutu, Health &amp; Family Welfare Department-in Election Duty te leh mipui himna tura Commission-in a mamawh apiang tihsakin thurawn tha tak tak a pe a, State Election Commission chuan a ngai hlu hle a ni. Inthlan chungchanga mipuite, political party te leh candidate te hriat tur pawimawh State Election Commission-in a tih chhuah, thu thar, rang taka min puan chhuahsak thintu Information &amp; Public Relations Department-a thawk zawng zawng te leh Media te hnenah State Election Commission chuan lawm thu a sawi bawk a ni. Hetih rual hian, COVID-19 hripui leng karah Village Council inthlanna buatsaih a tul a, State Election Commission chuan Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) leh Mizoram Peoples’ Forum (MPF)-te sawmin Mizoram Sorkar-in Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) a siam te, State Election Commission leh District Election Officer-in inkaihhruaina a siam te ti hlawhtling tura thawhpuiah a sawm a. Mipui te himna tur pawimawhzia hre rengin an tha leh zung, an hun hlu tak sengin Local/Village Level Task Force te nen thawk hovin ruahmanna fel tak siamin veng tinah inthlan an buaipui a, mipui pawhin heng ruahmanna te hi kan zawm tha hle niin a lang a, inthlanna felfai tak a awm theih avangin State Election Commission, Mizoram chuan an chungah lawm thu a sawi tak meuh meuh a ni. State Election Commission, Mizoram chuan Village Council member thlan thar zawng zawngte lawmpuina a hlan a, hripui leng kara Democracy innghahna pawimawh tak Village Councils General Election ti hlawhtling tura mipui vote thlaktu zawng zawngte chungah lawm thu a sawi bawk a ni. By Order etc. of State Election Commissioner (TERESY VANLALHRUAII) Secretary, State Election Commission, Mizoram.
