Investigation Report

            Accused Lalmuankima (29) s/o Lalhuapliana, Mahmuam, Bangladesh was apprehended by CID (SB) Staff on 21.5.2005 at Phuldungsei village in Mamit District (near Indo-Bangla border) while trying to transport the arms consignment into Bangladesh.

 

            The interrogation revealed that the consignment was given to him by one Liana (detail particulars not known) at Phuldungsei on 21.5.2005 @ 8:00 AM. The said Liana told him to deliver the consignment to one Mukto, leader of UPDF (Chakma underground outfit) at Mahmuam village and Mukto would give him Rs. 20,000/- as commission. However, he was not told the exact price of the consignment.

 

PREVIOUS DEALING OR INVOLVEMENT: His involvement in arms smuggling case in the past had never been known.
