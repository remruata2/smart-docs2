            Information has been received regarding transportation of cattle for smuggling from Mizoram to Bangladesh through Indo-Bangladesh Border areas which creates border security threats and causes drain of resources of the BSF.

            The following personnel of CID(SB) are instructed to conduct thorough inquiries on transportation of cattle meant for smuggling across the Indo-Bangladesh Border under the supervision of  Inspr. JH Chunglianthanga of Field Branch :

 

1) SI K.Zozamliana, SO, Lawngtlai.

2) ASI C.Lalremruata, SO, Lunglei

3) ASI F.Vanlalsawma, SO, Tlabung

4) C/33 Lalrinthanga, SO, Chawngte

5) C/96 LC Mawia Chawngthu, SO, West Phaileng

 

            All SOs are instructed to enquire the matter thoroughly in their respective places and submit their report within this month ie, February, 2020 to Inspr. JH Chunglianthanga of Field Branch.

 

            Inspr. JH Chunglianthanga of Field Branch then sum up their findings and submit the report to Sr. SP, CID(SB) at the earliest for further submission to higher authority.
