A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 15.10.2012

 

No one had filed nomination for VC election: No one had filed nomination to the Returning Officer till 13th October 2012 for the V/C election to be held on 25th Oct. in 13 villages of Aizawl District and 2 villages of Kolasib District. Time to file nomination will end at 3pm today, i.e, 15th Oct. 2012.

 

 

 

B. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 15.10.2012

 

Abusive drugs seized at Lengpui Airport: Triprolidine (HCI) and Pseudoephedrine (HCI), Kratcold (5,54,649 tablets) which are  used for preparing abusive substances were seized by Lengpui Airport Security from 2 minor boys at Lengpui Airport on 13th Oct. The 2 minor boys were boarded plane flew in from Kolkata to Lengpui Airport. The boys claimed that they were just the carriers of these substances which were sent by some people and that they did not even have the key to unlock the bag. As such was the situation, security had to destroy the lock, however, the boys later revealed they had the key. The boys claimed themselves from New Saiha and Chanakyapuri, New Delhi. The medicines were all unwrapped and weighed 94.20kg, it was worth 3 lakhs. The boys and their medicines were handed over to Sairang police and case has been registered.

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post.

 

 

 

C. Front page of The Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 15.10.2012

 

Two people swept away by landslide still not found: The dead bodies of 2 people namely, Lalnunsiama s/o Hualdingliana, Driver, Aizawl to Kawrthah Maxi Cab and Mujib Uddin s/o Basail, Karimganj, Assam, JCB Operator, who boarded JCB and swept away by landslide at Aizawl to Mamit road near Tut river are still not found till yesterday. Many volunteers from Dapchhuah, Luangpawl, Mamit, Kawrthah, Rawpuichhip, Rengdil, West Phaileng and Hmunpui came to search for the dead bodies.

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post, The Zozam Times.

 

 

D. Second page of The Zozam Times newspaper. Dt. 15.10.2012

 

Crowd gathered at Police Station to show displeasure at attempt to rape: Last night, People of Tanhril gathered at Aizawl Police Station as there was a rape attempt of 2 girls. The crowd threw stones and something at the Police Station as they wanted to see the accused detained at the Police Station. No big problems arose as the police showed the accused persons to them and the crowd dispersed after this.

 

On 12th Oct. at about 2:00pm, 2 girls from Tanhril - a girl of 17 yrs. and a girl of class 12 student were on their way to visit their relatives at Tuivamit. On their way, they met 2 men namely, Lalhmachhuana(50) s/o Khawchhana Armed veng South P/A Ramrikawn and Mangchhuan (25), Tahan P/A Sakawrtuichhun, and went together towards Tuivamit. The house of their relatives was locked as they were not at home and the 2 men grab the chance and attempted to rape these 2 girls at the house just below the girls' relatives' house which was rented by someone. People who were near the place heard the scream of the girls and immediately made a move to rescue, fortunately, the accused persons did not succeed.

 

The culprits were handed over to the police by Tuivamit YMA. Tanhril YMA is quite displeased at Tuivamit YMA for giving information to them only after they took care of the matter.
