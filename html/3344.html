A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 18.9.2013

Pseudoephedrine Seized: Champhai Police seized 10,22,193 tablets of Pseudoephedrine in Zokhawthar Maxi Cab regd. no. DC 1/YC 2201 yesterday. The driver of this Maxi Cab Vanlaltluanga s/o Daniel Taunuk, Myanmar was also arrested. The drugs worth ` 81,77,544/- in the local market.

 

B. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 18.9.2013

Election Training: Election training was held yesterday at Saikuti Hall, Lunglei for Security personnel of MRP and 2nd Bn. MAP under the leadership of Addl. SP Lalrinawma. V.Sapchhunga, Lunglei District DC was the Chief Guest in the training.

 

C. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 18.9.2013

Arrangement for Vehicles on Kawnpui to Khamrang Road: PWD and ABCI will mend the dilapidated road between Kawnpui and Khamrang road in a faster pace from today onwards. For better speed in the mending work an arrangement for vehicles plying this road is made. According to the arrangement made by Kolasib District Police and PWD(NH), vehicles will be blocked between 8:00AM to 1:00PM and from 2:30PM to 5:30PM. The arrangement include the up and down of any vehicle plying on this road.
