(c) Operation against KCNA/their activities across the border in BD, its likely impact in Mizoram :

On December 15, 2024, at around 11.00 am, a meeting was organized by the Bangladesh Army at Bawm community hall, Ruma. The Bangladesh Army Brig. General Mehedi Hasan of 69 Bandarban infantry Brigade was present along with Lt. Col. K.M. Arafat Amin of 28-BIR, Ruma zone at the meeting. Rev. Lal Zarlawm Bawm, the president of Bawm Social Council, Bangladesh and several Bawm villages Karbari were also present at the meeting.

 

            It is learnt that the Bangladesh Army delegates affirmed the under mentioned two points during the meeting.

            1. The Kuki National Front/ Kuki Chin Nationa Army (KNF/KNA) should surrender and lay down their weapons within December 2024.

            2. The Bangladesh Army would continue their operation unless the KNF/KNA surrenders before the Bangladesh Government.

 

            Information received further indicated that Lal Zarlawm Bawm held a separate meeting with the Bawm villages Karbaries and local Bawm elected members wherein it resolved to try to convince KNF/KNA to surrender and lay down their arms before the Government. It further resolved that if the KNF/KNA does not yield to their demands, Bawm social council, with the support of several Bawm community leaders would defy and resist KNF/KNA movement. 

 

            It is to be mentioned that up till now there has not been any reaction from the leaders of KNF/KNA.
