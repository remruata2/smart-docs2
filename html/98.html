1.Arakan Army cadres buying Medicines from Lawngtlai:- This has been verified by SO Lawngtlai that no such purchase of medicines happens.This is also corroborated by our source from Arakan Army that such purchase did not happen since the camp and operational area of Arakan Army presently is very far from Lawngtlai.

2.Some Mizo youth joining Arakan Army:- Information received indicated that there was no report of any Mizo ethnic joining Arakan Army or recruited till today. In the meantime, there was a report of Arakanese coming from Arakan State in Myanmar and settled in the villages viz. Hmawngbuchhuah, Zochachhuah, Kaki and Kakichhuah, all Lawngtlai District long time back. It cannot be ascertained whether some person belonging to these villages joined the cadre or not.

3.Arakan Liberation Army/Arakan Liberation Party having camp in Lawngtlai dist.Mizoram:- Input received indicated that the following hideouts/ camps of Arakan Liberation Party group are established at the following locations:

Camps along with families at Duduksora of Parva II area (in the middle of Parva III and Bandukvanga) under Lawngtlai District. It is 1.5 – 2 km to the North of Parva BSF Camp. Around 10-15 cadres staying at this camp and commanded by Mr. Vieya Ka. ALP gathered foods and other essential necessities at the residence of one Chakma villager at Sangarasury of Parva area and then supply to other hideouts.

Another camp is situated on the bank of Ahzasora river(which flows to Parva river) 2 km south from Parva 1 and 4.This report is received from our source @ Arakan Army.

It may also be added that the ALP is patronized and utilised by Myanmar Army as well as Assam Rifles.
