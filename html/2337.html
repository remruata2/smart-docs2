MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/451

Dated 11th June, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/446
Dt. 10.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

          Information received stated that Shri. Rohan Kumar Jha, DC, Hailakandi, Assam; Smt. Ramandeep Kaur, SP, Hailakandi; Assam Forest Officials and around 70 (seventy) Assam Police personnel and 30 (thirty) civilians from Gutguti village visited Pu John Halliday’s Farm, located around 1.7 Km from Chuhvel, Mamit District inside Mizoram territory today i.e., 11.6.2021 @ 3:00 p.m. They had a brief interaction with Pu John Holliday at his Farm House and informed him that the area falls within Assam territory, and at the same time Pu John Halliday informed them that state Boundary Pillar during the British period was far beyond from his farm and his farm is well within Mizoram terriorty, and the matter should be discussed at the higher level. After interaction, Assam officials left the area @ 3:30 p.m.
At the time of their visit, a section strength of 3rd MAP personnel headed by SI were present.

Information received also stated that such a huge contingent of security personnel visit caused fear and panic among residents in surrounding areas and female workers from Chuhvel village working along the inter-state border fled to their village in fear.  Upon learning their presence, around 30 (thirty) residents of Chuhvel village immediately rushed to the area and by the time they arrived, Assam officials already left the area. In this connection, leaders of YMA from Zamuang and Saikhawthlir also visited Chuhvel village today’s evening to take stock of the situation. Situation is normal.  

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

           8 (eight) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-5, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 11.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-13, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 11.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no  vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
