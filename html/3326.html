A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 24.5.2013

Operation reviewed meeting : C.Zorammuana, DIG(NR), Mizoram presided over a reviewed meeting of police rescue operation at Laipuitlang disaster at his office chamber on 22.5.2013. The meeting expressed satisfaction over the police rescue operation and hoped for better performance in the future. The meeting discussed about acquiring better equipments for rescue operation in case of recurrence as such at Laipuitlang and Ramhlun Venglai disaster and discussed how to improve performance in case of such disaster. They also expressed gratitude to the general public for having trust in police and giving them free hand for  rescue operation.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 24.5.2013

Pseudoephedrine Seized : A joint team of Special Narcotic Police Station Staff and   26 Assam Rifles seized 140.465 Kgs. of Pseudoephedrine worth around  ` 63 lakh on Wednesday @ 9:30PM and arrested the alleged owners Hmingthansangi of Electric Veng and David Lalrinfela of Mualpui.
