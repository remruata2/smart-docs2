ABDUCTION OF 11(ELEVEN) NON-MIZO MUSLIM PERSONS AT RAJIV NAGAR ON 10.10.2014

Case reference : West Phaileng Police Station Case No. 20/14 Dt. 11.10.2014 u/s  364 A/392/34 IPC R/w 25 (1A) Arms Act.

            On 10.10.2014 at around 9:30 PM, a combined operation team of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) numbering  12 (twelve) stopped one Tata 207 pick-up vehicle bearing Regn. No. TR-05-1780 driven by one Urun Kanti Chakma S/o Joy Kumar Chakma, Rajiv Nagar in between Zozampui 5th IR BOP  and Rajiv Nagar, Mamit District ( at about 3/4 kms away from Zozampui 5th IR BOP and near the tri-juntion i.e. Mizoram-Tripura-Bangladesh border ). There were 20 occupants in the vehicle and the vehicle was coming from Kanchanpur, North Tripura. After stopping the vehicle, the militants immediately took 15 occupants to the jungle leaving behind 2 (two) minor girls, 2(two) female & 1 (one) male occupants (all Chakmas) at the place of incident. Later on, the militants released 4 (four) Chakmas from the jungle and taken away/abducted the remaining 11 (eleven) Bengali Muslims towards Zopui village inside Bangladesh. Out of the 12 militants, 10(ten) of them were dressed in camouflaged uniforms and carrying sophisticated weapons and the remaining 2 (two) militants were in civvies.

      All the 11 (eleven) abducted  Bengali Muslims were resident of Neelam Bazar and Patharkandi of Karimganj District, Assam and came to Mizoram to engage in construction works of Rashtriya Madhyamik Siksha Abhiyan(RMSA) building at West Phulpui village, Mamit District. The name of the contractor of RMSA building is Pu. Hranghuaia of Phuldungsei Vengthar, Mamit District. The identities of abducted persons could not be ascertained at the time of writing this report.

            Mizoram Police team under the supervision of Pu.PC Lalchunglura,Addl.SP Mamit and Pu. Zasangzela Dy.CO 5th IR Bn immediately proceeded to the place of occurrence with one platoon of Special Commandos. Pu.Lalmalsawma Hnamte, SDPO West Phaileng and a team of CID SB led by Pu. Goverdona Sailo Dy.SP have also proceeded to the place of incident on 11.10.2014.

      Information received stated that leader of the abductors contacted family members of two abducted persons viz. Shahis Ahmed and Jiya Uddin of Patharkandi on 12.10.2014 @ 6:00a.m and informed their families that the abducted persons were safely in their hands at their (NLFT) Camp inside Bangladesh. The leader of the abductors told the family members that they should collect at least Rs. 30 lakhs and pay to the militant group for the safe return of the hostages.

      Further information received from another source revealed that the hostages along with their abductors reached Tarungpara camp of NLFT near Zopui village of Sazek range, Bangladesh on 12.10.2014at 03:00a.m.

            The 11 (eleven) Bengali construction workers abducted by a combined group of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) on 10.10.2014 were released on 31.10.2014 @03:30am after being held captive inside Bangladesh jungles for 21days.

            It was learnt that families of the hostages had paid Rs.2,75,000/- ( Rupees Two Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand), 6 (six) mobile handsets, 2(two) wrist watches and mobile top-up (Airtel/Aircel) worth Rs.5000/- as ransom for their release.

            The hostages were released at Udulsuri (Chakma village) inside Bangladesh. They were later escorted upto the border fencing near Rajiv Nagar by Bangladesh based Chakma insurgents i.e. JSS (Jana Sangha Samiti) where they were received by 181 Bn. BSF @ 02:00pm. The released hostages are being brought to W.Phaileng by SDPO W.Phaileng for medical check up and other necessary formalities.
