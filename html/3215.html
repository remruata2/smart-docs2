A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 24.7.2013

2 villages alarmed by threat: Pangbalkawn & Meidum village received letter to donate for suspected underground bru militants, the people of these villages are perplexed and alarmed, and reported the matter to Bairabi Police. Bairabi Police probed into this threat and visited the villages; the police got information about the suspected senders of donation letter from the villagers. Due to this threat, night armed duties are detailed to comfort women and children.

     A new hamlet with around 50 houses is being built at Pangangzau in Sabual near Pangbalkawn by bru families, the people of Pangbalkawn & Meidum villages told the police that they had suspicion on the brus who newly resided in this hamlet about the threat.  

 

  1. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 24.7.2013

FIR lodged to get back lost bundles of GCI sheets: Lalhawngliana of Chamring lodged an FIR to Aizawl SP on 23.7.2013 stating that the 3 and a half bundles of GCI sheets he had received from RD Block under Rural Housing Scheme was taken away by leaders of Chamring congress unit without his consent/knowledge.
