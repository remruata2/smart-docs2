****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/603

Dated: 4th August, 2021

 Information received stated that Lalmuanawma (25) S/o R Lalrintluanga, Bungthuam, Mamit district, who proceeded for Kanhmun to collect essential commodities driving his Bolero Pickup B/R No. TR-05 C-0380 was waylaid by 4 (four) unidentified armed miscreants near Bobawi bridge, which is around 3.5 km from Bungthuam and he was robbed at gunpoint of cash amounting to Rs 28,450/-. As per the statement of victim Lalmuanawma, the 4 (four) unidentified miscreants were armed with 3 (three) local made guns (Ulhbun) and 1 (one) Miniature Rifle and they covered their face with cloth (gamchha) at the time of commission of the offence. He also stated that the miscreants immediately went towards Luimawi village after commission of the offence and their whereabouts is presently unknown.
 The matter was immediately informed to Kanhmun Police Station. Pu Lalthanfala Khiangte, SDPO, Kawrthah and OC, Kanhmun PS and team immediately rushed to the PO. A team of Police and civilians of the nearby villages conducted search of their trail, but no clue has been found at the time of writing the report. A case vide Kanhmun PS C/No. 14/21 Dt. 4.8.2021 u/s 392/34 IPC r/w 25 1B(a) Arms Act has been registered and investigation is being underway.
