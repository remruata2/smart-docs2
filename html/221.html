DSI Dt 30.8.2018 - Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) Gen. Hqrs., Aizawl in their Press Release issued today, i.e. 30.8.2018 alleged that Shri. Pravindra Yadav, Accountant General, Mizoram who was recently posted in Aizawl, Mizoram has failed to respect the sentiments of Mizo community as well as the Tribal community. Leaders of MZP highlighted that Shri. Pravindra Yadav has initiated the non-renewal of 30 (thirty) Contract Employees under Accountant General Office, Mizoram and in this regard, MZP accused Shri. Pravindra Yadav of avoiding MZP who had made attempts to represent the welfare of the Contract Employees. Leaders of MZP urged Shri. Pravindra Yadav to take necessary steps for Contract renewal of Contract Employees under the Accountant General’s Office, Mizoram failing which leaders of MZP urged

Shri. Pravindra Yadav not to return to Mizoram. The Press Release also added that North East Students' Organization (NESO) extended support to MZP in which leaders of NESO stated that they will not allow Shri Pravindra Yadav to remain in North East India if he continue to disrespect the sentiments of the Tribals of North East India.

 

DSI Dt 31.82018 - Office of the Accountant General, Mizoram was closed for the second day today, i.e. 31.8.2018. Information has been received that the Comptroller and Auditor General of India held a meeting with the Accountant General, Mizoram this evening and the meeting is still going on while writing this report. It is also reportedly uncertain whether the Office of the Accountant General, Mizoram will reopen on Monday, i.e. 3.9.2018.

 

Reference to our DSI dated 3.9.2018, Item No. C(b). The Office of Accountant General, Mizoram resumed their office working effective from today, i.e. 4.9.2018. It has been reported that all the 30 (thirty) Contract Employees resumed their office work. No untoward incident is reported.
