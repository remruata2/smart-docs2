Sir,

 With reference to above, I am sending hereby reports on the CM’s remarks as under for your information and necessary action.

 On the morning of 21.3.2009, 2 children playing near Chakma Rest House, Chawngte found one AK-47 Rifle which turned out to be the AK-47 Service Arm lost by Const/247 C. Laltlanmawia of 2nd Bn MAP on 29.2.08 who was deployed for CADC election duty at Chawngte .

 After recovery of the AK-47 Rifle, 4 (four) persons namely (1) Santi Prasad (22) s/o Chandro Kanta of Kamalanagar-III, Chawngte (2) Subhas Mitre (22) s/o Berosen of Kamalanagbar-IV Chawngte (3) Dharmanath (19) s/o Mgul Dhan of Borapansury-I (4) Choipom (20) s/o Arun Bikash of Kamalanagar-I were arrested and all of them confessed their involvement stating that the said arm was forcibly snatched by them on the night of 29.2.08 because they were threatened by Const/247 Laltlanmawia with the arm while he was in a drunken condition.

 All the above given arrested persons were escorted to Lawngtlai by OC Chawngte P.S on 25..3.09 and he submitted petition to the Magistrate for revival of Chawngte PS C/No. 1/08 dt. 1.3.08 U/S 379 IPC which was registered due to the lost of arm but had been ended in submitting F.R.

 The 4 (four) arrested persons are being forwarded to Court for judicial custody.
