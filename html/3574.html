A. Front page of  VANGLAINI / THE AIZAWL POST  newspapers 

Dt. 15.7.2022

MoU signed between CYMA and PWD: For efficient monitoring of the constructions of highways and its maintenances in Mizoram, Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) and Public Works Department of Mizoram signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) yesterday i.e.14.7.2022. This is the second time such MoU were signed between the two parties. Some of the main points of this MoU are -

          a) Before the commencement of a large Project , a briefing and awareness programme on the project will be organised by PWD for the concerned YMA Branch/ Group/ Sub-Headquarters.

          b) An informatory boards , giving the details of the project will be erected on the affected towns and villages.

          c) A joint inspection will be conducted by a joint team of PWD officials and leaders of YMA according to conveniences.

          d) In case of unsatisfactory works, YMA will not single-handedly take actions but have discussion with PWD officials on the matter, who in turn will inform the responsible contractor.

          e) Written suggestion raised by YMA for improvement of highways will be properly received by PWD along with a reply given to Branch YMA on action taken report.

          f) Cutting of the any roads/highways for installation of PHED pipes will not be done without prior permission from PWD.

         

 

B. Front page of  THE AIZAWL POST  newspaper  Dt.15.7. 2022

Heroin Seized: A joint operational team of Champhai Police and 8th Assam Rifles on Wednesday (13.7.2022) seized 271 grams of Heroin No. 4 at Dawrkai Kawn, Champhai town which are worth an estimated value of Rs. 1,35,50,000. Two suspected accused were also apprehended along with the contraband items.
