****ORDER****

  All the foreigners who are stranded/staying in Mizoram due to the outbreak of COVID-19 are hereby informed that their exit from Mizoram at present is not possible. It is further informed that the Ministry of Home Affairs has decided to extend their visa on 'GRATIS' basis VIDE MHA OM No.25022/24/2020-F.V/F.I Dt.17th Apr. 2020 as below :

****"Regular visa, e-visa or stay stipulation, of such foreign nationals who have been stranded in India because of spread of COVID-19 in many parts of the world and also due to travel restrictions imposed by the Indian authorities and whose visas have expired or would be expiring during the period from 1.2.2020(Midnight) to 3.5.2020(Midnight) would be extended till midnight of 03.05.2020 on 'GRATIS' basis, after making online application by the foreigner. Exit to such foreign nationals, if so requested by them during this period, will also be granted to them upto 14 days beyond 03.05.2020 i.e. 17.05.2020 without levy of overstay penalty".****
