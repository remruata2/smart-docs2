A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.5.2014

Bandh at Kolasib Tomorrow?: The meeting of MZP Headquarters Kolasib and some NGOs of Kolasib yesterday made a statement that if government do not take initiative to fulfill their demands and if they do not receive positive news by 13th May, 2014, total bandh at Kolasib will be organised from 5:00am tomorrow that will last for 24 hours.

          They demand for the completion of NH - 54 & 154 as quickly as possible; for the establishment of Govt. HSS at Kolasib District as quickly as possible and to post doctors as they are supposed to be posted at Kolasib District Hospital.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.5.2014

Heroin Seized: Mizoram Police Special Narcotic PS Staff seized 13 grams of heroin at Falkland yesterday. The alleged owner Lalnunpuii (29) d/o Thahmuaka of Zemabawk was arrested. The value of the seized heroin is ` 60,000/-.

 

 

C. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 13.5.2014

Phantom Appeared at the Court: K.Chhawnthuama @ Mizo Phantom appeared before Laldinpuia Tlau, Magistrate of 1st Class's court regarding the infamous SMS he sent to the Chief Minister. Public Prosecutor RC Thanga appealed that the allegation report made by the Police on Phantom's case was sensible enough to take the issue further while Phantom's lawyer Lianhrima said that the case should be closed due to lack of evidence. Court's decision will be announced on 26th May, 2014.
