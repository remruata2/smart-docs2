8.7.2022  : A meeting was held under the chairmanship of Pu David L. Pachuau, Mizoram Joint Chief Electoral Officer & Acting CEO in connection with the upcoming Presidential Election on 8.7.2022 at Mizoram Legislative Assembly Committee Room. During the meeting, Pu H. Lalrinawma, Mizoram Legislative Assembly Commissioner & Secretary and ARO highlighted various important matters relating to the Election of the President of India, scheduled to be held on 18.7.2022 at Mizoram Legislative Assembly. The meeting decided that all officials on duty and MLA/MP who would cast their vote at the election must go through COVID-19 screening.

12.7.2022:     Pu H. Lalrinawma, Commissioner & Secretary, Mizoram Legislative Assembly & ARO held a Press Meet on 12.7.2022 @ 12:00 noon in view of the upcoming election of President of India. During the press meet, he highlighted their preparedness in connection with the stated election and schedule of election as follows:

            (i)        Date of poll - 18.7.2022 (Monday) 10:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

            (ii)       Place of poll - Committee Room, Main building, Mizoram Legislative                         Assembly.

            (iii)     Counting of votes will be held on 21.7.2022 at Returning Officer's                               Office, Room No. 63, First Floor, Parliament House, New Delhi.

 

18.7.2022 :    Election of President of India was held 18.7.2022 and in Mizoram, the election was successfully held at Assembly Secretariat Conference, Aizawl and all 40 (forty) MsLA casted their vote in the said election.
