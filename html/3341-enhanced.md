A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.9.2013_

**Huge Drugs haul****:** Champhai District Police recovered 5,13,700 tablets of Pseudoephedrine worth ` 46,23,300/- at Khankawn Police Checkgate from a newly purchased and unregistered 709 LPK Truck which came from Aizawl****on 11th September 2013 @ 4:30 PM. Champhai Police also arrested Lallianmanga (31) s/o Liana of Champhai Vengthlang - the alleged owner of both the drugs as well as the truck.

 Champhai Police also recovered 5,10,000 tablets of Pseudoephedrine worth ` 35,70,000/- on 24th August, 3013 and 40 kgs. of Ganja in the month of August 2013.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 13.9.2013_

**Saitual Total Bandh ended peacefully****:** Total Bandh organised yesterday at Saitual Town Area by Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee to demand for the functioning of district was so successful that it was called-off at 12:30 PM under a peaceful atmosphere.

 People of Saitual Town area co-operated very well with the bandh, government offices and business centres were all closed, no vehicle was in sight other than bandh duty vehicles, nothing untoward happened, said reports.
