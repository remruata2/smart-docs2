With reference to your directions on 4.3.2015, I , do hereby submit my report on character and antecedants of the leaders (Interim Body) of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee for your information and necessary perusal.

1.Rev. MZ.Lawmthang Sathing (78), S/o Mawnglinga(L), Lawngtlai Bazar. His educational qualification is BD (Bachelor of Divinity). He is a retired pastor under LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran) Church and is the owner of ML. Junior School in Lawngtlai Bazar. He is not involved in other activities besides being the President/Convener of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee.

2.Lalchunga Chinzah ( Ex- MLA, Ex- Chief Executive Member of LADC ) (82) S/o Lianhmunga (L) of Council Veng, Lawngtlai. He is presently an Adviser of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee. He belonged to LIKBK(Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran) church. He is at present occasionally involved in politics.

3.Biakchhunga Chinzah (60) S/o Zaluaia of Lawngtlai Council Veng. His educational qualification is Bachelor of Arts. He belonged to LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran) Church. He is also the Secretary of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee.

4.B.Lalhuma (56)S/o B.Sangchema (L) of Lawngtlai –III. His Educational Qualification is PU( Pre- University level). He is an Ex- MDC( Member of District Council) of LADC. 5. T.T Nohmung (67) S/o Sanaka(L) of Lawngtlai Council Veng. His Educational Qualification is Bachelor of Arts. He is an Ex- MDC( Member of District Council) of LADC. He belonged to Roman Catholic Church. He is also involved in politics but does not submit affiliation to any political party.

7.Rev. E.F Sangchem (72) S/o Hniarkunga (L) of Lawngtlai –IV. His Educational Qualification is BD (Bachelor of Divinity). He is a retired pastor under LIKBK(Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran).

9.F.Sangkima (36) S/o Biaktluanga of Council Veng ,Lawngtlai. His Educational Qualification is upto Cl-IX. He belonged to LIKBK(Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran). He is also involved in politics.

13.N.Lalzova (42) S/o. N.Lalmuana of Lawngtlai –IV . He is extensively involved in NGOs activities as well as in politics. His Educational Qualification is HSLC and he is also the President of Farmers’ Association, Lawngtlai District. He belonged to LIKBK (Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran).

As mentioned above majority of the members of Phawngpui UT Demand Committee belonged to Lairam Isua Krista Baptist Kohhran (LIKBK), they are fully involved in preparation works for organization of LIKBK Assembly which is slated to be held from 25.3.15 onwards in Lawngtlai town. The UT Demand Committee have not launch any initiatives with regards to achieving their goal. A joint meeting is expected to be convened by Phawngpui UT Demand Committee, comprising members from Chakma Autonomous District Council and Mara Autonomous District Council areas in the near future in which they will elect the office bearers for the said UT Demand Committee. Information received also stated that the extent of the demand area will cover the jurisdiction of Lai Autonomous District Council, Mara Autonomous District Council and Chakma Autonomous District Council.

It is also important to note that the above mentioned persons who are involved in politics have no permanent allegiance to any political parties. They are all susceptible to changing their stance depending on the situation in the political scenarios of Mizoram. The situation is closely watched.
