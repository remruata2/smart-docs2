SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/630

Dated:22nd August, 2019.

 

            Acting on specific input, Operation Team of CID(SB) stopped one Pick-up truck
(MZ-01/N-7137) coming from Zokhawthar, at a location near Tuikhurhlu today, i.e. 22.8.2019 @ 3:10 p.m. Upon conducting search of the vehicle, Operation Team recovered 1.092 Kgs. of suspected Heroin No.4 contained in 78 (seventy eight) soap cases. Proper seizure has been made at the spot by observing all formalities and case being registered. 2 (two) persons were arrested, viz.

  1. Thangliana (37 yrs.) s/o Bawiliana, Falam, Chin State, Myanmar and
  2. Zoliansanga (45 yrs) s/o Kaithanga, Khuangleng, Champhai District, Mizoram (driver of the Pick-up).

Value of the seized contraband is estimated to be approximately Rs. 39 Lakhs.

 

The driver had attempted to evade the team initially on being signalled to stop and sped past the team. Operation Team quickly chased the Pick-up for about 1 Km. and forced the driver to stop while accused Thangliana climbed to the back side from the driver’s cabin as if in an attempt to rid the contraband off the vehicle, but was forced to give up.
