A. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.15.3.2013

Fake Doctor arrested: KH Thangkhollal Baite (38) s/o Holkhothang Baite (L) of Rengkai, Manipur, who claimed to be a doctor and worked at Med-Aim 7th Day Adventist Hospital at Zotlang East, Champhai from June 2012 till 19th Feb., 2013. He was suspected by the hospital authority to be a quack on  his treatment of patients and prescription of medicines; Dr. H.Ramchullova, Hospital Director enquired about him at RIIMS, Imphal from where KH Thangkhollal Baite obtained his documents, he found out that all the documents were fake/duplicate and he used someone else’s name.

            Dr. H.Ramchulllova, Director of Med-Aim Hospital lodged an FIR at Champhai Police Station on 9th March, 2013. KH Thangkholal Baite was arrested at Imperial Hotel, Zarkawt, Aizawl while he was trying to flee to Manipur. The Police brought him to Champhai on 13th March, and he was forwarded to District Jail, Champhai.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.15.3.2013

9271 Police Personnel in Mizoram: Giving a written answer to the question of TT Zothansanga, R.Lalzirliana, Home Minister said that at the moment, there are 9271 police personnel and staff under Mizoram Police (MRP), Mizoram Police Armed Police Battalion (MAP) and Indian Reserve Battalion (IR) in Mizoram. He also said that there are 3763 Police in IR Bn., 2522 Police in MAP Bn. and 2986 Police in MRP(UB).

 

C. Front page THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.15.3.2013

Thief of church offering arrested: Lalzabiaka (30) s/o Lalkhama who claimed to hail from Electric Veng, Champhai was arrested by ‘Tlangval Association’ at Farkawn, Champhai Dist. He stole the offerings of Farkawn Presbyterian Church Sunday school which was kept in a safe by Church elder, H.Crosszara on 10th March, 3013. He broke the almirah and took Rs.10,000/- and a mobile phone, when he was arrested Rs. 5,300/- was recovered from him. He was handed over to Dungtlang Police.
