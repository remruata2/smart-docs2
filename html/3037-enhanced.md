****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/218

Dated: 19th April, 2020.

 As per input, 2 (two) police personnel of 5th IR Bn. viz., C/276 C. Lalhruaitluanga (32) s/o C. Lalnunsanga, Republic Veng, Aizawl and C/405 R. Zairemmawia (35) s/o R. Vanlalsawma, Hlimen, Aizawl, who were deployed for border sealing duty between Thaidawr and Zomuantlang village, Mamit District in c/w COVID-19 were allegedly assaulted by a group of Chakmas residing across the border in Tripura yesterday i.e., 18.4.2020 @ around 4:00 p.m. The two police personnel suffered physical injuries on their bodies and they were brought to PHC, West Phaileng early this morning i.e., 19.4.2020 @ around 7:00 a.m wherein they were given medical examination and necessary treatment. They were further referred to Civil Hospital, Aizawl for want of X-ray examination and were brought to Aizawl today @ 3:15 p.m and were admitted at the hospital. Their conditions are reported to be stable at the time of writing the report.

 SP Mamit accompanied by Addl. SP Mamit and SDPO West Phaileng proceeded to New Kawnpui early this morning to take stock of the situation. As per their report, the 2 (two) victims performed their duty at Thaidawr road duty point along with 3 (three) Chakmas from New Kawnpui-I, Tripura and 2 (two) Brus from Zomuantlang, Mizoram. Quarrel started as the 3 (three) Chakmas were asked and requested to stop playing a game of cards and to concentrate on their duties. One of the Chakma went to New Kawnpui-I and came back with re-enforcement which ultimately led to assault of the 2 (two) policemen. Accordingly, West Phaileng PS C/No 6/20 dt. 19.4.2020 u/s 186/326/332/353/34 IPS was registered to this effect. SP, North Tripura and Officer-in-Charge of concern Police Station have also assured to render necessary cooperation to nab the culprits.

 In this connection, Hlimen Local Council and Branch YMA issued a Joint Press Release later today condemning the alleged physical assault of the above two police personnel by Chakmas and demanded the Government to pay compensation and to bear their medical expenses. It also stated that youths of Hlimen, Aizawl will take necessary steps for finding out the culprits if the Government fails to bring them to book before end of 20.4.2020. Likewise, Republic Vengthlang Local Council and Branch YMA also issued a Joint Press Release demanding for arrest of the culprits, grant of compensation to the two victims and deployment of more police force along the border.

 Similarly, Dampa Group YMA issued a Press Release today condemning the incident and demanding for immediate arrest of the offenders. It also made arrangement for conduct of spot verification to further enquire into the matter. A group named ‘Mizo Thalai Pawl’ also issued a Press Release today demanding the authorities to find out the culprits and award them suitable punishment.

 _Cont/-2....._

-2-

 MZP General Headquarters also convened an Emergency Meeting of OBs and Advisers today @ 2:00 p.m at MZP Office and it resolved the following :-

1. Demanded the Government to immediately transfer Officer-in-Charge/Commander of Zomuantlang/New Kawnpui Border Outpost and to deploy additional police force within 24-hours.
2. All the culprits should be immediately apprehended or else Mizo youths will take necessary action.
3. Chakmas of Tripura should abstain from border guarding duty along the Mizoram-Tripura border.
4. Government should bear medical expenses of the two victims.

 MZP General Headquarters thereafter submitted a letter to the Hon’ble Home Minister requesting for necessary action with regard to the above mentioned points. Input received further indicated that OBs of MZP General Headquarters and some Advisers intended to visit New Kawnpui tomorrow i.e., 20.4.2020. They will be accompanied by leaders of MZP Sub-Headquarters, West Phaileng.

 At the same time, information has been received that 5 (five) leaders of MSU General Headquarters led by Pu J. Lalmuanzuala, Vice President intended to leave Aizawl for New Kawnpui tonight itself. They are planning to have a night halt at Mamit or somewhere convenient depending on the speed of their journey.

 3 (three) accused persons of New Kawnpui-I, Tripura have been apprehended at Vanghmun PS, Tripura to their alleged involvement in the above incident at the time of writing the report.

 Situation is under close watch.
