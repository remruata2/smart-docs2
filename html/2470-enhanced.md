****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/619

Dated 4th November, 2020

In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/617 Dt. 3.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 There is no movement of vehicles across Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border today i.e., 4.11.2020 due to roadblock organized on either side of the border.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Bairabi Joint VC and All NGOs emergency meeting was held yesterday evening i.e., 3.11.2020 @ 8:15 p.m. at Bazar Building, Zero Point, Bairabi South. Pu R. Lalrinawma, VCP, Bairabi South chaired the meeting and two SDC, Kolasib viz., Pu Timothy R. Lalhmangaiha and Pi Lalruatpuii Hnamte, and leaders of MZP, MSU, Joint YMA, Branch YMA, MHIP, MUP attended the meeting. During the meeting, they discussed government proposal to deploy 1(one) Company of BSF at Bairabi and after deliberate discussion the meeting was unable to come to a conclusion and decided to reconsider the matter again in another meeting. Leaders of Bairabi NGOs highlighted in the meeting that Police deployed at the border are not provided adequate ration, proper electricity etc. and the officials assured them to bring the matter to the concern authority.

There is no movement of vehicles across Bairabi border today i.e., 4.11.2020. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

 Buhchangphai MZP organized blockage of vehicles bound to Assam at Zukbual Mual, Bilkhawthlir to Phaisen road w.e.f., 3.11.2020 afternoon in connection with dismantling of bamboo wall constructed on the road at Assam side by residents of Dolchhora and nearby villages. 15(fifteen) vehicles are stranded due to the said roadblock. In this regard, 3(three) leaders of MZP Headquarters, Kolasib led by Pu Zosangliana Rokhum, President MZP Headquarters, Kolasib proceeded to Buhchangphai today i.e., 4.11.2020.

 DC, Kolasib held a meeting with leaders of MZP Headquarters Kolasib and MSU District Headquarters Kolasib regarding blockage of road at Buhchangphai today i.e., 4.11.2020 @ 11:00 a.m. During the meeting, DC, Kolasib requested leaders of MZP and MSU to discontinue roadblock

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

****Road block organized by Zoram Drivers’ Union (ZDU) at Tawngtaina Hmun, Outskirt of Vairengte is still continuing today i.e., 4.11.2020. There is no vehicular movement across Mizoram-Assam border at Vairengte and 165 (one hundred sixty five) vehicles are stranded due to the said road block till today.

 4 (four) leaders of All NGOs Vairengte led by Pu R. Lalfamkima, President, Joint VC Vairengte under the aegis of Vairengte Joint VC proceeded to Aizawl today i.e., 4.11.2020 @ 9:00 a.m. to discuss border issues with Chief Minister and Home Minister. They met Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister at his Office Chamber today afternoon. During the meeting, they highlighted the various problems faced at the border and the Chief Minister informed them that actions are being undertaken to maintain peace and tranquillity at the border areas and the government is committed to take necessary action to achieve amicable solution. They also submitted a written petition to Chief Minister, in which they requested the following:

1. To set up permanent Border Out Post at Vairengte at the current area of deployment.
2. To set up permanent IR Battalion Company Headquarters at Vairengte.
3. To constitute Border Area Development Board at Vairengte.
4. To renovate IOC Veng to Phaisen road.
5. To take necessary action for issuance of periodic patta in Aitlangzau, Sihpuizau leh Mautuiluizau.
6. To establish Emergency Operation Centre at Vairengte.

Situation is under close watch.
