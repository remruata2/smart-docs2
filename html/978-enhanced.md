**SAIHAPUI V VILLAGE**

 The Mizoram-Assam border is, according to Saihapui V Villagers, about 1 km from Saihapui V Village (the area is beyond the present IR duty post, which can be estimated as around half a Kilometre from this duty point).

 There is 1 (one) Mizo Anganwadi helper under Assam Government in Saihapui V Village with no building of their own where sporadic nutrition are also received.

 All eligible persons from this village possess Aadhaar Card from Mizoram State Government and are also included in the electoral roll of Mizoram State Government. Presently, the exact numbers of voters ID holder in this village cannot be ascertained.

 The inhabitants of this village are pro-Mizoram State who are willing to relinquish ration cards and other benefits available from Assam State Government.

**PHAINUAM VILLAGE**

 Phainuam Village is located at around 1 km South of Saihapui V Village.

 There is 1 (one) Mizo Anganwadi helper under Assam State Government in Phainuam Village with no building of their own. Moreover, there is also another 1 (one) teacher, employed in Assam Government School from Phainuam Village who is presently posted at Silchar.

 Two families from Phainuam Village had received Housing Assistance from Assam State Government.

 All eligible Aadhaar Card holders of this village are issued by Mizoram State Government. Enrolment of electoral roll are also done from Mizoram State Government. However, the exact numbers of voters ID holder in this village cannot be ascertained at present.
