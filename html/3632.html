26.8.2022 :    The newly elected Board of Directors of Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA), Aizawl held a meeting on 26.8.2022 at the Office Chamber of
Dr. R. Lalthangliana. The meeting selected YMCA OB 2022-2024 among the Board of Directors and appointed Dr. R. Lalthangliana as President and Dr. ZR Thiamsanga as Vice President. It also reviewed North East YMCA Annual General Conference and 77th World Peace Day held on 6.8.2022 at Synod Conference Centre and discussion was also held on their future course of action.
