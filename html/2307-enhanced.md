****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/63

Dated 29th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/60 Dt. 28.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV – 8, MMV – 5, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today, i.e., 29.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm.

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 11, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 29.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV –2) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 Pu K. Lalrinliana, Minister FCS&amp;CA visited Zophai IR Post today, i.e., 29.1.2021 @ 8:25 a.m. and handed over Rs. 40,000 for purchase of Cooler, Fan etc. The Minister requested Zophai BOP to conduct regular foot patrolling along the border, and assured them to take necessary action for provision of vehicle. He also inspected paddy fields located in between Zophai BOP and Assam Police Outpost, Golachhera to find out possibility for construction of Agriculture Link Road at the area and provision of electricity up to Ramri Lui, Assam-Mizoram border. The Minister was accompanied by Dr. H. Lalthlangliana, DC, Kolasib, Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib and other officials.

Pu Vanlalfaka, SP Kolasib and Pu Pabindra Kumar Nath, SP Hailakandi District, Assam held a short meeting today, i.e., 29.1.2021 @ 12:30 p.m. at Bairabi Police Station. The meeting decided that whenever any border dispute arises at Assam-Mizoram border, Forest Department officials from each state should first take initiative and hold necessary meeting. They also decided that either side should not take any new initiative along the border and regular border patrolling should be conducted by each side.

84 (eighty four) vehicles (HMV- 50, MMV – nil, LMV –25, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab- 9) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 29.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

170 (one hundred seventy) vehicles (HMV – 65, MMV – 43, LMV –26, LPG – 5, Maxi Cab – 3, Oil Tanker – 24, Bus - 4) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 29.01.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
