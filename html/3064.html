SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/569

Dated: 14th October, 2020           

 

  1. Information has been received that a Press Conference was held by JAC on Inner Line Forest Demand Committee (A conglomerate of 18 (eighteen) NGOs viz., CYMA, MUP, MHIP, MZP, MSU, ZORO, AMFU, NTUM, SOSA, FUW, MWU, MICLUM, BAID ISRAEL, ZWO, ZDU, HCDC, MARTYS’ FAMILY, GMN) today i.e., 14.10.2020 @ 12:00 noon at Aizawl Press Club. Pu F. Ngurbiakvela, Chairman JAC on Inner Line Forest Demand Committee presided the Press Conference and 10 media persons were present. During the Press Conference a Press Note was read out, wherein they stated that JAC submitted a Joint Memorandum (All NGO & Political Parties) to Hon’ble Prime Minister during his visit to Mizoram on 23.11.2018 and in this regard Government of Mizoram received correspondence letter from Ministry of Home Affairs seeking its comment but till date no comment was made. It also stated that Ministry of Home Affairs sent reminder letter to Government of Mizoram on 26.11.2018 and 20.2.2019 but till date no comment has been made.  It further stated that remaining silent despite Central Government initiative on the matter cannot be understood and the only way to resolve Assam-Mizoram Border dispute through a peaceful means is to follow the suggestions made in their memorandum. It also stated that after careful deliberation they urged Mizoram government to immediately submit comment to Union Home Ministry. They further urged Mizoram government to submit comment between Dt. 14.10.2020 @ 12:00 midnight and Dt. 21.10.2020 @ 12:00 midnight failing which they intend to resort to state wide indefinite Total Bandh.

     Further, JAC on Inner Line Forest Demand Committee submitted a written petition to Chief Secretary today i.e., 14.10.020 with a request to submit comment to Union Home Ministry regarding their memorandum submitted to Prime Minister between Dt. 14.10.2020 @ 12:00 midnight and Dt. 21.10.2020 @ 12:00 midnight, failing which they intend to organise state wide indefinite Total Bandh.

  1. Information has also been received that Zampui Mizo Convention (MC), Tripura held an Executive Meeting on 13.10.2020 @ 7:00 p.m. at Behliangchhip Branch YMA Hall. Rev. David Ralte, President, MC chaired the meeting and 26 (twenty six) members attended the meeting. During the meeting, Bru Songrongmas' intention to construct Shiv Mandir at Thaidawr Tlang, Phungdungsei, Mizoram on 19 to 20.10.2020 was deliberated and resolved the following :-
  1. Condemned Bru Hindu Associations' intention to construct Shiv. Mandir within Phuldungsei VC (Mizo village) area and considered as disregard of the Mizo people.

 

Cont/-2.....

 

-2-

  1. Their opposition regarding the Bru Songrongma intention to construct Shiv Mandir at Thaidawr Tlang, Phungdungsei will be widely disseminated through Media.
  2. The matter will be brought to the notice of Tripura Government with a request to prohibit the said initiative.
  3. Total Bandh will be called on 19 to 20.10.2020 under the aegis of Mizo Convention within Zampui area.
  4. The initiative of Zampui Mizo in this regard will be informed to Chief Minister, Mizoram, MZP, CYMA and other NGOs, and seek their support.
  5. Group prayer meeting will be held on 18.10.2020 (Sunday) 2:00 to 4:00 p.m. at Vanghmun Playground.

            As per input, leaders of Mizo Convention are gearing up to stage protest in case Government of Tripura fails to prohibit the said Bru Songrongma intention to construct Shiv Mandir at Thaidawr Tlang, Phungdungsei.

            Situation is under close watch.
