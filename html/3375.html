A. Inside page of VANGLAINI and front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 4.4.2014

FIR Lodged: Pu K.Chhawnthuama of Durtlang lodge an FIR at Bawngkawn police station on 2nd April 2014 as a result of an SMS received by him. The SMS, send by a person who claimed himself to be 'Superman' contained message which can be considered an intimidation in regards to his recent conflict with the Chief Minister of Mizoram. As a result a case 66 a IT Act and 506 IPC had been registered.

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 4.4.2014

Arrangement of Traffic: Based on powers conferred to him under sec. 117 MV Act, for the MP election of 9th April, Aizawl S.P Traffic declared the areas between Mizo HSS field -Zarkawt, Mizo HSS field - Hrangbana college and Chaltlang Local Council House -Chaltlang Dawrkawn as a no  parking areas for 7th and 8th April, 2014. Apart from this, for 9th to 11th April, the areas between Zodin square - Raj Bhavan South gate,Industries office - Zodinsquare via Aizawl Temple and Raj Bhavan South Gate Junction -Tennis court junction are also declared as no parking zones.
