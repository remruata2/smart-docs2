A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.1.2014

Murderers Confessed: The murderers of Hrangmana - whose body was found on 14th January 2014 at Tuipui D - confessed to the crime to Hnahthial Police yesterday. As reported earlier, Hrangmana of Tuipui D who stayed with Vanlalvena (38) and his wife Sangpuii went out together to the forest on 13th January 2014 but he did not return along with the couple; he was searched by the community and his body was found in a ravine, 100mtrs. away from the road on Tuesday.

            A case was registered at Hnahthial Police Station for injury marks were found on the body of Hrangmana. The police interrogated Vanlalvena and his wife Sangpuii who confessed and admitted to the crime. Some youths of Tuipui D gathered near Hnahthial PS after knowing the culprits but nothing untoward happened.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.1.2014

All Brus who fled their village were returned: Due to MZP and supporting groups attempt at searching operation for the three men abducted by bru armed gang and baseless rumours, all bru families of Tumpanglui fled their village for Tripura on Monday & Tuesday. These people were followed and coaxed by Lalthakima SDPO, Kawrthah and his team to come back to Tumpanglui. SDPO, Kawrthah reported that all of them were believed to come back to Tumpanglui yesterday.
