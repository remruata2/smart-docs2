State Disaster Response Force (SDRF):

_High wind/cyclonic storm always strike the State of Mizoram by damaging dwelling houses and crops. Due to excess rainfall, landslides are very serious hazard for the State of Mizoram. **51. Formulate Standard guidelines for granting expeditious sanction for the visit of Investigating Officers abroad:**

****_The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI ), functioning under Deptt. of Personnel, Ministry of Personnel, Pension & Public Grievances, Government of India, is the premier investigating police agency in India. It is the nodal police agency in India which coordinates investigation on behalf of Interpol Member countries. Mizoram Police have been following the Comprehensive guidelines issued by Internal Security Division, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India on 31.12.2007 regarding investigation abroad,etc_

It is suggested that Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India may issued a new Comprehensive guidelines for granting expeditious sanction for the visit of Investigating Officer abroad.

**55 In view of the limitations of Sniffer dogs and technical equipments like metal detectors and explosive detectors, there is need to find newer devices which are capable of detecting mines buried more than 3-4 ft in the ground.**

 _Stressing the need for employing the latest technology to tackle IED menace, the Union Home Ministry has, in its action plan (2014) to fight Left-wing extremism, proposed mine detector AN/PSS 14, developed by US-based L3 Cyterra for the counter-Naxal grid._

_It has recommended that CRPF's Institute of IED Management at Pune be asked to study the system and make suggestions. The AN/PSS 14 mine detector revolutionizes landmine detection by combining ground penetrating radar (GPR) and highly sensitive metal detector (MD) technology using advanced data fusion algorithms._
