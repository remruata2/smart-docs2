****MHIP General Headquarters impasse vis-à-vis Election of Office Bearers (2019-2021) sheduled for 17th April 2019****

MHIP General Headquarters had held its 22nd General Assembly,2019 on 16th &amp; 17th April at Vanapa Hall, Aizawl during which election for its General Headquarters Office Bearers(2019-2021)was scheduled to take take place. However, before the commencement of the election, Pi Rohmingliani,Member, Kulikawn Local Council submitted an FIR at Kulikawn Police Station alleging that discrepancies had occurred in the election process in such a way that the incumbent Office Bearers had manipulated the nomination papers by forging signatures. Subsequently, MHIP Branches viz, i)Dawrpui, ii) Durtlang, iii) Upper Republic, iv) Armed Veng South, v) Zarkawt, vi) Zemabawk have submitted their respective FIRs in regard to the same allegations.

The Hon’ble Court of Pu R.Malsawmdawngliana,Senior Civil Judge II issued Stay Order on dt.17.4.2019 for the election of Office Bearers for the term 2019-2021 which was scheduled for 17th April,2019 upon consideration of prayer submitted by Pi Rohmingliani of Kulikawn through her learned counsel Mr.K.Remruatfela.
