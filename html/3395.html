A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.2.2014

Traffic Police Report: During the year 2012 & 2013, 18,184 vehicles (2012 – 8,726 & 2013 – 9,458) were seized for breaking traffic rules in Aizawl.

            According to Traffic Police report, among the seized vehicles, vehicles parked at No Parking Zone were 5,105 (2012 – 1,253 & 2013 – 3,852). 1,279 persons (2012 – 378 & 2013 – 901) were arrested for driving without license. And 1005 (2012 – 283 & 2013 – 722) were arrested for running on the wrong side of the road.

            Head of Aizawl Traffic Police said that traffic rules offenders and vehicles seized/arrested were higher in 2013 than in 2012 due to the high increase rate of vehicle and that Traffic Police were stern in maintaining Traffic rules.

            During the year 2012 & 2013, vehicle accidents occurred the most in the month of July and August; second by December, January and September. And no vehicle accidents occurred in February recorded Traffic Police.
