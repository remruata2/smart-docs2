1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

Joint VC and YMA, Saikhawthlir and Chuhvel, Mamit District submitted a written petition to Home Minister, Mizoram on 15.6.2021, wherein they stated that a contingent of Assam Police and Assam officials entered Chuhvel area on 11.6.2021 and at an earlier occasions. However, due to presence of Mizoram Armed Police during their visit, nothing adverse had happened. Hence, it is apparent that the government of Assam intended to take action within inner line forest reserved area, and it has deployed a huge number of Armed Police within the reserved forest area. In this regard, they urged the state government to verify and cease the initiative taken by Assam government within forest reserved area. It also stated that Assam government intended to grant Autonomous District Council to Dimasa, Cachari and Bru tribes, who are living within inner line reserved forest area (including some portion of their village area), and they strongly opposed any such initiative. It further urged the state government to verify the same and accordingly take necessary action.
