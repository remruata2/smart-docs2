A. Front page of  VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 23.08. 2022

Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 2019 to be implemented by Government: Government of Mizoram is about to implement the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 2019 as per instructions received from the Central Government. Under this Act, fines for violations of traffic rules will increase to a considerable amount in comparison  to the existing amount. Some of the punishments for violation of rules are given below -

a) Driving without Licence - Rs. 1000 (Existing) to Rs.5000 (New Amendmend)

b) Using mobile while driving - Rs. 1000 (Existing) to Rs.5000/Rs (NewAmendmend)

c) Racing in public highways - Rs. 1000 (Existing) to Rs.5000 (New Amendmend)

d) Driving without seatbelt - Rs.5000 (New Amendmend)

e) Drunken driving - Rs. 2000 (Existing) to Rs.10,000 (New Amendmend)

f) Under age driving - Rs. 25,000 fines & 3 years imprisonment of the owner of the        vehicle.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI Dt. 23.08.2022

 10,600 Kgs Areca nut seized by Police : On the night of 20.8.2022, Police  seized 265 bags (10,600 kgs) of areca nuts  near Tuipui village, Khawzawl District which were alleged to have been imported from Myanmar. The areca nuts were transported using 5 vehicles and Lalhmangaiha (43) s/o Kapthula,Ruantlang, Champhai who was suspected to be the owner was also apprehended. These nuts, which were bound for Vairengte were transported from a warehouse in Ruantlang village, making a detour of Champhai Police check gate; however, they were seized by Khawzawl Police near Tuipui.
