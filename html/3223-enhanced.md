1.2.2022 : Cabinet Meeting was held under the chairmanship of Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister of Mizoram on 1.2.2022 @10:30 a.m. at CMO Conference Hall. The meeting after careful consideration approved the following agenda items :-

1. The draft Mizoram Civil Service Rules, 2021 - **DP&AR**

2. (a) The draft Mizoram Finance &amp; Accounts Service (Limited Departmental  Examination) Regulations, 2022 - **DP&AR**
 (b) The draft Mizoram Finance &amp; Accounts Service (Induction)  Regulations, 2022 - **DP&AR**
 (c) The draft Mizoram Finance &amp; Accounts Service (Cadre Training &amp;  Departmental Examination) Regulations, 2022 - **DP&AR**
3. Request for approval of 'The Mizoram Epidemic Diseases (Covid-19)  Regulations, 2022 - **H&FW**
4. Regularization of Society, viz. the Mizoram Tourism Development Authority  under Tourism Deptt. &amp; Grant of Financial Aid to the Society - **Tourism Department**
5. Proposal for Amendment of the Mizoram Fisheries Act, 2002 (Act No.12 of  2005) - **Fisheries**
6. The Mizoram Private Security Agencies (Private Security to Cash  Transportation Activities) Rules, 2022 - **Home Department**
7. Proposal for handing over of maintenance of Martarte Thlanmual, Berampu  Tlang, Luangmual, Aizawl from Martarte Thlanmual Siamtu Committee to  appropriate Department under Government of Mizoram - **Art & Culture**
