SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/164

Dated: 8th March, 2021    

 

Information has been received that Mizo Students’ Union General Headquarters held its extended OB Meeting today, i.e., 8.3.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. at MSU Office.
Pu J. Lalmuanzuala, President MSU presided the meeting and 11 (eleven) members attended. The meeting decided to serve a letter to Mrs. Nazuk Kumar, IAS, Director, Higher & Technical Education Department today with a request to decline her current post. The meeting also stated that if their request is not fulfilled, they intended to organize picketing to prevent her from attending office w.e.f., tomorrow, i.e., 9.3.2021.
A Press Release in this regard was issued today.

Hence, they submitted the letter to Director, Higher & Technical Education Department today’s evening at 3:30 p.m. wherein they requested Mrs. Nazuk Kumar, IAS, Director, Higher & Technical Education Department to decline her current post as Higher & Technical Education Department is one of the important department which have conducted many entrance examinations (Medical & Technical) and there have been instances in the past regarding categorization of applicants, which raised communal issues and due to which MSU demanded local person to hold the said post.

In this regard, Mizo Students’ Union General Headquarters had earlier issued a Press Release on 2.3.2021 wherein they expressed their desire for appointment of mizo officer as Director, Higher & Technical Education Department.

Information received further indicated that Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) supported the initiative taken by MSU in this regard and they intended to take necessary action.

Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
