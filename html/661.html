ARREST OF 2 (TWO) BANGLADESHI NATIONALS

 

2 (two) Bangladeshi nationals, namely (i) Mahmud Hasan (25) s/o Gulbarg Ali, Magura, Bangladesh and (ii) Mohammad Sayad Hussain (35) s/o Shayaib Hussain, Magura, Bangladesh were apprehended by 90 Bn. BSF personnel stationed at BOP, Silsuri on the ground of suspicion of their involvement in anti-national activities and produced before Marpara Police Station on 29.7.2019 @ 1:10 a.m. In this regard, Marpara PS Case No. 7/19 Dt. 29.7.2019 u/s 471/476 IPC r/w 14 A(b) of the Foreigners Act has been registered.

 

The 2 (two) apprehended Bangladeshi nationals, namely (i) Mahmud Hassan (25) s/o Gulbarg Ali, Magura, Bangladesh and (ii) Mohamad Sayad Hussain (35) s/o Shayaib Hussain, Magura, Bangladesh along with the escorted parties arrived at Aizawl PS on 30.7.2019 @ 9:30 a.m. Interrogation was conducted jointly by various Intelligence Agencies such as, RAW, BSF, SIB, etc. at CID (SB) office, Aizawl. The following, though yet to be ascertained, have been revealed during preliminary interrogation:-

  1. Both had illegally crossed the Indo-Bangla border at different time and place. Mahmud Hassan had crossed the IB border in October, 2018 at Boxanagar (W.Tripura) whereas Mohd. Sayad Hussain entered via Kamlasagar, Tripura in November, 2018. Mahmud Hassan had visited Bangalore and stayed there since November, 2018. Mohd. Sayad Hossain had been staying in Ramnagar area of Agartala for some time and then went to Dharmanagar in February, 2019. During May, 2019 he again shifted to Kanchanpur. The two met at Dharmanagar Railway Station on 22 July, 2019 and entered Mizoram on 25.7.2019.
  2. Mobile handset belonging to Mahmud Hassan contains some notes/messages pertaining to Islamic Jihad, as well as Explosive compositions which are being examined.
  3. Six (6) Indian SIM cards seized from their possessions are being examined and CDRs being obtained for analysis.
  4. The two (2) arrested persons are in possession of fake Adhaar Card.
  5. The arrested person, Mohammad Sayad Hussain has confessed during interrogation that he is a member of Ansar-Al-Islam, an extremist outfit in Bangladesh and that they were sent to find suitable place in Mizoram western border area and to find smugglers who could provide arms and ammunitions to their organization.
  6. Interrogation of Mahmud Hasan revealed that on 25th July, 2019, he left Kanchanpur, Tripura alongwith Mohammad Sayad Hussain, for Mizoram to do recce on the areas bordering Bangladesh. He had also confessed that he had illegally crossed Indo-Bangladesh border on 15th Oct., 2018 and had acquired fake Adhaar card.
  7. On 5th August, 2019, Special Investigation Team (SIT) constituted as follows :

  8. The arrested persons were forwarded to District Court, Mamit for Judicial Custody on 9th August, 2019.
