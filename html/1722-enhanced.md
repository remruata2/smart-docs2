 Hemi chungchangah hian Kawrthah PS C/No.1/06 dt.8.1.06 u/s 365/384/511/ 121/121(A)/506/34 IPC ziahluh ani a, a rubotu zinga tel nia rinhlelh mi pakhat S/S Pte. Lalawmpuia (28) S/o Ram Bahadur, Kolalian chu man ani a, amah leh a thiante pahnih lakah CS No. 1/06 dt.31.1.2006 submit a ni.
