MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/2

Dated 4th January, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/759
Dt. 30.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

                   

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-3, LMV-1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 4.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 7, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

15 (fifteen) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 14, LMV - 1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 4.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV-Nil, LMV-Nil, MMV–12) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          76 (seventy six) vehicles (HMV-36, MMV – Nil, LMV – 35, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab - 5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 4.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

          Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 4.1.2021.

         

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          85 (eighty five) vehicles (HMV – 41, MMV – 47, LMV – 14, LPG – 1, Maxi Cab – 1, Oil Tanker – 22, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 4.1.2021 between 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
