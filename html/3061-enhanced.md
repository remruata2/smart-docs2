****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/559

Dated: 9th October, 2020

1. Information has been received that Babujoy, Vice President, SONGRONGMA (Conglomerate of various Bru NGOs) held a Press Conference via YouTube through KOKTRIPURA NORTH-EAST INDIA channel today, i.e., 9.10.2020, in which he stated their intention to construct Mandir (Sibraikhong Temple) at Thaidawr Tlang, near Phuldungsei Village, Mamit District. The place is believed to be within Mizoram side. He further mentioned that community work which was called to be organised on 15th and 16th October, 2020 for various Tripura Bru NGOs, Chakma NGO, Mog Tribe was postponed to 19th and 20th October, 2020 due to its clash with BJP Government programme.

 He further stated that all those who intend to participate in the said community work should first gather at Kanchanpur, North Tripura District and briefing on their course of action will be conducted.

 As per input, upon hearing the said news Mizo inhabitants of Zampui Hills, Tripura raised their opposition. Upon their enquiry, SDM, Kanchanpur informed Leaders of Mizo Convention that he is not aware of the said intended action nor gave any permission for their community work.

 Further, Mizo Convention intends to demonstrate their opposition, if SONGRONGMA continue to conduct the said community work. In this regard, they plan to convene a joint meeting with Tripura Group YMA in due course.

2.  Information has also been received that one farm house belonging to Pu John Zolawma of Thinghlun, located at Zaite Road Assam Side around 150 meters from Tlangte Lui (state boundary river) was allegedly set ablaze by Assam Forest Officials today, i.e., 9.10.2020 @ around 2:40 p.m., and due to this the said farm house was burnt down to ashes.

 Situation is under close watch.
