            With reference to the above subject and Home Deptt. letter, I am sending herewith the required information as under for further necessary action.

 

 

 

1.         Chin National Council (CNC) held a meeting on 16.8.2010 at their office Upper Republic (Pu Hiphei Building) presided over by Ralhnin, General Secy., CNC. 5 Office Bearers attended the meeting. The meeting decided that CNC should conduct seminar on 'Civil society based organisation' for a period of 5 days starting from 27th - 31st August 2010 at the CNC office, Upper Republic.

 

            During the proposed seminar, lectures on the following topics will be delivered by eminent Chin intellectuals:-

 

            1. Importance of Civil Society by Mr. Thanglianpiang, ZRO/ZRA.

            2. Constitution governing CNC by Mr. Khua Uk Lian, CNF.

            3. Gender Equality by Mrs. Lalhmangaihzuali(Tei), CNC.

            4. Environment by Mrs. Lalzenghaki, Women League of Chinland.

            5. News reporting by Mr. Thatni, CNF.

            6. Financial Management/Report by Mrs. G.S. Mang, President, Chin                                        Student & Youth Federation.

 

            The seminar is being sponsored by Luxemburg based donor agency 'Euro-Burma Office' which also sponsored earlier seminar in the past. Lianhmung Sakhong, Chairman CNC Thailand based is expected to attend this seminar.

 

            CNC plans to conduct the training under a veil of secrecy after the State Government earlier refused permission for training of FBR.

 

            In this connection, Lianhmung Sakhong, Chairman CNC Thailand based arrived at Aizawl from New Delhi on 22.8.2010 and staying at Hotel Arini, Republic Veng, Aizawl. In view of recent heavy rainfall and floods, it is not clear whether the delegates from Myanmar would be able to attend the training in full strength.

2.         Rozathanga, Dawrpui Vengthar, Aizawl, Secretary, Chin Humanitarian Relief Committee and Miss Shalom, Zarkawt(Sangchia building) Jt.Gen. Secretary, Women League of Chinland were called by the Addl.Secretary, Home Deptt. on 12.8.2010 and straightforwardly told them not to conduct any kind of training or seminar for Free Burma Rangers on the Burmese Govt. issue. It was also learnt that Rozathanga had met His Excellency the Governor of Mizoram on 10.8.2010 who had also directed and warned him not to go against if Mizoram Govt. does not allow them to conduct their proposed training or seminar and will not tolerate them in case they disobey.

 

            However, since the resource persons from abroad who are supposed to give lectures/teachings and persons to attend the proposed training/seminar had already arrived in Aizawl, they had informal meetings in the office of Women League of Chinland located at Mc Hill, Zarkawt and in the house of Rozathanga at Dawrpui Vengthar. It was learnt that the main topics of discussion during those meetings were of medical subjects like giving first aids, pregnancy and delivery etc.
