A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 20.1.2014_

**“I asked for understanding of Mizos & abducted families if anybody loses life in the rescue operation” – Home Minister / News expected in 12 Hrs. We did not tell any lie – CID****:** Mizoram government does not deny that the abducted are still in the hands of their abductors. Pu R.Lalzirliana, Home Minister stated that there could be loss of life in government rescue operation and he asked for understanding of the Mizos and abducted families beforehand. At the same time, Mizoram government is doing its best to make sure that this thing does not happen. Answering other question, Pu Zira said that the abducted are now in the hands of NLFT and Mizoram government has not had a talk with Bru armed gang who abducted them from the forest.

CID operation Team Commander, HL Thangzuala, Addl.SP CID(SB) answered the questions about the abduction and said that CID Operation Team did not tell any lie in giving out information and that they needed about 40 Hrs. to confirm an information and that led to something else crop up in between. He said that good news is expected in 12 Hrs. regarding the rescue operation but at the same time what can happen is not known.
