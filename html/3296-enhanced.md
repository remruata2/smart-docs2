**C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.11.3.2013**

**Truck drivers not to go to Bairabi due to scuffle****:**Some lads and some truck drivers had a fight on the night of Friday ie, 8th March, 2013 at the railway crossing at Bairabi. Due to this incidence, 130 trucks which were stationed at Bairabi to carry/load rice returned to Kolasib without carrying/loading rice, the truck drivers said that they will not go to Bairabi unless there is a new agreement.

 On the night of 8th March, an auto-rickshaw driver and a truck driver had a brawl near taxation checkgate at Bairabi. The auto-rickshaw driver called his friends for help, and they attacked the truck driver inside his truck. The truck driver fought them back with the tools-lever. Both drivers had minor injuries and some others were admitted to Kolasib hospital.

Due to the incident, a joint meeting of NGOs, Auto-Rickshaw Drivers Association, Mizoram Truck Driversâ€™ Association, Kolasib Truck owners Association and families of the injured persons was held under the chairmanship of Bairabi Police Station OC last Saturday. An agreement was made; the truck driver paid Rs. 10,000/- to treat the injured auto-rickshaw drivers and the truck driver arrested by the police were released.

An emergency meeting was held at MTDA Gen. Hqrs. Kolasib on Saturday and they decided not to carry rice from Bairabi as they do not feel safe at present, they will not do so until a new agreement is made.

**D. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, newspaper Dt.11.3.2013**

**Deadline over for 5 hostages - They are safe and their health condition is also good****:**5 hostages of forest department workers have not been released till today, the deadline to give ransom for the 5 hostages was also over on Saturday ie, 9.2.2013.

 The kidnappers let their hostages talk to their families through mobile phone on Saturday and tell them to urge the Government to give ransom, they also told their family that they are safe and their health condition is also good.

 Families of the hostages are very worried and desperate; but they said they are waiting for the Government and that they have a full faith in the police.

**School destroyed with bomb****:**SSA school building at Bairabi near Mizoram and Assam states border was destroyed with bomb on Saturday ie, 9.3.2013 at around 12:00 am. The explosion was quite strong and it destroyed almost all the school building. The culprits are not known and no Mizo is heard to be arrested in connection with the crime, and nothing untoward happened.
