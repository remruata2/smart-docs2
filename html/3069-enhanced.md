****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/481

Dated: 4th September, 2020

 Information has been received that last evening, i.e., 3.9.2020 @ 6:00 p.m. Krawshmingthanga (29) s/o Nghakliana of Saizawh West, Lawngtlai District and his two Mizo friends went to Saizawh West High School site (located at around one km from Saizawh village) by his Maxi Cab for drinking liquor and on their way home they stopped one Chakma, named Manod Chakma with one Chakma Lady, named Uma Chakma (pillion rider), proceeding towards Saizawh West High School by Bike. They reportedly teased the Lady verbally and then instructed them to go home citing prohibition of drinking Alcohol at Saizawh West High School Site by Saizawh YMA and the two Chakmas went back to Chawngte.

 The same evening at around 8:30 p.m., a group of Chakma youths from Chawngte Village (Kamalanagar) led by Manod Chakma went to Saizawh West Village (Saizawh West is around 4 kms from Chawngte) and physically assaulted Krawshmingthanga, due to which he sustained minor injuries on his body. He was immediately evacuated to Chawngte CHC for medical treatment and after giving medical treatment he was discharged from Chawngte CHC and his health condition was reported to be stable. In this regard, Pu Laldinkhuma (elder brother of victim Krawshmingthanga) lodged an FIR at Chawngte P/S.
 In this connection, Chawngte P.S Case No. 2 dt. 4.9.2020 u/s 34/323/341 IPC has been registered and Police apprehended the following 12 (twelve) accused persons and necessary investigation is currently underway :-

 1. Ms Uma Chakma (24) d/o Tarachan Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 2. Manod Chakma (21) s/o Lilamoy Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 3. Hriday Chakma (21) s/o Mohan Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 4. Shibil Chakma (21) s/o Krishna Chandro Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 5. Mongola Chakma (20) s/o Judha Ranjan Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 6. Bijoy Shanhar Chakma (21) s/o Doyamoy Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 7. Shantost Chakma (20) s/o Jaboraj Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 8. Romend Chakma (22) s/o Nutun Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 9. Aloran Chakma (21) s/o Devarshi Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 10. Johnson Chakma (22) s/o Subhash Kusum Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 11. Bishan Chakma (17) s/o Bhuban Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 12. John Chakma (17) s/o Inglon Chakma of Kamalanagar.

 This morning i.e. 4.9.2020 @ around 3:00 a.m. 12 (twelve) leaders of MSU General Headquarters, Aizawl led by Pu J. Lalmuanzuala, Vice President, MSU proceeded to Chakma House, Hunthar Veng, Aizawl and caused minor damage to two door locks and front glass of one Bolero B/R MZ-05 6925 which was parked in front of the House and asked all the male guests (there were 8 (female) and 52 (male) guests at Chakma House at that time) to exit their rooms in demand for immediate arrest of the accused persons. 3 (three) Police personnel of Vaivakawn P.S also reach the spot but could not stop the mischief as they were out numbered. The MSU also intended to close down Chakma House indefinitely if the accused were not arrested before 7:00 a.m. today. However, they cancelled their intention to close Chakma House on hearing the arrest of accused persons by Chawngte Police, and they dispersed at around 7:00 a.m.

 Further, 8 (eight) leaders of MZP Headquarters, Lunglei led by Lalpeksanga Chenkual, President, MZP Headquarters, Lunglei left Lunglei for Chawngte today morning, i.e., 4.9.2020 @ 10:00 a.m.

 MZP General Headquarters, Aizawl issued a statement today i.e., 4.9.2020 asking all Chakmas in Mizoram not to venture outside their homes for their personal safety and informed Chakma Houses at Aizawl, Lunglei and Lawngtlai not to entertain new guests until the situation normalized. As per input, 14 (fourteen ) Leaders of MZP, General Headquarters, Aizawl led by Pu B. Vanlaltana, President, MZP left Aizawl for Chawngte today afternoon, i.e., 4.9.2020 @ 1:00 p.m. in connection with Saizawh West incident.

 1 (one) Platoon of 2nd Bn. MAP personnel are sent to back up the District Police at Chawngte to prevent any untoward incident at the time of writing the report.

 In this connection, All NGOs of Chawngte comprising of Joint YMA, Central Young Chakma Association (CYCA), MSU Sub-Headquarters Chawngte, Mizoram Chakma Students’ Union, MZP Sub-Headquarters Chawngte and community Leaders of Saizawh village held a joint meeting at MZP Sub-Headquarters Office, Chawngte @ 1:00 p.m. The meeting was chaired by Pu Ramngamzauva, President, Joint YMA and it was attended by 22 (twenty two) persons. The meeting passed the following resolutions :-

i) Compensation should be given to the victim by the accused persons.

ii) Incident of physical assault of Mizo residents of Saizawh village by Chakmas should  not happen again in future.

iii) The arrested accused persons should be awarded suitable punishment.

 Situation is under close watch.
