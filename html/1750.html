2. STATUS ON IMPLEMENTATION OF

KALADAN MULTI-MODAL TRANSIT TRANSPORT PROJECT (KMMTTP)

 

  1. A project called Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP) covering a total length of 87.180 kms between Lawngtlai and Indo-Myanmar Border in Mizoram commenced on 10.10.2010.

 

  1. The project is funded by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) with the initial agreement amount of Rs. 792.62 Crores and Mizoram PWD functions as the implementing agency.
  2. The project is divided into 3 (Three) packages - package I, II &III. The construction of packages I & III are under RDS Project Ltd and that of the package II is under Atlanta ARSS (Joint Venture).
  3. There are 8 (Eight) bridges to be constructed and of these, 4(four) bridges are in progress.
  4. The cost of the project is revised and approved with a total amount of Rs. 1,011.52 Crore.
  5. The targeted period of completion is 31.12.2019 and 78.25 % of the project is reported as completed till date.
  6. Land acquisition issue is the main hindrance of the Project. Revised Land aquisition estimate is prepared and already submitted to District Collector/ Dy. Commissioner, Lawngtlai in 2017, but is still pending with higher authority.
  7. Lawngtlai District Land Owner Association (LDLOA) has submitted a list of land owners (303 numbers) with a total claim amount of Rs. 5,850.70 lakhs to authority which, they claimed, as pending.
  8. Information is received that the Executive Meeting of LDLOA which was held on 6.12.2018 resolved to stay/stop all the construction works under the project indefinitely w.e.f 20.01.2019, if the authority fails to sanction pending compensation before the end of 2018.
