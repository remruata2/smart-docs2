A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 16.11.2022

Indefinte total bandh to be imposed from today within Hachhek Constituency, as a result Chief Minister’s sport field inauguration programme to be held at Kawrthah and Tuidam was cancelled: Hachhek Bial Kuhva Chingtu Pawl (HBKCP) and Jampui Mizo Convention (JMC), Tripura in their meeting held on 14.11.22 at Kawrthah YMA Hall resolved to hold indefinite total bandh from November 16, 2022 (Wednesday) @6:00AM which was already agreed upon in their previous joint meeting.

HBKCP & JMC MEETING RESOLUTION:

  1. Mizoram Government should find out the persons involved in dry areca nuts smuggling as per MHA directives and to award suitable punishment to them, and to take necessary action for disposal of all illegally imported dry areca nuts from Mizoram.
  2. As the Government knows that areca nuts farming is the main source of income for Hachhek region people, Government should not organised any official function within Hachhek Assembly Constituency, before the fulfillment of our plea and demands.

 

The two organizations also highlighted that due to Mizoram Government negligence on areca nuts smuggling and due to the blind eye shown upon godown (warehouse), areca nuts farmers from Mizoram and Tripura face hardship. Kawrthah Joint YMA in their press release stated that they supported the total bandh to be organized by HBKCP.

 

B. Front page of The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt.16.11.2022

Mizoram and Assam to held meeting: Mizoram and Assam border dispute meeting will be held on November 17 at Guwahati, for this Home Minister Lalchamliana will proceed to Guwahati on Wednesday. Mizoram delegates will be headed by the home minister, him and David Lalthangliana, OSD cum joint secretary, home will leave on Wednesday. The will later be joint by information minister, Lalruatkima, home secretary H. Lalengmawia, home department joint secretary Lalthiamsanga sailo, information officer Pratap Chhetri. Information minister, Lalruatkima said, “He shall mentioned Mizoram stand in border dispute and Assam will also mentioned their stand”. He also stated that officers from both state have communicated through phone in this regard. The meeting will be held in Guwahati, both the states had a meeting before on August 9, at Aizawl. As agreed upon earlier in the meeting, the meeting is scheduled to be held on November 17.
