A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 8.7.2013

2 corpses found: The corpse of K.Lalnunhlima(57) h/o Lalrindiki, Armed veng ‘S’ was found at the boundary of Armed veng and Tuithiang on Saturday @ 3:40PM. No foul play was suspected, he is believed to die from accidental fall in a narrow footpath which linked the two localities.

      On 5th July, 2013 @ 8:30PM, the corpse of Lalnunmawia(22) s/o Sawichhunga, Zuangtui Electric Complex was found at Falkland Veng in the area of World Bank road, he is believed to die in the hands of someone from the marks found on his body. Lalnunmawia went out with his friend in the night of 4th July, 2013, while they were sitting and rested at Falkland Veng in World Bank road, 2 unidentified persons came and disturbed them, after that he parted with his friend, and his corpse was found later, the police have arrested one person in connection with the crime. The people of Zuangtui gathered near Aizawl Police Station to see the arrested person on Friday night but nothing untoward happened and dispersed @ 2:30 AM. Lalnunmawia’s body was examined (PME) at Aizawl Civil Hospital on Saturday, several people from Zuangtui came along. The crowd gathered near Aizawl Police Station and shouted in unison about their dislike of the murder and to arrest the murderer as quickly as possible. The crowd requested to see the face of the arrested suspected murderer, the police obliged and the crowd dispersed peacefully.

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 8.7.2013

2 men arrested for impersonation: 2 men alleged of trying to fool ACB were arrested on 5th July, 2013. These two men went to ACB office to sort out the two names detained due to suspicion to receive money for railway track compensation.

      Kolasib DC notified that some suspicious people should not be given compensation for the construction of railway track in Kawnpui area, Lalthansanga s/o Vanlalhruaia and Lalrohlua s/o Vanlalhruaia both from Ramhlun Venglai were included among them.

      On 5th July, 2013, 2 men who identified themselves as Lalthansanga and Lalrohlua went to ACB office trying to convince them that their Pass (LSC) was not to be doubted. ACB got suspicious and checked them and discovered that the one who claimed himself to be Lalrohlua was Lalnuntluanga s/o R.Lalhmingthanga of Ramhlun ‘S’ from his driving license. The other one who claimed to be Lalthansanga was Lalsangzuala s/o H.Rochianga of Ramhlun Venglai. After the discovery of their true identities, the 2 men confessed that they were carpenters at Set Tee Sofa and Lalthansanga’s wife asked them to claim themselves as Vanlalhruaia’s (the owner of Set Tee Sofa) sons and gave them photocopy of land pass. These 2 men were detained at Aizawl PS on 5th July, 2013 to further investigate their true identities and were temporarily released the next day.
