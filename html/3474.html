A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.3.2014

Arson at Upper Republic: Two cars – Baleno & Bolero parked in front of Zorpi Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. Office at Upper Republic was set on fire on 8.3.2014 at around 3:00 – 4:00 am. Two wheelers parked nearby were also pushed down.

            Baleno car is owned by Lalparmawii, the left front and rear wheels were torched along with its bonnet cover and Bolero is owned by Thawnglian Vuangtu – it is newly bought and has not been registered, its head light assembly was burnt.

            The damage caused by the fire has amounted to ` 85,000/- said the Police. As the accident happened at dawn, some people saw the vehicles on fire and worked together to put out the fire. An FIR was lodged by the vehicles owners at Aizawl Police Station and investigation is carried on but no suspect has been arrested in connection with the crime.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 10.3.2014

Forest Guard Attack with Catapult/Slingshot: Forest guard Lalsangzuala of Phuldungsei seized catapults while on duty in the area of Forest. The owner of the catapult, Chakma boys were angry and shot at Lalsangzuala while he was patrolling at Khawthlang Tuipui on Saturday and sustained minor injury. Phuldungsei youth decided to go to Silsuri due to this incident but were stopped by the Police before reaching Silsuri, nothing untoward happened.

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.3.2014

Drugs Peddlers & Users Arrested: A joint Operation Team of Electric Veng YMA Kumpuan Committee & VDP arrested 45 drugs peddlers and users in the area of New Market on Saturday. Among them, 20 persons were handed over to Police.

 

D. Front page of NEWSLINK & Inside page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 10.3.2014

Counterfeit Currency Seized: Lungsen Police in Lunglei District on March 27 seized 82 Fake Indian Currency Notes of ` 500/- denomination from A.Lalbiaksanga, a retailer of Putlungasih village. The man confessed that he had purchased the fake currency at the cost of ` 10,000/- from Hazacherra Refugee Camp, Tripura around June last year when he visited his father-in-law. He had used it from time to time to purchase food grains from Lungsen Government godown. It came to light only when the storekeeper of Lungsen tried to deposit them to SBI, Lunglei on 30.12.2014.
