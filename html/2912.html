Front page of Vanglaini and Newslink newspaper, Dt. 25.9.2012.

 

On 22.9.2012, a joint team of 26 Assam Rifles and Mizoram Police seized 2 nos. of .22 Calibre Pistols and one magazine at Ramhlun area and arrested the alleged owner Lalrinawma S/o Chawnghlira of Ramhlun South, Aizawl. He was booked under Arms Act at Bawngkawn Police Station on the same day.
