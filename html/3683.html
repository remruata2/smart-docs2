A. Front page of  VANGLAIN/The Aizawl Post  newspapers  Dt. 21.09.2022

CIC met Governor: Mizoram Chief Information Commissioner (CIC), Pu Lalnumawia Chuaungo  met Mizoram Governor, Pu Hari Babu Kambhampati at Raj Bhavan yesterday (20.9.2022). In this meeting, the Governor  expressed his desire for an increase in RTI utilisers in the state to have a fair and transparent government while appreciating the efforts of CIC and his department. The CIC on his part informed the Governor that Section 4 of  RTI Act ('suo motu enclosure') was properly implemented by most of the departments under Mizoram Government. The CIC also  invited the Governor to grace the opening ceremony of RTI week, scheduled to be held on 6th October 2022 which the latter gladly accepted.

 

B.Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 21.09.2022

Pre-Recruitment training :  Champhai police and Serchhip police on yesterday (20.9.2022) conducted Pre-recruitment training/coaching for those who have applied for the Army Recruitment Rally (Agnipath Scheme) to be held at Rajiv Gandhi Stadium, Aizawl from 4-9 October, 2022. Training/coaching was given to 140 and 18 candidates in Champhai and Serchhip respectively.

 

 

A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper Dt. 21.09.2022

Pre-paid smart electric meter :  In pursuance of the instruction received from the Central government in connection with the Revamped Distribution Sector Scheme, Power & Electricity department of Mizoram have started taking steps to install pre-paid smart meter for all their consumers in the state. As sanction had already been received, this smart meter is expected to be utilised by all its consumers by December of 2023.
