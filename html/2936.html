SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/548

Dated: 17th July, 2019.

 

            Mizoram Police conducted discreet enquiry on Fake Driving License racket which involves illegal production, distribution and use of fake Driving License in and around Aizawl City. Today, i.e. 17.7.2019, upon receiving reliable information, Mizoram Police conducted search of one Xerox shop located at Zuangtui and run by Mr. J.H. Lalthanmawia @ Sama (33) S/o J. Lalbiaksanga of Zohmun, P/A Zuangtui. The proprietor of the shop was apprehended and the following materials and documents suspected to be used for making fake driving licenses were seized :-

  1. 10 (ten) numbers of suspected fake Driving Licenses
  2. 40 (forty) nos. of blank Smart Cards with suspected fake chips installed
  3. Printer
  4. Computer set and its accessories
  5. Some other related documents, etc.

            Further enquiry was conducted and the following persons, mentioned by the accused, were suspected to be involved in the racket and were apprehended :-

  1. C. Lalremruata @ Mapuitea (34) S/o C. Lungtiawia, Zuangtui.
  2. Andrew Lallawmkima @ Lawma (32) S/o Vanlalsawma, Zuangtui Bethel Veng.
  3. Lalthangmawia @ TM-a (25) S/o Saichhunga, Zuangtui 132 KV Station.
  4. Zorimawia @ Valpuia (26) S/o Lalchungnunga (L), Maubawk Kawn Veng.

 

Depositions of these suspected persons led to the recovery of 18 more Fake Driving Licenses so far (till 6:30 p.m.) and more is expected from further probe.

            Upon further interrogation of the accused and the Co-Accused, it was revealed that making of Fake Driving License was started since March, 2019 and till date a total of about 600 (six hundred) nos. were already produced and distributed to different persons. The rates also vary from Rs. 1000/- to Rs. 4000/- depending on the commissioners/middleman.

            A written FIR to this effect was lodged at Bawngkawn PS and C/No. 171/2019 Dt. 17.7.2019 u/s/ 467/468/34 IPC was registered against the above mentioned 5 (five) accused persons.
