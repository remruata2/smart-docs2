 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/624 Dt. 6.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

******One Bolero B/R AS-11 R-3806 driven by B. Hook of Nilam Bazar, Karimganj who was stranded at Kawrthah, Mizoram due to road block at the border crossed the state boundary at Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border today i.e., 7.11.2020 @ 2:40 p.m. Information received indicated that the road block organized by leaders of Kanhmun Village is still continuing in principle but no volunteer performed duty and at the Assam side the Assam police are the one who refuse entry of Mizoram bound vehicles.**

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

A series of bomb blast occurred today i.e., 7.11.2020 @ 1:20 a.m., 1:27 a.m. and 1:30 a.m. at Upper Painom (Phainuam) Lower Primary School (A Bengali medium School run by Assam government), Buarchep (also known as Upper Phainuam) and the blast was believed to have been caused by using gelatine dynamite. The school is located around 750 meters towards northern side from Buarchep Village and 350 meters inside Mizoram from Assam-Mizoram border. The school is a semi RCC Building measuring about 45 x 18.8 ft. The blast blown away GI Sheets roof of the School building veranda and caused several cracks and holes on the walls and the right most column of the building was also damaged. It also caused partial damaged to the School Kitchen building which is located at the right corner of the School building. Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) visited the area today @ 9:20 a.m. and after thorough inspection of the Place of Occurrence (PO) he left the area for Vairengte.

In this connection, a suo moto case has been registered at Vairengte Police Station vide C/No. 37/20 Dt. 7.11.2020 u/s 436/448/120 B IPC r/w 3(1) The Prevention of Damage to Public Property Act, 1984, 3(a), The Explosive Substances Act 1908 based on the enquiry report submitted by SI Ramtharnghaka of Vairengte PS and duly investigated into. 14 (fourteen) IR personnel are deployed at the PO and at the same time 10 (ten) SSB personnel are also deployed by Assam at the PO to maintain law and order at the PO. Information received indicated that one platoon of SSB (CAPF) led by one Assistant Commandant replaced Assam Police deployed at the Assam side.

Today i.e., 7.11.2020 @ 12:10 p.m. Smt. Keerthi Jalli, DC Cachar; Shri Bhanwar Lal Meena, SP Cachar; Shri Sudeep Nath, Sonai Circle Magistrate and SI Sarab Uddin Bhurbhaya, OC Dholai PS along with media persons from Assam visited the PO and returned to Assam at 1:52 p.m. today’s afternoon.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

There is no movement of vehicles across Bairabi border today i.e., 7.11.2020. Situation is normal.

However, Krishak Mukti Sangram Samittee (KMSS) and Chhatra Mukti Sangram Samittee (CMSS) organized rail block w.e.f., today i.e., 7.11.2020 @ 10:00 a.m. for Mizoram bound freight train at two locations viz., Muhamadpur Railway Station, Hailakandi District and Jarmira Railway Station, Hailakandi which is around 22 Km from Bairabi Rail Station. Around 100 (one hundred) KMSS and CMSS members participated in the said rail block. The rail block is still continuing at the time of writing the report i.e., 7.11.2020 @ 6:30 p.m.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

 Blockade of vehicles bound to Assam via Phaisen road at Zukbual Mual, Bilkhawthlir by Buhchangphai MZP is still continuing today i.e., 7.11.2020 and 13 (thirteen) vehicles are stranded due to the said roadblock till today.
