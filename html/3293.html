A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.6.3.2013

A man sentenced to life imprisonment : Yesterday ie, 5.3.2013, Addl. District & Session Judge-I Pu KL Liana sentenced Chiahluna (59) of Myanmar to life imprisonment. Chiahluna killed Zoramsanga (32) of Farkawn by hacking him on his back with an axe on 22nd December, 2011 at around 10:00PM.

            Chiahluna used to stay at the house of Zoramsanga; he was a labour whose profession was cutting woods/logs. On the night of the incident, they drank alcohol together with Zoramenga and Lalramenga and had a drunken tiff. Zoramsanga told Chiahluna to go to bed; he did but got up again and took an axe from the bathroom, and hacked at the back of Zoramsanga. Zoramsanga later succumbed to his injury. Chiahluna tried to run away but he was immediately apprehended and was sent to Jail.

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.6.3.2013

A child drowned at Tlawng River : Jacob Lalchhuanawma (14) s/o Lalngaizuali of Ramhlun North drowned in Tlawng River at about 12:00 noon yesterday. He had just passed class VII examination and stood 2nd position in his class. Volunteers from Sairang Dinthar Veng helped to recover his body and when it was found a coffin was made for him by Sairang Dinthar Veng.

 

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt.6.3.2013

House gutted: A traditional thatched house belonging to Damnemi (85) m/o Thangthuia at Vairengte Zalen Veng was gutted by fire at around 08:45PM on Monday. The locals rescued the old lady and her son from the fire.
