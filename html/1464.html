(i)      Security Scenario in Barak Valley (Assam) and Mizoram with special reference to insurgency:

          After the signing of Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) between Government of Mizoram and HPC (D) on  2.4.2018  , there has been no report of any active insurgency within the state.

 

(ii) Yaba Tablets : As far as Yabla tablets are concerned, there has been no records of its manufacturing and processing inside the state of Mizoram. The seizure of these contrabands by Mizoram Police are mainly during on its way to other states from Myanmar through Mizoram.

Seizuremade by CID SB  during 2021 :

(1)       On 18.3.2021 CID(SB) staffs had recovered and seized 5.856 kgs (50,000 tablets) of suspected Methamphetamine value of Rs.1,00,00,000/- in the local smuggling rate at New Capital Complex (Near Directorate of sports & Youth Services) from the join possession of (1) Pekliansanga(30) s/o Ngunthanga of Zokhawthar (2) Kajal Paul(42) s/o MC Paul of Sonai road, Silchar, Assam and (3) Bappu Choudhury of GC Collage road, Silchar, Assam P/A New Capital Complex, Aizawl. Vehicle (TATA 207) B/R MZ 01-E9399 which was used for transporting the suspected Methamphetamine and Rs.50,000/- found to be sale proceed money were also seized. Hence, Spl. Narcotic PS C/No.10/21 dt.18.3.2021 u/s 22(c)/29/25/62 ND&PS Act was registered.

            Regarding this, one of these accused, Kajal Paul(42) s/o MC Paul of Sonai road, Silchar, Assam is believed to be the main kingpin in the trafficking of Methamphetamine from Mizoram to Assam state and his arrest is considered to minimised the flow of these narcotics from Myanmar through Mizoram to Assam at the moment.

(2)       On 26.8.2021 , a Joint Operation team of CID (SB) and SIB, Mizoram recovered and seized 5 lakhs tablets of Methamphetamine (v/o Rs. 25 crores at international smuggling rate and Rs. 10 crores at the local smuggling rate) on 26.8.2021 @ 3:15 p.m. The contraband items were seized from 1 (one) heavy vehicle CRX 1512, BR/No. NL - 01A E-0243, driven by Mantu Kumar Dep (45) S/o MK Deb of Karimganj, Assam and second driver Subhas Das (33) S/o Sumesh Das of Karimganj, Assam near Buichali Bridge, Sairang, Aizawl District. In this connection, the above 2 (two) persons were arrested after observing all formalities and they were produced at Special Narcotic Police Station along with the seized contraband items. Accordingly, Special Narcotic PS Case No. 20/21  Dt. 26.8.2021 u/s 22(c) /29 ND & PS Act has been registered.
