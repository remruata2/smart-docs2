A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES &  NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 17.4.2013

Mamit Bank Robber still at large:  A hunt for the miscreants who robbed Office of the Mizoram Rural Bank at Mamit carried out by the Police is underway.

            In the Bank robbery incident that took place on the night of Monday i.e. 15.4.2013, more than ` 10,000/- in cash had been stolen. The money was reportedly belonged to Manager of the Bank. The offenders reportedly forced open the main gate. Since they failed to open the main safe locker, they unbolted all the almirah and drawers in search of money available. They also took away donations for the Bible Society of India.

            The Bank Manager Benjamin Lianzama said that there was no lost of important documents other than the said money taken away in cash. It has been further reported that no security guard has been deployed at the Bank and the CCTV installed could not work properly due to irregular electricity/power supply and thereby the unserviceable CCTV did not record anything.
