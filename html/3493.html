A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 8.5.2014

Thief Nabbed: Lalremruata (20) s/o Lalthangvela of Dilkhan was nabbed yesterday by the Police for stealing Pu JH Rothuama’s (MLA) mobile handset and ` 60,000/- cash. Police reported that in the morning of 3rd May 2014 between 3:30am to 5:00am, 2 mobile handsets and ` 60,000/- of Pu JH Rothuama were stolen from the VIP Cabin at Civil Hospital, Aizawl; after a thorough investigation Lalremruata - the culprit was arrested.

 

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 8.5.2014

Mamit DC Forbids To Go Near Border: Mamit DC, K. Lalthawmmawia made an order that from 7:00pm till 4:00am, no one is allowed to go within the area of International Border and within 5 km from Border no. 2300 – 2318 which includes the neighbouring villages of Rajiv Nagar and Amchurimukh. This is done for the safety of the public and to prevent intruders from entering Mizoram through Border. The order does not include Magistrate, Police and Armed Forces. The order will last for 2 months.

 

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 8.5.2014

Social Work to Rebuild Farm Huts Cancelled: Buhchangphai YMA intended to call for social work to rebuild the 4 huts dismantled by Assam Police at Buhchangphai farmland but the weather was not favourable and it was cancelled. It will be held at the earliest convenience.

 

 

D. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 8.5.2014

Murderer Arrested: Tuichawng Young Chakma Association (YCA) on Tuesday caught Rajesh of Raj Mandal Village at Tuichawng who is among the 5 alleged killers of a woman whose body was found near Bindasora, near Tlabung on Sunday. The woman was said to be raped and then killed. She was buried without knowing her identity, she was said to be from Longudu of Bangladesh. The whereabouts of the main culprit of the crime, Pori is not yet known.
