A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.3.2014_

****Land Verification on 11th April****:**Officials assigned to verify Mualkhang land which will be passed through by Bairabi to Sairang railway had a meeting at DC Kolasib office yesterday. The meeting decided that they will go to Mualkhang on 11th April to verify the people and lands at Mualkhang chosen to receive compensation.**

 The meeting was attended by member Secretary Lalrinawma, ASO Kolasib; VL Lura, Addl. SP, ACB; Inspr. RH Lalrinhlua, ACB; Gautam Sinharoy, EE Railways and Kamrul Islam, JE Railways. In the first assessment, 148 families were to receive compensation and the total amount of money to be used is ` 10,85,37,323/-.
