A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.16 & 17.12.2012

 

Road mishaps: At around 7:10pm on the evening of 15.12.2012, one maxi-cab plying between Aizawl and Saiha, met an accident and rolled down-hill near Sangau village. Four persons among the eleven passengers died while the rest sustained injuries. In another incident on the same day, four persons were killed in a similar mishap between Tualcheng village and Selam village in Champhai district.

 

 

 

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES/ TAWRHBAWM newspaper Dt. 17.12.2012

 

Vehicular Emission Testing Centre: The Mizoram Pollution Control Board(MPCB) have started installing Vehicular Emission Testing Centre in four places inside Aizawl city. From 10.12.2012, these centre started testing the level of smoke emitted by various vehicles, starting with vehicles suggested by the Traffic Police. All vehicles of Aizawl city are proposed to be checked at the first instance which will be followed by other districts.

 

 

 

 

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES/TAWRHBAWM newspaper Dt. 17.12.2012

 

Rupees 395 lakhs worth of narcotics: According to the reports of Central Anti Drugs Squad(CADS), a branch of CYMA dealing with narcotic substances, between 19th May, 2010 and 10th November, 2012, the monetary value of all the narcotic substances seized by them amounted to Rs.395 lakhs. The highest percentage of narcotic substances seized were Parvon, followed by Nitrosun/Alprozelam. The rest includes Heroine, Ganja, Cough Syrup and Opium. The report further indicated that 460 persons were handed over by the CADS to Police/Excise Department in this connection, 231 persons were sent to rehab and as many as 2468 persons were given counseling.
