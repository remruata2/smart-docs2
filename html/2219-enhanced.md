****STATUS OF CHIN REFUGEES RESIDING AT DELHI****

 There are around 3000 Chin (Burma Refugee) in Delhi, mostly residing at Delhi West, Janakpuri and Uttamnagar. The leaders of Chin refugee Committee are as follows :-

President  : Zachunglian Contact No. 8368424652

Vice President : Kawneing Contact No. 7683083495

Treasurer : Hakhuih Contact No. 9958240996

Among the refugees, around 15 families are Myanmarese National (Kawl) and the rest are the clans of Chin and Khumi. The refugees are looked after by United High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR).

As reported by the leaders of Refugee committee, before the outbreak of Covid-19 and execution of Lockdown, the refugees have no problems at all, but, after the execution of Lockdown, the refugees have faced many hardship especially in regard to essential commodities supply. Due to this problems, the following essential commodities items were distributed to the refugees by the UNHCR on 6.4.2020 :-

1. Rice - 15 kgs per family
2. Dal - 1 kg "  "
3. Cooking Oil - 1 litre " "
4. Sugar - 1 kg " "
5. Soap (Dettol) - 20 nos. Rs. 5/- per piece per family.

 As of now, it is learnt that most of the families have only small quantity of rice left with them. It is further learnt that they are intending to approach the Government of Mizoram for seeking relief.

 As per input, they are depending on supports from UNO, Local Church Leaders i.e., Presbyterian, Member Contribution, Generous Individuals etc.

 There is no positive case of COVID-19 among the Chin refugees at the moment.

 Mr. Dynasty @ MC-a (Contact No. 9818592409), who is the President of Matu Welfare Committee and a member of Chin Refugee Committee is the spokesperson for the refugees.
