****Rs. 21 lakhs stolen from Aizawl Hospital****:**Office of Aizawl Hospital &amp; Research Centre at Mission Veng was burgled yesterday ie, 11.3.2013. About Rs.21 lakhs was stolen and the culprit is not yet known. All the money kept in a safe after office hour on 9th March, 2013 was stolen. A key was used to open the safe, it was found out yesterday. Police and Forensic experts examined the place of occurrence yesterday.**

**C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.12.3.2013**

****Licensing Regulations-2012****:**Licensing Regulations-2012 would be enforced by Aizawl Municipal Council from the next Financial Year ie, from 1st April, 2013. Every commercial place and shop under Aizawl Municipal Council area should have license; Residential Certificate must be produced along with the application form. As such is the case, commercial places and shops cannot be owned in the name of non-Mizos. License can be obtained till September this year and another 3 months will be given if issuing of license cannot be finished within the allotted period.**
