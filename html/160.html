1. Pu K.Lianzuala, ex MLA and Chairman, MKVI Board :

           

Pu K. Lianzuala is the incumbent Chairman of Mizoram Khadi Village Industry Board, whose main function is to select beneficiaries of loans/funds under MKVI.  The enquiry officer had met and interacted with Pu K. Lianzuala and it was learnt that he had not received any intimidation, security threats from any opposition party, anti-social elements.

Therefore, at present the security threat perceived by him is minimal. It is therefore suggested that his security cover may be reviewed.

 

 

2. Pu Lalduhoma, President, ZNP :

Pu Lalduhoma, Ex MLA and President of ZNP is having one Personal Security Officer(PSO) since 2015. The enquiry officer had met and interacted with him during the assessment. At present, the security threat perceived by Pu Lalduhoma is minimal be it from insurgent group, political parties, NGOs or even from personal enmity.  It is therefore, suggested that his security cover may be reviewed.

 

 

 

3. Prof. JV Hluna, President, BJP Mizoram Pradesh :

 

            Prof. JV Hluna, President, BJP Mizoram Pradesh is having one PSO since 2016. As of now, no specific threat perception is found against Prof. J.V. Hluna. He also admitted that as long as he is inside Aizawl city, his requirement of PSO is minimal.

It is therefore suggested that his security cover may be reviewed.

 

 

 

4. Pu Liansangzuala, Special Judge, ND&PS:

 

The enquiry officer found no specific security threat against Pu Liansangzuala, Special Judge of ND&PS   during the assessment.  However, Pu Liansangzuala admitted that he felt a sense of insecurity due to the nature of his current assignment. He had recently made a number of exemplary judgment on ND&PS related cases against the drugs traffickers, etc and to facilitate his free and fair judgment against illegal drugs traffickers, etc his personal security must be ensured.

Due to the nature of his sensitive work, it is recommended that he may retain his scale of security i.e one PSO
