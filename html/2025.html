INTERROGATION STATEMENT OF SS Pvt. SUHRAB CHAKMA (31) S/o KOMAL CHAKMA OF DETRINALA (BANGLADESH)

 

 

                        I joined JSS sometime in December 2001 after I was convinced by the leaders by the leaders of the outfit that Bangladesh Gov't. failed to honour the peace accord. Hence I joined the movement to pressurize the Bangladesh Gov't. to fulfill its promises.

 

                        I was arrested by BSF party at Chotapansury, Mizoram, India on 18.7.09 who seized my weapon, cash and other personal items.
