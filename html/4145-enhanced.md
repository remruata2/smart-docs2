**SPECIAL REPORT**

It was reliably learned that 47 people of Myanmar refugees entered Hmawngbu on 9/5/24 from 11 pm onwards in Lawngtlai district from Myanmar. Upon receipt of the information, IC Bungtlang Out Post reached the spot and verified it, reported that these Myanmar Refugees fled their home due to fear of the Arakan Army as they are allegedly trying to recruit in the AA cadre forcefully. It is also learnt that the 47 people belonged to the Bawm community and are from Samang village, Palawa District of Myanmar. These 47 Myanmar refugees temporarily reside with their respective relatives at Hmawngbu. The details of the persons are as follows:
