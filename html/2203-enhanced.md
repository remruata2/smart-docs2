****MIZORAM PEOPLE FORUM (MPF)****

**FORMED :**On 21.6.2006, members of different Church Denominations of Mizoram and leaders of NGOs around 25 in numbers had a meeting at synod committee room. The meeting was presided over by the then Moderator of Mizoram Synod, Rev. Chanchinmawia. The meeting decided to form Mizoram People Forum (MPF) with a view to lead the Mizoram Government in a right direction.

 The first appointed leaders of MPF for the year 2006 – 2008 are :

President : Rev. Chanchinmawia, Moderator, Mizoram Synod
