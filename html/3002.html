SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/349

Dated: 24th May, 2019.

 

 

            There was bomb blast at a mosque named ‘Bhaithul Mukharram Jamma Masjid’ located near Zophai in the disputed Inner line Forest Reserve area around 0215 hours in the intervening night of 23-24 May, 2019. The blast had caused partial damage to the mosque building without casualty. Local people (Bengali Muslims) of the area had blocked the road in protest against the blast though there was no report of violence.

 

DCs/SsP of both Kolasib and Hailakandi Districts rushed to the spot and subsequently held a meeting at PWD IB, Bairabi in which they resolved to take all possible measures to prevent possible law and order problem and to maintain peace in the area. It was also decided in the meeting that Cases be registered at both Bairabi P/S and Zamira P/S, (Assam).

 

The road blockade by local Bengali residents was also lifted at 2:20 p.m following the request by the District Administration.

 

Identity of perpetrator(s) of last night's bomb blast is yet to be established and no suspicious movement particularly of Mizos was noticed during the incident. It has been reported that Muslims are presently observing 'Ruza' (Ramzan) or Fasting and the mosque being frequently used by locals to offer prayer.

 

It has been reported that one local resident identified as Rafik Uddin (20) s/o Rajak Uddin, Imam of the mosque had visited the mosque and made announcement @ 1:30 a.m before the bomb blast. Investigation is going on.

 

There is also no report of communal problem after the blast and the situation is normal and under close watch.
