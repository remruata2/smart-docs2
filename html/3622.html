8.4.2022  : Mizoram Hindi Shikshak Sangh (CSS) General Headquarters submitted a written petition to Chief Minister of Mizoram on 8.4.2022 wherein they stated that at present, there are 114 (one hundred fourteen) vacant sanction posts of High School teachers and 358 (three hundred fifty eight) Middle School Hindi teachers, and they requested to take necessary action for filling up of the said vacant posts. It also requested the Chief Minister to take necessary action at the Centre to increase Mizoram Budget for Hindi Teacher as many Hindi teachers are facing hardship due to termination from their post after the end of their engagement scheme/period.

18.4.2022 :    Mizoram Hindi Shikshak Sangh (CSS) in its Press Release issued on 18.4.2022 expressed gratitude over the Central Government proposal to make Hindi Language as a compulsory subject up to Class-10 level. It also stated that implementation of the stated proposal would facilitate students of Mizoram to be more proficient in Hindi language, and perceived the said proposal as great challenges for Hindi teaching community. It further expected the State government to fully implement the said proposal as proficiency in Hindi language is crucial for the students as well as the general public of Mizoram.

29.4.2022 :    Mizoram Hindi Shikshak Sangh (CSS) held General Conference on 29.4.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Vanapa Hall. The conference was divided into two sessions. Z. Lalramthara, Vice President, MHSS (CSS) and Lalrawngbawla, President MHSS (CSS) presided the sessions, and around 250 (two hundred) fifty members attended. During the conference, matters concerning their association were discussed. The conference passed resolution which stated that only trained candidates should be selected as Hindi Teacher in future recruitment conducted by the state government and expressed gratitude to the state government for recruiting new Hindi Teachers.

 

6.5.2022 : 4 (four) leaders of Mizoram Hindi Shikshak Sangh (CSS) led by Lalrawngbawla, President, met Shri. Ram Nath Kovind, Hon'ble President of India 6.5.2022 @ 9:30 a.m. at Raj Bhavan and submitted a written petition, wherein they requested the Hon'ble President to take necessary action so that at least half the salary of regular Teachers are earned by CSS Hindi Teachers. It also requested to take necessary action for regularization of Hindi Teachers considering their extremely unstable condition/status. It further highlighted the status of Hindi language within the state and vacancy of regular Hindi Teacher posts under the state government.

10.8.2022 :    Mizo Hindi Zirlai Pawl (MHZP) in its Press Release issued on 10.8.2022 expressed regret over deterioration of Hindi Language teaching due to termination of various Hindi Teachers, who had already served more than 10 years and recruited through formal DPC. At the same time, it expressed gratitude for recruitment of 806 (eight hundred six) CCS Hindi Teachers under Samagra with the initiative of Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister and Pu Lalchhandama Ralte, Education Minister. It also stated that MHZP leaders met the Chief Minister and Education Minister on 8.8.2022 in connection with appointment of 2 (two) non-Mizo Hindi Teachers in violation of the Recruitment Rules and urged the authorities to take necessary action for their termination before the end of August, 2022.

9.9.2022 :      Mizo Hindi Zirlai Pawl (MHZP) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 9.9.2022 stated that leaders of MHZP met Chief Minister and Education Minister on 8.8.2022 in connection with appointment of 2 (two) non-Mizo Teachers under CSS Hindi Teacher in violation of Recruitment Rules (RR) and urged to take necessary action for their termination before the end of August, 2022. In this regard, as per the request of SAMAGRA, MHZP decided to await the state government till 16.9.2022. It further stated that they will take necessary action, if the state government fails to take action within the stipulated time.

19.9.2022:     Mizo Hindi Zirlai Pawl (MHZP) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 17.9.2022 stated that MHZP General Headquarters decided to resort to picketing of Samagra Office w.e.f., 20.9.2022 as no positive response has been received within the stipulated time regarding their demand for termination of 2 (two) non-Mizos CSS Hindi Teachers under Samagra, who were allegedly recruited in violation of Recruitment Rules (RR). It also stated that said teachers were recruited in violation of RR Annexure-I (See Rule 2, 3 and 4) which prescribed working knowledge of Mizo Language at least Middle School standard as one of the eligibility for the post, and it considered the same as snatching away the rightful share of indigenous persons. It further stated that they intend to take necessary action so that similar incident of undue favoritism should not recur in future.

            Information received stated that they intend to start the picketing from 20.9.2022 @8:30 am.

20.9.2022 :    Mizo Hindi Zirlai Pawl (MHZP) General Headquarters Emergency Meeting held on 19.9.2022 decided to cancel their intention to organize picketing of Samagra Office w.e.f., 20.9.2022 as Education Minister reportedly assured to take necessary action on their demand for termination of 2 (two) non-Mizos CCS Hindi Teachers, who were allegedly recruited in violation of the Recruitment Rules. The meeting demanded issuance of Termination of the said Hindi Teachers before the end of Dt. 23.9.2022. A Press Release in this regard was issued the same evening.

21.10.2022 :  Mizoram High School Hindi Teacher's Association (MHSHTA) held its 10th General Conference at Chanmari YMA Hall, Aizawl on 21.10.2022 @11:00 am.
The first session was presided over by Pu H. Lalsailova, Vice President, MHSHTA and in the second session held in the afternoon election of officer bearers was conducted. As many as 120 (one hundred twenty) members attended the conference.

26.10.2022:   Mizo Hindi Zirlai Pawl (MHZP) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 26.10.2022 stated that MZHP swiftly pursued a complaint received regarding used of fake D.El.Ed Certificates by some candidates in the recruitment of 50 (fifty) Posts of Hindi Teacher under Samagra. In this connection, Education Minister and authorities of Samagra were approached and requested to cross check all documents of the applicants. It also stated that as the authority assured to carefully conduct interview and extended the duration of the interview, they cancelled their intention to boycott interview for the said post, which is scheduled to commence w.e.f., 26.10.2022. It further stated that if anyone is found using fake documents, they urged the state government to take necessary action against such person. It requested the state government to take necessary action for fair conduct of the recruitment process.
