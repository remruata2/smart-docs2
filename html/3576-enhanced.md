A. _Front page of VANGLAINI/ THE AIZAWL POST newspapers_

_Dt. 19.7.2022_

****All MLAs Casted their votes:** In the 16th Presidential election of India which was concluded yesterday, all MLAs of Mizoram casted their votes which was conducted at Mizoram Assembly Legislative Main Building Committee Room. ZPM MLA, Pu Lalchhuanthanga, who casted his vote at 10:15am was the first among the MLAs with MNF MLA, Dr. K.Pachhunga who casted his vote at 1:05pm as the last. As per instructions of Election Commission of India, the polling booth remained opened till 5:00pm in the evening although casting of the votes were completed by 1:30pm. The ballot box, which is being kept in a strong room and under proper protection will be taken to the office of the Returning Officer, New Delhi today i.e 19.7.2022 and will be counted on 21.7.2022.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI/____THE AIZAWL POST newspapers_

_Dt.19.7. 2022_

**Tri-colour at every house :******Under the Chairmanship of Union Home Minister, Shri Amit Shah, Chief Ministers and Governors/Lieutenant Governors of different states/UTs held a discussion on 17th July 2022 through video conferencing regarding 'Har Ghar Tiranga' programme. Under this programme, as a way of celebrating the 75th India's Independence Day, proposal is being made for the National Flag to be hoisted in every house hold of the country from 13th-15th August 2022. In Mizoram, Art &amp; culture Department, the concerned department will hold a meeting today i.e.19.7.2022 in this regard.
