**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.17.1.2013**

**Helmet approved by ISI only for Traffic Police**: The Aizawl Traffic Police SP, Pu Lallianmawia ordered all the Traffic Police personnel who are driving any two wheeler to wear helmet which are approved by Indian Standard Institute(ISI) or at least its equivalent. The SP explained that the reason behind this was to make an example to the people as this rule will be made mandatory for the public in the near future.
