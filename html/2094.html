CHAMPHAI DISTRICT-A LIRNGHING CHUNGCHANG

 

                        Ni 22.6.2020 zing dar 4:10-ah khan Champhai District a East Tuipui Constituency chhung leh Champhai South Constituency (Champhai Block leh Khawbung Block) chhunga khaw thenkhatah Lir a nghing a, he mi hnu atang hian vawi tam tak khawchhak tlangdungah hian lir hi nghingin mipui an ralkhel hle a, Lirnghing hian in tam tak tichhia in, thenkhat chu luah tlak loh khawpin a chhia a, Lir hi a nghin nasat em avang leh a nghin ngun em avangin Champhai Dist. a khaw tam tak chu an mahni inah pawh awm ngam lovin, tual zawl ah mutna leh bukte te siamin an in hi an rauhsan a ni. Lirnghing avang hian Champhai RD Block a in 26 leh Khawbung RD Block a in 228 lai chu repair ngei ngei ngaiin a chhia a, Zawlsei khua, Khawbung RD Block hnuaia awm chu in chhe tamna ber khua a niin, In 34 lai a chhia a ni. Chenna in bakah hian Sawrkar building leh School thenkhat te, Khawtlang hmun pawimawh thenkhatte, In chhung bungrua thahnem tak te a chhia a, Vawk note paruk(6) laiin an thih phah bawk a ni.

 

                        Ni 22.6.2020 atanga Ni 19.8.2020 chhung khan vawi 100 lai lir hi a nghing a, nikhatah vawi 1 mai nghing lovin vawi tam tak a nghing a, Ni 25.6.2020 phei kha chuan lir hi vawi 11 lai a nghing a ni.

 

                        Hetianga khawchhak tlangdungin chhiatna an tuar hi a tuartu khua te chuan hrehawm an tiin an thlabar hle a, thlamuan leh chhawmdawl te pawh an mamawh hle in an in hria a, chutih laiin Chief Minister te, DM&R Minister te leh Home Minister ten chhiatna thlenna tlangdung an la tlawh miah lo chu mi tam takin an dem rawn a. Mahse an ni hian helai hmun hi tlawh rih lo mah se, Sawrkar mi pawimawh, Minister, MLA leh Political Party hruaitu thenkhat te, NGOs hruaitu thenkhat te bakah Sawrkar Official thenkhat ten helai hmun hi tlawhin, chhawmdawlna te pe in an thlamuanpui viau niin an sawi.
