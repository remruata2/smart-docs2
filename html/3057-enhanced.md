****REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/MISC/2020/298

Dated: 19th May, 2020.

 As per input received, Pu Vanlalrova @ Marova, Ex VCP Bairabi, Kolasib District visited his paddy field at Zophai near Mizoram-Assam border today, i.e., 19.5.2020 and while he was on his way to his paddy field, he saw 2 (two) non-Mizos, viz., Monir, caretaker of paddy field of Darthanmawia, Aizawl and Moinu, caretaker of paddy field of V.L Nghaka, Baktawng, constructing a hut using young teak log and bamboos at their respective paddy fields. He asked the reason for their construction and they both replied that they are constructing as per instructions of Assam Police. He then immediately informed IR personnel on duty as well as Bairabi PS who in turn immediately rushed to the place and dismantled the 2 (two) huts. While dismantling the said two huts, Assam Police personnel were also present but they denied giving instructions for setting up of the two huts. The PO is 2 kms. from IR Duty Post and 10 kms. from Bairabi PS.

 In this connection, Bairabi MZP President was also contacted in which he has stated his ignorance of the details and he is still enquiring into the matter. As per input, Pu Vanlalrova and Bairabi Police will proceed to the spot tomorrow, i.e., 20.5.2020 to take stock of the situation.

 Information has also been received that Kolasib District Police authorities have taken up the matter and accordingly liaised with their counterpart SP, Hailakandi to prevent any untoward incident.

 Situation is being under close watch.
