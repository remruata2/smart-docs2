A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 06 & 07.11.2022

Road blockade at Vairengte called off: The blockade of National Highway at Vairengte by Land Owner Association (Vairengte-Kawnpui Package 3-6 HN 06 & 306) which was commenced from 3.11.2022 was called off from the evening of  5.11.2022 after leaders of Central YMA requested the leaders of Land Owners Association to call off the blockade. Leaders of CYMA, who acts as mediators between Government of Mizoram and Land Owners Association promised to take all possible steps for the implementation of the demands of land Owners Association. At the same time, Leaders of Land Owners Association insisted the State government to take proper actions on their demands within the month of November, 2022.

 

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 06 & 07.11.2022

Blood donations: To marked their raising day anniversary, KTP Dinthar Branch, Aizawl donated 39 units of blood (33 males and 6 females) for Synod Hospital, Durtlang on 5th November, 2022. Meanwhile, PYD (NEI), Kolasib Venglai also donated 33 units of blood (22 males and 11 females) for Kolasib District Hospital on the same day as a means of observing their Social Work Day programme.

 

 

 

C. Second page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 06 & 07.11.2022

Crime & Economic Offences PS inaugurated : Director General of Police, Mizoram Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastva on 4th November 2022 inaugurated  Crime & Economic Offences Police Station, located at CID Complex, Bungkawn,  Aizawl. Speaking at the inaugural function which was held at MPSOA Conference Hall, Bungkawn, the DGP expressed his gratitude for the implementation of various important assignment of Criminal Investigation Department after a span of 17 years which are mentioned in Mizoram Police Manual 2005. Mizoram government had issued a notification for the establishment of Economic Offences Police Station on 10th  October 2022.
