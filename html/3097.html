A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 2.8.2013

Zuangtui Road Block stopped - FIR Re-lodged: The road blockade of Zuangtui community in their locality from 31st July 2013 to urge for progress in the investigation on the death of Lalnunmawia at Falkland on 5th June 2013 ended at around 9:00 AM yesterday.

            Zuangtui community demands for the arrest of the culprit(s) and lodged an FIR on 6th July 2013 at Bawngkawn Police Station. Another FIR was lodged again yesterday by Zuangtui community leaders in the presence of Aizawl SP who visited the place of road block.

The new FIR stated that a father and son and some JAC members of Falkland who knew the incident before and after the murder were suspected of involvement in the murder. And also stated that if action is not taken against these suspected people, Zuangtui people’s wrath will be hard to contain.
