**A. Front page of MIZO AW newspaper.Dt.07.12.2012**

**Suspected murderer acquitted:** The main accused in the murder of C.Lalthlengliana of Kawlkulh town on 2004, Lalremruata, was acquitted by the Court yesterday by Addl. District &amp; Session Judge-I, Pu KL. Liana. The Court make this verdict due to the insufficiency of evidence on the part of the prosecution party. In the case records submitted to the Court, certain documents such as PME records, FIR copy, seizure memo and arrest memo were also missing.

****A man killed by falling timber:** Yesterday, Vanlalsanga(58) S/o Patea of Darlung village was killed when a huge timber, around 70 cubic mtrs. suddenly falls upon him and crush him on the ground.
