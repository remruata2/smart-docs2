Notes on Mizoram Bru People Convention(MBPC)

 

 

          Mizoram Bru People Convention (MBPC) was formed on 20.6.2008 at Bru Christian Fellowship Hall, Chanmari, Aizawl. The organization aims at striving for the welfare of the Bru community.

 

          The meeting held on 20.6.2008 was presided over by C.Biakmawia President, Bru Welfare Association of Mizoram(BWAM) and was attended by 30 members. The members present appointed the following as the new leaders of the organization.

 

          President                        :         Lallawmsanga Apeto, Ex MDC,LADC

          V/President                    :         Surjya Moni Reang of Damdiai (Ex –BNLF                                                                                                                  President)

          Treasurer                       :         Birbahu, Tuipuibari.

          Gen. Secretary               :         Bruto Meska (Ex. BNLF Foreign Secretary)

          Asstt. Gen. Secy            :         A.Lalnunmawia(V/President, BSA)

          Secy. Information          :         A.Biakthuama (President, BCO)

          Fin Secy                         :         Solomon Proful Ushoy (Ex-BNLF Gen Secy).

         

          MBPC held its first meeting on 17.7.2008 in the residence of A.Biakthuama at Chanmari Chaltlang road, Aizawl and appointed 60 Central Executive Committee members apart from BRU VCPs and NGOs Presidents as its Ex-Officio members.

 

          MBPC CEC meeting was again held on 28.8.2008 at Chanmari, Aizawl. This meeting proposed to field Bru candidates  in the coming MLA Election 2008 in Lawngtlai and Mamit Constituencies and to form an alliance either with the MNF or Congress Party. The organisation also held talks with these two parties but have not yet made any final decision. MBPC said that since it is an NGO, no other Bru organisations should field candidates in the name of MBPC.

 

          The meeting also decided to take steps for the implementation of the Mizoram Govt.-BNLF peace accord.
