SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/909

Dated: 12nd December, 2019.

 

With the growing objections of the alleged illegal villages, which are mainly inhabited by Chakmas, among the localities in Tlabung area, District Level Screening Committee of Lunglei District constituted Joint Inspection Team (JIT) comprising of representatives from concerned departments and NGOs to conduct spot verification of the allegation under the Chairmanship of SDO(C), Tlabung vide Memo No.B.12033/14/2019-DLAO (L) dt. 8.8.2019. The JIT had conducted their verification by visiting the alleged villages and collected the connected necessary documents.  

Meanwhile, Local Administration Department, Govt. of Mizoram issued approval order of the newly elected sub-village committee members for the period of 1 (one) year vide Order No.B.14016/19/2014-LAD/VC dt. 19.8.2019 wherein Serhuan was also included.

Consequently, Tlabung Branch YMA convened General Meeting for its members on 6.12.2019 @ 6:15 p.m at Tlabung YMA Run under the chairmanship of Pu Zokunga Sailo, President and discussed about their on-going issue about eviction of illegal villages/settlements within Tlabung area. They expressed their discontentment on approval of Serhuan Sub-village Committee by the Government and agreed to request Sub-Hqrs. YMA, Lunglei to pursue the matter before 11.12.2019. They further decided to take action not only to the villagers of Serhuan but also to all illegal villages within Tlabung area if there is no positive outcome. In addition to this, they requested Serhuan land owners to lodge an FIR against the settlers.

Consequent to the meeting, the 3 (three) land owners namely; (i) H. Lalhmingthanga s/o Hrangchhuana (L); (ii) R. Lalchhumi w/o Hrangchhuana (L) and (iii) T.C. Lalropuii w/o Lallunghnema (L) & H. Lalbiakdiki d/o H. Kapmawia (L), all from Tlabung, went to their respective land at Serhuan on 9.12.2019 and in pursuance of the general meeting, they directed the inhabitants to vacate their respective places on or before 11.12.2019. As such the residents of Serhuan also prepared themselves to vacate the said settlement on 11.12.2019 and they will be temporarily re-settled at Nunsury-I Playground.

Moreover, Deputy Commissioner, Lunglei also sent a letter regarding approval of Serhuan Sub-Village Committee to the Under Secretary, Local Administration Department vide letter No.B.13011/4/2012/DCLP dt. 9.12.2019. In the letter, the resolution of the District Level Screening Committee meeting held on 2.12.2019 was mentioned. The meeting, after considering the report of JIT and the various problems including deforestation, floods that occur almost every year, insufficiency of ration quota by Nunsury-I villagers, etc., agreed to evict the inhabitants of Serhuan sub-village. They further resolved Nunsury VC to arrange for the resettlement of the villagers within their jurisdiction. The meeting also decided to propose Serhuan area as ‘Prohibited Area’ in order to prevent any person from settling in the area.

The letter, while mentioning their resolution, requested the competent authority to review and discontinue their approval of Serhuan Sub-village Committee at the earliest along with the enclosing letters from Village Councils of Tlabung and Nunsury-I which objected the existence of Serhuan sub-village.

There are 140 (one hundred forty) households with 729 family members presently residing in the alleged illegal settlement of Serhuan and they have expressed their intention to vacate the settlement. The temporary shelter, which can accommodate 36 families, is being prepared at the playground of Nunsury-I village. 17 (seventeen) families already vacated while writing the report and are resettled in the following villages :-

  1. Lamthai                   -           5 families
  2. Nunsury - I              -           5 families
  3. Nunsury - II -           1 family
  4. Silgur                       -           1 family
  5. Belpei                      -           1 family
  6. Dinthar                    -           3 families
  7. Diblibagh                 -           1 family

 

It is learnt that 11 (eleven) Christian families including 1 (one) Missionary are being settled at Baptist Church of Mizoram Complex at Serhuan which was bought by the Church back in 1981 from the possession of Pu Rohnuna (L). They are waiting for further instruction from their Church leaders upon whether to resettle in the Church land at Nunsury-I which was purchased from Pi Ziri w/o Raua (L).

 

Information has been received that Tlabung Branch YMA is convening general meeting for their members today, i.e. 12.12.2019 @ 6:15 p.m at YMA Run, Tlabung.

 

In connection to the development at Serhuan and to prevent any unwanted element,
Pu Lalchhuanthanga MPS, Addl. SP, Lunglei is stationed at Tlabung and necessary security arrangement was done by District Police.

 

Situation under close watch.

DGP (M)
