REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT

 

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/588

Dated 21st October, 2020 

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/586 Dt. 20.10.2020, 55 (fifty five) members of Bungthuam Branch YMA performed duty near Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border today i.e., 21.10.2020 and they intend to perform till late evening. Information has been received that today around 1:00 p.m. one section of Assam Armed Police conducted patrolling near the proposed Assam Police Camp at Mauhak Mual (Segontillah Hill) around 500 (five hundred) meters from Mizoram Police Camp and immediately return to Zaite Village.

          Further, 25 (twenty five) members of Luimawi Branch YMA performed border duty at Medili Bridge near Kanhmun, Mizoram-Assam border w.e.f., today i.e., 21.10.2020. One platoon of 5th IR battalion was stationed near Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border to reinforce the existing force. As per information, there is no intention to block vehicle entering from Assam at Medili Bridge from either sides but due to the prevailing situation there has been no entry of vehicle at Medili Bridge till the time of writing the report i.e., 21.10.2020 @ 6:00 p.m.

          Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
