QUALITY POLICY

Asteria Aerospace is committed to delivering products to the highest quality standards and achieving full compliance with quality management processes across all phases of product development and deployment. Asteria’s quality practices focus not only on the finished product, but also on the processes used to create and deliver it.

 

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT PROCESS

At Asteria, we follow a rigorous product development process which starts with detailed system and subsystem requirements, followed by design, implementation/manufacture, subsystem level testing and finally system level testing. Supporting documentation for validation and verification is created at each stage in the process. This process allows us to maintain traceability from start to finish as well as mitigate issues as early as possible in the development cycle in order to create and deliver quality products to our clients.

 

ISO 9001:2008 CERTIFIED

Asteria Aerospace  is committed to following a quality management system across all areas of operation in order to create and deliver superior products to its customers . Asteria’s quality management system is certified to meet the requirements of the international ISO 9001:2008 standard (certification attached).
