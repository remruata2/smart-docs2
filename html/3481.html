A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.3.2014

Heroin Seized: Kolasib Police seized 16.7 grams of Heroin at Rengtekawn on the night of 19.3.2014 and arrested the alleged owners - R Vanlalpeka (27) of Kolasib Diakkawn; Lalnunpuii (36) of Kolasib Khuangpuilam and Lalnunpari of Kolasib Khuangpuilam.

 

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.3.2014

Mizoram IR in Assam: Answering the question of MNF member Lalrinawma in the Assembly Budget Session, Home Minister R.Lalzirliana said that he does not know how long 3rd Bn. IR personnel posted in Assam from 2010 will remain there. Home Minister told the House that a request has already been made to Ministry of Home Affairs for the shifting of 3rd Bn. IR in Assam after 3 years of staying.
