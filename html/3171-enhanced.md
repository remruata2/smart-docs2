**A.Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt.06.02.2013:**

**Forensic expert trace out murderer:******Lalrochhunga (25) s/o Tawklala of Sailulak is found guilty and sentenced him of life with rigorous imprisonment and a fine of Rs.5000/- by the court, (KL.Liana, Addl.District &amp; Session Judge-I) under 302 IPC for murdering Lalawmpuii (48) on 24th May 2010.

 The dead body of Lalawmpuii was found at her jhum, initially the general people of the village presumed that the cause of her death was due to accidental one. Therefore, she was buried as per the customary law. Later, some body reported that they heard a sound of gunshot at the place of occurrence and subsequently they also seen unidentified man carrying with gun in vicinity of the following evening. Under such circumstances, an FIR was lodged to Bawngkawn PS, the real culprit Lalrochhunga was apprehended in the jungle with gun by the villagers of Thingsulthliah/Tlungvel in the following night.

 The exhumation of the corpse for post-mortem was done; two bullets were taken out from the victim's head. Forensic experts proved that these bullets matched with the bullets caught from Lalrochhunga.

**C. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt.06.02.2013:******

**A crowd gathered at District Jail:******At around 6:00pm last night, people of Saiha Vengpui gathered at District Jail, Saiha in support of Hrallai of their locality. The crowd threw stone at the Jail warder who was injured but no big trouble arose.

 On 1st Feb.2013, 3 lads of Saiha Vengpui were barked at by a dog and the lads started to treat the dog roughly, the dog’s owner K.Laldingliana and the lads quarrelled. In anger, K.Laldingliana beat Hralai with a bamboo stick, he stumbled down the road. The injured lad is now being treated at Civil Hospital, Aizawl and still in a state of unconsciousness, while K.Laldingliana was kept in District Jail.

 Yesterday evening, rumour was spread that Hralai died and in order to support for Hralai, people of Saiha Vengpui gathered at District Jail, the angry crowd even damaged a bit of K.Laldingliana's house. When they heard Hralai did not die, the crowd anger subsided and they dispersed.
