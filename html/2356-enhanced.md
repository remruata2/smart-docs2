****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/170

Dated 10th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/167 Dt. 9.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

27 (twenty seven) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-16, LMV-10) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 10.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

43 (forty three) vehicles (HMV-11, MMV-23, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 10.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
