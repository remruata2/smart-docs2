A. _Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 20.3.2013_

_Pistol seized_:****A joint team of Bawngkawn PS Staff and Tuirial Branch YMA seized a local made pistol from Chinsianmuan****(24) s/o Haumunga of Champhai Kanan on the night of 18th March. Chinsianmuan was arrested 2 years back for robbing different shops in Aizawl; he stacked up his stolen properties in a cave at the outskirt of Aizawl and lived there. He was arrested and detained in Central Jail for some time, after his release he was arrested again recently.
