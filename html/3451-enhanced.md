****18.2.2022:** Leaders of Hmar People's Convention Party (HPC) called a press meet today, i.e., 18.2.2022 at Sinlung Hills Development Council (SHDC) Office, MINECO, Aizawl, under the guidance of Pu Lalremruata Varte, Adviser HPC and 5 (five) HPC leaders and 15 (fifteen) media persons were present. HPC Leaders stated their opposition to the No Confidence Motion moved against the present SHC government by 6 (six) members of SHC. It was also pointed out that if the SHC Special Session is to be convened as prayed by the above 6 (six) SHC members, it should be convened at SHC Headquarters, Sakawrdai. In case of failure to convene SHC Special Session at Sakawrdai, they expressed their intention to boycott the session.**

**28.2.2022:** Hmar Peoples' Convention (HPC) General Headquarters OB Meeting was held on 26.2.2022 @11:00 a.m. at the residence of Pu Rohringa, Khatla, Aizawl. The meeting was presided by Pu Rohringa, President, HPC General Headquarters and 6 (six) members attended. The meeting passed the following resolutions :-

 (i) Strongly opposed any attempt to dissolve the agreement signed  by MNF and HPC on 5.11.2019 for 1st Election of Sinlung Hills  Council (HPC) General Election.

 (ii) Pu Biaknunga, CEM led SHC government being achieved many   developments and in this connection, it expressed gratitude to the  Chief  Minister of Mizoram and Deputy Chief Minister.

 (iii) It also expressed gratitude for having initiative in providing   employment to HPC Returnees.

 (iv) Strongly condemned No Confidence Motion moved in an attempt  to dissolve the present SHC government by 3 (three) MNF  members (without Headquarters' consent) and 3 (three)  Independent members.

 (v) It intended to organize indefinite Total Bandh within SHC   Headquarters i.e., Sakawrdai areas w.e.f. 1.3.2022 in order to  uphold the dignity of SHC and the agreement signed by MNF  and HPC.

 In connection with the above, Joint YMA Sakawrdai; Hmar Students Association, Sakawrdai Unit and Joint MHIP, Sakawrdai issued a Joint Statement on 28.2.2022 which stated that Sakawrdai All Parties and All NGOs Committee Meeting held today @ 10:30 a.m. at Joint YMA Information Centre, Sakawrdai decided not to participate in an indefinite Total Bandh intended to be called by HPC General Headquarters w.e.f. 1.3.2022. A similar Press Release was issued by MNF Sakawrdai Group the same day.

 HPC General Headquarters; Hmar Ex-Volunteers and VC Sakawrdai held a Joint Meeting today, i.e., 28.2.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Sakawrdai YMA Information Centre. Pu Rohringa, President HPC General Headquarters presided the meeting and 12 (twelve) members attended. The meeting decided not to go ahead with the intended indefinite total bandh, which is intended to be called w.e.f. 1.3.2022 within SHC Headquarters i.e., Sakawrdai area as per the written request submitted by VC, Sakawrdai.
