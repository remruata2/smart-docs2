**History :****(He write up hi kum 2010 vel a siam anih hmel )**

 _Burma ramah sipai sorkarna (Military Junta) a piantirh kum 1962 a General Newin an sorkarna a chelh atangin Burma ram chhungah zirlai ten lungawilohna an lantir thin a, kum 1988 ah zirlai nawlpui in Gen. Newin a sorkar paihthlak tumin rampumah demonstration/agitation an huaihawt ta, hetah hian Chin State a hnam hrang hrang te pawh agitation ah hian an tel ve ani. Zirlai te lungawilohna lantir avangin kum 1988 ah chuan Gen. Newin chu paihthlak niin a aiah Gen. Than Shwe chu a lo lal leh ta a, sipai sorkarna atanga sipai sorkar bawk a lo pian zui leh tak avangin sipai sorkar te chuan zirlai hruaitu tam tak a man khawm a, a then te chu hmun hrang hrang, Thailand, India, Bangladesh ah te an tlanchhe ta chiam a ni._

_Hetianga sipai leh zirlai inkara buaina leh harsatna lian tak a awm takah chuan Burma ram chhunga Chin mi te chuan Burma ram chu sipai sorkarna paihthlak a nih theih nan kum 1988 March ni 20 khan hma latu tuna Chin National Front(CNF) a lo ding ta a, an president atan Tialkhala niin underground group tur te pawh din nghal a ni. Kum 1988 October thlaah Champhai ah Burma zirlai raltlan te chu lo chhukin Champhai ah refugee camp sak a ni a, hetah hian zirlai engemawzat chu an khawsa ta a ni. Tichuan CNF dinchhuaktu Tialkhala, president leh a nau te Liannova@ Panova P/A Chhinga Veng, Aizawl te chu Burma atangin kum 1988 December thla ah Champhai ah lokal in Champhai refugee camp a awm te leh hmun dang remchang apianga mi te rawn hruai khawmin CNF chu an ti lian ta zel ani._
