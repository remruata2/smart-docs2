Security scenario in Barak Valley (Assam) and Mizoram, with special reference to insurgency – Note on activities of HPC-D,counter measures and successful operations made by Mizoram Police during the current year i.e 2015

 

 

Introduction:

The Barak Valley consists of 3(three) southern districts of Assam namely-Cachar, Karimganj and Hailakandi. All the three districts share state border with Mizoram. The area is by and large peaceful. However, movement of the HPC-D outfit especially along Mizoram and Cachar district border had been reported in recent times.

Hmar Peoples Convention (Democratic) comprising pre-dominantly the Hmar community living in the North Eastern part of Mizoram and Churachandpur District of Manipur has been demanding Autonomous District Council for Hmar community since 20 (twenty) years back and took up arms to wage war against the State of Mizoram and indulged in various criminal activities within Mizoram.

9.2.2015

 

       On 9.2.2015@6:00pm, 7(seven) cadres of HPC(D) armed with sophisticated weapons AK-47, INSAS rifles etc. infiltrated into Khawpuar village, Aizawl District, Mizoram. The HPC(D) cadres demanded Rs.50,000/- to Village Council leaders, Rs.20,000/- to retailer and 5% of one month's salary to school teachers. Accordingly, Village Council leaders gave Rs.7,000/- to HPC(D) cadres.

     The HPC(D) cadres stayed for a while in the village and they also threatened the village by opening blank fire(about 22 shots). In this connection Sakawrdai PS C/No.3/15 dt.11.2.15 u/s 384/386 IPC had been registered.

 

 

5.3.2015

     

      A group of armed HPC(D) outfit numbering about 15(fifteen) fired upon the 2nd IR Battalion Border Outpost  at Khawlian village, Aizawl District, Mizoram. There was no casualty or injuries. The 2nd IR Bn. personnel also quickly returned fire and there was an exchange of around 100 rounds of fire and the firing lasted for about 10 (ten) minutes. Immediately, the HPC(D) cadres withdrawn in a Pik-up Truck towards Parvachom, Manipur.

        In this connection, Saitual Police Station Case No.7/2015 dt.6.3.2015 u/s 121 A/427 IPC r/w 25(1A)/ 27(2) Arms Act had been registered.

 

 

28.3.2015

   

     On dt. 28.3.2015 @ 8:50 am, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance members such as, RL Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Pi. Vanlalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) insurgent group numbering around 12 (twelve) cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village near Mizoram - Manipur border. 3 (three) Mizoram police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while 5 (five) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about 1 (one) hour and 45 (forty five) minutes .

     The HPC(D) outfit snatched away 4 (four) nos. of  9mm pistol, 2 (two) nos. of .38 revolver, 2 (two) nos. of AK-47 rifle and 8 (eight) nos. of its magazines with 244 (two hundred and forty four) live rounds from the place of occurrence.

8.5.2015

Mizoram- Manipur Border area at Tiaulian

 

 

     On the night of 7.5.2015, the Operational Party of Mizoram  which was patrolling the area of Mizoram bordering Manipur received specific information that the main accused persons  wanted in Sakawrdai PS Case No.6/15 dated 29/3/2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120(B)/326 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act were hiding in Tiaulian village of Manipur, just across the border.

     The information was very specific and the accused would have slipped away, therefore, Operation Team decided to verify the information and approached Tiaulian village early on the morning of 8.5.2015 at the break of dawn. On seeing the approaching Police party, some persons started running and were asked to stop. Instead of stopping, these persons started unprovoked firing on the Police party who had no option but to return fire in self-defense.

       One of the attackers was hit by the defensive fire while others managed to escape.The attacker, who was hit, succumbed to his injuries and was identified as deserter Constable H.C. Malsawmkima of Mizoram Police who had deserted along with two service weapons and ammunitions of Mizoram Police on 11.7.2014 and joined HPC(D).      

 

       The deceased had also been identified as one of the main accused in the ambush of 28.3.2015.
