Services

SERVICES

Asteria offers services using its UAS platform for the following applications:

  • Wild life preservation
  • Aerial imagery / videos for real estate, film and media
  • Sports events videography
  • Boundary mapping of states / forests / property
  • Traffic monitoring
  • Event security
  • Disaster management support

Asteria performs this work on a deliverable based model and works to ensure customer satisfaction in the ultimate product.

 

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

Asteria’s focus is on innovation based growth. As an extension of this focus, Asteria undertakes development of research ideas as turnkey projects with external research institutions. Asteria has collaborated with CSIR-NAL, Bangalore for UAV related research projects such as:

  • Integration of autopilot systems in micro UAVs
  • Development of onboard instruments for UAVs
  • Development of a mobile ground control station for UAVs
