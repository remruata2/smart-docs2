EXAMINATION OF CREDENTIALS OF V.MALSAWMDAWNGLIANA, H/NO.E-58, CHANMARI, V.NEIHKUNGA BUILDING

 

Mr. V Malsawmdawngliana is a reputed businessman who runs the Modicare Service centre, located at Zarkawt. He is also dealer of Dalmia Cement Company and distributor of Nokia phones. His Modicare service centre is the main office in Aizawl where new products are introduced and conferences were held for various consultants under Modicare Company. He, being an experienced businessman, was introduced to Modicare by his friend from Nagaland who is  a Nokia distributor there in 2012. Since then, he has been running the Modicare service centre as well as warehouse for stocking all products of Modicare at their office at Zarkawt. But from 2018, due to the massive increase in demand of the company's product in Mizoram it became difficult to run both the service centre and the warehouse in one location. As a result, the company decided to look for another partner to look after stocking of product and running a warehouse in Aizawl.

Therefore, in 2018, the company made an agreement with TVS Logistics, which is a third party logistics company, to locate a suitable warehouse and hire manpower to take up stocking of products. From June 2019, Mr. V Malsawmdawngliana was relieved from his previous responsibility of stocking the product. He runs Modicare service centre alone at his store at Zarkawt.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

EXAMINATION OF CREDENTIALS TVS LOGISTICS SERVICES 1ST FLOOR, CHALTLANG, SAIRANG ROAD, OPPOSITE ZODIN PLASTIC INDUSTRY

 

            Mr. Lalremsiama Pachuau is the Operational Manager under TVS Logistics Service Limited with their office running at the building of Mr. Rinsanga, IAS Retd, Chandmari. TVS Logistics was formerly known as Drive India Enterprises under TATA Company. It was sold by TATA Company to TVS Logistics, and its name was changed to TVS Logistics Services, which was further named as TVS Supply Chain Solution Ltd. It is a third party company operating in Aizawl. The company has working partnership not only with Modicare but also with Avon, Oriflame and Wonderchef.

            Negotiations between Modicare and TVS Logistics began in 2018 when the volume for demand of Modicare product increased significantly in Mizoram. Since then, Mr. Lalremsiama was intimated by the company to allocate a suitable warehouse in Aizawl which would meet the requirement, which was found at Chatlang, Sairang Road opposite to Zodin Plastic Industry. This building is utilised as the main warehouse for stocking all  Modicare products coming to Aizawl, since June,2019.

            Presently there are around 32 Modicare dealer points across Mizoram. The owners of these dealers placed their order through online and the products are delivered from the warehouse by TVS Logistics to their respective locations. Payments for these orders are also made online. These dealers in Aizawl make their order online and either collects the products from the warehouse by themselves or get it delivered.

It is understood that TVS Logistics are only concerned with receiving and stocking of Modicare products in the warehouse and sending the products to various dealer points as per the order they received.
