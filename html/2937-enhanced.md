****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/559

Dated: 23rd July, 2019.

 Yesterday, i.e. 22.07.2019 @ 12:00 noon, Ngopa PS staff of Champhai District intercepted 3 (three) Myanmarese along with 2 (two) foreign bikes (125 Kenbo) at Kawlbem Road (about 15 kms from Ngopa). One of the bike riders was found to be in possession of 1 (one) pistol.
 The persons were identified as Myanmarese Army personnel who claimed to be in pursuit of 2 (two) Myanmarese Army deserters. The apprehended persons are – i) Capt. Thant Khant Aung, ii) Rifleman Sai Ko Linn and iii) Rifleman Nangliankap belonging to 228 Bn. LIR of Myanmar Army, Khawmawi/Rih Post. They were learnt to have entered into Mizoram by crossing the border at Tuisa river and thereafter travelled via Vaikhawtlang to Kawlbem. It was also learnt that one Godeihpianga (41 yrs.) S/o Nengkhankap of Tuimui, Myanmar was brought along with them as a guide.

 The 3 (three) persons were taken to Khawzawl PS by SDPO, Khawzawl and party this morning, i.e. 23.7.2019 @ 8:00 a.m and necessary legal action is being taken under the supervision of SP, Champhai. The persons are presently detained at Khawzawl PS and a joint interrogation is being done by all stakeholders.
