A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 4.6.2013

Langkaih Bridge opened: Kanhmun-Damcherra Bridge which had been closed for a month was opened yesterday at 04:25PM by Langkaih Group YMA as a result of the Peace Committee held at Kanhmun on 3.6.2013.

            Peace Committee was held yesterday afternoon and the apology made by Damcherra Bazar Committee to Langkaih Group YMA and its request to open the bridge was considered and consent was given to their request.

            As decided in the meeting, if bandh is to be organised in the future, the markets will be opened or closed depending on the Joint decision made by the Damcherra Bazar Committee and Langkaih Group YMA.
