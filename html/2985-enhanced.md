****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/200

Dated: 20th March, 2019.

 Information received indicated that Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India vide F.No.7/32/97-NE dt. 13.3.2019, has extended the deadline for repatriation of Bru refugees in Tripura Transit Camps to Mizoram which will be ended on 31.3.2019 till 30.9.2019 and the assistance to them in the form of food grains and cash doles will also be extended till 30.9.2019.

 In this connection, R. Laldawngliana, Vice President, Mizoram Bru Displaced People’s Forum (MBDPF) convened a public meeting today, i.e. 20.3.2019 @ 7:00 a.m at Hazachera Transit Camp and apprised the Central Government decision to the public and urged them to wait for the decision of the Government and the MBDPF for the future course of repatriation process. R. Laldawngliana further informed the public that Joint Monitoring Group (JMG), i.e. representatives from Central Government, Mizoram Government, Tripura Government, MBDPF and NGOs, is scheduled to hold a meeting on 26.3.2019 at New Delhi and further urged the public to wait for the outcome of the meeting.

 Information received indicated that the following leaders of MBDPF will proceed to New Delhi on 25.3.2019 to attend the said JMG meeting :-

1. A. Sawibunga, President
2. R. Laldawngliana, Vice President
3. Bruno Msha, General Secretary
