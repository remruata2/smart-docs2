****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/754

Dated 29th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/750 Dt. 28.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-4, LMV-Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 29.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 3 (three) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 3, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

1 (one) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 1, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 29.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 4 (four) vehicles (HMV-1, LMV-Nil, MMV–3) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 52 (fifty two) vehicles (HMV-20, MMV – 1, LMV – 23, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 29.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 29.12.2020.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 196 (one hundred ninety six) vehicles (HMV – 68, MMV – 44, LMV –26, LPG – 12, Maxi Cab – 11, Oil Tanker – 32, Bus - 3) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 29.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
