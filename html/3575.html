A. Front page of  THE AIZAWL POST  newspaper  Dt. 17 &18.7.2022

CYMA AS MEDIATOR BETWEEN HNMEU AND STATE GOVERNMENT: Leaders of Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) and NHM Employees Union (NHMEU) held a discussion on the ongoing strike organised by NHMEU yesterday i.e. 17.7.2022. After this discussion, CYMA leaders again met Dy. Chief Minister, Pu Tawnluia and Health Minister, Dr. R.Lalthangliana where they conveyed the demands and objectives of NHMEU.

                   Government of Mizoram had issued a show cause notice to members of NHMEU, last saturday, informing them of their violation of Mizoram Essential Service Maintenance Act due to their ongoing strike and to justify themselves within three days why disciplinary action should not be taken against them.

                   Till the evening of last Sunday, it is reported that NHMEU had not clarified themselves against the show cause notice served to them.
