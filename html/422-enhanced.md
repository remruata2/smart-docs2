****Major incidents of crimes committed by HPC(D) since its formation in 1996****

**1. Ambush of 1st IR Bn.**:

  On 10.2.97 combined group of HPC-D and NSCN(I/M) laid ambush on 1st IR Bn. personnel near Chawnpui village in which 4 Constables of IR Bn. personnel of Mizoram were killed and also snatched away 4 nos. of SLRs, 12 nos. of Magazines.

 In this connection, Vairengte P.S. C/No. 10/97 dt. 10.2.97 u/s 121/121/A/302/307/392/34 IPC r/w 27/25(I-B) (A) Arms Act has been registered.

**2. Abduction of NEEPCO officials**:

 On 31.3.2000 at about 0500 hrs. the following 6 (six) officials of NEEPCO were abducted by about 10 (ten) armed HPC(D) militants from the Dam site of Tuirial.

 (1) Mr. Sitant Shu Dhar, SDO P/A Ram Krishna Nagar, Karimganj.

 (2) Mr. Ratan Roy, Driller P/A Umrangshu, N.C. Hills, Assam.

 (3) Mr. Nipendra Nath, Driller P/A Patharkandi, Karimganj Dist. Assam.

 (4) Mr. Robert Lalsanga, J.E. (Civil) s/o S.Thansanga of Saipum.

 (5) Mr. Lalrinkima, J.E. s/o Dengthuama, Hnahthial.

 (6) Mr. Premananda Mandal, Khalasi P/A West Bengal.

 In this connection Vairengte PS Case No. 16/2000 dated 31.3.2000 U/S 365 IPC r/w 25(I-B) (A) Arms Act has been registered.

 They were released on 14.6.2000, abducted by HPC-D.

**3. Abduction of NEEPCO employees**:

 On 25.10.2000 in between 9 AM to 10 AM, 11 (eleven) persons (4 Mizos and 7 non-Mizos) employees of Pu Lalsangliana Sailo, Contractor working for NEEPCO at Tuirial Dam Site were abducted by HPC-D militants at about 7 KMs away from Saipum towards Tuirial Dam Site.

 1) Saikapthanga (51) s/o Pukaia of Vaivakawn - Manager.

 2) Lalramchhana (17) s/o Biakchungnunga, Bukpui North.

 3) Lalhmachhuana (21) s/o K. Lalhmingthanga, Edenthar, Aizawl.

 4) Vanlalhriata (22) s/o R. Roberta (L), Dinthar-II

 5) M. Kalitha - Bulldozer Operator.

 6) Gopal -  -do-

 7) Marak

 8) Basil Uddin - Carpenter

 9) Lakhiram - Dumka Labour

 10) Soma Marinda - Dumka Labour

 11) Gopal Roy - Dumka Labour

 The HPC-D militants later released 10 persons and detained Saikapthanga, Manager of Pu Lalsangliana Sailo in captivity. The motive behind the incident appeared to be due to non-payment of ransom promise made by NEEPCO authorities to HPC-D during the earlier abduction on 31.3.2000. Vairengte PS C/No. 71/2K dt. 26.10.2000 u/s 365/427/435/34 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(a) A. Act.

 Pu Saikapthanga was later released safely on 23.11.2000 after 29 days of captivity.

**4. Abduction of PWD Engineers and Power Grid Corporation officials**:

 On 11.4.2001, 6 (six) armed militant of HPC-D under the command of SS Army Chief Biaknunga entered into Mizoram by crossing Tuivai river at Tipaimukh and kidnapping 7 (seven) persons working for Power Grid Corporation and taken to Manipur side.

 1. M.N. Bora, Senior Engineer.

 2. R.K. Nath, Sr. Technician

 3. S.K. Nath, Technician

 4. K. Daimary, Attendant

 5. Rintluanga, Overseer-II, PWD

 6. Rochungnunga, Driver PWD

 7. R. Zodinliana, Manager of Contrator of Pu Lalthansanga.

 Out of the 7 persons Sl. No. 3,4 &amp; 7 were released on the same day and the rest were released on 23.6.2001.
