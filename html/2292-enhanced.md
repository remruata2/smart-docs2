****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/119

Dated 19th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/117 Dt. 18.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-10, MMV-8, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 19.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

36 (thirty six) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-24, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 19.02.2021 @ 3:30pm. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Bairabi Joint VC in their Notice issued today i.e., 19.2.2021 stated that Bairabi Joint VC sitting held today urged owners of paddy field at Zophai disputed area not to engage Non-Mizo as caretaker of their paddy fields as per Kolasib DC order. It also informed paddy field owners not to re-engage their Non-Mizo caretakers of paddy field.

59 (fifty nine) vehicles (HMV-41, MMV-3, LMV-14, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-1) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 19.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

128 (one hundred twenty eight) vehicles (HMV-53, MMV-42, LMV-15, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-12, Oil Tanker-2, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 19.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
