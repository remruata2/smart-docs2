**ACHIEVEMENTS OF CID (SB) wef****23rd April, 2022 to 29th November, 2022**

23.6.2022 :****CID (SB) under Mizoram Police and BSF conducted Joint Operation on  23.6.2022 and seized 595 (five hundred and ninety five) numbers of 7.62x 51mm ammunitions at Zemabawk East, Aizawl from  the illegal possession of Joseph Zarzothanga (28) s/o Malsawmthara of  Zemabawk East and Vanlalhruaia (40) S/o Chalhnuna of Bungkawn  Vengthar. The seized ammunitions were worth Rs.2.3 lakhs in the local market. The arrested accused and seized articles were handed over to  Bawngkawn PS for taking further necessary legal action. Information received stated that the seized items were allegedly owned by Lalhmingthanga Sinate, Founder President HPC-D and the same are to be delivered to People's Defence Force (PDF), Myanmar resistance group. Bawngkawn P/S case No.438/22 Dt.23.6.2022 u/s 25(1A)/  25(1AA) Arms Act has been registered.
