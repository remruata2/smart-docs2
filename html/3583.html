A. Front page of  VANGLAINI/ THE AIZAWL POST  newspapers 

Dt. 28.7.2022

NGT issued a notice to Central and State Government: Due to the alleged violation of Environmental Laws in the construction of Kaladan Multi-Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP), the highways that will connect Myanmar with India through Mizoram, the National Green Tribunal  (NGT) issued a notice to both the Central and Mizoram Government.

          Center for Environmental Protection (CEP), an organisation for environmental protection in Mizoram, lodged a complaint against the KMMTTP construction of highways to the NGT and accused them of violating various Environmental laws such as  Forest Conservation Act 1980, Environment (Protection) Act 1986, Biological Diversity Act, Water (Prevention and control of Pollution) Act 1974 and not obtaining prior clearance from the Committee of National Board of Wild Life.

         

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt:  28.7.2022

Blood donation: To mark the 50th anniversary of their establishment,  Mizoram Fire and Emergency Service (FES) department organised voluntary blood donation programme yesterday i.e 27.7.2022. A total of 30 units of blood was donated for Civil Hospital, Aizawl by 29 male and 1 female staff of FES which was conducted at FES Directorate, Aizawl.
