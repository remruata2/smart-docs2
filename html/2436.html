MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT
No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/452
Dated 12th June, 2021
In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/451
Dt. 11.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:
No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four)
hours and situation is normal.
2. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:
Chuhvel Joint NGOs Meeting was held today i.e., 12.6.2021 @ 7:00
a.m. at Community Hall, Chuhvel. Pu Lalthlanawma, President, Chuhvel
Branch YMA presided the meeting and 28 (twenty eight) members from VC,
YMA, MHIP and MUP attended the meeting. The meeting resolved the
following in connection with the recent tension along Assam-Mizoram border:
i) The State Government should immediately set up BOP at Chuhvel.
ii) The government should protect all land owned by residents of
Chuhvel and Saikhawthlir village within inner line reserved area.
iii)To conduct mass community work for verification of inter-state
boundary border in between Chuhvel, Mamit District and Gutguti, Assam on
14.6.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. and to invite DC, Mamit and SDO(C), Kawrthah to
attend the said community work.
3. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

Bridge.

no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
