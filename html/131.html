INSURGENCY RELATED INCIDENTS

 

1. On 5.3.2007 at Tinghmun village, 6(six) dead bodies believed to be HNA cadre were discovered presumably held hostage by the HPC-D and brutally murdered them at the outskirt of Tinghmun village. Hence SKDI PS C/No. 2/07 dt.5.3.07 u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act has been registered.

 

2. On the evening of 9.3.2007, one NSCN(IM) deserter was killed and buried at the outskirt of Rajiv Nagar(Mizoram) suspected to be murdered by NSCN(IM). Hence WPLG PS C/No.11/07 dt.12.3.07 u/s 302/201/34 IPC r/w 5 ES Act has been registered.

 

3. On 7.2.2008, one Jamil Uddin Lasker was abducted by ULFBV from Saihapui. In this connection, KLB PS C/No. 13/08 dt.7.2.08 u/s 365 IPC r/w 25(2A) Arms Act was registered.

 

4. On 26.2.2008, one Thangcha Kipgen, member of KLA was found dead in front of MNF General Hqrs, Aizawl. Hence Aizawl PS C/No. 63/08 dt.26.2.08 u/s 302 IPC was registered.

 

5. On 19.3.2008, an armed group named ULFB visited 3 jhum hut near Chuhvel village of Mamit Dist. These armed militants extorted money, domestic fowls and 3 bags of chillies. Hence, Kanhmun PS C/No.8/08 dt. 21.3.08 u/s 386 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act has been rergistered.

 

6. On 30.3.2008, 5 unidentified persons covering their faces with clothes extorted Rs.5300/- from Jamir Uddin Barbhuiya at Sihdarh Zau, Pangbalkawn in Kolasib Dist. These unidentified persons left a demand letter in the name of UDLF. In this connection Kolasib PS C/No.28/08 dt. 1.4.08 u/s 395 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act has been registered.

 

7. On 26.4.08, Lalhmunsiami w/o Ramthanga of Vaitin and Dinga of Durtlang were kidnapped by HPC-D but on the same day, these two persons were released, case was not registered in this regard.

 

8. On 21.6.2008, 7 armed Hmar militants visited the house of Pu K.Lalmalsawma at Saiphai and burnt down several properties and they took him from his house and hit him on his left forehead and released him as lots of blood oozed out from his head. In this connection, VRTE PS C/No.26/08 dt.21.6.08 u/s 121/365/436/451/323/34 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) has been registered.

 

9. On 2.9.2008, 1st IR Bn. Pay Escort Party was ambushed by HPC-D at Saipum village in Kolasib Dist. in which one Sub-Inspector and three Constables were killed. In this connection, VRTE PS C/No.42/08 dt.3.9.08 u/s 121/302/326/427/34 r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act was registered.

 

10. On 4.10.2008, one Kishore Rangia Chakma, Headmaster M/S Silsuri was kidnapped by an armed group named Santi Bahini from Lokisuri Bazar. Hence, Marpara PS C/No.18/08 dt.5.10.08 u/s 364A/34 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act was registered.

 

11. On 13.11.2009, one Zarzokima of Bungthuam was shot dead by Bru armed gang. The culprit left a note in which they claimed themselves as Bru Revolutionary Union. In this connection, Kanhmun PS C/No.23/09 dt.13.11.09 u/s 302 r/w 25(1B)(a)/27(1) Arms Act has been registered.

 

12. On 28.1.2010, one Monir Uddin was abducted by unknown armed militants numbering about 12 persons. Hence BBI PS C/No.2/10 dt.29.1.10 u/s 365 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act has been registered.

 

13. On 6.6.2011, two ABCI officials were kidnapped by UDLA from their camp and in this connection Bairabi PS C/No.7/11 dt.6.6.11 u/s 365/386/395/34 IPC r/w 25(1B)(b) Arms Act was registered.
