**29.4.2022 :** (a) ZPM General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 29.4.2022 stated that Pu Lalduhoma, ZPM Leader visited Khopai village yesterday evening and Laki, Theiri, Ahmypi villages today in view of the upcoming MADC Election and organized Public meetings in such villages. Delivering a speech at such public meetings, Pu Lalduhoma stated that around 60 percent of Mizoram population depend on Agriculture &amp; Allied sector, and ZPM will prioritise the said sector and necessary actions will be taken for self dependent of essential food items, if ZPM is in power. He also stated that there are various potential agricultural lands under MADC and they intend to make use of the same to be the rice bowl of Mizoram. It also stated that Pu Lalduhoma, Leader ZPM inducted new members during his tour and he was accompanied by leaders of Siaha District ZPM and ZPM General Headquarters.

(b) ZPM General Headquarters in another Press Release issued on 29.4.2022 stated ZPM is undertaking extensive electoral campaign in view of the upcoming MADC Election and in this connection, Pu Vanlalhlana, MLA Aizawl North-I inaugurated Campaign Office of Pu NT Lalringliana, Siaha South-I ZPM official candidate. Delivering a speech at the function, Pu K. Sapdanga, ZPM Working President stated that neutral voters are eagerly waiting the next General Election and alleged the ruling government of hesitating to face the next General Election. He also stated that the youths desire a new system and are not satisfied with the existing system. He further appealed the general public to cast their vote in favour of ZPM candidates. It also stated that Pu J Kharchhina President, ZPM Siaha district presided the function and Pu NT Lalringliana, ZPM Official Candidate also delivered a speech.

(c) ZPM Thalai Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 29.4.2022 stated that Pu Malsawmzuala Ralte, President ZPM Thalai &amp; AMC Councillor inaugurated ZPM Campaign Office at Thosai village yesterday i.e., 28.4.2022. Delivering a speech at the function, Pu Malsawmzuala Ralte stated that we should appreciate achievement of development through hard work as the same is reliable and consistent. He also stated that he has joined ZPM as he believed that ZPM could bring development to the people of Mizoram. He further stated that it is high time to neglect candidates who only aim to achieve self oriented goals over all round development of the state and people, and appealed residents of Siaha District to cast their votes in favour of ZPM candidates.
