A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 11.2.2014

Pseudoephedrine seized: Aizawl SP’s District Special Branch (DSB) seized 15 lakh tablets of Pseudoephedrine worth ` 60 lakh from the house of Lalrintluanga of Thakthing Veng yesterday at around 1:30pm. The alleged owner Lalrintluanga was arrested along with Anthony Michael of Manipur, who was inside the house while seizing Pseudoephedrine.    

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 11.2.2014

Masked men robbed a woman at Khawhai: Lalthanmawii w/o Zuimawia of Khawhai North was robbed by two masked men on Sunday night during church service and snatched ` 10,000/- of their family money and another ` 9,000/- the YMA money they kept. These two men took her out from the house and detained her. She was later released at around 9:00pm the same night. A case is registered at Khawzawl Police Station regarding this crime and the offenders are being searched by the Police.
