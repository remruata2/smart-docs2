**Subj: **Renewal permission for engagement of 15 (fifteen) nos. of Special Police Officers.****

Sir,

With reference to the above subject, it is to state that this Unit is currently engaging 15 (fifteen) nos. of Special Police Officers on MR basis (with DA) in 3 (three) neighboring States and 2 (two) Countries as shown under:

1. Bangladesh  – 3 SPOs
2. Myanmar  – 3 SPOs
3. Tripura  – 2 SPOs
4. Assam  – 4 SPOs
5. Manipur  – 3 SPOs

It may be pointed out that in this regard CID(SB) has been dealing with numerous insurgent groups operating at neighboring States and Countries andengages various sources and feelers in various insurgent infested areas. Considering the dangers involve and unfortunate past incidences of loss of lives of sources/feelers, regular pay cheque in the form of SPO to reliable sources/feelers has help a long way in motivating and inducing volunteers to assist Mizoram Police in collection of real-time and actionable intelligence inputs.

It is also pertinent to mention here that the above SPOs are absolutely crucial and plays vital role in our endeavor to ensure that Mizoram continue to be an insurgent-free State and to contain the spill over affects and unwanted activities and influence of numerous insurgent groups operating across the borders. SPOs have proved their mettle and worth over the years in collection of real-time and actionable intelligence input pertaining to such insurgent groups.Therefore, strengthening of intelligence network across the State’s border by way of appointment of trustworthy person from the concern locality of the area as SPO is crucial for maintaining the hard-earned peace of the State.

In view of the above, it is therefore, prayed that necessary steps may be taken to obtain renewal permission for engagement of 15 (fifteen) nos. of Special Police Officers on MR Basis for another period of 1 (one) year, i.e.,1.3.2022- 28.2.2023 at the following rate:

1. Daily Allowances  - Rs.240 per day.
2. Ration Allowances - Rs.550 per day.
