**MYANMAR REFUGEE**

22.01.2022 :  Three wounded cadres of People's Defence Force (PDF) who   were admitted at Trinity Hospital, Aizawl were discharged on  22.1.2022 and are presently staying at Zuangtui. Meanwhile, another  cadre,  Ko.Kywa Kyi @ Kaw Chaw Chi, who suffered bullet injuries in  his arm was admitted on the same day.

  ( People's Defence Force (PDF) hliam pathum(3) Trinity hosipal a awm  te pawh 22.2.2022 khan discharged an ni a, Zuangtui ah an awm mek a,  midang pakhat(1) Ko.Kywa Kyi @ Kaw Chaw Chi, chu Silai in a ban ah  kah a ni a, admit leh a ni thung.)

28.1.2022 :  People Defense Force (PDF) launched an ambushed against around  30 (thirty) Myanmar Army personnel (who are  stationed at Rihkhawdar  Camp), who  proceeded to Khawmawi (Tiau)  Khawmawi  (Tiau) and  Haimual villages, Chin state of Myanmar. It was followed by exchanged  of fire between the two parties which lasted for  about half an  hour and  subsequently the Myanmar Army retreated to their camp at Rihkhawdar.  Input received also stated that as a result of the  above incident, 1 (one)  Myanmar Army personal suffered bullet injury and  no casualty was   reported from PDF side. In this connection, PDF  alleged Zomi  Revolutionary Army (ZRA) of assisting the Myanmar Army.

  Following the above incident, around 2250 (two thousand two   hundred and fifty) residents of Khawmawi village, which is situated near  Tiau river (Indo-Myanmar boundary river) and its surrounding villages,  Chin State, Myanmar are taking refuge at Zokhawthar and Melbuk  villages, Champhai District (Zokhawthar - 2,000 and Melbuk - 250),  Mizoram. Leaders of Zokhawthar village and Melbuk village   accommodated the above  mentioned refugees at available Community  Halls, Schools building, Godowns etc. Around 200 (two hundred)  Myanmar Refugees are accommodated at temporary shelter at the bank of  Tiau river, Indian side. Further, influx of refugees is continuing in the  above mentioned two villages till date.

  Recapping earlier development, Myanmar Army, who are stationed  at Rihkhawdar Camp exploded bombs four times on the evening of Dt.  25.1.2022 @ around 8:00 p.m. and in fear of this, around 400 (four  hundred) residents of Rihkhawdar and Khawmawi, Myanmar rushed to  Zokhawthar village, Champhai District the same night for refuge.

31.01.2022: A Consultation Meeting was held on 31.1.2022 @ 12:30 p.m. under the  guidance of Young Mizo Association, Sub-Headquarters Lunglei in  connection with matters related to receiving of Myanmar Refugees. Pu  Lalrotluanga, President, Sub-Headquarters YMA Lunglei presided the  meeting and representatives from various Headquarters Churches and  Political Parties within Lunglei District attended. The meeting resolved  the following :-

 (i) All churches should not induct Myanmar Refugees as permanent   members in their respective churches. However, they may be  allowed to induct as temporary member.

  (ii) It urged all churches not to issue Baptism and Birth Certificate to   Myanmar Refugees.

  (iii) It urged all political parties not to take political advantage over  the Myanmar refugees.
