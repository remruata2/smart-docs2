****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/734

Dated 17th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/721 Dt. 16.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister visited Thinghlun today i.e., 17.12.2020 @ 12:30 to take stock of the situation and handed over gifts to Mizoram Police and BSF personnel on duty at the area. Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister also visited Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 17.12.2020 @ 1:30 p.m. to take stock of the situation and he left the area at 3:00 p.m. today’s afternoon.

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-5, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 17.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 9, LMV - 1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 8 (eight) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 17.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 4 (four) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 4) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

51(fifty one) vehicles (HMV-25, MMV – 1, LMV – 21, Maxi Cab- 4) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 17.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 210 (two hundred and ten) vehicles (HMV – 83, MMV – 50, LMV –37, LPG – 10, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 24, Bus - 2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 17.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV – 1, MMV – 11, LMV-20) entered Saiphai from Assam today i.e., 17.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
