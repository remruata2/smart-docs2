SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/45

Dated: 19th January, 2022

 

Champhai District NGO Coordination Committee (NGOCC) held a meeting in connection with their demand for cancellation of the Transfer Order of Pi Maria CT Zuali, DC, Champhai today i.e., 19.1.2022 @11:00 a.m. at Sub-Headquarters YMA Office, Tlangrawt, Champhai. Pu Lalnunmawia, President Champhai District NGOCC presided the meeting and 26 (twenty six) members from Champhai Sub-Headquarters YMA, Mizo Hmeichhe Insuihkhawm Pawl (MHIP), Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP), Champhai Thalai Pawl, Mizoram Upa Pawl (MUP), Mizo Zaimi Insuihkhawm (MZI), Champhai Nature Conservancy, Champhai Dawrkai Association and Mizoram Journalist Association (MJA) attended.  The meeting after careful deliberation decided the following as their future course of action as their demand for cancellation of the Transfer Order of the above mentioned officer is not fulfilled within the stipulated time:

  1. All shops within Champhai town should be closed on 21.1.2022.
  2. Mass procession will be organized on 24.1.2022.
  3. Picketing of all offices within Champhai on 25.1.2022
  4. To organize Total Bandh within Champhai Town and Boycott of Republic Day 2022 Celebration at Champhai on 26.1.2022.

 

In connection with their demand, the meeting also urged MsLA within Champhai District to extend necessary support. They also decided to take extreme measures, if their demand is not met even after taking the above intended actions.

 

          Recapping earlier development, Champhai District NGO Emergency Meeting held on 14.1.2022 gave ultimatum to the state government to cancel the transfer of the above stated officer on or before 18.1.2022.
