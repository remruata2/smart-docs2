9.5.2022 : Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) Budget Session was held on  9.5.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at CADC Session Hall, Kamalanagar. Shri. Buddha Lila Chakma, Chairman CADC presided the session and 16 (sixteen) members were present. During the session, Pu Kali Kumar Tongchangya, MDC moved a No Confidence Motion against Pu Rosik Mohan Chakma, Chief Executive Member (CEM), CADC, and after discussing the matter at length, Pu Rosik Mohan Chakma was removed from the post of CEM, CADC. The Chairman adjourned the session sine die in the afternoon @ 2:30 p.m.

            It may be mentioned that, 13 (thirteen) MsDC submitted a written petition to
Pu Rosik Mohan Chakma, CEO, CADC on 8.5.2022 wherein they urged him to tender resignation from the post of CEM, CADC.

7.6.2022 : BJP Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) in its Press Release issued on 7.6.2022 highlighted that the District Executive Meeting of BJP Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) held today at Kamalanagar resolved to form the government in the forthcoming polls in CADC. It stated an important resolution adopted in the meeting condemned the Mizoram government led by Pu Zoramthanga for the failure to fulfil the poll promises made during the last assembly polls.

9.6.2022 :      Pu Buddha Lila Chakma, MDC from Vaseitlang Constituency was sworn in as the new Chief Executive Officer (CEM) of Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) in a function held on 9.6.2022 @ 3:00 p.m. at the courtyard of CADC Rest House, Kalamanagar. The function was presided by Pronit Bikash Chakma, Executive Secretary, CADC and Pu Amol Srivastava, DC Lawngtlai administered oath of office to the new CEM. The function was attended by around 100 (one hundred) persons.

1.7.2022 :      Budget Session of Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) commenced on 1.7.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Session Hall, CADC, Kamalanagar. Pu Kali Kumar Tongchangya, Chairman CADC presided the session and 20 (twenty) members attended. During the session, Pu Buddha Lila Chakma, CEM CADC presented CADC Budget for the year 2022-2023 amounting to Rs.11,342.56 lakhs before the session. The chairman adjourned the session for the day @ 11:30 a.m. and it will be resumed on 5.7.2022.

5.7.2022 :      Budget Session of Chakma Autonomous District Council (CADC) was continued on 5.7.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Session Hall, CADC, Kamalanagar. Pu Kali Kumar Tongchangya, Chairman CADC presided the session and 22 (twenty two) members attended. During the session, CADC Budget for the year 2022-2023 amounting to Rs.16,475.33 lakhs was passed. The chairman adjourned the session sine die @ 12:00 Noon.

10.11.2022 :  The Winter Session of the 10th Chakma Autonomous District Council was commenced on 10.11.2022 @10:30 a.m. at CADC Session Hall, Kamalanagar. Pu Kali Kumar Tongchangya, Chairman CADC presided the session and 22 (twenty two) members attended. During the session, the Chairman conducted floor test in connection with resignation tendered by Buddha Lila Chakma, present CEM, CADC due to his inability to obtaine majority support and the same was approved by the session. Thereafter, the session was adjourned sine dine the same day @10:40 a.m.
