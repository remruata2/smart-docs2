INTERROGATION STATEMENT  OF Henry@

Chondro Kishore Jamatia@ Malsawma

 

 

                        I was born and brought up at Teliamura in South Tripura. In the month of June/July 2006 I had approach DHD (Nunisia Faction) at their camp in Haflong, NC Hills and stayed there for about a month and returned home as they were reluctant to accept/enroll me. But when I went there again after a month, I was accepted.

                        In 2007, I alongwith Vanlalfela, Muikau and 14 Bru youths had undergone training under the DHD at Harangazau, NC Hills for 3 months. After the training we sent those youths back home. We had given Rs.30,000/- to the DHD as training fee/costs.

                        While I was at the DHD camp, I came to know that some dissidents of Zomi Revolutionary Army (ZRA) and a Bru gang led by Donoram operating in the inter-state boundaries of Assam -Mizoram-Tripura had joined hands. But Donoram was killed soon after the merger by someone amongst the group itself. After that incident the group were operating for a while calling themselves 'The United Liberation Front of Barak Valley (ULFB). The outfit had surrendered to the Assam Govt. in 2008. However, the Assam Govt. they felt, was not keeping its promises and, therefore, regrouped in which majority of them Brus. When I had learnt that Raj Muni was among them, I too had joined the group.

                        Raj Muni started recruitment of Bru youths from December 2008 and succeeded in enrolling about 14 youths and let them to ZRA camp in Manipur where they had training during the early part of 2009. We have no permanent camp of our own.

                        Raj stationed himself in Assam while Muikau and some cadres armed with sophisticated weapons entered Tripura. I had also joined them at Kalapahar, Tripura in November 2009. We entered Kaiskau and Khallchangpara (both refugee camps) area in January 2010. I handed over charge of the group to Thomas on 10.1.2010 who was sent by Raj. I took rest till last part of February 2010 at Dosda where my parents were staying.

                        Presently, our outfit has no formal/official name. We had procured arms mostly from ZRA but I do not know how the deal was made as the same was done solely by Raj himself. Our arms holding at present is - 1) AK- 47 Rifle   -2 nos. with 150-200 ammns. 2) SLR   -1no. 3) Stangun     -1no. 4) M-16 Rifle   -1no. 5) G-4    -1no. 6) G-2   -1no. 7) .22 Miniature Rifle   -1no. 8) Lethod   -1no.

                        Our outfit has never been against the repatriation process but rather support it because our people were living in poverty for a long period of time and backward especially in education.

                        On 12.5.2010 while on our way to Assam to bring back Muikau and others to Tripura, we were arrested at Kanhmun by Mizoram Police.
