Sir,

  As desired, I am furnishing herewith the following report in connection with alleged recent activities and movements of Arakan Army (AA) as under :

i) Information received indicated that Arakan Army (AA) deserted all their camps along Indo-Myanmar border in the late 2019 and later they have shifted their camps to Bandarban Area, Bangladesh.

ii)  Information received also indicated that leaders of Arakan Army deter entering or moving near India territory by all the Arakan Army (AA) cadres, who are within Myanmar as well as Bangladesh territory.

iii) Further, all the leaders of Hmawngbuchhuah, Zochachhuah and Dumzautlang Villages, Lawngtlai District were contacted and they reported that so far they have not sense/heard any activities or movements of Arakan Army (AA) near their area.

 The activity of Arakan Army (AA) is under close watch. Submitted for your kind information and necessary action please.
