****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/235

Dated 31st March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/229 Dt. 30.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****9 (nine) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-5, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 31.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

13 (thirteen) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-12, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 31.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) visited BSF place of deployment at Saihapui V today i.e., 31.3.2021@ 12:00 noon. to take stock of the situation and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

42 (forty two) vehicles (HMV-19, MMV-Nil, LMV-20, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 31.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

72 (seventy two) vehicles (HMV-20, MMV-16, LMV-14, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-2, Oil Tanker-19, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 31.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) visited BSF and IR duty place at Vairengte today i.e., 31.3.2021@ 3:00 p.m. to take stock of the situation.

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
