A. _Front page of VANGLAINI /THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 04.08.2022_

****Rs.134.58 Crores received from Central Government:**  Ministry of Finance, Government of India on yesterday i.e 3.8.2022 released Post Devolution Deficit Grant (PDDG) to 14 different States including Mizoram, which received Rs 134.58 crores. As a result, after seven days of continuous non disbursement of funds, Aizawl South treasury resumed releasing of funds for Bills which contains lower amount of funds. Before receiving PDDG and Share of Taxes from Central Government, it was not possible for the State treasuries to release funds. These PDDG are received by the States on a monthly basis while there is no time bound for Share of Taxes from the Central Government.**

_B.Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 04.08.2022_

****Discussion on Mizoram Home Department:**A meeting to discuss various issues regarding Mizoram Home Department was held yesterday i.e 3.8.2022. The meeting, which was conducted under the Chairmanship of Chief Minister Pu Zoramthanga, was attended by Home Minister Pu Lalchamliana, DGP Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastava, Finance Commissioner Pu Vanlal Chhuanga and other senior officials of Home Department.**

 During the meeting, discussion was conducted on - shortage of staff within Mizoram Home Department, ongoing development projects, security related matters and different projects undertaken by Mizoram Police recently such as free medical camp ,drugs awareness programme, awareness on cyber crime etc.
