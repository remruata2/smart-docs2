1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 7 (seven) leaders of Saiphai VC and YMA led by Pu Lalramliana, VCP, Saiphai and 7(seven) leaders of Kolichhora and Karkat Panchayat (Assam) led by Abdula, President, Young Muslim Association(YMA), Kolichhora held a joint meeting today i.e., 23.11.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. at Kachasora Bridge, Assam-Mizoram boundary. During the meeting, Assam side proposes to withdraw road blockade and making of joint statement but Mizoram side stated that they didn’t have anything to say regarding withdrawal of road blockade as they are not blocking the road. After detailed deliberation, they decided that Assam side will convey their desire to Assam Forest Minister and at the same time Mizoram side highlighted that movement across the border is still permissible on every Thursday as per DC, Kolasib standing order.

 Bamboo barricade put up by residents of Karkat Village, Assam to restrict movement of vehicles and people across the border is still remained at Kachasora Bridge, towards Assam side, Mizoram-Assam boundary and there is no entry of vehicles and people across the border today i.e., 23.11.2020.

 Situation is under close watch.
