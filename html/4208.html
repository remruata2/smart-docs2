Para 6-NCB drugs seizure rewards : NCB reward was earlier awarded to an officer   of CID (SB) vide order No. PHQ/IGP(Int)/PB/REWARD/30/2022/886 dated 17.08.2023. Further, CID (SB) has recently entrusted  the In-Charge of Field Branch to process recommendations for NCB rewards and ensure that seizures of narcotics drugs with appropriate quantities are processed for the reward. However, due to the mandatory provision of submission of Chargesheet during forwarding of NCB reward, the recent seizures of drugs could only be recommended as soon as Chargesheets are submitted in each respective cases.
