MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/167

Dated 9th March, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/163
Dt. 8.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-16, LMV-10) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 9.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-13, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 9.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m.
1 (one) vehicle (MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

75 (seventy five) vehicles (HMV-59, MMV-1, LMV-13, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-2) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 9.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

118 (one hundred eighteen) vehicles (HMV-40, MMV-26, LMV-23, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-9, Oil Tanker-18, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 9.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
