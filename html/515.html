Amount of claims:-

 

As per the existing rates fixed by the Government, the rate of rental payable to the owner of land is Rs. 0.60 per sq. Ft per annum in the initial stage. This rate is to be increased by 50% after every 5 years.

 

            At present, the above mentioned two cases are kept pending and none of the petitioners have received any rentals from the occupation of their land by security forces.
