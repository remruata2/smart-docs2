SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/82

Dated: 7th February, 2019.

 

The newly appointed 14 (fourteen) Interim Council Members of Sinlung Hills Council took an ‘Oath of Affirmation’ today, i.e. 7.2.2019 @ 11:00 a.m at the Office of DC, Aizawl which was administered by the Deputy Commissioner, Aizawl District. The 14 (fourteen) appointed Council Members for the Interim Period of Sinlung Hills Council w.e.f. 8.1.2019 to six months or until fresh election is constituted as under:-

 

Chief Executive Member    - Pu Lalthlamuana Hmar, Sakawrdai

Chairman                               - Pu Lalmalsawma Darngawn, Tinghmun

Deputy Chairman                 - Pu Lalbiaknunga, Saiphai

 

Executive Members :

i)         Pu Lalrosiem, Khawlian                  ii)        Pu Ngurhrangkima, Phuaibuang

iii)       Pu R. Lalfakzuala, Sakawrdai        iv)       Pi Melody Lalchhuangzeli, Sakawrdai

 

Members :

i)         Pu D. Hmangaiha, Sakawrdai         ii)        Pu H. Lalengzauva, Ratu

iii)       Pu Lalruatliana, Saipum                  iv)       Pu Zahmingliana, Vanbawng

v)        Pu H.C. Lalmalsawma, Zohmun

 

Nominated Members :

i)         Pu Rodingliana, Khawlian              ii)        Pu Khawlremthanga, Suangpuilawn

 

Information received indicated that there is no expressed dissatisfaction from HPC(D) regarding the appointment of these new Interim Council Members due to their expectation that appointments under SHC will be made in the immediate future from amongst their members or their nominees.
