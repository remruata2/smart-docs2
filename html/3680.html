A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 16.09.2022

CM met Prime Minister : Chief Minister of Mizoram, Pu Zoramthanga on 14th September 2022 met Prime Minister of India, Pu Narendra Modi at new Delhi and discussed with him various issues pertaining to the requirement of Mizoram state. Among the discussions, shifting of Assam Rifles Headquarters from Aizawl city, appointing Mizo dialect speaking person as Chief Secretary of the state, implementation of housing assistance to MNF returnees and financial assistance for payment of HUDCO loans were some of the topics. The Chief Minister also met union Defence Minister, Pu Rajnath Singh in the latter's office on 15th September 2022 and discussed with him about the requirement of lands by the state in cantonment board areas and deployment of paramilitary forces (where Mizos are enrolled) along the Mizoram- Myanmar/Bangladesh borders. The Defence Minister assured the Chief Minister that he will look into the matter.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 16.09.2022

Cleanliness drive:  Inauguration programme for Cleanliness  awareness programme, Swachhata Hi Seva 2022 was held yesterday (15.9.2022) in all the districts of the state and will last till 2nd October 2022. In Aizawl, it was inaugurated at Zodin Square by Pu Lalmuanzova, Secretary, PHED which was followed by a rally by staffs of PHED and some invitees. During this period, awareness programme on safe drinking water and cleanliness will be organised in all the District/Sub Divisional/block level throughout the state.
