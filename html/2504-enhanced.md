Information has been received that today, i.e., 17.10.2020 @ around 6:30 p.m., around 30 (thirty) persons, allegedly residents of Lailapur, Assam pelted stones at IR temporary duty Camp set up due to COVID-19 pandemic near Vairengte Autorickshaw Stand at Mizoram-Assam border. 4 (four) IR personnel were in the camp at the time of the incident. Enraged over the incident, Vairengte Joint YMA made public announcement asking all available members to proceed to Vairengte Autorickshaw Stand. Around 300 (three hundred) residents of Vairengte, some armed with sticks, dao etc. rushed to the spot and allegedly set ablaze 1 (one) house and around 7 (seven) temporary shops set up by Assam people at the disputed Mizoram-Assam border. All available police force at Vairengte were immediately deployed in the place of occurrence.

As per information available, 7 (seven) Mizos of Vairengte viz. 1) Ramdina(50) of Bangla Veng 2) Lalngaihthanga (48) of Bangla Veng, 3) R.K Lalhmachhuana (37) of Tlangnuam Veng 4) Malsawmkima (30) of Bangla Veng, 5) Lalroliana (28) of Hall Veng, 6) Lalsangzuala (30) of Bangla Veng and 7) Lalengmawia (36) of Venghlun sustained injury on their bodies due to the ensuing physical clash between the two communities and Malsawmkima of Bangla Veng was brought to hospital for medical treatment. As per input, one non-Mizo sustained physical injury on his person and evacuated to Silchar Medical College and Hospital.

As per input, the situation is beginning to get normal at the time of writing the report and Addl.SP, Kolasib has also reached the spot. DC, Cachar and SP, Cachar also arrived at Lailapur and crowd of both sides started dispersing and round 100 residents of Vairengte are still present at Vairengte Autorickshaw stand at the time of writing the report i.e., 11:00 p.m.

At the same time, MZP General Headquarters issued a Statement later this evening wherein it forbids all non-Mizos of Mizoram from venturing outside their homes with immediate effect and until further notice.

Due to the incident, 4 (four) leaders of MZP General Headquarters led by Pu B. Vanlaltana, President MZP proceeded to Vairengte around 2:30 p.m. today i.e., 17.10.2020.

Pu Lalrinsanga, Minister proceeded to Vairengte and arrived at around 10:00 p.m. this evening.

2(two) Coy of Mizoram Armed Police at Mualvum, Kolasib District proceeded to Vairengte this evening at 10:30 p.m. to reinforce the existing Mizoram Police force.

Situation under close watch.
