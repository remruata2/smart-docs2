MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/43

Dated 19th January, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/41
Dt. 18.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV – 6, MMV – 4, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 19.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 1 (one) vehicle (MMV – 1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

9 (nine) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 6, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 19.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 8 (eight) vehicles (MMV – 7, LMV -1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          95 (ninety five) vehicles (HMV-63, MMV – Nil, LMV – 26, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 19.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

133 (one hundred thirty three) vehicles (HMV – 52, MMV – 23, LMV –14, LPG – 4, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 33, Bus - 3) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 19.01.2021during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
