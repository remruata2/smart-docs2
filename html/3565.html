  1. Front Page of Vanglaini Daily Newspaper as on 1.7.2022

YMA AND VC SUPPORT BURNING OF ARECA NUT IMPORTED FROM MYANMAR:

Taitesena Group YMA and Kawrthah Joint Village Council issued a Statement wherein they expressed their support of the initiative taken by Hachhek Bial Kuhva Chingtu Pawl (HBKCP) and burning of seized areca nut, which was allegedly imported from Myanmar at Zamuang Village, Mamit District. HBKCP on duty seized 285 bags of dry areca nut, which was intended to be delivered to Assam/Tripura on the evening of 27.6.2022 and after unloading from the truck, the consignment was burnt on the early morning of 28.6.2022. Lalzawna, President HBKCP stated that they performed duty in pursuance of information received regarding transporting of dry areca nut form Vairengte Godown and accordingly seized the stated areca nut.

 

CONDUCTED CHECKING OF ILLEGAL SELLING OF OIL:

In pursuance of the order of DM, Lawngtlai District, Enforcement Team led by Pu H. Vanlalbiakzauva, SDC Lawngtlai conducted checking of illegal selling of Petrol and Diesel within Lawngtlai town and no one was arrested.

 

  1. Front Page of The Aizawl Post as on 1.7.2022

REQUESTED AUTHORITY OF NHIDCL TO CLOSELY SUPERVISE THEIR CONTRACTORS:

Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha held a meeting with Chandal Kumar, Managing Director, NHIDCL and Ashok Kumar Jha, General Manager (Technical) at New Delhi. During the meeting, the MP stated that Contractors and Sub-Contractors under NHIDCL executed the work as per their convenience by violating the forest and environmental laws. He urged the authority of NDICL to closely supervise their contractors and take necessary action against those contractors who violated the laws.
