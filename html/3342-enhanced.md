A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 16.9.2013_

**FIR lodged regarding construction of Sports Complex****:** Lengpui Games &amp; Sports Association lodged an FIR to ACB on 12th September 2013 regarding the on-going construction of Lengpui Mini Sports Complex; Lengpui Games &amp; Sports Association feels that the work being done and the expenditure do not match.

B. _Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 16.9.2013_

****New Police building****:** Home Minister Pu R.Lalzirliana inaugurated a new police building for Superintendent of Police and DIG(SR) office at Lunglei on September 13. The four storey new building cost ` 177.94/- lakh. Lunglei District currently has six Police Stations and two outposts with total strength of 388 Policemen.**

C. _Front page of NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 16.9.2013_

****Bandh at Champhai****:** Two students, bodies of Champhai town with the support of NGOs and political parties, are gearing up to organise a bandh in Champhai on 17th September 2013 to demand posting of more lecturers in Champhai college to meet shortage in faculty. Closing of all educational institutions will be enforced from 18th September 2013 if the State administration gives no positive response to the bandh.**
