MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/239

Dated 1st April, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/235
Dt. 31.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-3, LMV-11) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 1.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

28 (twenty eight) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-9, LMV-13) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 1.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

Construction of agriculture link road at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border was resumed yesterday evening i.e., 31.3.2021 by engaging 3 (three) excavators (JCB). Pu K. Lalrinliana, Minister visited the site today i.e., 1.4.2021 @ 12:40 p.m. to take stock of the situation and left the area this afternoon @ 1:45 p.m.

Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) visited the disputed site at Assam-Mizoram border this morning @ 6:25 a.m. to take stock of the situation and he was accompanied by Pu Lalfakawma, Bairabi North VCP, and left the area @ 7:15 a.m.

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-21, MMV-2, LMV-2, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 1.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

85 (eighty five) vehicles (HMV-29, MMV-25, LMV-10, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-15, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 1.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
