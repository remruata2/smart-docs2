SPECIAL REPORT

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/091

Dated: 11th February, 2020.

 

                        As per input, a group of 5 to 6 unidentified masked men, allegedly armed with 2 (two) local made Guns and Dao, threatened and robbed 3(three) families who are employed as caretakers of WRC (Wet Rice Cultivation) belonging to : i) Pu Vanlalhruaia, Zarkawt, Aizawl; ii) Pu Tawnluia, Chawnpui, Aizawl; and iii) Pu Vanhnuaithanga, Suarhliap, early this morning i.e. 11.2.2020 @ 2:00 a.m – 3:00 a.m. The victims are identified as :-

1)        Chajar Ali Lasker (27)                                2)        Myar Uddin Majhumdar (25)        

            s/o Shiraj Uddin Lasker,                                         s/o Noor Akmor, Majhumdar        

            Gharmura, Assam.                                                    Gharmura, Assam.

 

3)        Sams Uddin (22)

            s/o Jamshaid Ali,

            Katalichhora, Assam.

 

                        It is reported that the miscreants were speaking in Hindi. They tied down the victims and extorted cash and other items (Mobile Handset and Household Goods) with a total worth value of Rs. 50,300/-.

                        A team led by OC Mamit Police Station rushed to the spot and investigation is currently underway.
