**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.23.1.2013**

**Extreme actions of Assam Rifles felt by NGOs:** Before and during the Christmas festive season of last year, the Assam Rifles has been conducting town duties inside Champhai.These duties included checking of vehicles and frisking of persons. Disappointed at these actions of the Assam Rifles, certain leaders of Champhai NGOs and Youth Organizations intended to meet senior officers of the Assam Rifles and will take the matter to the Home Minister if the meeting does not reap fruitful effect.

 According to reports, whenever apprehensions of narcotics substances are made by the Assam Rifles, handing over to the local police is never done by them. Besides, these duties were performed without the knowledge of the local police.

**B. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt.23.1.2013**

**Apprehension of eight bootleggers everday**: During the month of December 2012 ,The Mizoram Excise and Police Deptt. apprehended 249 bootleggers, an average of 8 persons daily. 5252 litres of local liquor (Rakzu),5830 bottles of IMFL,186 bottles of beer,50 Kg of yeast,10 drunk persons and 13 vehicles used for such purposes were apprehended during the month.

**Hit-and run driver surrenders:** A non local truck driver Abdul Kasim who absconded on January 16 after running over a 21 year old girl of Lunglei, surrendered before Lunglei Police on January 21. The Police produced him before the court which further sent him to Lunglei District jail on the same day. The truck driver was accompanied by his employer.
