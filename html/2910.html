A. Front page of Zozam Times newspaper, Dt. 28.9.2012.

 

National Highway No.54 blockade called off with condition:  The indefinite blockade on the National Highway-54, the economic line of Mizoram organised by All Barak Youth Students Association(ABYSA) started from 26.9.2012 was called off with condition last night at 7:00pm.

 

It has been called off as the Addl. DC of Cachar promised to convey ABYSA's disappointment to Mizoram Govt. regarding Inner Line Permit(ILP). If Mizoram Govt. do not take action till sunday, they will resume road blocking(economic blockade).

 

During the road block period, there was not much problems among the Mizos and it is heard that the general public did not fully support ABYSA's action of blocking road.

 

The same topic has been highlighted in Vanglaini and The Aizawl Post newspaper.

 

 

 

 

B. Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper, Dt. 28.9.2012.

 

Skull found in Sairang:  A skull which seems to be a human skull was found in Sairang Dinthar area near Sihlui. The Sairang Police took the skull and sent to Forensic Science Laboratory(FSL) yesterday. According to report made by SI. Lalchawimawia, OC, Siarang PS, on 26.9.2012, two women from Sairang found a skull among a cluster of bamboos at Sihlui valley near Buichali road. The women informed the Police and the Police acquired it at about 10:30am yesterday. They believe that it is the skull of human, the Police also found some bones near the skull but no trace of clothing found. The FSL report is now being waited.
