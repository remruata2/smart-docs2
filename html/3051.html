SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/064

Dated: 31st January, 2020.

 

 

                        MSU leaders are understood to be furious for being booked in a criminal case in connection with vandalism of Chakma House, Aizawl in 2017, vide Vaivakawn PS C/No. 146/17 dt. 28.9.2017 u/s 448/342/427/506 IPC. It may be recalled that the incident was triggered by alleged assault of Chawngte MSU Sub-Hqrs. leaders by Chakmas at Chawngte on 26.9.2017.

                   Information received indicated that MSU leaders have decided to disobey Court's summon to appear on 4.02.2020. They are strongly supported by leaders of Aizawl City College Joint Students Union (ACCJSU) in this regard. MSU leaders were also learnt to have let loose talk indicating that they would again resort to vandalism targeting Chakma house again. Input received indicated that MSU General Hqrs. and ACCJSU will hold a Press Conference on 3.2.2020 to highlight their stand and plan of action.

          Situation is under close watch.
