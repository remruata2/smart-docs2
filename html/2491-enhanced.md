****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT AS ON 24.10.2020****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/596

Dated 24th October, 2020

In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/595 Dt. 23.10.2020, Situation report on Mizoram-Assam Border is made as under :-

1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report**:

Today i.e., 24.10.2020 @ 11:30 a.m. leaders of Mizoram Synod viz., Rev. Lianhmingthanga Sailo, Senior Executive Secretary, Rev. R. Ramnghinglova, Director, Campus Ministry and Rev. J. Lalremsiama, Synod Statistician visited Thinghlun and handed over monetary assistance of Rs. 10,000/- each to Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David of Thinghlun. 

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report–**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District –**

Today i.e., 24.10.2020 @ 9:40 a.m. SDPO, Vairengte and Shri. Bhargav Goswami, Dy. SP (Hqrs.), Cachar District, SI Sharabuddin Bharbhuya, OC Dholai PS and party conducted a joint inspection of SSA Lower Primary School near Saihapui ‘V’ where a bomb blast occurred on Dt. 22.10.2020 @ 10:15 p.m. In connection with the said bomb blast a case has been registered at Dholai Police Station vide Dholai PS Case No. 252/2020 u/s 447/427/34 IPC r/w Sec 3 of Explosive Substances Act.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report –**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report-**

****Today i.e., 24.10.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. Shri. Suzam Uddin Lasker, Katlicherra MLA visited Assam-Mizoram Border near Phaisen Village and he was accompanied by Addl. DC, Hailakandi.
