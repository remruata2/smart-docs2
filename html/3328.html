A. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 28.5.2013

Heroin seized : The joint team of Special Narcotic PS Staff and 26th Assam Rifles seized Heroin worth ` 1,60,000/- in the market rate at Aizawl New Market yesterday i.e., 27.5.2013. The alleged owners Rothianga (50) s/o Lianthanga Falam, Myanmar and Siamkunga (25) s/o Billian of Falam, Myanmar were also arrested.

 

B. Front page of  THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 28.5.2013

No repatriation for Bru : Government of Mizoram’s plan to repatriate Bru from Tripura to Mizoram in the last week of May, 2013 will not be carried out as it has to wait for Tripura government.

            As reported by Joint Secretary of Home Department, Pu Lalbiakzama, Mizoram government is to firstly have a talk with Tripura to make arrangement for repatriation but Tripura Government feels it is not yet convenient for repatriation and hence meeting between the two governments could not be arranged.
