A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 05.09.2022_

**50 civilians owned land pass within the Assam Rifles cantonment** : According to an information obtained through RTI Act, there are, presently 50 individuals who possessed Land pass inside the area occupied by the Assam Rifles in the heart of Aizawl city. Out of the 50 Land passes, 3 of them belongs to former MLAs while 1 pass each belongs to Public Health Engineering Department (PHED), Bethlehem Veng Primary school and two different Village Councils. Among these land pass owners, PHED possessed the largest area (5481.8 Sq.m) while Ramprasad, Bethlehem Veng is the largest owners of land among the civilian individuals (1,577.9 Sq.m). The smallest area belongs to C.Lalbiakliana of Chhinga Veng which is 32.21 Sq.m.

B. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 05.09.2022_

****Outbreak of Dengue in different parts of Mizoram:** Reports from State Vector Borne Diseases Control Programme (SVBDCP) which is under Health Department indicated that recently, different parts of Mizoram State witness a surge in the rate of dengue cases, particularly Edenthar and ITI Veng within Aizawl city and Sekhum village in Lunglei district. Officials of SVBDCP said that although dengue is spread by a type of mosquitoes - Aedes aegypti, it has been verified that the majority of Aedes aegypti in Mizoram did not carry this disease and those spreading it are believed to have entered Mizoram from other states. According to the records of SVBDCP, there have been 1,185 cases of dengue in Mizoram during the period between 2013 till July of this year, fortunately, a case of death as a result of this disease has not been registered.**
