Subject:  Allotment of additional fund.

Sir,

  As you are aware that, an amount of Rs. 10 lakhs has been sanctioned to the undersigned through Police Headquarters towards meeting expenditure incurred in the Peace Dialogue process between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) and that the aforesaid amount of Rs. 10 lakhs has been accorded to the undersigned prior to first Political level Peace Dialogue held on 7.3.2018.

 Further, as you are aware that, the first round of Political level Peace Dialogue was held on 7.3.2018 which was followed by signing of Memorandum of Settlement on 2.4.2018 and that around 102 cadres of HPC(D) have since been accommodated wef 7.4.2018 at CTI Sesawng and their food supplies, conveyances, etc. arranged since last part of March, 2018. Considering huge expenditure being incurred during the process and in view of Laying down of Arms Ceremony scheduled to be held on 13.4.2018 at CTI Sesawng, **it is anticipated and estimated with due care** that additional fund to the tune of Rs. 8 lakhs would be required to meet the whole expenses, viz.-

 1. Public feast for about 1200 persons

  including dignitaries &amp; VVIPs - Rs. 3.5 lakhs

 2. Hiring of Sound system - Rs. 1 lakh

 3. Conveyance/POL (Buses, Trucks, LMVs) etc.- Rs. 1 lakh

 4. Sintex/Electrification at CTI, Sesawng - Rs. 0.6 lakh

 5. Re-imbursement to HPC(D) cadres

  (Food/Conveyance, etc.) - Rs. 1.2 lakhs

 6. Hiring of Mattresses/ Medicines/

  Gas Cylinders/Utensils/Firewood/

  Decoration of grounds, etc.etc.) - _Rs. 0.7 lakh_

 TOTAL - Rs. 8 lakhs

 I, therefore, request your honour kindly to pursue the matter for allotment of aforesaid additional fund, ie, Rs. 8 lakhs to the undersigned through proper channel.

 This matter may kindly be treated as ‘**TOP PRIORITY**’.
