15.7.2022 :    Mizoram Underground Returnees' Association (MURA) held its Emergency Meeting on 15.7.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at MURA Office, Treasury Square. Pu R. Vanlalvena, General Secretary MURA presided the meeting and 20 (twenty) members attended. During the meeting, Pu Lalthuama, President MURA stated that the land allotted to members of MURA at Tuivamit will be settled soon due to the initiative taken by Pu Lalruatkima, Minister. The meeting after deliberate discussion decided that the State government should be requested to provide government jobs to their dependents within this year as per their agreement. It also decided to take necessary action so that MURA members receive assistances under SEDP or contract works as per their agreement with the State government. It further decided to extend necessary support to MNF in the coming 2023 General Election.
