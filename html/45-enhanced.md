Sub : Illegal collection of deposits and unlawful transactions of money.

Sir,

 With reference to your DO No. G.25014/1/2007-5-APF Aizawl the 5th December 2008, I beg to inform you that till 23rd December 2008, 2056 ( two thousand fifty six) First Information Report has been received by Aizawl Police Station in connection with illegal collection of deposits and unlawful transactions of money in Mizoram which is also commonly known as “Chiahpuam” and necessary investigation is underway on registration of relevant cases among which there are 3 (Three) cases involving Govt. Officials in unlawful deposits and transaction of Govt. Money as given under:-

1) Aizawl PS Case No. 374/08 Dt. 10.11.08 U/S 409 IPC against Pi Saichhungi, UDC –cum Cashier of Co-operative Society, Aizawl involving Rs. 27 lakhs of Govt. Money.

2) Aizawl PS Case No. 376/08 Dt. 12.11.08 U/S 409 IPC against Pi Chawngrinsangi, UDC-cum Cashier of Directorate, Health Services involving Rs. 130 lakhs of Govt. Money.

3) Aizawl PS Case No. 384/08 Dt. 17.11.08 U/S 409 IPC against Pu Lalthantlinga, Marketing Officer of Trade and Commerce Department involving Rs. 89 lakhs of Govt. Money.

_Cont….. 2/-_
