A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.2.2014_

**Deep Mandal is expected to be released****:**Pu R.Lalzirliana, Home Minister said that Mizoram Police has been doing its best for the safe release of Deep Mandal who is abducted along with two Mizos by Bru armed gang on 23.11.2013. NLFT High Command has already agreed to release Deep Mandal and the information is also given to Area Commander. So, Deep Mandal is expected to be released soon and if he is released, Mizoram Police will receive him at the border.
