****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/278

Dated 9th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/273 Dt. 08.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-11, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 9.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-23, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 9.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

77 (seventy seven) vehicles (HMV-31, MMV-1, LMV-36, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 9.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

125 (one hundred twenty five) vehicles (HMV-34, MMV-47, LMV-7, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-16, Oil Tanker-19, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 9.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Mr. Sunny Deo Choudhuri, DFO Cachar, Assam; RO, Hawaithanga and 14 (fourteen) Armed Assam Police visited Auto-Stand, Vairengte near Mizoram-Assam border yesterday i.e., 8.4.2021 @ 5:00 p.m. DFO, Cachar threatened and directed the workers of Pi Zatluangi of Vairengte Venghlun, who are constructing her House at the land allocated to her by Vairengte VC-I near Auto Stand, Vairengte not to continue their work.

Upon hearing the above incident, SDPO Vairengte and party rushed to the spot and by the time they arrived at the spot, officials of Assam already left the area. SDPO, Vairengte informed workers at the area to resume their house construction and if required, they will deploy Armed escort. He also informed BSF personnel on duty at the border not to allow such kind of Assam officials to enter into Mizoram side without prior permission.

Information received stated that Assam officials entered the said area by foot from western area, Lailapur side by evading BSF duty at the border.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
