            In the past elections such as – Mizoram Legislative Assembly General Election 2008, 2013; MP Election 2009, 2014; Village Council Election 2009, 2012, 2015 and Aizawl Municipal Corporation Election 2010, 2015 and various district Council election, Mizoram People Forum and different Political parties have made an agreement which bore positive result. The MPF became know to people within and outside Mizoram because of these achievements as elections process became much more satisfactory, thanks to the cooperation of all involved.

 

1. General Conduct:

 

(1)       For holding a clean, free and fair election every precaution and measure should be taken.

 

(2)       All the rightful citizens of the country enrolled in the electoral roll must cast their votes.

 

(3)       The conveyance and traveling expenditures of electoral voters, who are stationed in places other than the constituency where they have enrolled, shall not be borne by others such as political parties, candidates or their workers.

 

 

2. Election Expenditure :

 

                        Election Commission of India had set expenditure limits for MLA    candidates to not more than Rs.20.00,000/-.To minimize the expenditure to this limits, the following points must be followed –

 

(1)       Collecting or demanding donations from MLA Candidates by            MPF Members is strictly prohibited.

 

(2)       For house to house campaign, candidates must be accompanied by   leaders of MPF Local Forum.

 

(3)        Influencing of voters through money or other incentives and inducements must be avoided. General public, organizations and individuals are requested not to ask or expect any favour from the candidates. Similarly, candidates and political parties are also requested to refrain from announcing any financial grants, distribution of inducement articles.

 

(4)       Feasting, picnic and procession escalate the election expenditure     unnecessarily; they must be avoided.

 

(5)       Obtaining any items from certain shops in the credit of the candidates         should be avoided.

 

(6)       Banner, flag and poster unnecessarily escalate the election expenditure;     therefore the maximum number of banners, flags and posters is   restricted within each locality as under-

  • For areas having 7(seven) VC/LC members, upto 5 Banners, 50 Flags and 30 posters.
  • For areas having 5(five) VC/LC members, upto 4 Banners, 40 Flags and 30 posters.
  • For areas having 3(three) VC/LC members, upto 3 banners, 30 Flags and 20 posters

 

                        Dimensions –

  • Banner not bigger than 4x8 ft.
  • Poster not bigger than 3x4 ft.
  • For two wheelers flag, not bigger than 1x2 ft.
  • For Three/Four wheelers flag, not bigger than 2x3 ft.
  • The height of the flag should not exceed 3 ft.

 

3. Campaigning for Election :

 

(1)       As per the Law of the land, no minor who are under age for electoral roll    should be engage in election process.

 

(2)       Any party can have an Election Information Center, provided no loud         speakers are installed in them.

 

(3)       No street procession should be organize after declaration of election.

 

(4)       For campaigning efforts, no public entertainers should be hired.

 

(5)       Spreading false information through Social media should be avoided as      it is against the law. Violators will be announced before they are      reported to the Election commission or CID Cyber Crime.

 

(6)       As prohibited by Election Commission of India, alcohol and drugs   should not be utilized for election campaign. Persons indulging in these intoxicating substances should neither be allowed near the polling            area.

 

(7)       Great efforts must be taken to enable all voters to exercise their rights         freely. 

 

 

 

 

4. Free and fair election and security issues:

 

(1)         Use of force, intimidation and undue influence to the voters to secure their votes must be avoided.

 

(2)         Securing the support of underground armed groups and use of arms shall be prevented at all cost.

 

(3)         Take all necessary actions for security and peaceful election, especially along the border and sensitive areas.

 

(4)         As casting of ballot should be secret, no mobile phone should be taken inside the polling booth.

 

5. Policy implementation:

 

(1)         Only realistic and do-able policies and programme must be included in the Party manifesto.

 

(2)       More than the personal and individual life of the candidates, election          campaigning should revolve around the party policy land programme.         Campaigning by use of false propaganda, unproven truth and scandalous           issues must be avoided.

 

6. Candidates :        

 

            While making the nomination, the political parties are requested to consider the following qualities in their prospective candidates -

 

(1)       Stable and upright person.

 

(2)       Honest and hard working.

 

(3)       Person with great integrity.

 

(4)       Free from the influence of alcoholic and other drugs.

 

(5)       Abstain from fornication.

 

(6)       Having great respect for law of the land.

 

(7)       Dedicated to the welfare of the society and fellow human beings.

 

(8)       Committed to one’s faith and religion

 

 

 

7. Joint electioneering effort:

 

(1)         As and when possible, joint election campaign platform will be organized for various candidates, under the guidance of MPF Constituency Forum. These programmes may be widely publicized in the local Television and media; other than these, joint election campaign platform must be avoided.

 

(2)         All public meetings, even those organized by individual party, may be held under the guidance of MPF. In the absence of MPF, individual political parties may make suitable arrangement.

 

(3)       No  tea  or  eatables  shall  be  served  on the  polling  day. Giving out of     election  numbers  or  slips  shall  be undertaken by the MPF duty or in a        joint effort with the party workers

 

8.   Conclusion :

 

(1)         MPF District Forum, Constituency Forum and Local Forum shall neither make additional agreement nor deviate from this ‘Articles of Agreement’.

 

(2)         If any political parties or candidates default or goes against the spirit of this ‘Articles of Agreement’ and if there is solid proof or documentary evidence to prove the same, it shall be widely publicized within the MLA Constituency where it has been committed.

 

(3)         This ‘Articles of Agreement’ shall be widely publicized and circulated by the Mizoram People Forum and Political Parties.

 

(4)         For having free and fair election, this agreement will also include Independent Candidates, if there is any.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

WORK SCHEDULE OF MIZORAM PEOPLE FORUM

 

            Mizoram People Forum is making enormous efforts to have free and fair election and good governance in line with the will of God. To achieve these objectives, political education seminar and campaign are organized in various places so that the agreement made between MPF and different political parties and the model code of conduct of Election Commission of India could be implemented.

            Looking at the progress of MPF, there is still  much to be done. As such, more efforts have to be taken considering the following points:-

 

I.          Establishing and Consolidations MPF local Forum-

 

            1)        Establishing new MPF local forum in places where it has not                          been raised yet.

            2)        To strengthen Local forum, leaders of District forum and                                Constituency forum must joint hands together.

            3)        MPF Central forum must also take necessary steps to strengthen                   District and constituency forum.

            4)        To consolidate local forum, more than two members from                               constituency forum and prominent citizen may be appointed, if                    required.

 

II.         Considering the present political scenario, having political education campaign is a must using “For the kingdom is the Lord’s” as a theme. Different MPF constituent members may conduct this campaign in their respective domain, keeping in mind the following points-

 

            1)        The Land belongs to the Lord and we are his keepers. It must be governed according to His will.

            2)        To achieve this, we must use our clear conscience during election process, refraining from any dirty political agendas.

            3)        Awareness on the consequences of bribing and inducements during election campaign may be conducted.

            4)        Populace must be informed how dirty politics can disintegrate society, the state and even religion.Its preventive measure may also be stressed.

            5)        Keeping our faith in mind, everyone must cast our vote.

            6)        The state will definitely benefit if elections are conducted freely and fairly.

 

III.       Joint lectionaries effort:

 

            To implement the agreement made with different political parties, the following steps may be taken-

            1) Interaction/Meeting between MPF governing Board and each party leaders may be organize as deemed necessary.

            2) Seminar on the agreement and election model code of conduct may be organize with different party leaders at block, unit and group levels.

            3) The effect of excessive expenditure during election campaign by candidates and its aftermath may also be discussed.

 

IV. Instructions for polling day:

 

1) To prevent disturbances and unwanted incidents in and around polling areas, each local forum must be extra vigilant.

 

2) Discussion with BLO may be conducted so as to provide easy access to their

number to each and every voter on the polling day.

 

V. Conclusion:

            Comparing to previous elections, leaders of each Local Forum must give extra efforts in the coming 2018 election, especially in house-to-house campaigning. According to the agreement, candidates are permitted to have house-to-house campaign in the presence of Local Forum leaders. It is also expected that candidates will be accompanied by their local party workers in the process.

            To achieved optimum result in these efforts of MPF,it is imperative to have better cooperation with Church leaders and voluntary organizations. It is desirable to appoint leaders of different Church denominations and NGO leaders as leaders of MPF as mentioned in MPF Rule book.
