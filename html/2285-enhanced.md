****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/104

Dated 11th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/101 Dt. 10.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV -3, MMV -9, LMV - 4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 11.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 3 (three) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1, LMV- 1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-14, MMV-14, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 11.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 11 (eleven) vehicles (MMV-11, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

50 (fifty) vehicles (HMV-29, MMV-1, LMV-17, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 11.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

148 (one hundred forty eight) vehicles (HMV-61, MMV-32, LMV-29, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-16, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 11.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

34 (thirty four) vehicles (HMV - 1, MMV - 11, LMV - 22) entered Saiphai from Assam today, i.e., 11.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. and 13 (thirteen) persons have undergone RAT test and none was found positive. Situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
