BORDER ISSUES WITH ASSAM

 

            Assam and Mizoram share a 164.6 km inter-state boundary. Residents of Mizoram claimed that 1875 notification should be the boundary between Assam and Mizoram, while Assam claimed 1933 notification as the constitutional boundary between the two states.

 

  1. Kolasib District:

 

  1. Assam-Mizoram border dispute at Zophai (Kachurtol) (Kachurtol), Bairabi, Kolasib District:

 

1) On 27thFebruary, 2018, the MizoZirlai Pawl (MZP), built a wooden rest house somewhere in the Inner Line Permit Reserved Forest(509 SqKms). The same evening, a joint team of the Assam police and Assam Forest officials demolished the structure.

 

2) On 8.3.2018 around 11:45 am, a minor scuffle broke out between the Student union (MZP) and Assam Police where the Assam Police had resorted to Lathi-charge as a result of which around 15 (fifteen) MZP members reportedly sustained minor injuries. 

 

3) On10.3.2018,a scuffle broke out again between Assam Police and members of Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) at Zophai (Kachurtol). Assam Police used force to disperse members of MZP using Lathi and later on resorted to firing as a result of which one MZP member sustained gunshot injury on his right ankle and around 49 (forty nine) others sustained injuries. It was also reported that 8 (eight) media persons also sustained injuries. Several vehicles belonging to MZP members were also ransacked by Assam Police.

 

4) On 12.4.2018, Joint Verification Team, comprising of officials from Mizoram and Assam started conducting spot verification of Inner Line Reserve Area along Mizoram-Assam border.

 

5) On 6.4.2019 officials from Assam Forest Department started renovation work of one building located at the disputed Mizoram-Assam Forest Reserve area along the Aizawl-Silchar National Highway and reported that the building was occupied on the same night.

 

The Council of Ministers meeting (Mizoram) held on 8.7.2019 upheld the Cachar Inner Line, 1875 enacted as per Section 2 of Bengal Eastern Frontier Regulations, 1873 (Regulation 1 of 1873) as the basis for settlement of border issues.

 

(2)   Mizoram-Assam border dispute at Vairengte, Kolasib District:

 

(1) DC, Kolasib and DC, Karimganj, Assam held a meeting on 3.9.2015 at CIJW Conference Hall, Vairengte and resolved that status quo ante shall be maintained in the respective forest areas. In spite of this, on 4.7.2019 around 10 (ten) Assam Forest Department officials led by Forest Range Officer had started renovation work of one building located at the disputed Mizoram-Assam Forest Reserve Area along the Aizawl – Silchar National Highway.

 

  1. On 17.10.2020 ,around 30 (thirty) residents of Lailapur, Assam pelted stones at IR temporary duty Camp set up due to COVID-19  pandemic near Vairengte Autorickshaw Stand at Mizoram-Assam border. There was also a scuffle between residents of Vairengte and Lailapur, Assam where 7(seven) residents of Vairengte sustained injury.

 

  1. Residents of Lailapur organized economic blockade w.e.f. 16.10.2020 and no vehicles were allowed to enter Mizoram. Residents of Assam also organized economic blockade of all major road connecting Vairengte, Kanhmun, Bairabi and Phaisen  and rail road connecting Bairabi rail station.

 

Border Security Force (BSF) personnel were deployed along the border on 9.11.2020 as per direction of Central government.

 

Blockade organized by residents of Lailapur at Lailapur was lifted on the evening of 11.11.2020. The situation along the inter-state border remained tense but under control and subsequently went normal.

 

(3)  Assam-Mizoram border dispute at Saihapui V (Khullichara/Mizo-

     Punji) (Khullichara/Mizo-punji), Kolasib District:

 

  1. On the evening of 16.10.2020 between 6:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. 7 (seven) Assam Police and Forest officials visited COVID-19 duty post set up at the outskirt of Saihapui 'V' Village, Kolasib District (around 300 meters from Saihapui V (Khullichara/Mizo-Punji)) manned by 1st IR personnel and they instructed the personnel on duty to vacate their duty post within two hours claiming that the duty post was within Assam territory

 

  1. On 17.10.2020 @ 12:00 p.m. around 200 (two hundred) civilians and 50 (fifty) Assam Police personnel came again at 1st IR Duty Post located at the outskirt of Saihapui 'V' and dismantled the Covid-19 duty Post.

 

  1. A bomb blast occurred at SSA Lower Primary School on 22.10.2020 @ 10:15 p.m. The said school is run by Assam Government and is located at around 300 meters from Saihapui ‘V’ Village and around 400 meters from Mizoram Police Camp at Assam-Mizoram border towards Mizoram.

 

(4) On 3.7.2021 @ 7:00 a.m. a team of Assam Forest Officials and Assam Police personnel visited Buarchep and Saihapuizau without prior intimation, whatsoever inspite of the attempt to maintain status quo by Mizoram.

(5) Mizoram-Assam border dispute at Phaisen, Kolasib District:

           

Residents of Dolchhora village, Assam blocked the road connecting Phaisen, Mizoram to Dolchhora, Assam near Mizoram-Assam border at around 15 meters away from inter-state boundary pillar on the Assam side 20.10.2020. The said bamboo wall was dismantled by residents of Dolchhora and nearby villages on 3.11.2020.

  1. Mizoram-Assam border dispute at Tuikhurzau (Detchhora khed), Chuhvel, Mamit District:

 

1) Shri.Rohan Kumar Jha, DC, Hailakandi, Assam; Smt. Ramandeep Kaur, SP, Hailakandi; Assam Forest Officials and around 70 (seventy) Assam Police personnel and civilians from Gutguti village, Assam, visited Pu John Halliday’s Farm, located around 1.7 Km from Chuhvel, Mamit District, inside Mizoram territory on 11.6.2021 They had a brief interaction with Pu John Holliday at his Farm House and informed him that the area falls within Assam territory.
