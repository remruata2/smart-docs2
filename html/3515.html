A. Front page of  VANGLAINI/ The Aizawl Post  newspapers Dt. 10.08.2022

Assam- Mizoram meeting on border issues:  Yesterday i.e 9.8.2022, a meeting on issues relating to the Assam-Mizoram interstate boundary dispute  was held by representatives of Assam and Mizoram at Aijal Club , Aizawl.  Delegates from Mizoram were led by Home Minister, Pu Lalchamliana while the Assam side were headed by Pu Atul Bora, Border Development & Protection Minister. The meeting issued a joint statement which are as under -

i)         Both the states reaffirm the joint statement of 5th August 2021.

ii)        To minimise tension in and around the border areas, concerned district D.C of       both the states shall meet at least once in every two months.

iii)       Cultivators along the border of the two states shall be allowed to practice   farming.

iv)       Next round of meeting will be held in Guwahati during the month of October,        2022

CM congratulates Jeremy Lalrinnunga: In a felicitation programme organised at CMO Conference Hall, Aizawl ,Mizoram Chief Minister Pu Zoramthanga congratulated Jeremy Lalrinnunga, Ainawn veng, Aizawl who bagged a Gold medal in weightlifting category in the recently concluded Common Wealth Games 2022. The Chief Minister, speaking in this programme, also expressed his expectation of organising a proper State function to honour the Gold Medallist along with another player from Mizoram, Lalremsiami , Indian Women Hockey player, who won bronze medal in the same Common Wealth Games, 2022.
