****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/754

Dated: 4th October, 2019.

 PRISM party and MPC party are intending to organize a programme tomorrow, i.e. 5.10.2019, at a particular place, expressing their opposition to the Citizenship Amendment Bill (CAB) during the visit of the Union Home Minister tomorrow, i.e. 5.10.2019. Input received indicated that they have selected Treasury Square junction as the venue for organizing the said programme. Information received indicated that the participants will display placards and banners expressing their opposition of the CAB.

 The two parties also decided to put up ‘Black Flag’ at their respective offices to show their disapproval of CAB. They are also planning to submit a Joint Memorandum to the Hon’ble Union Home Minister requesting him to grant exemption to Mizoram State in the CAB. As per input received, the number of participants in the said programme is expected between 50-80 persons.

 In the meantime, ZPM Youth is intending to place posters at various places in Aizawl City area between Thuampui to Zarkawt tonight, i.e. 4.10.2019 mentioning their opposition of the CAB-16.

 Meanwhile, input received indicated that one organization called, ‘Mizo Hnam Inkhawmpui Committee’ issued a notice to its members and its supporters today, i.e. 4.10.2019 and highlighted about their programme during the visit of the Hon’ble Union Home Minister tomorrow, i.e. 5.10.2019. The important points are as follows :-

1. To assemble their members tomorrow, i.e. 5.10.2019 @ 12:00 noon at Zarkawt near Missionaries Memorial Stone by wearing ‘Pawndum’.
2. To bring placards to express their opinion on CAB and visibly display them to Shri. Amit Shah.
3. To wear only black colour clothes by the participants so as to make ‘Pawndum’ more prominent.
4. Invited as many persons as possible to join their programme.
5. Requested their members from outside Aizawl to join their programme at their respective places.
 Information received indicated that Mizo Hnam Inkhawmpui Committee is a newly formed organization under the leadership of Pi Lalhmingliani w/o Robin Remruata, Tuikual. They are a group of people with a particular set of belief on Christianity which is contrary to popular Christian belief. About 30 – 50 persons are expected to participate in their programme.
