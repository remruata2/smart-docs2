A. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 1.4.2014

2 Persons Apprehended in the Dead of Jessu: Jessu Lawmsangzuala (40) who was found severely injured on Saturday morning at 5:00am at Sialsuk succumbed to his injuries the same day at 1:30pm. He was suspected to be assaulted and Sialsuk Police apprehended the two suspected persons for interrogation.

 

 

B. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 1.4.2014

Mamit Market Looted: On Saturday night, the upper floor of the building Mamit Field Veng Market where clothes, jewellery, electronics and cosmetics were sold was looted by unknown miscreants and stole a number of materials/items from the shops. Mamit Village Council lodged and FIR to Mamit Police Station and Police are investigating the case.

 

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 1.4.2014

Murderer Confessed: Lalremruata, the murderer of C.Vanlalduha who was found dead in the morning of 28.3.2014 near the Jhum house at Lungding forrest where he stayed the night with Lalremruata, confessed to the Police on Sunday night. Lalremruata said that he killed him for being lazy and drunk all the time and for not doing any work.
