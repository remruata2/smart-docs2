Refugee zingah hian Myanmarese National (Kawl) family 15 vel an la awm ve a, a bak zawng chu Chin hnam leh Khumi hnamte an nideuh tlangpui. Anmahni enkawltu deuh hi United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) te an ni.

COVID-19 thlen hma leh Lockdown neih hma chhung zawng khan ei leh in ah engmah harsatna an neilova, amaherawhchu, lockdown hman a nih a, inkharkhip hun tun thleng a hman a la nih avangin ei leh in ah harsatna an tawk nasa hle tawh tih Refugee committee hruaitute hnen atangin hriat a ni.
