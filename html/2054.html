BURGLARY OF BDO's OFFICE, ZAWLNUAM, MAMIT DISTRICT

 

Dt. 13th August.2012:

On 09.08.2012 (Thursday) between 4:00pm to 6:40pm, BDO's office Zawlnuam has been burgled by unknown miscreants and cash amounting to 198.47 lakhs was reported stolen. A police team led by SP, Mamit and Forensic team from Aizawl immediately rushed to Zawlnuam and after preliminary investigation, C.Lalfakzuala (47) s/o Lalhmingthanga of Borai, a Cashier working in BDO's office was picked up for interrogation on 10.08.2012.

 

The stolen money was learnt to be a development fund meant for Zawlnuam RD Block which were sanctioned under various scheme during March to May 2012, and the amount mentioned above was as per the initial version of the Cashier.

 

The Cashier after sustained interrogation by the Police, had disclosed to the Police that he along with other accomplices from Borai Village connivance with some Tripura based criminals having UG background were involved in the burglary. He further disclosed to the Police that the Cashier had master-minded the whole burglary plot in order to conceal his earlier misappropriation of Government money.

 

Following revelation by the Cashier, the Police had eventually arrested the under mentioned three (3) accomplices from Borai on the night of 12.08.2012:-

1. Zodinliana (42) s/o Chala

2. Laltlanmawia @ Tlantea s/o RK Thianga (L)

3. Lalthlamuana (51) s/o Banthanga

 

It is reported that other seven (7) accomplices from Tripura who were known to be a Debarman and Brus are still at large as they reportedly crossed over to Tripuara side along with the stolen money immediately after committing the crime. A massive operation is on and neighbouring Tripura Police as well as BSF has been alerted.

Last night, i.e. dt.22.08.2012, Mizoram Police operation party assisted by OC, Khedacherra PS, launched an overnight operation at Monacherra Village, Tripura and arrested the following suspects who were reportedly involved in the sensational burglary case of Zawlnuam BDO office on the night of 9th August, 2012 at Zawlnuam. The operation party recovered around Rs. 42.00 lakhs from different locations within Monacherra Village area reportedly buried by the arrested persons :-

1) Binod Kumar Reang (30)

3) Kantarai Reang (55)

The Mizoram Police operation party had earlier recovered Rs. 12.00 lakhs from the garden of Pianvula at Borai and Rs. 98,000.00 from Kanhmun.
