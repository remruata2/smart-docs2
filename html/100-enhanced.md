****CHRONOLOGY OF EVENTS ON THE CLASHES BETWEEN MYANMAR ARMY AND ARAKAN ARMY ALONG INDO-MYANMAR BORDER****

****Dt. 3.5.2018 :** On 3rd May 2018 at around 9:10 am, there was a gun fight between cadres of Arakan Army led by SS Captain Dinja, numbering around 200-250 and Myanmar Army in between Kumbrang village and Longmasu village on the bank of Kaladan River, 2 km north of Kon Pyin village, around 13 kilometres from Mizoram border. According to information received, this gun fight resulted in the death of 4 Myanmar Army personnel (including two officers) and 6 to 8 Myanmar Army personnel were also injured. Myanmar Army retrieved the dead bodies of their colleagues on the next day i.e. 4th May 2018 by sending local civilians to the spot. There has been no casualty from Arakan Army side, report said. It is also reported that the Myanmar Army are preparing for another heavy operation against Arakan Army before rainy season.**

**Dt. 14.5.2018 :**On 14. 5.2018 @ 8:30 am, there was another gun fight between Myanmar Army and Arakan Army in Kumbrang Village area, Myanmar adjacent to Border Pillar 12, 7/8 kms. from Myanmar-Mizoram border, on the banks of river Kolodyne. As per input received, in this encounter there were 8 casualties with more than 30 wounded including Battalion Commander from Myanmar Army side while 2 cadres of Arakan Army sustained minor injuries. This gun fight lasted till 12:30 pm where there was a short lull after which the fight was resumed. This encounter was intensified by the intention of Myanmar Army to recover their fallen comrades and they managed to achieve partial success by retrieving some of their injured/dead bodies late in the night. The injured Myanmar Army personnel were sent to Taron Aing on the morning of 15.5.2018. As a result of this gun fight, Arakan Army seized several weapons with huge amount of ammunitions from Myanmar Army.
