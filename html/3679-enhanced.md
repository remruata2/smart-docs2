1. _Front Page of The Aizawl Post Daily Newspaper Dt.15.09.2022_

**Heartwarming act of kindness :**

 On Wednesday i.e 14.09.2022, Neihloh Branch YMA members in a heartwarming act of kindness voluntarily cleaned up the Jhum of a 60 years old poor man who is bedridden due to an accident living with wife and three young grand children in their village. Around 50 YMA members participated in this charity works in which the Constituency MLA Pu Lalduhoma was also present.

****Heroin Seized**: CID Special Branch Operation Team under Mizoram Police on 14th Sept, 2022 seized 1.292 kgs (hawng 99) of suspected Heroin at Seling town from one Baleno Car driven by Kamal Hossen (27) of Sonammura, Nalchar, Tripura.**
