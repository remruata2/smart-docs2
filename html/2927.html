SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/129

Dated: 20th February, 2019.

 

 

Information received indicated that yesterday, i.e. 19.2.2019 @ 4:30 p.m, 1st Bn. Assam Rifles duty at Lopu village, Siaha District produced 2 (two) persons namely, (i) Haolin (20) s/o Saikhin of Konbi and (ii) Thuanthun (18) s/o Aunju, both belonging to Paletwa District, Myanmar suspected as members of Chin National Front (CNF) at Phura Police Station. The Assam Rifles personnel also produced the following which were seized from the 2 (two) suspects :–

 

  1. 3 (three) nos. of detonator with wire
  2. Myanmar currency v/o Kyat 2,20,300/-
  3. Indian currency v/o Rs. 3,940/-
  4. 2 (two) nos. of Mobile Handset (Vivo 1606 & Vivo 1707)
  5. 2 (two) nos. of wrist watch
  6. 1 (one) no. of memory card
  7. 1 (one) no. of AK-47 Rifle ammunition
  8. 2 (two) nos. of M16 Rifle ammunitions

 

The 2 (two) arrested persons are being transported to Siaha for further investigation while writing this report.
