            With reference to your order no. mentioned above, threat perception assessment was conducted and the report of this assessment are as under:-

 

 

1. Pu Liansangzuala :

            As a special judge of ND&PS, Pu Liansangzuala had made a number of exemplary judgement on ND&PS related cases against drug traffickers, etc. Owing to these judgements and the nature of his sensitive work, he has been provided one PSO – Nk. Lalnunsanga of 3rd Bn. MAP. Considering his designation, it is suggested that he may retain his PSO.

 

 

2. Pi R. Vanlalchami :

            Pi R.Vanlalchami, the present CJM Aizawl has been holding this post since March 2015. Previously she was posted in Lunglei as CJM, Lunglei District during 2008-2011.

            As CJM, various criminal cases, petty, serious or sensational cases has been dealt by her. As per Court procedure, all criminal cases are put up before her as a CJM where she again transferred them to other appropriate judges for trail.

            At the moment, no immediate threat is found to exist against her personally and she herself could not mentioned any in particular.

            However, considering the nature of her job as a female Chief Judicial Magistrate and holding such sensitive post, it is recommended that she may be provided with a PSO.
