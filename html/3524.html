A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 24.08. 2022

Police handed over 300 assault rifles to MRHGCD : In a function held yesterday i.e 23.8.2022 at Arya Mess, Aizawl, Mizoram police handed over 300 assault rifles to Mizoram Home Guards & Civil Defence (MRHGCD) along with a considerable rounds of ammunitions. The assault rifles includes 200 Self Loading Rifle (SLR) and 100 Indian Small Arms System (INSAS) along with four magazines for each rifles. In this programme, relevant documents for the handing over of the rifles were signed by Pu John Neihlaia IGP (Hqrs.) from Mizoram police and Maj. Zosangliana Hualngo Commandant General, MRHGCD in the presence of Mizoram Home Minister, Pu Lalchamliana.
