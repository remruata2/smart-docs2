Starred Question No. 259 :

 

a)       Mizoram chhungah Mizoram Police Department-te hian Arms and Ammunition kum 2009-2013 chhung hian engzatnge an man tawh?

 

Ans:               Mizoram chhungah Mizoram Police Department-te hian kum 2009-2013 chhungin Arms 239 leh Ammunitions 5504 an man tawh.

 

b)       Arms & Ammunition hi man an neih chuan engzatnge an la kawl a, engzatnge an tih ral tawh?

 

Ans:               Arms &Ammunition man anih hian a manna hmun (place of occurrence) bialtu Police Station OC ten case siamin chhuihna an thawk thin a. Case chhui dan, chhui zawh anih leh nih loh chungchang CID(SB) unit hian engmah kan hre zui ve lova, DIG(NR/SR) Crime Branch emaw, Aizawl Court Prosecution Branch ten an chhang thei ang.

 

c)       An tiral anih chuan, tu hnenah nge an pek, an hming leh address min hrilh thei em?

 

Ans:               He thu pawh hi Aizawl Court prosecution Brach ten an chhang thei ang.
