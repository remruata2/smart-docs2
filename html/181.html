ASSESSMENT OF THREAT PERCEPTION IN RESPECT OF JUDGE, SPECIAL COURT, ND & PS ACT/PRESIDING OFFICER MACT,  ADDL. DISTRICT AND SESSION JUDGES OF AIZAWL AND CHIEF JUDICIAL MAGISTRATES.

 

Pu K.L.Liana, Judge, Special Court, ND & PS Act/Presiding Officer MACT: At the moment, no immediate threat is found against him in connection with his position as a Judge, Special Court, ND & PS Act/Presiding Officer MACT. During the assessment, he told the Inquiry Officers that he did not received any threat from either convicted persons or their families.

 

Pu Vanlalmawia, Addl. District and Session Judge of Aizawl : Pu Vanlalmawia, the present Addl. District and Session Judge, Aizawl  also could informed the Inquiry Officer that so far he did not received any specific threatening remarks verbally, written or through telephone. He felt unnecessary to allot any PSO at the moment.

 

Pu Lalbiakzama, Addl. District and Session Judge of Aizawl : Pu Lalbiakzama, being an Addl. District and Session Judge of Aizawl, has been dealing with various serious cases in his capacity. Although, he sometime felt uncomfortable, no threat has been received by him as a result of his judgments. He also felt unnecessary to provide PSO at the moment.

 

Pi R.Lalduhawmi, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Champhai District : Pi R.Lalduhawmi, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Champhai District also did not received any verbal/written threatening remarks due to her Court judgment. But, in view of the sensitivity of the work of CJM, Champhai District and being a woman Judicial Officer holding such sensitive post, it is recommended that she may be provided 1 (one) PSO.

 

Pu F.Rohlupuia, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Mamit District: Pu F.Rohlupuia, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Mamit District also informed the Inquiry Officer that  so far he did not receive any threat from any convict,etc . He also felt unnecessary to provide PSO at the moment.

 

Pu Lalngaihmawia Zote, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Serchhip District : Pu Lalngaihmawia Zote, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Serchhip District mentioned that no imminent threat on his security is perceived at the moment.

 

Pu Liansangzuala, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Kolasib District : Pu Liansangzuala, Chief Judicial Magistrate of Kolasib District  informed the Inquiry Officer that he did not received any  imminent threat  for his personal safety and  security . However, Inquiry Officer recommended that he may be provided 1 (one) PSO in view of the sensitivity of the work of CJM,Kolasib
