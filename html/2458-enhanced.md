****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/721

Dated 16th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/719 Dt. 15.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 9 (nine) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-3, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 16.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 6 (six) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 6) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 18 (eighteen) vehicles (HMV – 12, MMV – 6, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 16.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.6 (six) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 6) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 Construction of BSF Camp with a length of 45 ft X 18 ft at Saihapui ‘V’ was started on 15.12.2020 by engaging labour behind Saihapui ‘V’ field opposite to the present Camp. Situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

69 (sixty nine) vehicles (HMV-22, MMV – 4, LMV – 38, Maxi Cab- 5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 16.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 125 (one hundred and twenty five) vehicles (HMV – 58, MMV – 25, LMV –13, LPG – 10, Maxi Cab – Nil, Oil Tanker – 19, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 16.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
