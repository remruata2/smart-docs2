2.         On 14.6.2004@6:50am, about 47 persons travelling in 2 trucks were kidnapped at Khanpui in Zampui Hills, Tripura which is about 11/2 km from Zomuantlang in Mizoram while proceeding to New Kawnpui for shopping by suspected NLFT militants. 14 passengers all of tribals like Reang etc were immediately released but held hostage the remaining passengers of about 33 persons mainly of plains men.

            The kidnappers were reportedly wearing camouflage uniforms and armed with Carbine-1, SLR-4, AK Rifle-4 and Pistol-1. The kidnappers were 12 in number and were speaking in Debbarman, Hindi, Bru and Paite language and, therefore, strongly suspected to be NLFT militants. The militants with the hostages are likely to proceed to Bangladesh which is only a walking distance from the place of occurrence.
