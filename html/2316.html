MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/498

Dated 1st July, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/493
Dt. 29.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

           17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-9, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 1.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

24 (twenty four) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-19, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-12, MMV-1, LMV-8, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

Today i.e., 1.7.2021 @ 12:00 Noon, Mizoram Police erected tent at the edge of Pu Chhandama’s farm land, which is around 300 metres from their earlier camp at Darthantluanga’s farm land, towards Lalramhluna’s farm land and closer to Assam Police makeshift camp at Pu Chhandama’s farm land. On being learnt about Mizoram Police activity by Assam Police, the latter rushed to the spot and there was a scuffle between Assam Police and Mizoram Police this afternoon around 2:30 p.m. and Assam Police who heavily outnumbered Mizoram Police dismantled the tent, and as a result of which, Mizoram Police retreated to the above mentioned Pu Darthantluanga’s farm land. Due to the said incident, Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib rushed to the area and necessary reinforcement was made. 

Information received also stated that Assam started construction of tin-roof building for Assam Forest Department  at Pu Ramhluna’s farm land.

140 (one hundred forty) vehicles (HMV-79, MMV-12, LMV-35, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-1, Oil Tanker-12, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
