SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/42

Dated: 20th January, 2019.

 

 

Reference to our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2019/37, dated 16.1.2019: In connection with the impending stay of all construction works under Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP) by Lawngtlai District Land Owners’ Association (LDLOA) from 20.1.2019, Pu Neihchungnunga, IPS, DIG (CID/SR) held a meeting at Circuit House, Lawngtlai yesterday, i.e. 19.1.2019 @ 4:15 p.m with leaders of LDLOA and Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) namely,

LDLOA :                                                        CYLA :          

  1. H. Remmawia (President)                i)         J. Sangthangpuia (President)
  2. H. Lalnunsanga (Gen. Secy.)            ii)        H.C. Vanlalruatpuia (V. President)
  3. A. Hmingthangzuala (Asst. Secy.) iii)         Joseph Lalhmingthanga (Asst. G.S)
  4. N.C. Nguntlunga (Adviser)   iv)        Lalrampana (Fin. Secy.)

           

DIG (CID/SR) was assisted by Pu Amit Goel, IPS, SP, Lawngtlai District;
Pu Lalmuanpuia, MPS, CO, 2nd Bn. MAP and Pu V.L. Lura Hauzel, MPS, Addl. SP, Lawngtlai District.

 

The meeting elaborately discussed about the intended staying of the ongoing construction works of KMMTTP by LDLOA w.e.f. 20.1.2019 due to their grievances over prolong pending of disbursement of their land compensation claims within the area of KMMTTP by the Government. The meeting also appraised the lack of time for the newly formed State Government to consider and address their grievances and the obstacles prevailing in the compensation claims of the land owners. Consequently, the meeting concluded with LDLOA conceding to defer their agitation till 1.2.2019 to give the Government time to study the grievances of LDLOA and look into the matter.

 

Situation under close watch.
