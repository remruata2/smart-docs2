REPORT ON MIZORAM BORDER DEFENCE ARMY(MBDA)

Introduction:

Pu Lalhmingthanga Sanate, Founder President of Hmar People Convention (Democratic), presently residing with his family members at Hmarkhawlian, Cachar District,Assam (who refused to come over ground during the Peace Accord signed by HPC(D) and government of Mizoram in the year 2018) held a meeting with RC Singson @ Ramchanlova (former Army Chief of HPC(D)) of Thenzawl, Mizoram on 15.12.2021 at Hmarkhawlian, Assam in connection with the tense inter-state border situation. After discussing the matter at length, the duo decided to raise new outfit in the name of “Mizoram Border Defence Army (MBDA)” with the following plan of actions:

  1. To launch attack againstAssam Police deployed along Assam-Mizoram border with Arms.
  2. Non-mizo residing along the inter-state border should be threatened/intimidated.
  3. Sufficient number of arms and ammunitions should be procured from National Socialist Council of Nagaland –NSCN (IM).
  4. Donation should be collected from residents of Saiphai, Mizoram and its surrounding areas for procurement of arms and ammunitions. 

The meeting appointed RC Singson @ Ramchanlova Singson as Commissioner/Commander-in-Chief, and Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea was appointed as Commander, Saiphai area and John Lalhmangaihsanga of NC Hills as Commander, Tuiruang valley, Assam.

As per input, the present Arms holding of HPC (D) are as follows:

  1. AK-47 – 10 (ten) Nos.
  2. .32 Pistol – 1 (one) No.

The above stated Arms are kept under the custody of Pu Lalhmingthanga Sanate at Hmarkhawlian, Cachar District, Assam.

Activities of Mizoram Border Defence Army (MBDA):

            In pursuance of the above stated Meeting’s decision, RC Singson, Commissioner/Commander -in-Chief of MBDA left Hmarkhawlian, Cachar District, Assam on 18.12.2021 for Saiphai. He along with the following persons served ‘Demand Letter’ using HPD (D) Letter Head to Non-Mizo residents of Chemphai areas on 23/24.12.2021 and Saiphai and its surrounding areas on 25/26.12.2021:

  1. Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea - SS Commander
  2. Lalhriatzuala, Saiphai (civilian)
  3. Jamala(Vai) - SS Pvt. MBDA, Saipum.
  4. Basira, civilain (Address not known)
  5. Zodawlliana @ Dawla of Saiphai (civilian)

 

The above mentioned persons carried .32 Pistol and Miniature (.22 Rifle) mounted with Telescope while serving the ‘Demand Letter’. They had collected Rs. 52,000/- as follows:

                1.         Non-mizos (based on Demand Letter) – Rs. 35,000/-

Arrest of Cadre of MBDA:

            As per instruction of RC Singson, Commissioner/Commander -in-Chief of MBDA the following persons proceeded to Phailianzau, Saiphai area on 25.1.2022 to collect money in pursuance of ‘Demand Letter’ served:

                1.         Lalngaisanga @ Sangtea, Commander, MBDA

            2.         Jamala, SS Pvt. MBDA of Saipum

            3.         Basira (civilian), Address not known.

            4.         Lalhriatzuala (civilian), Saiphai

            5.         Sunday Lalrinsanga (civilian), Saiphai

 

They carried 1(one) no. of AK-47 and one .32 Pistol. The following persons were nabbed by Mizoram Police while trying to collect money from the Non-mizos:(1) Lalhriatzuala, Saiphai and Sunday Lalrinsanga, Saiphai and also seized 1(one) no. of AK-47 Magazine and 3(three) rounds; Mobile Phone - 4 (four) nos. and 3(three) rounds of Miniature (.22Rifle) from their possession. However, the below mentioned persons managed to evade arrest:

  1. Lalngaisanga @Sangtea, Commander, MBDA
  2. Jamala, SS Pvt. MBDA of Saipum
  3. Basira (civilian), Address unknown.

 

 In connection with the above incident, Vairengte PS C/No.3/2022 u/s 395 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act Dt. 26.1.2022  has been registered and the above stated 2(two) arrested persons were remanded into Judicial Custody on 29.1.2022. 

           

Arrest of RC Singson, Commissioner/Commander -in-Chief of MBDA:

            Mizoram Police has been putting all-out effort to arrest the 3(three) absconding accused and RC Singson, Commissioner/Commander -in-Chief of MBDA. In this connection, they laid trap on 27.1.2022 and RC Singson, Commissioner/Commander -in-Chief of MBDA was arrested by Mizoram Police the same day, and after conducting thorough interrogation, he was remanded into Judicial custody on 29.1.2022. However, the above mentioned 3 (three) absconding accused persons are still on the run and attempts have been made for their immediate arrest. It is suspected that their hideout is within Assam territory.

Conclusion and Suggestions:

            The outfit named ‘Mizoram Border Defence Army (MBDA) was raised and coined by LalhmingthangaSanate, founder President, HPC (D) in his attempt to take personal advantage    of the recently tensed Assam-Mizoram inter-state border situation.  It may be worth mentioning that investigation of the above mentioned case revealed that cadre of ‘Mizoram Border Defence Army (MBDA) implanted explosives attached with detonator (approx. 2 kgs) which was recovered near the construction site of Bridge connecting Pu Chalchhunga’s farm land to Pu Lalnuntluanga’s farm land across Saihapui river, Phainuam-Buarchep area on 6.11.2021 @10:25 a.m.

            It is suggested that immediate arrest of Lalhmingthanga Sanate, founder President, HPC (D) and the absconding accused is the need of the hour as they may pose threat to internal security of both states viz. Mizoram and Assam, and Non-mizos residing along the inter-state border and Assam Police deployed along the inter-state border.
