A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 21.7.2022

A reply from the government being awaited by High Court: After the collapsed of a building during the renovation process of Lengpui Airport on 10th March 2022, Gauhati high Court, Aizawl Bench called an explanation from the state government, a reply of which is still being awaited by High Court. The order issued by the High Court demanded Mizoram Government to clarify the following points :

i)       The exact date of commencement of work and the date of issuing of Work- Order to the contractor, Kamlova Chhangte.

ii)      The total amount of funds released to the contractor till date.

iii)     The registration date of Kamlova Chhangte as class I,II and III contractor under Mizoram Government.

iv)     The eligibility of class I, II and III contractors for works whose estimated values are above Rs. 7,000 lakhs under Mizoram Government.

v)      Whether there is an architectural plan for the said construction.

vi)     To submit the architectural plan to the Court if there is any and to verify if  the contractor is working in accordance to it.

vii)    Whether the architectural plan is approved by a structural engineer and the name of the engineer if it was approved.

         

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspapers  Dt.21.7. 2022

Rs. 18,95,585 as relief fund: The relief funds donated by 117 churches within Aizawl city under Mizoram Synod for floods victims of Barak valley, Assam has reached Rs. 18,95,585. The Mizoram Synod had previously donated Rs. 8,00,00 in advance for floods victims in Cachar areas and the remaining balance of Rs. 10,95,585 will be donated again in the near future.
