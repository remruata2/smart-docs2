A. _Front page of VANGLAIN newspaper Dt. 28.09.2022_

**Departments summoned due to non implementation of RTI ACT**: Yesterday i.e 27.9.2022, ten (10) Government Departments under Mizoram Government were summoned by Mizoram Information Commission (MIC) for not properly implementing section 4(1)(b) of RTI Act, 2005. Meanwhile, another 10 Department heads are to be summoned today (28.9.2022) for the same reason. The summoned department officials appeared before Pu Lalnunmawia Chuaungo, Chief Information Commissioner and Pu Zorammawia, Information Commissioner who gave them a briefing on RTI Act and its implementations .

 As per section 4(1)(b) of RTI Act, 2005, various departments under the government are expected to provide certain information for the public through their respective website (suo moto disclosure) such as - contact numbers of department appellate authority (DAA) &amp; State Public Information Officer (SPIOA), scheme and ongoing programmes of the department, act/rules/regulations published by the department, beneficiary list under the department, department budget and other relevant information for the public.

B._Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.09.2022_

**Areca nuts and wildlife seized:** A joint team of Mamit police and members of Hachek Areca Nut Association on 16th September 2022 seized 187 bags (14,960 kgs) of illegal areca nuts which were smuggled from Myanmar at Kawrthah town and Kanhmun town. Six vehicles used for transporting these areca nuts were also detained. The estimated value of these seized items are around Rs.32 lakhs.

 Meanwhile, police personnel on duty at Saiphai police outpost (Kolasib district) on 25.9.2022 recovered 19 different exotic wildlife species which were illegally transported from one vehicle. The driver of the vehicle, Birhan Uddin laskar (24) Channighat, Cachar, Assam and his three accomplice, Kamal Hussain (34),Karimganj , Abdul Ruf (25) and Niaz Hussain(21) Bagha Bazar were also apprehended.
