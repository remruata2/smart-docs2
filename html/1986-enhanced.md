**Brief History :**

 A chunga mi khi ka particulars tlangpui chu a ni. Kum 1978 khan Thaidor ah ka lo pianga, Pianpui unau mipa pathum leh farnu pahnih ka nei a ni. Naisingpara a kan awm hnu hian kum 2006 BNLF ten Mizoram Sorkar a an Surrender dawn khan lut vein Naisingpara Camp bulah Thla khat vel training runpui an tih lai khan ka training ve a. Insurrender zawh hnuah let lehin kum 2011 kum tawp lam khan Duhawma hovin NLFT kan zawm ve ta a ni.

Kan zawmhnu hian Bangladesh ah awmin kan hotuten min tirhnaah kan kal kual reng thin a. Puakphurh leh Lo lam hna kan thawk ber thin a ni. Kan Demand area chhunga khaw hrang hrang te Sum demand chungchangah kan Hruaitute nen kan in ti thiam ta vak lo va, sum hmangsualah te min puh a, abik takin Sektaraipa, Home in-charge nen kan in hmuhkhawloh chhoh avangin kum 2013 December thla khan Bangladesh atang ka chhuak a, bang tawp ni si lo in Bangladesh a kir tawh chuang si lovin Naisingpara Refugee Camp velah ka awm ta vel a ni.

Tlem lai deuh kum 2014 kumtir lam khan in surrender dan tur leh kan case a fel theihna tur ngaihtuahin mi chi hrang hrang ka be thin a, engemaw chen hnu ah Zorama @ Noiboram nen kan intawng fuh a, a ni hian HPC-D te nen a thawh ho dan tur leh kan case chinfel dan tur ten en min ngaihtuahpui theih dawn thu min hrilh a. Dt. 12.7.2014 ah kan thlenna Bani Hotel, Silchar ah kan inhmukhawm ta a, heta kan inhmuhkhawm lai hian Mizoram Police (CID (SB)) te chuan min lo man ta a ni. Min man rual hian Aizawl min panpui nghal a, Aizawl kan thlen hian Aizawl Thana ah min dahlut nghal a ni.
