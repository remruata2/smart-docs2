1. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 22.7.2013_

**Procession to be held**: Saitual District Demand Committee intended to organize a peaceful procession at Saitual town area today. In the General Election Campaign of 2008, Rahul Gandhi the then AICC Gen. Secy., now the Vice President AICC had promised that, 'The stillborn District Headquarters announced by the MNF government shall be made fully functional by the Congress government'; and to urge the government to let Saitual district function, procession will be organized. Saitual Town Youth Organisation fully supports the initiative of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee and will join the procession.

1. _Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 22.7.2013_

**Habitual offender arrested**: An alleged habitual robber Kaplianmanga(25) s/o Thangkhenpianga of Hnahlan was arrested by Village Council members of Ngur village on 18th July 2013 and handed over to Champhai Police Station. Champhai Police said that Kaplianmanga and his wife had robbed around 13 houses at Ngur village, and had stolen some amount of money from the occupants of Hnahlan village. They also tried to rob Mizoram Rural Bank Hnahlan Branch, but failed.
