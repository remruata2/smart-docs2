****ABDUCTION OF SIX ABCI OFFICIALS FROM BETWEEN NEW SACHAN AND MAUZAM VILLAGES, LUNGLEI DISTRICT****

****ON****25.3.2012****

 On the night of 25.3.2012, 6 (six) ABCI employees namely,

(1) Pushpa Sharma, Manager,

(2) Pawan Singh, Supervisor of Punjab,

(3) Sunil Singh, Supervisor of Punjab,

(4) Shiya Ram, Supervisor of Rajasthan,

(5) Purna Ram, Supervisor of Rajasthan,

(6)Tiklu, Excavator Operator have been abducted at gun-point by National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) from their camp in between New Sachan and Mauzam villages located at 3 kms. from Mauzam and 7 kms. from west of New Sachan under Bunghmun Police Station of Lunglei District. The abductors numbering about 20-25, armed with sophisticated weapons likeAK-47 and wearing black colour uniform took them away to Bangladesh. In this connection, Tlabung PS C/No.3/12 dt.30.3.12 u/s 363/395 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act has been registered.

     The abductors has made telephonic contact the ABCI officials through unidentified agent on 3.4.2012 and demanded that 50 lakhs be given as first installment of ransom whereas the balance amount would be given later on.

 The abductees were released on 30.4.2012 after 35 days long captivity inside Bangladesh. The 6 (six) ABCI abductees after their release at the border reached BSF camp at Rajiv Nagar (tuipuibari), Mamit District @ 1:00 pm on 30.4.2012 where pre-medical check up was done, and their health conditions were reported to be fine. They were evacuated to Aizawl on 30.4.2012 itself by BSF for thorough medical check up, and debriefing was done at Aizawl.
