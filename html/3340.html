A. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 12.9.2013

Cabinet Meeting: A meeting of Council of Ministers held at CS Conference Hall yesterday presided over by Chief Minister Pu Lal Thanhawla agreed amongst others to give back Ration Allowance and Leave Compensatory Allowance taken from Inspector of Police. It also agreed to create one post of Dy.SP at Directorate General of Mizoram Home Guard. It also allowed one functional post of MPS Senior Administrative Grade of DIG (Armed Branch).

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 12.9.2013

Intention to block National Highway & organise Total Bandh: NGO of Bilkhawthlir Tuithaveng and representatives of each family will wait for the State Government till Thursday to fix the problem of no good/usable transformer in their village. And they intend to organise Bandh on Friday and block National Highway-54.

            Lai Student Association Sub-Headquarters Bungtlang ‘S’ also intends to call on Total Bandh on 16th September 2013 as the government pays deaf ear to their demand of posting a teacher at Government Bungtlang ‘S’ High School. If their demand is not fulfilled, they intend to boycott the upcoming MLA General Election.            

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.9.2013

Total Bandh at Saitual: Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) intends to organise a 12 hours Total Bandh today ie, 12th September 2013 at Saitual area from 5:00 AM to 5:00 PM to urge the government to function Saitual as a District Headquarters which was declared on 2008.
