SEQUENCE OF EVENTS LEADING TO THE ARREST OF TWO HPC(D) LEADERS AND AS TO HOW THEY ARE BOOKED:

            On Dt. 8th June, 2012, one vital intelligence input was received about the movement plan of HPC(D) leaders namely, 1) Lalropuia S/S Army Chief and 2) Bieknung S/S Dy. Army Chief from Churachandpur to Vangai hills to visit their training camp at an undisclosed location in Vangai hills for which Ropui had instructed his cadre (name not known) to arrange air tickets from Imphal to Silchar for dated 10th,11th or 12th, June, 2012. It was later learnt that Ropuia had confirmed the ticket for 10th of June, 2012 as he was also scheduled to have a secret meeting with some Bru leaders at Hmarkhawlian on Dt.12th June, 2012.

            It was also learnt that the HPC(D) leaders were planning to hold a meeting with  leaders of APLC at Kangrengdawr, Manipur to discuss steps to be taken for the resumption of talks with the Govt. of Mizoram.

            The DGP(M) was immediately briefed about the report and a strategy was worked out to arrest them as instructed by the DGP(M). In the meantime the Home Minister, Govt. of Mizoram had also verbally gave “a go-ahead signal” on this.

            After careful deliberation by officers of CID(SB) and  the SP, Aizawl on Dt. 9th June, 2012, an operation team under the command of Pu C. Lalruaia, Addl. SP, Aizawl District was set up as under:-

            The combined team left Aizawl on the afternoon of 9th June, 2012 and halted for the night at Vairengte instead of proceeding straight to Silchar Airport that evening in order to avoid unnecessary exposure. The team left Vairengte on the early morning of 10th June, 2012 and arrived Silchar Airport around 9:00 AM. The team Commander Pu C. Lalruaia, Addl. SP, Aizawl quickly contacted the CISF Officers(Airport Security) on their arrival at the Airport and briefed them about their operation plans, and also requested their co-operation.The two HPC(D) leaders were arrested immediately after the flight landed at the Silchar Airport and were taken towards Mizoram after having jurisdiction. The arrest, as well as other formalities were done quietly and quickly as planned.
