SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/505

Dated: 4th July, 2019.

 

 

 

            L. Ramdinliana Renthlei, President, MZP Gen. Hqrs. alongwith 3 (three) others met officials of Higher & Technical Education Department, Government of Mizoram as invited by the latter this afternoon, i.e. 4.7.2019 @ 4:00 p.m. It is learnt that the H&TE officials had apprised the MZP leaders of updates on list of Bio-data they have received for MBBS seats which includes 4 (four) Chakmas. H&TE officials also apprised the MZP of the Appeal submitted by Government of Mizoram challenging recent High Court's ruling, and requested them not to resort to any sort of agitation to oppose inclusion of Chakmas in Category-1.

            The MZP, while appreciating the Government's moving of the Apex Court challenging the High Court's order, seemed determined to oppose inclusion of Chakmas in Category-1. Information received stated however that MZP would decide their course of action during coming week.

            Situation under close watch.
