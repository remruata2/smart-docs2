KIDNAPPING CASES COMMITTED BY NLFT/BDFM SINCE 2012

                     The National Liberation Front of Tripura and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram combined have intensified their activities during the recent past in the Indo-Bangladesh border of Mamit District in Mizoram. During the last two years alone, these two combined militant groups had committed as many as 7(seven) times of kidnapping from Mamit District alone and 33(thirty three) held hostage. Most of the kidnapping has been committed by these groups for ransom. Apart from the suffering of hostages and their family members, every kidnapping in this area had caused communal tension between Brus and Mizos which is a big headache for the Government. The Bru militant group, with the help of NLFT had temporary camp at Sazek range of Bangladesh bordering Dampa Tiger Reserve Sanctuary of Mamit District, Mizoram and most of their kidnap victims are Mizos and non-Mizos working in this border area.

                     Some of the sensational kidnapping cases during the last 2(two) years in this area are as under:

1. Case Reference : West Phaileng Police Station Case No.04/13 dt. 20.2.2013 u/s 364 (A) /34 IPC r/w 25 (1A) Arms Act.

Brief fact of the case : On 18.2.2013, the following Environment & Forest Department, Government of Mizoram Muster Roll workers namely

1)Vanlalruatkima(28), Hunthar Veng, Aizawl

2)Lalrammawia(22), Hunthar Veng, Aizawl

3)Vanlalfaka, Forest Department MR(BLFM returnee) Dampa Rengpui

4) Lalliansanga, Teirei Forest

5) Sangmawia, Bethlehem Veng, West Phaileng

were abducted from Chikha Camp in between Tuipuibari & Damparengpui by NLFT assisted by Bru armed group who called themselves as Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram(BNDLFM). After prolonged pressure and persuasive method being used by Government of Mizoram in close co-ordination with Mizos living in Bangladesh, the hostages were freed by the captors at Indo-Bangla border village called Silsury on 29.3.2013.

 

Current Status :

 (i)All the hostages were released after held in captivity for 39 days on 29.3.2013.

 (ii)Chargesheet had already been submitted vide CS No.10/13 dt 18.6.2013

2. Case Reference  : West Phaileng Police Station Case No. 10/13 dt.6.6.2013 U/S 364(A) /34 IPC r/w 25(1) Arms Act

Brief fact of the case : On 5.6.2013 at around 10:00 AM, three (3) persons of Gautam Construction Company workers namely (i) Nizam Uddin(28) of Karimganj (ii)Mitun Bagani(18) of Silchar (iii)Dairgudon  Chakma (30) @Sanjoy of Rajiv Nagar were abducted by a combined militants group of NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) and BNDLFM (Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from near Amchuri Mukh Border Outpost of BSF /  around 5 Kms south of Rajiv Nagar.

       Further report received stated that the family of the hostages after prolong negotiation paid Rs.3 lakhs and seven mobile handsets as a ransom for safe release of the hostages. The two captives were handed over to Mizoram Police by BSF personnel at West Phaileng Police Station on 11.11.2013.

Current Status :

(i) One hostage namely Sanjoy Chakma was released on the night of 24.6.2013 from Udalsury village near Bangla-India border and reached BSF Camp, Rajiv Nagar safely on 25.6.2013 @ 11:00 am and on  10.11.2013, the other two abducted labours were released by the abductors.

(ii)The reason of pendency of investigation of the case is to arrest more accused persons.

3. Case Reference  : West Phaileng Police Station Case No.24 /2013 Dt.25.11.2013 u/s 364 A/379/34 IPC r/w Sec 25(1A) Arms Act

Brief fact of the case : On 23.11.2013, members of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru National Development Liberation Front of Mizoram(BNDLFM) abducted three persons namely (i) Deep Mandal S/o Nikhil Mandal, Indas, Bakura, West Bengal (ii) Sanglianthanga @ Maliana S/o VL Hmuaka, Mamit Charkawn, (ii) Lalzamliana S/o Buanga (L) C/o K.Vanlalchhuanga, Zuangtui Muanna Veng, Aizawl from Old Chikha Anti-poaching Camp area within Dampa Sanctuary, Mamit District. The three hostages were taken to the hideout of the militants in Bangladesh. The abductors had demanded Rs. 5 Crore ransom for the release of the hostages.

Current Status :

(i)On 20.1.2014, two of the three abducted persons namely Sanglianthanga and Lalzamliana were handed over to CID(SB) source stationed at Lungtian, Bangladesh and safely arrived in Mizoram on 21.1.2014. Deep Mandal was also released on 22.3.2014 after he was held captive inside Bangladesh for 119 days.

(ii)The reason of pendency of investigation of the case is to arrest more accused persons.

4. Case Reference  : West Phaileng Police Station Case No. 12/14 Dt. 15.6.2014 u/s 392/ 364 A/34 IPC r/w 25 (1A) (a) Arms Act.

Brief fact of the case : On 14.6.2014 at around 12:30 PM, a combine operation team of suspected National Liberation Front of Tripura(NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram(BDFM) stopped one vehicle bearing Regn.No.TR-023-1949 coming from Ragiv Nagar, Mamit District at about 4/5 Kms before Zozampui 5th IR Bn BOP. The occupants of the vehicle were

(i)Ranju Nath (47) S/o Noresh Nath Dosda, Kanchanpur,North Tripura

(ii)Nikunjoy Pukasto (32) Nihar  Rajan Purkesto Dosda, Kanchanpur, North Tripura

(iii) Mona Das (21)S/o Rodindro Kumar  Das, Kanchanpur

(iv) Amal Chakma S/o Subal Chakma, Kanchanpur

(v) Sorbo Sashi Chakma (38) S/o  Giana Mohan , Char Kilo, Khantlang

(iv)Hori Nepali(28), S/o Krishna Bahadur,Kanchanpur.

         When the militants stopped the vehicle, one Mona Das (21) managed to escape from the scene and the rest (five persons) were taken to Jungle. Soon after, Sorbo Sashi Chakma, Amal Chakma and Hori Nepali were released by the abductors and Ranju Nath (47) & Nikunjoy Pukasto (32) were taken as a hostage by the abductors. Input from sources indicated that the abductors with the hostages reached NLFT Transit Camp near Tarumpara on 17.6.2014. It is reported that the abductors have demanded Rs.10 lakhs as a ransom for the two hostages.        

         Nikunjoy Pukasto escaped on 25.6.2014@3:30am. Ranju Nath was released on 3.7.2014 after payment of ransom Rs.4 lakhs.

Current Status :

(i) The reason of pendency of investigation of the case is to arrest more accused persons.

5. Case Reference  : West Phaileng Police Station Case No. 20/14 Dt. 11.10.2014 u/s  364 A/392/34 IPC R/w 25 (1A) Arms Act.

Brief fact of the case : On 10.10.2014 at around 9:30 PM, a combined operation team of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) numbering 12 (twelve) stopped one Tata 207 pick-up vehicle bearing Regn. No. TR-05-1780 in between Zozampui 5th IR BOP  and Rajiv Nagar, Mamit District (at about 3/4 kms away from Zozampui 5th IR BOP and near the tri-juntion i.e. Mizoram-Tripura-Bangladesh border) and abducted 11(eleven) Bengali Muslim persons.

Current Status :

(i) Latest information received stated that leader of the abductors contacted family members of two abducted persons viz. Shahis Ahmed and Jiya Uddin of Patharkandi on 12.10.2014 @ 6a.m and informed their families that the abducted persons were safely in their hands at their (NLFT) Camp inside Bangladesh. The leader of the abductors told the family members that they should collect at least Rs. 30 lakhs and pay to the militant group for the safe return of the hostages and subsequently reduced the demand to Rs.11 lakhs with the fixed deadline of 31.10.2014.

 (ii) Further information received from another source revealed that the hostages along with their abductors reached Tarungpara camp of NLFT near Zopui village of Sazek range, Bangladesh on 12.10.2014 @03:00am.

(iii)   The 11 (eleven) Bengali construction workers abducted by a combined group of National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) on 10.10.2014 were released on 31.10.2014 @03:30am after being held captive inside Bangladesh jungles for 21days.

         It is learnt that families of the hostages had paid Rs.2,75,000/- ( Rupees Two Lakhs Seventy Five Thousand), 6 (six) mobile handsets, 2(two) wrist watches and mobile top-up (Airtel/Aircel) worth Rs.5000/- as ransom for their release.

         The hostages were released at Udulsuri (Chakma village) inside Bangladesh. They were later escorted upto the border fencing near Rajiv Nagar by Bangladesh based Chakma insurgents i.e. JSS (Jana Sangha Samiti) where they were received by 181 Bn. BSF @ 02:00pm. The released hostages are being brought to W.Phaileng by SDPO W.Phaileng for medical check up and other necessary formalities.
