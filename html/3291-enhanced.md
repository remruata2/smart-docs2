**A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.4.3.2013**

**Police arrest 5 UDLF members****:**Five persons namely, Ramliana @ Doinoram (33) s/o Ananta Kumar Reang, Riflemara, Hailakandi, Assam, Martin Luthera (28) s/o Jiruham, Gutguti, Hailakandi, Assam, Johana (34) s/o Saiaram (L), Kolalian, Mamit District, Taubathai (18) s/o Benandra Reang, Doktapur, Hailakandi, Assam and Taurai (18) s/o Baduram, Gutguti, Hailakandi, Assam who appeared to be of an insurgent group – UDLF (United Democratic Liberation Front of Barak Valley) were apprehended by Vairengte Police on the night of 27.2.2013 at Mission Compound, Silchar, they were handed over to Kolasib Police yesterday ie, 3.3.2013.

 General Secretary of UDLF, Sisimony Reang met and persuaded these 5 people to join their group (UDLF) on 3.1.2013 at Gutguti in Hailakandi Dist. He told them they will surrender to Assam Govt. and if they do, they will get Govt. job; these 5 people agreed and left Gutguti for Manipur, on their way to Manipur they were arrested by the Police at Mission Compound, Silchar where they stayed over the night.

**Three men arrested for killing fish****:**Leaders of Sialsuk village, Forest Department and Sialsuk Police arrested Lalpianmawia (26) s/o Lalhmunliana, Lalmuanpuia (28) s/o Lallianmawia and Laltlanmawia (42) s/o Zonunthar all from Serchhip on 3.3.2013. ****District Demand Day****:**Khawzawl District Implementing Demand Committee, NGOs of Khawzawl and Village Council, Khawzawl will be organising District Demand Day in between 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM today ie, 4.3.2013. People of Khawzawl will organise a procession from different localities at 10:00AM onwards and meet together at the market place (Bazar).
