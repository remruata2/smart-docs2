A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.4.2014

Bru Repatriation in the Second Week of May: Mizoram Bru who are staying at transit camp to be repatriated to Mizoram from the second week of May and they are to return to the village where they used to stay before they left Mizoram.

          A meeting regarding Bru repatriation was held at Home Minister Pu R.Lalzirliana’s office chamber yesterday, the meeting made a plan that repatriation commenced from second week of May and that Mamit DC will take care of any activity to be done before hand. The meeting was attended by Home, Finance, Revenue and DP& AR officials along with Mamit, Kolasib and Lunglei DC.

                                                         

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.4.2014

Pseudoephedrine Seized: Kulikawn Police seized 9.690 Kgs. of Pseudoephedrine from the possession of Zomuanpuia (29) s/o Remliana of Melriat yesterday at around 4:00pm. He is now detained at Kulikawn PS. Sources said, Pseudoephedrine was sent from Delhi through post office, and it arrived at Kulikawn Sub-Post office which was collected by Zomuanpuia. Police suspected Zomuanpuia and examined the package and found Pseudoephedrine.

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.4.2014

Fire Service Week Closed: Fire Service Week being observed from 14th April was closed yesterday. The closing ceremony was held at Chanmari YMA Hall and Pu Zorammawia DIG (NR) attended the function.
