B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAMTIMES newspapers Dt. 15.4.2013_

_Mizoram Government & HPC(D) to have a talk on Friday_: Joint Monitoring Group (JMG), formed to monitor the keeping of Suspension of Operation (SoO) between Mizoram Government and HPC(D), will have a meeting this Friday ie, 19.4.2013 at Tourist Lodge in Sakawrdai to discuss whether the agreement has been complied with by both sides.

 Reports said that the meeting will be presided by SP,CID(SB) H.Ramthlengliana, Chairman, JMG, to be attended by Government representatives Addl.SP CID(SB) HL Thangzuala and OSD Home Dept. David Lalthangliana. Francis Songate, and two HPC(D) PROs Lalbeiseia and Sangneihkunga will be the HPC(D) representatives in the meeting. The meeting will discuss about the when and where for the next peace talk will be held and also if Suspension of Operation SoO) is being maintained or not.

 Mizoram Government and HPC(D) had a talk on 31st Jan. this year in Aizawl and resolved Suspension of Operation (SoO) for 6 months. To monitor the resolution, Joint Monitoring Group (JMG) was formed under the chairmanship of SP,CID(SB).

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 15.4.2013 :_

_311 Police recruited without advertisement in Congress government:_ Under congress party governance since December 2008 till 2012, 311 police personnel were recruited without advertisement and without employment exchange acknowledgement. Answering to the questions through RTI Act, Home Dept. SPIO, PC Lalchhuanawma said that from January 2009 to December 2012, newly employed police without Employment Exchange’s recognition were - SI (UB) – 2, Constable – 11, IV grade – 239, Head Constable (MPRO) – 44, Constable (GD) MPRO – 5, Follower (MPRO) – 9 and driver – 1.

 28 persons were recruited in 2009, 23 persons in 2010 and 129 persons in 2011 and 131 persons in 2012. Out of 311 employees, 166 are from Aizawl and 145 from other villages, 3 non-Mizos out of which 2 are from Aizawl and 1 from the state of Tamil Nadu.

 When the photocopies of important documents of these recruited police were asked SPIO said that all employees recruited under Home Dept. was done without publishing advertisement as such it was done by heads of units and there were no important documents to furnish.
