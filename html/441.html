Subject:   Follow up on surrendered militants.

 

Sir,

 

                  With reference to your letter No.D.32020/12/11-HMP dt. 1.8.2011on the above subject, I am furnishing the required information as under for your information and necessary action.

 

 

1.                   a) The Govt. of India, Ministry of Home Affairs made Scheme for Surrender-cum-Rehabilitation of Militants in N.E Region under Security Related Expenditure(SRE) for the North Eastern States in the year 2000 and revised the same in 2005 vide its Letter No.11011/66/98-NE-IV dt. 10.5.2005, a copy of which was sent to all Chief Secretary of the North East. It may be mentioned that the Scheme is not extended to Mizoram and is applicable only in respect of the banned militant organisations and scheme is operated only within the disturbed areas. However, some militant outfits like Bru National Liberation Front(BNLF) and Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram(BLFM) were given rehabilitation under the direction and assistance of the Central Government. Other actions the police could take are to issue Surrender Certificates with the permission of the State Govt. and kept the same for records etc.

 

 

     b) The State Govt. has no its own policy on rehabilitation of surrendered militant cadres.
