Modus operandi

:

Raising funds by forced collection of money either from Bru or non-Bru families and also from company workers in their areas of operation.

 
  1.  

Involvement in heinous crimes so far

:

1) On 30.3.08, masked UDLA Cadres armed with one local made gun extorted Rs.5300/- from one Uddin Barbhuiya at Pangbalkawn, Kolasib, Mizoram. They were later arrested and booked vide Kolasib P/S C/No.28/08 dt. 1.4.08 u/s 395 IPC r/w 25(1B)(a) Arms Act.

2) On 7.5.08, two Bamboo Suppliers of Sahebpara, Assam were abducted by UDLA. They were released on 10.5.08.

3) On 22.4.09, five employees of M/S Sweety Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. were abducted by UDLA Cadres from Bhaicherra, P.S Katlicherra, Assam. Two of them were arrested by Police.

4) On 10.5.09, two employees of M/S Sweety Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. were abducted by UDLA and hence Katlicherra P.S C/No.67/09 u/s 364(A)/34 IPC had been registered. One of the abducted persons escaped from captivity on 27.5.09. The other one was also released unconditionally on the night of 27.5.09.

5) On 6.6.2011, UDLA had kidnapped two employees of ABCI from Meidum, Kolasib District, Mizoram and demanded Rs.5 crores of ransom. They were rescued by Mizoram Police on 16.6.2011 from the outskirt of Banglabhasa village of Hailakandi District in Assam after a gun battle which lasted about 30 minutes.

  1.  

Remarks

:

This armed outfit seem to be not a well organised group like other insurgent groups of the North East and is more likely to be armed gang. They used to collect so-called donation in their operational areas at the rate of Rs.100/- from Bru families and Rs.300/- from non-Bru families annually. Money collected were used for purchasing arms and ammunitions reportedly from Shillong. Even though the kidnapping on 6.6.2011 for ransom was a failure, they might repeat the act in future.
