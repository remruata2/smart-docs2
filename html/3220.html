A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 31.7.2013

Physical Efficiency Test dropped in MPS recruitment: Council of Ministers meeting held on 29th July at Secretariat Conference Hall decided/passed that physical efficiency test will be dropped in the recruitment of Mizoram Police Service (MPS) but physical test will be done as usual. Physical efficiency test includes running, jumping and others.

 

B. Back page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 31.7.2013

Mizoram Police through to Quarter Final: Mizoram Police Football Team beat Kanan Football Team by 5-1 at Rajiv Gandhi Stadium, Mualpui yesterday ie, 30.7.2013 and through to the quarter final of the Independence Day Football Tournament.
