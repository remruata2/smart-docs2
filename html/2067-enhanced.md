****ACTIVITIES OF BNLF****

**2000**

1. On 10.1.2000 the VCP of Khawhnai village near Teirei received letter by post which signed by Commander-in-charge, BNLF. The letter which was posted from Damparengpui Post Office demanded that the VC should collect donations from the villagers as follows :-

 1) Farmers/Cultivators- Rs. 300/- per family.

 2) Businessmen, Rice Mill/Vehicles etc. owners - Rs. 500/- each

 3) Rs. 1,000/- each from every Govt. servants.

 Accordingly, the VC collected a total amount of Rs. 5,000/- which they kept in their custody for two days but since no one came to collect the money it was returned to the respective owners.

2. Information received that on 3.4.2000 at about 1100 hrs. 13 members of suspected NAGA and BNLF militants armed with AK-47 Rifles crossed NH-54 near Chhimluang village in between Bilkhawthlir and Vairengte and proceeded towards Saipuilui area in the North-East. They took Lalngaisanga of Vairengte as a guide with them and he was released on the same day at about 1300 hrs. They also took another 2 persons Lalchhuanawma and Lalnuntluanga as guide after releasing Lalngaisanga from Saipuilui and they were also released on the same day at about 1730 hrs. at Chawnpui village.

3. On 15.4.2000, MST Bus No. MZ-01(A)/5015 plying on Marpara to Aizawl was stopped by armed BNLF militants at gun point at about 5 Kms before reaching Saithah village @ 10:30 AM and fired at both front tyres of the Bus. The driver and conductor of the Bus were immediately forced out of the Bus at gun point and all the passengers were also asked to get out of the Bus. The militants had abducted 2 persons namely (1) Jolly Lalzawmliana (25), SA (PHE) s/o Ramthanmawia of Mission Venglai, Aizawl and (2) Lalchhanhima (37), JE (PHE) s/o V. Kapthanga (L) of Kulikawn, Aizawl. They had also taken away 1 (one) .22 Rifle from the passengers named T. Laldingliana, Microscopist and later on burnt the Bus. In this connection West Phaileng P.S. Case No. 8/2000 dt. 15.4.2000 u/s 121/364-A/435/34 IPC r/w 25(I-B)(a)-27 Arms Act has been registered.

 On 15.5.2000 at about 10:00 AM, the two hostages such as Lalchhanhima, JE and Jolly Lalzawmliana, SA were released by BNLF after 30 (thirty) days of captivity.

4. Information received that BNLF militants had demanded money from Bru of Teirei Veng and had given around Rs. 6000/- to them. (April 2000)

5. The militant demanded Rs. 3 lakhs from the driver but informed his inability to pay the amount demanded. Later the militant took away one Lalnunfela (24), Teacher LKM Private School, Lunglei s/o Hrangkunga, Vanghmun, Tripura. West Phaileng PS Case No.9/2000 u/s 121/364(A)34 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(a) 27 Arms Act was registered.

 On 4.5.2000 at 0330 hrs after 15 days of captivity, Lalnunfela was released at Khantlang village from where he had proceeded towards New Kawnpui and reached the village at about 1030 hrs.
