DAIDO KHAWBUL A MIZORAM – MANIPUR RAMRI ISSUE :

 Tun hnai a Daido(Mizoram) leh Manipur State te ramri chungchanga in hriatthiam lohna awm deuh chu hetiang hi ani –

 Tun hnai khan Manipur Sawrkar chuan Bungpuilawn leh Parvachawm (Manipur ve ve) te leh Daido (Mizoram) te in kar kawng chu siam tha in a rawn metalled a. He an kawng siam hian tunhma a Mizoram leh Manipur border ni thin a ngaih, pillar pawh awm thinna chu rawn pel in, meters 130 lai Mizoram chhung ni a hriat an rawn metalled thleng ani. He meter 130 a rawn thlen chin, a tawp na, Mizoram chhung hi tun ah chuan heng State te pahnih ramri ang in mi thenkhat chuan an sawi a. Nimahsela, hemi pawh hi pel leh in Manipur Sawrkar chuan feet 25 (meter 130 bak ah) Mizoram chhung kawng a rawn metalled chhunzawm bawk.

 He kawngpui hian Manipur a Bungpuilawn leh Parvachawm khua te ram chu chhak leh thlang ah then in an in ri na ani nghal a, Mizoram lam side ah erawh chuan kawng pui chhak lam leh thlang lam hi Daido ram ve ve ani. Tichuan, kawngpui chhaklam ah hian Daido chuan Bungpuilawn nen ri an intawm a, kawngpui khawthlang lam ah ve thung chuan Daido leh Parvachawm in ramri an in tawm.

 Hetiang a kawng metalled anih tak avang hian, Daido khua hi chuan Bungpuilawn nen a an in ri na chungchang ah buaina an nei lova. An buaina ber ni ta chu, Daido leh Parvachawm in kar ram, kawng metalled thar in a rawn pelh chin meter 130 leh a thlanglam ram te chu Daido khua hian chan an hlau ta ber ani. Helai ram awm dan hi Rough Sketch Map siam ani a, a copy rawn thil tel bawk ani e.

 Tin, Mizoram ramri chin ah hian Honourable Home Minister, Mizoram thupek angin 2nd IR Bn. Khawlian Post a mi te chuan Boundary Pillar (‘welcome to Mizoram’ lung phun)an phun a, a thlalak pawh kan rawn thil tel nghal e.

****CONFIDENTIAL****

****OFFICE OF THE Sr. SUPERINTENDENT OF POLICE****

****CID(SB), MIZORAM, AIZAWL****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/    Dated Aizawl, the June., 2019

To 

 The Hon’ble Home Minister,

 Mizoram.

Subj:  Daido khaw bul a Mizoram-Manipur border issue.

Sir,

 Tunhnai a Daido (Mizoram) leh Manipur inkar a ramri chungchang a inhriatthiam lohna awm deuh chu, Saitual Sector Officer, CID(SB) chuan Dt.31.5.2019 khan a hmun ah verification a nei a, heta a report rawn siam te leh thil awmdan te chu I lo chhiar atan ka rawn thawn e.

Enclo. :

1. Rough Sketch Map.
2. Boundary Pillar thlalak

Yours faithfully,
