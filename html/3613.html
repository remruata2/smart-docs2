A. Front page of  VANGLAINI/ The Aizawl Post  newspapers  Dt. 04.10.2022

Fit India Freedom Run: Fit India Freedom Run 3.0, organised by Mizoram State Sports Council was inaugurated yesterday (3.10.2022) by Sports & youth Services (SYS) Minister, Pu Robert Romawia Royte. Delivering a speech on this occasion, the Minister said that promising children and dedicated sport persons will not be neglected by the government while  more opportunities are about to be promoted. Different sports associations such as, 9 Grassroot Football Academy, 5 disciplines  under Non Residential Coaching, SAI Centre, USAM and personnel from MSSC participated in this programme. The Fit India Freedom Run 3.0 was started from Sikulpuikawn, Aizawl from the south by one group while from the north, another group started from Chanmari, Aizawl and both the group converged at AR Ground.

C. Second page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 04.10.2022

Only 88 Covid -19 positive persons: Two new Covid-19 positive cases were detected in Mizoram on 2nd October, 2022 (Sunday) which is the lowest rate after 19 cases were detected on 19th June 2022. Till last evening (3.10.2022) there were only 88 Covid- 19 patients in Mizoram according to the records. Yesterday, 38 covid negative persons were discharged which makes the present positivity rate of the state to 6.78%. According to the records of IDSP till 5 PM of yesterday (3.10.2022), there has been 2,38,462 Covid patients so far, out of which 2,37,651 (99.66%) were discharged and 723 died of the disease (0.30 %).
