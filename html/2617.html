Investigation Report

 

            Accused Lalhlupuia (32) s/o C. Thanzauva, Dawrpui Vengthar, Aizawl was apprehended by CID (SB) Staff on 29.9.2005 @ 2 AM at Zemabawk, Aizawl while conveying weapons mentioned in SI No. 10 (i) which were concealed inside a sound box. The consignment was brought by Lalhlupuia from Champhai by hiring a Taxi.

 

            Formal interrogation led to seizure of weapons, Sl NO. 10 (ii & iii), from the residence of the accused person and the subsequent arrest of his wife Zodinpuii (37) D/o C. Liana @ Chhunliana on 30.9.2005. The consignment was intended to be sold to their contact in Shillong known only as Sharma(detail particulars not known).

 

PREVIOUS DEALING OR INVOLVEMENT:  Further interrogation revealed that the couple had sold several weapons like different types of ammunitions, on four or five occasions to the said Sharma earlier. It was also ascertained that the couple had involved in this type of activities since 2001. Two of their accomplices viz. 1) Romanga @ Manga s/o Ralkhama, Halkha, Myanmar P/A Khatla, Aizawl and 2) Rokhawma (35) s/o Taihranga (L), Indaigone, Myanmar P/A Dawrpui Vengthar, Aizawl were also arrested.
