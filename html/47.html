            Tun dinhmunah hian an hnena pawisa amount hrang hrang deposittute mi 40 vel niin 2.10 crores pawisa an collect tawh a ni. Heng pawisa dahtu te nen hian Court Affidavit siamin an hnena an dah atanga thlathum hnuah pawisa (Loan) hi pe ngei tur a intiamna an ni hlawm a, mahse hriat theih chinah chuan tumah loan hi an la pe lova, pawisa a lo la thleng thei lo tiin hun an pawtsei zel a, tunah chuan Customers lam pawhin an ngaithiam lo tan hle a ni. Tunah tak chuan Nungkhawngam hi phai lamah kalin a pawisa neih theih dan tur a ngaihtuah mek niin an sawi.

 

            Source of income, loan a pek theih dan tur hi a agent te hnenah pawh a sawi tha duh lo hle a, a customers te phei chu loan a pek hunah pawh a source zawt lo tura  tiin a hrilh duh ngai lo. Mahse, tumkhat a ma tawngkam atanga kan hriat danin, phai lam Maruari hausa, pawisa ngah si mi 4 vel nena tangruala hetiang loan hi buatsaih niin a in sawi a, a agent pakhat hnena a sawi dan chuan phai lam sorkar hnathawk lian, pawisa ngah si, a thiante nen an buaipui niin a sawi. Tunah hian Aizawlah hian campaigner 5 a nei ni a hriat a ni. Affidavit engmah documents dang an nei lo.
