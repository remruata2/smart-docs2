Subj :- Fake COVID-19 Free Certificate - regarding.

 Input received from sister agency indicated that fake COVID-19 Free Certificate are available at Rs. 300/- INR in Mizoram's neighbouring areas of Karimganj District, Assam. It is believed that many individuals may have obtained such forged documents to make entry into the State to avoid further COVID test at higher cost.

 Therefore, you are hereby directed to conduct discrete enquiry on the above stated subject and submit your report at the earliest for further submission to higher authority.
