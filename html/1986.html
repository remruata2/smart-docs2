Brief History :

         

          A chunga mi khi ka particulars tlangpui chu a ni. Kum 1978 khan Thaidor ah ka lo pianga, Pianpui unau mipa pathum leh farnu pahnih ka nei a ni. Thaidor ah hian Class V thleng ka kal a, kum 18 vel ka nihin Naisingpara ah kan chhungin kan pem thla a, hetah hian tun thleng hian kan la awmzui ta a ni. Naisingpara a kan awm hnu hian kum 2006 BNLF  ten Mizoram Sorkar a an Surrender dawn khan lut vein Naisingpara Camp bulah Thla khat vel training runpui an tih lai khan  ka training ve a. Insurrender zawh hnuah let lehin kum 2011 kum tawp lam khan Duhawma hovin NLFT kan zawm ve ta a ni.

Kan zawmhnu hian Bangladesh ah awmin kan hotuten min tirhnaah kan kal kual reng thin a. Puakphurh leh Lo lam hna kan thawk ber thin a ni.  Dt. 18.02.2013-a Forest MR panga (5) kidnap te, Dt.5.6.2013 a Gautam Construction Worker pathum (3) kidnap te, Dt.23.11.2013 a Deep Mandal leh Mizo pahnih (2) kidnap te khan NLFT te khaihhruaina hnuaiahan chhungte be pawp a sum Demand vel te leh atulna dang awm apiangah kan kan thawk ve a ni.

Kan Demand area chhunga khaw hrang hrang te Sum demand chungchangah kan Hruaitute nen kan in ti thiam ta vak lo va, sum hmangsualah te min puh a, abik takin Sektaraipa, Home in-charge nen kan in hmuhkhawloh chhoh avangin kum 2013 December thla khan Bangladesh atang ka chhuak a, bang tawp ni si lo in Bangladesh a kir tawh chuang si lovin Naisingpara Refugee Camp velah ka awm ta vel a ni.

Tlem lai deuh kum 2014 kumtir lam khan in surrender dan tur leh kan case a fel theihna tur ngaihtuahin mi chi hrang hrang ka be thin a, engemaw chen hnu ah Zorama @ Noiboram nen kan intawng fuh a, a ni hian HPC-D te nen a thawh ho dan tur leh kan case chinfel dan tur ten en min ngaihtuahpui theih dawn thu min hrilh a. Tiang chuan ka naute mi pathum hruaiin Zorama hmu tur chuan Silchar kan pan ta a,

Dt. 12.7.2014 ah kan thlenna Bani Hotel, Silchar ah kan inhmukhawm ta a, heta kan inhmuhkhawm lai hian Mizoram Police (CID (SB)) te chuan min lo man ta a ni. Min man rual hian Aizawl min panpui nghal a, Aizawl kan thlen hian Aizawl Thana ah min dahlut nghal a ni.
