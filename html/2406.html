MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/89

Dated 4th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/73
Dt. 3.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

13 (thirteen) vehicles (HMV - 2, MMV - 10, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 4.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV - 2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV - 7, MMV - 4, LMV - NIL) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 4.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV - 3, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Pi Lalbiaksangi, Home Secretary visited Saihapui ‘V’ today, i.e., 4.2.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. to take stock of the situation and she stated that BSF Duty Post and BSF Camp at Saihapui ‘V’ border which is under construction will be completed soon. The Home Secretary was accompanied by
Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib, Pu David H. Lalthangliana, OSD Home and Pu Isaac C. Lalrempuia, SDO (C) Vairengte.

Pu F. Ngurbiakvela, Chairman, JAC on Inner Line Reserved Demand Committee and leaders of Vairengte Thalai Pawl visited Saihapui ‘V’ border and BSF Duty Post at the border to take stock of the situation today, i.e., 4.2.2021 @ 3:00 p.m.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/73 Dt. 3.02.2021 Item No. 4 Bairabi PS registered a case vide No. BBI PS C/No. 2/21
Dt. 3.2.2021 u/s 448/436 IPC r/w 3(a) Explosive Substances Act, 1908 and
3 (1) The Prevention of Damage to Public Property Act, 1984. 2 (two) Mizoram Police personnel were deployed at Mulibali LP School to prevent any untoward incident at the area, while 2 (two) Assam Police personnel were deployed by Assam. Information received indicated that so far no case has been registered by Assam Police against the blast.

102 (one hundred and two) vehicles (HMV - 61, MMV - NIL, LMV - 29, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab - 12) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 4.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          Pi Lalbiaksangi, Home Secretary and party also visited Phaisen today, i.e., 4.2.2021 @ 1:30 p.m. to take stock of the situation.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

Pu F. Ngurbiakvela, Chairman, JAC on Inner Line Reserved Demand Committee visited Vairengte today, i.e., 4.2.2021 @ 10:30 a.m. and he was received by Vairengte Thalai Pawl led by Pu C. Lalhruaitluanga, President Vairengte Thalai Pawl. He stated that the purpose of his visit is to take stock of the situation at Vairengte, Phaisen and Saihapui ‘V’ border  and he further stated that it is necessary to aware the exact inter-state boundary by residents of Mizoram.

169 (one hundred sixty nine) vehicles (HMV - 82, MMV - 50, LMV - 12, LPG - 6, Maxi Cab -1, Oil Tanker -18, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 4.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

42 (forty two) vehicles (HMV - 8, MMV - 13, LMV - 21) entered Saiphai from Assam today, i.e., 4.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. and 31 (thirty one) persons have undergone RAT test and none was found positive. Situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.

.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
