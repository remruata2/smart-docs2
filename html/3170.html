A. Front page of Zozam Times / The Aizawl Post Dt. 05.02.2013

Vehicle thief caught: Out of the two escapist in connection with stolen of 407 truck bearing regd. no. MZ 01 G 7712, one Maman Uddin Laskar (18) s/o Baskandi, Cachar, District was nabbed by Joint YMA Bilkhawthlir and Bilkhathlir police at the outskirts of Phaisen, he was taken to Bilkhawthlir police outpost. Two out of three thieves involved in the vehicle theft have been captured so far.

 

B. Front page of The Zozam Times & The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt.05.02.2013

A corpse identified: An unidentified person who was found unconscious at 1:30 am near the office of SBI, Dawrpui Branch on Sunday and eventually died at around 10:30am on the same day has been identified as Lalremsiama s/o Biakzara of Durtlang. The corpse was identified by his family at 3:00pm yesterday.

 

 

C. Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.05.02.2013

Culprit not captured yet: Perpetrators in connection with a recent arson case and combustion of light vehicles which had been occurred on 29th Jan. 2013 at Bethlehem Veng are not yet detected. Police intensified investigation by forming Special Investigation Team. Stringent interrogation upon suspected person is still going on.
