MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/333

Dated 29th April, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/329
Dt. 28.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          4 (four) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 29.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 

4 (four) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-3, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 29.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and
no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

Information received stated that on the evening of 27.4.2021 @ 9:20 p.m. Kanhmun VLTF Duty Post located at Kobangsang around 1 km from Kanhmun Village was allegedly set ablaze by unknown miscreants and it was immediately extinguished. Later around 10:30 p.m. the same night, the cultivated land of
Pu Rozamliana of Kanhmun located around 500 meters from the said Kanhmun VLTF Duty Post and Forest Teak Plantation located near VLTF Duty Post were burned and the fire was extinguished by around 70 (seventy) volunteers from Kanhmun Village @ 12:00 a.m. the same night. Situation is under close watch.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

78 (seventy eight) vehicles (HMV-66, MMV-Nil, LMV-11, Maxi Cab-1) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 29.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

115 (one hundred fifteen) vehicles (HMV-36, MMV-32, LMV-13,  LPG-5, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-24, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 29.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          33 (thirty three) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV-9, LMV-19, Maxi Cab-Nil) entered Saiphai from Assam today, i.e., 29.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
