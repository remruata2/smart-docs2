SEIZURE OF ARMS AND AMMUNITIONS

 

            On 28.6.08 Special Operation Team of CID(SB) pursue the information received from the statements adduced by the arrested BLFM members and DHD cadres who were arrested on 25.6.08 successfully recovered huge consignment of arms and ammunition at about 10:00 AM at about 6 Kms from Sairang PS towards Buichali Bridge. The consignment was hidden in a vehicle (TATA 207) bearing R/No. MZ-01D 8076 driven by the owner Huapzauva S/o Thanchhunga of Chaltlang Dawrkawn, Aizawl who was arrested alongwith Lalliana (34) S/o Liannghaka, Tahan, Myanmar the owner of the consignments.

 

            In this connection,

1) Bawngkawn PS C/No. 351/08 dt 28.6.08 u/s 120 B/121A/34 IPC

 

2) Sairang PS C/No.40/08 dt.30.6.08 u/s 120B IPC r/w 25(1AA) Arms Act r/w 13(1)/16(1)(b)/18 UAP Act 1967   r/w 6 (a) PP (Entry into India Rule 1950).

 

3) Vaivakawn PS C/No.37/13 dt.3.5.13 u/s 25(1AA) Arms Act r/w 34 IPC had been registered. 

 

            Details of the arms and ammunitions seized are as under :-

            1) AK-47                               - 19 nos.

            2) AK-47 magazine              - 12 nos.

            3) AK-47 ammns(7.62 mm)- 8307 nos.
