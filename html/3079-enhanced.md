A. _Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 4.4.2013_

_ACB Searched Private Residences_:****On the ongoing investigation regarding the extension of land by Counter Insurgency &amp; Jungle Warfare School (CIJWS), ACB searched 5 residences at Vairengte and seized a number of suspicious pass (documents) yesterday ie. dt.3.4.2013. Dy.SP C.Vanramlawma &amp; SI Jerry Lalrinsanga led the ACB staff to Vairengte with search warrant to search 5 residences who claimed to be the owners of certain areas in the land to be acquired by CIJWS for extension. They seized suspicious documents from 2 houses out of 5 houses they searched.

 ACB obtained search warrant for PC Parmawia s/o Sawithanga of Venglai Vairengte, Lalzuithanga s/o Ganga Ram of Field Veng, Vairengte, Lalhmudika s/o Lawmthanga of State Veng, Vairengte, Lalnunsanga s/o Neihlala of State Veng, Vairengte and B.Dokunga s/o Laihnuna(L) of Venghlui, Vairengte. No documents were seized from the respective houses of PC Parmawia, Lalhmudika and Lalnunsanga. 5 original copies and 9 photo copies of suspicious documents were seized from Lalzuithanga, and 9 original copies of suspicious documents were also seized from B.Dokunga, out of which 6 were not signed by VP.

 Regarding extension of land of CIJWS, ACB had searched the house of LTH Buangpui of Project Veng, Kolasib recently and seized several suspicious documents and he was arrested.
