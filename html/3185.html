A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES,& THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt.27.02.2013:

Verification of vehicles with Red Light by Azawl Traffic Police: The Aizawl traffic police are planning to take legal action against those who use Red light but not included among officials who can use it under govt. notification No.D 26011/4/95-TRP Dt.18.11.2004.

         Those who use Red light without government's concent are warned and informed to stop using it before they are apprehended.

         Those who can use Red light are :

1.V.I.P with National Flag on their vehicles.

2.Opposition Leader

3.Vice Chairman,State Planning Board.

4.Deputy  Govt. Chief Whip

5.Adviser to Chie Minster

6.Chairman/Vice Chairman, Co operation/ Board if he is an MLA

7.Chief Secretary

8.Director General of Police

9.State Chief Information Commissioner

10.State Information Commissioner

11.Chairman MPSC

12.CEM of LADC,MADC and CADC (Within their council areas)

13.Principal Secretary and Administrative Deptt. Commissioners/Secretaries

14.D.C/District Magistrate and District S.P

15.District and Session Judge, AIzawl Judicial District

16.District and Session Judge ,Lunglei Judicial District.

17.Fire service vehicle

18.Ambulance
