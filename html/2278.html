MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/70

Dated 2nd February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/67
Dt. 1.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV – 4, MMV – 10, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 2.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 13, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 2.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 8 (eight) vehicles (MMV –5, LMV-3) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

            106 (one hundred six) vehicles (HMV- 66, MMV – 1, LMV –33, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 2.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

149 (one hundred forty nine) vehicles (HMV – 68, MMV – 26, LMV –28, LPG – 3, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 20, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 2.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

           

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

           

            Situation is under close watch.

.
