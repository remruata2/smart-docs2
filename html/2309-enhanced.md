****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/07

Dated 6th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/05 Dt. 5.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/5 Dt. 5.1.2021 PWD, Assam continued road repairing work between Zaite Village, Assam and Assam Police Duty Post which is around 300 meters towards Assam from place of deployment of BSF personnel today i.e., 6.1.2021. Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV- 6, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 6.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 5 (five) vehicles (HMV – , MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV –2 , MMV – 5, LMV –nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 6.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-28, MMV – 2, LMV – 31, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-12) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 6.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2(two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 6.1.2021.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

 150 (one hundred fifty) vehicles (HMV – 58, MMV – 44, LMV –28, LPG – 2, Maxi Cab – 3, Oil Tanker – 14, Bus - 1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 6.1.2021 between 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
