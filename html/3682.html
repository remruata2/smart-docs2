A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 20.09.2022

Heroin seized: Champhai police on 17th September 2022 seized 51 grams of suspected Heroin from Lalzetluanga (19) s/o Khiangkhama, Melbuk village who was travelling towards Aizawl from Zokhawthar village in a maxi cab . The seized contrabands were estimated to be worth around Rs. 25.5 lakhs in the international market.

 

B.Second page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 20.09.2022

D.C Aizawl issued an order in connection with refugees :  Dr. Lalhriatzuali, Deputy Commissioner,  Aizawl district issued an order on how to deal with the refugees from Myanmar who fled to Mizoram. The order instructed the Local/Village Level Committee on Myanmar Refugees to abide by the order and to report the violators to the Chairman of District Task Group on Myanmar Refugees. The order contains the following points :

            a) No refugees shall buy any plot of land  or houses without the consent of the           government.

            b) No refugees shall start any business without the consent of the government.

            c) No prohibited items shall be brought inside the state by any refugees.

            d) Any vehicles brought inside the state by any refugees shall be notified to                           the Local/Village Level Committee on Myanmar Refugees.

            e) No Myanmar citizens shall obtain Aadhar card/Voters ID/Driving License              and other documents in contravention with the existing laws.
