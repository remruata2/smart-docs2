****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/177

Dated 12th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/174 Dt. 11.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-13, MMV-9, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 12.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

27 (twenty seven) vehicles (HMV-10, MMV-12, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 12.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-7, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Yesterday i.e., 11.3.2021 Joint VC Bairabi started construction of agriculture link road near Zophai BOP and they intended to construct the road upto Rami Lui, near Assam-Mizoram border. Construction of the said road was continuing today i.e., 12.3.2021 and no report of objection from Assam side has been received till date. 

105 (one hundred five) vehicles (HMV-66, MMV-1, LMV-30, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 12.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

158 (one hundred fifty eight) vehicles (HMV-63, MMV-42, LMV-17, LPG-5, Maxi Cab-7, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 12.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
