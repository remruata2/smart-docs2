ARMS AND AMMUNITION SNATCHED AWAY BY HPC(D) OUTFIT

ON 28.03.2015

 

          On 28.03.2015, HPC(D) outfit snatched away the following Arms and ammunition after attacking an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance comprising 3(three) MLAs escorted by Mizoram Police at the outskirt of  Zokhawthiang/Kani village:-

 

  1. 9mm Glock Pistol Regn. No. AADF373 with 26 rounds of ammunition.
  2. 9mm Browning Pistol Regn. No. E2653 with 10 rounds of ammunition.
  3. .38 Revolver Regn. No. 161-338207 with 10 rounds of ammunition.
  4. AK-47 Rifle Butt No. 52 Regn. No. BE445420

                                                                                                                           8 nos. of mags. with 244 rounds         

  1. AK-47 Rifle Butt No. 36 Regn. No. BN449018
  2. 9mm Browning Pistol Regn. No. E1231
  3. 9mm Browning Pistol Regn. No. 16236070
  4. .38 Ruger Revolver Regn. No. 161-33735
