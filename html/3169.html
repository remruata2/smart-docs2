A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt.04.02.2013

      One habitual offender arrested: The patrolling party of Bilkhawthlir police apprehended one   person yesterday in connection with car theft.              

              The police patrolling strongly suspected when they saw a Santro car running with   puncture tyres in front of them. After pursuing the said santro, the police caught Raju      Patnador (30) whose other two accomplices managed to escape before apprehension.         The arrested accused is presently in police custody for interrogation.
