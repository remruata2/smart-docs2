Sub :  Report on seizure of Heroin and Ganja

Sir,

 On dt.11.02.2021, specific information was received from source stating that one person was strongly suspected of committing an offence in contravention of the provisions of the ND &amp; PS Act, 1985 by possessing suspected Heroin at Armed veng and its surrounding areas. In this regard, information report was submitted immediately to Dy.SP, CID(SB). Dy.SP CID(SB) then immediately issued Authorization Letter u/s 41(2) of ND &amp; PS Act, authorizing me to conduct search, seizure and arrest of suspected persons at Armed veng and its surrounding areas, Aizawl.

 Then, self with party immediately proceeded towards Armed veng, Aizawl. On reaching Armed veng, Aizawl arrangements of 2(two) local independent witnesses was made namely: (1) Z. Lalchhuanawma (49) S/o Lalzika (L), Armed Veng and 2) Moses Lalsangzuala (46) S/o R. Sawichhunga, Armed Veng.

 One person strongly suspected of possessing contraband drugs was located standing on the road within Armed veng (near Cemetery), was approached and he introduced himself as Lalhruaikhawma (20) s/o Lalhrilmawii, Chhinga Vengthlang, Aizawl. He was told that he was suspected of possessing contraband drugs in contravention of ND &amp; PS Act and that an Authorization Letter u/s 41(2) ND&amp;PS Act has been issued for conducting search, seizure and arrest, which was shown to him, read out and explained to him. He was then asked whether he kept concealed contraband drugs or not and to take out all his hidden articles if any. Accordingly, he took out 3(three) cases of plastic soap cases which he kept concealed near one parked scooty. Each and every soap case contained powder packed in transparent polythene. Weighment of the suspected Heroin was taken which total weights 39.2 grams value of Rs. 1,50,000/- (one lakh fifty thousand) in the local smuggling rate.

 Seizure of the suspected Heroin was then made from the possession of Lalhruaikhawma (20) S/o Lalhrilmawii, Chhinga Vengthlang, Aizawl, on dt.11.02.2021 @ 6:05pm in presence of the aforementioned witnesses. The suspected Heroin was marked as A-I to A-III. Weighing, Packing and sealing of the seized suspected Heroin was done in presence of the suspected person and two civilian witnesses.

The accused and the seized articles were brought and handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S, Aizawl, and FIR was also lodged against the accused. In this connection, **Spl. Narcotic PS C/no. 06/2021 Dt.11.02.2021 U/s 21(b) ND& PS Act** was registered.

The suspected ganja, weighing 5 kgs, was packed in 5(five) Rambo white polythene bags which were again packed in brown colour sugar bag. No arrest was made in this connection.
