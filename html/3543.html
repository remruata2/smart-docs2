A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 20.12.2022

Former Dy.Speaker to be inducted as Cabinet Minister: Er. Lalrinawma, who recently resigned from the post of Dy. Speaker of Mizoram Legislative Assembly on 15.12.2022  will be inducted as one of the Cabinet Minister of Mizoram government today. Apart from him, three other Ministers of State - Pu K. Lalrinliana, Pu Lalchhandama Ralte and Pu Lalruatkima will also be inducted as Cabinet Minister as well. Chief Minister of Mizoram, Pu Zoramthanga, however, had not announced what will be the portfolios of  his Cabinet Ministers including the newly inducted Cabinet Ministers. On 13.12.2022, with the pretext of re shuffling of the portfolios of his Ministers, Mizoram CM had requested Dr. K. Beichhua to tender his resignation from the post of Minister of State which was promptly obliged by the said MoS.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 20.12.2022

2nd instalments of SEDP from 1st December 2022:  According to the time table laid down by Planning & programme Implementation department of Mizoram, second instalments of  family oriented funds of SEDP are to be distributed starting from 1.12.2022. According to this time table, first instalment of SEDP (Rs.25,000 each) is to be distributed to their respective selected beneficiaries starting from 1st September 2022 to 31st October 2022 and informed each implementing departments to submit proposal for second instalment from 15th November 2022. Funds for the said second instalments of SEDP were disbursed to each of the concerned departments during 15-30 November 2022 with instructions to distribute them to the selected  beneficiaries during the period between 1st December 2022 - 15th February 2023. At the same time, Planning department also highlighted the possibilities of altering the proposed fixed timing of distributions of these SEDP funds.

Seizure of Areca nuts: At around 2:30 am of yesterdays morning, personnel of Mamit police seized 13 bags (1,040 kgs.) of illegally transported areca nuts from one maxi cab near Darlak village. Lalmuanpuia (39) s/o Rinliana(L), Mualpui, Aizawl and Zoramsanga (30) s/o Zakamlova, Bawngkawn, Aizawl who were the suspects in these illegal/smuggling process were also apprehended and a case was duly registered at Mamit Police station in this connection.
