SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/150

Dated: 4th March, 2019.

 

 

            Reference to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/145, dated 28.02.2019, information received that three Bru NGO’s namely Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Forum (MBDPF), Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Coordination Committee (MBDPCC) and Mizoram Bru Indigenous Democratic Movement (MBIDM) held a meeting today, i.e. 04.03.2019 @ 12:00 noon at MBDPF office which was presided over by A. Sawibunga, MBDPF President and attended by 20 (twenty) members. The meeting expressed their desire that maximum of displaced Brus should return to Mizoram on the next phase of repatriation.  However, they decided to resubmit their various demands to the Central and Mizoram Government and entrusted members of MBDPF and MBIDM to draft a Memorandum of their demands tomorrow, i.e. 5.3.2019 which will further be scrutinised by their Joint Committee for onward submission before 15.3.2019. As decided earlier, the Bru NGO’s will remove establishment of Bru Autonomous District Council from their list of demands. The meeting also decided that their representatives should also leave for Mizoram before 15.3.2019 to meet the Hon’ble Chief Minister, Ministers and Officials of the Mizoram Government. The date and timing of the Joint meeting and their intended visit of Mizoram has not been decided for the time being.
