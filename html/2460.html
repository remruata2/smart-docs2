MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/737

Dated 18th December, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/734
Dt. 17.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-1, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 18.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.9 (nine) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 9, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

          5 (five) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 2, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 18.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.1 (one) vehicle (HMV – Nil, MMV – 1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister visited Mizoram Police and BSF duty at Saihapui ‘V’ and handed over gifts today i.e., 18.12.2020 @ 11:30 a.m. During his visit the Home Minister informed DC, Kolasib to take necessary action for setting up of IR Duty Temporary Camp at Saihapui ‘V’. He was accompanied by Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR), Dr. H. Lalthlangliana, DC, Kolasib, Pu Vanlalfaka, SP, Kolasib  and Pu MS Dawngkima, CO 1st IR Battalion.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-40, MMV – 2, LMV – 23, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 18.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
