REPORT ON ENQUIRY AGAINST

INSPECTOR  R.LALHMACHHUANA, 3rd IR Bn.

 

            Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana (Currently posted at 3rd IR Bn.) appointed under Mizoram Police Department was engaged as in-charge of Mizoram Police Gas Agency during his tenure of posting at 1st Bn. Mizoram Armed Police, Aizawl. During this period, he is alleged to have acquired some assets during his tenure of posting as i/c of MPGA.

 

            During the course of the enquiry, the moveable assets and immovable assets of Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana was analyzed. The total cash maintained at Banks including loans secured from various banks during 1.1.2005 to 1.1.2017 were scrutinized. He had opened savings bank account at Punjab & Sind Bank, Sikulpuikawn, Aizawl and Saving Account at State Bank of India, Main Branch, Aizawl. He had secured loans from various six (6) Branches which amounts to 33,43,253/-. Account statement of SBI Main Branch account No.10763624915 which is known to be operated by him regularly was carefully studied. The total deposit made, other than his salary during these ten years is Rs.1,22,11,539/- by means of cash deposit by self and others, EFT, and cheque deposits while his salary deposit for ten years is Rs.18,07,955/-. The remaining balance on his account as on 1.12.2015 is Rs.6671.15, his expenditure of all the money is not known.

 

            While conducting this enquiry, AIG-I letter vide No.CB/PHQ/MRP/GAS/05/291 dt.29.6.2018 containing a request to verify the authenticity of house sites belonging to Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana of 3rd IR Bn. bearing LSC No.103201/01/858 of 2003 at Falkland, Zemabawk and LSC No.1210 of 1983 at Bawngkawn Aizawl was also pursued. After verification, it was found that LSC No.1210 of 1983 was transferred to Andy Vanlalsiama s/o R.Lalhmachhuana, Tuikual North as a gift in 2009. The other land, bearing LSC No.103201/01/858 of 2003 at Falkland, Zemabawk was still in the name of R.Lalhmachhuana s/o Major Sangzuala, Tuikual North but the original copy of LSC was mortgage at PBD, SBI, Main Branch in 2004.

 

            After careful analysis and taking into considerations the banking transactions made by Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana exclusive of his salary and his other loans secured from various banks, it is opined that Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana has made transactions that could not tally with his known source of income as a government servant of his pay scale. The assets maintained and  transacted by Inspr. R.Lalhmachhuana with his bank accounts held with State Bank of India and Punjab & Sind Bank, Aizawl could not be in proportionate to his actual income as a government servant.
