1.2.14 On 1.2.14, Pu Lalhmingthanga Sanate faction of Hmar Peoples Conference (Democrats) issued a statement wherein it alleged Pu H. Zosangbera faction of HPC(D) as detractor and warned for highest degree of punishment unless they ask for forgiveness. The statement claimed that Lalhmingthanga Sanate is the founder President of HPC(D) and still remain the President of HPC(D).

 A group of HPC(D) numbering 10-15 led by S/S Commander Thangsiem are presently staying at Parvachawm village, Manipur, close to Khawlian village of Mizoram. As per input received, S/S Commander Thangsiem had contacted some Village Council Presidents / Retailers of Khawlian and Phuaibuang villages demanding some amount of money from them and instructed them to be handed over to him at Parvachawm by any Congress party workers. The group is learnt to be armed with only pistols.

21.2.14 Input has been received that the HPC (D) had issued extortion demand from a contractor (name not yet known) @ 5% from the estimated amount of Rs. 27 lakhs. As per the input received, the contractor had been told to pay the demanded amount at a location by the bank of Tuiruang river near Sartuinek village, Manipur on or before 22.2.2014.

 Input received also stated that, the 5(five) HPC (D) cadres apprehended by joint team of Mizoram Police and 34th Assam Rifle during raid on Parvachawm village on 19.02.2014 have been handed over to Parbung Police Station in Manipur at 8:00 PM by the 34th Assam Rifle in Parbung following a demonstration by a group of local women of Parbung and neighbouring villages gathered outside the Assam Rifles Camp on 20.02.2014 and pressurised the Commanding Officer of the 34th Assam Rifle on a plea that the HPC (D) cadres cannot be arrested or detained as they are under Suspension of Operation (SoO) agreement. It is further learnt that, all the 5 (five) HPC (D) cadres apprehended have been released by the Manipur Police under the plea that HPC(D) are under Suspension of Operation (SoO) agreement with Manipur Government. However, all recovered items such as Ammunitions, other uniform clothes and documents are still kept by 34th Assam Rifles Company Headquarter at Parbung.

21.2.14  The HPC (D) had suspected two persons of Suangpuilawn (names yet to be ascertained) of passing information to the Police that led to the raid on Parvachawm village on 19.02.2014. The two persons who were learnt to have visited Parvachawm during the first week of February, 2014, are being targeted by the HPC (D), input suggests.

 Further the HPC(D) has also demanded 5% of the proposed construction of sub-centre and staff quarters building under Health&amp;Family Welfare Department of Sakawrdai and Tinghmun.

25.2.14 Reliable information received indicated that 5 members of HPC(D) cadres under the command of Biakliana Comdr. i/c Tuiruangdung came towards Patpuihmunkai located near Mizoram-Manipur border on 24.2.14 at about 2-3pm carrying sophisticated weapons(AK-47). It is also reported that these 5 HPC(D) cadres visited Zilthawchhuah(Mizoram side) and stayed in one jhum hut belonging to Zara of Sakawrdai for about one hour. Zara is a supporter of MNF party and regularly staying at Zilthawchhuah with his family members. It is further learnt that Biakliana and his party consumed local made liquor inside the jhum hut and left for Manipur side after sitting in the jhum hut of Zara for one hour. These HPC(D) cadres were also sighted by some civilians of Vaitin village who were working on jhum cultivation.
