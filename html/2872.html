A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 3.10.2012

 

Abusive substance seized at Lengpui Airport : The Security duty at Lengpui Airport seized a large number of illegal substances-Pseudoephedrine and Cetirizine among the cargo arrived at Lengpui Airport. It was handed over to CID Crime

 

Input received stated that these substances came from Ahmedabad to Guwahati on 30th Sept 2012 in Jet Airways, and from Guwahati to Lengpui Airport on 2.10.2012. As the destination of the Cargo was not clearly written, Lengpui Airport Security Police checked it and found these abusive substances.

 

The address written inside is SEPA Medical Store, Moreh, Ward No. 3, Chandel District, Manipur but on the outside, it is written Aizawl. These Substances are 2,50,000 tablets in all.

44.46% of stolen Money from Zawlnuam BDO,s Office had so far been recovered:        Zawlnuam Police led by SI Zoramsanga, O/C Zawlnuam PS recovered the sum of Rs. 1,50,500/- in Mutarampara, Tripura yesterday i.e, dt. 2.10.2012.
