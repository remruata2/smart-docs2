****ACTIVITIES OF HPC(D)****

 Hmar People’s Convention (Democratic) HPC-D : The HPC-D was formed in 1996 by remnants of the HPC who were disgruntled with the MoU signed between HPC and Govt. of Mizoram in 1994. The HPC-D resorted to violence from the outset of its formation like extortion, kidnapping and ambush of security forces. They had killed 10 Police personnel till 2.9.2008.

 The HPC-D had split into two factions on 29.9.2011. One faction is led by the outset President Lalhmingthanga Sanate and the other faction led by H. Zosangbera. HPC-D H. Zosangbera faction is more active and now in possession of 34 nos. of arms of different types and 950 ammunitions.

 Peace Talk was held between the Govt. of Mizoram and HPC-D on 11.11.2011 but no agreement could be reached. Next round of talk is unlikely at present.

 The HPC-D intensified their criminal activities since early January this year i.e. 2012 by interfering in the V/C election for which State Election Commission was compelled to postpone election for V/C of 13 villages within Aizawl District and 2 others from Kolasib District. Besides, the HPC-D forcibly threatened as many as 37 YMA Branches to dissolve within their demand area. They also collected force donation from VC’s, Govt, Employees and Contractors within their demand area which draw the attention of the Govt. resorting in the arrest of 3 top leaders viz. H. Zosangbera, Chairman at IGI Airport, New Delhi on 17.7.2012 and two armed wing leaders S/S Army Chief Ropuia and S/S Dy. Army Chief Biaknunga at Silchar Airport on 10.6.2012. The three leaders had been kept under judicial custody till date.

 There have been no records of activities with ultras on border with NLFD, etc.

****ISSUE WITH ASSAM****

**14.11.2005 -**At around 10:30am to 11:30am, some unidentified persons caused severe damage to waiting shed constructed under IWDP by Govt. of Mizoram and carried away the materials. Estimated cost of damage caused was Rs.25000/-. In this connection, Bairabi PS C/No.76/05 dt.20.11.2005 u/s 427/379 IPC was registered.

**20.6.2007** - MZP constructed waiting shed "Zorun" at the outskirt of Vairengte. The same was dismantled few hours later by the Assam Police but all the materials were salvaged by the Mizoram Police. In this connection, Vairengte PS Case No.29/07 dt.21.6.2007 was registered against the Assam Authority.

**27.6.2007** - A combined team of Assam Police, Forest officials, CRPF and IR Bn. numbering about 30 headed by Executive Magistrate of Hailakandi District of Assam came to Zophai area and arrested three non-Mizo and one Mizo, a JCB operator who were working at the paddy field of Pu CH.Chhunga. They were booked vide Katlichera PS C/No.55/07 dt.27.6.07 u/s 447/430/506/379 IPC.

**16.12.2010** - There occurred dispute over broom cultivation between residents of Vairengte village of Mizoram and Bilaipur village of Assam at a place called Aitlang area which falls within Mizoram. In this connection, 3 Mizo persons were taken by civilians of Bilaipur and were kept in Bilaipur Outpost but were taken back by Mizoram Police.

 The matter has been amicably settled later after discussion between the Mizoram and Assam authorities.

**10.2.2011** - The Assam Police told workers of the Minor Irrigation Project at Mautui Zau area of Kolasib District, Mizoram in the Assam-Mizoram border. On 15.2.2011, a representative with Police and Forest officials went to the disputed site and made a request to halt the on-going work till the matter was settled at higher level.

 Meeting between DC Kolasib District and DC Hailakandi District was held on 10.8.2011 and after cordial discussion, they agreed to maintain status quo in the disputed area.

**20.3.2011** - School building constructed by Assam Govt. at the Mizoram-Assam border near Bairabi was attacked with explosives. No arrest was made. Another school building was also constructed near Zophai.

 There have been no noteworthy issues with Tripura, Manipur and Nagaland States in a year or two.

****ACTIVITIES OF YOUNG MIZO ASSOCIATION(YMA)****

Young Mizo Association(YMA) was formed in 15th June 1935. Its Hqrs. is located in Aizawl and has 5 sub-hqrs., 46 groups 786 branches and 3,70,544 members in different villages, towns and cities in Mizoram. YMA is the biggest NGO in Mizoram and work hand in hand with the government. YMA has been working towards the development of Mizos at various levels. It has been taking measures to fight against abusive substances, the government also support YMA in this fight, the Mizo people feels peace regarding in this matter. As the YMA stands for the Mizo people in times of trouble, death and destruction, it is one of the most prominent foundation of Mizo community.

 Motto :  1) Hunawl hmanthat.

  2) Zofate hmasawnna ngaihtuah.

  3) Kriatian nundan tha ngaihsan.
