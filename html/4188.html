To

 

The Sr. Superintendent of  Police

CID (SB), Aizawl

Mizoram


Subject :         Threat assessment relating to Independence Day celebration, 2024-reg.


Ref :                No. MSB/CB/B-82/708   dt.8.08.2024

 

Sir,

                        In compliance with your letter no. and subject mentioned above, report on assessment of security threat in respect of AR Ground and Raj Bhawan premises which were the proposed venues for the forthcoming Independence Day celebration, 2024 by Mizoram State Government along with all the protected persons of the state is hereby furnish as follows.

 

                        During the course of threat perception assessment, sources and contacts, links and informants for insurgent groups having operating area adjoining to the interstate and international border were contacted besides constantly monitoring their activities and movements. The ongoing Myanmar conflict since the military coup in 2021 and the ensuing arms struggles thereof by various resistance groups and ethnic armed organization (EAOs), political struggles by the Zo-ethnic communities of Bangladesh resulting in sizeable influx of refugees from both countries was taken into account. The crisis in Manipur having its repercussion of varying nature was also considered. Despite the existence and presence of several insurgent and resistance groups having base and operating areas alongside interstate and international border, it is learnt that at this moment none of these underground insurgent and resistance groups pose any threat to disrupt or thwart the scheduled celebration of the said national day. It is also further learnt that threat perceived against various protected persons of the state government from underground insurgent and resistance groups is minimal at the moment. It is pertinent to mention that impending issues undertaken by social organizations, student bodies, civil organizations were also taken into consideration.

 

                        It is concluded that, the threat perceive against various protected persons of the state government as well as the venues for the proposed national day from insurgent groups, student bodies, civil society organizations, political rivalry or even from personal animosity is considered minimal at the moment.

 

                        This is for your kind information and further necessary action please.

 

 

Yours faithfully,

 

 

Inspr. (JOHNNY RENGSI)

CID(SB), Field Branch
