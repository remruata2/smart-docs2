  1. Front Page of VANGLAINI Daily Newspaper  Dt. 12.09.2022

Duty Posts of YMA visited by Minister and MLA:  Duty posts erected by NE Khawdungsei town YMA in collaboration with Village Defence Party (VDP) in between Ne Khawdungsei and Ngopa town was visited on Saturday (10.9.2022) by Excise & Narcotics Minister, Dr.K. Beichhua and Pu L.Thangmawia, the constituency MLA. These duty posts, which are designated as - Check gate No.1 and Check gate No.2 were created in order to check the flow of smuggled narcotic substances around these areas. Check gate No.1, run by YMA members of NE Khawdungsei and Check gate No.2 under the responsibility of VDP, NE Khawdungsei have been manned continuously day and night for a period of 90 days and 20 years respectively. The visiting Minister and MLA also handed over financial assistance to the volunteers on duty in these Check gates.
