****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/252

Dated: 11th April, 2019.

 Input received indicated that 25 Assam Rifles have apprehended the following 11 (eleven) members of splinter group of HPC-D after conducting raid of their hideout at Rengkai, Churachandpur today, i.e. 11.4.2019 @ 1:00 p.m :-

1. Laldemlo Hmar, S/S Commander
2. Thangsiem, S/S Commander
3. Jacob
4. Thangrosuong
5. Hrangbiek
6. Hrangthakung
7. Chawngthakung
8. Lalhuoplien
9. Lalchawisang
10. Lien-a
11. Buong-a

 It is learnt that 1 (one) A1 Rifle with 2 (two) Magazines and 50 rounds of ammunitions have been seized from their possession. Their leader, Lalkhama Hmar @ LK Hmar, S/S C-in-C had managed to escape with unspecified number of weapons and security personnel are reportedly in pursuit. The security forces had been pursuing a tip-off that the group had been indulging themselves in intimidation allegedly campaigning for INC within Rengkai area.

 This group had recently break away from the HPC-D calling themselves as HPC-D Gen. Hqrs., Sinlung, Government of Hmar Ram and were known to be patronized by President’s faction of ZRO. While this group is in its fledgling stage, they have issued several diktats claiming control and majority support of Hmar community during recent period.

 It may be mentioned that most of them including their leader presently in hiding, except Sl. No. 8 – 11 were among the HPC-D who had come over-ground following the signing of MoS in April, 2018.
