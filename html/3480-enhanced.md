A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 20.3.2014_

**Road Mishap; One Killed****:**Lalrinthara (59) s/o Thangchhunga (L) of Khawzawl Vengthar met with an accident at around 10:00am yesterday at Zemabawk, he was ran over by 407 truck bearing regd. no. MZ 06 1179 and died on the spot.

 Lalrinthara was a pillion rider of CBZ Extreme bike bearing regd. no. MZ 01 F 4250 and they were standing behind 407 truck while waiting for other vehicles to pass. Their bike was hit from behind by a taxi bearing regd. no. MZ 01 D 7063 driven by V.Luaizamuana s/o LK Kama of Zemabawk which forced Lalrinthara down to fall from the backseat of the bike and was ran over by 407 truck bearing regd. no. MZ 06 1179 which was running in the opposite direction. Lalrinthara died on the spot but the rider of the bike was not seriously injured.

 Traffic Police said that Doctors confirmed that the taxi driver V.Luaizamuana was driving under the influence of alcohol, while 407 truck ran over Lalrinthara as he fell on its way and it was purely accidental. Both the drivers of taxi and 407 truck were arrested by the Police.
