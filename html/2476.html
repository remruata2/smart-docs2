MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/628

Dated 9th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/626
Dt. 8.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

BSF 93rd Battalion B-Coy (84 (eighty four) personnel) arrived Thinghlun today i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. and they are stationed at Govt. Comprehensive Primary & Middle School, Thinghlun. Upon arrival of BSF, 1(one) Platoon of 1st MAP deployed at Thinghlun was withdrawn.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          Around 200(two hundred) civilians, congress workers, media persons led by Smt. Sushmita Dev, President of All India Mahila Congress; Shri. Debabrata Saikia, Leader of Opposition, Assam Legislative Assembly and Shri. Kamalakhya Dey Purkayastha, MLA visited Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 12:00 noon and they returned to Assam at 12:40 p.m. after holding media interview at the bridge.

 

          Young Chorei Association General Headquarters in their Press Release issued today i.e., 9.11.2020 expressed regret regarding inclusion of their Association without their consent in a memorandum submitted to Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Panisagar, North Tripura on 5.11.2020 for blockage of inter-state border (Damcherra-Kanhmun) by various Brus Associations and other Associations. It also condemned blockage of road leading to Mizoram in Assam and Tripura due to the present border disputes.

 

There is no movement of vehicles across Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 9.11.2020.  Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Today i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 1:00 p.m. 1(one) Platoon of BSF arrived at Saihapui ‘V’ and they are temporarily stationed at Primary School, Saihapui ‘V’. Levelling work for SSB Camp deployed by Assam (at their present place of deployment) at Assam side is being conducted today @ 11:30 a.m. and in this regard, Pu H. Lalthianghlima of Phainuam lodge a verbal complaint to SSB personnel as the area is within his cultivation farm.

 

Further, Shri Dilip Kumar Dey, DIG (SR), Silchar, Smt. Keerthi Jalli, DC, Cachar, Shri Bhanwar Lal Meena, SP, Cachar arrived SSB(CAPF) place of deployment at Assam side, Assam-Mizoram border today’s evening @ 4:45 p.m. and they returned after inspection of the proposed SSB Camp levelling work at 5:25 p.m. Levelling work is still continuing at the time of writing the report i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 5:30p.m.

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          There is no movement of vehicles across Bairabi border today i.e., 9.11.2020.  Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

13 (thirteen) stranded vehicles due to blockade of vehicles bound to Assam via., Phaisen road at Zukbual Mual, Bilkhawthlir by Buhchangphai MZP was released today and MZP called off their road block w.e.f., today i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 1:10 p.m. A Press Released in this regard was issued today by MZP Sub-Headquarters, Kolasib.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          22 (twenty two) vehicles (17- Trucks (Tanker-11, Gas-6), 3 -Car, 2-MMV (Pickup/207) stranded at Mizoram House, Silchar entered Vairengte today i.e., 12:20 p.m. under the escort of Assam Police. In this connection, Zoram Drivers’ Union (ZDU) also called off their roadblock at Tawngtaina Hmun, Outskirt of Vairengte today’s evening at 4:00 p.m. and subsequently released all 175 (one hundred seventy five) stranded vehicles. Today’s evening at 7:30 p.m. 148 (one hundred forty eight) stranded vehicles crossed Vairengte Police Checkgate and proceeded towards Lailapur. The remaining stranded vehicles i.e., around 27 (twenty seven) vehicles are expected to cross Vairengte Checkgate soon. In this regard, ZDU submitted a letter informing lifting of their roadblock to DC, Kolasib today’s evening.

 

 Further, Information received indicated that residents of Lailapur and Hawaithang continued blockage of Mizoram bound vehicles carrying essential commodities at Lailapur after giving passage to the aforementioned vehicles stranded at Mizoram House, Silchar.

 

          Joint VC Vairengte and Vairengte Joint YMA organized community work for construction of BSF Camp today i.e., 9.11.2020 @ 10:00 a.m. at the proposed area, which is beyond Vairengte Auto Stand. They intended to construct 2 (two) 20 men Barracks, 1(one) Dining Hall, 1(one) Officer Room, 2(two) duty posts. Around 250 (two hundred fifty) volunteers  participated  in the said   community
