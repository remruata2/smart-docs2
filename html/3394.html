C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 7.2.2014

Hmingchungnunga – abducted by timber traders was released: Hmingchungnunga (42) h/o Lalrami of Bairabi was cheated by two timber traders from Hailakandi namely, Alta and Hussein. As if they wanted to buy timber from him at 11:00am on Tuesday.

            Bairabi community leaders said that Alta & Hussein are from Hailakandi who are doing Timber business. Selthuama of Hortoki had a debt of Rs. 4,41,000/- to them and regarding the debt Hmingchungnunga is the witness; and to get back the debt these two (2) timber traders abducted Hmingchungnunga.

            Alta and Hussein detained Hmingchungnunga at Alta’s house for a while but when they heard about Bairabi Police and community’s reaction, they lodged an FIR to Hailakandi Police Station and Hmingchungnunga was locked up at Hailakandi PS, sources said. They also informed Hmingchungnunga’s family that the debt must be paid before Wednesday 12:00 noon.

            After hearing the news, Bairabi Police, VC and NGO joined hands and did their best to solve the problem. On 4.2.2014, Bairabi Police went to Hailakandi and stayed the night at Hailakandi PS. Kolasib SDPO and Magistrate had a talk with the heads of Hailakandi Police and settled the issue on Tuesday, and Hmingchungnunga was released safely @ 3:00pm the same day.
