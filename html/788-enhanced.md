SEIZURE OF HEROIN

 Operation Team of CID(SB) stopped one Pick-up truck (MZ-01/N-7137) coming from Zokhawthar, at a location near Tuikhurhlu today, i.e. 22.8.2019 @ 3:10 p.m. Upon conducting search of the vehicle, Operation Team recovered 1.092 Kgs. of suspected Heroin No.4 contained in 78 (seventy eight) soap cases. Proper seizure has been made at the spot by observing all formalities and case being registered. 2 (two) persons were arrested, viz. (1) Thangliana (37 yrs.) s/o Bawiliana, Falam, Chin State, Myanmar and (2) Zoliansanga (45 yrs) s/o Kaithanga, Khuangleng, Champhai District, Mizoram (driver of the Pick-up).
Value of the seized contraband is estimated to be approximately Rs. 39 Lakhs. (Spl. Narco P/S C.No.24/19 Dt. 22.8.19 u/s 21(c)/29 ND&amp;Ps Act 1985)
