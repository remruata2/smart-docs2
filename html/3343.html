A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 17.9.2013

ACB filed 32 regular cases: Between the year 2009 and 2012, ACB received 83 FIR and has 32 regular cases in court which it had investigated. 67 names of people appeared in ACB’s 32 regular cases, among them are 4 (four) prominent politicians and Government officials like IAS, Doctor, Engineer, Director, Joint Director, BDO, Police officers and Managing Director. 11 (eleven) Government department’s names which appeared in the regular cases are Horticulture, DRDA, ZIDCO, FCS&CA, Land Revenue & Settlement, Health Services, Agriculture, Home and AH&Vety. department.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 17.9.2013

Abusive Drugs seized: A joint team of Special Narcotics PS Staff of SP CID (Crime) and BSF seized 11,382 tablets of Parvon Spas and 30 tablets of Pyeevon Spas at 1:00 PM yesterday. The alleged owner Lalfakzuala (38) of Zemabawk was also arrested. The value of the seized drugs is ` 4.7 lakh in local market.

 

C. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 17.9.2013

Lawngtlai Police Officers Training: As planned by Election Commission of India, training on Election concerned matters was held at Lawngtlai SP Conference Hall at Lawngtlai yesterday. The training was meant for and attended by Officers-in-Charge of different Police Outposts in Lawngtlai District and Police officers of SP Office, Lawngtlai.

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 17.9.2013

SP Notification on Sairang Road: Aizawl Traffic SP notified that vehicles departing from Kawnpui which will run into Sairang road can start only after 11:00AM. The notification is to be followed from Wednesday. The notification is made as ABCI wants to work on the dilapidated road. Unloaded vehicles departing from Aizawl are not included by this notification; they can use the road any time of the day.
