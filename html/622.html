            With reference to Home Department letter no. D.32020/21/2012- HMS   (Loose-1) dt. 23.1.2013.

            A meeting of HPC(D) delegates and officials of Govt. of Mizoram to finalize ground rules for peace talks is scheduled to be held on 31st January, 2013 at Mizoram State Guest House, Aizawl.
