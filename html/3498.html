A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.5.2014

Farm House Rebuilt at Buhchangphai: Farm houses occupied by farm caretakers of Buhchangphai Ramthar Zau dismantled by Assam Police & Forest personnel were rebuilt yesterday. A social work was called for the purpose and around 200 volunteers came. The social work was organised by Buhchang Branch YMA and some volunteers came from Bilkhawthlir village and Phaisen village. The newly built farm houses and their occupants are watched over by 1st IR Bn. personnel with Pu Lalrohlua, SDPO Vairengte, and they stayed over the night as well.

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 16.5.2014

A non-Mizo slashed Mizo: Hagi Jur, the husband of Lalchhanhimi slashed Hmingchawia (55) of Kepran on his head with a knife at around 10:00pm on Wednesday. According to reports, Hagi Jur sent his wife Lalchhanhimi to take liquor for him at Hmingchawia’s house; she was taking very long so he went to Hmingchawia’s house, they quarreled a bit and Hagi Jur just slashed with a knife on Hmingchaia’s head. Hmingchawia was taken to Aizawl Civil Hospital yesterday morning and his condition is not critical. Hagi Jur was handed over to Darlawn Police Station.
