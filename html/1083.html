Today, i.e. 22.9.2016 @ 10:30 AM, residents of Zohnuai Veng, Lunglei, numbering around 200 (two hundred) gathered infront of CJM office, Lunglei and demanded to see Stephen Lalchawiliana (26), S/o H. The residents of Zohnuai were reportedly irritated over the Hearing of the Case by CJM yesterday, i.e. 21.9.2016, prior to the predetermined date for hearing, i.e. today. The angry crowd pelted stones at the office of CJM and they reportedly destroyed window glasses and windowpanes of CJM office. They also forcibly tried to enter into the office of the CJM. However, Police have guarded the gate of the office and prevented the mob from entering the office. It was further reported that some sections of the mob have entered and ransacked the official quarters of Pu R. Malsawmdawngzuala, CJM, Lunglei located at AP Tlang, Lunglei and the quarters of one staff of CJM, Lunglei. They destroyed most of the furnitures, windows, TV, refrigerator, utensils, etc. and burnt the mattressess of Pu R. Malsawmdawngzuala. Leaders of Sub-Headquarters YMA, Lunglei also rushed immediately to CJM Office to pacify the angry crowds and acted as mediator. However, the effort of leaders of Sub-Headquarters YMA, Lunglei to control the situation had failed. The Executive Magistrate, Lunglei then declared impostion of Section 129 of CrPC for the dispersal of the unlawful assembly @ 4:15 PM. Acting upon the imposition of Section 129 of CrPC, the Police have arrested 17 (seventeen) members of the unlawful assembly and were kept into police custody. Criminal cases are being registered at Lunglei PS and the situation is under control for the time being. However, it was reported that no serious injury was sustained by Police personnel and staff of CJM, Lunglei. However, it was reported that Pu R. Lallunghnema, President, Zohnuai Branch YMA sustained a cut injury and was taken to Hospital for first-aid treatment.
