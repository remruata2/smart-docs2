ARAKAN ARMY

            The Arakan Army is a Rakhine insurgent group in Myanmar (Burma), founded on 10th April 2009. It is the armed wing of the United League of Arakan (ULA) and is currently led by Major General TwanMratNaing. The purpose of the AA is to protect Arakan people and to establish peace, justice, freedom and development.

            The AA is a participant in the Kachin conflict, fighting alongside the Kachin Independence Army (KIA) against the Tatmadaw (Myanmar Armed Forces). Most AA soldiers were originally trained at the KIA Military Academy; however, the AA has additional training camps in Rakhine State.

            The group has stated that it advocates for:

  • self-determination for the multi-ethnic Arakanese population.
  • safeguarding and promotion of the national identity and cultural heritage of the Arakan people.
  • preserving "national dignity" and best interests of the Arakan people.

 

REPORT ON ALLEGED SETTING UP OF CAMP(S) BY ARAKAN ARMY (AA) INSIDE MIZORAM

Information has been received from Sister Intelligence Agency that the AA (Arakan Army) is setting up camps at Kakichhuah areas, along the Indo-Myanmar border on Mizoram soil. In this regard, an enquiry was conducted to ascertain the stated input. But after conducting thorough enquiry by engaging reliable sources and others means, it is reported that there is no evidence to suggest the existence of AA camp inside Mizoram.

Known AA leaders were also contacted and they reported that they have no camp inside Mizoram and reiterated that they have no intention to establish camp(s) in Mizoram in the foreseeable future. As per input, the nearest AA camp inside Myanmar is around 15 kms from the Indo-Myanmar border.

RECENT ACTIVITIES:

It is learnt that AA had already shifted their camps along the Bangladesh – Myanmar border since 2018 and Myanmar Army and AA still continued their cross firing till today. On 3.12.2019, two leaders of NLD party namely U Hawitin (41), MP of Paletwa Town and U Ye Tein, President of Bu Ti Tawng Township were kidnapped by the AA. Though AA promised to release U Hawitin before Christmas, he is under their custody till today and his whereabout and condition is not known. In the meantime, U Ye Tein was reported as deathin the fighting between AA and MA on 24.12.2019.
