A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.5.2014_

**Dinthar People Gathered at Court****:**Some people of Dinthar Veng, Aizawl gathered at court yesterday to see Malsawmtluanga (21) s/o Ronghinglova of Ramrikawn who was to appear before court, he was alleged of raping of 7 year old girl at Dinthar on 4th May 2014.

 Dinthar Veng people said that they will do whatever they can to fight gainst crime and will not ignore the suffering of innocent children. And for the safety of women and children they pointed out the need for the empowerment of Crime Against Women.

 Dinthar Veng Local Council, YMA, MUP, JAC and MHIP submitted a letter to the court requesting not to release Malsawmtluanga and Lalzuiliana - (the alleged rapists) on bail while their cases are being dealt with at court.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.5.2014_

**Appeal for Bail Considered****:**The appeal to release Selam community leaders on bail was considered by CJM Court of Champhai yesterday. The appeal will be considered again on 3rd June 2014. Meanwhile, all the police sent to Selam have withdrawn.
