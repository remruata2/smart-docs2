**A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 9.5.2013**

**Pseudoephedrine seized**: Taxation officials on duty at Vairengte check gate recovered a consignment of 49,770 tablets of Pseudoephedrine from one Maxicab coming from Guwahati yesterday. Vairengte Police arrested five passengers including the driver Khairul Islam s/o Inta Ali of Assam.

**B. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 9.5.2013**

**Minister visited MAP School**: Education Minister, Pu Lalsawta paid a visit to 1st Bn.MAP English Medium School at Armed Veng South yesterday. The School was established by 1st MAP in 1999 with 72 students and 5 teachers. Now, it has 17 teachers with 421 students. The School is run by donations contributed by each employee of the Battalion from their salary. Pu Lalsawta praised MAP for establishing the School and giving importance to education for the better future of the children.
