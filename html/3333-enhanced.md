**Kidnapper arrested****:** A lass who kidnapped a 6 year old girl from her home ie, Kawnpui was arrested along with her victim in a non-Mizo truck between Kawnpui and Bualpui on Monday at around 11:00 AM.

On hearing from the tea stall worker that a girl was kidnapped, the truck driver suspected his passengers and told the tea stall workers and found they were the ones being searched.

 According to the kidnapper, she took the girl to Aizawl on Sunday night and took her back to Kawnpui from Company Peng of Hunthar Veng. The kidnapper was sent to Kolasib District Jail, the angry crowd of Kawnpui went to District Jail to see the kidnapper but the Police did not allow as she is a Juvenile. The Police and the crowd scuffled a bit but nothing untoward happened, sources said.
