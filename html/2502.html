REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT

 

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/563

Dated: 12th October, 2020           

 

          On 9.10.2020 @ around 2:40 p.m. one Jhum Hut belonging to Pu John Zolawma S/o  of Thinghlun, located at Zaite Road Assam side around 150 meters from Tlawngte Lui was set ablaze by Assam Police and Assam Forest Officials and due to this the said farm house was burnt down to ashes, and also destroying Areca nut plants belonging to Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David-a of Thinghlun. Due to this incident, SP, Mamit immediately stationed OC, Kanhmun PS and party and Two Sections of 4th IR personnel at the spot to monitor Law and Order situation.

            In order to maintain law and order situation at Assam-Mizoram Border near Thinghlun, Mamit District Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit and Pu Lallianmawia, IPS, CO, 4th IR Battalion are stationed at Thinghlun and Two Platoons of 4th IR Battalion are stationed at Zaite Road where Pu John Zolawma's Jhum Hut was burnt down on 9.10.2020 and another Two Sections from 4th IR Battalion are stationed at Thinghlun Village. In addition to the existing force deployment additional one platoon of 1st MAP is sent to Thinghlun today i.e., 12.10.2020.

            In connection with the above incident, in pursuance of their executive meeting held on 10.10.2020 leaders of MZP General Headquarters led Pu B. Vanlaltana, President MZP visited Thinghlun on 10.10.2020 and they were stationed at Zawlnuam. In their circular issued yesterday i.e., 11.10.2020 they stated their intention to reconstruct the said Jhum Hut on 14.10.2020 through mass community work and appealed interested persons to submit their name before 12.10.2020 @ 12:00 noon.

            Pu Vanlalhlana, MLA, Pu Andrew H Thangliana, MLA and Pu Lalchhuanthanga, MLA also visited Thinghlun on 10.10.2020 in connection with the incide­nt.                             

            Further, a Public Meeting was held at Community Hall, Thinghlun on 10.10.2020 @ 11:45 a.m. under the chairmanship of Dr. Lalrozama, IAS, DC, Mamit and Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit; Pu J. Malsawma, President, Langkaih Group YMA; Pu Vincent Lalpiantluanga, President, MSU, Mamit; Pu Lalthangmawia, President, Mamit Thalai Pawl; Pu Lalthlanmawia, President, MZP Headquarters, Zawlnuam and various leaders of Thinghlun NGOs were present and around 80 (eighty) persons attended the meeting. During the meeting, the Chairman appealed the people of Thinghlun to wait for Government action and to submit FIR to Kanhmun PS and  Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit also stated that necessary legal action will be taken. After the meeting, the place of incident was visited and consequently two makeshift tents were set up for temporary accommodation of 1 (one) Platoon of IR personnel at Zaite Road, Thinghlun and Signboard erected by Assam Forest Department yesterday i.e., 9.10.2020 was also removed. Further, residents of Thinghlun village also intended to construct temporary camp which could accommodate 1 (one) platoon of armed personnel on 12.10.2020.  SDO(Civil), Kawrthah submitted FIR to OC, Kanhmun PS on 10.10.20 in connection with the incident and a case has been registered vide No. Kanhmun PS C/No. 12/2020 Dt. 10.10.2020 u/s 506, 120 B, 434, 436, 447, 427 IPC.

            On Dt. 11.10.20 @ around 3:30 p.m., around 15 (fifteen) Assam Police personnel came with 2 (two) LMVs  (Sumo and Gypsy) near Pu John Zolawma's Jhum Hut which was burnt down on 10.10.2020 at Mizoram-Assam border. Although they can be seen from 4th IR temporary camp set up after the incident, they did not make any verbal or physical contact with OC, Kanhmun PS or 4th IR personnel currently stationed at the area. They returned back to Assam with police siren on after taking photographs of the area where Mizoram Police set up new temporary camp due to the incident.

            Whereas there are evidences in various local and national news channels wherein District Administration authorities of Karimganj, Assam stated that the above act was done at their behest, Assam Police personnel summoned leaders of Zaite Village, Assam on 11.10.2020 and enquired about the person(s) responsible for burning of the said Jhum Hut of Pu John Zolawma and cutting down of Areca nut plants belonging to Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David-a of Thinghlun.

            Information has been received that today i.e., 12.10.2020 @ 6:00 a.m.
Er. Lalrinawma, Deputy Speaker along with leaders of MNYF proceeded to Thinghlun Village, and Dr. ZR Thiamsanga, MLA, Er. H. Lalzirliana, MLA and Pu C. Lalmuanpuia, MLA also proceeded to the said village today's morning.

            Information has also been received that under the aegis of Langkaih Group YMA temporary camp for security personnel is being constructed at Zaite Road Thinghlun and around  200 (two hundred)  members participated. 

            Pu SBK Singh, IPS, DGP Mizoram has taken up the matter and spoke to Addl. Chief Secretary (Home), Govt. of Assam, DGP Assam and Special DGP (Border), Assam expressing concern of the people and Government of Mizoram. Further, DIG (NR), Mizoram is in contact with his counterparts DIG (SR), Silchar, Assam and SP, Mamit is in touch with SP, Karimganj in connection with the said incident.

            Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
