MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/92

Dated 5th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/89
Dt. 4.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV - 2, MMV -4, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 5.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV -8, MMV - 13, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 5.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm.8 (eight) vehicles (MMV-8, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          3 (three) journalists of Times Now (News Agency) led by Arindam Das, Reporter of Times Now visited bomb blast site at Mulibali LP School today i.e., 5.2.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. and after preparing ground situation report they left the area at 11:30 a.m. 5 (five) police personnel each from Mizoram and Assam were deployed at the area to prevent any untoward incident.

94 (ninety four) vehicles (HMV-54, MMV-3, LMV-33, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 5.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

188 (one hundred eighty eight) vehicles (HMV-87, MMV-48, LMV-17, LPG-4, Maxi Cab -11, Oil Tanker -19, Bus - 2) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 5.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.

.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
