Sir,

 With reference to your letter regarding application under RTI Act 2005, I am furnishing the required information as under.

1) Kum 2010 - 2015 (upto 15th June) chhung hian Mizoram Police ten Dan lova Silai chi hrang hrang kawltu/zuartute hnen atangin silai 318 (zathum sawmpariat) an man tawh a ni.

2) Mizoram Police ten Silai an man chi hrang hrang te Pistol/Revolver etc. te hi a mantu ten a siamtu Company/Country hming an rawn ziah lan ngai loh avangin a siamtu Company/Country hming chu kan chhang thei lo a ni.

3) Mizoram Police ten Pistol/Revolver an man te hi tih fel ngai zawng zawng te tihfela ziah fel anih hnu ah case siamtu Police Station OC in Silai te chu case kaihhnawih lehkha pawimawh zawng zawng te nen Court-ah a thehlut thin a. Court-in case a tihfel hnuah, Silai te chu District Magistrate Chairman na hnuaia Board-in a tihral dan tur a ngaihtuah thin avangin hemi chungchang hi chu DC Court lamin an chhang thei ang.
