****ABDUCTION OF TWO BENGALIS FROM RAJIVNAGAR ON 14.6.2014 @ AROUND 12:30 PM****

 On 14.6.2014 at around 12:30 PM, a combined NLFT - Bru militants under the command of S/S Lt. B.B. Jamatia @ Bomtum of NLFT militants operating from Sazek Range of Bangladesh numbering around 7 (seven) and carrying 4 (four) nos. of AK-47, knife, etc stopped one pick-up vehicle bearing Regn. No. TR-023-1949 coming from Rajiv Nagar, Mamit District at about 4/5 kms before reaching Zozampui 5th IR BOP. The occupants of the vehicle were 1 (one) Nepali, 2 (two) Chakmas and 3 (three) Bengalis who came from Kanchanpur, North Tripura for marketing at Rajiv Nagar, Mizoram. They were abducted while returning back towards Kanchanpur, North Tripura. When the militants stopped the vehicle, one Bengali guy managed to escape from the scene and the rest (five persons) were taken to the jungle by the militants. Soon after, one Nepali and two Chakmas were released by the abductors and the following Bengali guys namely:-

 1) Ranjit Nath (47yrs), Merchan

Dosda, Kanchanpur, North Tripura

 2) Nikinbo Purkasta (32yrs), Shopkeeper

 Dosda, Kanchanpur, North Tripura

are still in the captivity of the suspected NLFT, and sources stated that the 2 (two) kidnapped Bengalis are being kept at Tarunpara Camp, Bangladesh. In tis connection West Phaileng PS C/No. 12/14 dt. 15.6.2014 u/s 392/364A/36 IPC r/w 25 (1A) Arms Act has been registered.

Mizoram Police team under the command of C. Vanlalvena, SDPO, West Phaileng immediately proceeded to the Place of Occurrence to take stock of situation. Dy.CO, 5th IR Bn, Addl. SP, Mamit and Addl. SP, CID (SB) were also proceeded to Rajiv Nagar.

 Input received also indicated that the abductors had demanded ransom of _Rs.10(ten) Lakhs_ for the release of the hostages.

 Mr. Nikinja @ Nikinbo @ Nikunjoy Purkasthya (32 yrs), S/o Nihar Ranjan Purkasthya, Dosda, Kanchanpur, North Tripura managed to escape from the captivity of the captors/abductors on the early morning of 25.6.2014 @ 3:30 AM and after a night halt in a jhum hut (near Indo-Bangla Border), he safely reached New Kawnpui, Tripura-Mizoram border today, i.e. 26.6.2014 @ 8:30 AM. He was one of the two hostages abducted by a combined operation team of NLFT and Bru militants on 14.6.2014 near Indo-Bangla Border at about 4/5 Kms before reaching Zozampui 5thIR Bn. BOP from Rajiv Nagar. He was taken to Vanghmun Police Station, Tripura by Tripura Police and his statement was recorded by Mizoram Police.

 On 3.7.2014, one of the abducted persons Ranjit Nath(47) S/o Noresh Nath, Dosda, Kanchanpur was released by the abductors after payment of ransom of Rs.4 lakhs.
