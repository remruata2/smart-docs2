A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 18.08. 2022_

**Body of drowned boy on 2nd August, 2022 found:******The corpses of drowned Father and son on the evening of 2nd Aug., 2022 in Tlawng River, Aizawl was seen last evening, though the dead body of the son was recovered, the dead body of the Father disappeared before it was fished out. Local people and NGO’s volunteers resumed the search for the dead body of the boy’s Father after it was spotted floating in the river. The search of their dead bodies was previously halted after it was carried out one whole week since the day of the incident.

B. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 18.08.2022_

**Business plan competition being organised by Tourism** : For the first time in Mizoram, Tourism Start-Up Fest &amp; Competition 2022 was inaugurated at Aizawl by Tourism Minister Robert Romawia Royte on Wednesday. The Minister in his speech stressed out the importance of this fest, which could in turn uplift tourism and provide employment opportunities for many. He further added his desire for the youth of Mizoram to participate as many as possible in this event which could yield fruitful outcome for their future career. Willing contestant may apply for the competition by submitting their ‘business plan’ to Tourism Department latest by September 14.
