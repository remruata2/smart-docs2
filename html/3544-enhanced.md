A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.12.2022_

****164 cases through Social Media**: During the period between 2020 - October 2022, 164 social media related criminal cases have been registered in Mizoram. As per information received from CID (Crime) through RTI, Cyber Crime Police Station was established in Mizoram from the year 2020 where cyber crime related cases has been registered against 23 males and 12 females where 21 of them were mizos while the remaining 2 persons were non-Mizos. Out of the 164 cases, the most common crimes were financial fraud (102cases), followed by sexual harassment and identity theft with 32 and 21 cases respectively. Other cases includes, child pornography (7cases), false rumours (6 cases) and harassments through social media (5 cases ). Mizoram police officials stated that as they are adequately equipped, they had no difficulties in their investigations, however, delays in receiving information from service providers and banks often hampers their investigations.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.12.2022_

**Covid-19 free Mizoram:******On 19th December 2022, 72 swab samples were tested for covid-19 in Mizoram where all the result were negatives and there are no covid 19 patients currently undergoing treatments. So far, 19,92,696 samples have been tested in Mizoram where 2,38,964 were detected as positives.

**C**. _Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.12.2022_

****END destroyed 2,442 bottles of liquor:** Mizoram Excise&amp; Narcotics Department (END) on yesterday destroyed 2,442 bottles of various types of liquor which were seized by them after the implementation of MLP Act 2019 at the vicinity of their office located at Khuangpuilam, Kolasib. Under the guidance of Mizoram END Joint Commissioner, Pu Z.Lalhmangaih, these liquors were destroyed as they were already cleared by the concerned Court. Altogether,1,992 bottles of whisky, 426 bottles of Rum ,and 24 bottles of vodka were destroyed on this day.**
