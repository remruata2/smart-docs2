A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.23.11.2022

Around 300 refugees from Bangladesh in Mizoram: As a result of the combine actions of Bangladesh Army and Arakan Army against Kuki Chin national Army (KNA) in and around the Indo- Bangladesh border areas, around 300 refugees have entered India (Mizoram) seeking shelter. Most of these refugees, who are mainly of ethnic Mizo clans from Bangladesh entered Parva village of Lawngtlai district, Mizoram. In connection with this crisis, Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) had requested Mizoram Chief Minister to take all possible steps to comfort and provide assistances to these refugees and also requested various YMA Sub Headquarters, Groups and Branches to provide assistances to the refugees. The meeting of Council of ministers held yesterday (22.11.2022) also discussed the plight of these refugees and agreed to assist them in all possible ways.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 23.11.2022

Cabinet meeting approved setting up of Border Management Cell: The meeting of Council of Ministers was held yesterday (22.11.2022) at CMO Meeting Room, Aizawl under the Chairmanship of Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister. This meeting approved the establishment of  Border Management Cell under Mizoram Home department who will look after the border dispute as well as the proposal to promote 118 Constables to the rank of Head Constables under Mizoram Police department. Other points of deliberation of this meeting includes -

 

a)  Mizoram Energy Conservation Building Code Rules , 2022.

b)  Mizoram Code of Wages Rules, 2022.

c)  Mizoram ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Bill,2022.

d)  Construction and extension of Lunglei District Court building.

e)  The Mizoram Municipalities Rules, 2018 (Amendment).

f)  Proposal for transfer of functions to Lunglei Municipal Council.

g) Revoking of the decision of Council of Ministers' meeting of 2019 regarding the        handing over of   CHANEM building to Law& Judicial department.

h) The Draft Mizoram Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Rules, 2022.
