****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/89

Dated: 11th February, 2019.

Information has been received that All NGO Coordination Committee (NGOCC) held a meeting today, i.e. 11.2.2019 @ 2:30 p.m at Central YMA Office, Tuikhuahtlang which was presided over by Pu Vanlalruata and attended by 17 (seventeen) members.

 The meeting deliberately discussed about the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, 2016 (CAB). A report on the recent visit of New Delhi and their meeting with Union Ministers and other high officials under the banner of NESO regarding their objection of the CAB, 2016 was given by Pu Vanlaltana, Vice President, MZP and Pu Ricky Lalbiakmawia, Representative &amp; Fin. Secretary, NESO and highlighted the intention of the Union Government to adopt the Bill albeit severe opposition from the North Eastern states. Subsequently, the meeting resolved to observe “Protest Day”, to show their protest against Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, 2016 in front of Vanapa Hall tomorrow, i.e. 12.2.2019 @ 1:00 p.m. Information received further indicated that they considered this as the first step in their intended continuous protest against the CAB while other future course of actions will be chalked out later.

 The NGOCC issued a Press Release to this effect today and requested the people of Mizoram to come out bearing black flags for the protest.

 NGOCC meeting also requested other Districts to observe the Protest Day under the banner of their respective NGOCC but there is no report of any intention to observe the “Protest Day” from other districts till the writing of this report.

 Necessary security arrangement has been made by SP, Aizawl. Situation is kept under close watch.
