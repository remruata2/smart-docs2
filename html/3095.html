A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 30.4.2013

515 rape cases in 6 years: There were 515 rape cases in Mizoram for the past 6 years i.e., in between 2007-2012, rape was most prevalent in the year 2012. According to the police record, there were 83 rape cases in 2007, 77 in 2008, 83 in 2009, 92 in 2010, 77 in 2011, out of 103 rape victims last year i.e., 2012, 83 were minor.

           

           

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.4.2013

Woman found dead: On the morning of dt. 28.4.2013 ie, Sunday, Sangtei (25) w/o Jiblal, a daily laborer at Challui stone quarry near Hmar Chaltlang village was found dead in their camp. Kawnpui police arrested her husband on charge of murdering his wife.
