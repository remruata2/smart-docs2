****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/310

Dated 19th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/304 Dt. 16.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV-11, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 19.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-27, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 19.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-42, MMV-4, LMV-24, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 19.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

166 (one hundred sixty six) vehicles (HMV-70, MMV-39, LMV-18, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-27, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 19.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
