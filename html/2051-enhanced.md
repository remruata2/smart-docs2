**ACTIVITIES OF LAWNGTLAI DISTRICT LAND OWNERS' ASSOCIATION (LDLOA)**

 Information has been received that around 200 (two hundred) members of Lawngtlai District Land Owners' Association (LDLOA) led by Pu H. Remmawia, President visited RTS Company Camp on 13.3.2020 @ 11:30 a.m, which is located 10 kms. away from Lawngtlai and vandalised 2 (two) Assam type buildings by breaking the wall tiles. Around 120 (one hundred twenty) workers of RTS Company fled away from the Camp, but later returned after departure of LDLOA members. Thereafter, a General Body Meeting was held at Rulkual Peng on KMMTTP road and the following Resolutions were passed :-

 i) To revoke cancellation order of DPR No.1 of 2016.

 ii) Immediate payment of compensation.

 iii) To stop construction work on KMMTTP road w.e.f., 13.3.2020.

 iv) Government should transfer Pi Shashanka Ala, IAS, DC Lawngtlai and  Pu C. Lalhumhima, EE, PWD Highway Division.
 Effigies of Pi Shashanka Ala, IAS, DC Lawngtlai and Pu C. Lalhumhima, EE, PWD Highway Division were burnt by LDLOA members on this day's General Body Meeting and slogans demanding for their transfer were raised. LDLOA will decide their future course of action in due course.

 In this regards, the following LDLOA leaders were arrested by Lawngtlai Police on 13.3.2020, and registered a case against them at Lawngtlai Police Station vide Lawngtlai PS Case No.18/20 dt.13.3.2020 u/s 188/427/34 IPC. However, the arrested persons were released on bail on the same evening i.e. 13.3.2020.
