****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/129

Dated 24th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/126 Dt. 23.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

24 (twenty four) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-10, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 24.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-15, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 24.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-3, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

57 (fifty seven) vehicles (HMV-42, MMV-1, LMV-10, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 24.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

160 (one hundred sixty) vehicles (HMV-45, MMV-39, LMV-29, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-10, Oil Tanker-32, Bus-2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 24.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
