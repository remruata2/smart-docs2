A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.10.1.2013

 

More cases filed against HPC(D) leaders: HPC(D) leader H. Zosangbera and S/S Dy.Army Chief Biaknunga were granted release on bail by the Chief Judicial Magistrate on 8.1.2013, but the police filed new cases against them and could not be bailed.

               In most of their cases, HPC(D) leaders were granted release on bail by the court, and in the cases which they were not allowed bail, CJM granted them bail on 8.1.2013 but as the Mizoram Police feels it was better not to release them, they filed another case against them to the Court which prevented their release.

               H.Zosangbera was arrested on 17.7.2012 at Delhi Airport and S/S Dy.Army Chief Biaknunga alongwith S/S Army Chief Lalropuia were arrested on 10.6.2012 at Silchar Airport.

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 9.1.2013 & Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 10.1.2013

 

Police lost gun found after 5 days: 9mm Carbine was lost by 2nd IR Bn. Naik. S. Ngurvunga on the night of 30.12.2012. S.Ngursavunga was at the house of a widow of NE.Tlangnuam village and this widow's son stole the gun with its case and went away after a thorough investigation. The gun with its 26 ammunitions were recovered on 3.1.2013. S.Ngursavunga was suspended from his job on 8.1.2013.

              The son of a widow is an alcoholic and drug addict, and he was the main accused from the outset, he confessed to the crime and he hid the gun in the graveyard of NE.Tlangnuam where it was fully recovered with its ammunition. The accused is being detained in Saitual Police Station.
