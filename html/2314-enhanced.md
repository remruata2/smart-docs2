****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/035

Dated 14th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/22 Dt. 13.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV – 4, MMV – 15, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 14.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV- 3) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 18 (eighteen) vehicle (HMV – 2, MMV – 16, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 14.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 6 (six) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 6) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
