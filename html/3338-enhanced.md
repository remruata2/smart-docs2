_Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.09.2013_

****Burgled of Darlawn BDO Office****:****

On the night of 8.9.2013 (Sunday) Darlawn BDO Office was burgled and stolen away iron safe. The culprit Lalnuntluanga (33) S/o Dengchhuana of Laipuitlang was arrested while he was proceeding to Aizawl and produced to Aizawl Police Station along with the stolen property for further necessary action.
