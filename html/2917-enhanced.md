****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/90

Dated: 11th February, 2019.

Information has been received that National Trade Union of Mizoram (NTUM) is intending to hold a peaceful Sit-in Demonstration tomorrow, i.e. 12.2.2019 @ 11:00 a.m outside the main gate of New Secretariat Office. Information further added that their motive is to simply wait for the outcome of the Cabinet Meeting which is scheduled to be held tomorrow @ 1:00 p.m in which they expected discussion on revision of the minimum wages. Information added that NTUM had demanded raising of Minimum Wages since 2018 as follows:

1. Un-Skilled - Rs. 380/-
2. Semi-Skilled - Rs. 420/-
3. Skilled-II  - Rs. 520/-
4. Skilled-I  - Rs. 640/-

 There is no indication of the possibility of any untoward incident while writing the report since they are expecting a positive outcome from the Cabinet Meeting.

 Necessary security arrangement has been made by SP, Aizawl and the situation is kept under close watch.
