PAITE TRIBE COUNCIL’s MIZORAM SUPPORT FOR MNF IN 2018 MLA ELECTION

 

          A resolution was passed by the Joint Meeting of Paite Tribe council General Hqrs. and Paite Tribe Council, Mizoram on 21.9.2018. According to this resolution, PTC resolved to assist MNF party in the Mizoram 2018 MLA election. In this regard, Pu Dochinthanga, Sr. Vice President, PTC Mizoram was entrusted to take necessary steps, and to be the signatory if an MOU could be signed between both the parties i.e PTC and MNF party. Further, PTC convener, Pu Muana Hangsing even issued a campaign deployment roster for the members which is as under:-

  1. Lengteng A/C                 -        PTC Hqrs. with Pi Vanlalveni

                                                President PTC, Mizoram Hqrs.

  1. Champhai A/C               -        Pu V Ginsiam and team
  2. Champhai South A/C    -        Pu Muana Hangsing Team
  3. East Tuipui A/C            -        Pu Douchinthang

Sr. Vice President and Team

  1. Chalfilh A/C                  -        Pu G.Thangsangmuana, Gen. Secretary and Team
  2. Aizawl West – II A/C     -        Pu RC Thomte, Secretary and Team
  3. Aizawl East – I A/C       -        Pu Lalhmingsanga, Vice President and Team

 

According to report received, four Leaders of PTC were indeed campaigning for MNF party in Mimbung, Hrianghmun, Taikhang and Kawlbem villages. Though the actual date of this campaign process could not be ascertained, it can be assumed to be between 23rd -25th October 2018.

 

      In another incident, Leaders of PTC such as 1) Thangkhanmuan, (Ex. President, PTC) Adviser, 2) G. Thangsangmuan, GS 3) Paukhanlal, Fin. Secretary 4) V. Ginsiam, Treasurer went to Mimbung Village on 18 November 2018 and muster VC members, Political Party Leaders and Church leaders at Mimbung community Hall. Here, leaders of PTC requested all Paite communities to support and vote for MNF candidate so as assist in the full implementation of Sialkal Range Development Council. They further added that, this message was purely a request, without any threats nor intimidations.

 

     However, this message was not accepted by all the members of the gathering and as such, Pi Vanlalveni, President PTC again deliverd speeches in Mimbung and Teikhang Villages explaining how PTC does not support any political party and that everybody should cast their vote freely.
