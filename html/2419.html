MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/133

Dated 26th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/132
Dt. 25.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

Thangram Indigenous Cultural Meet which was held during 24-25.2.2021 at Mualmawi, Assam was successfully concluded yesterday’s evening at 10:30 p.m. under peaceful atmosphere. Members of MZP General Headquarters who attended the meet left Mualmawi, Assam for Aizawl this morning.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-8, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 26.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 4 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

33 (thirty three) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-21, LMV-10) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 26.02.2021 @ 3:30p.m.
5 (five) vehicles (MMV-5, HMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

87 (eighty seven) vehicles (HMV-57, MMV-1, LMV-22, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 26.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

163 (one hundred sixty three) vehicles (HMV-64, MMV-37, LMV-22, LPG-7, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 26.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
