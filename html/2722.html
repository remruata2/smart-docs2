REPORT ON SEIZURE OF EXPLOSIVES

 

 

As per input received, on 20.1.2022 @ around 11:30 p.m. huge cache of explosives and other related illegal items were recovered by Assam Rifles at the outskirt of Zawngling village, Siaha District, Mizoram from 1 (one) vehicle (Tripper/909), B/R/No. MZ-07 7936, driven by MC Lallungrualpuia (41) s/o MC Laldingliana, Lawngtlai-III, with Lalthlengliana (30) s/o Nunnema, Lawngtlai-I, as the handleman of the said vehicle. The owner of the explosives, who allegedly hired the vehicle and boarded the vehicle, was reported to be Sangkima (29) s/o Lalsanga, Lawngkai, Paletwa Dist., Myanmar and the following items were seized by the Police from the said vehicle:

 

i)  Belox 83 mm Dia x 2.08 kgs (Total : 97 boxes, i.e., 1164 numbers of
                explosives).

ii) Indra ED, Aluminium detonator – 20 bundles, i.e., 1000 numbers of
                detonators.

iii ) Detonating Fuse (Codex) – 12 bundles, i.e., 4500 metres.

iv)  Rs.7,3500/- Indian Currency Notes of Rs.500 denomination.

v)  9,35,500 Kyats of Myanmar Currency (5000 kyat x 184 = 9,20,000; 1000 
                 kyat x 12 = 12000; 500 kyat x 7 = 3500)

 

In this regard, a Case vide No.1/2022, dated 21.1.2022, u/s 5 of Explosive Substances Act, r/w 14A Foreigners Act was registered at Tipa Police Station, Siaha District on the strength of an FIR submitted by Nb. Sub Khusraj Shrestha, ‘F’ Coy, 17 AR, and the above 3 persons were arrested and they were produced at Siaha Police Station on the same day.

 

It is believed that the seized explosives items were intended to be supply to Chin Defence Force (CDF), Mindat, Myanmar.

 

After conducting thorough interrogation, the arrested accused persons viz., Sangkima (29), s/o Lalsanga, Lawngkai, Paletwa Dist., Myanmar, Lalthlengliana (30), s/o Nunnema, Lawngtlai-I and MC Lallungrualpuia (41), s/o MC Laldingliana, Lawngtlai-III were remanded into judicial custody.
