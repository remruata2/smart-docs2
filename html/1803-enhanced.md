**21. Peace Dialogue :**

****Due to the initiative of SP CID(SB). 4 (four) rounds of Peace Dialogue between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) have so far been conducted within the year 2016 and 2017. The 1st round of Peace Dialogue was held on 10th August,2016 and the 2nd round of Peace Dialogue was held during 5th to 6th October,2016, the 3rd round of Peace Dialogue was on 16th December,2016 and the 4th round of Peace Dialogue was held on 28th April,2017 where in the framework of agreement was worked out. The Peace Dialogue was concluded under conducive atmosphere and the next round of Peace Dialogue was tentatively scheduled to be held during mid July, 2017. SP CID(SB) has been entrusted to arrange all the logistic support to the representatives of HPC(D) during the Peace Dialogue.
