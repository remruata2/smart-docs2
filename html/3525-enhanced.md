A. _Front page of VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 25.08.2022_

****Meeting of DCs on border issues**: Yesterday i.e 24.8.2022,a meeting on border issues between Deputy Commissioners of the concerned districts of Mizoram and Assam was held at 225 CRPF camp, Vairengte. The meeting was attended by DC Kolasib, DC Mamit, SP Kolasib, SP mamit and DFO Kolasib from Mizoram while Assam state was represented by DCs and SPs of Cachar, Hailakandi and Karimganj districts. The main points of this meeting were :**

a) To enable cultivators of the border areas to look after their farms/fields properly.

b) Cooperation between the two states in protecting the reserve forest of the interstate border areas.

c) Improvement of police duty posts along the border while at the same time not to undertake other projects through these duty posts.

d) Resumtion of bridges construction at Tlawngte river and Zophai - Bairabi. This issue will be forwarded to higher authorities.

e) Ways to solve the problems faced by areca nut growers of Mamit district in selling their products inside Assam state.
