A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.6.2013

AR intends to have Police personnel to accompany them in checking of vehicles: Assam Rifles representatives said that in every checking of vehicles they perform, they want police personnel to accompany them. This was said in the meeting of different departments which maintain law and order at the office chamber of Champhai DC, Champhai DC presided over the meeting. In a recent incident, Assam Rifles seized 35 ammunitions of M-16 Rifle from a maxi cab and there was disagreement between the maxi cab driver and Assam Rifles which led to the decision of the Assam Rifles intends to have police personnel to accompany them in every checking of vehicles they perform.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.6.2013

An escaped prisoner nabbed: A Central Jail prisoner Lalhunvula (32) s/o Zairema (L) of Champhai Vengthlang who sneaked out from the office of Directorate of Prison on 13.6.2013 was nabbed by Excise duty on 19.6.2013 (Wednesday) @ around 10:00PM at Zemabawk Forest Check gate in a Zokhawthar maxi cab and 2 grams of heroin was seized from him.
