A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.12.2022_

**Six vehicles torch ablaze**: On 10th November 2022, six vehicles accused of transporting illegal areca nuts and poppy seeds were set ablaze on a roadside in between Zawlnuam town and Zamuang town, at about 1.5 Kms. from Zamuang town. The vehicles, which includes four Pick Ups and two trucks, were turned over before they were set on fire as they were accused of carrying 35 bags of illegal areca nuts and poppy seeds. During the initial stage, the drivers of the vehicles were not present in the place of occurrences and when their owners were contacted, they replied by saying that they have no idea about what the vehicles were carrying. A criminal case was also registered at Kanhmun Police Station in this connection.

**B**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.12.2022_

**Meeting in connection with G20:** Shri Narendra Modi, Prime Minister of India on 9th December, 2022 conducted a video conference in connection with the different meetings of Group of 20 (G20) to be held in India during the early months of 2023. The video conferencing, which was presided over by Shri Amit Shah, Union Home Minister, was attended by various Governors /Lt. Governors and Chief Ministers of India, including Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister of Mizoram. One of the meeting of this G 20 will be hosted by Mizoram during the month of March 2023 and the main topic of the meeting will be about Multilateral business partnership where greater stress will be placed on bamboo/horticulture, handloom &amp; handicrafts and nursing &amp; paramedics.
