 **ACHIEVEMENTS OF CID (SB) wef****17th December, 2021 to 22th June, 2022**

05.01.2022 On 05.01.2022, an operation team of CID(SB) seized 8(eight) cases of plastic soap containing suspected Heroin weighing 75 grams value of Rs. 4,00,000/- (four lakh) in the local smuggling rate from the possession of Michaela (29) S/o Lalramkapa, Ramhlun South @ Hunthar veng, Aizawl at Zarkawt Cemetery. The accused and the seized articles handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S, Aizawl. In this connection, Spl. Narcotic PS C/no. 01/2022 Dt.5.1.2022 U/s 21(b) ND&amp; PS Act was registered.

07.01.2022 An Operation Party after waiting a while at Melthum Silaimual, a suspected vehicle, Hyundai i20 B/R No.068780 was spotted and halted, inside which were three persons namely 1. Lalhriatpuia (29) s/o Paututhanga, Bazar Veng, Serchhip 2. Ephraim Lalhmangaiha (32) s/o Piangliana, P&amp;E Veng, Serchhip and 3. Zothanpari(44) d/o Vanngura(L), Bazar Veng, Serchhip. As the witnesses arrived, the suspected person were then informed that the operation party had suspected them of possessing substance in contravention of the provisions of ND&amp;PS Act. Subsequently, search of the suspected car was conducted in presence of the two local witnesses and inside the car was found Rambo polythene bag containing 16 numbers of plastic soap cases, which was strongly suspected to contain Heroin. On opening the plastic soap cases, packages of suspected Heroin packed in transparent polythene were found.

 In the presence of civilian witnesses, the suspected Heroin was then seized. It was then weighed, sealed and the packets were marked as A-I to A-IX, and B-I to B-VII. After weighing , it was also found that the packets of the suspected Heroin (No.4) weighs 210 grams valued at Rs.8,00,000/- (Eight lakhs) at the local market. The possessor of the illegal drug Lalhriatpuia (29) s/o Paututhanga, Bazar Veng, Serchhip was arrested after informing the ground of his arrest and intimation was communicated to his wife.

 The accused and all the seized articles were handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S, Aizawl, and FIR was also lodged against the accused. In this connection, Spl. Narcotic PS C/No. 02/2022 Dt.07.01.2022 u/s 21(b)/ 25 ND&amp;PS Act was registered.

08.01.2022 On Dt.07.01.2022, a precise information was received that a Tata Sumo B/R No.MA 01 C 4471 Driver, is in possession of contraband drugs.

 Since the location of the said driver was not exactly known, the operation party proceeded to locate the location of the mentioned vehicle in the city, and the vehicle was spotted at the roadside parking at Ramthar Area, near Govt. Zirtiri Residential College at around 08:00Pm.

 The operation party stationed at Bawngkawn Area intercepted the Vehicle on the morning of 08.01.2022, with only the driver in the vehicle, and who introduced himself as Jiban Uddin (33) s/o LT Cherag Ali, Vill-Fatehpur of Karimganj District. With the interception of the vehicle, arrangements of two reliable witnesses was made namely 1. Lalrikhuma Sailo and 2. Lalbiakhluna, both of Bawngkawn Area Vigilance Committee member.

 After the arrangement of the witnesses, and after preparation of Grounds of Belief, as per provision of u/s 42 ND&amp;PS Act, search of the vehicle was conducted and inside the vehicle, on the floor of the front seat was a red and black travel bag. On opening the travel bag, 20 bundles of suspected contraband drugs was found. Each bundles contained 5 other smaller bundles, inside which there were 10 small black plastic sachets containing 200 tablets on which 'R' were written on the tablets. The tablets were strongly suspected to be Methamphetamine. Counting of the tablets was done in the presence of the witnesses and there was a total of 2,00,000 tablets (two lakhs). Marking of the suspected Methamphetamine was done and were marked A-I to A-X and B-I to B-X. The total weight of the suspected Methamphetamine was 22.02 Kgs with a value of Rs.6,00,00,000/- (six hundred lakhs).

 The accused and all the seized articles were handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S, Aizawl, and FIR was also lodged against the accused. In this connection, Spl. Narcotic PS C/No.03/2022 dt. 08.01.2022 u/s 22(c)/25 ND&amp;PS Act was registered.
