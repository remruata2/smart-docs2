****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/578

Dated: 17th October, 2020

****1.**  Information has been received that yesterday evening i.e., 16.10.2020 between 6:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. 7 (seven) Assam Police and Forest officials visited COVID-19 duty post set up at the outskirt of Saihapui 'V' Village, Kolasib District (around 300 meters from Saihapui V) manned by 1st IR personnel (the duty post was previously manned by Saihapui VLTF) and they instructed the personnel on duty to vacate their duty post within two hours claiming that the duty post is within Assam territory and informed that they will again verify after two hours. They also stated that prior permission have to be obtained from DC, Cachar if they want to set up duty post at the said location. Upon hearing the incident, SDPO, Vairengte along with 16 (sixteen) IR personnel immediately proceeded to Saihapui 'V' and when they reached the area, Assam personnel had already left the area. Due to the above incident, residents of Hawaithang Village, Assam resorted to economic blockage near Lailapur Forest Checkgate w.e.f., 16.10.2020 @ 11:00 p.m. and the road block is still continuing at the time of writing the report.**

Further, today i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 12:00 p.m. around 200 (two hundred) civilians and 50 (fifty) Assam Police personnel came again at 1st IR Duty Post located at the outskirt of Saihapui 'V' and dismantled the duty Post. SDPO, Vairengte and IR personnel who were stationed at the spot tried their best to prevent the above action but they were out-numbered by the opposite party. Upon hearing the said incident, around 120 (one hundred twenty) civilians from Saihapui V, Buarchep, Saiphai, Vairengte and leaders of Vairengte MZP proceeded to said area but situation was under control. 

In connection with the incident, around 2:00 p.m. today i.e, 17.10.2020 Pu Vanlalzawna, VCP, Saihapui 'V' and Shri. Dilip Pal, Gram Panchayat, Hawaithang Village held a meeting and resolved the following :-

(i) Mizoram Police and Assam Police shall withdraw themselves from the area and contain their own people from escalating tension.

(ii) The current tension and communal disharmony between the two sides will be discussed and try to be settled by VCP, Saihapui 'V' and Gram Panchayat, Hawaithang.

(iii) Village leaders of Saihapui 'V' and Hawaithang will later discuss the issue of reconstruction of the dismantled VLTF duty post (IR duty post).

 6 (six) Leaders of MZP Kolasib Headquarters also left Kolasib for Saihapui 'V' at 2:30 p.m. today.

 In connection with the incident, MSU Headquarters Vairengte issued a notice today i.e., 17.10.2020 which stated that they will organize community work for reconstruction of Saihapui 'V' VLTF duty post and all those who intend to participate in the community work will have to gather near Multipurpose Hall and depart today evening i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 6:30 p.m. As per input, if it is inconvenient to reconstruct Saihapui 'V' VLTF duty post, they still intend to construct makeshift camp for IR personnel at a convenient place.

****2.** Information has also been received that DC Karimganj, SP Karimganj and DFO Karimganj visited Kalamati area, around 500 metres from Medili Bridge near Kanhmun Village, Mizoram-Assam Border and resorted to road blockage w.e.f., yesterday i.e., 16.10.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. In this connection, today i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 12:00 noon DC, Mamit and SP, Mamit visited the place of road block and at the time of their visit 6 (six) Assam Police personnel were on duty and no other officials were present. The road block is still continuing at the time of writing the report.  As per further input, residents of Rangamati Village, Assam organised procession today i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. and demanded complete opening of Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border and evacuation of the land claimed by them i.e., around 2 (two) Kms beyond Medili Bridge within 48 (forty eight) hours, failing which they intend to resort to economic blockage.**

**3.**  As per input, today i.e., 17.10.2020 @ 12:55 p.m. one lady Dy. SP from Assam Police had a short visit of Bairabi, Zophai area, Mizoram-Assam border and immediately returned without entering into Mizoram side.

**4.**  Further, information has been received that today, i.e., 17.10.2020 @ around 6:30 p.m., around 30 (thirty) persons, allegedly residents of Lailapur, Assam pelted stones at IR temporary duty Camp set up due to COVID-19 pandemic near Vairengte Autorickshaw Stand at Mizoram-Assam border. 4 (four) IR personnel were in the camp at the time of the incident. Enraged over the incident, Vairengte Joint YMA made public announcement asking all available members to proceed to Vairengte Autorickshaw Stand. Around 300 (three hundred) residents of Vairengte, some armed with sticks, dao etc. rushed to the spot and allegedly set ablaze 1 (one) house and around 7 (seven) temporary shops set up by Assam people at the disputed Mizoram-Assam border. All available police force at Vairengte were immediately deployed in the place of occurrence.

 As per information available, 7 (seven) Mizos of Vairengte viz. 1) Ramdina(50) of Bangla Veng 2) Lalngaihthanga (48) of Bangla Veng, 3) R.K Lalhmachhuana (37) of Tlangnuam Veng 4) Malsawmkima (30) of Bangla Veng, 5) Lalroliana (28) of Hall Veng, 6) Lalsangzuala (30) of Bangla Veng and 7) Lalengmawia (36) of Venghlun sustained simple injury on their bodies due to the ensuing physical clash between the two communities and Sl.
