****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/542

Dated:11th July, 2021

 

 In continuation of our Special Report vide No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/541 Dt. 10.7.2021 last night i.e., 11.7.2021 @ 2:40 a.m. and 2:43 a.m. 2 (two) loud explosions were heard inside the forest at the disputed area by Mizoram Police deployed at Pu Thangkhawliana's farm land, Buarchep and Saihapui 'V'. In this regard, extensive search of the area was conducted by Mizoram Police but not yet found out the reason of such loud explosion . It is suspected that the said incidents occurred at the side of Assam deployment and caused by Assam miscreant(s) in order to threaten Mizoram Police deployed near the area. In this connection, a case has been registered at Vairengte PS vide Case No. 16 Dt. 11.7.2021 u/s 3(a) Explosive Substances Act and investigation is being underway.
 Assam resumed motorable road construction work at Pu Chalchhunga's paddy field by engaging one Hitachi Excavator today. At the same time, Mizoram also resumed motorable road construction by engaging one excavator (JCB) in between Buarchep village and Saihapui river without any interruption. However, this afternoon @ 3:00 p.m., around 30 (thirty) residents of Phainuam and Buarchep gathered at the bank of Saihapui river, and a small confrontation occurred with road construction workers of Assam. Before tension escalate, the matter was immediately resolved after the intervention of Pu David J. Byhnadaolai, Addl. SP, Kolasib and party, who are being deployed at the area.

 Shri. Harmeet Singh, ADGP (Border, L&amp;O), Assam; DIG (SR), Assam; DC, Cachar; SP, Cachar and other officials visited the road construction site at Pu Chalchhunga's paddy field this afternoon @ 4:00 p.m.
 Information received stated that a large contingent of Assam Police arrived Hawaithang, Assam, which is around 3 km from Saihapui 'V' today. Assam also prepared LMG mount site at various locations at the side of their deployment.

 Situation is under close watch.
