In this regard, extensive search of the area was conducted by Mizoram Police but not yet found out the reason of such loud explosion . It is suspected that the said incidents occurred at the side of Assam deployment and caused by Assam miscreant(s) in order to threaten Mizoram Police deployed near the area.
