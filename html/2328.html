MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/576

Dated 29th July, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/573
Dt. 28.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          There was no entry of vehicle from Assam side at Medili Bridge today, i.e., 28.7.2021 as Assam Police continued to carry out road blockade at Medili Bridge, Assam side.

39 (thirty nine) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-30, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’,  Kolasib District :”

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

There was no entry of vehicle at Bairabi from Assam as blockade of road leading to Bairabi is still continuing at Ramnathpur, Hailakandi District today. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

            Pu Lalhuliana Fanai, DIG, Mizoram Police; Shri. Shah Nawaz, DIG, CRPF Silchar Range;  Shri. BB. Shethi, DIG, CRPF Tripura Sector visited Mizoram Police place of deployment situated at hillock near Vairengte Auto Stand and High School Tuikhuah hillock today, i.e., 29.7.2021 @ 12:30 p.m. During their visit, authority of CRPF assured the safety of Mizoram Police deployed at the area. Later the same afternoon, journalists from NE News

Note:

Today i.e., 29.7.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. Barak Democratic Front and Cachar Students Association staged Sit-in demonstration in front of Mizoram House, Silchar and around 50 (fifty) persons participated. During the Sit-in demonstration, placards condemning Mizoram Police and Assam government were displayed. The Sit-in demonstration was called off under peaceful atmosphere this afternoon @ 1:30 p.m. In this connection, 1 (one) section of CRPF 225th Battalion, Vairengte was sent to the area to reinforce the existing security force.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
