****HC MALSAWMKIMA****

 On 11.7.2014, one constable no.271 HC Malsawmkima S/o H.C.Kapthianga of Sakawrdai, Aizawl District, Mizoram posted at Sakawrdai Border Outpost (BOP) of 1st Bn Mizoram Armed Police (MAP) decamped the BOP and fled to Manipur carrying 1 nos. of. 5.56mm INSAS Rifle ( Butt No.73 and Reg.No-18244477) with 5 nos. of Magazine and 100 rounds of live ammunition and 1 nos. of AK-47 Rifle (Butt No.06 and Reg.No.KD445723) with 5 nos. of Magazine and 150 rounds of live ammunition. C/271 HC Malsawmkima was received by S/S Sgt.Kunga of HPC (D)(Hmar People’s Convention-Democratic) at Patpuihmun, Manipur on 12.7.2014 and accorded a warm welcome by HPC (D) cadres at HPC(D) designated camp located at Ankhachhuah (Kasur valley), Manipur on the night of 13.7.2014. It is a serious violation of the ‘Agreed Ground Rules for implementation of Suspension of Operation (SoO)’ on the part of HPC (D) as the HPC (D) failed to abide by the Agreement. In this connection Sakawrdai PS C/No.9/14 dt.12.7.14 u/s 380/406/409/121/122 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act has been registered.

 Another group of HPC(D) (Sangbera’s faction) cadres numbering 12 (twelve) armed with sophisticated weapons had infiltrated into Tinghmun village on the night of 22.11.2014. The group first approached the VCP who arranged a vehicle (Sumo Maxi cab) MZ 01D 9774 driven by Materema as requested by the HPC(D) and entered a Labour camp located about 5(five) kilometres towards Sakawrdai side @ 02 : 00 am (23.11.2014). The group then handed over an extortion letter demanding 10% of the estimated amount from the contractor, Pu Malsawmdawngliana s/o V. Neihkunga of Chanmari, Aizawl who is doing road construction works between Sakawrdai and Tinghmun. The letter is hand-written in HPC(D) letter-head purportedly signed by L.Hmar, and the amount to be paid before 10th December 2014. It is reported that 8(eight) Workers including Pu Lalhmingtana of Sakawrtuichhun, Aizawl Manager of the road construction were staying at the camp on that night. The group had taken 220 Gelatin sticks (approx) along with 100 detonators and also snatched all mobile phones of the workers. The mobile phones were however returned through the VCP of Tinghmun @ 09:00 am after the group left the village. **It is also reported that the group was led by S/S Sgt Maj Lalbieklien and Ex-Const Malsawmkima 1st MAP (deserter).** The group after having morning meal at the VCP’s house left Tinghmun in another Pik-up belonging to a Bengali trader upto Zokhawthiang. It is learnt further that the group left towards Manipur side via Tuiruang river after a brief meeting with VCP of Zokhawthiang.

 On 28.3.2015 @ 8:50 Am, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance members such as Pu R.L. Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Pu Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Nl. Lalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) Insurgent group numbering around 12 cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village. Three (3) Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while five (5) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about one (1) hour and forty five (45) minutes. The MLAs were accompanied in this tour by Government officials such as Pu Zothansanga Ralte, Under Secretary, Pu Lalrinawma Sailo, Committee Officer, Pi Lalchuailovi, UDC, Pu Zohmingthanga, Peon and Drivers - R. Hmingsanga and Lalpekhlua. In this connection Sakawrdai Police Station Case No. 6/15 dt. 29.3.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120B/326 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act has been registered.

 Information has been received that laying of the ambush was carried out under the command of Malsawmkima Hrangchal who deserted the 1st Bn. MAP (on 11.7.2014) and joined the HPC(D) outfit. The HPC(D) cadres were carrying sophisticated arms like AK- 47 and G-3 rifles. The HPC(D) outfit targeted the pilot vehicle which went ahead of the other vehicles.

1. 2 Nos. of AK-47 with several rounds of ammunitions.
2. 2 Nos. of 9 mm Glock Pistol.
3. 3 Nos. 9 mm Pistols.

 The HPC(D) cadres also fired upon the vehicles of 3 (three) MLAs causing partial damage.

 The injured persons were air-lifted from Sakawrdai at around 12:45 Pm and safely reached Aizawl at around 3:15 Pm and they were administered medical treatments at Civil Hospital, Aizawl on the same day.

 A funeral service for the 3 (three) martyrs of Mizoram Police was held on 29.3.2015 in front of 1st Bn MAP Commandant Office, Aizawl. Chief Minister, Ministers, Parliamentary Secretaries, MLAs, Chief Secretary, DGP and senior officials paid their last respects by laying wreaths on the coffins of the three martyrs.

 Speaking during the funeral service, Pu Lal Thanhawla, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Mizoram strongly condemned the ambush and said that such kind of violence is a disgusting challenge. "We accept their challenge and we shall respond to it as per conveniene," he said. The Chief Minister also announced an ex-gratia of Rs. 7 lakhs to the next kin of the three slained Policemen. He also consoled the bereaved families by assuring jobs under Police Department. Pu R. Lalzirliana, Hon'ble Home Minister also said that the blood of the slained Policemen who had served the State will not go in vain.
