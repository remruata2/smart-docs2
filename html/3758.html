VAITIN DT.24.3.1999 INKAHNA.

Vawiin Vaitin khaw chhung a inkahna thlen cham vawi -22 na lai a lo nita reng mai, an hming ka tarlang dawnlo a minlo hrethiam dawn nia.                                                                    

Dt 24.3.1999 khua a lo var fel hnu chuan Vaitin Khua Pa pakhat chu an khaw thenawm, Sakawrdai lam pan tum in Scooter in a in khalh chhuak a, khaw dai hnaite a thlen hian kawngsir ah chuan hmelhriat ngailoh Silai tha tak tak keng leh Sipai thawmhnaw inbel anlo awm a, anni chuan lo tih din antum chu inher in khaw lam ah chuan a tlan let leh a, he tia khua a luh hian thil awmdan an hrilh hlawm a. A chhan chu a hma deuh in Vaitin khua hian henglai vel a rammu pawl pakhat. Mizoram Accord Implement Demand Army (MAIDA) inti pawl, Mizoram sorkar leh HPC inremna a lungawilo pawl anni a, Anni ho hi Vaitin vel ah an che tla chu Vaitin khaw ho hian lo ngai thei vaklo a anlo inti thiamlo tawh a, khangho MAIDA  antih ho kha an lokal leh ah an ngai deuh ani.                                             

Ti chuan pa ho chu inpun khawm deuh a, khaw thenawm Sakawrdai a Police awmte pun turin Tlangval tlawmngai ke in an tlantir bawk a,  mahse chungho Silai kengte  chu khua ah chuan an rawn thlengve leh mai a, an han en chian deuh chuan MAIDA hmel lam awm annih hmel loh riau a, MAP mi aw te an han ti deuh a, an Silai ken kha a tha hlawm viau bawk nen, MAIDA antih ho kha chuan Silai mumal an kawl vaklo tih kha an hreve deuh bawk si a. Chutia anlo thlenve tak ah chuan Scooter chuang tlan let daih lak ah chuan an rawn thinrim hle a, Scooter chu namthlu in an hal a Silai pawh an hmet puak zui a, mahse tumah kah erawh anneilo ani.                                                Zualko te an thlen hma deuh chuan Sakawrdai Police camp awmte pawn Silai han ri deuh dap dap chu anlo hre pha a, hetih hunlai hian Hmar lam ah hian HPC nen inremna a awm tawh a vangin, Police camp ah hian Police hi an awm tlem khawp a, mi 10 awrh an awm ani awm e, chutia Silai a rik hnu lawk ah zualko anlo kal tak bawk ah chuan Camp awm thenkhat te chu an insiam nghal a, MAP lam hi an tlem viau a an commander ber chawlh lai ani a, Havildar Vai pakhat ho in an awm a, MRP lam pawh ASI ho in tlem an awmve bawk a. Ti chuan ASI chuan va kal phawt ang u, zualko pawn MAIDA ho anni ang antih kha, hlauhawm em em lo tur ah ngai ang ati a, MAIDA hian Ralthuam an lek ngailoh vang ani a, mahse Silai erawh a puak ngei bawksi a fimkhur tur chuan an inchah viau ani awm e. MAP constable Pali leh MRP ASI pakhat leh Constable pakhat te chu Police Station motor a thatloh a vangin, Shinlung Hills Development council motor Gypsy hawh chawp in, ASI chuan a khalh a, MAP pali te chuan AKM vek keng in a hnung lam ah an thu hlawm a, zualko Vaitin tlangval pahnih nen chuan. A hma ah midang pahnih an thu a, Vaitin khua chu an pan ta a.                                                                 

Vaitin khua a lo lut te hi Manipur a Helpawl pakhat, PREPAK (People Revolution Army of Kangleipak) antihte anlo nia, chutia khua an awmlai a, Sakawrdai lam tang a motor lo tlan an han hmuh tak ah chuan Police an nih anring nghal a, anlo awmna hmun hi Vaitin a MST Bus dinna hmun niin Bus Aizawl pan a chhuak mai tur pawh a la chhuak theilo a, PREPAK ho chuan thil awmdan fuhlo dawn a an hriat a vangin lo kap hmasa zawk nih tumin tanhmun an rem nghal hlawm a, kawng kam chhak leh rem chang lai a piang  hnai tak tak ah anlo changin, anlo biru hlawm a, Vaitin mipui te pawh tawm bo turin anti hlawm a, inchhung a tangin thinphu dep dep in anlo bihthla hlawm ani awm e.                                                          

Police te pawh lo tlan zelin Vaitin kawn ah chuan an rawn lang hlawl mai a, Police te chuan an van rehve anti hman deuh chauh ani, an dinglam leh hma lam a tang chuan Silai a rawn puakta zawt zawt mai a, Constable pathum a hma thu pakhat leh a hnung lam a thu pahnih chu an chhuak thei hram a, midang erawh chhuak tur an awm ta lo ani, an veilam pang ah hian In a awm a, a thlang lam erawh awih thla viau a, tah hian Pakhat chu zuangthla in ram lam ah tlan a ahim ta ani. Pahnih hnunglam zuang chhuakte erawh pakhat in ruak, a bang Dap, a chhung a chengte anlo chhuah tir vek ah tlan lut a, mahse an kah rawn nasa em a, a chhung ah hian a boral zuive ta ani. Pakhat erawh tlanlo in kawngthlang ah tanhmun khawh in a rawn kap let ve ta vak mai a,  a tlum leh zeuh thin anti, ti chuan tlan sawnlo in a rawn kah let tak ah chuan PREPAK ho tan pawn bawhmai chi anita silo a, an hual ta a, ti chuan a sir lam pang a tang an kap fuh theita hram ani.                                                                        

He mi inkahna ah hian mi pasarih an boral a, MRP constable pakhat dam in a chhuak tihloh chu Police panga leh Zualko Vaitin tlangval tlawmngai pahnih an boral ani, Heng hunlai hian mobile phone te la awmlo a chanchin inhrilh chhawn kha harsa em a pawita ani, Police te an huaisen in mipui thlabar hlau a thawk chhuakte an boral phah ta a, Tlangval tlawmngai te pawn an Khua tan an tlawmngaihna  ah an nunna an hlan zuita bawk ani. PREPAK lam hi pakhat a hliam ani awm a, Police AKM Pali leh 9mm Pistol anla ani. Gypsy pawh an kap chhe hneh viau ani awm e.                                                              

 

" Kan sakruang Mizoram tan.".
