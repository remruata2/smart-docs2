A. Front page of  VANGLAIN  newspaper  Dt. 27.09.2022

Mizoram Government to seek loan again through OMB : Mizoram state Government  is intending to borrow more loan through Open Market Borrowing (OMB) and necessary steps have been under taken by  Reserve Bank of India (RBI). This loan, which amounts to Rs.100 crores, will be the fifth time Mizoram state had borrowed during the financial year of 2022-2023.  Interest of any loans borrowed through OMB, which is 7-8 %, have to be paid twice in a year and all the debts had to be cleared within a span of 10 years. The ceiling limit of borrowing through OMB by Mizoram state during the current financial year is Rs.990 crores; presently, Rs. 540 crores is the total amount borrowed and with Rs. 100 crores borrowed again, the total credit of Mizoram state will amount to Rs. 640 crores in all.

C .Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 27.09.2022

Voluntary Blood donation : NSS members of Pachhunga University College (PUC), Aizawl on yesterday (26.9.2022) donated 72 units of blood to Civil Hospital, Aizawl. The blood donors were 72 students ( 57 male and 15 female) of various subjects - Mizo, History, Political Science and Geography from  B.A 3rd semester. PUC had organised three blood donation camps during the academic session of 2022-2023 wherein 285 units of blood have been donated in all.
