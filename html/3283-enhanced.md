A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.6.2013_

**Police interrogated B.Sangkhumi**: Pi B.Sangkhumi of Chaltlang, former Chairman MPSC and President MHIP Gen. Hqrs. is accused of spreading untruthful text through SMS and was interrogated by the police yesterday.

 B.Sangkhumi appeals for anticipatory bail at the court and was given interim bail for the time being. Her appeal for interim bail will be considered by the court on 21.6.2013.

 B.Sangkhumi is accused of spreading lies through SMS after the court had made judgement between the case of MHIP Gen. Hqrs and Lalbiakengi, former Gen. Secy. of MHIP Gen. Hqrs.

 On 28.5.2013, Lalbiakengi lodged an FIR stating that the text message sent through SMS is meant to defame her and case is registered by Aizawl PS U/S 500/509/34 IPC r/w 66 IT Act.

 According to report received by the Police, SMS was sent through 7 different mobile nos. After investigation, Police found that the mobile phone no. and IMEI no. with which the offensive SMS was sent out first belongs to B.Sangkhumi.
