A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 10.2.2014_

****Traffic Police Report****:**During the year 2012 &amp; 2013, 18,184 vehicles (_2012 – 8,726 & 2013 – 9,458_) were seized for breaking traffic rules in Aizawl.**

 According to Traffic Police report, among the seized vehicles, vehicles parked at No Parking Zone were 5,105 (_2012 – 1,253 & 2013 – 3,852_). 1,279 persons (_2012 – 378 & 2013 – 901_) were arrested for driving without license. And 1005 (_2012 – 283 & 2013 – 722_) were arrested for running on the wrong side of the road.

 Head of Aizawl Traffic Police said that traffic rules offenders and vehicles seized/arrested were higher in 2013 than in 2012 due to the high increase rate of vehicle and that Traffic Police were stern in maintaining Traffic rules.

 During the year 2012 &amp; 2013, vehicle accidents occurred the most in the month of July and August; second by December, January and September. And no vehicle accidents occurred in February recorded Traffic Police.
