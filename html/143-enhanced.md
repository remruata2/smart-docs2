Subject: Details of evidences regarding the direct or indirect involvement of Chinese Nationals or Chinese arms being identified or seized during counter insurgency operation.

Sir,

 With reference to Home Department, Government of Mizoram letter No.D.32019/7/2015-HMS dt.4.11.2015 regarding the above cited subject, as per the information available at the office of CID(SB), Mizoram, Aizawl, so far no direct or indirect evidence against Chinese Nationals has been recorded in Mizoram during any counter insurgency operation.
