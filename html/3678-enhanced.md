A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 14.09.2022_

****No funds received by AMC Councillors**: The Councillors of Aizawl Municipal Corporation (AMC) have not received funds for the year 2022-2023 from the State government till last evening (13.9.2022). These funds, which were meant as AMC Councillors Ward Development Funds were released by the Central government a few months ago to Mizoram government. However, the state government had not handed it over to AMC. As per the norms, these funds were supposed to be released by the state government within 5 days in an areas with proper infrastructures and 10 days for an areas with poor infrastructures. The estimated AMC Councillors Ward Development Funds for 2022-2023 is Rs. 570 lakhs which may be calculated as Rs. 30 lakhs each for the 19 AMC Councillors.**

****Bru voters deleted from Mizoram electoral roll:** 1,407 Bru refugees who were living in various relief camps in Tripura were deleted from the electoral roll of Mizoram as per the instruction of Election Commission of India. These Bru voters which were deleted were from 8 constituencies of Mizoram with Hacheck constituency of Mamit district having the highest numbers. Total numbers of Bru voters deleted from each constituency is given below :**

 a) Hachhek constituency - 861 voters

 b) Dampa constituency - 68 voters

 c) Mamit constituency - 267 voters

 d) Tuirial constituency - 83 voters

 e) Kolasib constituency  - 34 voters

 f) Serlui constituency - 70 voters

 g) Thorang constituency  - 11 voters

 h) W.Tuipui constituency  - 13 voters
