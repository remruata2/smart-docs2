BRIEF HISTORY:

                   A chunga mi khi ka particulars chu a ni. Kum 1991, May ni 25 khan ka lo piang a, ka nu leh pa te in ka pianna min hrilh chianloh avangin ka pianna khua hi ka hre theilo a, ka tet lai hun engemaw chen chu Bethany Khua, Kolasib District ah kan awm tih chu ka hria. Bethani khua ah hun engemaw chen kan awm hnuin kum 1997 emaw 1998 emaw vel niin ka hria Naisingpara refugee camp ah kan chhungin kan insawn thla a, hetah hian tun thlengin kan la khawsa a, ka chhungte pawh an la awm a ni. Naisingpara a ka awm lai (Hel pawl ka zawm hma) hian nitin inhlawhfa chawpin hna ka hmuh an gang ka thawk ve thin a ni.

                   Kum 2013 kumtir lam khan Lalduhawma of Naisingpara nen kan in hmu a, ani hian ‘Hel’ atang turin mi sawm a, eizawnna tur dang a awmloh avang leh kan hnam tana ka tih ve theih awmchhun ni a ka hriat avang chuan Bangladesh lamah chuan min sawmna angin ‘Bru hel’ (BDFM) chu zawm turin Lalduhawma chu ka zui chho ve ta ani. Bangladesh kan han thlen chhoh hnu chuan Mizoram ‘Bru hel’ pengkhat (Salkha party) ho camp ah chuan kan awm a, training te min pe turah ka ngai a, mahsela kan thlen hnu ah chuan training min pe ta lo hrim hrim a,Lo lam hna min thawh tir ta char char ani.

Kan Camp hi Zopui khua atanga 1 kilometer vela hla ani a, ka hriat chinah chuan camp ah hian Sektapha (overground agent), Odhi (SS private), Orjungpha (SS private) te hi ka hriat theih te an ni a, silai erawh chu an kawllo niin ka hria. Camp kan thlen tukah chuan training min pe tura ka ngaih laiin camp bul vela thlawhhma/Lo enkawl tur chuan min chhawr ta a. Hel ka zawm hnu atang hian Lo lam hna hi min thawh tir ber chu ani mai a, Tumkhat erawh chu November 2013 vel khan kan hotute’n khua a buhfai phur tura min tir chu NLFT hovin Thangnang khua ah minlo nghak a, anni kaihhruaina hian Mizoram ah kan lut chho ta zel a, keimahni Bru ho bik min hotu hi chu Duhawma ani. NLFT atanga ka hriat theih te chu- Kormo, Lokhibong, Sudon, Wareng, Raichad leh midang pakhat a hming ka hriatloh te anni.

Thangnag atanga kan chhuah hnuin Tuipuibari leh Damparengpui inkara awmGate lianpui kan thleng a, chumi chu forest gate an ti a, chutah chuan kan chhuakchho ani.Helam hrim hrim ah hian ka vawi khat kalna a ni.Kan kal leh hlek hnu NLFT ho chuan keini bungrua (bungbel leh buhfai) phur ho chu tlangchhipah min thukru a, anni chuan kawngpui an pan a, an thlen hnu chuan kawng sir ah an biru a, motor pakhat lo tlan chu an ti ding a. Hetia motor lo tlan antih din a mi pathum an kidnap hnu chuan mi kan kidnap na bul piah kawrah zan hnih kan riak a. Chumi hnu chuan Bangladesh Lungtian ah kan chhuak chho ta ani. Lungtianah chuan chhun dar 12 thleng vel kan awm a, hemi hnu hian Torungpara ah kar khat vel kan awm hnu in tlak lam kawrah kan insawn thla leh ani. Heta tang hian Bolantia (Bangladesh) ram chhung Lo ah chuan ni 3 vel kan awm a, hemi hnu hian Torungpara a kawr pakhatah chuan kan chhuah hma zawng kan awm ta ani. Hemi tum bak hi chu pawnlam thil ah ka inhnamhnawih ve lo a, Camp-a Lo hna thawh chu ka tih ber thin a ni.

Tun kum June thla khan kan camp (Bangladesh) atangin ka tuar peih tawhloh avang chuan ka lo tlanchhuak a, Naisingpara ah ka chhungte bulah ka khawsa leh ani. Thlamuana hian ka nau hnen atangin ka phone number alo nei a, ka nau mobile phone atang chuan minlo bia a, i case zawng zawng hi ka tihfel sak ang che min rawn zui rawh, a ti a, ka awih avangin Thlamuana chu ka zui ta a min man ta a ni.
