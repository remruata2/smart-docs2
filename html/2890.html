Lalhmingthanga Sanate

Khawlian, Aizawl District, Mizoram

 

He is the President of the rival faction of Hmar Peoples Convention (Democratic) (HPC(D)), designated themselves as “HPC(D) – President’s Group”. He masterminded various subversive activities inside and outside Mizoram. HPC(D) is having some weapons and is indulging in subversive activities.
