****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT AS ON 1.11.2020****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/612

Dated 1st November, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/610 Dt. 31.10.2020, Situation report on Mizoram-Assam Border is made as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report:**

****Today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 10:30 a.m. 1 (one) pick up carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram at Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border.  Dr. Lalrozama, IAS, DC, Mamit convened a meeting with All Political Parties and All NGOs today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 12:30 p.m. at DC Conference Hall, Mamit. The meeting was attended by 38 (thirty eight) members from Mamit Thalai Pawl, MZP, MUP, MHIP, MJA, Mamit. During the meeting, DC, Mamit apprised the members regarding yesterday’s meeting of DC, Mamit and DC, Karimganj at Isabeel Tea Estate, Karimganj District and stated that the meeting was convened after getting approval from Home Department, Govt. of Mizoram and the purpose of the meeting was to discuss free vehicular movement across the border and maintenance of peace and tranquillity at the border areas. However, leaders of Sub-Headquarters YMA, Mamit and MSU, District Headquarters Mamit refused to attend the said meeting. MSU District Headquarters, Mamit burned copy of meeting minutes of DC, Mamit and DC, Karimganj which was held yesterday at Isabeel Tea Estate, Karimganj District today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 11:30 a.m. at Charkawn, Mamit. The function was attended by 10 (ten) MSU leaders including Pu Vincent Lalpiantluanga, President, MSU District Headquarters, Mamit.  Sub-Headquarters YMA, Mamit issued a Press Release today i.e., 1.11.2020 which stated that Sub-Headquarters YMA, Mamit Executive Emergency Meeting was held today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 11:00 a.m. to discuss DC, Mamit and DC, Karimganj yesterday’s meeting and resolved the following:
1. Condemned keeping of written document which can be presumed that the area of Mizoram Police Personnel deployment viz. Zaite (Choto Bubirbond) and Medili (Medlicherra) as falling within Assam territory.

**3. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District:**

Today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 11:33 a.m. FSL team from Guwahati comprises of Dr. Geeta Shree, Sr. Scientific Officer, Shri. Dhana Sar Kalita, Lab Technician, Shri. Rajib Baruah, Cameraman visited bomb blast site at SSA Lower Primary School, Saihapui ‘V’. They were accompanied by Shri. Bhargav Goswami, Dy. SP (Hqrs.), Cachar and OC, Dholai PS. They left the area after seizing material for forensic evidence viz., piece of GCI Sheets, some piece of textbooks etc. from the site.
Further, FSL team from Aizawl led by Pu Rosangzuala, Assistant Director, FSL, Aizawl also visited SSA Lower Primary School, Saihapui ‘V’ today i.e., 31.1.2020 @ 5:05 p.m. to inspect the site. They were accompanied by SI Lalramropuia, Investigating Officer of the case. They left the area around 6:00 p.m. after inspection of the site.
4 (four) Leaders of MSU General Headquarters led by Pu Vanlalliana Khiangte, President MSU visited Saihapui ‘V’ today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 2:30 p.m. They were accompanied by 4 (four) leaders of Vairengte MSU Headquarters led by Pi GF Lalchhandami, President, MSU Headquarters Vairengte. They left the area around 5:00 p.m. today evening.

****4. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report:**A meeting under the chairmanship of Pu Ricky Zorinsanga, President MZP Sub-Headquarters, Bairabi was held today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 11:00 a.m. at MZP Pisa, Rimawi Veng, Bairabi. Leaders of Bairabi Joint VC, Bairabi Joint YMA, MZP Sub-Headquarters, Bairabi and MSU Sub-Headquarters, Bairabi attended the meeting. During the meeting, blockage of road leading to Bairabi by residents of Ramnathpur, Assam was discussed and they resolved the following:**

1. Until and unless road block at Assam side is lifted there should be no movement of goods with or without permission, entry of non-mizo by road or by boat from Bairabi to Assam.

_Cont/-3....._

****SECRET****

-3-

1. Venturing out of non-mizos from their home within Bairabi area is not allowed for their personal safety till withdrawal of road block at Assam side.
2. There should be no entry of vehicles from Mizoram to Assam during enforcement of road block at Assam side.

They also decided to set up two duty points at Tlawng Bridge at the outskirt of Bairabi (Mamit District and Kolsib District connecting Bridge) and near Zophai Police Duty Post.  They started performing duty at the aforementioned duty points’ w.e.f., today i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 2:00 p.m, and there is no vehicular movement across the border today.

****5. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report:**Information received indicated that residents of Dolchhora and nearby villages conveyed their intention to remove bamboo wall constructed on the road at Assam side near Phaisen Village to residents of Phaisen Village and it is likely to remove the said bamboo wall tomorrow i.e., 2.11.2020. Situation is normal.**

****6. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report:**Road block organized by Zoram Drivers’ Union (ZDU) at Tawngtaina Hmun, outskirt of Vairengte is still continuing today i.e., 1.11.2020. There is no vehicular movement across Mizoram-Assam border at Vairengte and 106 (one hundred and six) vehicles are stranded due to the said road block at the time of writing the report i.e., 1.11.2020 @ 6:00 p.m.  Pu Vanlalliana Khiangte, President, MSU General Headquarters submitted FIR to Vairengte PS yesterday i.e., 31.10.2020 in connection with recent border disputes along Mizoram-Assam border and requested to provide security escort to stranded vehicles in Assam.   Pu John Laldinmawia, Secretary Vairengte Thalai Pawl and Pu Alex Lalramnghaka, Secretary, MZP Sub-Headquarters Vairengte submitted FIR to Vairengte PS today i.e., 1.11.2020 in connection with alleged arson of Pu R. Lalhmingliana’s farm hut which was occupied by Samir Uddin barbhuya (25) S/o Hasson Ali of Govindapur, Bagha, Assam at Phainuam on 31.10.2020 @ 7:40 p.m.**
Situation is under close watch.
