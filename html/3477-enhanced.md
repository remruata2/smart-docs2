A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 14.3.2014_

**Chargesheet submitted****:**Chargesheet for the case registered as a result of an FIR submitted by Chief Minister, Lal Thanhawla was submitted to the court by police yesterday. The case which includes 506,507,201 and 204 IPC along with IT ACT 669(A) was registered after an SMS was received by Chief Minister from K.Chhawnthuama of Durtlang on 11.12.2013. The accused was arrested by the police on 16 December 2013.

B. _Front page of The Aizawl post newspaper Dt. 14.3.2014_

**Election boycott by MSU****:** According to a resolution passed by the Federal body meeting of Mizo Students Union on March 12 2014, the MSU intends to boycott the coming Lok Sabha election in the whole of Mizoram if the Election Commission of India conduct casting of votes of Tuikuk (Bru) in their transit camps located in Tripura.
