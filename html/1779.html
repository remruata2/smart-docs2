Sir,

                  With reference to the subject and your letter referred above, I am sending herewith the detail report of the case on seizure of 53 packets of Methamphetamine 'WY' by a Joint Operation Team of Mizoram Police and BSF Intelligence Wing as below.

                  On 8.7.2014@ 3:45pm, in pursuance of an information, a joint confidential operation was launched by Special Narcotic Staffs of CID(Crime) Mizoram, CID(SB) Mizoram and BSF(DCG Team) Lunglei at Bethlehem Veng area Aizawl, intercepted one Lalzawmliana(52) S/o Vanlallawma(L) of Tahan 'F' Group, Myanmar, P/A Bethlehem Veng, who is strongly suspected to carry some contraband substances. He was detained at the spot and prepared Ground of Belief u/s 50  of ND&PS Act. Then checking of his physical body was done and recovered 10689 (1.60 kgs) tablets of Methamphetamine marked as 'WY' (coloured red and small size). The seized drugs was estimated at Rs.21,37,800/- at local market rate and were packed in 53 blue plastic polythene containers and wrapped in brown cello tape. In this connection, Special Narcotic Police Station Case No.19/14 dt.8.7.14 u/s 22(c) ND&PS Act r/w 14 Foreigners Act has been registered. The accused was arrested and forwarded to Special Judge, ND&PS Act on 9.7.2014 for judicial remand. Detail seizure report was also sent to Director General, NCB, RK Puram, New Delhi by using Form 'F' on 10.7.2014 by SP, CID(Crime) Mizoram.

                  The case is pending for receipt of expert opinion from Forensic Science Laboratory, Aizawl, Mizoram.
