 With reference to your letter no cited above. I am furnishing the notable achievements of Police Department available in this Unit from 1.4.2011 till date as under for your kind information and further necessary action.

  Arms - 40 nos.

  Ammunition - 491 nos.

  Gelatine - 466 sticks

 6.7.2011 -  Members of Bru armed gang viz; 1) Edwin Jatiham(34) S/o Slaikawng, 2) Lalrinawma Chorkhy(36) S/o Shanto Moni were arrested by Mizoram Police Special Branch from the border areas of Mizoram and Assam near Vairengte.

 23.8.2011- Members of Bru armed gang viz; Doremthanga S/o Joy Joha and Laldinpuia S/o Puida Rai both of Naisingpara were arrested by Mizoram Police at West Phaileng and seized - Local made gun - 2 nos., improvised miniature rifle - 1 no. from their possession.

**(3) Surrender of insurgents:**

 4.7.2011 - S/S Pvt. Lincoln Zosangliana of HPC(D) cadre S/o H.Rodailova, Ramhlun South, Aizawl surrendered to SP, CID(SB).

 26.8.2011 - Members of Bru armed group namely SS Lt.Bisoram Reang@ Bidii pa@ Bura S/o Jagadis of Medili of Karimganj, Assam, SS Cpl. Ramosai Reang S/o Surendro of Kolahan surrendered before SP Mamit and also submitted 2 nos. of 9mm Pistol (local made) and 99 nos. of live ammunition.

 4.10.2011 - BRU/BFA/BLA cadres viz; 1) Henry Lalmalsawma@Chandro Kishore Zamatia(37) S/o Katihori Zamatia of Adibasi, N.Tripura, 2) Junglelove@Birato(22) S/o Birburam of Chuhvel, Mizoram, 3) Momendron@Motorai(28) S/o Sudomoni of Borai, Mizoram surrendered before SP(Mamit) along with 1 no. of G-4 Rifle with one magazine and 15 live rounds of ammns., 1(one) no. of .22 miniature rifle (local made).

**(4) Others:**

 On 6.6.2011@ 0230 hrs, two officials of ABCI viz; Premlal Dhiman, ABCI Project Manager and Bajrang Lal Bhurgi, Commercial Manager were abducted by UDLA armed group numbering about 10 from Meidum village. The joint team of CID(SB) and 1st IR Bn. (RAF) rescued the abductees from the hands of hostagetakers at Banglabasha of Hailakandi district of Assam on 16.6.2011. During the operation, 30 nos. of live ammns. of AK-47 and 44 nos. of SLR ammns. were seized from the place of occurrence.

 On 23.8.2011, one impersonator JH. Lalthankima(22) S/o JH. Roluaha of Mamit Luangpawl Veng who had cheated Pi Lal Riliani W/o Pu Lal Thanhawla, Chief Minister, Mizoram and several persons was apprehended by CID(SB) staff at Lawngtlai Bazar Veng and criminal case registered against him.
