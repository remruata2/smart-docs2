****Issues to be discussed in the proposed talk with HPC(D) on 31.1.2013 at****

****State Guest House.****

1. Sl.No 6 and 9 article of the draft ground rules from the Govt. side may be the basic stand of Govt. which are reproduced below:

 **Sl.no. 6.** The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) shall immediately cease all  subversive activities including collection of donations and other activities detrimental to  the maintenance of law and order, and to desist from all forms of direct or indirect threats  to any organizations or to any private individual.

****Sl.no.9.**The Hmar People Convention (Democratic) shall not directly or indirectly  interfere in any Parliamentary, State Assembly and local election process which may  deprive voters from exercising their franchise.**

2. A joint monitory group as reflected in our ground rules Sl.no. 3 may be formed as under to monitor effective compliance of ground rules.

Co-Chairman   - SP CID (SB)

Co-Chairman  - to be appointed by HPC(D) side

Members Govt. side : 1) OSD Home Dept.

 2) Addl.SP CID(SB)

Members HPC(D) side- Two leaders to be appointed by HPC(D) side

3. The Govt. side may agree ground rules sl.no. 1, 2, 4, 5, 7 and 8 (copy attached)

4. SP CID(SB) may arrange 6 reliable officers to be with HPC(D) delegates right from their arrival till their departure and they would be responsible for arrangement of all amenities of the delegates in alias with State Guest House Staff.

5. One section of armed personnel may be on static duty at State Guest House right from the arrival of HPC(D) delegates till their departure. CO 1st MAP or 3rd MAP may provide armed personnel. The detailed section may report to Addl.SP CID (SB) at State Guest House at 1:00 PM of 30th Jan. 2013 for briefing

6. The delegates be restricted from any media or security agencies other than CID (SB) staff. Any officials release pertaining to the talk shall be made only after due consultation between the delegates from both Govt. of Mizoram and HPC(D)

7. Any visitors who may like to visit the HPC(D) delegates during the talk shall be allowed only after due approval of CID (SB) staff who are responsible for security.

8. Probable points likely to be raised by HPC(D) delegates

 a) Release of HPC(D) leaders

 b) Issue of alleged CYMA attitude on ethnic minorities

 c) Dissolution of SHDC
