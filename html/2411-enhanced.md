****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/108

Dated 12th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/104 Dt. 11.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-2, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 12.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-13, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 12.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Today i.e., 12.2.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. around 25 (twenty five) residents of Bairabi under the aegis of Joint VC Bairabi started construction of temporary motorable road near Zophai BOP and they intend to construct the road upto Ramri Lui, which they considered as Assam-Mizoram Boundary. While constructing the said road, 15 (fifteen) Assam Police led by SI Biromongal Singha, OC Ramnathpur (Assam) appeared at the site todays’ afternoon around 1:00 p.m. and instructed the workers not to continue their work. However, after briefing the fact to Assam Police by officials of Mizoram Police and residents of Bairabi the work was resumed and temporary motorable road was constructed upto Ramri Lui.

Todays’ afternoon around 3:30 p.m. 30 (thirty) police personnel of Assam led by OC, Ramnathpur stopped 407 Truck carrying Hume pipe near Zophai BOP and Assam Police retreat around 7:00 p.m. today’s evening after Dr. H. Lalthlangliana, DC, Kolasib informed Ms. Soma Roy, SDM, Katlicherra who was present at the disputed site to withdraw their force. Residents of Bairabi stated that they will definitely construct IR Camp at Ramri Lui, if Temporary IR Camp is not constructed tomorrow i.e., 13.2.2021 by the state government.

Pu Lalhuliana Fanai, DIG (NR) visited Zophai BOP today i.e., 12.2.2021 @ 2:20 p.m. and after conducting necessary briefing to staff of Zophai BOP he proceeded to the disputed site, Assam Mizoram Border. He was accompanied by Pu Lalthafamkima, AIGP (Operation).

83 (eighty three) vehicles (HMV-51, MMV-Nil, LMV-27, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 12.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

142 (one hundred forty two) vehicles (HMV-86, MMV-16, LMV-15, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-2, Oil Tanker-17, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 12.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
