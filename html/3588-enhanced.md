A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.6.2022_

**Anti Drugs Awarness Campaign by Assam Rifles concluded****:******Closing function for Anti Drugs Awareness Campaign organised by the Assam Rifles was held at Loch Hall, Assam Rifles Headquarters, Aizawl yesterday i e.20.6.2022

 Gracing this function as a Chief Guest, Pu Tawnluia, Dy.Chief Minister delivered a speech where he appreciated the efforts of the Assam Rifles while at the same time emphasizing the need to increase efforts in combating drugs menace by the Government, Churches, Voluntary Organisations and even the Assam Rifles from their respective approach.

 This Campaign was kicked off on 7th June 2022 wherein bike rally between Lunglei - Aizawl, painting competition and debate competition were organised to spread awareness on drugs abuse.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 21.6.2022_

**e-Daakhil'edaakhil.nic.in inagurated:**An online apps, e-Daakhil'edaakhil.nic.in where any consumers can lodge a complaint, introduced by Mizoram Food, Civil supplies &amp; Consumer Affairs Department was inaugurated by Pu K.Lalrinliana, FCS&amp;CA Minister on 20.6.2022.

** This web portal, established by signing an MoU with State Bank of India, was taken care of by NIC, Mizoram. To utilised this, a consumer having a complain had first to registered himself/herself and lodge a complaint where it would be analysed by Consumer Commission in this portal.******

****Since this e-Daakhil has been linked with Common Service Center, even consumers from remote areas will be able to have access to it. No fees will be levied to a complainant if the net value of his complaint is below Rs. 5 Lakhs.
