A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 29.5.2014

Lost Baby Found Dead: The body of a 2 year old baby at Tuichawng who was missing since Tuesday night was found yesterday not far from their house. Search operation for the baby was held by Tuichawng community but without success, then the body of the baby was found at around 3:00pm yesterday 100 mtrs. from their house below the road side.

          The baby name Raskin went missing in the middle of Monday night while sleeping between his parents. The parents realised it only after awoken by their two week old baby who was crying incessantly.

          No external wounds were found on the body and no person has been arrested in connection with the death of the baby said B.Laldingngheta, SDPO Tlabung. Post Mortem was done at Tlabung Civil Hospital.
