A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES/The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 18.6.2013_

**Suspected arrest**:In connection with the abduction of three persons on 5th May 2013 near Rajiv Nagar, the Mizoram police has apprehended one Somaray Chakma for questioning on the pretext of him helping the abductors on their route and feeding them. However, reports indicated that the apprehended person is neither a member of the abductors nor involved in the abduction.

 In the meantime, a ransom of rupees 50 lakhs was demanded again by the abductors and insisted upon it being paid promptly, though, a date-line is not yet fixed by them.

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 18.6.2013_

****Clearing of Debris**: The tidying works for the 5 damaged 2nd MAP Bn. quarters of Lunglei Luangmual town on May 5th 2013 by landslide is about to be completed by a combined team of YMA, Luangmual Branch and State Disaster Response Force. Residences of these quarters were shifted in a temporary make-shift Duty Barrack for the time being. Meanwhile, the Commandant, 2nd MAP Bn. and the D.C., Lunglei are giving massive efforts for the relief and compensation of the victims of this disaster.**
