****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/03

Dated 18thJanuary, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/38 Dt. 15.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 Pu Lalrintluanga Sailo, Dy. Govt. Chief Whip visited Mizoram Police duty at Thinghlun border today i.e.,15.1.2021 @ 11:50 a.m. and handed over cash and 2 (two) silpaulin sheets. In responding to their request, Pu Lalrintluanga Sailo assured them that immediate action will be taken to provide electricity and water supply at their camp.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – 4, MMV – 3, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV- 2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 9 (nine) vehicles (HMV – 5, MMV – 4, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 6 (six) vehicles (LMV – 1, MMV – 5) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 58 (fifty eight) vehicles (HMV-24, MMV – 4, LMV – 21, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 18.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 162 (one hundred sixty two) vehicles (HMV – 53, MMV – 34, LMV –38, LPG – 5, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 27, Bus - 1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 18.1.2021 between 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
