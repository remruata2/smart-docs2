A. Front page of  VANGLAIN/ The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 22.09.2022

Dialogue between Mizoram and Assam Chief ministers: A dialogue  between  Mizoram Chief Minister, Pu Zoramthanga and Assam Chief Minister, Pu Himanta Biswa Sarma was held in Delhi yesterday (21.9.2022) in connection with border disputes of the two states. Apart from the border disputes, issues regarding smuggling of areca nuts through the two states and other important  relevant points were also discussed. Both the Chief Ministers appreciated the meeting of the two states and considered it being done smoothly. As for the illegal areca nut business, both the state governments agreed to check the flow of smuggled areca nuts while Assam Chief Minister express his intention to make all possible means for areca nuts harvested in Mizoram to reach their destinations with the assistance of the Assam police.

 

B.Front page of  VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 22.09.2022

Meth seized :  Special Narcotics Team under Mizoram police on Tuesday night (20.9.2022) seized 1.016 kg of suspected Methamphetamine from the possession of Nathan R. Khawbung of Lakhipur, Assam , present address - Bungkawn, Aizawl. The seized items are estimated to be around  Rs. 3 crores in the international market.

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 22.09.2022

Heroin seized :  A combine team of Mizoram Excise & Narcotics (END) and Central Anti Drugs Squad (CADS) under Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) on the morning of 20.9.2022 recovered 202 grams of suspected Heroin at Tuirial village from Vanchungbia (32), Haikhawl, Myanmar. The same team also seized 147 grams of Heroin from Thasuii (32) and Duhneimawii, both from myanmar at Turial Airfield.
