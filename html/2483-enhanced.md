****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/655

Dated 17th November, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/652 Dt. 16.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 4 (four) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-2) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 17.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 25(twenty five) vehicles transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 Construction of BSF Camp was started under the aegis of Phainuam VC today i.e., 17.11.2020 @ 10:30 a.m. near Upper Phainuam Lower Primary School, Phainuam and around 40 (forty) volunteers participated. After completing the structure (made of bamboo) the work was stopped by SSB personnel deployed by Assam, who are camped at Upper Phainuam Lower Primary School, Phainuam since bomb blast occurred on 7.11.2020.

 In this connection, Pu Lallawmawma, Addl. DC, Kolasib, Pu C. Vanlalvena, Addl. SP, Kolasib and Pu Isaac C. Lalrempuia, SDO(Civil), Vairengte proceeded to the spot and they held talks with Shri. Vikash Chhetri, Circle Officer, Sonai Circle and Shri. Sahabuddin Bharbuiya, OC, Dholai PS who were at the spot. They decided that BSF be stationed at Upper Phainuam Lower Primary School Building at a separate room along with SSB as both forces are from Central Armed Police Force instead of constructing a separate BSF camp. Accordingly, 10 (ten) BSF personnel are stationed at the School and volunteers from Phainuam who constructed the proposed BSF camp also dispersed under peaceful atmosphere at 1:45 p.m. today’s afternoon.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 22(twenty two) vehicles (HMV-17, LMV-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 17.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.
