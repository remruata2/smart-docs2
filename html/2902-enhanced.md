**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.14.12.2012**

****MPSC Result**: The MPSC published, yesterday, the result of Mizoram Combined Competitive Examination conducted a few months back. The result declared 24 candidates as successful, out of which 10 candidates each are for MCS and MPS while 4 candidates are for MIS. A panel list of 3 persons for each services is also made which will be valid for one year.**

**B. Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 14.12.2012**

****Resumption of Tuirial HEP**: The construction of Tuirial Hydro Electric Power Project, which was stopped for some time due to the alleged threat of HPC(D) on 4th November 2012 was resumed from 11th December 2012, according to report. Speaking to Vanglaini newspaper, HPC(D) Southern Commander, L.Boia Hmar said they only informed Patel Engineering Ltd. to pay their demand but not to stop their construction works.**

**C. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST/VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 14.12.2012**

**Inauguration of first flying school**: The Chief Minister of Mizoram, LalThanhawla inaugurated the 1st Mizo Air Squadron NCC &amp; First Flying Training Sortie at Lengpui Airport, yesterday. 1st Mizo Air Squadron NCC received Microlight Aircraft which is ZEN AIR CH 701 STDL model for training the cadets.
