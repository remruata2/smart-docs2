A. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 26.2.2014

FICN Seized: Bawngkawn Police seized counterfeit notes with a face value of ` 44,000/- from 2 non-Mizos – Kanrum Haq s/o Nazir Uddin and Abdul Munim s/o Abdul Mujeeb on Monday at Chaltlang Lily Veng, Aizawl. The 2 non-Mizos bought stuff from a shop and the shopkeeper found that ` 500/- notes he received was fake, he reported it to Bawngkawn Police Station. The Police probed into the matter and seized ` 1,000/- and ` 500/- brand notes with a face value of Rs. 44,000/- from them.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.2.2014

6,700 Bottles of Phensedyl Found: 6,700 bottles of Phensedyl were found by the local people in the truck which met with an accident near Hmuifang on Saturday night. The truck carried barbed/fencing wire, and the driver and handymen ran away, sources said.
