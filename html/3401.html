A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 18.2.2014

Crime Report: As reported by the Crime Review meeting recently held by Police officers, 27 murder cases and 88 rape cases were registered during January to December 2013. The rate of the two cases is lower than it was in 2012.

            27 murder cases registered in 2013 is less by one in number compared to the 28 murder cases registered in 2012. Whereas, rape cases registered in 2013 were 88 which is less by 14 numbers compared to the 102 cases registered in 2012.
