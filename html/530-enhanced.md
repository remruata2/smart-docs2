 With reference to the subject and your letter no. cited above, I am sending herewith available documents and records of Jonesh Molshoy Reang former S/S Lt. of BLFM group and subsequently a prominent member of BLA commanded by Thomas group.

 Following are the activities and past records of the killed militant from available records.

1) S/S Lt. Jonesh Molshoy@Abraham Molshoy@Sadamapa was arrested on 30.11.2005 by Assam Rifles at Khedachera, Tripura.

2) He was the mastermind of the sensational abduction of 2 hydel project officials on 8.8.2005 which resulted in communal tension between Bru and Mizos in the western part of Mizoram.

3) He surrendered to the Govt. of Mizoram on 16.3.2006 and received all rehabilitation package as per scale. He went underground again joining the remnants of BLFM who called themselves as BLA under the command of S/S Lt. Thomas and he was assigned to procure arms and ammunitions for the BLA.

4) He was arrested by Mizoram Police on 15.4.2011 in the Mizoram-Tripura border in connection with Kanhmun P/S C/No.28/3 u/s 395/397 r/w 27(2) Arms Act. He was released on bail and rejoined his colleague and since then he jumped bail for a number of times.
