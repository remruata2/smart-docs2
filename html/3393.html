A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.2.2014

A Woman Molested: A cleaning woman working at ISPAT Industries (MII) Factory at Lengte was molested by a non-Mizo worker in the same factory while she was in the bathroom at around 1:30pm yesterday. The angry people of Lengte gathered at the factory at 5:00pm yesterday but the accused was already handed over to Sairang Police before the crowd of Lengte reached the factory and that evaded further problems.

 

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.2.2014

Search Operation For Deep Mandal Called Off: A social work organised Mamit Sub-Headquarters to search for missing person – Deep Mandal who was kidnapped by Bru armed gang on 23.11.2013 and has not been released till now continued yesterday and it was called off in the evening.

            Police report said that no force or threat was used; YMA members went from Tuipuibari to Damparengpui, had a public meeting and then dispersed.

            Meanwhile, Pu Zorammawia, IGP said that Mizoram government is doing its utmost for the release of Deep Mandal and that leaders of the abductors are contacted, and search operation is still going strong and there is hope for success. Deep Mandal is alive and well in the hands of his abductors, sources said.
