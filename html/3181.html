A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.21.2.2013:

 

Man Found Dead:  Vanlalchhuanga (30) s/o Ramthangmawii (Jail warder) was found dead at their rented house in Chanmari West yesterday at around 10:00AM. He was believed to die of burning. A lot of smoke came out from their house at 10:30AM and when people looked into their house, Vallachhuanga was found leaning over the stove and he was already dead. His mother was on duty while this incident occurred. Vaivakawn Police examined the incident as soon as it was reported and the corpse was sent for postmortem.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.21.2.2013:

 

Arson culprit apprehended:  A person who burnt scooty and auto-rickshaw on the night of 15th Feb. 2013 at Electric Veng Champhai was arrested by Champhai Police. As the culprit is a juvenile, he was handed over to Juvenile Justice Board.
