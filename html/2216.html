TLABUNG RD BLOCK DEMAND COMMITTEE

 

            Tlabung RD Block Demand Committee was formed on 17th  August 2018 under the Chairmanship of Pu Lalbiakzuala,VCP Zodin,Tlanbung. The Committee is entrusted to press Government of Mizoram to create separate Tlabung RD Block by bifurcating the present Lungsen RD Block. In this regard, the committee organised a mass gathering on 01.09.2018 at Bazar Bungkawn,Tlabung which was attended by around 300 people. In this gathering, representatives of various associations delivered speaches passed a resolution, saying - 'Until the demand for Tlabung RD Block is successful, residentsof Tlabung and its adjoining areas will continued our demand to the extend of bycotting the forth coming Mizoram MLA Election'. Further the Committee intended the following actio plans :-

            1) 07.09.2018 - Press Conference at Aizawl press club

            2) 11.09.2018 - Peaceful march in Tlabung town.

            3) 18,09.2018 - Protest against the Government at International Border near Tlabung.

            According to information received lately, the intention to have press conference at Aizawl Press Club has been kept pending due to the sudden resignation of Pu lalbiakzual from the post of their PResident.
