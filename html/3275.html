A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 6.6.2013

Abduction in Mizoram: On 5th June, Wednesday at around 9:30 AM, some unidentified persons abducted 3 (three) men – Nizam Uddin, Mithun and Birudon Chakma who were working on border fencing under Gautam Construction Company at Badalsury in Mamit District, one Chakma among the abducted persons is a driver and the other 2 are helper and labourer. The abductors, as well as their motives are not known yet.

            Badalsury is in the western side of Mizoram near Rajivnagar, between the border of Mizoram and Bangladesh.
