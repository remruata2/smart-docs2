A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 11.08.2022

Funds for salary released:  Yesterday, i.e.10.8.2022 various treasuries in Mizoram have started releasing funds for salaries of regular Government employees for the month of July. According to report received by Vanglaini newspaper ,some of the treasuries who could not completed releasing the funds yesterday are expected to finished it today. Meanwhile, Mizoram also received its Share of Central Taxes from the Union Government which is Rs. 583.34 crores yesterday out of a total amount of Rs. 1,16,665.75 crores released to 28 different States by the Central Government. Share of Taxes is the main source of income by Mizoram State and the total amount of funds received from these during the year 2022-2023 has been Rs.4,083.24 crores.

             

           

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 11.08. 2022

Emergency vehicles flagged off: Pu Lalchamliana, Minister , Disaster Management & Rehabilitation (DMR) flagged off 12 Emergency Response Vehicle (ERV)  from Secretariat Complex yesterday. These ERVs, which are under the department of Disaster Management & Rehabilitation, will be allotted to State Emergency Operation Centre (SEOC) and District Emergency Operation Centre (DEOC) of each district. All these ERVs are procured from the accumulated State Disaster Response Funds received from the Central Government. Apart from these ERVs, the funds have also been utilised for providing three High Pressure Pump Fire Tender to Mizoram Fire & Emergency Service and one EVR to Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA).

 

C. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 11.08. 2022

Notification by GAD: General Administration Department of Mizoram issued a notification yesterday instructing all offices under the State and Central Government, commissions, boards, PSU, universities and  Financial Institutions to hoist the Indian National Flag during 1-15 August 2022 in accordance with the Flag Code of India to mark the 75th anniversary of India's Independence Day. The notification also instructed personnel of these offices and institutions to hoist the National Flag in their respective residence during this period.
