****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/164

Dated: 8th March, 2019.

Information has been received that this morning, i.e. 8.3.2019, Police party from Bunghmun Police Station comprising of - (i) SI P.C. Lalzuitluanga, OC, Bunghmun PS, (ii) C/51 Lalnunzira, Authorised Driver and (iii) C/63 Michael Zodinthara, 2nd Bn. MAP (attached to Bunghmun PS) proceeded towards Tleu village by Police Gypsy bearing Reg. No. MZ-01/K-4402 to conduct enquiry into the report of damage of water pipe (Bunghmun water supply pipes laid by PHED) by plying trucks. 4 (four) civilians of Bunghmun village, namely- (i) Hranglura Sailo (70 yrs) S/o Lalkhuma Sailo, (ii) Duhkima (65 yrs) S/o Zawngauva, (iii) Z.D. Lalhmingchhuanga (25 yrs) S/o J. Lalhmingthanga and (iv) C. Lalmuankima (27 yrs) S/o C. Vanlalhmuaka also hitch-hiked this Police Gypsy to attend memorial stone erection programme at Tleu village.

 On reaching a hair-pin road curve which is at about 8 kms. from Bunghmun village at around 10:00 a.m this morning, the vehicle met with an accident by veering off the road and rolled downwards for about 20 feet. Unfortunately, one of the civilians, Duhkima (65 yrs) S/o Zawngauva succumbed to his injuries sustained from this accident at around 1:30 p.m today. 2 (two) other passengers, C/63 Michael Zodinthara, 2nd Bn. MAP and Hranglura Sailo (70 yrs) sustained injuries and they were carried to Civil Hospital, Lunglei by Ambulance for necessary treatment. The other passengers escaped with minor or no injuries.
