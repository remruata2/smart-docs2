A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 22.10.2012

 

Homage to Martyrs: The Police paid tributes to Police personnel who laid down their lives while on duty all over the country during 1.9.2011 to 31.8.2012. In Aizawl, the commemoration day was organised at 1st MAP Parade Ground. Mizoram DGP, Alok Kumar Verma graced the occasion.

 

DGI(Trg) Pu Saizela read out the names of Police personnel who sacrifice their lives while on duty during 1.9.2011 to 31.8.2012. During this period, 566 personnel from different State Police and other forces. However, there was no Police personnel died while on duty in Mizoram during the said period. After the ceremony, Police officials laid floral wreaths at the Police Martyrs Cemetery.  

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post, The Zozam Times and Mizo Aw.

 

 

 

B. Second page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 22.10.2012

 

Protest rally at Chawngte: The Chawngte Group YMA planned to organise a protest rally over the suspicious death of V.Lalramlawma, a chowkidar at Chawngte ICDS office. The rally will begin from Chawngte 'P' and end at SDO office, Chawngte 'C' today.

 

The leaders of Lunglei Sub-Hqrs. YMA and MZP Hqrs. Aizawl are planning to participate in this rally. A total bandh will be organise on 23.10.2012 from 5:00am to 5:00pm.

 

Meanwhile, Tuithiang Branch YMA stated that the cause of suspicious death of their member Pu V.Lalramlawma as reported in local newspapers, cable TV and facebook are baseless, so to say, entering through the window in the house of Santi Bikas Chakma who alone assaulted him.

 

The BCM(Baptist Church of Mizoram) sent its leaders Rev.Dr.Lalrinthanga and Rev.Lalrochama to Chawngte to take stock of the situation and ease the tension.

 

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post, The Zozam Times, Mizo Aw and Hnehtu.
