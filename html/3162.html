A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 13.12.2013

Police nabbed masked assaulter: Lalfakzuala (30) s/o Lalvena of Bungkawn, Aizawl who broke into the house of RL Lalhlupuia of Ramhlun South on 8.12.2013 in between 10:30 – 11:00 AM and assaulted Lalsangliani (38) w/o RL Lalhlupuia was nabbed by Mizoram Police at Bazar Bungkawn, Aizawl @ 12 noon yesterday. Lalfakzuala confessed to the police that he stole money and mobile handset and also assaulted Lalsangliani. A case was registered at Bawngkawn Police Station and he was sent to Central Jail as permitted by the magistrate yesterday itself.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.12.2013

Abductors demand ransom before Christmas – MKHC request to release abducted: Mizoram Kohhran Hruaitute Committee (MKHC) in a press release stated that they feel disappointed that the three abducted persons who were abducted on 23.11.2013 from Dampa Forest by suspected Bru armed gang are not released till date. It prays that the abducted persons be released unconditionally as early as possible. Meanwhile, the abductors demanded ` 5 crore as ransom to be paid before Christmas.
