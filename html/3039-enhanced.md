****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/433

Dated: 6th August, 2020.

 Information has been received that Joint Young Mizo Association, Chawngte, in its demand for immediate repair of dilapidated road condition between Chawngte to Rualalung, Lunglei District, intended to resort to picketing of Government Offices and Banks within Chawngte if no action is taken for its improvement on or before 10.8.2020. Further, if no action is taken till 14.8.2020, they will boycott Independence Day, 2020 celebration at Chawngte.

 In this regard, Joint YMA, Chawngte, Lawngtlai District submitted a written petition to Chief Minister through SDO(C), Chawngte yesterday i.e., 5.8.2020, requesting to take measures to expedite the ongoing construction of road between Chawngte to Chhumkhum road. They also stated that a 3 kms long stretch of road between Chawngte to Rualalung is in extreme dilapidated condition and despite many requests made to the concern APS Pvt. Ltd. Company who undertakes road construction work between Chawngte to Chhumkhum under World Bank fund, no action has been taken till date.

 As per input, the main demand of Joint YMA, Chawngte is immediate starting of repair work, and even if the work could not be completed within their stipulated deadlines, they will not resort to the above intended actions. Leaders of Joint YMA, Chawngte are positive to lend help by calling social work if an Excavator (JCB) and boulders/stone aggregates are provided and supplied at the site. They expressed that people of Chawngte are facing severe inconvenience and hardship as Maxi Cab are currently unable to ply between Chawngte and Lunglei due to the above 3 kms long road condition between Chawngte and Rualalung.

Situation is under close watch.
