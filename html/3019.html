SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/772

Dated: 12th October, 2019.

The seized items, which were hidden inside a black rucksack, placed near the vehicle engine, include :-

  1. Rs.2,000/- denomination notes             =          1,572 nos.
  2. Rs.500/- denomination notes                 =          1,915 nos.

  1. Shalik Ahmed (24)

  1. Kabir Hussain (23)

s/o Asaddar Ali of Barigam, PS – Patharkandi, Karimganj District, Assam.
