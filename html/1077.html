            Among them, the above 3 Commanders of HPC(D) did not live to see Peace Agreement between Government of Mizoram and HPC(D) in the year 2018.

            As soon as HPC(D) was formed, 3 (three) persons led by Lalropuia Famhoite, Army Chief went to Bangladesh in 1997. There, they discussed how to operate with NSCN(IM). When they returned to Mizoram, they started recruitment of HPC(D) Cadre from Mizoram, Manipur and Assam.

            It may also be mentioned that, among the HPC Cadre - who surrendered and not surrendered,  no one seemed to be a member of Sinlung Hills Accord Implementing Demand Committee (SHAIDC) which pressurised the Government to implement the Peace Agreement of the year 1994.

            Sinlung Hills Accord Implementing Demand Committee (SHAIDC) has already filed a complaint in the Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench against Government of Mizoram on 25th May, 2016. PC Prusti and Neihchungnunga Darlong are the Lawyers hired by SHAIDC.
