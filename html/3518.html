A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 16.08. 2022

Prohibition order of AMC: The Aizawl Municipal Corporation (AMC) had issued an order prohibiting any earth removing works for building constructions along with filling of crater/hollows in the grounds using loose soil  during the monsoon season as a precautionary measures to avert natural calamities. This order, which is to be enforced during the period from July to October, however exempted those cases which are considered as emergency works and some projects under PWD. According to officials of AMC, 958 cases are exempted from this prohibition order while 19 applications were rejected, 8 persons have also been fined Rs. 5,000 each for violation of this order.

 

 

B. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 16.08.2022

Pseudo seized:  A joint operation team of CID (SB) under Mizoram police and Assam Rifles on 13th August seized 10,000 tablets of Pseudoephedrine from the resident of Jonathan Saizuala Sailo (38) at Durtlang North, Aizawl. The owner of the resident, who was also the accused was booked under appropriate section of laws.
