A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.12.3.2013

Rs. 21 lakhs stolen from Aizawl Hospital: Office of Aizawl Hospital & Research Centre at Mission Veng was burgled yesterday ie, 11.3.2013. About Rs.21 lakhs was stolen and the culprit is not yet known. All the money kept in a safe after office hour on 9th March, 2013 was stolen.  A key was used to open the safe, it was found out yesterday. Police and Forensic experts examined the place of occurrence yesterday.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.12.3.2013

SHADC demands District Council & do not want too many Hydel Project: Sinlung Hills Autonomous District Council Demand Committee (SHADCDC) do not want the setting up of Hydel Project in their demand area, saying that it will hinder the livelihood of the people living in the area. SHADCDC demands Autonomous District Council.

            Chairman of SHADCDC, Lalmalsawma Darngawn said that 160MW is enough for sufficient power supply in Mizoram, and that it is not necessary to set up 6 (six) hydel projects in the Northern side of Mizoram

SHADCDC reported that 6 (six) hydel projects are planned to set up in their demand areas like – Tuiruang (1500 MW); Tuivai (210 MW); Tuivawl (42 MW); Turial (60 MW); Serlui B (12 MW) and Tlawnglui (80 MW).

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.12.3.2013

Licensing Regulations-2012: Licensing Regulations-2012 would be enforced by Aizawl Municipal Council from the next Financial Year ie, from 1st April, 2013. Every commercial place and shop under Aizawl Municipal Council area should have license; Residential Certificate must be produced along with the application form. As such is the case, commercial places and shops cannot be owned in the name of non-Mizos. License can be obtained till September this year and another 3 months will be given if issuing of license cannot be finished within the allotted period.
