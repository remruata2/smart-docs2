SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/729

Dated: 26th September, 2019.

 

 

Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Forum (MBDPF) sent a letter to Shri. A.P. Maheshwari, IPS, Special Secretary (IS), MHA, through the Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Kanchanpur, Tripura North on 25.9.2019 and requested him to convene a meeting of Joint Monitoring Group (JMG) at Kanchanpur, North Tripura. In the letter, MBDPF stated that Bru people living in the Tripura Transit camps are not willing to return to Mizoram with the present arrangement of repatriation process unless their demands are implemented. The MBDPF, in their letter, reiterated their various demands which were already conveyed to the concern authorities, such as :-

  1. Insufficiency of land at the selected rehabilitation centres and proposed the following places for rehabilitation, namely; Sirte Tlang in Lunglei District; Mualmam, Artepa Lungthirek, Kangzau, Tuilut, Zoar and Hmuitlang in Mamit District.
  2. Spot visit of the rehabilitation centres by the concerned stakeholders and construction of dwelling houses before commencement of the repatriation.
  3. To give liberty to displaced families to select their place of resettlement as per their own convenience.
  4. Acceptance of displaced families who left Mizoram in 2009 and grant of equal compensation to families displaced in 1997.
  5. To conduct re-identification of approx. 1281 families who did not turn up in the 2nd phase identification conducted in July, 2019.
  6. Reconsideration of the families who were rejected in the identification conducted in July, 2019 and extends the same compensation.

            MBDPF stated that their demands have already been accepted in the JMG meeting held on 10.9.2018 at Aizawl, Mizoram. They highlighted about their meetings with Mizoram State Government authorities and officials regarding their various demands and stated that the latter have no objections to make the demands successful if the Central Government and the JMG has accepted and approved.

            The letter appealed the authority to urgently convene JMG meeting at Kanchanpur, Tripura before the commencement of the repatriation i.e. 3.10.2019 wherein representatives of NGOs like Mizoram Bru Indigenous Democratic Movement (MBIDM), Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples Coordination Committee (MBDPCC), Bru Tribal Development Society (BTDS) and Bru Displaced Welfare Organisation (BDWO) can also participate. They further mentioned about a possibility of failure of the scheduled repatriation if their demands are not met by the concern authorities.
