****19.5.2022 :** National Students' Union of India Mizoram State (NSUI) submitted a written petition to Education Minister on 19.5.2022 wherein they stated that Lungmuat Primary School, Kolasib District is in a very pathetic condition and not suitable for occupation during rainy season despite receiving grant for its renovation amounting to Rs.10 lakhs from Mizoram government under NABARD loan. It also stated that they viewed the same seriously and intended to take necessary action in this regard till the very end. It urged the Education Minister to immediately enquire into renovation work of various schools**
