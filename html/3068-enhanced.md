****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/602

Dated: 28th October, 2020

  In continuation of our Special Reports vide No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/559 Dt. 9.10.2020 and No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/569 Dt. 14.10.2020, Information has been received that Shri. Mevar Kumar Jamatia, Tripura Forest Minister; District Magistrate, North Tripura; SP, North Tripura; SDM, Kanchanpur; SDPO, Kanchanpur; DFO, Kanchanpur; SDFO, Kanchanpur and Leaders of Bru Hindu religious community visited Thaidawr Tlang, under Phuldungsei VC, Mamit District on 26.10.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. Upon learning their visit, leaders of Mizo Convention of Zampui Hills, Tripura rushed to Thaidawr Tlang, but the Minister and party already left when they reached the area. During their visit, the Minister and party performed religious rites at the area.

  Further, around 50 (fifty) Tripura State Rifles (TSR) personnel are deployed at Thaidawr Tlang and they are stationed at old BSNL Office and PRJB School at Phuldungsei (both buildings are within Mizoram side). Earlier, they were performing duty at the approach road of Thaidawr Tlang, however, since Monday night i.e, 26.10.2020, around 20 (twenty) TSR personnel are deployed at Thaidawr Tlang/Hill peak.

  In this regard, Mizo Students’ Union (MSU) General Headquarters issued a press release yesterday i.e., 27.10.2020 which condemned the visit and performing of religious rites at Thaidawr Tlang by Tripura Forest Minister and party. It further urged the Government to deploy Mizoram Police at the said area so as to indicate the area as falling within its territorial jurisdiction.

  Yesterday i.e., 27.10.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. Post Commander, New Kawnpui BOP, 5th IR Battalion and party, who intended to conduct spot verification at Thaidawr Tlang as per instruction of SP, Mamit were stopped by TSR personnel on their way to Thaidawr Tlang and they then returned. TSR personnel on duty told them that visit of Thaidawr Tlang is not allowed without prior permission from Tripura Government.

  Yesterday evening i.e., 27.10.2020 @ 7:00 p.m. Leaders of Phuldungsei Village, Mamit District organised public meeting at Community Hall, Phuldungsei. Pu Sainghinglova Sailo, VCP, Phuldungsei presided the meeting and more than 140 (one hundred forty) members were present. During the meeting, they discussed Tripura Forest Minister and party visit of Thaidawr Tlang yesterday and their future course of actions. After long deliberation, the meeting concludes the following:

 (i) To oppose any religious group intention for construction of Mandir/Temple at Thaidawr Tlang.

 (ii) Not to circulate any unreliable news regarding Thaidawr Tlang through Media/WhatsApp etc.

 (iii) Decided to wait both the State Governments’ action as Tripura Forest Minister during his short interaction with leaders of YMA at Vanghmun stated that it is better to constitute a joint verification team from both the State Governments and to set up a negotiation table.

 (iv) To constitute a Peace Committee consisting of representatives from both Mizo and Bru communities and necessary follow up action in this regard will be taken up by Phuldungsei VC.

 Input received indicated that student bodies like MZP and MSU may pressurise the Government to take proactive steps and to deploy a good number of Mizoram Police in the disputed area.

 Situation is under close watch.
