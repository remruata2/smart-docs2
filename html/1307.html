2)        On 21.2.2007, 6 members of HNA (Hmar National Army) had been apprehended by villagers of Jurkhal near Dolakhal of Cachar District, Assam for alleged misbehaviour to the civilians. The villagers later informed the HPC(D) about the apprehension and the HPC(D) on their part took over custody of the said 6 persons from the hands of Jurkhal villagers with a promise to hand over to the army. However, the HPC(D) didn’t hand over the apprehended 6 persons to the army  and instead reportedly tortured them and finally killed them.

      On 4.3.2007, some HPC(D) cadres commanded by Vanlalchhunga @ Sunga, Area Commander of HPC(D) instructed some non-Mizo Bamboo cutters near Tuiruang river of Mizoram side to inform Tinghmun VC about the dead bodies to be recovered and accordingly VCP Tinghmun was informed who reported the matter to OC Sakawrdai on 5.3.2007.

1.Nangmawi s/o Buongzavung, c/o Zakunga, Khawlian, Mizoram.

6.Rohminglien s/o Hmangkung, Sihpuikawn, Manipur.

 

            The place where dead bodies were recovered is about 10 KMs from Tinghmun village (Mizoram) on the bank of Tuiruang river on the Mizoram side. Police recovered 9 nos. of empty cartridges of AK-47 ammunition. In this connection Sakawrdai PS Case No. 2/07 dt. 5.3.2007 u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(A) Arms Act was registered and investigation being taken up. The dead bodies were subjected to PME at Sakawrdai and were sent to their respective villages for burial.
