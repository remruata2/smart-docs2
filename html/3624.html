7.2.2022 :      Students' Self Support Union (SSU) General Headquarters held its Special Assembly on 5.2.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at Aizawl Press Club. Pu Lalnunhlima, President, SSU, presided the assembly and 24 (twenty four) members attended. Pu Laldawngliana, MLA, graced the function as Chief Guest and election of SSU OB for 2022-2024 was held in which Pu Darhmingliana was elected as President, SSU General Headquarters.

 

27.5.2022 : Students' Self Support Union (SSU) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 27.5.2022 stated that SSU donated blood today at Aizawl Civil Hospital on the occasion of its 37th Rising Day. It also stated that 44 (forty four) persons (Male-37, Female-7) donated blood at today's blood donation camp. It further stated that rising day celebration function will be held on 28.5.2022 at J18 Resort.

31.5.2022 : Students Self Support Union (SSU) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 31.5.2022 expressed gratitude for publication of HSSLC, 2022 Result today by MBSE. It congratulated all successful students of the said examination. It also congratulated all schools who are having top ten rank holders for the first time. It further expressed regret over lower pass percentage as compared to last year result and appealed all students, teachers and parents to work more harder.

25.10.2022:   Students' Self-Support Union (SSU) 35th General Assembly was held on 24.10.2022 @ 3:30 p.m. at Govt. Hnahthial College Auditorium, Hnahthial. Pu Darhmingliana, President, SSU, General Headquarters presided the Assembly and 49 (forty nine) delegates attended. They passed the following resolutions:

            (i)        Blood Donation should be organized every year on the occasion of SSU                 Rising Day.

            (ii)       The monetary reward of Self Support Award should be increased from                    Rs.10,000 to Rs.20,000/-

            (iii)     SSU should open Bank account for the needy students.

            (iv)      SSU should take necessary action so that the state government should                     devise policy for award of prize to self sufficient village so as to                               promote self sufficiency.

                        On  25.10.2022 @ 10:30 a.m., a general meeting was held at G&SA Hall, Electric Veng, Hnahthial. Dr. R. Lalthangliana, Higher & Technical Education Minister & Constituency MLA graced the function as Chief Guest and Pi Lalmuanpuii, Chairman Mizoram Cooperative Apex Bank as Guest of Honour. In the afternoon session, 'Intodelhna (Self dependent) Campaign and Career Awareness' function was held and Pu Lalhmachhuana Zofa, Editor, Diktawn Magazine & Resource person delivered a speech on the topic.
