****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/540

Dated: 9th July, 2021

 

 Information has been received that Assam Government intended to construct road up to Upper Phainuam Lower Primary School w.e.f., tomorrow i.e., 10.7.2021 (where bomb blast occurred on 22.10.2020). In this connection, they stationed one Hitachi Excavator (JCB) this evening i.e., 9.7.2021 @ 7:00 p.m near CRPF place of deployment who are being deployed by Assam at disputed area, near Saihapui 'V'. In this regard, on earlier occasion 5 (five) Assam Police and Forest Department personnel conducted spot verification on Dt. 4.7.2021. Upon learning the said activity, SDO(Civil), Vairengte intended to initiate road construction work from Buarchep (Phainuam) up to the said school w.e.f., tomorrow itself, i.e.,10.7.2021.
 Assam side also started construction of motorable road in between Balichhora, Assam to Aitlang Lui Hnar (Pu Ramhluna broom field) by engaging excavator (JCB) since Dt. 3.7.2021 and they almost reach Assam duty post presently established at Aitlang area.

 Reliable input received has stated that Assam intended to deploy 3 (three) more Companies of Assam Police Commando at disputed area, and in this connection, 2 (two) Companies of Assam Police Commando arrived Hailakandi, Assam today and another one company will arrive soon.
 Situation is under close watch.
