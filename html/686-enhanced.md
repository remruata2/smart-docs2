 Heng mite hi kum 2008 khan Pu Hmingchungnunga te remruatna in SPLA an zawm a, Tamu, Burma ah UPPK ho hnena training turin thawn an ni. Thla thum chhung UPPK hnena an training hnuah an thiante engemawzat kum 2009 ah an in surrender tak avangin ala bang mi 13 te chu UPPK hnuaiah hian hun engemaw chen chu an la awm a. November 2010 atangin S/S 2nd Lt. Remkunga@ Bren-a hovin loh theihlohna avangin Churachanpur ah HN an zawm ta a ni.

 HNA an zawm hnu hian Salem Camp, T. Khawnuam, Churachanpur atanga motor a darkar hnih vel kal ah KNO designated camp ah KNA, KNF(MC) leh ZDV mi leh sate nen mi 50/60 vel an khawsa a, heta an awm chhung hian operation duty KNF hote nen an chhuak ve zauh zauh bawk a. Tin, an camp ah hian HNA hotupa ber Lalthangsang Intoate pawhin a tlawh ve zauh zauh.

 Salem Camp a an awm chhung hian hnathawh mumal an nei lova, thingzaitu ah te, motor khalh leh siam ah te an hotuten an chhawr thin a, tun thleng hian HNA bik hian Manipur Khawmawi khuaah in pakhat thla khata Rs.1000/- chawiin an luah a, hetah hian HNA mi leh sa 5-10 an awm deuh reng a, an ei leh in tur chu an hotuten sorkar atanga an dawn ang zelin an pe thin. Sorkarin ration a pek chhan hi Kuki National Organisation(KNO) leh Security Force ten Suspension of Operation(SoO) an neih vang ani a, KNO hnuaiah hian HNA hi an awm vang a ni.

 HNA te hian tunah hian motor pakhat TATA Safari hlui lam tawh (a var) an nei a, Khawmawi an awmna ah hian an kawl a, Lalnunpuia@ Puitea S/o Huliana of Saiphai chu driver atan an hmang. Ani hi HNA cadre ve tho ani. An motor hi anmahni inphurh kual nan leh an ration te sem kual nan an hmang deuh ber.
