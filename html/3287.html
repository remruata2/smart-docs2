A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 25.6.2013

ILS examined: Instrumental Landing System (ILS) of Lengpui Airport was examined yesterday; it was stopped before completion due to bad weather condition. Calibration flight will be resumed today. If ILS Calibration flight can be completed today, result is expected within one week and if the result is good Lengpui Airport ILS can be used.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 25.6.2013

Darlawn Demands District by today: Darlawn District Demand Committee (DDDC) members and office bearers of YMA Branches of Darlawn had a meeting last night to decide on the next step regarding their demand for district. Darlawn demanded that Darlawn be announced as District by today ie, 25.6.2013. The Committee members of DDDC will gather at the YMA Information Centre today ie, 25.6.2013 to wait for the government decision. If good news is not heard from the government, they will discuss how to go on.  

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 25.6.2013

School re-opened: 26 Lumpsum grand-in-aid schools which had been closed for 3 (three) days were re-opened yesterday as authorized by the Association; their demand will be sorted out with the co-operation of Mizo Students Union within this month, said the Association.

 

D. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 25.6.2013

All houses dismantled at Mualzen hamlet: The houses occupied by 6 (six) families, who are bootleggers of liquor at Mualzen hamlet, were all dismantled last Friday. Mualzen hamlet is on the route to Champhai from Aizawl, 13 kms. away from Champhai. Occupants of Mualzen hamlet are all foreigners without Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC), they all returned to Kanan and Dinthar Veng of Champhai where they stayed before, said sources.
