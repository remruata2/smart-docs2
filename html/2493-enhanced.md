****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT AS ON 28.10.2020****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/605

Dated 28th October, 2020

In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/601 Dt. 27.10.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report**:

Today i.e., 28.10.2020 @ 2:40 p.m. around 30 (thirty) Assam Police personnel led by Addl. SP, (Border) conducted patrolling near Mizoram-Assam border. Temporary Police Patrolling post is being constructed today at around 500 meters towards Assam Side from the newly constructed Temporary Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Thinghlun.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report:**

Today i.e., 28.10.2020 @ 10:00 a.m. road block has been organized at Kalamati which is around 500 meters towards Assam from Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border. The matter was conveyed to the concern Circle Magistrate and OC, Rangamati PS was immediately sent to the spot to verify the situation. Road block is still continuing at the time of writing the report.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District:**

Today i.e., 28.10.2020 @ 10:35 a.m. Shri. Suman Mohapatra, IFS, CCF, Silchar visited Saihapui ‘V’. He was accompanied by Shri. Sunnydeo Choudhuri, DFO, Cachar; RO, Hawaithang and 19 (nineteen) Assam Police and Forest staffs. They had a meeting with Pi N. Vijji, IFS, DFO, Kolasib accompanied by Forest Department officials at Pu Lalhluna’s veranda at Buarchep Junction, located at around 900 meters from the border towards Mizoram to chalk out arrangement for conduct of joint patrolling. After long deliberation, both parties could not come to a conclusion as Assam Forest officials claimed that the area fell within their jurisdiction.

Further, today i.e., 28.10.2020 @ 12:25 p.m. Shri. GP Singh, Addl. DGP (L&amp;O), Assam; DIG (SR); Cachar, DC and SP Cachar visited bomb blast site i.e., SSA Lower Primary School, Saihapui ‘V’ after conducting brief inspection of the PO they then returned to Cachar via., Vairengte today’s afternoon at 12:55 p.m. During their visit, SP, Kolasib and OC, Vairengte were also present at the area. 1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report:**

Today i.e., 28.10.2020 around 12:00 noon around 30 (thirty) residents of Bhaichherra (Kuchuthol) area, Hailakandi District organised road block at around 7 (seven) km from state boundary near Bairabi. Information received indicated that road block organizers demanded withdrawal of Mizoram Police deployed at Zophai near Muslim Masjid within 24 (twenty four) hours. The road is leading to Bairabi, Kolasib District.
1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report:**

Leaders of Panchayat, Lailapur, Cachar District, Assam organised road block at Lailapur w.e.f., today i.e., 28.10.2020 @ 12:30 p.m. They intended to continue road block till withdrawal of Mizoram Police deployed at the outskirt of Vairengte at Assam-Mizoram border and complete settlement of boundary dispute.

Situation is under close watch.
