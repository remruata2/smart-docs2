A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 20.8.2013

Bandh cancelled: Mizoram Auto Rickshaw Owners Association (MAROA) General Headquarters cancelled their intention of total bandh today as the State Government agreed to reduce road tax from ` 850/- to ` 450/-.

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 20.8.2013

Bagha to Saipum road to be blocked: Saipum and Saiphai community said that the road between Bagha and Saipum is in a dilapidated state due to the over running of NEEPCO’s vehicles (HMV) and they demand NEEPCO to mend the road, the road will be blocked today for HMV to urge NEEPCO.
