****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/60

Dated 28th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/56 Dt. 27.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV – 9, MMV – 3, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 28.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV – 3, LMV -Nil) transited their goods at Medilli Bridge.

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 4, LMV - Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 28.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV – 7, LMV -Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
