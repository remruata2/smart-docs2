Sir,

 Apropos above, I am submitting the information regarding visit of Myanmar by Mr. Zoramthanga, President, Mizo National Front (MNF) Party and his meetings with different insurgent groups of Myanmar as below for your kind information.

 Mr. Zoramthanga along with Mr. Rosangzuala, Secretary, MNF Party left Aizawl on 26.2.2015, and were joined by Mr. Rakesh Sharma, Adviser of MNF Party from Delhi at Kolkata, they reached Rangoon, Myanmar on 27.2.2015. They were received by Mr. Nothankapa, Minister incharge Chin National Affairs and ex-president of Chin National Front (CNF). He also arranged for their fooding and lodging.

 A talk was held with leaders of (United Nationality Federal Council (UNFC) namely - (1) Dr. Laja, Secretary General (General Secretary, KIO); (2) Khu Ooreh, Secretary (Vice President, KNPP) and (3) Thla Lei, Joint Secretary (Secretary, Foreign Affairs, CNF) on 28.2.2015. The UNFC consists various insurgent groups of Myanmar including Karen Independent Organisation (KIO), Karen National Progressive Party (KNPP), Chin National Council (CNC), etc.

 Another talk was held on 3.3.2015 with Uhlamawng Shwe, Special adviser and Andrew Ngunchunglian, Legal Adviser of Myanmar Peace Centre (MPC).

 On 5.3.2015, a meeting was held with General Augmin, Chairman of MPC at his office committee room at Nyipytaw city. Mr. Rakesh Sharma and Mr. Rosangzuala also attended every meeting in Myanmar.

 After the meetings/talks with leaders of various insurgent groups, Mr. Zoramthanga and his aides reached Kolkata from Rangoon on 6.3.2015 and then reached Aizawl on 7.3.2015. Mr. Rakesh Sharma did not accompany them to Aizawl. No arrangement was made for next round of meeting/talk with the insurgent groups of Myanmar.

 Mr. Zoramthanga did not have any official/formal meeting with any Central Government officials during the course of his tour to Myanmar. He gave his tour report on 9.3.2015 at 'Mizo Hnam Run' at Aizawl.
