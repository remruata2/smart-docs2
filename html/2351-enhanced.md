****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/138

Dated 2nd March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/136 Dt. 1.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

21 (twenty one) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-13, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 2.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and one (1) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-19, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 2.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 8 (eight) vehicles (MMV-7, LMV-1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
