A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 27.6.2013

Crime figure falls down: Crime figure in Aizawl district has been going downward for the past 10 (ten) years. Crime figure falls down due to CYMA propagation against abusive substance and liquor.

            From the year 2005, Crime record in 9 (nine) Police Stations of Aizawl district had been going downhill. There are 9 (nine) Police Stations in Aizawl district – Aizawl PS, Bawngkawn PS, Vaivakawn PS, Kulikawn PS, Sairang PS, Saitual PS, Sakawrdai PS, Darlawn PS and Sialsuk PS. Among them, Aizawl PS has the most number of cases; Sialsuk PS has the least number of cases in all. From the year 2002 to June 2013, the crime figure in Aizawl district is 19251 in nos.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 27.6.2013

YMA awarded for outstanding services against drugs abuse: President of India Pranab Mukherjee awarded people/group who works against alcoholism and drug abuse on International Day Against Drug Abuse & Illicit Trafficking yesterday ie, 26.6.2013 in New Delhi. Among the awardees, CYMA was awarded ‘National Awards for Outstanding Services in the field of Prevention of Alcoholism and Substance (drug) Abuse’; CYMA President T.Sangkunga received the award on behalf of YMA. The award carries Citation along with ` 4 lakh in cash.
