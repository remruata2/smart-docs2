MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/124

Dated 22nd February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/119
Dt. 19.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

25 (twenty five) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-15, LMV-6) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 22.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-19, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 22.02.2021 @ 3:30pm.
6 (six) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-2) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

89 (eighty nine) vehicles (HMV-38, MMV-5, LMV-42, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

174 (one hundred seventy four) vehicles (HMV-59, MMV-51, LMV-25, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-28, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 22.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
