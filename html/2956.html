SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/48

Dated: 23rd January, 2019.

 

 

In continuation of our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/45, Dt. 22.01.2019:
Pu Lalchamliana, Hon’ble Home Minister held a meeting with leaders of all NGOCC today, i.e. 23.1.2019 @ 2:30 p.m at Secretariat Conference Hall. The meeting deliberately discussed about the intending boycott of the celebration of Republic Day, 2019 by the NGOCC. The Hon’ble Home Minister requested leaders of the NGOCC not to go ahead with their intended boycotting of the upcoming Republic Day, 2019 celebration and picketing of invitees, participants, etc. from entering the venue, i.e. A.R. Lammual, for the State celebratory function which was to be held @ 8:00 a.m on 26.1.2019. Consequently, the NGOCC held a meeting @ 4:00 p.m at Central YMA Office and discussed the request of the Hon’ble Home Minister. The meeting resolved to go forward with boycotting and picketing of the celebration function of Republic Day.

Information added that the Hon’ble Home Minister had called a meeting with leaders of Mizoram Kohhran Hruaitute Committee (Church leaders) tomorrow, i.e. 24.1.2019 @ 10:30 a.m at his office chamber to discuss about the on-going issue. It is further reported that
Pu L.H. Shanliana, IPS, IGP (Intel and L&O) had also scheduled a meeting with the Office Bearers of Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) General Headquarters @ 1:00 p.m in his office chamber (PHQ). It is also reported that other districts are likely to replicate the decision of Aizawl District in connection with the boycott/picket of Republic Day celebration in their respective Districts.
