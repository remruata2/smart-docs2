A. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 23.1.2014

Mizoram Police seize 44 cases of IMFL: Yesterday @ 9:00 am, DSB of Aizawl SP seized 44 cases of Mc Dowell's Rum from the possession of Lallawmzuali (34) d/o Lalkhuma, who claimed herself to be from Electric veng at Phunchawng Kawn. She said that these IMFL were taken from Silchar. Assam.
