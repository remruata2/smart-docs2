****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/014

Dated 8th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/11 Dt. 7.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/5 Dt. 5.1.2021 PWD, Assam continued road repairing work between Zaite Village, Assam and Assam Police Duty Post which is around 300 meters towards Assam from the place of deployment of BSF personnel today i.e., 8.1.2021.Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

There was no entry of vehicle at Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border and Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border due to inability to conduct Covid-19 Screening Test today i.e., 8.1.2021as Kanhmun Covid-19 Screening Centre (makeshift tent) located at Lalremsiama’s land at Lily Veng, Kanhmun need to be shifted due to construction of house by the owner at the said location.Information received indicated that new location for Kanhmun Covid-19 Screening Centre is not decided despite frequent verbal intimation of the matter to the concern authorities.

3 (three) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 3) transited their goods at Medili Bridge and 12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 12) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge today i.e., 8.1.2020 @ 3:30 p.m.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 79 (seventy nine) vehicles (HMV- 38, MMV – 3, LMV – 33, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 8.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 8.1.2021.
