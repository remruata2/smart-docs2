1. Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) in a statement issued today, i.e. 1.5.2018 thanked the general public within the demand area for their support during the Total Bandh observed on 27.4.2018, and appealed to them to extend their support in the event of further agitation. The SDIDC leaders are also intending to meet the Hon’ble Chief Minister for which they entrusted their two advisers, namely - Pu C. Zahmuaka and Pu C. Chawngkunga to seek prior appointment.

2. The SDIDC (Saitual District Implementation Demand Committee) leaders had met Pu R. Lalzirliana, Hon'ble Home Minister and Pu Lalsawta, Hon'ble Finance Minister today at their offices in pursuance of their demand for implementation of a District status for Saitual. It is learnt that the SDIDC leaders were disappointed with the Ministers' response to their demand, and information received suggested further that they may resort to adopting a stronger measures in this regard.

3. Leaders of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) led by Pu Rohmingliana, Chairman, SDIDC met senior leaders of Political Parties (MPCC, MNF, ZNP) today, i.e. 8.8.2018 in their respective party offices at Aizawl. It has been reported that Pu Zoramthanga, President, MNF and Pu Lalduhoma, President, ZNP assured leaders of SDIDC that Saitual District will be functional if their respective party comes to power. It is also reported that leaders of SDIDC held a telephonic conversation with Prof. J.V. Hluna, President, Mizoram BJP who had also reportedly assured a fully functioning Saitual District if the party comes to power. The 5 (five) SDIDC leaders left Aizawl for Saitual in the afternoon.

4. Office Bearers' Emergency Meeting of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) was held yesterday, i.e. 13.8.2018 @ 7:00 p.m at the residence of

Pu Rohmingliana, Chairman, SDIDC and it was attended by 4 (four) members. The meeting elaborated on the following points :-

 i) SDIDC will not allow Saitual public and Government officials (other than SDO/C) to attend the Independence Day 2018 programme at Saitual Sub-Divisional Officer (C) Office. SDIDC stated that Saikhawpui Thalai Pawl (Youths of Saitual) will be engaged to prevent Government officials and Saitual public from participating in the Independence Day 2018 programme.

 ii) SDIDC will launch road blockade at Khuangthli Kawn to prevent Pu Chalrosanga, MLA from attending Independence Day 2018 programme at Saitual.

 It has been reported that leaders of SDIDC expressed serious concern about the presence of Police Force at Saitual today, i.e. 14.8.2018 and leaders of SDIDC reportedly reiterated their decision to prevent Government officials and Saitual public from attending the Independence Day Programme tomorrow, i.e. 15.8.2018.

6. Reference to our DSI dated 16.4.2018, Item No. B(e). The Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) has called a Total Bandh within Saitual town area on 27.4.2018 from 5:00 a.m to 4:00 p.m. In a Statement issued dated 22.4.2018. 7. The Total Bandh organised by the Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) from 5:00 a.m today, i.e. 27.4.2018 was called off at 4:00 p.m without any untoward incident reported during the Bandh.

 In a statement issued today, the SDIDC justified their demand by stating that it would solve the administrative problem by downsizing the large area covered by Aizawl District, and also insisted that even the AICC President, Shri. Rahul Gandhi and Pu Lal Thanhawla, Hon'ble Chief Minister had promised them a District status earlier.

 The SDIDC urged upon the Government to take steps to improve road conditions in the area on priority basis before the onset of monsoon. They also expressed appreciation to Congress Units and Block level leaders within Tawi Constituency for their willingness to surrender/submit their seals in support of the demand for a new District.
