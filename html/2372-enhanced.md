****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/358

Dated 4th May, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/352 Dt. 3.05.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****8 (eight) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-5, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 4.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 3 (three) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-3, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

8 (eight) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-4, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 4.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

65 (sixty five) vehicles (HMV-48, MMV-3, LMV-8, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 4.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
