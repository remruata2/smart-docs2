****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/541

Dated:10th July, 2021

 

 In continuation of our Special Report vide No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/540 Dt. 9.7.2021 Assam government started construction of road from Pu Chalchhunga of Buarchep, Kolasib District paddy field, near Hawaithang Junction, which is around 500 meters north of Buarchep village today i.e., 10.7.2021 @ 9:30 a.m. and destroyed around 200 (two hundred) areca nut plants at the area. In this regard, around 20 (twenty) residents of Buarchep, who are having paddy fields near the area organized peaceful protest at the area about half an hour. After the protest, the construction work was resumed, and constructed the road upto Saihapui river, at the edge of Pu Chalchhunga's paddy field. ADGP Assam; DIG (S/R) ; DC, Cachar; SP, Cachar were present at the spot, and overseeing progress of the work.
 Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib; Addl. SP, Kolasib; Dy. Co, 1st IR Battalion and SDO(Civil), Vairengte alongwith around 50 (fifty) Mizoram Police personnel and around 20 (twenty) civilians from Vairengte, Phainuam, Saihapui ‘V’ and Buarchep were also present at the spot today. SP, Kolasib and officials of Assam held a brief talk, and Assam officials assured that the work will not cross Saihapui river today.

 In order to stop further advancement of Assam government activities at the area, Mizoram Police constructed trench at Pu Thangkhawliana farm land by engaging excavator (JCB), which is located near Saihapui river, towards Buarchep village today @ 3:30 p.m., and after completing construction of trench, they constructed motorable road, which will reach upto Buarchep village.

 3 (three) leaders of Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) General Headquarters led by Pu Peter Chhangte, Vice President MZP arrived at the disputed area today’s afternoon @ 3:00 p.m.
 A loud noise was heard this afternoon @ 3:07 p.m. inside forest near Assam's road construction site. The matter is being enquire to find out the exact cause.

 Input received stated that Assam government intended to construct road upto Upper Phainuam Lower Primary School, which is around 150 meters from Saihapui river towards Buarchep village. In this regard, they intended to continue road construction with larger contingent of Police force tomorrow i.e., 11.7.2021.

 Further, Assam Police started erecting tent at Pu Chalchhunga’s paddy field today’s evening @ 5:30 p.m. and around 50 (fifty) Assam Police are being deployed at the area.

 106 (one hundred six) Mizoram Armed Police are being deployed at Pu Thangkhawliana’s farm land, adjacent to Assam Police place of deployment.
 This evening @ 7:00 p.m. ADGP, Assam again visited the spot and held a brief talk with Pu David J. Byhnadaolai and Pu Bruce K. Vabeirokhai, Dy. Commandant, 1st IR Battalion, who are presently stationed at the spot, and informed that they will set up camp at the area, and will not advance further. Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IPS, DIG (NR) arrived Vairengte this evening to monitor the present inter-state border situation.

 Situation is under close watch.
