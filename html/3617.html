A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.11.10.2022

 SA to resume chiming of temple clock:  After Superintendent of Police, Aizawl District issued a notice to leaders of the Salvation Army (SA) Temple Corps, Aizawl to mute the chiming of their Temple clock during the period between 10:00 pm in the evening and  6:00 am in the morning, Mizoram Home Department instructed Aizawl S.P to clarify himself because of this notice. Mizoram Home department instructed Aizawl SP to submit a copy of notification order which notified him to be a competent authority in this regard and to verify whether the sound  of the clock exceeded the permitted level.

            As per information received by VANGLAINI, due to the noise emitted by the clock, a Commandant from Assam Rifles lodge a complaint to senior officer of Mizoram police who in turn gave instruction to Aizawl SP to issue the notice to leaders of Salvation Army.

            Meanwhile, information received by VANGLAINI indicated that the Chiming of the Temple clock will be resumed after leaders of Salvation Army Temple Corps had requested the government to take steps for the restoration of the chiming of their clock.

 

 

B. Front/second page  of  VANGLAIN newspaper  Dt.10.10.2022

Training for MPS officers : Mid-Career Training Programme was organised for officers of Mizoram Police Service yesterday at Administrative Training Institute auditorium, Aizawl. In this programme, which will last for 5 days, experts from Mizoram University, Police and senior government officials imparted training to directly recruited MPS officers.

            Inaugurating this training programme, Mizoram Home Minister Pu Lalchamliana delivered a speech wherein he expressed the need for police personnel to upgrade themselves in their everyday works and that there is no room for slackness in maintaining Law & order and manning the inter-state and international borders.

            The inaugural function of the training programme was also attended by Mizoram DGP Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastva, DIG (Training) Pu LR Dingliana Sailo, AIGP(Training)  Pu Bhisham Singh and other senior oficials.
