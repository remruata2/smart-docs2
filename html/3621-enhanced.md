**16.6.2022 :** Primary School Teachers' Association (PSTA) General Headquarters, Lawngtlai in its Press Release issued on 15.6.2022 considered the Press Release issued by National Students Union of India (NSUI) District Headquarters Lawngtlai on 14.6.2022, which alleged presence of irregularities in the recruitment of Primary School Teachers Recruitment Examination conducted by Education &amp; Human Resource Development Department, Lai Autonomous District Council (LADC), as an attempt to defame and false accusation of Education Department (PS), LADC and the invigilators. It also highlighted the various procedures followed for the said examination and fair supervision of the examination by invigilators. It further expressed regret regarding issuance of the said PR by NSUI Lawngtlai, which could be perceived as an attempt to defame the invigilators and Education Department (PS), LADC. Further, it also expressed their desire that such incident should not recur.
