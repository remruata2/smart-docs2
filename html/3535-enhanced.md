A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 08.12.2022_

****Former PWD E-In-C convicted**: Yesterday (7.12.2022) , Special Judge (Prevention of Corruption Act) Dr. HTC Lalrinchhana convicted former Mizoram PWD Engineer in Chief, Pu Liansanga of Khatla, Aizawl in connection with corruption case and was sent to Central Jail on the day itself. The court verdict came after a complaint was submitted by PRISM to Mizoram Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) in connection with an alleged misappropriation of funds and corruption in the construction process of Kau Tlabung and Tuipanglui Mini Hydel Project. The Special Court conducted hearing of the case for a considerable period of time after it received statements from ACB and convicted the accused, Pu Liansanga under IPC Section 409/477A and PC Act section 13(2) r/w PC Act section 13(1)(c)(d). Other sections of law under which the accused was booked, IPC section 420/34/120B were however quashed by the Court.**

**B**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 08.12.2022_

****4,542 bags of areca nuts seized by police:** During the period between 1st January - 30th November 2022, Mizoram police have seized 4,542 bags of areca nuts illegally imported from neighbouring Myanmar. In this connection, five criminal cases have been registered and Mamit is the district where the largest numbers of areca nuts were seized. According to a statement by the police, eight suspected persons have been arrested for smuggling of these areca nuts where five cases have been registered against seven of these accused. All the recovered areca nuts were from different districts of Mizoram excluding three states - Lunglei, Siaha and Lawngtlai districts. The total estimated amount of all the seized articles are worth around Rs.2,21,25,000. The police statement further requested the public to promptly inform the police and the authorities if they any illegal transportation of areca nuts and not to deal with it by themselves alone.**
