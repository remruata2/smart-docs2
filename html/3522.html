A. Front page of  VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 22.08. 2022

Rs. 25 & 7.5 Lakhs for Commonwealth Games medallists  : In a function organised at Vanapa Hall, Aizawl on 19.8.2022 to felicitate the two medallists of the recently concluded Commonwealth Games 2022 from Mizoram, Government of Mizoram handed over cash reward of Rs 25 lakhs and Rs. 7.5 lakhs to Jeremy Lalrinnunga (Gold medallist)  and Lalremsiami Hmar (Bronze medallist) respectively. The function was graced by Chief Minister Pu Zoramthanga, Home Minister Pu Lalchamliana and Sports Minister Pu Robert Romawia Royte , all of whom delivered speeches apart from the two medallists.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 22.08.2022

Voluntary Blood Donations programme by various ChurchesYouth members : In different parts of Mizoram, voluntary blood donations programme was organised on 20.8.2022 by Youth organisations of various Church denominations, where a total of 309 members donated blood for different Hospitals.

            a) TKP Bawngkawn Area - 100 units of blood donated in ZMC

            b) TKP Thuampui Area - 26  units of blood for Aizawl Civil Hospital

            c) KTP Bethlehem Venglai Branch - 55 units of blood  for Synod Hospital.

            d) KTP Kolasib Diakkawn Branch - 37 Units of blood for Kolasib Diakkawn               Hospital.

            e) KTP Champhai Tlangsam Branch  & Tlangsam North Branch- 21 Units of                blood for   Champhai District Hospital.

            f) Aizawl Adventist Youth Federation (AYF) - 44 Units of blood for Aizawl                           Civil Hospital.

            g) AYF Lawngtlai Branch - 10 Units of blood for Lawngtlai District Hospital.
