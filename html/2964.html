SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/70

Dated: 31st January, 2019.

 

 

Information received indicated that one Lalruatpuia (25) S/o F. Laldinliana of Siahatla, who was convicted under POCSO Act and sentenced to a term of 15 (fifteen) years imprisonment which he is presently serving in District Jail, Siaha, has gone on hunger strike since 21.01.2019 protesting that he had been wrongfully convicted. As his condition has deteriorated, Jail Superintendent had written to the Deputy Commissioner of Siaha District for transfer of the said prisoner and he has been transferred to Siaha District Hospital tonight itself, i.e. 31.01.2019. It is also reported that residents of Siahatla locality supported the cause of Lalruatpuia and the situation turning volatile cannot be ruled out if his condition worsens to the extent of losing his life.

Moreover, there is a report of discontent among the District Jail inmates as they accused Pu H.T.C. Lalrinchhana, Judge, Fast-track Court, Siaha of high-handedness during trial, harsh sentencing and discrimination. The inmates reportedly intended to submit an F.I.R against the Judge and they also stated that they will not attend Court proceedings during the presence of the Judge within the Court premises.

Necessary security arrangement has been made by the concerned District Police to meet any Law and Order situation. 1 (one) platoon of Armed Police Personnel has also been sent to Siaha to assist the District Police force.

DGP (M)
