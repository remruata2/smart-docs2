A. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 12.08.2022

Brus established permanent settlement in Tripura:  Around 46% of Brus who resided in various relief camps of Tripura had established permanent residence in Tripura. According to the agreement signed between Mizoram, Tripura, leaders of these relief camps and Central Government on 6th September 2019 , Brus living in the relief camps are to be settled permanently in Tripura State. Each family in the camps should be given Rs. 1.5 lakhs as housing assistance along with a plot of land for building constructions. They are to be scattered in 12 different villages/towns in 4 different districts.

           

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 12.08. 2022

MPF gears up for 2023 election: The governing body of Mizoram People Forum (MPF) on yesterday i.e 11.8.2022 organised a meeting/discussion on how to spread awareness programme in order to have a free and fair election in the upcoming 2023 MLA election with 9 of their selected Resource Persons. In this programme, which was presided over by MPF Vice President Upa Lalhuapzauva, the General Secretary Rev. Lalramliana  Pachuau briefed the members about the various projects of MPF and explained the task and responsibilities of the appointed Resource Persons.

            To have a fair and peaceful election in 2023, the Resource Persons will prepare guidelines for the public and political parties and other relevant points. Using these papers prepared by the Resource Persons, awareness campaign and workshops will be organised at each constituency and district level under the guidance of the local MPF leaders.
