Champhai PS C/No.113/14 dt.25.8.14 u/s 5 of ES Act was registered on the basis of suo motto FIR submitted by **ASI Zirthanga Pachuau of Sector Officer CID(SB),** Champhai to the effect that on 25.8.14 @ 10:00am, he received a reliable information from his source that one vehicle(1109 truck) MZ-04/6653 was carrying items suspected to be carrying illegal goods towards Zokhawthar. Accordingly, he immediately rushed towards Zokhawthar on the arrival of Zokhawthar himself with Zokhawthar Police tried to locate the said vehicle and found the vehicle at Zokhawthar. On checking the vehicle, nothing was found and then interrogated the driver Laltlanzuala(23) S/o Lalramliana of Zion Veng, Champhai. The said driver stated that they have already unloaded items suspected to be illegal goods at one house belonging to Lalhrilliana(40) S/o Lalzika of Zokhawthar. Hence, himself with Zokhawthar Police **recovered 7 nos. of sack containing 7 realms of Cordex each, total of 49 realms worth Rs.3,00000/- approx.** on being led by the said driver. He formally seized the said explosive in the presence of reliable witnesses after doing seizure. Himself with Zokhawthar Police brought the seized items alongwith the said driver to Champhai PS.

 Two accused persons namely - Lalhmunmawia(31) S/o Remsanga, Venglai Champhai and his wife Zaihmingthangi(40) W/o Lalhmunmawia were already arrested by Champhai Police.
