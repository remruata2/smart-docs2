A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.14.10.2022_

**Verification of SEDP Funds:** According to the notification issued by Planning &amp; Programme Implementation department, funds received by beneficiaries of different trades under the Socio Economic Development Programme (SEDP) will be verified so as to ascertain if each beneficiary utilised the funds received by them for the intended purposes. The first instalment of SEDP, which is Rs. 25,000 each will be distributed to all the beneficiaries before 31th October 2022 while the second instalment (Rs.25,000) is expected to be completed before 15th February 2023.

 The verification of utilisations of these funds will be conducted after the disbursement of the funds by concerned department under the guidance of District Level SEDP Committee; they will also be assisted by village/local committee if required. The Department inspection report will then be submitted to SEDP Executive Board. Improper utilisation of funds by beneficiaries who are found by the department inspection will be banned from receiving further family oriented SEDP.

B. _Front page of VANGLAIN newspaper Dt.14.10.2022_

**Blood donation**: Yesterday (13.10.2022) 34 male and six female students of Govt. J. Thankima college donated 40 units of blood for Aizawl Civil Hospital while 33 male and seven female members of Chhinga Veng KTP Branch donated another 40 units of blood for Synod Hospital,Aizawl.

C. _Front page of VANGLAIN newspaper Dt.14.10.2022_

**Diamond Jubilee of Synod HSS**: Synod Higher Secondary School yesterday organised a programme to celebrate the Diamond Jubilee of the establishment of their school which was graced by Mizoram Synod Moderator, Rev. Dr. Vanlalnghaka Ralte. To mark this anniversary, student of Synod HSS had donated 207 units of blood recently and also donated 11 bags of rice, 1bag of pulses and Rs.82,500 in cash to TNT center, Zuangtui, Aizawl. Presently, Synod HSS, which was established in the year 1962, is having one Principal, one Vice-Principal, 53 teachers, 12 non teaching staffs and 1,366 students.
