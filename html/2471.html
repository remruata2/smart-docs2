MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/622

Dated 5th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/619
Dt. 4.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          There is no movement of vehicles across Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border today i.e., 5.11.2020 due to roadblock organized on either side of the border.

          As per report of SP, Mamit yesterday i.e., 4.11.2020 Taitesena Group YMA members who participated in a community work for repair of Zamuang to Bairabi road as a precautionary measures removed some soiled wooden plank of a wooden bridge between Saikhawthlir, Mamit District, Mizoram and Gutguti, Assam which is around 1(one) km from Saikhawthlir Village as the bridge was in a dilapidated condition and not safe even for walking. The two villages are connected by a fair weather road and Gutguti, Assam is around 2 km from Saikhawthlir Village.

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          Today i.e., 5.11.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. Shri Deepak Kumar Kedia,
IGP (L&O), Assam visited Saihapui‘V’. He was accompanied by Shri Dilip Kumar Dey, DIG (SR), Silchar; Keerthi Jalli, DC Cachar; Shri Bhanwar Lal Meena, SP Cachar; Shri Suman Mohapatra, CCF, Silchar; Shri Sunnydeo Chaudhury, DFO and other Assam government officials. They entered Mizoram side upto Buarchep Peng (which they claimed to be within Assam territory) which is around 2 km towards Mizoram from Assam-Mizoram border and had an interaction with Pu Lalhluna, who resides near Buarchep Peng. During their interaction, Pu Lalhluna informed Assam officials that they have been residing in the area since 1926 and Mizoram government provide electricity, ration and other benefits, and when asked about which side they used to cast their vote, he replied that they cast their vote in Mizoram only. During their visit, around 100 (one hundred) Civilians assembled at Assam side and later dispersed. Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) and
Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib were also present during their visit.

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          There is no movement of vehicles across Bairabi border today i.e., 5.11.2020.  Situation is normal.

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          Blockade of vehicles bound to Assam via Phaisen road at Zukbual Mual, Bilkhawthlir by Buhchangphai MZP is still continuing today i.e., 5.11.2020 and 15 (fifteen) vehicles are stranded due to the said roadblock till today.

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          Road  block organized by  Zoram Drivers’ Union (ZDU) at Tawngtaina Hmun, Outskirt of Vairengte is still continuing today i.e., 5.11.2020. There is no vehicular movement across Mizoram-Assam border at Vairengte and
172 (one hundred seventy two) vehicles are stranded due to the said road block till today.

          Further, Shri Deepak Kumar Kedia, IGP (L&O), Assam and other officials visited Vairengte Auto Stand today i.e., 5.11.2020 @ 12:00 Noon.
Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) and Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib were present at the area during their visit. They are looking for place of deployment of SSB (CAPF) to be deployed by Assam government and
Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR) suggested them the vacant land which is around 360 meters (1172 ft) towards Lailapur from Vairengte Auto stand.

  

          Situation is under close watch.
