A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 13.09.2022

Skywalk project to be resumed  : Mizoram Tourism department is intending  to resume the Skywalk project at Sakawrhmuituai hill which has been halted for a considerable period of time while the ropeway project between Chaltlang and Durtlang will not be resumed. The Skywalk project, although there are plans for its resumption, will not commence immediately as there are arbitration issues to be settled with the contractor. According to information received by Vanglaini newspaper, Lokayukta of Mizoram had earlier directed Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) Mizoram to probe into the allegations received against these two projects, the final reports of which is being awaited by Lokayukta.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 13.09.2022

Group A and B Salaries of Government employees released : Mizoram Government on yesterday (12.9.2022) released  August, 2022 salaries of Group A&B of its employee while the remaining which were not released will be released  today (13.9.2022). Previously, the government had released salaries of muster roll, work charge/ provisional employees and Group C&D on 1st  and 6th September 2022 respectively.

 

 

 

C. Front page of  The Aizawl Post newspaper  Dt. 13.09.2022

Police Hospital inaugurated: Director General of Police (DGP) Mizoram, Pu Devesh Chandra Srivastva on 9th September,2022 inaugurated a 10 bedded new hospital building at 1st Bn. MAP Headquarters, Armed veng, Aizawl. This new hospital building which was constructed using funds from NEDP 2018-2019 scheme (Rs.70 Lakhs) consisted of its basement, ground floor and first floor, and is manned by 1(one)  Medical Officer from 3rd Bn. MAP, 5 staff nurses, 1 pharmacist and 15 other staffs.
