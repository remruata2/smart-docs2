Subj :              Details of Stranded foreigners & status of Rerfugees

 

Ref:                 Letter No. MSB/CB/Covid-19/215 Dt.16.04.2020.      

 

                        With reference to your letter cited above, I would like to submit the Details of Stranded foreigners in Mizoram and status of Chin refugees residing at Delhi. My findings are as under:-

 

1. All the 17 Myanmar Nationals have been released from different quarantine facilities ie. 5 persons from YHAI- Luangmual and 11 from Tourist lodge –Berawtlang on dt. 6.4.2020 and one Miss Lalhnempari from RST-Saidan, Kolasib on the same day.

 

2. But there is no transportation facilities for them to travel toward Zokhawthar, Myanmar Border as it is Lockdown period.

 

3. All the 15 persons are continued their stay at Tourist Lodge, Berawtlang under the supervision of DC, Aizawl till today. i.e 20.4.2020.

 

4.  Feeding and Lodging are given to them free of cost.

5. According to them, they eagerly waiting the permission of Govt. Authorities to avail any transport vehicles for Zokhawthar and they also said that they can manage their travel expenses for their own.

 

6. Status of Chin refugees residing at Delhi situation is normal. They lived in different places inside Delhi i.e Janakpuri, Sitapuri, Hastel, Udela etc.

 

7. As on today, there is no state of difficulties among them as they received supports from UNO, Local Church Leaders i.e Presbyterian, Member Contribution, Generous Individuals etc.

 

8. They have been formed Delhi Chin Refugees Welfare who closely associated with Delhi Mizo welfare and fight together for the ongoing crisis.

 

                        As the above mentioned I would like to conclude my verification that all the 17 Myanmar Nationals are presently staying at Aizawl and Status of Chin refugees are normal till today .i.e 20.4.2020.

 

Enclosed: List of the stranded 17 foreigners.
