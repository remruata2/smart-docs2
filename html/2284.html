MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/101

Dated 10th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/97
Dt. 9.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV -7, MMV -14, LMV - 9) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 10.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-1, LMV- 1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

15 (fifteen) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-13, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 10.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 1 (one) vehicle (MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

In connection with Zophai, Assam-Mizoram boundary conflict that occurred yesterday, Zampuimanga Group YMA held its Emergency Meeting at Bairabi South Branch YMA Committee Room under the guidance of leaders of CYMA today i.e., 10.2.2021 @ 7:30 a.m. The meeting was presided by Pu Lalhmachhuana, Vice President CYMA and 20 (twenty) members attended. The meeting discussed inter-state boundary issues and decided to conduct detail study of the inter-state boundary and in the meantime decided not to take immediate action in the name of YMA since the situation has improved. After the meeting @ around 1:00 p.m., leaders of CYMA led by Pu Lalhmachhuana, Vice President visited the disputed road construction site undertaken by Assam at Assam-Mizoram border, under Police escort to take stock of the situation and they left Zophai for Bairabi this afternoon @ 2:25 p.m.

Further, Pu K. Lalrinliana, Minister FCS&CA visited Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border this morning @ around 9:00 a.m. and he left the area for Bairabi @ around 1:00 p.m. this afternoon. Pu C. Lalsawivunga, MLA and Dr. Vanlalthlana, MLA also visited the site this afternoon @ 2:00 p.m.

11 (eleven) MZP leaders led by Pu B. Vanlaltana, President MZP visited the disputed road construction site today @ 2:00 p.m. Around 100 (one hundred) civilians from Assam also gathered near the area but the situation was under control by security personnel deployed at the area. Leaders of MZP left the area for Bairabi this afternoon @ 2:50 p.m.

 

20 (twenty) Mizoram Police personnel were deployed at the disputed site. In addition to the existing force, one Platoon each of 2nd IR Battalion and CRPF personnel were deployed at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border to reinforce the existing force. Pu Vanlalfaka, SP Kolasib and Pu R. Vanlalduata, SDPO, Kolasib are presently stationed at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border to prevent further escalation of tension along the border.

 

 

 

 

Yesterday’s evening, i.e., 9.2.2021 @ 9:22 p.m., a bomb blast occurred at Kachurtol LP School, located at around 300 meters west of Zophai BOP towards Assam side, beyond Bairabi Lui/Ramri Lui (boundary river), near Assam-Mizoram border and miscreant(s) responsible for the said bomb blast has not yet been identified at the time of writing of the report. The blast damaged brick wall of the school and the blast is believed to have been caused by Gelatin dynamite. Pu R. Vanlalduata, SDPO Kolasib, ASI Lalfamkima, Bairabi PS and party visited the PO today i.e., 10.2.2021 @ 9:00 a.m. and necessary enquiry is being underway and 2 (two) experts of Forensic Science Laboratory, Aizawl led by Richard Lalhnehzova, JSO visited the PO this evening @ 3:25 p.m. and they left the area around 4:30 p.m. after conducting necessary scientific examination of the PO.

29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-23, MMV-1, LMV-5, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-Nil) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 10.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

239 (two hundred thirty nine) vehicles (HMV-110, MMV-46, LMV-44, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-7, Oil Tanker-28, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 10.02.2021during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
