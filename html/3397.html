A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 12.2.2014

Deep Mandal is expected to be released: Pu R.Lalzirliana, Home Minister said that Mizoram Police has been doing its best for the safe release of Deep Mandal who is abducted along with two Mizos by Bru armed gang on 23.11.2013. NLFT High Command has already agreed to release Deep Mandal and the information is also given to Area Commander. So, Deep Mandal is expected to be released soon and if he is released, Mizoram Police will receive him at the border.    

 

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 12.2.2014

Department of Transport & SP, Traffic Notice – Taxi Drivers' Training: One day Training is to be held for Aizawl Taxi driver at Mualpui, Aizawl today organised by a joint team of Transport Department and Traffic SP. All taxi drivers are informed to attend the meeting and the taxi permit of those who do not attend the meeting will be suspended or cancelled. Taxi service will not be available today for this reason.
