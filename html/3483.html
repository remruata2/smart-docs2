B. Front page of MIZO AW newspaper Dt.25.3.2014

 

Prohibitory order : Based on CrPC Sec.144, the Sub - Divisional Magistrate, Aizawl Sadar Sub-Division, prohibited a procession /gathering in between Aizawl Post office and Raj Bhavan south gate by any political parties or NGOs. This prohibition will not include government programmes however, if any posters or banners are to be displayed by any government departnment in these areas, prior permission must be obtained from the authorities. The prohibition will last for six months starting from 24th march 2014 unless it is revoke.
