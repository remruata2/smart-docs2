A. _Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 2.4.2014_

**FIR Lodged****:**Muthi land was burnt on 30.3.2014 and it was thought to be done intentionally. As there are certain suspects, Zemabawk North Branch YMA lodged an FIR at Bawngkawn Police Station on 31.3.2014.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 2.4.2014_

****Poll Boycott****:**A public meeting of Selam Village in Champhai District on the night of 31.3.2014 under the aegis of Village Council passed a resolution to boycott the coming Lok Sabha Election to be held on 9.4.2014 if the State government fails to post at least five new teachers in the Selam Village Primary School on or before 7.4.2014.**
