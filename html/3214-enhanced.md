A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 23.7.2013_

****CYMA resents ransack**: The house of Lalrintluangi Sailo of Phunchawng was ransacked on Sunday night by unidentified people. She is the owner of a house rented by Mizo Insuihkhawm Pawl (MIP) as office. The padlock of her house was broken, her bedding and utensils were torn and broken while some household goods were casted around; an FIR was lodged at Vaivakawn Police Station regarding this incidence. Central YMA resents this ransack.**

1. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 23.7.2013_

**FIR lodged**: Vanramchhuangi, Director of Human Rights &amp; Law Network lodged an FIR to ACB regarding the professional tax cut from the salary of 2nd IR Battalion personnel at Khawzawl which amounted to Rs. 18,99,820/-. The amount cut as professional tax has not been submitted to government account till today. She requested the ACB to probe into the issue, to find the people responsible for it and to give them punishment for their wrong doing.

1. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 23.7.2013_

**Threat to Pangbalkawn**: Pangbalkawn village in Kolasib District has been threatened recently by unidentified people; due to the threat, night armed duties are detailed, said sources. Regarding this, The Zozam Times had a telephonic conversation with Vanlala, VCP of Pangbalkawn village who informed that the matter is not to be disclosed to the media as Police order; and said he could not inform anything else other than that there is threat and duties are detailed.
