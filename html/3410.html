A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 7.1.2014

3 Two-Wheelers to be recovered: Among the two-wheeler stolen from Aizawl during Christmas and New Year festival, three of them were recovered by Lakhipur Police. These 3 two-wheelers are expected to be returned/recovered to Aizawl this week.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 7.1.2014

Traffic Police Report – 811 road accidents, 17 people died: As recorded by Aizawl Traffic Police Station, there were 811 road mishaps in and around Aizawl during January to November 2013; among them were 337 two-wheelers, 276 LMVs, 130 taxis, 39 HMVs and 29 buses. 17 peoples lost their lives in those accidents, 13 male and 4 female. 431 people sustained injury out of which 232 were male and 199 were female. Drunken driving causes most of the accidents, 236 accidents occurred due to drunken driving.

8,697 people who broke traffic rules were fined during January to November 2013, the fine amounted to ` 21,16,230/-.
