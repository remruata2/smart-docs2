MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/421

Dated 3rd June, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/419
Dt. 2.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

          Information received stated that yesterday i.e., 2.6.2021 @ around 12:00 noon, 1 (one) Bolero believed to be carrying officials of Assam entered Chuhvel village, Mamit district and subsequently left the village after observing the area and without any verbal exchange with residents of Chuhvel village. Again, this morning, i.e., 3.6.2021 @ 10:00 a.m., 2 (two) Assam Forest officials, 9 (nine) Assam Armed Police and 4 (four) CRPF led by Circle Inspector visited the paddy field of Pu John Halliday, located at the bank of Betchhora river/Sakeilui towards Mizoram side, in between Chuhvel and Saikhawthlir Village and around 1 (one) km from Chuhvel Village and proceeded upto the courtyard of Pu John Halliday’s Farm House. They subsequently left towards Assam side upon seeing Mizoram Police personnel (3rd MAP personnel from Saikhawthlir BOP), who are deployed to conduct area domination patrolling at the area due to additional deployment of Assam Police personnel at Gutguti BOP Camp (around 4 km from Saikhawthlir Village) last evening i.e., 2.6.2021. Upon learning the incident, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah and SDPO, Kawrthah and OC, Zawlnuam OP and party immediately rushed to the area.  In this connection, a section strength of Mizoram Police is deployed at Gutguti Junction, near Saikhawthlir Village, to conduct area domination patrolling and additional 2 (two) sections of 4th IR Battalion personnel are deployed at the area to prevent escalation of tension.  

 

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

           10 (ten) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-8, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 3.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

24 (twenty four) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-20, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 3.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
