SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/134

Dated: 21st February, 2019.

 

 

In continuation to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/123 Dt.18.2.2019, information has been received that leaders of Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Forum (MBDPF) namely, A. Sawibunga, President; R. Laldawngliana, Vice President and Bruno Msha, General Secretary arrived at Aizawl from Bru Transit Camp, Tripura yesterday, i.e. 20.02.2019 with the intention of meeting the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Mizoram.

They held a meeting with Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Coordination Committee (MBDPCC) leaders namely, Laldingliana, President and George-a, Secretary, who had reached Aizawl earlier on 14.02.2019 for similar purpose, at Hotel Ahimsa today, i.e. 21.02.2019. The meeting, which discussed about the various demands made by the Brus and the impending repatriation process, decided to either merge into a single organisation or select a representative body for all Bru organisations before holding talks with the Mizoram Government. They decided to return to their camps at Tripura where they will hold a joint meeting of MBDPF, MBDPCC, Mizoram Bru Indigenous Democratic Movement (MBIDM) and Bru Tribal Development Society (BTDS) within this week and to further hold a public meeting as prescribed by that joint meeting. They will try to find a common ground and select representatives for a united single body which will represent the whole community which will then proceed to Aizawl to meet Pu Zoramthanga, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Mizoram and other dignitaries and officials of the Mizoram Government.

After the meeting, MBDPCC leaders left Aizawl for Tripura while MBDPF leaders will halt the night and return tomorrow, i.e. 22.02.2019. Both leaders of the organisations will be returning without meeting any dignitaries or Government officials.
