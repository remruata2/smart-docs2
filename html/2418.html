MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/132

Dated 25th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/129
Dt. 24.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          Around 80 (eighty) members of MZP General Headquarters led by
Pu B. Vanlaltana, President MZP who were proceeding towards Mualmawi, Assam to attend Thangram Indigenous Cultural Meet were stopped by SSP personnel on duty (deployed by Assam) at Assam-Mizoram border yesterday’s evening i.e., 24.2.2021 and later allowed to proceed to Mualmawi, Assam after intervention of Mizoram Police.

Thangram Cultural Meet was commenced yesterday’s evening i.e., 24.2.2021 @ 6:00 p.m. At the same time, Addl. District Magistrate, Karimganj issued Order vide No. KAL 9/2017/140-A Dt. 24.2.2021 which denied holding of Thangram Indigenous Cultural Meet at Mualmawi, Assam due to border dispute and ongoing tense situation at the inter-state border.

Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha who will grace the function as Chief Guest was stopped by SSB personnel deployed at Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 25.2.2021 @ 9:00 a.m. In this regard, DC Mamit requested DC, Karimganj to allow free passage of the VIP. After waiting for more than 4 (four) hour at the border, Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha proceeded to Mualmawi today’s afternoon @ 1:20 p.m. after obtaining permission to attend the function for 2 (hour) duration from DC, Karimganj.
Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha graced the function as Chief Guest and the function ended today’s evening around 4:15 p.m. He will depart for Aizawl the same evening.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

31 (twenty four) vehicles (HMV-17, MMV-10, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 25.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-24, LMV-6) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 25.02.2021 @ 3:30p.m.
5 (five) vehicles (MMV-4, HMV-1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
