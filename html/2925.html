SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/128

Dated: 19th February, 2019.

 

 

In continuation to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/122 Dt.18.2.2019, a meeting between SP, Kolasib and All NGOs, Bairabi including representatives from VC, YMA, MZP, MSU, MHIP, etc. was held today, i.e. 19.2.2019 @ 10:30 a.m at Bairabi PS.
SP, Kolasib was assisted by Addl. SP, Kolasib, SDPO, Kolasib and O/C, Bairabi PS. The NGOs submitted the following requests :-

 

  1. Strengthening of IR Post at Zophai and provision of motor vehicle for Zophai Post.
  2. Improvement of communication facilities.
  3. Regular patrolling of IR Police along the Inner Line Reserve area.
  4. To take initiatives for better relations between landowners and tenants.
  5. To move the Assam Police to stop collecting of money from Mizo vehicles at Assam Police Checkpost near Gharmura.
  6. Strengthening of Bairabi PS staff including Women Constable.
  7. To check the Brus from cultivating within Zophai area as it lies within the Inner Line Reserve area.

 

After the meeting, SP, Kolasib and party proceeded to Hailakandi @ 11:15 a.m to have a meeting with SP, Hailakandi. Report on the outcome of the meeting is being awaited till the writing of this report.
