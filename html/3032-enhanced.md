****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/725

Dated: 25th September, 2019.

Input received indicated that leaders of various NGOs of Bru Relief Camps in North Tripura, viz. MBDPF, MBDPCC and MBIDM are secretly lobbying with some unknown persons in an attempt to influence the Government to concede, at least some points of their demands, which include among others, cluster village and permission of MBDPF and family heads to identify selected sites and construct houses prior to commencement of repatriation. The leaders are understood to have gone to Agartala and Silchar earlier today, i.e. 25.9.2019 to approach certain persons ideally those who could have an influence on Govt. officials handling the repatriation process.

In the meantime, they also intend to insist on inclusion of representatives of MBDPCC and MBIDM at a meeting of officials from Govt. of India, Govt. of Tripura, Govt. of Mizoram and MBDPF scheduled to be held at Damcherra on 26.9.2019.
