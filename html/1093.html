2. Overview of  Law and order situation in the State of Mizoram

Law and order situation in the State of Mizoram is by and large remained relatively peaceful during 2017 (i.e January 2017-11th December, 2017).  However, some activities of Political parties, NGOs, Student organisations,etc  affecting law and order situation in Mizoram during 2017 were highlighted as under:

Political Parties Activity

During the year 2017, all political parties like MPCC, BJP, MNF, ZNP, MPC,etc of  remained busy in organizing workers’ conferences, public meetings and awareness programmes to garner public support in their favour for the upcoming election/by election. Besides, the opposition political parties like MNF,ZNP, MPC and their frontal organizations raised several allegations against the incumbent governmnet  on  various issues   like protest against dilapidated/bad condition of roads in different part of Mizoram, Protest against selling of liquor,etc  However, all these political parties activities did not escalate into any serious law and order problem.

 NGO activities:

Anti-Liquor Shop Protest:

In Mizoram, Wine shops were opened under the Mizoram Liquor Prohibition Control  Act- 2014 from 16th  March 2015 amidst protest from some local churches and NGOs.  In some localities, the activists of Churches and NGOs  staged strong  protest by forcing some vendors  to  close  down their wine shops. However,these agitational activities were handled with patience and forbearance as well as caution and restraint.

                   

ILP Checking Drive:

 Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA)  which is the most influential NGO of Mizoram conducted Inner Line Permit (ILP) checking  against non-tribal traders/workers  illegaly staying in Mizoram without valid ILP.  Due  to pro-active role, constant vigil of  local administration and police such drive went off peacefully .

 

District Demand Protest:

Several committee like Saitual District Implementation Demand Committee, Khawzawl District Demand Committee, Darlawn District Demand Committee, Hnahthial District Demand Committee launched agitation like total band, etc during 2017 in demand of establishment of District Headquarters in their respective areas.

           

 

           

Labour & Service organizations activity:

(i)Mizoram CSS Hindi Teachers Association organized Hunger strike during .....in demand of regularisation of their services. The agitating members breached the security of the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Mizoram  on..... However, the police managed  to disperse the agitators peacefully  and all the leaders of ...MCSSTA were arrested and released them on bail after they promised to call off their agitation.

(ii)Members of  Federation of Mizoram Government  Employees & Workers (FMGE&W) launched a series of agitation like attending office with wearing black badges, Pen-down strike and Mass Casual leave during November-December,2017 in demand of implementation of Recommendation of 7th Pay Commission for Government employees of Mizoram. However, they called off their agitation on 7.12.2017 after they received verbal assurances from the Hon’ble Chief Minister and Hon’ble Home Minister  to look into their demand.

 The agitation did not escalate into any major law and order situation.  

Students Organisations activity:

 

             During the month of .....Mizo Zirlai Pawl(MZP) launched a strong protest against the selection of students from Chakma Community for the Mizoram state quota of MBBS seat. The activists of MZP assembled in front of the office of  H&TE Department , Chaltlang,Aizawl on.. trying to prevent all the Chakma students from  attending  the counselling  session for allotment of Medical(MBBS) seat. However,  activists of MZP started attacking the police personnel after they learnt that all the Chakma students  had attended the counselling session under the escort of Police. As a preventive measures the police also resorted  to lathi charge and tear gas and the irate mobs finally dispersed from the premises of of the office of  H&TE Department , Chaltlang,Aizawl. The situation was handled by the local administration and police tactfully.
