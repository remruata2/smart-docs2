  With reference to your letter and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:-

**Starred Question No. 316 :**

**a) Mizoram chhungah Hel (Rebel) an awm em?**

**Ans:**  Mizoram chhungah Hel an awm hriat ani lova. Mizoram hel nia sawi thin HPC(D) leh BNA te pawh hian Mizoram chhungah khawi hmunah mah camp an nei lo. 

**b) Tun hma a hel training-na khawpui chhunga awm thin ang kha tunah awm a awm em?**

**Ans:**  Awm lo.

**SUPPLEMENTARY NOTES :**

**1. HPC(D) leh BNA te hian khawi hmunah nge camp an neih?**

**Ans:**  HPC(D) te hian Hqrs./Hideout chu Sibapurikhal (Ankachhuah), Manipur leh Hmarkhawlien, Assam-ah te an nei a, BNA phei hi chuan khawi hmunah mah camp ngial nghet an nei lo.

**2. Hel-te hian khawiah nge training an neih thin?**

**Ans:** HPC(D) te hian training camp hi Chandhikhal ramhnuai, Cachar, Assam, Khawbawn ngaw thlang, Kangrengdor, Manipur atanga ke a darkar 3 (thum) kalah an nei a ni.

 BNA member thenkhat chuan Manipur hel UPPK hnuaiah training an nei a, an mahni pualin training camp an nei lo.
