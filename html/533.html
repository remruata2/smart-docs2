PREVIOUS CASE  INVOLVEMENT OF VANLALFELA

1.    Marpara PS Case No. 2/2000 dt. 20.6.2000    u/s 394/121/121-A/387/116/506          IPC r/w 27 Arms Act 

    On 17.6..2000 @ midnight, 27 )twenty seven) BNLF militants dressed in camouflage uniform and armed with assorted weapons including AK-47 Rifles entered into the house of Pu Lalit Chandran, VCP Marpara South and demanded Rs. 10,000/- from him,  since he did not have the amount of cash demanded, he handed over Rs. 4,200/- from the Mandir Fund kept by him for safe keeping. The militants also threatened to kill him if he fails to give them Rs. 10,000/- whenever demanded. The militants thereafter took him alongwith them up to a place between Marpara and Teraboma and from there proceeded towards Bangladesh via Karnaphuli river. Hence the case.
