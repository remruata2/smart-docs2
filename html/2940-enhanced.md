****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/371

Dated: 5th June, 2019.

On 01.06.2019, Mizoram Police seized 1,10,000 (one lakh, ten thousand) Methamphitamine tablets worth Rs. 165 Lakhs at Champhai. Two persons, viz. Haukhemkham (25 yrs) and his wife, Chingsianding (27 yrs) were arrested and Champhai PS Case No. 44/19 Dt. 01.06.2019 u/s 22(c) ND&amp;PS Act has been registered.

During interrogation, it was revealed that the drug consignment was to be transported to Agartala through another courier in Aizawl, subsequently arrested and detained in Police custody, and also being cooperative. Further inquiry revealed that the arrested persons were mere transporters employed by the main smuggler assuming fake identity as Muktab Uddin Mezarbhuiya (48 yrs)s/o Farkan Ali Mezarbhuiya, r/o Tarapur, Silchar, Assam and is operating from Tripura. He is known to be a Myanmarese Muslim. His location had been tracked at Sonamura, Tripura - 799131.

Accordingly, Tripura Police were alerted and the accused, who turned out to be the kingpin of the illegal drug smuggling, had been arrested by Tripura Police during wee hours of this morning (5.6.2019). It has been revealed that the real identity of the accused is Shomsul Alam s/o Late Abdul Hamid, a Myanmarese origin of Arakan state. It was also revealed during preliminary interrogation that he is in possession of an EPIC purportedly acquired through fraudulent means in the name of Muktab Uddin Mezarbhuiya s/o Farkan Ali Mezarbhuiya, r/o Tarapur, Silchar, Assam. He is being brought to Aizawl.
