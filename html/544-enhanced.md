1. **Branches:**

Special Branch is divided into various Branches for the smooth functioning of its day to day works. The important Branches are as follows:

1. Reserve Branch
2. Foreigner Cell/ Verification Branch
3. Technical &amp; Smac Branch
4. General &amp; Account Branch
5. Confidential Branch
6. Field Branch
7. MT Branch

1. **Verification Branch**

1. _Foreigners’ Registration:_

 Visa related services were done by this Branch. In September 2018, Foreigner Registration System was changed by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India. In this new system, foreigners who have visited Mizoram have to register themselves in the online portal. However as the system is complex, this Branch serves as a facilitator and conduct interview and field enquiry whenever requested by the Foreigners Regional Registration Office, Kolkata.

1. _Verification of Passport applicants:_

 Personal Particular Form (PPF) of Passport applicants within the state of Mizoram were downloaded, printed and send out to all concerned district SsP for police verification by Verification Branch, CID (SB). Upon receiving completed Police Verification Form (PVF) from concerned district SsP, PVR were scanned, entered and uploaded in the PSP system by VB.

 Besides this, appointment verification, verification of character and antecedents of Para-Military personnel were done by this Branch.

1. **Confidential Branch**

 The main duty of the Confidential Branch is to deal with all the correspondences of intelligence input received from various sources relating to the security of the States in particular and the nation as a whole, law &amp; order situation, movement and activities of insurgents outfits in and around the State, kidnap incidents, arms smuggling, etc. it is the duty of this Branch to disseminate such inputs to the concerned as and when called for. It also deals with queries under RTI Act, compilation of weekly, monthly and half yearly returns of CID (SB), furnishing of some specific required information during the sitting of Parliament and State Legislative Assembly is also the duty of the Confidential Branch.

1. **Field Branch**

 The main task of Field Branch is intelligent collection and information collection covering the whole of Mizoram. It established as many as 16 (sixteen) Sectoral Offices throughout Mizoram deploying officers and other ranks. Headquarters Field Staff are further divided into different groups to cover the activity of political parties, various NGOs, various organisations within Government services, movements of students’ union bodies etc.

 Field staff prepared daily reports covering the activity of the stated various organisations. The reports are further submitted to the higher authority in the form of Daily Summary Information or Special Report as and when necessary.

 Besides the day to day normal works, it pursued different works such as assessment of security threats perception, verification of character and antecedents’ of any individual etc. which are assigned by higher authorities.

 Field Branch further diligently watched and closely monitored the activities of suspected persons on smuggling of Arms, Drugs etc. It closely watched the behavior of history sheeter. It also monitored the movements of the underground cadres in and outside the state of Mizoram.

1. **Technical Branch:**

 Technical Branch of CID (SB) started functioning from 16th April 2010. The main function of this branch is collection of intelligence through technical surveillance viz. analysis of mobile telecommunication voice and data.

 SP, CID(SB) acts as Nodal Officer (Law Enforcement Agency), Mizoram Police, any correspondence relating to tele-communication are handled and managed by Technical Branch. Initially, it was equipped with Voice Logger which is capable of monitoring 16 mobile numbers at a time. Better equipment called Verba Centre was later installed and started functioning since 24th September, 2014. Working system of technical branch is on round-the-clock basis.

 During the past 12 months, as many as 130 soap cases containing Heroin No. 4 worth Rs. 131.6/- Lakhs (approx) are seized and 19 culprits were arrested through Technical Surveillances.

 Mizoram Police have made a number of remarkable achievements as a result of real time intelligence. Armed activities of HPC (D) and Bru elements have virtually been contained, which is without doubt, the result of successful operations conducted in pursuant to the real time intelligence provided through technical surveillance conducted by Technical Branch.

 It may not be out of place to mention that the HPC (D) were compelled to come to negotiation table to seek peaceful dialogue, while Bru Armed gangs operating in the Western belt of Mizoram and Assam border areas have been contained to certain extent. The targeted operations successfully carried out against HPC (D) leaders as well as those of BDFM and such other Bru elements based in Bangladesh, Tripura and Assam were the results of real time intelligence provided through Technical Surveillance.

****FUNCTIONS OF THE SUBSIDIARY MULTI AGENCY (SMAC) AT CID(SB)****

 The Subsidiary Multi Agency Centre (SMAC) started functioning at the Office of the Superintendent of Police CID (SB) from the 1st of May, 2011. The main function of this agency is the sharing of intelligence inputs with the different intelligence agency around the country. The sharing of intelligence with the other agencies is done through a secured channel called TMS (Threat Management System). This TMS was developed by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA). Meetings are held monthly on regular basis wherein inputs are shared among various intelligence agencies.
