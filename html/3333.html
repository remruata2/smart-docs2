A. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 3.9.2013

Kidnapper arrested: A lass who kidnapped a 6 year old girl from her home ie, Kawnpui was arrested along with her victim in a non-Mizo truck between Kawnpui and Bualpui on Monday at around 11:00 AM.

            The culprit lodged near the girl’s house and led her away on Sunday night to wait for vehicle for transportation, and disappeared with the girl. Kawnpui community and police carried out a search operation. The culprit and the victim were found in a non-Mizo truck/vehicle by tea stall workers. On hearing from the tea stall worker that a girl was kidnapped, the truck driver suspected his passengers and told the tea stall workers and found they were the ones being searched.

            According to the kidnapper, she took the girl to Aizawl on Sunday night and took her back to Kawnpui from Company Peng of Hunthar Veng. The kidnapper was sent to Kolasib District Jail, the angry crowd of Kawnpui went to District Jail to see the kidnapper but the Police did not allow as she is a Juvenile. The Police and the crowd scuffled a bit but nothing untoward happened, sources said.

 

B. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 3.9.2013

Women Cell visited by MSCW Chairperson: Lalnipuii, Chairperson, Mizoram State Commission for Women and other members paid a good will visit to Women Cell, Aizawl Police Station to see its functioning on 2nd September. Officer-in-Charge, SI Lalhmachhuani Sailo and 12 staff look after the Women Cell. The commission lent ears to their problems and grievances.
