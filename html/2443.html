MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/140

Dated 3rd March, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/138
Dt. 2.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-15, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 3.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-25, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 3.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m.
4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

Leaders of ZPM Party Thalai General Headquarters led by
Pu. Malsawmzuala, their President visited Zophai BOP and disputed site at Assam-Mizoram border yesterday i.e., 2.3.2021 @ 6:00 p.m. and handed one water filter to staff of Zophai BOP. They left the area at 6:45 p.m. the same evening.

91 (ninety one) vehicles (HMV-58, MMV-2, LMV-23, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 3.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

124 (one hundred twenty four) vehicles (HMV-50, MMV-23, LMV-19, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-13, Oil Tanker-13, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 3.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
