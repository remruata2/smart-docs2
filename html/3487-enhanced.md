A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 31.3.2014_

**Body of Drowned Man Found at Chhimtuipui****:**On Friday, Buansanga (34) driver of Fire &amp; Emergency Service Department went to fetch water in the department vehicle on the shorter junction of Lawngtlai and Saiha at Tuipui Ferry, Chhimtuipui. After filling up water in the vehicle, he went for a swim and drowned at around 2:00pm. His body was found on Saturday at around 5:55am and it was brought home to Cheural for funeral.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 31.3.2014_

**Garage Ransacked – Two-wheelers Burnt****:**On Saturday at around 1:00-2:00am, some unknown miscreants ransacked garages and set fire on two-wheelers at Chawnpui Veng, Aizawl. They burnt the seat of one Scooty in a garage and at another garage, they ransacked five two-wheelers, one Scooty and two Syntex (water container) which were kept inside the garage were completely burnt down. In this connection, FIR was lodged at Vaivakawn Police Station, Police had apprehended the three accused on Saturday. Among them, Lalmalsawma @ Puitea (18) s/o Sanghnuna of Lower Chawnpui was forwarded to Central Jail but the other two are juvenile and were forwarded to Remand home.

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 31.3.2014_

****10,702 Persons without ILP Pushed Back****:**Till now, 10,702 non-Mizos without ILP were sent home from Mizoram; the State government has issued 16,746 ILP from Aizawl between January-December 2013 and 12 non-Mizos who misused their ILP were arrested and punished in Aizawl during 2013.**

 As reported, answering RTI question by SsP of each District, non-Mizos without ILP arrested and pushed back were 568 from Aizawl District, 9,933 from Kolasib District, 150 from Serchhip District, 51 from Mamit District and none from Champhai, Lunglei, Lawngtlai and Saiha District.

D. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 31.3.2014_

**Drug Peddlers Arrested****:**A joint operation team of Electric Veng Branch YMA Kumpuan Committee and Village Defence Party (VDP) of Electric Veng, Aizawl apprehended 22 drug peddlers at Aizawl New Market area on Saturday and were handed over to the Police.

E. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 31.3.2014_

**A Man Found Dead at Lungsen Forest****:**On 28.3.2014 at round 8:00am C.Vanlalduha(50) of Lungsen Chhimveng was found dead near his jhum house at Lungding forest where he went and stayed the night with Lalremruata. Lalremruata of Lungsen Chhimveng was apprehended by the Police for interrogation.

 Lalremruata went home at around 9:00am on Friday morning and when C.Vanlalduha’s family asked him about C.Vanlalduha, he told them that he just went home alone and left him. C.Vanladuha’s family searched for him and found him dead 100 ft. away from the jhum house. One rib on the right side of C.Vanlalduha was broken and some parts of his body were injured.
