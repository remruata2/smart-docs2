GROUND RULES FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF SUSPENSION OF OPERATION BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT OF MIZORAM AND THE HMAR PEOPLEâ€™S CONVENTION (DEMOCRATIC)

 Mizoram Sorkar leh Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-te chuan inpawhtawn taka an ngaihdante an sawiho hnuin January 31, 2013 hian heng a hnuaia point tarlante hi inkahhai thuthlung (Suspension of Operation) hlawhtlin theihnan January 31, 2013 a\anga hman tan turin an remti e.

OBJECTIVES :  (a) Heng thuthlungte hi remna zawnga inbiakna a awm theihna turin inkahhaina thuthlung zawn hmasak ngei tura ruahman a ni a, Mizorama Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-te harsatna sutkian hlenna tura inremtlanna thu sawiho theihna tur atana siam leh remtih tlan a ni.  (b) Inremna tlo zawk a awm theih nana inrintawnna a awm theihnana siamho a ni.

BASIC PRINCIPLE :  1. Mizoram State Police ten Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-te (lakah offensive operation) bei turin hma an la lo ang a. Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-ten remna kawng zawh tibuai thei eng thil mah an ti lovang. Hmar People Convention (Democratic) member ten Mizoram leh \henawm state-a thlamuang taka an vahvel theihnan State Sorkar-in he inremna hi zahsak turin Central Sorkar hnenah ngenna a siam ang. Amaherawhchu, he inremna a tello hel pawl dangte beih kawngah Mizoram Sorkar chuan a duh dan angin hma a la thei ang.
 2. Inkahhai hunchhungin \henawm State-te : Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura leh Manipur chhunga Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-ten thlamuang taka an veivah theihnan Mizoram Sorkar-in ngenna a siam bawk ang a. Remna kawng zawh (peace process) tibuai zawnga Hmar People Convention (Democratic) an chet loh chuan security agency dangte pawh Hmar People Convention (Democratic) beihna kawnga hma la lo turin Mizoram Sorkar-in a hriattir bawk ang.

 3. Heng inremna point-te hi zawm \hat anih ngei theihnan Hmar People Convention (Democratic) leh Mizoram Sorkar aiawh member inzat (Joint Monitoring Group) siam a ni ang.

 4. Inrin tawnna leh remna leh muanna kawng zawhna turin puakthei thil leh silai keng lovin Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-te Mizoram chhungah zalen takin an kal vel thei ang.

 5. Hmar People Convention (Democratic) leh Mizoram Sorkar inkahhaina thuthlung hi thla ruk (6) chhung atan siam phawt a ni ang a. Mizoram Sorkar leh Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-ten rem an tih tlan chuan a pawhsei theih ang a, hemi chhung hian remna kawng zawnga inbiakna vawi hnih (2) tal neih hman tur a ni ang.

 6. Invenhimna kawng leh remna leh muanna lam chi tibuai thei zawnga chetna reng reng Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-ten an titawp nghal ang a. Pawl leh mimal invauna lam chi reng reng leh invauna anga ngaih theih thil reng reng an ti tawp nghal ang.       Inthlanna (election process) hrang hrang Parliamentary Election, State Assambly Election leh local election-ah vote thlaktuten an duhna tak hmang thei lova invauna lam chi reng reng emaw, invauna anga lang chi reng reng Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-ten an titawp nghal ang.

 7. Mizoram Police ten Hmar People Convention (Democratic)-te bei turin hma an la lo ang a. Amaherawhchu, Mizoram himna te, Police Station hnathawh pangngai leh Battalion Outpost ten law and order vawnhimna kawnga hna an thawh pangngai chu a pangngaiin a kal ang.

 8. Mizoram Sorkar-in Liaison Officer atan officer pakhat (1) a ruat anga, SP(CID) nen inberawnin remna kawng zawh tura inbiakna atana mamawh hrang hrangah (transportation, accomodation, etc) mawh a phur ang.

 9. Heng inremna point hrang hrangte hi a tu zawk zawk pawhin an bawhchhia anih chuan heng inremna point-te hi chawp leh chilh a tawp nghal anga ngaih a ni ang.

Date : 31st January, 2013
