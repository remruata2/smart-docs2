BOMB BLAST DURING 2014

(From 1st Jan, 2014 till 1.8.2014)

 

(1)       On the night of 30.1.2014 @ 10:55 am, an unknown type of explosive bomb was exploded from one vegetable shop at Treasury's Square market. Destroying some shops into bits, and caused severe damages to the shop itself from where the blast took place as well as its adjacent shops while other nearby shops also are reportedly heavily damaged.

            All the window panes of Assembly secretariat and the Information and Public Relations office were smashed under the impact, window glasses of other nearby buildings including private residences were also shattered. The whole walls of two rooms, housing drivers and guards, in the Assembly Secretariat were also severely damaged by the blast. Neither the type of bomb/explosive used nor the culprits and their intention were not yet known however the case is being thoroughly investigated by Police and Forensic personnel. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. In this connection Aizawl PS Case No. 28 dt. 31.1.2014 u/s 436 IPC r/w 3 of Explosive Substances Act has been registered.

Damages caused by the explosion are listed below:

       (1) 266 window panes, 65 ventilation glasses and 6 nos. of tube light frames of Assembly House and 1 extended room used as Driver' room of Assembly House were badly damaged.

       (2) 231 window panes,168 ventilation glasses and 22 nos. of window frames of Information & Public Relations New building.

       (3) 135 window panes and 5 nos. of window frames of Information & Public Relations Old building.

       (4) 72 window panes, 12 ventilation glasses and 25 nos. of wall tiles of Printing & Stationery building.

       (5) 8 window panes, 2 nos. of window frames and 5 nos. of wall tiles of Pi Thanzami Tochhawng's house.

       (6) 7 window panes of MJA Office/Press Club.

       (7) 7 nos. of hawkers shed of Treasury Bazaar were totally damaged and 14 nos. of hawkers shed were partially damaged.

 

(2)       On the night of 20.2.2014 at 11:45pm, some unknown miscreants had blasted an explosive suspected to be gelatin at the entrance of New Market meat shop (old building). The explosion had caused damages such as: (i) Main gate of old building of New Market meat shop, (ii) some portion of the retaining wall near the main gate, (iii) 3 window frame, 14 window glass, 4 ventilator and ceiling tile of teachers' room of SSA girls hostel, (iv) 11 window panes and wall tiles of Trade and Commerce Deptt. Quarters. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. The case is being investigated by Aizawl Police Station Staff. In this connection, Aizawl PS C/No.48/14 dt.21.2.14 u/s 436 IPC r/w 3 of Explosive Substances Act has been registered.

(3)       A powerful bomb exploded near the residence of Pu Hmingchungnunga, Chairman, Sinlung Hills Development Council (SHDC), Bungkawn Vengthar on the morning of 22.3.2014 @ 3:50 AM damaging over 220 numbers of window panes/ventilation glasses and some household properties of the targetted building and the adjoining private buildings at Bungkawn Vengthar, Aizawl. However, there was no casualty due to the explosion. Pu Hmingchungnunga is currently residing in the third floor of the building of Pu Hmingdailova Colney (L) with his eleven family members on rental basis. In this connection Aizawl PS C/no.84/14 Dt. 22.3.2014 u/s 447/427 IPC r/w Sec 3(a) E.S Act have been registered.

            The bomb was suspected to be triggered by supporters of HPC(D), however, detail investigation is on to ascertain the persons behind the blast. It may be mentioned that there was a strong rivalry between HPC(D) and Pu Hmingchungnunga since long time back and an attempt to assassinate Pu Hmingchungnunga by HPC(D) cadres was also foiled during October, 2007 by arresting 2 (two) HPC(D) cadres along with geletine sticks, detonators, pistol, remote control, etc. Aizawl Police have so far detained one person, namely Lalchungnunga (23 yrs) S/o Lalbiaksanga, Zemabawk, Aizawl.

           

 

(4)       On the night of 18.4.2014@11:00pm, there was a bomb blast within the vicinity of Chaltlang Mual Veng on a path way leading westward of Chaltlang Mual Veng near Chaltlang High School resulting in partial damage to one Assam type building belonging to  Pu R.Lalchhanhima, EE P&E Department as well as one RCC building belonging to Dr.Ramfangzauva of Chaltlang Mual Veng which were situated on both side of the pathway. The blast also left a dent on the surface of the pathway. However, luckily no human being was hurt in the blast. Expert team from FLS Aizawl, on being requisitioned, also visited the place of occurrence and all requisite formalities have been observed and according to the experts, explosive used in the blast was apparently Gelatin explosive. The total value of damage inflicted due to the blast is estimated to be around rupees one lakh. In this regard, Bawngkawn PS C/No.78/14 Dt. 19.4.2014 u/s 3(a) E.S Act has been registered.

In this connection, Kulikawn Police Station Case No.98/14 dt.1.8.2014 u/s 436/506/427 IPC r/w 3(a) of Explosives Substances Act has been registered and investigation is being conducted.

            The bomb was suspected to be triggered by supporters of Hmar Peoples' Convention (Democratic), however detail investigation is on to ascertain the person behind the blast. It may be mentioned that HPC(D) had claimed responsibility for the recent bomb blast at Bungkawn Vengthar, Aizawl on 22.7.2014@ 2:35 am.         

            CID(SB) Ops. Team led by Pu Lalrinpuia Varte, Addl. SP, CID(SB) apprehended the main culprit - Lalringthanga (45) s/o P.T. Kaia (L) of Mualtluang, Diphucherra, Cachar District, Assam on the morning i.e. 3.8.2014 @ 5:50 AM at Dinthar Veng, Vairengte, Kolasib District. Accused Lalringthanga confessed before Police that he had exploded Gelatine Bomb on the early morning of 22.7.2014 @ 2:35 AM at Bungkawn Vengthar (Ref. Aizawl PS C/No. 228/14 u/s 436/427 IPC r/w Sec. 3 of Explosive Act Dt. 22.7.2014). Accused Lalringthanga also confessed that he had exploded Gelatine Bomb on the early morning of 1.8.2014 @ 3:45 AM near the residence of Pu Lt. Col. (Rtd) Z.S. Zuala, Hon’ble Parliamentary Secretary (Home etc) (Ref. Kulikawn PS C/No. 98/14 u/s 436/506/427 IPC r/w 3(a) of Explosive Act Dt. 1.8.2014). Accused Lalringthanga also confessed that he had blasted the aforesaid explosions at the behest of H.D. Hmangaiha (53) s/o Bawnga, Govt. Complex, Luangmual who was already arrested by Mizoram Police on the night of 1.8.2014 @ 10:00 PM from his residence. H.D. Hmangaiha is presently holding the post of Asst. Secretary, Mizoram Hmar Welfare Committee.
