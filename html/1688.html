Hmar Inpui 7th Assembly Hmarkhawlien ah nei

 VIRTHLI HMAR  

Hmarkhawlien, May 15, 2015(Virthli News): Hmar Inpui (Hmar Supreme House) 7th Assembly chu vawisun khan RPC Training Hall, Hmarkhawlien, Cachar hmuna nei a nih. Hi huna hin Hmar Inpui Region tin, HYA GHQ, HSA GHQ le HWA palai hai chu kimtakin an fekhawma Hmar Inpui President inrawina hnuoina rorelna le Hmar Inpui Assembly Secretariat thlangna hai an nei.

Assembly hin Barak Valley-a Hmar khuo le ramhai humhal le maksan hnunghai hluo kir nawk a ni theina dingin Hmar Inpuiin theitawpin hma la ding le hi le inzawma hmalakna report kimchang chu July 2015 bo hma ngeia peluta Hmar Inpui Executive Council hmasatak a ngaituo le bawzui ni ngei dingin an rel a nih.

 

Hmar Inpui Constitution (review), November 6, 2014 Special Assembly-in ‘in principle’-a a lo pawm tah chu Hmar Inpui 7th Assembly (May 15, 2015; Hmarkhawlien, Cachar, Assam) hin hmang le zawm tan nghal ta dingin an namdet bakah Financial Rules & Policy chu Executive Committee buotsai dingin an rel a, Assembly nawka namdet ngei nisien tiin an rel bawk a nih.

 

Hi Assembly hin Hmar Inpui Constitution-a um angin 2017 Assembly (8th Assembly) chena Speaker dingin Prof. Vanlalnghak, Shillong, Meghalaya le Secretary dingin Pu Lalthalien (Imphal, Manipur) hai chu lungruol takin an thlanga Deputy Speaker I chu Executive Committee ruot dingin an sie a; Deputy Speaker II le Deputy Secretary chu Barak Valley Region le NC Hills & Karbi Anglong Region haiin June 2015 bo hma ngeia anni hung ruot ding le Headquarter a an peklut dingin an sie bawk a nih.

 

Mizoram Hmar Welfare Association Vice-Chairman Pu Lalmuanpuia Punte in May 3-12, 2015 sunga Union Home Minister, Jt. Secretary (NE), Ministry of Home Affairs le central-a national party thuoituhai Mizoram, Manipur le Assam-a Hmar political issues dang dang le inzawma an inhmupuina thu hi huna hin report a pek a; Tv. Vanlallien Pulamte-in Cachar, Hailakandi le Karimganj districts, Assam-a Hmar hai Scheduled Tribe enlistment le inzawma hma an lo lakna hai report pekna a nei bawk.

 

Assembly rorel hi thataka zo a ni a, Assembly palai iemanizat fekhawm hai chu Hmarkhawlien ah la cham in zinga (16th May) Hmar Youth Association, Hmarkhawlien in  Hmar Martyrs’ Day programme puithu taka a buotsai tum a chun an la thang ding a nih.

 

 

 

 

HMAR INPUI 7TH ASSEMBLY HMARKHAWLIANA NEIH

________________________________________________________________

 

VIRTHLI HMAR :- Hmarkhawlian 15th May, 2015 (Virtlhi News) :

 

          Hmar Inpui (Hmar Supreme House) 7th Assembly chu vawiin khan RPC Training Hall, Hmarkhawlian, Cachar hmunah neih a ni. He hunah hian Hmar Inpui Region tin, HYA GHQ, HSA GHQ leh HWA palaite kimtakin an kalkhawma, Hmar Inpui President kaihhruaina hnuaiah rorelna leh Hmar Inpui Assembly Secretariat thlanna te neih a ni.

 

Assembly hian Barak Valley-a Hmar khua leh Ram humhalh leh kalsan/hnuchhawn tawh te luah/laklet leh a nih theihna turin Hmar Inpuiin theihtawpa hmala tur leh hengte nena inzawma hmalakna report kimchang chu July, 2015 ral hma ngeia pelut a Hmar Inpui Executive Council neih hmasak berah ngaihtuah leh bawhzui ngei turin a rel a ni.

 

Hmar Inpui Constitution (review) 6th November, 2014 Special Assembly-in principle-a a lo pawm tawh chu Hmar Inpui 7th Assembly (May 15, 2015, Hmarkhawlian, Cachar, Assam) hian hman leh zawm nghal turin a nemngheh bakah Financial Rules & Policy chu Executive Committee buatsaih turin a rel a. Assembly leh hunah nemngheh ngei nise tiin a rel bawk a ni.

 

He Assembly hian Hmar Inpui Constitution-a awm angin 2017 Assembly (8th Assembly) thlenga Speaker turin Prof. Vanlalnghaka, Shillong, Meghalaya leh Secretary atan Pu Lalthaliana (Imphal, Manipur) te chu lungrual takin an thlang a. Deputy Speaker I (pakhat) chu Executive Committee ten an ruat atan an dah. Deputy Speaker II leh Deputy Secretary chu Barak Valley Region leh NC Hill & Karbi Anglong Region ten June, 2015 ralhma ngei a lo ruat tur leh Headquarter a thehlut turin an dah bawk.

 

Mizoram Hmar Welfare Association Vice Chairman Pu Lalmuanpuia Punte chuan 3-12 May, 2015 chhunga Union Home Minister, Jt. Secretary (NE), Ministry of Home Affairs leh Central a National Party hruaitute Mizoram, Manipur leh Assam a Hmar Political issue dang dang nena inzawm a hmuh thu he hunah hian report a pe a. Tv. Vanlalliana Pulamte pawhin Cachar, Hailakandi leh Karimganj District, Assam a Hmar hote Scheduled Tribe enlistment nen inzawma hma a lo lakte report pekna a nei bawk.

 

Assembly rorel hi tluang taka neih zawh a ni a, Assembly palai engemawzah kalkhawmte hi Hmarkhawlianah an la cham bang a (16th May, 2015) Hmar Youth Association, Hmarkhawliana Hmar Martyrs Day Programme urhsun/puithu taka buatsaih an tumnaah hian tel an tum nghal a ni.
