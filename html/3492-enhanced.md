A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 7.5.2014_

**ZWO Sent Letter to MHA****:**Zofa Welfare Organisation (ZWO) sent letter to Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) yesterday regarding the Mizoram and Assam border area problem; it requests Ministry of Home Affairs to take lawful action against Shamsher Singh, DC of Hailakandi District, Assam.

 ZWO stated that Hailakandi DC, Shamsher Singh was wrong in saying that Buhchangphai and Ramthar Zau are in Assam area; ZWO said that both villages are in Mizoram area, and also that the action of Assam people at Buhchangphai was violation of Article 14 of Indian Constitution and Section 3, sub-section (1), clauses (iv), (v), (viii), (ix), (xiv) &amp; (xv), and Section 3, sub-section (2), clause (iii) of SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989.
