SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/668

Dated: 10th September, 2019.

 

 

 

            In a Press Release issued yesterday, i.e. 9.9.2019 MZP Hqrs., Kolasib highlighted their intention to commence the construction of  ‘Chawlhbuk’, i.e. waiting shed at Phaisen, Kolasib District within the month of September, 2019. It may be recalled that MZP Kolasib Hqrs. Conference held on 27.9. 2018 at Bairabi already agreed to construct the said Chawlhbuk at Phaisen. Site for constructing the shed and date of commencing the work will be decided later by the Executive Meeting.

            Further information received indicated that Kolasib MZP aimed to construct the shed near 1st IR Bn. Check Gate at Phaisen which is regarded as non-disputed land in Assam-Mizoram boundary. Though Phaisen Village Council has not received any official information in this regard, they stated that they will not allow to undertake any activity within their jurisdiction which can further bring Zophai like situation in the future. They further stated that they will allow construction of the said Chawlhbuk if it does not encroach the boundary, otherwise they will regret.

            In the meantime, information received indicated that Phaisen VC is planning to construct shed for Auto-Rickshaw drivers near the transit point of auto-rickshaws plying between Phaisen and Dholcherra (Hailakandi District, Assam). Dholcherra side was already contacted by Phaisen VC in this regard and has no objection. MLA LAD fund amounting Rs. 1 Lakh was also received by Phaisen VC for the same.
