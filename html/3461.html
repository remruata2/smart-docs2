15.12.2022: On 15.12.2022, the Special Judge of Prevention of Corruption Act, Dr. HTC Lalrinchhana has promulgated quantum of sentence of Er. Liansanga of Khatla and Er. Lianchungnunga of Chaltlang who were booked under ACB Case No. 8 of 2012 Dt. 12.08.2014 u/s 120B/420/468/477A of IPC and u/s 13(2) r/w 13(1)(c) & (d) of P.C. Act, 1988. The Special Judge has convicted Er. Liansanga as under:

            (i)        Sentenced u/s 420 r/w 120B of IPC to undergo 5 (five) years imprisonment with a fine of Rs. 20 lakhs, in default, another imprisonment of 10 (ten) years.

            (ii)       Sentenced u/s 13 (2) r/w 13 (1) (c) & (d) of PC Act, 1988 r/w 120B of IPC to undergo 5 (five) years imprisonment with a fine of Rs. 20 lakhs, in default, another imprisonment of 10 (ten) years.

            Being an old aged retired Government servant, not likely to repeat similar type of offences, both the aforesaid sentences except fine shall run concurrently.

The Special Judge has also convicted Er. Lianchungnunga as under:

            (i)        Sentenced u/s 420 r/w 120B of IPC to undergo 4 (four) years imprisonment with a fine of Rs. 10 lakhs, in default, another imprisonment of 5 (five) years.

            (ii)       Sentenced u/s 13 (2) r/w 13 (1) (c) & (d) of PC Act, 1988 r/w 120B of IPC to undergo 4 (four) years imprisonment with a fine of Rs. 10 lakhs, in default, another imprisonment of 5 (five) years.

            Being an old aged retired Government servant, not likely to repeat similar type of offences, both the aforesaid sentences except fine shall run concurrently.
