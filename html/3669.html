A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 01.09.2022

Pay day for August salary not certain : Drawing and Disbursing officers (DDO) of different Departments under Mizoram Government have submitted pay bills of their respective department for the month of August 2022 to their concerned treasuries. However, according to information received by Vanglaini newspaper, Mizoram Finance Department could not mention the exact date when funds for August salaries of Government employees would be released. Normally, salaries of regular Government servants were often disbursed in the last working day of the month while  non regulars such as muster roll, work charge and provisional employees received their salaries at around 20th day of the month.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 01.09.2022

Areca nuts seized : Champhai police on 30th August 2022 (Tuesday) seized 76 bags (6,000 kgs.) of areca nuts  which were smuggled from Myanmar using  two vehicles. The drivers of the two vehicles, Lalramluaia,  Bulfek village and Lalramnghaka, Champhai Bethel veng were also apprehended along with the illegal items.

 

 

 

C. Front page of  The Aizawl Post newspaper  Dt. 01.09.2022

10 beds donated for ICU of Civil Hospital, Aizawl : 10 beds along with different accessories such as kentel ventilators, oxygen cylinder, ECG machine etc. which are worth Rs. 70 lakhs donated by HDFC Bank under their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) programme were inaugurated by Mizoram Health Minister, Dr. Lalthangliana on Yesterday (31.8.2022). Apart from these, HDFC Bank has, under this CSR programme, donated 5 motorised ICU bed, 5 multi para monitors, 5 suction machine, 5 BiPAP machines, 5 CAPA machines, 2 ventilators and 3 median transport ventilators for Lunglei Civil Hospital and Siaha District Hospital on 16th June 2022.
