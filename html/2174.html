NOTIFICATION
Dated Aizawl, the 5th September, 2020
WHEREAS, in pursuance of the Notification No.B.12011/1/2019-
SEC/VC/Vol – III issued by the State Election Commission, Mizoram on 2nd & 3rd
September, 2020 under sub-rule (1) of Rule 3 and Rule 37 of the Mizoram (Election to
Village Councils) Rules, 2014, General Election to Village Councils was held for the
purpose of constituting 8 (Eight) Village Councils within Aizawl District on 4th September,
2020;
AND WHEREAS, the results of the elections in respect of 8 Village Councils
in the said General Elections have been declared by the Returning Officers concerned;
and
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of sub-rule (3) of Rule 77 of the Mizoram
(Election to Village Councils) Rules, 2014, the State Election Commission, Mizoram
hereby notifies the names of the members elected in respect of the said Village Councils,
along with their party affiliations, in the SCHEDULE enclosed to this Notification.
Sd/- LAIMA CHOZAH
State Election Commissioner,
Mizoram.
