A. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 17.6.2013

Landslide swept down 5 houses of 2nd Bn. MAP Hqrs.: A landslide caused by rainfall swept down 5 houses of 2nd Bn. MAP Quarters Type-1 at Luangmual, Lunglei at around 3:00 PM yesterday. Due to this incident, the shin bone of ASI Lalhmingliana was broken and he also suffered head injury and was admitted to Lunglei Civil Hospital, their maid Lalawmpuii also suffered head injury but was sent home after giving first aid. Two persons also suffered minor injuries. The cleaning of the debris will be continued today by Luangmual community and MAP.

 

B. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 17.6.2013

Habitual offender/burglar arrested: Lalzamliana (30) s/o Ropianga, Bethlehem Veng, Aizawl who burgled a shop in Aizawl Foreign Lane on the night of 28.5.2013 was arrested by Lunglei PS Staff on 12.6.2013 at Mausen village between Lunglei and Haulawng. Lalzamliana burgled a shop which sells Mizo woven cloth and took different varieties of cloth worth ` 2 lakhs. A sum of ` 12,000/- and 29 clothes were recovered from him. Lalzamliana had escaped from Central Jail Aizawl and District Jail, Lunglei and has several cases.

 

C. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 17.6.2013

Burglar arrested: Lalnuntluanga (19) s/o Biaksailovi of Hmunzawl attempted to break into Mizoram Rural Bank, Serchhip on 13.6.2013 at around 1:40AM. He tried to break the door lock by using a crowbar, hearing the noise some people woke up to inspect, but fled before they caught him. Serchhip police searched for the culprit and he was arrested on 14th June, 2013 at 8:30AM under Serchhip Market.

            Lalnuntluanga confessed that on 11.6.2013, he burgled the house of Lalhmachhuana of Serchhip Hmar Veng and stole ` 5,000/-. He also confessed that he burgled the houses of R.Zoliana, C.Thanvawra and Lalfakmawia on 12.6.2013 at Chhiahtlang Chhim Veng.
