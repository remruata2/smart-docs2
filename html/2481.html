MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/638

Dated 13th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/636
Dt. 12.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

          Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit, Pu Shashank Jaiswal, SP, Mamit and
Pu Andy Lalfakzuala Varte, BDO, Zawlnuam conducted site verification for proposed BSF 93rd Battallion B’Coy Camp (presently stationed at Govt. Comprehensive Primary and Middle School, Thinghlun) today i.e., 13.11.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. and they earmarked three areas viz. Pi Khumpuii’s cultivated land around 500 meters towards Thinghlun from Mizoram Police Camp and Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David’s cultivated land near Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road Thinghlun for the stated purpose.

          Pu Dingluaia, DIG (Armed) visited Mizoram Police deployed at Zaite Road, Thinghlun today at 12:20 p.m. and after necessary briefing he left the area for Kanhmun.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          40 (forty) vehicles carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 13.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and there is free movement of vehicles across the border.

          Pu Dingluaia, DIG (Armed) visited Mizoram Police deployed at Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 13.11.2020 @ 1:30 p.m. and after necessary briefing he left the area at 2:40 p.m. today’s afternoon.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          61 (sixty one) vehicles (HMV-24, MMV-4, LMV-33) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 13.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          Day three of community search of missing person named  Lalsiamthara (2) s/o Zothanmawia of Vairengte Venglai, who went missing since 11.11.2020 @ 9:00 a.m. was conducted today 13.11.2020. Around 300 volunteers participated from Vairengte, Bilkhawthlir and North Chhimtluang YMA and Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte, MLA also participated in the community search. The missing boy is not yet found at the time of writing the report. Regarding missing of the said person OC, Vairengte intimated the matter to OC, Dholai requesting to conduct search in their jurisdiction.

          125 (one hundred twenty five) vehicles entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 12.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
