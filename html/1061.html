ASSESSMENT OF SECURITY THREAT PERCEPTION OF DISTRICT/SUBORDINATE COURTS IN MIZORAM

 

1. Gauhati  High Court, Aizawl Bench and District Court,   Aizawl:                    

                        After conducting threat perception assessment, it is concluded that no direct threat which could emanates from impending issues as against the Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench and District Court, Aizawl is perceived which could suggest or necessitate a revamp in respect of the deployment of static guard duty at these institutions respectively.

 

                        It is pertinent to mention that whenever the Court cause lists includes sensational cases which can invite ruckus or law and order issue with the Court premises, police personnel are deployed by the District Police administration from time to time in order to contain the situation.

 

                        At present, the Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench have 3(three) static guard post manned by CRPF comprising of two subordinate officers and 18(eithteen) personnel.

 

                        The District Court, Aizawl had 1(one) static guard post manned by 3rd MAP comprising of 2(two) NCOs and 4(four) personnel.

           

2. Lunglei District Court :

                        Presently, personnel from 2nd Bn. MAP are deployed for guarding the Court premises which are as under -

            Havildar - 1

            Naik       - 1

            Constable - 3

 

                        As no imminent  threat are perceived against the safety of the court at the moment, the present security deployment seems adequate.

 

3. Siaha District Court :

                        When the District court functions from their new building complex, located at the outskirt of Siahatla, there will be a requirement for manning the security posts in the Court complex.

 

4. Champhai District Court :

                        Apart from PSOs provided to the Judges, the Court complex is being guarded by one section of armed personnel form 2IR Bn. which is deemed sufficient for the moment.

 

5. Kolasib District Court :

                        As the threat perceived against the Kolasib District Court is minimal at the time being, reinforcing the security of the Court premises may be unnecessary.

6. Serchhip District Court :

                        The location of Serchhip District Court is somehow isolated with no staff quarters or other private buildings nearby. This may be one of the reason it has been burgled three time in the past. Because of the above incidents and for the safety of the Court, arrangement  of provision of security guard at the Court may be consider.

 

7. Lawngtlai District Court :

                        Due to its proximity with neighboring foreign countries such as Bangladesh and Myanmar, cases involving accused of foreign origin are often dealt-with in this Court. In order to boost the security and morale of the staff, the present security guards may be reinforce by a few more men.

 

8. Mamit District Court :

                        Apart from proper fencing around the Court premises, Mamit District Court is being provided with armed security from 4th IR Bn which consisted of 2 NCOs and 4 constables. These security measures are view to be adequate considering the present situation.
