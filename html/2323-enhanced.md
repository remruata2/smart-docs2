****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/546

Dated 13th July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/544 Dt. 12.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

Around 50 (fifty) volunteers from Chuhvel village constructed Mizoram Police makeshift camp at around 2 km west of Pu John Halliday’s Farm Hut today i.e., 13.7.2021. DC, Mamit; SP, Mamit and CO, 4th IR Battalion visited the area this afternoon. Pu Zoramthara, Addl. SP and SDPO, Kawrthah are presently stationed at the area and it is intended to deployed Mizoram Police at the area w.e.f., this evening.

Yesterday i.e., 12.7.2021 Mizoram started construction of road at around 2 km west of Pu John Halliday’s Farm Hut where Assam reportedly intended to construct road at the area. The road construction work could not resume today as excavator (JCB) engaged for the said purpose is out of order.

****

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-4, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 13.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

13 (thirteen) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-8, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

Information received stated that Assam made reinforcement force at Mayapur, Hailakandi District which is north of Zophai, Bairabi.

44 (forty four) vehicles (HMV-26, MMV-3, LMV-10, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Pi Chawngthanpari, President Vairengte Block MHIP lodged an FIR at Vairengte Police Station on 12.7.2021, wherein she stated that several lady workers were severely beaten and attacked by Assam Police at Pu Chalchhunga’s paddy field on 10.7.2021 and as a result of which, 5(five) ladies sustained severe injuries on their persons. In this connection, Vairengte PS C/No. 17/21 Dt. 12.7.2021 U/S 325/354 IPC has been registered.

112 (one hundred twelve) vehicles (HMV-52, MMV-21, LMV-18, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-12, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-10, LMV-15) entered Saiphai from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

**Note:**

Reliable information received stated that Assam government has stationed a larger contingent of Assam Armed Police at various schools in Gharmurra area, Zambira area and Katlicherra area in Hailakandi District. It also made reinforcement of force at Gutguti BOP, near Saikhawthlir village.

Assam also deployed more Assam Police by setting up Temporary camp at Balicherra, Hailakandi District which is located north of Phaisen, Kolasib District. It also deployed more Assam Police at Bhilaipur, which is located near Phaisen village and reportedly intended to construct temporary camp at around 4 km towards Mizoram from Dolchhorra Police Outpost w.e.f., 16.7.2021.
Assam also made reinforcement at Mayapur, Hailakandi District which is located north of Zophai, Bairabi.

 Situation is under close watch.
