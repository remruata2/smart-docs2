Investigation Report

            On 7.6.07 @ 4:500 AM, Sub. Madan Singh Biast, Post Commander, 8th AR with his party had patrolling duty in between Vaikhawtlang and IB area in Champhai District. The patrolling party saw one person wearing civilian cloth coming from the opposite direction carrying a bundle of firewood and on seeing them had turned back and started fleeing. The party chased him but managed to escape leaving the firewood bundle in a bush. On checking the same arms and ammunitions mentioned above suspected to be smuggled by or for some underground outfits were recovered and hence the case.

 

            Since all efforts for detection of the case bore no fruitful results and there has been no hope of such, Final Report as mentioned above was submitted.
