9.9.2022 :      Science Teachers' Association of Mizoram (STAM) held its 35th General Conference on 9.9.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Synod Conference Hall. Pu Zoramthanga, Hon'ble Chief Minister graced the occasion as Chief Guest. During the Conference, they passed the following resolutions:

            (i)        Necessary arrangement should be made for proper conduct of Science                    Practical in each school under Mizoram Board of School Education.

            (ii)       The Education System being in used in Mizoram should be Conceptual
                        Understanding.

            (iii)     Necessary action should be taken for elevation of Directorate of Science                & Technology, Govt. of Mizoram into a full fledge Department.
