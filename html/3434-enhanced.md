**9.11.22 :** Information received stated that on the evening of 7.11.22 @ around 6:00 p.m., Mrachathai Mog H/o Mizinthui (Sangtei) of Hmawngbuchhuah village was reportedly abducted by 3 (three) unknown mask men with arms (reportedly carrying AK 47) at Hmangbuchhuah village, Lawngtlai District. Upon receiving information of the incident, Bungtlang South Police immediately rushed to the spot and the investigation revealed that one person named Mrachathai from Mog community was forcibly taken away to unknown destination from Hmawngbuchhuah village, Lawngtlai district by some unknown 3 (three) mask men, who were reportedly carrying AK 47 Rifles on 7.11.22 @ around 6:00 p.m. while he and his newly wedded wife were on their way home. At the time of the incident, the alleged abductors shot fire on the ground to scare the victim's wife. It may be mentioned that Mrachathai (victim) recently got married with Mizinthui (20) of Hmawngbuchhuah on 6.11.22. In this connection, Lawngtlai PS Case No.72/22 Dt.08.11.22 U/S 364 A IPC r/w 27 Arms act 1959 has been registered and investigation is underway.
