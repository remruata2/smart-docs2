A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.08. 2022_

****Rs.20 Lakhs in the account of Mizoram :******During the period between 21st July - 2nd August, 2022, the total amount of balance in the account of Mizoram state Government under Reserve Bank of India was only Rs.20,00,000. According to information obtained through RTI Act, the balance of Mizoram State on 3rd August increased to Rs. 1,05,71,47,997.86 as a result of Post Devolution Deficit Grant received from the Central Government. From 3rd August, the balance slowly increased and reached its peak on 10th August which was Rs.7,13,38,64,951.64. However, the amount started declining again and by 19th August 2022 it had dropped to Rs.42,37,09,497.61. Meanwhile, Government of Mizoram is intending to disburse Rs. 25,000 each to 60,000 different household under its Flagship Programme -Socio Economic Development Programme (SEDP) which will require Rs. 1,50,00,00,000 which is higher than the balance in the account of Mizoram state on 19th August 2022 by Rs. 1,07,62,90,502.39.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 26.08.2022_

**wildlife smuggler arrested**: Vairengte police on 24.8.2022 (Wednesday) arrested John Lalramnghaka (39) s/o Challianbuanga and his accomplice, Lalremruata (20) s/o Lalawmpuia, both of whom are residents of Vairengte town, who were accused of trying to smuggled wildlife. From the possession of the accused, police seized 2 (Two) Capybara and Grey Monkey and were booked under appropriate sections of law.
