**Brief History:**

**Ka particulars chu a chunga ka sawi ang kha a ni. Kum 2007 khan Kuki Nation Front(Military Council) hruaitu ten keini Thado hnam te Manipur a awm chhungkhat a mipa pathum aia tam awm te chuan UG's an zawm tur a ni an tih avangin keipawh in April, 2007 khan Good Friday ni takin KNF(MC) hi ka zawm vet ta a ni. KNF(MC) hi Germana@Hemlal President hruaina hnuaiah mi za chuang kan ni a, Germana@ Hemlal hi Tuiveizang khua a ni a KNF(MC) hi KNO hnuaiah an awm a ni. KNF(MC) ah hian kum hnih vel ka tang a, kum 2009 September thla khan ka bang leh a, ka ban hnu hian kan inah ka haw leh a. Ka U(Pau Johna) nen Rubber Plantation kan siam a, December 2009 velah James Seilienthuom(L) former SS Army Chief chuan kan veng mi bazar kalte hnenah thu rawn chah in, KNF(MC) atanga ka banna chhan a hnena insawifiah turin min ti a. December 15, 2009 chuan James a hnenah chuan ka va kal ta a, karlehah Media Conference neih zawha kan in lama haw leh turin min hrilh a. Sirongphu bulah Media Conference an nei a, Local News Manipur ami pakhat leh Assam ami pathum an lo tel a ni. Media Conference an neih chhan hi UTLA pawl din a ni tih mipuiten an hriat theihna tur a ni. An hnena ka awm chhung hian, KNF(MC) a ka lo awm tawh avangin silai kengin khuaah ka duty ve thin a ni. Tamenglong leh Churachanpur Dist. inkar khaw hrang hrangah kan duty thin a ni.**

**February 2010 ah keima thuin in lamah ka haw ta a ni. Ina ka haw hnu deuh hian James Seilienthuom(L) SS Army Chief UTLA chu NSCN(K) ten inbe turin Lungkaiphunah an ko a, thawhho dan tur sawipui turin an thlem chhuak a. James leh a naute mi pariat an kal a, mahse inbiakna tha an nei theilo aniang, NSCN(K) te chuan James-a leh a naute mi pathum chu an kaphlum a, midang pang ate chu an tlanchhuak ta a ni. Hetia James-a, Army Chief ber a lo thih takah chuan, a aiah Kai Robert chu Army Chief angina tun thlengin a la awm ta a ni.**

****April 2010 thla laihawl vel khan khua a awm reng chu him lova kan hriat avangin ka thiante pahnih 1) Haupu leh 2) Putu te UTLA atanga bang ve hote nen chuan Mizoram lamah sahimna zawngin kan lo kal a. Silchar atangin MST Bus in Kolasib thleng kan lo kal a. Kei chu "HOTEL ELDORADO" ah ka riak a. Ka thiante chu Autorickshaw in an hmelhriatte in lam an pan niin ka hria. An kalna tur pawh ka hrelo. A tuk zingah Maxi Cab in Aizawl ka rawn chho leh ta a. Chanmari a Pu Lala(Custom Office hnuai) hnenah ni thum vel ka awm a. A hnuah ka U Hatneihthiem(Ma-i) te in, Bawngkawn Hmar vengah ka insawn phei a, dt. 26.4.10 khan Assam Rifles ten min man a, dt. 30.4.10 zan khan Bawngkawn P/S ah min dah phei ta a ni.****

**Q. Dt. 13.11.09 a Chekarcham, Assam- 15th IR Bn. Camp beitu te kha UTLA an ni em? Tu hovin nge an kal? Eng thil te nge an lak?**

**= Kha thil thleng kha UTLA tih ngei a ni a. James Seilienthuom(L) hovin mi sawmpahnih(12) vel an kal niin ka hria. Ka hriat dan chuan IR Bn. mi pakhat an kaphlum a, silai LMG-2 nos., SLR-11 nos. leh Carbine-2 nos. (Total-15) an la niin ka hria. Mahse, he thil thlen lai hian kei chuan UTLA hi ka la zawm lo a ni.**

**Q. Manipur ah hian UG dangte, a bik takin HPC(D) ten en hian eng angin nge in inzawm dan leh thawhho dan?**

**= He pawl hi pawl naupang te, ding tir a ni a. Tunah thawhpui UB dang a la nei lova. HPC(D) nen phei chuan area pawh kan inkalpawh thei lova, ka hriat dan chuan ZRA Thanglianpauva kaihhruai nen chuan inhrethiam takin an awm a. Kum kalta khan ZRA hnuaiah hian mi pakua(UTLA) vel chuan training an nei a ni tih chu ka hria.**

**Q. Silai leh a mu te khawi atangin nge in lak a, pawisa engtinnge in neih?**

**= Silai leh a mu chu a hmaa ka sawi 15th IR Bn., Assam a camp atanga an lak kha ka hriat chu a ni mai a. An lo neihsa te hi khawi atangin nge an neih a, engtinnge an neih ka hre ve lo a ni. Tin, tunah hian pawl anga pawisa khawn emaw ngaihtuah engmah a awm ka hre lova. A tul hunah chuan, James-a hian mi hrang hrang hnenah a dil kual mai thin niin ka hria.**

**Q. NSCN(K) te khan James-a te an kahhlum tum khan an silai an chhuhsak em?**

**= James-a te an kahhlum tum khan, AK-1 no., SLR-2nos., 9mm Pistol-1 no. leh Walkie Talkie Set pakhat an laksak niin ka hria.**

**Q. UTLA hi engvanga din nge a nih a, enge a demand?**

**= Keini tribal hnam te hi India sawrkar hian min ngaihsaklo em em a, kan chanvo ve tur ang pawh kan phak lova, hei vang hian mipuite pawh kan retheih phah em em niin kan hotuten an sawi a. India sawrkar in min hriat theihna tur leh mipui te chanvo haichhuahsak tura hma la turin he pawl UTLA hi din a nih thu Pu James-a chuan a sawi.**

**Q. UTLA sipai te hi khawi hmunah te nge an duty a, Manipur pawn lamah Operational area in nei em?**

**= UTLA sipai te hi Suongphu leh Nungthut inkar Lawngkaiphun, Leikot, Daiveijang, Sehjang, Tuiphai Chalzang, Luongpi, Buolphai, New Sehjang khua (Tamenglong leh Churachanpur District inkar) te hi Manipur ah chuan kan awmna(duty na) a ni a. Tin, NC Hills ah Suongpizang, Leikul, Hlasang khuaah te hian a changing kan duty thin.**
