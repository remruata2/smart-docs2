****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/006

Dated: 6th January, 2020.

Information has been received that Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) and North East Students Organization (NESO) are planning to go ahead with their intention to receive Pu C. Lalrosanga, MP(LS) at Lengpui Airport with ‘Mizo Pawndum’ tomorrow, i.e. 7.1.2020 in protest against his casting of vote in favour of CAB-2019 in the Lok Sabha. The MP is scheduled to arrive Lengpui Airport tomorrow @ 2:05 p.m by Air India.
Information received indicated that MZP Gen. Hqrs. did not hold any formal meeting to chalk out their programme. They are planning to mobilise their members through social media like, Facebook and WhatApp groups and therefore, the numbers for participants could not be ascertained. It is also learnt that Zoram Revolutionary Movement (ZRM) will give their support to the MZP.

As mentioned earlier in our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2019/911 dt. 12.12.2019, the Press Release issued by MZP on 12.12.2019 highlighted their intention to receive the MP(LS) at Lengpui Airport with ‘Mizo Pawndum’ as and when he return from New Delhi, while on contrary, they hailed Pu Ronald Sapa Tlau, MP(RS) with ‘Tawlloh Puan’ for opposing the Bill when he returned to Mizoram on 20.12.2019.

Information received indicated that members from MNF party are planning to receive the MP while MPCC party do not have any intention to receive or organise a programme in this regard. Meanwhile, ZPM party also do not have any plan to receive the MP, but they will convene a meeting of General Secretaries tonight, i.e. 6.1.2020 @ 7:00 p.m at the residence of Pu Lalthlamuana, Gen. Secretary i/c Establishment, ZPM at Dinthar, Aizawl.

It is learnt that Mizo Students' Union (MSU) is not having any intention to receive the MP nor organise any kind of agitation.

Situation is under close watch.
