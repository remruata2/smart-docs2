E. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.5.2014

Reports of Police and I&PR Not Matched: Reports of I&PR regarding the number of windows and glasses broken at I&PR Directorate by the exploded bomb on 31st January 2014 at Treasury Square Market were not on par with the police report, the difference between the two reports is 413.

          Answering RTI ACT question, I&PR department said that the number of windows and glasses broken by the bomb in I&PR Directorate building was 947 while the police recorded only 534. So, the number of windows that I&PR Dept. mended was more in numbers as compared to the Police report.

 

 

F. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 5.5.2014

Mizoram Government action baffling - Mizoram Government works with HPC(D) faction? : Leaders of Hmar Inpui said that they were baffled by Mizoram Government's treatment of Hmar people at Lungthulien, Churachandpur District, Manipur and that kind of behaviour was instigated by Mizoram Government's connection with the other side of HPC(D) faction.

          Hmar Inpui leaders said that they were astonished and condemned by the Mizoram Police operation and felt that it was a way to threaten Hmar people. They said, "Even if Mizoram Police wants to carry out operation, it should not target Hmar people. It seemed that the operation was intended to create animosity between Hmar people and we condemned it".
