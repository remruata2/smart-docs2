A. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 20.5.2014_

****Man Stabbed His Own Brother****:**Zosangliana (22) s/o C.Ngaihzuala (L) of Sangau-II Vengthar, Lawngtlai District stabbed his own brother Lalneihchhunga (20) last Sunday night at around 7:30pm. Lalneihchhunga was brought to Sangau Primary Health Centre and succumbed to his injury. After Post-Mortem was done, funeral was held yesterday evening. Report said that it was accidental as Zosangzuala scolded his little brother, they quarreled and the untoward incident happened. Sangau Police arrested Zosangzuala and registered a case against him.**
