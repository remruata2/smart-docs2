SHANTI BAHINI

(ParbatyaChattagram Jana SamhatiSamiti(PCJSS))

 

          ParbatyaChattagram Jana SamhatiSamiti(PCJSS) which means United People’s Party of the Chittagong Hill Tract is a political party formed to represent the people and indigenous tribes of the Chittagong Hill Tracts in Bangladesh. It has an Armed Group called Shanti Bahini(Peace Corps). It is considered as insurgent group in Bangladesh. The Shanti Bahini is made out of mostly members from the Chakma tribe. The Chittagong Hill Tracts Peace Accord was signed between Bangladesh Government and PCJSS on 2nd December 1997. The major objectives of the peace accord were:

 

a) Protection of land rights of the hill people.

b) Stimulation of their cultural uniqueness.

c) Rehabilitation of internally displaced people and refugees who had left their country.

d) Withdrawal of the military from the CHT(Chittagong Hill Tracts) with exception of permanent military establishments.

e) Self Government through regional and district councils.

 

          The PCJSS accused Bangladesh Government that the peace accord agreements are yet to be implemented despite more than 20 years had lapsed since the signing of the Peace Accord. Hence, their armed wing is still in operation and some of their armed cadres are in operation from Mizoram (Tlabung Sub-Division).

Shanti Bahini:

Shanti Bahini is one of the insurgent groups in Bangladesh and signed Peace Accord with the Government on 2.12.1997. A number of cadres who could not accept the Accord rebel again and raises United People Democratic Front (UPDF) and ParbatyaChattagram Jana SamhatiSamiti(PCJSS) in 1988 and demanded the establishment of separate Autonomous Administration for tribal peoples in Chittagong Hill Tracts area.

 

Besides UPDF and PCJSS, one organization known as Songskar Group under RupayanDiwanChakma, President and TalindraLalChakma, Gen. Secretary was formed in 2008 having about 100 cadres. Bangladesh Army patronized this group and frequentlyfights against UPDF and PCJSS cadres.
