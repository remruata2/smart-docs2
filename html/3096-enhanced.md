B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 1.8.2013_

****Zuangtui Road Blockade, 6 injured**: Zuangtui community blocked Zuangtui road yesterday ie, 31.7.2013 to show their displeasure at the Police for not arresting any suspect on the death of Lalnunmawia (22) s/o Sawichhunga of Zuangtui on 5.7.2013 at Falkland. As the government did not say anything or send anyone to talk to them, some displeased lads tried to go to Falkland, the police stopped the lads of Zuangtui at Thuampui, there was a scuffle between the Police and lads of Zuangtui for a certain period and 6 (six) persons of Zuangtui sustained injury.**

 Some lads of Zuangtui entered 132 KV Station and turned off the power at 6:10 PM, after a talk was had with government authority, the power was turned on again at 6:40 PM. Zuangtui community intends to block the road again today ie, 1.8.2013.
