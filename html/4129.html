A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.23.04.2024

Leaders of MTOA met Railways General Manager: Yesterday, i.e 22.4.2024 leaders of Mizoram Truck Owners Association (MTOA) met General Manager of Northeast Frontier Railways at Guwahati, Assam. During the meeting, senior officials of NE Frontier Railways told leaders of MTOA their expectation of the entry of trains at Sairang, Mizoram before March 2025, which will result in huge amount of transportation works for the trucks in Mizoram as all the goods will be transported up to Sairang by the railways. The leaders of MTOA also told the Railways officials the problems faced by them at Bairabi train station, which the railways officials promised to looked into the matter.

Cleanliness drive : Inaugural function of cleanliness drive by Mission Veng Local Council was held at Sikulpuikawn, Mission Veng, yesterday. The cleanliness drive was inaugurated by Pu R.Lalnuntluanga during the function which was presided over by Pu Lalhminghlua, LC Vice Chairman. At the function, Pu Lalchhuanthanga, MLA and Pu Ramengmawia, LC Chairman delivered speeches at the finction. The cleanliness drive will be observed at church services, various schools located within Mission Veng and through voluntary community services.

D. Front page  of  VANGLAIN newspaper  Dt.23.04.2024

Draft electoral roll released : Siaha district Election Officer, Pu VL Hruaizela Khiangte, who is also the Deputy Commissioner of Siaha district yesterday released draft electoral roll, which will be used for Lopu-II village council by-election. As per the draft electoral roll, there are 135 voters-71 males and 64 females voters in Lopu-II Village Council areas. The final electoral roll is expected to be released on 14th may 2024.
