A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.11.2022_

****Case registered against former Tourism minister and Director**: Mizoram Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) have registered a case against Pu John Rotluangliana, former Tourism Minister during the previous Congress government and former Tourism department director, Pu Biakthanmawia Pautu. These two persons were accused of misappropriating and diverting funds ( Rs. 67,49,000) intended for the construction of Tourist Lodge at Khawzawl. On 24th May 2019, Mizoram Lokayukta received a complaint in connection with the incompletion of the construction of Khawzawl Tourist Lodge and in turn directed Mizoram ACB to probe into the matter. Anti Corruption Bureau submitted their preliminary investigation report to Lokayukta on 5th February,2020 and were again instructed to continued the investigation on 27th February 2020. In the light of these investigations, ACB had registered a case against the two accused persons and as per instruction received from lokayukta on 13th May 2022, a full investigation is presently under way.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.11.2022_

**Meeting of MPF with political parties:******Mizoram Peoples Forum (MPF) held a meeting with leaders of various political parties of Mizoram at Central YMA Committee Room, Aizawl yesterday (29.11.2022) which was presided over by MPF President, Rev .Dr.Vanlalnghhaka Ralte. In this meeting, Upa Lalramthanga, former MPF Secretary highlighted the previous agreements made between MPF and political parties of Mizoram while representatives of the political parties also contributed points for new agreements between MPF and the political parties. The meeting decided that each political party will take the proposed new agreements to their respective parties after which a meeting will be held again to finalise the agreements.
