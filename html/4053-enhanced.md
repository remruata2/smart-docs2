1. **Sangau PS Case No.3/24 dt.05.02.2024 u/s 4 of Explosives Substances Act r/w 34 IPC and 14 Foreigners Act****:**

**(a) Gist of the case**: On dt.05.02.2024, 42nd Assam Rifles, Sangau post intercepted one Bolero B/R No. MZ01 B 6369 driven by Siamthanga (50) S/o Chhanerha, Sentetfiang village, Lawngtlai District along with his wife Chimengi (40) near Pangkhua village, Lawngtlai District from where they seized 6 Boxes (1200 nos.) of gelatine sticks, 1800 nos. – detonators and 3750 mtrs detonating fuse which were handed over to Sangau PS. 

These items were transported from Sentetfiang village at the instance of one Suichung @ Pasuia, Salen village, Mynamar. It was found that the explosives items were purchased by Suichung @ Pasuia and TunTun(32) S/o Chhanawl, both belonging to Salen village, Myanmar from Chawnpui Veng, Aizawl.

 It was further revealed that the contraband items were purchased from Pi Lalsangpuii w/o Lawmzuala of Chawnpui, Aizawl and the couple were later arrested.

During investigation, the following persons involved in the case were arrested and remanded in Judicial custody.

1. Siamthanga(50) h/o Amengi, Sentetfiang, Lawngtlai District
2. Amengi(40) w/o Siamthanga, Sentetfiang, Lawngtlai District
3. Suichung(50) @ Pasuia s/o Hrangthanga, Salen village, Myanmar
4. Tun Tun(32) s/o Chhanawl, Salen village, Myanmar
5. Bawihleiuka(28) s/o Siamthanga, Sentetfiang, Lawngtlai District
6. Lalsangpuii(60) w/o Lawmzuala of Chawnpui, Aizawl
7. Lawmzuala (58) S/o Lalzama of Chawnpui, Aizawl

 Chargesheet was submitted in this case vide Sangau PS CS No.4/24 Dt.25.03.2024.

**(b)** **Suspected origin and destination** : Upon enquiring into the matter, it has come to light that the explosives were bought from M/S Trustwell, Thakthing Veng, Aizawl, which is the sole explosives dealer in Mizoram which is licensed to possess as well as sale of explosives by Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisations (PESO) and the explosives are destined for Myanmar.

1. **Action taken by Mizoram Police to curb the menace of smuggling of explosives and detonator in Mizoram****:**

The following are the follow up action taken by Mizoram Police to curb the menace of smuggling of explosives and detonator in Mizoram:

(1) Field I/C, CID (SB) conducted detail enquiry into the seizures of explosives in Mizoram and conducted joint interrogation of the arrested accused persons to determine the forward/backward linkages and identification of origin and destination.

(2) On 30th May 2024, a meeting was conducted for all Arms Dealers and Explosive Magazines License Holders of Aizawl District at DGP Conference Hall, PHQ, Aizawl under the Chairmanship of Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IGP (L &amp; O) which was attended by senior Police Officers of Mizoram – DGP (M), DIGP (NR) and DIGP (ER) and 35 members of Arms and Explosive License holders.

 The meeting highlighted the responsibilities of the dealers/license owners and the consequences they could face if found to be involved in illegal trade. It also highlighted the necessity for collaborative efforts between Arms dealers, Explosive magazine license holders and Police in curbing the illegal possession and pilferage of arms and explosives which the dealers expressed their willingness to cooperate with Mizoram Police.

(3) Careful analysis of CDR’s of accused/persons involved in the illegal trade of explosives/detonators is being conducted by CID (SB) which contributes a major role in curbing and identification of forward/backward linkage.
