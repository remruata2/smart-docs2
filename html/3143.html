SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/793

Dated: 29th Oct., 2021

 

          Information received stated that there are more than 600 (six) Bru Voters (who are presently residing in Tripura Bru Transit camps) having votes at Zodin (Rastali) and Hortoki Polling Stations and out of which, around 150 (one hundred fifty) Brus voters intended to exercise their franchise at Tuirial Constituency Bye-election, which is to be held tomorrow i.e., 30.10.2021. The stated Bru voters intended to hire 15 (fifteen) vehicles for their transportation and as per input, they have contributed Rs. 1200/- each.  

 

          Information received also stated that the above mentioned Bru voters intended to leave their camps for their respective Polling Stations tomorrow early morning i.e., 30.10.2021 @3:00 a.m. Around 10 (ten) Bru voters are from Hortoki Polling Station and the rest are from Zodin (Rastali). In this connection, in pursuance of the decision of Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA), the following Groups/Sub-Headquarters YMA intended to perform duty w.e.f., this evening i.e., 29.10.2021, so as to verify whether the Bru voters are coming as per their own will and expenses:

1.       Taitesena Group YMA                        -  Zamuang Junction

2.       Langkaih Group YMA                        -  Damcherra Bridge

3.       Kolasib Sub-Headquarters YMA         - Tlawng Bridge, Bairabi

4.       Zampuimanga Group YMA OB          -  Tlawng Bridge, Bairabi

 

          Further, leaders of YMA, who intended to perform duty at the above mentioned location stated that they will not cause any difficulties, if the Brus voters are coming to exercise their franchise as per their own will and at their own expenses. Leaders of Brus also claimed that no arrangement was made for the above mentioned Bru voters by any political parties.
