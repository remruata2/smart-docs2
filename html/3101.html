A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 8.8.2013

‘Meth’ in Aizawl: An abusive substance ‘Meth’ (Metamphetamine) which is made out of Pseudoephedrine has been used and seized in Aizawl, said Mizoram Police. Mizoram police had seized more than 2,000 tablets of Meth which were meant to be sold in Mizoram. Special Narcotics Aizawl PS  said that there are 25 – 30 users of Meth in Aizawl, a tablet of Meth is sold at ` 150 – 200, it is addictive that its effect on the body is hazardous; Meth is dangerous and may cause immediate death.

            Mizoram Police proved that the substance they seized is truly Metamphetamine through the test machine. It has been sent to Forensic Science Laboratory to make sure that it is Meth and expected the result to be positive.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 8.8.2013

No foreigner left Mualkhang: No foreigner has left the area of Mualkhang NEC and its surroundings even after the deadline set by Mualkhang JAC was over: an FIR was lodged at Sairang PS on 7th August regarding this stating how long they can wait for the police to take action. Sairang Police did not arrest any foreigner till yesterday and it seems that foreigners have no intention to leave.

            Aizawl SP and Sairang PS OC had discussed the issue yesterday, and as requested by the Police, Mualkhang JAC agreed to wait for the police to take action, sources said.
