A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.12.2022_

****Mizoram to avail Rs.150 crores through OMB**: Mizoram government is again intending to borrow Rs. 150 crores as loans through Open Market Borrowing (OMB) and necessary arrangements have been undertaken by Reserve Bank of India. The interest on these loans, which are borrowed through OMB have to be repaid twice annually during a period of around 10 years at the rate of 7-8 %. So far, during the present financial year, Mizoram government had borrowed loans through OMB six times, the total amount of which reached to Rs.740 crores in all. The amount of loans permitted by RBI to be borrowed through OMB by Mizoram state during the financial year 2022-23 is up to Rs.990 crores while Rs. 150 crores is the amount already borrowed during these period.**

****Rs. 8.21 lakhs received by Traffic Police:******During the NO Tolerance Drive/Checking conducted by Aizawl Traffic police and Transport department during the period between 12th -17th December 2022, 229 defaulters were fined based on 716 types of different laws concerning vehicles and the total amount received from all these fines reaches Rs. 8.21 lakhs.**

****Police recovered methamphetamine:** Champhai police on 17.12.2022 recovered 20 kgs. of suspected crystal methamphetamine from one Tata 712 truck which was proceeding towards Aizawl from Zokhawthar. According to information received from the police, the contrabands were packed inside Chinese tea bags and were transported along with other items. The driver of the vehicle and its owner, Sanghleithanga (39) s/o Laldingliana, Zemabawk, Aizawl was apprehended at the spot. Based on further investigations, Lalthlamuana (56) s/o Kapcheuva(L) and Vanlalngura (32) s/o Hminglaia, both of whom are from Edenthar Veng, Aizawl were also arrested by personnel of Aizawl District Special Branch yesterday in this connection and will be taken to Champhai.**
