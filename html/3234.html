COMMENTS ON THE DRAFT STANDING ORDER NO.1/2022 REGARDING PROPER AND TIMELY INVESTIGATION OF CASES -

 

1.         'Directorate of Forensic Science, Guwahati'  may be replaced by Directorate of  Forensic Science Laboratory, Government of Mizoram in Point (v) under SCENE MAHAZAR/SPOT PANCHANAMA  (Page -7)

 

2.         Last two points under MAINTAINING CHAIN OF CUSTODY may also be deleted since they  are considered to be inappropriate with the sub-heading (i.e., custody errors). (Page-8)

 

3.         Point (i) sub No. (e) under  MAINTAINING THE CHAIN OF CUSTODY, 'Identity of the person who collected the evidence' may be deleted as it is similar to sub No. (g). (Page-8)

 

3.         "Faithfully" of  point No. (iv) under MAINTAINING THE CHAIN OF CUSTODY may be replaced with 'carefully'. (Page -9)

 

4.         'Is' of point No.(iv) under MAINTAINING THE CHAIN OF CUSTODY may be change as "should be". (Page -9)

 

5.         'Complete whereabouts' in point (vii) under  CORRECT RECORDING OF STATEMENTS AND MANAGEMENT OF WITNESSES  may be changed as 'complete address' (Page-9)

 

6.         'Lady Officer' of point No.(ix) under IMPORTANCE OF WITNESSES may be replaced as 'Lady Police Officer.' (Page -12)

 

7.         In point No. 3 of page-14, "Statements of injuries" under PROPER DRAFTING OF CHARGES SHEET AND ATTACHING ALL RELEVANT DOCUMENTS may be replaced as statements of victim/injured. (Page-14)

 

8.         "Which the accused are persons who convicts ?" of point No.7 under PROPER DRAFTING OF CHARGES SHEET AND ATTACHING ALL RELEVANT DOCUMENTS should be change as 'when the accused persons are convicted?'
