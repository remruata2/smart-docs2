A. _Front page of VANGLAINI/ The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 10.08.2022_

**Assam- Mizoram meeting on border issues:** Yesterday i.e 9.8.2022, a meeting on issues relating to the Assam-Mizoram interstate boundary dispute was held by representatives of Assam and Mizoram at Aijal Club , Aizawl. Delegates from Mizoram were led by Home Minister, Pu Lalchamliana while the Assam side were headed by Pu Atul Bora, Border Development &amp; Protection Minister. The meeting issued a joint statement which are as under -

i)  Both the states reaffirm the joint statement of 5th August 2021.

ii)  To minimise tension in and around the border areas, concerned district D.C of  both the states shall meet at least once in every two months.

iii)  Cultivators along the border of the two states shall be allowed to practice  farming.

iv)  Next round of meeting will be held in Guwahati during the month of October,  2022
