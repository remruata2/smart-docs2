A. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 19.09.2022_

****Mizoram-Assam Chief Minister level meeting**: Chief Minister of Mizoram, Pu Zoramthanga and his Assam state counterpart, Pu Himanta Biswa Sarma will have a meeting today (19.9.2022) at New Delhi to discuss about the Mizoram- Assam border dispute issue. According to information received, both the Chief Ministers have contacted each other previously through telephone as a result of the meeting between Pu Atul Bora, Assam Border Area Development Minister and Pu Lalchamliana , Mizoram Home Minister on 9th August 2022. This will be the second Chief Ministers level meeting on the issue as both the state Chief Ministers had met for the first time at New Delhi during November of Last year (2021).**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.09.2022_

****DGP visit:** Pu Divesh Chandra Srivastva, Director General of Police (DGP), Mizoram on 17th September , 2022 visited Serchhip Police Station and inaugurated a newly constructed Waiting-shed and Guest House at Serchhip Police Station. Speaking on this occasion, the DGP stressed the importance of tackling the rising menace of burglaries and to conduct regular patrolling.**

 On the same day, the DGP also graced the 'invited lecture on fostering youth success in the 21th century ' organised at Serchhip college by Guidance &amp; Counselling Cell and NCC (Army &amp; Air Wing). Delivering a speech in this function, Pu Divesh Chandra Srivastva encouraged the students to be hardworking, self restrain, determination and obedience to reach the peak of their limits. He also advised the students to utilised the benefits of social media instead of being a slave to it and appreciated the Guard of Honour presented to him by the NCC cadets.
