****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/621

Dated 12th August, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/617 Dt. 11.08.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

Today i.e., 12.8.2021 @ 7:50 a.m. Pu R. Lalzirliana, Power &amp; Electricity Minister arrived Chuhvel, Mamit District and held a brief meeting with leaders of Chuhvel village at Community Hall Chuhvel. The Minister handed over Rs. 30,000 to Mizoram Police deployed at Chuhvel border and Rs. 5,000/- to Chuhvel VC. He was accompanied by Pu Lalrintluanga Sailo, Dy. Govt. Chief Whip; Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte, MLA and concern officials. They left for Kolasib this morning @ 9:20 a.m. 

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :** 

 16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1, LMV-12, LPG-NIL, Maxi Cab-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

40 (forty) vehicles (HMV-NIL, MMV-14, LMV-23, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-3, Oil Tanker-Nil, Bus-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura through Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

40 (forty) vehicles (HMV-19, MMV-2, LMV-16, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

CRPF 140 B-Coy deployed at Vairengte Auto Stand (Ngei ngei tlang) and Saihapui ‘V’ was closed on 10.8.2021 and Mizoram police has been deployed at their vacated locations. Information received stated that their replacement CRPF 225 C-Coy is en route to Vairengte from Chawngte. 

194 (one hundred ninety-four) vehicles (HMV-70, MMV-56, LMV-19, LPG-5, Maxi Cab-2, Oil Tanker-42, Bus-NIL) entered Vairengte from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
