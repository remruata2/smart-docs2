SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/899

Dated: 9th December, 2019.

 

                    In pursuance of MZP Gen. Hqrs. Emergency Executive Meeting held on 7.12.2019 wherein they have decided to organise total bandh on 10.12.2019 in protest against CAB-2019 which was tabled in the Parliament today, MZP intended to organise Total Bandh tomorrow. In this regard, MZP Gen. Hqrs. has chalked out Duty points as noted below for its proposed Total Bandh to be observed tomorrow, i.e. 10.12.2019  –

  1. Kulikawn                                                           2. Sikulpuikawn

3. Treasury Square                                                  4. Khatla kawn

5. Tennis Court                                                       6. Temple Square

7. Vaivakawn                                                           8. Company peng

9. Bawngkawn                                                       10. Zemabawk

11. Chaltlang Dawrkawn                          12. Industry Peng

13. Chanmari kawn                                               14. Bazar Bungkawn

    15. Zarkawt                                                            16. College Veng.

 

                        In a Press Release issued today, i.e. 9.12.2019, MZP maintains that exclusion of 3 (three) States of the Northeast having the Sixth Schedule and ILP operational from CAB is not enough and that they would strive to safeguard land, nation and religion. Appreciating the support from Churches and NGOs, MZP appeal to the people to support the total bandh. MZP in its PR also stated that the draft Citizenship Amendment Bill will be burnt during the total bandh.

                        Further in adherence to MZP’s call for state-wide Total Bandh in protest of CAB on 10.12.2019, several constituents of MZP and its Hqrs./Sub-Hqrs. in various Districts have voiced support for the Total Bandh to be observed in their respective areas as noted below –

  1. MZP Hqrs., Lunglei will observe Total Bandh from 6:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
  2. MZP Hqrs., Kolasib will observe Total Bandh from 6:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
  3. MZP Hqrs., Champhai will not observe Total Bandh but calls for closure of all Govt. and Private Offices, Educational Institutions and Banks. Hospital OPD and those doing academic Examinations are exempted.
  4.   MZP Hqrs., Mamit will observe Total Bandh from 6:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
  5.   MZP Hqrs., Serchhip will observe Total Bandh from 6:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
  6.   MZP Sub-Hqrs., Thenzawl will not observe Bandh but organise Sit-In protest at Field Veng kawn and issued an appeal to various NGOs to participate in the demonstration.
