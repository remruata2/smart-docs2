Major Mizoram- Assam border incidents ( Dt. 5.8.2021 - 22.8.2021 )

 

5.8.2021 :      (a) Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister; Pu Lalruatkima, I&PR Minister and Pu Vanlalngaihsaka, Home Secretary held a meeting with delegates of Assam comprising of Shri. Atul Bora, Minister Border Protection & Development; Shri. Ashok Singhal, Minister Urban Development and Shri. GD Tripathy, Commissioner & Secretary, Border Protection & Development on 5.8.2021 @ 12:30 p.m. at Aijal Club, Aizawl in connection with issues pertaining to tension, law & order at  the    inter-state border.

            (b)       Damcherra Bazar Committee, Tripura organized road blockade at Damcherra Bridge, Mizoram-Tripura connecting bridge (the main supply route to Mizoram due to blockade of roads in Assam) w.e.f. the afternoon of  Dt. 5.8.2021 @ 3:00 p.m. in demand for free movement of people of the 2 (two) States across the bridge. After negotiation were conducted between Kanhmun VC/NGO/SDO(C) Kawrthah and Damcherra Bazar Committee/Panishagar SDM,vehicles stranded on both sides were released with an intention to conduct to conduct a joint meeting of both the parties on the next day.

            (c)       Residents of villages on Assam side near Phaisen village stage protest and organized procession near Balicherra bazar on 5.8.2021 in connection with the recent border incident and the protesters proceeded up to the inter-state border near Phaisen village.

 

6.8.2021 : Pu Andy Lalfakzuala Varte, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah and Shri. Swapnajit Sarkar, BDO, Damcherra RD Block held a meeting at Damcherra RD Block Conference Hall on 6.8.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. in connection with blockade of Damcherra Bridge by Damcherra Bazar Committee. The meeting was attended by representatives of businessmen and VC of Kanhmun and Damcherra.

 

7.8.2021: Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) in its Press Release issued on 7.8.2021 stated that CYMA OB Emergency Meeting held the same evening @ 8:00 p.m. strongly condemned mob attack of Mizoram bound vehicles (stranded at Mizoram House, Silchar due to the prevailing border row) by residents of Lailapur while en route to Mizoram under Assam Police escort. It also appealed Sub-Headquarters/Group/Branch YMA to await further statement of CYMA in this regard. It also stated that CYMA intention to closely monitor the inter-state border dispute.

 

(Note : Information receives indicated that S.P Cachar had given his assurance to Mizoram Government Officials for the safe passage of stranded vehicles bound for Mizoram through Assam territory in the evening of 7.8.2019. After the above said mob attack, Mizoram bound vehicles were able to start entering Mizoram without further incidents)

 

 

9.8.2021:       a) On 8.8.2021 around 5:00 p.m. barricade constructed on the road at the edge of Medili Bridge, Assam side was dismantled and vehicles started entering Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge

                    b) Uprooted railway track leading to Bairabi, Mizoram at Mohamadpur Station, Hailakandi District was repaired on 9.8.2021 @ 4:00 p.m.  and hence, the track was restored fit for movement of train.

 

11.8.2021:Assam started construction of bridge (which appears to be Bailey bridge) near the existing bamboo bridge across Saihapui V river w.e.f. 11.8.2021 which will connect Pu Chalchhunga farm land to Pu Lalnuntluanga farm land, both inside Mizoram. The bridge construction site is located at around 100 meters towards north-east from Mizoram Armed Police duty post, located beside Saihapui V river. It may be mentioned in this regard that Assam Police were being deployed at the 2 (two) stated farm lands.

 

12.8.2021 : CRPF 140 B-Coy deployed at Vairengte Auto Stand (Ngei ngei tlang) and Saihapui ‘V’ was closed on 10.8.2021 and Mizoram police has been deployed at their vacated locations. Information received stated that their replacement CRPF 225 C-Coy is en route to Vairengte from Chawngte. 

 

17.8.2021:  (a)          A WhatsApp post was circulated on the evening of 16.8.202 @around 7:00 p.m. which stated that Assam started construction of road at a hill lock of Pu Dokunga of Vairengte Venghlun farm land at Middle Aitlang, which is located around 4 km north from Pu Lalchhandama’s farmland, where Mizoram Police set up camp and appealed Vairengte youths to proceed to the area. Upon learning the said incident, 20 (twenty) Mizoram Police led by SDPO, Vairengte and 30 (thirty) youths of Vairengte immediately proceeded to the area, and when they arrived at the area at around 8:00 p.m. the same evening, Assam Police and civilians has already left the area. It was found that around 30 (thirty) metres long road was constructed inside Pu Dokunga’s farm land by Assam side.

            2 (two) Platoons of Mizoram Police were deployed at Pu Dokunga’s farm land w.e.f. 17.6.2021 to maintain order at the area and site preparation for setting up of Mizoram Police camp at the area started on 17.8.2021 by engaging excavator (JCB).
At the same time, Assam Police constructed 2 (two) duty posts at Babu-a of Vairengte Hmar veng farm land, which is situated at around 70 (seventy) meters from the proposed Mizoram Police Camp at Pu Dokunga’s farm land. It may be mentioned that Mizoram finished construction of jeep able road from PHE Pump House, Aitlang upto Pu Dokunga farm land, which is around 7 kms in length.

            (b)       Information received stated that on the morning of 17.8.2021 @ 2:00 a.m., shots were fired by the Assam Police towards 3 (three) Mizo civilians, viz., Lalduhawmi w/o HK Laltharzela; Lalchhandama S/o HK Pazawna and Vanlalrovi w/o Hnamtinkunga, all residents of Vairengte Hall Veng. The incident reportedly took place at Aitlangpui, close to Bilaipur village when the above 3(three) persons were en-route to meet a person called ‘Nibus’, resident of Bilaipur, Assam on the latter's invitation.  Lalduhawmi w/o HK Laltharzela Hallveng sustained injury while fleeing the scene, and she was treated       and released this morning from PHC, Vairengte.

20.8.2021 : On  20.8.2021 @12:30 pm,  about a section strength of Assam Police came with Bolero Pik-up and TATA 207 at the site where bridge construction work is being carried out by Pu R. Lalthachungnunga of Bairabi North under MPWD, over Bairabi river (which is situated in between  Pu J. Lianchungnunga of Bairabi North paddy field and Pu  Ch.Chhunga (L) of Saron Veng, Aizawl paddy field), located at around 1 km north of Zophai BOP Junction at Bairabi to Gharmurra road, inside Mizoram. Assam Police informed the 4 (four) workers available at the area to immediately evacuate the area. After the four workers evacuated the area, Assam  Police carried away the construction materials such as iron rods available at the area. It may be mentioned that the Bridge construction work was started in early part of this year (2021)  and it was halted due to unknown reason, and the work has been resumed w.e.f. 11.8.2021.
