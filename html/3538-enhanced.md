A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.12.2022_

****CYMA strongly against shelter for LGBTQI**: In pursuance of instruction received from Central Ministry, Urban Development and Poverty Alleviation Department (UDPA) of Mizoram floated a tender for the construction of building (shelter) for persons such as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex (LGBTQI) on 6th December 2022. In this connection, leaders of Central YMA met Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister yesterday and requested him to cancel the proposed building construction. Objection to the said building construction have received numerous complaint from the public as they considered it not suited to Mizoram state and its societies. The estimated amount for the construction of this LGBTQI building at Sakawrtuichhun, Aizawl is Rs. 35,00,000 and will be undertaken from funds received from National Urban Livelihoods Mission.**

**B**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.12.2022_

**No tolerance drive in Aizawl :** Starting from yesterday, Aizawl Traffic Police have started conducting of NO Tolerance Drive/Checking of vehicles within the city at two points - in front of Vanapa Hall and Dawrpui. According to reports received from officers of Traffic Police, documents and related papers of vehicles were checked where 133 defaulters were fined which amounted to Rs. 1,57,400 in all. Out of these 133 persons, 108 were 2-wheelers, 22 LMVs and 3 Taxis. The most common violation of rules by these defaulters were driving without licence, illegal customization of vehicles, under sized number plates and invalid insurance certificates.
