**A. Front page of The Aizawl Post/ The Zozam Times/ VANGLAINI newspapers**

**Dt.30.1.2013**

**Talks between CYMA and HPC (D) :**The meeting of Central Executive Committee of  CYMA yesterday agreed to meet the imprisoned chairman of HPC(D),H.Zosangbera.  The meeting will be meant for discussing ways to reinstalled various branches of YMA inside the demand areas of HPC(D) which were dissolved after threats were received  from HPC(D). A committee under the chairmanship of CYMA Vice President was  created for this purpose .However, the exact time and date of this meeting is not yet  confirmed.

**B. Front page of The Aizawl Post/ Vanglaini newspaper Dt.30.1.2013**

****Ex Chief Minister chargsheeted:** Former Chief Minister of Mizoram, Pu Zoramthanga  who was accused of disproportionate assets was chargesheeted yesterday by ACB of  Mizoram. A supplementary chargesheet was submitted to Special Court by the ACB  and the accused was represented by his lawyer in the court. Hearing of this case will be conducted on March 7 2013 according to Pu T.Saikunga, Special Court judge.**

**C. Front page of The Aizawl Post/ The Zozam Times/ VANGLAINI/ Newslink newspapers Dt.30 1.2013**

****13 Polling Stations declared sensitive :**In a preview meeting for the ensuing Chalfilh  constituency by-election, the Aizawl D.C ,Dr.Franklin Laltinkhuma presented a detailed preparation of this by-election. From his statement,13 polling  stations out of 29 inside  this constituency were declared as sensitive. The constituency will be divided into 3  sectors, each of which will be supervised by a sectoral  Magistrate. The declared  sensitive polling stations includes - N.Khawlek, Daido,  Vanbawng, NE Tlangnuam,  Lamherh, Khawlian - I, Khawlian -II, Suangpuilawn -I,  Suangpuilawn -II, Phuaibuang - I, Phuabuang -II,Zawngin and Luangpawn.**

**D**.**Front page of The Aizawl Post/ The Zozam Times newspapers Dt.30.1.2013**

**Arrest of VCP by ACB :** The VCP of Kanhmun village, Pu Sapmawia was arrested by  ACB in connection with misappropriation of development funds for the village. According  to reports, the said VCP misused various development between 2009 and 2012 which  were worth around Rs. 32.53 lakhs. The ACB arrested the VCP on 28.1.13 in response  to an FIR submitted by Joint Action Committee of Kanhmun village and sent him to  Central jail yesterday.
