1.Bangladesh refugees :

 From 16.11.022, an encounter between Bangladesh Army backed Arakan Army (AA) and KNA (Kuki National Army) started taking place. It was also reported that the main cause of the encounter between Bangladesh Army backed AA and KNA was that a few days back KNA had allegedly killed 1 (one) AA cadre and they also took away 1 (one) machine gun. In retaliation to this incident and to counter KNA from entering inside Banderban District, Bangladesh, the claimed operation area of AA, AA started the encounter.

 Due to the encounter between Bangladesh Army Backed AA and KNA, large number of residents (mostly Bawm tribe) including children and women of Sunsong, Salopui, Thingdawl and Chhaihkhiang villages of Bangladesh had fled their villages to take shelter inside Mizoram.
