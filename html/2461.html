MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/739

Dated 21st December, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/737
Dt. 18.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV- 9, MMV-5, LMV – Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via., Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 21.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 7, LMV - Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

          5 (five) vehicles (HMV –Nil, MMV – 5, LMV - Nil) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via., Damcherra Bridge, Tripura-Mizoram border today i.e., 21.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. 18 (eighteen) vehicle (HMV – Nil, MMV – 15, LMV-3) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

88 (eighty eight) vehicles (HMV-30, MMV – 4, LMV – 46, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 21.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          150 (one hundred fifty) vehicles (HMV – 41, MMV – 54, LMV –32, LPG – 2, Maxi Cab – 4, Oil Tanker – 17, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 21.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
