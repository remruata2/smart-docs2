****BURGLARY OF BDO's OFFICE, ZAWLNUAM, MAMIT DISTRICT****

 On 09.08.2012 (Thursday) between 4:00pm to 6:40pm, BDO's office Zawlnuam has been burgled by unknown miscreants and cash amounting to 198.47 lakhs was reported stolen. A police team led by SP, Mamit and Forensic team from Aizawl immediately rushed to Zawlnuam and after preliminary investigation, C.Lalfakzuala (47) s/o Lalhmingthanga of Borai, a Cashier working in BDO's office was picked up for interrogation on 10.08.2012.

 Following revelation by the Cashier, the Police had eventually arrested the under mentioned three (3) accomplices from Borai on the night of 12.08.2012:-

1. Zodinliana (42) s/o Chala

2. Laltlanmawia @ Tlantea s/o RK Thianga (L)

3. Lalthlamuana (51) s/o Banthanga

 The Mizoram Police operation party had recovered Rs. 12.00 lakhs from the garden of Pianvula at Borai and Rs. 98,000.00 from Kanhmun.

 On 22.08.2012, Mizoram Police operation party assisted by OC, Khedacherra PS, launched an overnight operation at Monacherra Village, Tripura and arrested the following suspects who were reportedly involved in the sensational burglary case of Zawlnuam BDO office :-

1) Binod Kumar Reang (30)

 S/O Dinomoni Reang of Monacherra

2) Khozaram Reang (26)

 S/O Potirai Reang of Monacherra

3) Kantarai Reang (55)
