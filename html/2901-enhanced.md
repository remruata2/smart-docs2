**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.13.12.2012**

**Court hearing of cases of leaders of HPC(D)**: Court hearing of around 10 cases in which HPC(D) Chairman, H. Zosangbera and S/S Dy. Army Chief Biaknunga are alleged to be involved, was held at the Chief Judicial Court yesterday, no verdict, however, was yet made. Meanwhile, the bail proceeding of these two accused are still in progress in the High Court according to reports.

**B. Front page of NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 13.12.2012**

**Ex-CM Zoramthanga charge sheeted in DA case**: Former Mizoram Chief Minister Zoramthanga, already charged with misusing government properties for personal purpose, has been charge sheeted in disproportionate asset case by ACB, Mizoram under Prevention of Corruption Act on 12.12.2012. "The Court will consider the charge sheet on 28th January 2013", source said yesterday.

**C. Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.12.2012**

****Stern control of arms**: The General Assembly of Association for Environmental Preservation(ASEP) held yesterday passed a resolution to demand the State government to impose a strict control of civilian arms. ASEP urges the State government to make an efforts for all the licenced guns holder to deposit their arms during fire season to prevent misuse of arms against wildlife.**

**D. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES/VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.12.2012**

Former leaders of Group, Joint and Branch YMA inside the HPC(D) demand area who were disbanded by the threat of HPC(D) express their grievances against Central YMA yesterday. The Ex-YMA leaders convention mentioned that CYMA was not making enough efforts in connection with their plight and that it came as a surprise for them that their issue was not even included in the recent YMA General Conference.
