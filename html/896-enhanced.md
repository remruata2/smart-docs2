****ARAKAN ARMY****

 TheArakan Armyis aRakhineinsurgent groupinMyanmar(Burma), founded on 10th April 2009.It is the armed wing of theUnited League of Arakan(ULA) and is currently ledby Major GeneralTwanMratNaing. The purpose of the AA is to protect Arakan people and to establish peace, justice, freedom and development.

 The AA is a participant in theKachin conflict, fighting alongside theKachin Independence Army(KIA) against theTatmadaw(Myanmar Armed Forces). Most AA soldiers were originally trained at the KIA Military Academy; however, the AA has additional training camps inRakhine State.

 The group has stated that it advocates for:

- self-determination for the multi-ethnic Arakanese population.
- safeguarding and promotion of the national identity and cultural heritage of the Arakan people.
- preserving "national dignity" and best interests of the Arakan people.

_REPORT ON ALLEGED SETTING UP OF CAMP(S) BY ARAKAN ARMY (AA) INSIDE MIZORAM_

Information has been received from Sister Intelligence Agency that the AA (Arakan Army) is setting up camps at Kakichhuah areas, along the Indo-Myanmar border on Mizoram soil. In this regard, an enquiry was conducted to ascertain the stated input. But after conducting thorough enquiry by engaging reliable sources and others means, it is reported that there is no evidence to suggest the existence of AA camp inside Mizoram.

Known AA leaders were also contacted and they reported that they have no camp inside Mizoram and reiterated that they have no intention to establish camp(s) in Mizoram in the foreseeable future. As per input, the nearest AA camp inside Myanmar is around 15 kms from the Indo-Myanmar border.
