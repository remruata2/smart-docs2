SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/

Dated: th March, 2020.

 

 

                        An operation team of CID (SB) had seized 5 (five) numbers of .22 Caliber Rifle A.K model (made in China) along with 10 (ten) nos. of magazine today i.e. 11.3.2020 @ 2:15 p.m from the possession of Sanglianzuala (27) s/o Bilthawna, Chamdur P-I, Lawngtlai  District, Mizoram.

                  

                    The said Rifles were hidden by Sanglianzuala at the poultry farm of Lalthakima s/o Lalrohlua, New Market, Dawrpui at Liansailova Road, Thuampui, Aizawl. In this connection Bawngkawn PS Case No. 98/20 dt. 11.3.2020 u/s 25(1)(a)/25-1B(f) Arms Act was registered. Thorough interrogation of the accused is underway to reveal detail  dossier of the accused as well as to ascertain the routes and destination of the seized arms.
