****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/220

Dated 26th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/216 Dt. 25.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-12, MMV-9, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 26.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-16, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 26.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

82 (eighty two) vehicles (HMV-55, MMV-1, LMV-17, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 26.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Pu Lalhuliana Fanai, DIG (ER) visited the disputed site, Assam Mizoram border today @ 11:30 a.m. to take stock of the situation and he was accompanied by Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

131 (one hundred thirty one) vehicles (HMV-49, MMV-30, LMV-13, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-9, Oil Tanker-26, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 26.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
