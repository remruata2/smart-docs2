**12.4.2022 :** Mizoram Armed Police Association (MAPA) General Headquarters submitted a written petition to the Home Secretary on 12.4.2022 wherein they expressed their support to the Home Department's proposal regarding distribution of Mizoram Police Service (MPS) Junior Grade Promotee quota as 80 percent and 20 percent between Unarmed Branch (UB) Inspector and Armed Branch (AB) Inspector for the time being. It also stated that as per the existing MPS rules, 36 (thirty six) posts are reserved for Inspector (UB) and 9 (nine) posts are reserved for inspector (AP) out of the total 45 (forty five) MPS Junior Grade Posts for Promotees. It further stated that MAPA approached the Court of Law to increase the quota of Armed Branch (AB) Inspector and decision of the Court is being awaited. In this regard, it stated their desire that the existing proposal of the Home Department should be implemented first in the present situation even if the Mizoram Police Service Association (MPSA) make another proposal.
