A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST VANGLAINI & NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 17.12.2013_

**Stabbed to death****:** Vanlalhuma (25) s/o Lalawmmeka of Vairengte Chhimveng was stabbed on his chest by his friend Lalthanmawia(30) s/o Chawngrikunga of Vairengte Chhimveng; Vanlalhuma succumbed to his injury the same night. Lalthanmawia was arrested by Vairengte Police and case was registered under Vairengte Police Station. Meanwhile, David Lalhumhima(31) s/o R.Thanthuama of Kolasib Khuangpuilam cut the throat of Lalpianruala(24) s/o Chhantluanga of Kawnpui near CZS traffic point at Kolasib on the same night. The victim was admitted to Kolasib District Hospital and further referred to Aizawl for necessary emergency.
