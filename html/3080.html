A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.4.2013

ILP checked: Lunglei Police arrested 42 non-Mizos who do not have ILP yesterday ie, 4.4.2013, they have already arrested 10 non-Mizos without ILP on Wednesday ie, 3.5.2013. The arrested non-Mizos without ILP were forwarded to Court and if the court finds them guilty, they will be pushed back to their respective home towns.
