WRITE-UP ON POLITICAL LEVEL PEACE TALK

 

FIRST ROUND

 

            The Political Level Peace Talk between Mizoram State Government and HPC-D was held under a cordial environment at the State Guest House, Aizawl during 7th & 8th March, 2018 and the Peace Talk ended with a logical conclusion on 8.3.2018. The State Government and the HPC-D were represented by 10 (ten) members each. While the delegate of Mizoram Government was led by Pu Lalrinmawia Ralte, MLA and Political Adviser to Chief Minister, Pu L.T. Hmar, Working Chairman, HPC-D led the HPC-D delegates during the two-day Peace Dialogue.
