Investigation Report

                                                Accused Kapkhenpau (43) s/o H. Khama (L) of Tidim, Myanmar P/A Thuampui Veng, Aizawl was apprehended by SI Laldawngliana , DSB, Aizawl (Complt) with party and BSF ‘G’ Branch personnel on 21.3.2009 @ 4:30 AM inside Champhai – Aizawl Night Bus R/No. MZ-01-D/1486 at Zemabawk, Aizawl and recovered arms and ammunitions as mentioned above in SI No. 10 and handed over to OC, Bawngkawn PS.

            In pursuance of interrogation statement of the accused, the following were arrested on 21.3.2009 viz. :- 1) C. Zachhuma (49) s/o Ngankama of Kanan Veng, Champhai, 2) Thangchhuanliana (30) s/o Lianchina, Champhai, 3) Albert D. Manga (34) s/o D. Ngenngaihpan, Hnahlan. The statements of the three arrested persons revealed that the seized weapons were given to Albert D. Manga by one Kapthanga, Talek, Myanmar on 9.3.2009 who in turn handed over to Thangchhuanliana on 13.3.2009 at Hnahlan who left the place by availing Mizoram State Transport Bus the same day. The weapons were kept at the house of C. Zachhuma at Champhai. Zachhuma had phoned accused Kapkhenpau on 19.3.2009 and the latter proceeded to Champhai on the same day by night bus and arrived there in the morning of 20.3.2009 @ 5:00 AM. After some discussion, it was agreed that payment would be made after selling the weapons and then Zachhuma and Thangchhuanliana escorted accused Kapkhenpau to a bus station that left for Aizawl by night bus and was apprehended as stated earlier.

            It was ascertained that the accused had either no concrete plan to sell the weapons or prospective buyers. He had taken the weapons in the hope that he might get some financial benefits as commission by selling them.

            None of the arrested persons had previous involvement in arms smuggling cases.

 

PREVIOUS DEALING OR INVOLVEMENT: His involvement in arms smuggling case in the past had never been known.
