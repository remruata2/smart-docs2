1. BOMB BLAST AT ASSAM RIFLES MANDIR

            On 23.5.2013, a powerful bomb exploded inside the premises of 26th Assam Rifles Mandir near Zodin Square, Aizawl. The blast damaged 26 window glasses and crater of 6 inches deep was created by the blast. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. In this connection, Aizawl PS C/No.169/13 dt.23.5.13 u/s 295/436 IPC r/w 3 of Explosive Substances Act has been registered.

 

2. BOMB BLAST AT TREASURY BAZAR

            On the night of 30.1.2014 @ 10:55 PM, an unknown type of explosive bomb was exploded from one vegetable shop at Treasury's Square market. Destroying three shops into bits, and caused severe damages to the shop itself from where the blast took place as well as its two adjacent shops while ten other nearby shops also reportedly took heavy damages.

            All the window panes of Assembly secretariat and the Information and Public Relations office were smashed under the impact, window glass of other nearby buildings including private residences were also shattered. The whole walls of two rooms, housing drivers and guards, in the Assembly Secretariat were also severely damaged by the blast. Neither the type of bomb/explosive used nor the culprits and their intention were not yet known however the case is being thoroughly investigated by Police and Forensic personnel. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. In this connection Aizawl PS Case No. 28 dt. 31.1.2014 u/s 436 IPC r/w 3 of Explosive Substances Act has been registered.

            Damages caused by the explosion are listed below:

(1) Assembly House :

            (i) 266 window panes.

            (ii) 65 ventilation glasses.

            (iii) 6 nos. of tube light frames.

            (iv) 1 extended room used as Driver' room was badly damaged.

(2) Information & Public Relation New building:

            (i) 231 window panes.

            (ii) 168 ventilation glasses.

            (iii) 22 nos. of window frames.

(3) Information & Public Relation Old building:

            (i) 135 window panes.

            (ii) 5 nos. of window frames.

(4) Printing & Stationery building:

            (i) 72 window panes.

            (ii) 12 ventilation glasses.

            (iii) 25 nos. of wall tiles.

(5) Pi Thanzami Tochhawng's house:

            (i) 8 window panes.

            (ii) 2 nos. of window frames.

            (iii) 5 nos. of wall tiles.

(6) MJA Office/Press Club:

            (i) 7 window panes.

(7) 7 nos. of hawkers shed of Treasury Bazaar were totally damaged and 14 nos. of hawkers shed were partially damaged.

  

 

3. BOMB BLAST AT NEW MARKET MEAT SHOP

On the night of 20.2.2014 at 11:45pm, some unknown miscreants had blasted an explosive suspected to be gelatin at the entrance of New Market meat shop old building. The explosion had caused damages such as: (i) Main gate of old building of New Market meat shop, (ii) some portion of the retaining wall near the main gate, (iii) 3 window frame, 14 window glass, 4 ventilator and ceiling tile of teacher's room of SSA girls hostel, (iv) 11 window panes and wall tile of Trade and Commerce Deptt. Quarters. Fortunately, there is no human casualty due to the blast. The case is being investigated by Aizawl Police Station Staff. In this connection, Aizawl PS C/No.48/14 dt.21.2.14 u/s 436 IPC r/w 3 of Explosive Substances Act has been registered.
