A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 19.7.2013

12 people fined: The court fined 12 guilty for damaging several properties and vehicles near Aizawl Police Station on the night of 16th February (Saturday) this year and fined them ` 2,200/- each, if they do not pay the fine they will be detained in Jail for 22 days.

Case was registered against the crowd who gathered at Aizawl Police Station on 16th Feb., 2013 (Saturday night) for wanting to see the face of Lalruatsanga of Mission Veng who posted insulting words on Facebook supporting Lalchhandami and Lallawmkimi both from Bethlehem Vengthlang – the accused in the arson of a house and several vehicles at Aizawl Bethlehem Veng and College Veng.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.7.2013

Driving licence to be checked: The Traffic Police will start checking driving licence of students, who drive two wheeler from 24th July, 2013; even those who possess driving licence will be asked to produce birth certificate. If any one is caught breaking the law, legal action will be taken against them.
