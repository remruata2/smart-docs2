PRELIMINARY INTERVIEW REPORT OF ROHINGYA MINOR BOYS

 

Name :            1) Rahamed Tullah 14 yrs s/o Jahet Hussain

                        2) Md Faizal 13 yrs s/o Ziana Alam, both r/o Buthidaung,                                    Rakhine State, Myanmar

           

            Fled Buthidaung about a year ago due to turmoil and stayed in Jamtoli Refugee Camp, Bangladesh.

            During first part of Jan 2018 one person (unidentified) offered them job abroad for which they paid 10,000/- B/desh Takka.

            They left Rufugee Camp in a Bus upto Chittagong, and from Chittagong by Train upto Cox’s Bazar, and from Cox’s Bazar upto Agartala(Tripura) by Motorcycle.

            From Agartala, another person(unidentified) took over. They traveled in a passenger vehicle (jeep/sumo) upto Mizoram via Silchar.

            One person identified as Johny received them. (They were not familiar with places in Aizawl they were taken to yesterday while in a process cross-verification of their claim of brief stay in Aizawl).

            They left Aizawl in a Sumo for Zokhawthar ( or Champhai) on 01/02/2018 @ 5:00 pm (Thursday) and reached Melbuk next day i.e. 04/02/2018 @ 5:00 am. Johny left them to the charge of one C. Lalhmingmawia @ Mawia who again put them in the house of his brother in-law, ( a handicapped dumb couple). Later, on 03/02/2018 Champhai police on receipt of a tipoff went there and apprehended them.

            Champhai police on reaching Melbuk also learnt from villagers that there were four others  kept in separate location, who managed to escape alongwith Mawia and his wife named Lalnunkimi, who remained still untraced.

            The two minor Rohingya boys are kept in Remand home while their interview will continue.

            In the meantime, mobile phone of Lalnunkimi w/o C.Lalhmingmawia (now absconding) was tracked at Vaphai yesterday. Champhai Police acting in close coordination with Aizawl Police immediately activated Dungtlang Police. In the process, Dungtlang police had detained one Alam Uddin at Vaphai last night on grounds of suspicion. The said Alam Uddin had come from Mandalay, Myanmar, and was seen going to the house where the absconding C.Lalhmingmawia @ Mawia and his wife, Lalnunkimi were hiding yesterday.
