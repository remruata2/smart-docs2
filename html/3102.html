A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 12.8.2013

Health Minister alleged of involving in Pseudoephedrine smuggling: Health Minister Lalrinliana Sailo is alleged of misusing his power by MNF Vigilance Dept. in a press meet on Saturday at Mizo Hnam Run. The Minister is alleged of giving four (4) drug licenses to his son Jacob Lalrinpuia and used these licences to smuggle abusive drug- pseudoephedrine.

            MPCC Media Dept. reacted to this allegation of Health Minister saying that MNF is just trying to defame the Minister as they are afraid he will be elected again in the same constituency in the upcoming Assembly Election and are afraid to contest against him.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 12.8.2013

Peace Talk on 14th August: B.Lalhmingthanga, Secretary to the Government of Mizoram will lead the delegates of Mizoram in the peace talk with HPC (D) this Wednesday. LT Hmar will lead the HPC (D)’s delegates. The meeting is expected to discuss the extension of Suspension of Operations (SoO) which ended on 31st July, ie, last month. The demands of HPC (D) are also expected to be the topic of the meeting.
