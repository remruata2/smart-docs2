****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/153

Dated 4th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/140 Dt. 3.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-11, MMV-12, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 4.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 6 (six) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-6, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-20, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 4.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

81 (eighty one) vehicles (HMV-58, MMV-2, LMV-23, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 4.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

180 (one hundred eighty) vehicles (HMV-57, MMV-49, LMV-24, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-8, Oil Tanker-39, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 4.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-12, LMV-10, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-Nil) entered Saiphai from Assam today i.e., 4.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
