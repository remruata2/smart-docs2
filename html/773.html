Dt.28.6.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 25.6.2018, Item No. B, Leaders of Mizoram Tipper Drivers & Owners Association (MTDOA) called a Press Conference today, i.e. 28.6.2018

@ 12:00 noon at Kolasib Press Club. Leaders of MTDOA highlighted that MTDOA will not allow non-indigenous drivers or vehicle owners to trade in River Sand in Mizoram w.e.f 1.7.2018 in retaliation to the uneven royalty charges between Mizos and Non-Indigenous traders dealing in River Sand and Bricks by the Mahalders from Bagha, Assam. MTDOA also urged the Mizo public not to lend their indigenous identity to traders from Assam. It has been reported that MTDOA is likely to launch blockade of all vehicles carrying River Sand and Bricks from Bagha at Aizawl and Kolasib with effect from 1.7.2018.

 

Dt.2.7.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 28.6.2018, Item No. B(d). Members of Mizoram Tipper Drivers & Owners Association (MTDOA) launched blockade of all vehicles carrying River sand and Bricks from Bagha from 1.7.2018 at Mizoram Tippers Association (MTA) Office, Kolasib. It is, however, reported that no vehicle carrying River sand and Bricks from Bagha has been plying on this road since the blockade.

 

Dt.4.7.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 2.7.2018, Item No. B(a). Mizoram Tippers Association (MTA) continued blockade of all vehicles carrying River Sand and Bricks from Bagha and Lailapur from 1.7.2018. However, it is reported that no vehicle has been stranded till date.


Dt.5.7.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 3.7.2018, Item No.B. Leaders of Mizoram Tippers Association (MTA) led by Pu C. Lalzarmawia, President met Pu Sanghnuna, District Transport Officer (DTO), Urban in his office today and requested him to cancel all the registrations of Tippers owned by non-Mizos in his jurisdiction. It is reported that leaders of MTA questioned as to whether proper formalities were followed in registration and obtaining permit of commercial vehicles owned by non-Mizos in Mizoram.

 

Dt.9.7.2018 - Mizoram Tipper Association (MTA) convened an Emergency Executive Committee meeting yesterday, i.e. 8.7.2018 @ 1:30 p.m at MTA Office, Bawngkawn, Aizawl. The meeting was chaired by Pu J.H. Lalnunpuia, Vice President, MTA and it was attended by 24 (twenty four) members.The meeting previewed the upcoming Joint Meeting between Deputy Commissioner, Kolasib District and Deputy Commissioner, Cachar District. MTA submitted a letter to the Deputy Commissioner (DC), Kolasib District highlighting the grievances of MTA with regards to uneven opportunities for Bricks and River Sand dealership between locals and Mizo dealers at Bagha, Assam. In this regard, MTA urged the DC, Kolasib to pursue necessary initiatives. Leaders of MTA also urged the authorities to cancel the Mizoram Registration of Vehicles (Tipper) owned by non-indigenous.

Dt.10.7.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 9.7.2018, Item No. B(a). A joint meeting of District Administrations of Kolasib District and Cachar District and representatives of Mizoram Tippers Association (MTA) was held today, i.e. 10.7.2018 at SDO(C) Office, Vairengte. The following points were resolved by the joint meeting :-

                        (i)         Legal action will be taken by Cachar District Administration against unregistered River Sand Mahaldars.

                        (ii)        Registered Mahaldars will be instructed to put up signboard highlighting Mahaldar name, rates of River Sand, etc. at the site where River Sand is sold.

                        (iii)       The joint meeting agreed to form a Market Committee comprising of members from Mizoram and Assam which will include MTA members and the Committee will check the rates of River Sand and Bricks from time to time.

                        (iv)       The joint meeting also agreed that the blockade of the National Highway by both sides will be called off for peaceful Law & Order situation.

 

Dt.13.7.2018 - It has been reported that leaders of Mizoram Tipper Association (MTA) stated that Brick traders of Lailapur and Bagha are violating the provisions of agreement on prices of Bricks made by the Joint Meeting of District Administrations of Kolasib and Cachar and representatives of NGOs held on 10.7.2018 at Vairengte. In this regard, leaders of MTA are scheduled to hold a meeting in the near future to discuss their further course of action.

 

Dt.23.7.2018 - Reference to our DSI dated 20.7.2018, Item No. B(c). The proposed Executive Meeting of Mizoram Tipper Association (MTA) scheduled on 22.7.2018 was postponed to 31.7.2018 @ 1:00 p.m at their office in which they will deliberate on the rate of bricks charged by Lailapur and Bagha dealers @ Rs. 10.5 in contravention of the agreed rate of Rs. 10 per brick in the meeting between DC, Kolasib  and DC, Cachar on 10.7.2018 which was also  attended by MTA and the brick dealers. The association is also intending to hold its General Conference on 25.8.2018 at Daifim Picnic Spot.
