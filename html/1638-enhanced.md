  With reference to your letter and subject cited above, I am furnishing the required information as under:-

**Starred Question No. 197 :**

**a) How many kidnapped cases occurred after 2009?**

**Ans:** There were 4 kidnapped cases after 2009.

**b) Who are the kidnappers?**

**Ans:** **i)**On 6.6.11, two ABCI employees were kidnapped/abducted by UDLF from ABCI camp near Meidum village in Kolasib district.

 **ii)**  On 25.3.2012 6(Six) ABCI workers/employees were kidnapped/abducted by National Liberation Front of Tripura(NLFT) from ABCI camp in between New Sachan and Mauzam, at Lunglei district.

 **iii)**  On 25.11.2012, 3(Three) Bengali teak traders were abducted/kidnapped by a combined group of NLFT and BNCT (Borok National Council of Tripura) from Tuipuibari - II (Rajivnagar), Mamit District.

 **iv)**On the morning of 19.2.2013, 5 (Five) employees of Forest deptt. were abducted by NLFT/WNDLFM (Western National  Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) from their camp, in between Tuipuibari and Damparengpui, Mamit Dist.

**c) What are the causes of the kidnappings and demands of the kidnappers?**

**Ans:** i)  The abductors of ABCI workers on Dt.6.6.2011 demanded Rs.5 crores as ransom money. However, the 2 abductees were rescued by an operation team of Mizoram police on 16.6.2011.

 ii) The abductors of ABCI workers on Dt.25.3.2012 demanded Rs.1.2 crores as ransom money.

 iii) The abductors of three Bengali teak traders on Dt.25.11.2012 demanded Rs.30 Lakhs as ransom money.

 iv)  The abductors of Five Forest deptt. employees/workers on Dt.19.2.2013 demanded Rs.1 crore as ransom money.

**SUPPLEMENTARY NOTES:**

**1. Did the demands of the abductors/kidnappers fulfilled?**

**Ans:** Apart from Rs.9 Lakhs given to the abductors of the three Bengali teak traders by their families, no ransom money/demand was given to any other kidnappers/abductors.
