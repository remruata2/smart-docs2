27.5.2022 :    State Level Core Committee on 'Ruihhlo Do' held a meeting under the chairmanship of Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister of Mizoram on 27.5.2022 @ 1:30 p.m. at Assembly Annexe Conference Hall, Aizawl. The meeting decided to combat drugs/psychoactive substance including alcohol. It also decided to include the School Education Minister and department officials, representatives of MHIP, MZP as member of the Ruihhlo Do Core Committee. The meeting further decided to constitute Working Committee under the chairmanship of Social Welfare Minister to take immediate necessary follow up action on the decision of the Committee. It also decided to organize awareness in all schools under School Education Department. The meeting was attended by Health & Family Welfare Minister Dr. R. Lalthangliana; Social Welfare & Tribal Affairs Minister Dr. K. Beichhua; Law & Judicial Minister Pu T. J. Lalnuntluanga; Chief Secretary Dr. Renu Sharma and officials from Police, Excise & Narcotics and other concerned departments and representatives of Central YMA, Mizoram Kohhran Hruaitute Committee (MKHC) and Mizoram Journalists Association.
