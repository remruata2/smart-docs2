A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 7.8.2013

Corpse found: On Monday ie, 5.8.2013, the body of S.Zachhunga (33) of Thingkah village was found at the border of Thingzai River and Mauhak River near Lawngtlai. S.Zachhunga went to Thingzai River with his two (2) friends on Sunday, ie 6.8.2013, one (1) came home at night and the other came home in the middle of the day on Monday. Lalsiamthari, the wife of S.Zachhunga, sent her three (3) relatives to look for his husband, at around 3 O’clock in the afternoon they found the body of S.Zachhunga in a ditch. S.Zachhunga sustained injuries on his head and other parts of his body, and as there is a suspicion of murder, Lawngtlai Police arrested four (4) persons for interrogation.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 7.8.2013

Drugs seized: Lunglei police seized 3,859 capsules of Parvon Spas at Lunglei on 5th August from Kuthmingthanga @ KT Hmar (36) of Bairabi currently residing at Chaltlang, Aizawl. The arrested person carried visiting cards identifying himself as chairman of Iron Scrap Marketing Society, chairman of Mizoram Blind Care Society Limited and primary member of Indian Youth Congress Committee. Police also found four (4) ATM cards and some amount of cash from his possession.

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 7.8.2013

Step father-in-law accused of rape: A woman in Lunglei alleged her husband’s step father of raping her and lodged an FIR against him at Lunglei Police Station. Case was registered against the accused C.Rokima of Lunglei Venglai. According to report, rape case has been registered every month at Lunglei Police Station from April till August this year.
