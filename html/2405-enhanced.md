****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/73

Dated 3rd February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/70 Dt. 2.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV – 5, MMV – 11, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 3.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (MMV-4) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

33(thirty three) vehicles (HMV – 13, MMV – 15, LMV - 5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 3.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm.4 (four) vehicles (MMV –4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

****A bomb blast occurred 2(two) times at Mulibali LP School around 20 metres from RamriLui towards Assam side(which is located at northern side around 1500 metres from Bairabi PS) today i.e., 3.2.21 @ 1:27 a.m. and 1:35 a.m. The blast damaged the newly constructed Toilet cum Bathroom and Water Tanky, which were located near the School and the blast is believed to have been caused by gelatine dynamite. ASI Arvind Kumar, Bairabi PS and party rushed to the spot to enquire the matter. Pu C. Vanlalvena, Addl. SP, Kolasib and party reached the spot today @ 10:30 a.m. Further, a team of Assam Police led by SI Bir Mongol Singha, OC, Ramnathpur PS, Assam also reached the spot at 9:50 a.m. Forensic Science Laboratory team, Aizawl arrived at the PO this afternoon at 1:54 p.m. and after observing and examining the PO they left the PO at 3:50 p.m. Smt. Megh Nidhi Dahal, IAS, DC Hailakandi; Shri. Pabindra Kumar Nath, APS, SP Hailakandi and Soma Roy, SDO Katlicherra Circle visited the PO today @ 2:38 p.m. and they left the area around 3:00 p.m. today’s afternoon. Information received stated that Assam Police has not registered a case in this regard and SI Bir Mongol Singha OC, Ramnathpur stated that they may register a case, if they received instruction from higher authority. Necessary actions are being taken by Bairabi PS for registration of the case.

 5 (five) members of Political Affairs Committee (PAC) MPCC led by Pu PC Zoram Sangliana, Member PAC visited paddy fields located in between Zophai BOP and Assam Police Outpost, Golachhera regarding difficulty faced by owners of paddy fields from Mizoram and to observe inter-state border situation today i.e., 3.2.2021 @ 10:30 a.m. They also visited construction of agriculture link road by Assam at the Assam side and Bairabi Bridge which was recently inaugurated by Chief Minister. During their visit, Pu PC Zoram Sangliana stated that if the Mizoram government is unable to take necessary action for the safety of paddy fields owners from Mizoram at the border, MPCC will take necessary action and he also questioned the ruling government regarding construction of agriculture link road by Assam at the border. He further accused MNF party as trying to deceive the people, since no vehicle could ply on Bairabi Bridge till date, which was inaugurated by Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister on 5.1.2021.They left Bairabi for Aizawl today’s afternoon at 3:17p.m.

It may also be noted that on 24.12.2020 OC Bairabi PS and party visited the site of road widening work undertaken by Assam government under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) at Assam-Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP and directed the workers not to resume road widening work without the approval or decision of High Level Meeting between the two states (Mizoram and Assam) as it could lead to serious law and order issues. OC, Bairabi PS further informed that if they failed to comply with their direction, all the workers will be arrested and all their machines engaged in the said work will also be seized. Hence, Assam side did not continue their work till date.

 89 (eighty nine) vehicles (HMV- 45, MMV – 3, LMV –30, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-11) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 3.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
