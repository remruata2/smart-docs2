****REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/573

Dated:15th October, 2020.

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/570 Dt. 14.10.2020, a community work for reconstruction of Pu John Zolawmaâ€™s Jhum Hut at Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border which was burnt down by Assam Police and Forest Department Officials on 9.10.2020 was continued today i.e., 14.10.2020 and around 25 (twenty five) volunteers {5 (five) from MZP General Headquarters and 20 (twenty) from locals} participated. The community work was concluded around 4:00 p.m. under peaceful atmosphere and construction of Jhum Hut is completed (doors and windows still to be fitted). MZP leaders from Aizawl also left Thinghlun this evening.

 Further, today around 3:30 p.m. Shri Kamalakhya Dey Purkayastha, MLA, Karimganj North visited Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border near Mizoram Police Camp and had a short interaction with Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit and Pu Lallianmawia, IPS, CO, 4th IR who are stationed at the area. During their short interaction, both parties raised their concern and stressed about not to take any unilateral action by either side which could ignite tension between the two states and to wait for any decision from Higher Level. The MLA returned back to Zaite, Assam around 3:40 p.m.

 Situation is under close watch.
