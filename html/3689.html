A. Front page of  VANGLAIN  newspaper  Dt. 29.09.2022

Discussion with Governor in connection with low PGI : Yesterday i.e 28.9.2022, Mizoram Governor, Dr. Hari Babu Kambhampati held a discussion programme with senior officials of Mizoram School Education Department, at Raj Bhawan, in connection with the low Performance of three districts in Mizoram in the latest  Performance Grading Index (PGI). According to the report of School Education Secretary, the scores of Mamit district (126), Serchhip district (125) and Lawngtlai district (106) were way too low in the PGI 2018-2019. However, Serchhip and Lawngtlai districts achieved slight improvements in 2019-2020 while Mamit district still remained low (136). Official of School Education Department told the Governor that the reasons behind this was the failure of concerned authorities of the three districts to report some of their achievements which lowered their scores. The Governor, on his part, advised  the officials to give proper instructions to supervisors and Headmasters regarding  administrations and their responsibilities.

                       

 

B.Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 29.09.2022

Funds for Health Care Scheme :  Mizoram Government recently released Rs. 400 lakhs for Mizoram Health Care Scheme (MSHCS) which are expected to be again distributed to different patients registered under MSHCS in the near future. This funds, however will be able to cover medical bills received from  registered members till the first half of the month of June, 2021. According to information received through RTI Act from Health & Family Welfare Department, the total credit of Mizoram Health Care bill till August 2022 amounted to Rs. 74.04 crores which keeps on increasing. However, what is decreasing is the number of families registered under this scheme. 1,13,720 families registered themselves for the duration of October 2019- September 2020 while 87,695 families were registered  for October 2021-March 2022; there were only 39,505 families for April 2022-March 2023.
