****TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN****

 Mizoram share international border with Myanmar and Bangladesh, the length of which are 496.531 kms and 319.791 kms respectively. Through the length and breadth of these international borders, different militant outfits of the neighboring states and countries are presently operating actively. Apart from these, smugglers trafficking illicit substances such as Heroin, Methamphetamine, arms &amp; ammunitions etc. also frequently used Mizoram as a route for transporting their contraband items to its intended destinations.

 Pu H.Lalengmawia, IAS is holding the post of Commissioner &amp; Secretary in the Home Department under Govt. of Mizoram. As such, if the police Department of Mizoram conduct an intensive crackdown operation against these militants or smugglers, there might be a possibility of threat against Pu H.Lalengmawia being a Commissioner &amp; Secretary in the Home Department of Mizoram.
