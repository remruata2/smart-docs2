****MIZO ZIRLAI PAWL****

****( MIZO STUDENTS' ASSOCIATION)****

** **Mizo Zirlai Pawl**(_Mizo students' Association_) is aMizomulti-national student organization and apex students body inMizoramstate. Established on 27th October 1935 in Shillongby Mizo earlier educated students. Originally as the Lushai Students Association (LSA), this was later replaced with the “Mizo Zirlai Pawl” (Mizo Students Association) on 1st September 1946. As Mizoram State Government notified MZP raising day as 27th October, it has been observed as "Zirlaite Ni" (Students' Day) from 2008 in Mizoram. MZP is registered as SR No. 35 of 1969–70 underIndian Societies Registration Act. with its General Headquarters in the state capital ofMizoram,Aizawl. It has 12 Headquarters inside and outside Mizoramstate, such asChurachandpur(inManipur), Behliangchhip (inTripura), and insideMizoram - Serchhip, Biate, Champhai, Darlawn, Kolasib, Mamit,Zawlnuam,Saitual,LungleiandKhawzawl. It has 50 Sub-Headquarters and numerous branches.**

 The following Mizo (used as an umbrella term for all peoples who speak one of theMizo languages) student organizations are affiliated to it:

- Hmar Students Association(HSA)
- Lai Students' Association (LSA)
- Mizoram Bawm Students' Association (MBSA)
- Pang zirlai Pawl (PZP)
- Mara Students' Organization (MSO)
- Ranglong Students' Union (RSU)
- Thado-Kuki Students' Union (TKSU)
- Siamsinpawlpi(SSPP)

 It is itself a constituent unit ofNorth-East Students' Organisation(NESO).

 Most of College Students Union inMizoram, Women Polytechnic,AizawlandMizoram UniversityStudents Council; Chairman/Vice President and General Secretary are the Ex-Officio Members in General Headquarters.

 In different parts ofIndia, Cities and Institutions based Mizo Students Associations are also affiliated to Mizo Zirlai Pawl such as Mizo Students Union Pune (MSUP), Delhi Mizo Zirlai Pawl (DMZP) and like wise.

**History:**

The Lushai Students Association, previously founded in 1926 at Shillong and which had almost collapsed was revived in October 1935. Their main objectives were to look after the welfare of the Lushai Students, to prepare them for future leaders, and to preserve the Lushai traditional and cultural values. In its first General Election held on 24th October 1939, R. Buchhawna was elected as President, Lalhmuaka as General Secretary, Hrawva as Treasurer and Saptea as Student Secretary. The General Meeting at Aizawl on 1st September 1946 changed its name into **Mizo Zirlai Pawl**, in short **MZP** and shifted its base from Shillong to Aizawl.

**Functions :**

Theassociationis administered by theGeneral Headquarters. The General Headquarters is formed by 6 (six) elected Office Bearers. The Office Bearers were elected for 2 (two) years by Electoral College after every 2 (two) years who then appointed not more than 50 (fifty) Executive Committee Members for a term of 1 (one) year. 10 (Ten) appointed Secretaries were selected from the Executive Committee Members and they in-charge of various posts apart from advising and assisting the Elected Office Bearers.

 The Executive Committee appoints Advisers and NESO Council members for a term of 1 (one) year. Various Sub-Committees and Clubs are also formed in the General Headquarters.

 MZP Assembly is the top authority in the association, and Federal Council Sitting is the second highest authority to rule the association.
