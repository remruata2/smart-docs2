                BLFM ni 20.11.2001 kum a din a nih khan Army Chief-ah a tang nghal a. Amaherawhchu an pawl (BLEM) insiam that chhoh zelah administration kal in kum 2002 ah Gen. Secretary a chelh a, Kum 2003 ah BNF te nen a in chhungfin atangin Information & Communication Secretary-ah ruat ani ta a ni.

 

                2006 kum tir a BLFM chhunga harsatna a lo thlen hnu lawkah an hruaitu lawk 4 man an ni a, chumi hnu lawkah hruaitu man bang V/President Zoramthanga hovin mi 53 an in surrender leh a heng zinga telve lo te hi Lalthazuala hian ho khawmin mithar tam tak lakhawm a ni. Hetih lai hian Lalthazuala hi Information Secretary ti a hriat a ni. Lalthazuala leh a hote mi 804 hi 26.10.2006 ah Mizoram Sawrkarah an in surrender.

 

                Mizoram sawrkarin an inbenbelna tur a ngaihtuah mek lai hian Dimasha hel pawl (DHD) (Nunisha Faction) te bepawp thar lehin pawl thar siam tumin silai neihdan a ngaihtuah a. Ni. 25.6.2008 khan silai lei tuma Aizawl a an awm lain S/S Lt. Phaijen Langthasa (DHD) leh midang te nen man a ni a, silai lei tura an chah AK-47 Rifle 19 (sawmpakua) leh a mu sangriat chuang chu man a ni a. Reitak a tan hnuah Dt. 21.8.2009 ah khan bail in an chhuak ta a ni.
