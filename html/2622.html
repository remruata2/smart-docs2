Investigation Report

            The accused Thangliana s/o Vungkhama (L) of Zokhawthar, Champhai District was apprehended by Champhai Police on 31.2.2008 on the outskirt of Tlangsam, Champhai Distt. and the above listed arms and ammunitions were seized from his possession.

 

             According to his statement recorded from his interrogation, the accused Thangliana agreed to transport a weapon consignment for his acquaintances Laltea of Shillong and Hrangthanga Falam who claimed to be living in Aizawl for a charge of Rs. 5000/- from Myanmar to Mizoram. They went to Tahan, Myanmar where his two acquaintances obtained the weapons and ammunitions. From Tahan, the consignment was carried on foot by the accused with two other persons named Thangtinkhuma of Tahan and Tinlianpianga of Pidota, Myanmar towards Tlangsam village, Champhai District in Mizoram, the outskirts of which was their rendezvous point with Laltea and Hrangthanga and where the consignment was to be handed over to them. But on reaching the outskirt of Tlangsam village, the run into the YMA duty from Tlangsam village, his two partners fled while he was apprehended with all the items. Report was made to the police, he was arrested and the consignment seized.

 

            It was clear that the arms and ammunitions were obtained from Tahan region but it cannot be ascertain exactly from whom. Where it was intended is not exactly known, but if Laltea of Shillong is involved, it is most likely that the consignment will be moved to Meghalaya, most probably Shillong, and then sold off to insurgents of the NE Region from there.

 

PREVIOUS DEALING OR INVOLVEMENT: His involvement in arms smuggling case in the past had never been known.
