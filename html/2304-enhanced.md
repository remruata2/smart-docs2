****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/53

Dated 25th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/51 Dt. 22.01.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV – 5, MMV – 11, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 25.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 10 (ten) vehicles (MMV – 8, LMV - 2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

18 (eighteen) vehicles (HMV – 11, MMV – 4, LMV - 3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.e., 25.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV – 3, LMV -Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 72 (seventy two) vehicles (HMV-34, MMV – 4, LMV – 28, LPG - Nil, Maxi Cab-6, Bus - Nil) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 25.01.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
