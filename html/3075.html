SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/648

Dated: 26th Aug., 2021

 

          Acting on reliable intel input, a Joint Operation team of CID (SB) and SIB, Mizoram recovered and seized 5 lakhs tablets of Methamphetamine (v/o Rs. 25 crores at international smuggling rate and Rs. 10 crores at the local smuggling rate) today, i.e., 26.8.2021 @3:15 p.m. The contraband items were seized from 1 (one) heavy vehicle CRX 1512, BR/No. NL - 01A E-0243, driven by Mantu Kumar Dep (45) S/o MK Deb of Karimganj, Assam and second driver Subhas Das (33) S/o Sumesh Das of Karimganj, Assam near Buichali Bridge, Sairang, Aizawl District. In this connection, the above 2 (two) persons were arrested after observing all formalities and they were produced at Special Narcotic Police Station along with the seized contraband items. Accordingly, Special Narcotic PS Case No. 20/21
Dt. 26.8.2021 u/s 22(c)/29 ND&PS Act has been registered and investigation is underway.
