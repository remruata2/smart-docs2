A. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 23.5.2014_

**MNYF & MSU Condemned Government Action Against Selam Villagers****:**Mizo National Youth Front****(MNYF) in it's Press Statement expressed that it condemned the government's tactic of handling the situation at Selam caused by the 16th May incident. MNYF said that the problem between Selam and E&amp;F Dept. should not be solved by the use of force and if the government wants to nab the wrongdoers, it can do it by deploying CID and Police and that it was wrong to use tear gas on Selam people, threatening and taking action on innocent women is absolutely wrong.

 Mizo Student Union (MSU) also made a statement that it condemned the government's action of solving the problem between Selam and E&amp;F Dept. and that the government should try to make peace without using force.

* _Photo copy of Newslink about Selam incident appended herewith is also mentioned by all the dailies published in Mizo dialect on their headlines._
