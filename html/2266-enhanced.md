****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/320

Dated 22nd April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/316 Dt. 21.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****6 (six) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-4, LMV-0) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 22.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-26, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 22.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicle (MMV-7, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

71 (seventy one) vehicles (HMV-51, MMV-3, LMV-14, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 22.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
