**21.10.2022 :** On 21.10.2022 @10:00 am, Mizoram Civil Service Association held its 46th General Conference at Mc Cabe Hall, Aijal Club, Aizawl. Pu Zoramthanga, Hon'ble Chief Minister graced the conference as Chief Guest. The inaugural function was chaired by Pu V. Lalengmawia, Vice President, MCSA and the Hon'ble Chief Guest delivered a speech. As many as 100 (one hundred) MCSA members attended the Conference. Pu H. Lianzela, President, MCSA presided over the afternoon Business Session and the following agenda were deliberated:

 i) Strengthening of District Administration in newly created Districts.

 ii) Separate cadre of IAS for Mizoram.

 iii) Initiative to help boost and accelerate the state's economic growth.

 iv) E-Governance in Mizoram.
