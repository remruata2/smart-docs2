ORDER

 

 

            In view of the escalation rumours and opinions spread about two missing persons recently among the general public, a special team of CID(SB) is hereby formed to enquire and find out the truth behind all possible links pertaining to the sensational missing persons case. The newly formed special team of CID(SB) would stress the following points while conducting enquiry :

1) To record all characters and antecedent including parental roots of the two                        missing persons.

2) To re-examine all close friends of the two missing persons.

3) To closely monitor technical CDR of both the missing persons.

4) To re-examine technical CDR of the missing persons of close relatives.

5) To sit together atleast twice in a day and share the progress report to the                              undersign atleast once in a day.
