25.3.2022 :    A 4-day long LADC Budget Session commenced on 25.3.2022 @10:30 a.m. at the LADC Session Hall, Lawngtlai. The Session was chaired by Pu VL Hmuaka, LADC Chairman and 24 (twenty four) members were present. The Chairman made obituary references on the demise of 2 (two) Ex-MDC, i.e., Pu C. Sangchhunga and Pu Lalthlamuana. 2 (two) Starred Questions were asked and oral answers given in today's session and Pu V. Zirsanga, CEM LADC, presented the LADC Budget Estimates for 2022-2023 amounting to Rs. 20983.96 lakhs. 6 (six) Official Bills were also introduced in the session which was adjourned for the day at 12:40 p.m.

 

28.3.2022 :    2nd Sitting of 11th LADC Budget Session was held on 28.3.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at LADC Session Hall, Lawngtlai. Pu VL Hmuaka, Chairman, LADC chaired the session and 27 (twenty seven) members were present. During the session, 2 (two) starred questions were asked and oral answers were given, and the below mentioned bills were introduced and passed by voice vote after deliberate discussion :-

(i)        The LADC (General Service) Rules, 2022.

(ii)       The LADC (Group A,B,C & D posts) Recruitment (Second Amendment)      Rules, 2022

 

            The Session was adjourned for the day @ 3:25 p.m. which will be resumed on 29.3.2022 @ 10:30 a.m.

30.11.2022:   A 3 (three) day long Lai Autonomous District Council (LADC) Winter Session was concluded on 30.11.2022.
The below mentioned bills were passed by voice vote during yesterday's session:

            (i)        The LADC (Constitution conduct of Business etc.) (Fourth amendment)                  Bill 2022.

            (ii)       The LADC Education and human resource department (Group B&D)                       recruitment (First amendment) Bill 2022.

During the session, 4 (four) starred questions were asked and given oral answer, and Lai Autonomous District Council (Land Holding & Settlement) (Second Amendment) Act, 2022 was passed by voice vote after detailed discussion in the house.
