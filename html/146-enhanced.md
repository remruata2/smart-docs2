****Insurgent outfit operating in Mizoram and its neighbouring states****

**Mizoram - Manipur Border:**

1) **HPC (D**_)_: The Hmar Peoples Convention (Democrat) a militant having base in Manipur and Cachar bordering the northern part of Mizoram is now under Suspension of Operation(SoO) which was signed on 11th November 2010.

2) **ZRA**: The Zomi Revolutionary Army, a militant outfit of Paite community having base in Manipur is not having activities inside Mizoram bordering Manipur during the last few years.

3) **HNA**: The Hmar National Army, another militant outfit of Hmar community having base in Manipur is also having no activities inside Mizoram.

**Mizoram - Tripura Border:**

1) **BRU/BRA**: A certain Bru armed group of protagonist as a Bru Revolutionary Union/Army brutally murdered Zarzokima of Bungthuam village of Mizoram on 13.11.2009 which resulted communal clash between Mizo and Bru of the areas. Most of the member of the armed group suspected of involving in the said murder have been arrested whereas the king pin leader was brutally murdered by the other faction of Bru armed militant sometimes back. These Bru armed group always pose threat to the area bordering Mizoram - Tripura and Mizoram - Hailakandi District of Assam.

**Mizoram - Assam Border:**

1)**UDLFBV:**The United Democratic Liberation Front of Barak Valley which was formed as a gang in 2005 having strength about 50 cadres was involved in kidnapping and extortions during 2008 and 2009. The group is also suspected to be involved in the killing of Zarzokima, Bungthuam on 13.11.2009.
