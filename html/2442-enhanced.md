****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/136

Dated 1st March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/133 Dt. 26.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

32 (thirty two) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-18, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 1.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

30 (thirty) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-19, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 1.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV-3, HMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

A Farm Hut occupied by Ms Saira Khatun (30) w/o Faijul Hoque located at Pi Sangchhingpuii of Saitual paddy field around 2 km north from Zophai BOP towards Assam-Mizoram border was burnt into ashes yesterday’s evening i.e., 28.2.2021 around 7:30 p.m. Ms Saira Khatun and her 11(eleven) year old son were present inside the Farm Hut while it started burning and they immediately evacuated the Hut. Ms Saira Khatun stated that the fire started from the kitchen and destroyed all their household properties and 3 (three) goats. Pu R. Vanlalduata, SDPO, Kolasib &amp; party and Pu Lunghnema, Assistant Commandant, 1st IR Battalion visited the spot today around 3:00 p.m. and necessary enquiry is being undertaken.

92 (ninety two) vehicles (HMV-54, MMV-1, LMV-28, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 1.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

153 (one hundred fifty three) vehicles (HMV-51, MMV-36, LMV-22, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-35, Bus-3) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 1.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
