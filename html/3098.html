A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.8.2013

Foundation stone of 4th IR Bn. Hqrs. laid: Home Minister Pu R.Lalzirliana laid the foundation stone of 4th IR Battalion Headquarters at Rengdil on Saturday and said it is great pleasure that 25 people gave their land for IR Bn. without expecting anything in return. He also said that the construction for the place of 3rd IR Battalion at Thingkah in Lawngtlai District will begin soon using ` 31 crore and its foundation stone will be laid before election. Home Minister said DPR has been readied for the betterment of Fire Department with ` 1,000/- lakh, ` 1,400/- lakh for construction of Female Ward at Jail, ` 500/- lakh for Aizawl Traffic Police and ` 30 crore for construction of DGP Office.

An escaped prisoner re-arrested: Lalhuapzauva (27) accused of raping a 5 yr. old girl who ran away from Serchhip Police Station on 1.8.2013 was re-arrested in the morning of 3.8.2013 by leaders of East Lungdar YMA. Lalhuapzauva was accused of raping a 5 yr. old girl at Thenzawl which he visited with his wife.
