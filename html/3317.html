A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 14.5.2013

Black flag on East Tuipui : Following decision of Tuipuiral NGO Committee, every house of every village in East Tuipui Constituency started unfurl black flag to push government to fulfill their demands.

            Demands of Tuipuiral NGO are - to repair dilapidated roads in their area; to fix the problems caused by insufficiency of school teachers and to set up Police Station in their area.

            To urge the government and to fulfill their demands, each house in their area unfurl black flag from 13th May indefinitely, they will hold a press meeting at Aizawl on 15th May and if these do not have consequences they will organise hunger strike from 25th-31st May. If that does not have good result, they will close down government offices and schools in their area and even plan to boycott MLA election.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 14.5.2013

72 foreigner families left Phunchawng and Rangvamual : 72 families have left Phunchawng till yesterday and how many families have left Rangvamual could not be ascertained. Central YMA gave ultimatum to foreigners, who live at Phunchawng and Rangvamual without permission to leave Phunchawng and Rangvamual before 15th May, 2013.
