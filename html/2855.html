SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/911

Dated: 12th December, 2019.

 

 

Mizo Zirlai Pawl (MZP) Gen. Hqrs., during a function of the annual closing of their office today, i.e. 12.12.2019, decided an impromptu burning of the posters of Union Home Minister, Shri. Amit Shah and Pu C. Lalrosanga, MP(LS). About 300 (three hundred), including opposition party workers and civilian onlookers, gathered in front of Vanapa Hall @ 2:00 p.m and burnt the posters of Shri. Amit Shah and Pu C. Lalrosanga. The MZP President, B. Vanlaltana delivered a short speech denouncing the passage of the Citizenship Amendment Bill-2019 and sharply criticised Pu C. Lalrosanga, MP(LS) for supporting the bill against the wishes of Churches and the people who had voted him to power.

 

A Press Release issued today by MZP Gen. Hqrs. indicated that they intended to receive Pu C. Lalrosanga, MP(LS) on his return from New Delhi at Lengpui Airport with 'Mizo Pawndum', while on the contrary, they would hail the Rajya Sabha MP, Pu Ronald Sapa Tlau with 'Tawlhloh puan' for opposing the Bill.

 

Situation under close watch.

 

DGP (M)
