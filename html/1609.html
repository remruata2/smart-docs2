SUPPLEMENTARY NOTE:

 

After 5 (five) rounds of peace talk on 2.4.2018, Government of Mizoram and HPC-D, the main insurgent group of Mizoram, signed a Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) at a function held at State Guest House, Aizawl. Shri. Arvind Ray, the then Chief Secretary of Mizoram and Shri H. Zosangbera, Chairman, HPC-D signed the MoS on behalf of the State Government and HPC-D respectively. The signing of MoS brought  an end to the over two-decades armed conflict of the HPC-D.

 

            Finally, the Arms Laying Ceremony of HPC-D was held at CTI, Sesawng on 13th April, 2018 where 102 armed Cadres and 12 Council Members of HPC(D) come to over ground at this ceremony.
