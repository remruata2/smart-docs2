A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 08.09.2022_

****Decline in funds received from Central while credit of the state increased**: According to the State Finance Audit Report of the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) of India for the year ended March 31,2021 which was presented by Chief Minister/Finance Minister, there has been a decline in the funds received by Mizoram from Central Government wherein the revenue receipt during the year 2020-21 was Rs. 7,740.67 crores which is 11.41% less than Rs. 1,917.59 crores which was received during the year 2019-2020. During the year 2020-2021 , the expenditure incurred by the state on the salaries of Government servant, medical reimbursement, pension and other Government spending from the state budget was Rs. 8,514.80 crores (88.29 %) while Rs. 1,125.97 crores were utilised as Capital Expenditure. The credit of Mizoram during the year 2019-2020 was Rs.8,678.26 crores which increased to Rs. 9,881.09 crores during the year 2020-2021.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 08.09.2022_

**Areca nut seized:** On 6.9.2022, Saitual police seized 120 bags of areca nuts from one truck at Keifang town which were suspected to have been smuggled from Myanmar. The driver of the truck and suspected to be the owner of the areca nuts, Lalenkawla (36) s/o Romawia (L), New Champhai was also apprehended. The seized illegal items, which were 9,600 Kilograms in weight were estimated to be worth Rs. 7,80,000.

C._Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 08.09.2022_

****Meth Seized :** Personnel of District Special Branch (DSB) under Aizawl S.P on the evening of 6.9.2022 seized 3,80,000 tablets of suspected Methamphetamine which were packed in 38 bags in Aizawl from Laldika (52) Khawmawi village, Myanmar along with the owner the tea stall from where they were seized, Laldinsanga (37) Armed Veng, Aizawl. The seized items were estimated to be worth around Rs. 9.5 crores in the international market.**
