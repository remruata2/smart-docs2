A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 6.8.2013

HPC(D) & Mizoram Government to meet on 14th August 2013: HPC(D) and Mizoram Government will resume peace talk on 14th August, 2103 at Aizawl. Home Department said that HPC (D) delegates had been informed about the impending date, time and place. For the upcoming meeting, the meeting of Council of Ministers had decided that Mizoram Government delegates will be led by Mizoram Government Secretary and not Joint Secretary of Home Department.

 

B. Back page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.8.2013

Mizoram Police going strong: Mizoram Police FC beat Chaltlang FC by 6-1 yesterday in the on-going I-Day Football Tournament. Mizoram Police team is Group C champion with a record of beating all the teams in their group and will play against Group D runner up to reach the Semi Final.
