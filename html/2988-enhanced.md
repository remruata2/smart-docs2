****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/206

Dated: 25th March, 2019.

 Information has been received that a joint meeting among Village Councils and NGOs of Tuichawng and Belpei (Matrisora) villages, Lunglei District was held on 24.03.2019 @ 2:00 p.m at Tuichawngchhuah which was presided over by Pu Mausing, VCP of Tuichawng and attended by 33(thirty three) members. The meeting was held in pursuance of the instruction of DC, Lunglei in a meeting held on 21.03.2019 at Lunglei with the VCPs of Tuichawng and Belpei villages. The meeting discussed about the shifting of the settlers of Tuichawngchhuah to other places. After due deliberation, the meeting adopted the following:

  The meeting expressed their unwillingness to violate the Hon’ble Guwahati High Court order which stay the Government’s eviction order, but further expressed their readiness to shift from Tuichawngchhuah if the Government issued a written order. The meeting, on the other hand, demanded the following:

1. House site for settlers of Tuichawngchhuah since there is no site for construction of houses within Tuichawng village.
2. Financial assistance/ compensation for construction of house.
3. To locate the reserve area near the garden of Pu Rupendralal for house site
