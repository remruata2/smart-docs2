****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/034

Dated: 14th January, 2020.

 In continuation of our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/031 dated 13.1.2020, NGOs of Bungtlang South organized procession today, i.e. 14.1.2020 @ 2:30 p.m from Vaseikai to Nghalimlui, Lawngtlai District in protest against the alleged murder of Lalrawnliana (32) S/o A. Lalhmuaka of Bungtlang South, whose dead body was found on 11.1.2020 near Nghalimlui. The procession, wherein about 500 (five hundred) people from Bungtlang South participated, ended today @ 3:00 p.m. They were also joined by around 10 (ten) leaders of Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) led by Pu J. Sangthangpuia, President, CYLA and around 5 (five) Lai Student Association (LSA) leaders.

 During the procession, the crowd chanted slogans expressing their demand for arrest of the real culprit(s) at the earliest and vehemently condemns the incident. In the meantime, the crowd suddenly attacked the houses of Nghalimlui by pelting stones and as per information received, as many as 47 (forty seven) out of 260 (two hundred and sixty) households were damaged by the crowd. These houses were mostly private owned Assam Type buildings and thatched roof and are located near the road side. Fortunately, there was no reported human casualty due to the incident.

 The crowd left Nghalimlui village after the procession without any further incident.

Situation is under close watch.
