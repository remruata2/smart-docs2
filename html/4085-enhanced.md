****PROFILE OF LALRINAWMA (AWMTEA)****

Name   : Lalrinawma alias Awmtea, Nissi

Age  : 26

Father’s Name : R. Dinliana, ex-Servicemen

Mother’s Name : Lalchhuanmawii, Teacher at Samlukhai Primary School-II

Address : Samlukhai, Aizawl District

Edu. qualification  : B.Com

Occupation : Cement dealer

 Lalrinawma alias Awmtea is a self-proclaimed prophet who hails from Samlukhai village, Aizawl District. As per his statement, he was touched by the Holy Spirit since 27th September, 2007 after he went for fasting and prayer at a prayer mountain in Samlukhai village. Since then, he and his team went around Mizoram to proclaim the words and prophecy he allegedly received from God.

 On 9.3.2024, Awmtea and his friend/team member Ephraim Lalawmpuia of Ramlaitui, Lunglei District, chanted a slogan near the Parliament House, New Delhi. Awmtea said that their main slogan was that BJP Government is chosen by God to rule over India for the time being and God has been using them. While Awmtea was chanting a prophecy received by him from God near the Parliament House, Delhi Police had reprotdly apprehended him and he was brought to Parliament Street Police Station for interrogation.

 Awmtea and Ephraim had reached New Delhi on 7.3.2024 to seek appointment with the Prime Minister to inform the prophecy received by him. However, as they cannot reportedly get the appointment, Awmtea chanted the prophecy he received from God near the Parliament.

 Some of the prophecies that Awmtea had allegedly received from God and proclaimed by him in the public are as under:

1. In 2011, Awmtea reportedly received prophecy and proclaimed that BJP Party led by Shri Narendra Modi will win 2014 Lok Sabha election.

1. On 1st November, 2014, Awmtea dug a hole (grave) about 2 feet deep at Samlukhai village and tried to lay down there. He said that this symbolizes that there will be war, natural calamities and other problems in India and many people will lost their lives.
2. On 21st September, 2017 at around 12:00 noon, Awmtea and his team members, numbering about 7 (seven) had burnt the effigy of Hindu god/goddess and Indian National Flag near YMA Office, Venglai, Lunglei. A case vide Lunglei PS C/no.155 Dt.22.9.2017 u/s 295-A/120B IPC r/w 2 of The Prevention of Insult to National Honour Act 1971 was registered. Awmtea said that burning of statue of Hindu God (Murti) was to demonstrate that there are people who dare to defend Christianity and to safeguard Mizoram from transforming into a Hindu state while BJP Government has allegedly taken initiative to transform India and Mizoram into a Hindu state. He also clarified that the burning of the Tricolor denotes that there will be war ahead and the war will develop unity and brotherhood among Mizo ethnic tribes from different regions and subsequently Mizoram will attain independence from India.

The JMFC, Lunglei had sentenced the seven accused persons namely Lalrinawma, R.Lalhruaitluanga, Vanlalfela, Rodingliana, C.Lalrammuankima, Zohmingmawia and Lalruattluanga to pay a fine of Rs. 1000/- each IDSI for 10 days each for the offence U/S 2 of the Prevention of Insults to National Honour Act, 1971. Furthermore, the seven accused persons were sentenced to pay a fine of Rs. 1000/- each IDSI for 10 days each for the offence U/S 120 B(2) IPC.

On the night of 2nd January, 2022, Awmtea presented Puandum (Black shawl), a Mizo traditional black shawl usually wear during funeral procession, to Pu Zoramthanga, the then Chief Minister of Mizoram and wrapped around him while he was attending evening church service at Zarkawt Presbyterian Church. Awmtea said that this signifies that the tenure of Pu Zoramthanga led MNF Government will end in the next election.
