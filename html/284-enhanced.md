Subject: Security threat assessment in respect of Pu Hmingchungnunga, Chairman  Sinlung Hills Development Council(SHDC) for provision of House Guard.

Ref: Memo No.SEC/R-23/2012/308 dt.28.3.2014.

Sir,

  With reference to the subject cited above, I am sending herewith the assessment report and recommendation for your kind information and necessary action.

Inspr. Lalrinthanga Ralte and SI P.Vanlalruata,CID (SB) conducted Security threat assessment in respect of Pu Hmingchungnunga, Chairman SHDC for provision of House Guard . It is found that there has been intense rivalry between Pu Hmingchungnunga and his political rivalries i.e HPC (D), etc and even on 22.33.2014 a powerful bomb was exploded at the main entrance of the residence of Pu Hmingchungnunga at Aizawl causing a threat to his life.

In view of the above assessment report it is recommended that Pu.Hmingchungnunga, Chairman SHDC may be given House Guard. However, his scale of security may be reviewed in consultation with Home Department, Government of Mizoram.
