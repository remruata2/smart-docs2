A. Front page of  THE AIZAWL POST  (H.RAMTHLENGLIANA) inside page of the THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 10.6.2013

MPSOA wants Government to cancel Gazetted Status : Special extended meeting of MPSOA held on 8.6.2013 at Aizawl PS Conference Hall discussed with the Government’s order of upgradation of Inspector of Police to Group B Gazetted status depriving them of their privileges like Ration Allowance and Leave Compensatory Allowance which they are enjoying now. MPSOA Special extended meeting decided to request the government to withdraw its order of upgrading Inspector of Police to Gazetted status.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 10.6.2013

Attempt on Sairang MRB robbery failed: Some unidentified miscreants burgled Sairang MRB by breaking through the main door. They tried to break the safe where money is kept but to no avail and took around ` 1,000/- from a drawer.

The burglary was noticed only yesterday at 7:00AM in the morning, Sairang Police started investigation and FSL, Aizawl examined the place of occurrence.

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 10.6.2013

FIR lodged against Jet Airways On 5th June, 2013, Jet Airways flights of Lengpui, Guwahati and Kolkatta were cancelled without giving prior information to the passengers, no additional flight was arranged for the ticket holders/buyers and as the perplexed passengers were not properly received by Jet Airways staff, an FIR was lodged against Jet Airways at Sairang PS by around 20 displeased youths.

 

D. Front page of NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 10.6.2013

Cop shoots self: Constable NL Khama s/o Vialdova, Sesih of 5th IR Bn. posted at Tuipuibari BOP in Mamit District shots himself with his service rifle (5.56mm INSAS Automatic Rifle) at around 1:30AM on 9.6.2013.

            NL Khama died instantly as the bullet of the self-loading rifle pierced through his skull from his lower jaw. His lifeless body was lying in a pool of blood with his rifle beside him when his colleagues found him. NL Khama was recently estranged from his wife, which could be the reason behind his taking the extreme step.
