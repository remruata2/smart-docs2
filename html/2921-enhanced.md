****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/101

Dated: 14th February, 2019.

Reference to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/83 dated 07.02.2019: Information has been received that 4 (four) leaders of All Bru NGOs of Tripura camps headed by A. Sawibunga, President, Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Forum (MBDPF) met Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM), Kanchanpur at his Office yesterday, i.e. 13.02.2019 in which they discussed about the Memorandum they had submitted to the Union Government on 07.02.2019. The SDM informed them that he had taken immediate action and had forwarded the Memorandum to the District Collector, North Tripura on the same day and assured them that free ration and cash dole will be resumed immediately.

As assured by the SDM, Kanchanpur, cash dole for three months, i.e. November, 2018 – January, 2019 has been disbursed from today, i.e. 14.02.2019 along with the distribution of free ration. As the situation being such, the Bru NGOs had cancelled their entire action plan for agitation which was intended to commence from 18.02.2019.
