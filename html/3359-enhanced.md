****CID (Special Branch)****

1. **BRIEF HISTORY OF THE UNIT :**

The State CID (Special Branch) came into being in the year 1973 and Pu AK Roy, APS was the first Superintendent of Police of CID (SB). The Headquarter office is set up at CID Complex, Bungkawn, Aizawl and it is headed by Sr. Superintendent of Police under the supervision and guidance of DIG (CID). CID (SB) is playing an important role in collection and dissemination of intelligence pertaining to the movement of insurgents, illegal trafficking of arms and other anti-social elements, bearing security of the state on law and order problem. At present, CID (SB) is having 16(sixteen) Sector Offices at various strategic locations within the state.

**II. ORGANISATIONAL SET UP:**

1. **SUBSIDIARY MULTI AGENCY CENTRE (SMAC) :**

The Subsidiary Multi Agency Centre is established in the Special Branch as one of the centres amongst various Intelligence Agencies under Ministry of Home Affairs, for effective means of sharing intelligence inputs among various intelligence Agencies.

1. **TECHNICAL BRANCH :**

 Technical Branch came into existence as a separate entity on 16thApril, 2010 with installation of modern surveillance equipments which yielded immeasurable results in giving timely information and detection of Police cases. Apart from the technical surveillance done by this unit, necessary required surveillance assistance have been extended to other sister Units.

1. **FOREIGNERS REGISTRATION OFFICE (FRO)/VERIFICATION BRANCH :**

 FRO oversee Foreigners entering into Mizoram and subsequent movement inside the state, and their time of departure from Mizoram. It also deals with verification of Characters and Antecedents (C/A) of Passport applicants from Mizoram and also newly appointed persons to government jobs. The FRO/VB is having a direct link with Foreigners Division, Ministry of Home Affairs, Delhi for on-line verification to Pakistan nationals and post verification of overseas citizens of India (OCI).

1. **RESERVE BRANCH :**

 Reserve Branch mainly looks after administration, service matters of staff, deployment and discipline of the unit. It is also functioning as Armourer of the unit by maintaining the present holding of Arms and ammunitions.

1. **CONFIDENTIAL BRANCH :**

 The Confidential Branch is synonymous with the information centre of the unit. All information either general or intelligence collected are processed, analyzed and stored in this Branch.

1. **MT BRANCH :**

 MT Branch deals with vehicles and mobility of the Unit. Apart from maintaining unit vehicles, this branch also looks after power generator of the unit.

1. **ESTABLISHMENT AND ACCOUNT BRANCH :**

 This Branch deals with all Financial Accounts, Transaction of Unit, Stationery, Pension files and Service matters of all Ministerial staffs.

1. **PERSONAL BRANCH :**

The main function of Personal Branch is publication and dispatch of Daily Summary of Information (DSI), Special Report (SR) and Confidential letters purported to the knowledge of SP. It also looks after library of the Unit and other important documents.

1. **FIELD BRANCH :**

 Field staff deals with collection of timely information/intelligence and monitoring of activities of various political parties, NGOs, Student organizations, Labour &amp; Services, Insurgent groups, Arms smuggling and anti-social elements bearing security of the state and law and order problem.
