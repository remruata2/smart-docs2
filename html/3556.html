20.5.2022 : Mizoram Government Nursing Service Association (MGNSA) held its 21st Biennial General Conference on 20.5.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at Dawrpui Multipurpose Centre, Aizawl. Pi Lalfakawmi Fanai and Pi Lalhruaitluangi Pachuau presided the conference and around 300 (three hundred) members attended. Dr. R Lalthangliana, Health Minister graced the function as Chief Guest and they passed the following resolutions:

            (i)        The concerned authority should be requested for granting of 13 months pay in line with Special Compensatory Allowance (Police)/Honorarium (Synod Hospital, Durtlang), for nurses under the state government.

            (ii)       Necessary action should be taken for grant of Group B Gazetted Status to Tutor/PHNO/Ward Superintendent.

            (iii)     Necessary action should be taken for setting up of additional Nurses Sick Room at Civil Hospital Aizawl and ZMC.

            (iv)      The state government should be requested to make State Award for Mizoram Nursing Service.

            (v)       Immediate necessary action should be taken for establishment of separate Nursing Directorate.

            (vi)      Exam Centre should be set up at Lunglei for candidates of southern Mizoram in Nurse Recruitment.
