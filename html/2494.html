REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT

 

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/570

Dated:14th October,2020  

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/566 Dt. 13.10.2020, A joint meeting of DC, Mamit and DC, Karimganj was held today i.e., 14.10.2020 @ 1:00 p.m. at BNRGSK Hall, Kanhmun, Mamit District. The meeting was attended by         Dr. Lalrozama, IAS, DC, Mamit, Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, IPS, DIG (NR),             Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP Mamit, Pu David H. Lalthangliana, OSD cum Dy. Secretary, Home Dept., Shri Anbamuthan M.P, IAS, DC Karimganj, Shri Dilip Kumar Dey, IPS, DIG (SR), Silchar, Assam, Shri Mayank Kumar, SP, Karimganj and other officials from Mizoram and Assam Government. After detailed round of deliberation the meeting decided the following :

  1. Issues regarding demarcation of the border shall be taken up by respective state government to the competent higher authority.
  2. Deployment of police forces in the concerned area shall be looked into by both State Governments so as to minimise any untoward tension while maintaining law and order situation. 
  3. Both governments on either side shall work towards ensuring peace and tranquillity in the area until the border issues are resolved.

Around 200 (two hundred) volunteers including Pu Lalrindika Ralte, local MLA participated in a community work organized by MZP General Headquarters today i.e., 14.10.2020 at Zaite Road, Thinghlun for reconstruction of Pu John Zolawma’s Jhum Hut at Zaite Road, Mizoram-Assam border which was burnt down by Assam Police and Forest Department Officials on 9.10.2020. They concluded the community work for today around 3:30 p.m. under peaceful atmosphere and will continue tomorrow.

Today i.e., 14.10.2020 around 7:30 a.m. Assam Police and Forest Department Officials were seen stationed near Zaite Village, Assam and conducted surveillance of Mizoram Police camp by engaging 2 (two) drones.

Pu Lalbiakthanga Khiangte, DIG (NR), Dr. Lalrozama, DC Mamit and               Pu Shasank Jaiswal, SP Mamit also visited the site in the evening.

            Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
