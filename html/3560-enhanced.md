****21.4.2022 :** Pu Lalthansanga, Administrator, Mizo Hmeichhe Insuihkhawm Pawl (MHIP) General Headquarters in his letter issued to all MHIP Sub-Headquarters/Blocks/Joints/Branches on 21.4.2022 stated that MHIP General Assembly, which is scheduled to be held on 28.4.2022 has been cancelled as State government has put administration of MHIP General Headquarters under Administrator for a period of 6 (six) months w.e.f. 19.4.2022 as per the provision of The Mizoram Societies Registration Act, 2005, Sec 24 A(1)(b). It also stated that during the stated period, MHIP office should function as usual and all administrative works, income and expenses incurred should be properly recorded.**

 It may be mentioned that MHIP General Assembly cum Election should be held during the above mentioned period.

**29.9.2022 :** Zonuam Branch MHIP in its Press Release issued yesterday evening, i.e., 29.9.2022 expressed their demand for immediate arrest of the culprit(s) on alleged sexual assault incident against minor child of their locality. It also condemned the mother of the said minor child for not knowing such incident. It further appealed the authority to continue to take necessary action for immediate arrest of the culprit(s). In this connection, they intend to organize procession on 1.10.2022 @1:00 pm from Zonuam Junction and appealed the general public to participate in the procession.

**03.10.2022 :** Procession was organized on 1.10.2022 @ 1:00 pm under the aegis of Zonuam Branch MHIP in demand for immediate arrest of the culprit(s) on alleged sexual assault incident against minor child, and around 500 (five hundred) persons participated. During the procession, they displayed placards demanding immediate arrest of the culprit(s), and strongly condemned occurrence of the such incident within their locality, and they also shouted slogans such as strong condemnation of occurrence of serious rape incident, protection of children is the responsibility of parents, the culprit(s) of the said crime should immediately be arrested, etc. The procession was started from Zonuam Kawn and they proceeded upto Zonuam Point, and the crowd dispersed under peaceful atmosphere the same afternoon @ 2:40 pm after passing a resolution that the culprit(s) of the said rape incident against minor child should immediately be arrested and awarded suitable punishment.
**07.12.2022 :** Mizo Hmeichhe Insuihkhawm Pawl (MHIP) Sub-Headquarters, Mamit in its Press Release issued on 7.12.2022 strongly condemned the murder of Pi Rokimi (51) and her grandson Mark Lalawmpuia (7) on 6.12.2022.
