A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.2.2014

Mc Dowells Rum and Heroin Seized: 800 bottles of Mc Dowells Rum worth ` 4,80,000/- were seized by Aizawl SP’s District Special Branch in the middle of the night of 26.2.2014 under the culvert near Buichali Bridge. Jilal Uddin (26) of Karimganj owned 500 bottles and Gias Uddin (34) owned 300 bottles and they were also arrested.

            Meanwhile, at around 5:00pm yesterday, a joint team of Special Narcotic PS Staff under CID(Crime) and DC(V) team, Lunglei seized 10 grams of heroin in the corridor of Kapsanga building, Saron Veng and its alleged owner Darneihkimi (35) d/o Vanlalnghaka of Tahan ‘C’ group was arrested.

 

A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 28.2.2014

Youth Body Found: Saienga (22) s/o Saichhunga (L) of Zonuam locality in Aizawl was found dead near Ch.Saprawnga Truck terminal at Rangvamual on 26.2.2014 at 11:50pm. Blood oozed out from the nose and ear, and a deep injury mark was found on the forehead. Case has been registered at Vaivakawn Police Station.
