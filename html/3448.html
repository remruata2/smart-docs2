12.7.2022             A team of 3 (three) Assam Rifles personnel conducted raid at Zawngling village, Siaha district, close to the India-Myanmar border and apprehended two cadres suspected to be Myanmar-based outlawed outfit, Maraland Defence Force (MDF) viz., Bie Bie
(25 yrs) s/o Ngodi and Thatha (20 yrs) s/o Zubi (both belonging to Sosai village of Myanmar) and recovered the below mentioned items from their possession:

                (i)           1 (one) China-made motorcycle.

                (ii)          20 Nos. of 12 guage 70mm Cartridge.

                (iii)         Myanmar currency of 11,100/- Kyat.

                (iv)         Indian currency of Rs.650/-

                (v)          1 (one) mobile handset (Redmi).

                The above mentioned apprehended persons and recovered items were produced at Tipa Police Station and accordingly registered Case vide Tipa PS C/No. 7/2022
Dt. 12.7.2022 u/s 25 (1B) (a) Arms Act r/w 14 'A' Foreigners Act & u/s 192 of Motor Vehicles Act.
