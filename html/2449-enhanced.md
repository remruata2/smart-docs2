****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/360

Dated 5th May, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/358 Dt. 4.05.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****8 (eight) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-3, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 5.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-11, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-54, MMV-Nil, LMV-16, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

105 (one hundred and five) vehicles (HMV-37, MMV-24, LMV-16, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-1, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
IR personnel on duty at Vairengte Auto Stand restricted 7 (seven) Assam Forest personnel and 6 (six) Assam Police Armed personnel led by Bewry Bodo, Dholai Forest RO, who intend to visit ongoing construction of houses at various house sites, which were allotted by Vairengte VC-I near Vairengte Auto Stand today, i.e., 5.5.2021 @ 1:00 p.m. from further entry into Mizoram and they left the area for Assam around 1:10 p.m. this afternoon. Upon learning the matter, Pu Ramtharnghaka, SDPO Vairengte and party immediately rushed to the area. Assam officials already left the area by the time SDPO, Vairengte and party reached the area. SDPO, Vairengte informed BSF personnel on duty at the border not to allow such kind of Assam officials from further entry into Mizoram in future and to inform him immediately in case of any attempt to enter into Mizoram.
1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
