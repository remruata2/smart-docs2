 In pursuance of Home Department, Mizoram letter No. F.20015/1/2020-HM(MT) dated 2nd September, 2020 regarding Inter-State boundary dispute between Mizoram and Tripura - Choice/Opinion of the Phuldungsei, Jampui Villagers, the following officers of CID(SB) are hereby directed to conduct discreet enquiry from public bodies, NGOs and relevant stakeholders of Phuldungsei, Jampui Village and ascertain their opinion regarding inclusion of Phuldungsei Village within the ambit of the Govt. of Mizoram or whether they wish to remain under the administrative control of the Govt. of Tripura.
