A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 28 & 29.08.2022

92% of works compleated at Setlak Water Treatment Plant : On 26.8.2022, Dr. Renu Sharma, Chief Secretary, Mizoram visited Augmentation of Water Supply and Construction of 37 MLD Water Treatment Plant at Setlak,Tanhril which is a project undertaken by SITMIU. The Chief Secretary was received by  SIPMIU Project Director, Pu Lalrothanga who explained that 92% of the project, which is expected to meet the water requirement of Aizawl city till 2033, has been completed. This water plant is destined to feed the population of northern side of the city and will be able to generate 370 lakhs litres of water per day and will doubled the amount of water received by Aizawl city.

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 28 & 29.08.2022

Teacher arrested for sending home a student naked : PC Lalbiakengi, Tlangpui primary school SSA teacher, Lunglei district, who was accused of sending home one of her student naked was arrested by police on 27.8 2022 (Saturday). According to information received, based on an FIR lodged by District Child Protection Unit (DCPU), Lunglei as a result of the above mentioned allegation, Lunglei Police station arrested the accused and booked her under Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection) Act Section 75. Meanwhile, Tlangpui Village Council, YMA and MHIP issued a press statement accusing Lalnunsangi, mother of the victim/student, of fabricating false information through social media and that she (the mother) was acting in contravention of the school norms and was responsible for the said allegation.

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI /The Aizawl Post newspapers  Dt. 28 & 29.08. 2022

321 units of blood donated  : On 27.8.2022 (Saturday), various voluntary organisations of Mizoram donated 321 units of blood at different hospitals. Mizoram Gorkha Association donated 71 units and 40 units at Civil Hospital, Aizawl and Kolasib District Hospital respectively. Other organisations who  donated their blood at Civil Hospital, Aizawl were -Mizo Chelsea Supporters Club (78 units), Bualpui NG Welfare,Aizawl (22 units), Chaltlang Branch YMA (64 units) and T Romana College NSS (100 units). At the same time, KTP Kanan Veng Branch also donated 41 units of blood at Synod Hospital, Aizawl.
