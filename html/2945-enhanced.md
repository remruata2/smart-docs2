****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/401

Dated: 12th June, 2019.

** In continuation of our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/384 dated 11.6.2019: Information has been received that a Public Meeting was held last night, i.e. 11.6.2019 @ 7:30 p.m at Zote Community Hall which was presided over by Pu R. Hrangthanga, VC President and attended by about 300 (three hundred) villagers. The meeting unanimously formed Joint Action Committee****(JAC) to pursue their demands to the Government. The leaders of the JAC are as follows :-**

 Chairman  : Pu R. Hrangthanga

 Vice Chairman : Pu T. Laikunga

 Secretary  : Pu B. Zoramthanga

 Asst. Secretary : Pu K. Laltana

 Treasurer  : Pu Rochuailova

 Fin. Secretary : Pu K. Lalhundika

 Joint Secretaries : 1. Pu K. Lalsawia

  2. Pu Ngurchuailova

 Committee members: Executive Committee Members of all NGOs

 The meeting adopted a resolution which expressed their disappointment on the resolution of the Cabinet Meeting of Mizoram Government held recently which allowed shifting of the office of District &amp; Session Court from IIDC, Zote to CHANEM Building, Champhai and urged the Government to revoke its decision.
