****THREAT PERCEPTION ASSESSMENT****

 Presently, Pu C.Lalrosanga, MP is staying at Tuikual North, Aizawl as he had completed his Covid-19 home quarantine on Dt.23.6.2020. It is learnt that all the necessary tests undergone by him were negative and he received Discharged Certificate from the concerned medical officer.

 During the enquiry, it is learnt that there is no issue regarding CAB/CAA and no precautionary advices were received by him &amp; his family from known persons or from unknown persons. Pu C. Lalrosanga, MP felt neither a sense of uneasiness or discomfort towards his safety and that of his family. Upon further enquiry, the said MP C. Lalrosanga had neither received any direct or indirect life threatening words nor received any threat from NGOs, Student organisation etc.

 Taking into account the security threat that could emanates from different angles, it is concluded that the threat perceived either direct or indirect is minimal and no possibility of a security breach at present. Therefore, in the present situation, it is concluded that it may not be necessary to retain the House Guard i.e. One Havildar &amp; four Constables which is provided to him.
