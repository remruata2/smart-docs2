A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspapers  Dt. 19.08. 2022

 

Credit facility at GNRC stopped due to pending of payment by Government : Officers from 3rd Mizo IR Bn. Issued notice to their staff regarding the temporary termination of credit facility at Guwahati Neurological Research Centre (GNRC) due to non payment of bill for quite a while. Sources said, “We got message from GNRC today stating that they had not received hospital/treatment bill from Mizoram Government since June 2021, due to which the accumulate debts have spike up high.” Sources told our correspondence that this does not mean that credit facility will be stop permanently, had the Government pay its debts, then credit facility will be avail as before.

 

B. Front page of  The Aizawl Post  newspaper  Dt. 19.08.2022

 

Tax resistance will be dealt with accordingly : Stern action will be taken against those who evade, refuse or neglect to pay tax to the Government. Action may include freezing their bank account, seized valuable asset or bid their asset, said Kailiana, Commissioner of Tax. On Thursday, Mizoram Taxation Department held a consultation meeting on Goods & Services Tax (GST) with officers from different Banks inside Mizoram and DDO’s (Drawing and Disbursing Officer).
