ACHIEVEMENT OF CID (SB) WEF 1st JANUARY, 2020 TO 28th DECEMBER, 2020

 

I.       On 4.3.2020, SI. Lalchhuanmawia of CID(SB) with his party performed duty at Zemabawk 'Tawngtaina mual' and recovered 122 grams of suspected Heroin, total value of Rs.5,00,000/- (Local market rate) from the possession of Lalpekhlua 35yrs s/o K. II.     On 11.3.2020 @ 2:15pm, SI KC Joseph Vanlalpeka of CID(SB) and his team recovered  and seized 5 nos. of .22 (AK model, made in China, serviceable), 10 nos. of magazine, 3 nos. of Rifle sling and 1 no. of mobile phone - LAVA Y1 which was consealed by Sanglianzuala (27) S/o Bilthawna of Chamdur P - 1, Lawngtlai District at the garden of Lalthakima of Dawrpui located at Thuampui, Liansailova Road. III.  On 29.5.2020, an operation team of CID(SB) seized 50.08 grams (4 plastic soap cases & 1 penicillin bottle) strongly suspected to be Heroin worth Rs. 3,00,000/- from the possession of Lalnunmawi(24) S/o Lalnuntluanga of Bawngkawn East at Bawngkawn East. IV.    On 12.6.2020, CID(SB) Operation team recovered and seized 40 soap cases containing suspected Heroin weighing 496.8 grams valued at Rs.30,00,000/-(thirty lakhs) at the local smuggling rate from the vehicle Datsun RediGo (white) B/R No. MZ 01R-2267 which was driven by C. Lalramthanga (32) s/o Hrangsaithanga of Aizawl Venglai, Aizawl along with one passenger, Vanlalnghaka (23) s/o Vanlalkulha of Zokhawthar, Champhai at the outskirt of Tuikhurhlu.

          All the 40 soap cases containing suspected Heroin and the vehicle used to transport the same, Datsun (RediGo) B/R No. MZ01 R 2267 alongwith documents was then seized from the possession of the suspected persons in presence reliable witnesses.

          On careful interrogation, the accused Vanlalnghaka revealed that out of 40 boxes, 15 boxes have to be delivered to one person at Tuirial Airfield. Hence, the operation team proceeded towards Tuirial Airfield and then Vanlalnghaka informed the person who would be collecting the Heroin without telling him his situation. Then, one person later known as Hallunga (60) s/o KeiluaiTuirial Airfield Vengthar came out from the village and approached the vehicle and on careful interrogation, he admitted that he came out to collect those Heroins which was sent for him, and also admitted that he had collected Heroin on previous consignments and then he was also apprehended.

          The accused and all the seized articles along with the vehicle used for transportation were brought and handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S, Aizawl, and FIR was also lodged against the accused. In this connection, Spl. Narcotic PS C/No. 20/2020 Dt.12.06.2020 U/s 21(c)/25/29 ND& PS Act was registered.
