****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/903

Dated: 17th December, 2021

A fire broke out from Election Branch, DC Office, Aizawl early this morning i.e., 17.12.2021 @ around 3:15 a.m. Upon learning the stated incident, Fire &amp; Emergency Services was informed @ 4:05 a.m. by Armed Police personnel guarding the DC Office complex and accordingly, Fire &amp; Emergency Services rushed to the spot. However, despite their best efforts to douse the raging fire, the fire escalated and as a result of which the Election Branch, Planning Branch, Arms Branch, ILP Branch, Judicial Branch, Stationery Branch, Census Branch, Establishment Branch, Nazir, DC Office Chamber, 2 (two) Addl. DC Office Chambers, SDO (Sadar) Office Chamber and 2 (two) vehicles viz. Maruti Suzuki Gypsy B/R No. MZ-01 A-8866 and Maruti Suzuki Gypsy MZ-01 A-5966 (both are under process for condemnation) were burned down to ashes.

Static Guard Duty (C/16151 Vanlalnghaka, 3rd MAP, DC Main Gate Duty and C/81 Lalbiaksanga, 3rd MAP, Strong Room duty) stated that they heard fire crackling sound @ around 3:15 - 3:30 a.m. and upon checking the source of the sound, it was found that Election Branch, DC Office was engulfed with fire. Their Arms and Ammunitions were evacuated to Court premises and as instructed, PC Lalchhuanvawra, Court’s Static Guard Duty called Fire &amp; Emergency Services. Security personnel present at the spot tried to extinguish the fire using Fire Extinguishers available at the spot, but in vain. However, Fire &amp; Emergency Services managed to save District Emergency Centre and some office buildings situated at the northern site of the DC Aizawl Office Complex.

 The exact cause of fire is not known at the time of writing the report. However, it is believed that the fire broke out from Election Branch, DC, Aizawl Office. It is also reported that staff of Election Branch left the office early this morning @ around 1:00 a.m. due to election related work.
