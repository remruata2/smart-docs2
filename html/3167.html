A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES, THE AIZAWL POST & NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 20.12.2013

Kidnappers issue fresh warning: Kidnappers of two Mizo drivers and a telecom company employee today called up the families of the kidnapped Mizos and expressed their anger over the Mizoram Government's 'apathy' to their demand. If the Mizoram Government remains indifferent to their demand, it would be harmful for the hostages. A caller identified himself as Lalduhawma who worked for the militant said the kidnappers will not be responsible for whatever happens to the hostages if their ransom emand of Rs. 5 crores is not paid before Christmas.

                        Meanwhile, Central YMA leader meet Chief Minister of Mizoram and requested the Government to take step to rescue the abducted before Christmas.

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 20.12.2013

Protection Squad: In Lunglei, 'Protection Squad' to secure peaceful celebration of Christmas and New Year festival within Lunglei town was formed on 19.12.2013. The Protection Squad would be on high alert especially on the night of 24.12.2013 and 31.12.2013.
