SUBMISSION OF DEPARTMENTAL ENQUIRY REPORT IN RESPECT OF ASI R. KAPZUALA OF CID (SB)

MIZORAM, AIZAWL

 

 

INTRODUCTION:-

 

            A departmental proceeding was initiated against ASI R. Kapzuala of CID(SB) vide order No. RSB/60/2009/ 2449 dt.25/11/2009. Charged  memorandum No. RSB/60-DE/1721 dt.9.10.2009 was issued to the charge official and acknowledged by the charge official giving his signature on the same day ie.9/10/2009.

 

ARTICLE OF THE CHARGES:- As per annexure I, II, III

 

ANNEXURE –I:       LIST OF ARTICLE OF CHARGE FRAMED AGAINST ASI R. KAPZUALA (u/s) CID (SB), MIZORAM

 

ARTICLE –I 

 

            That on Dt. 7.7.2009 ASI R. Kapzuala, CID (SB) knowingly received Fake Indian Currency Note (FICN) amounting to Rs. 66,000/- (Sixty six thousand only) from VL Remruata s/o Darnghaka, Durtlang Leitan which he had accepted and kept in his possession more than a month with ulterior motive and having a vested interest by not taking immediate steps which is a must for Police Officer in which he did not arrest the person who handed him the FICN and not seize the FICN at the spot for further necessary legal action.

 

            The above act of ASI R. Kapzuala amounts to a grave misconduct, gross negligence dereliction of duties unbecoming of a Police Officer and thereby rendering himself liable for serious disciplinary action.

 

ARTICLE – II

           

            That ASI R. Kapzuala, CID (SB) had tried to illegally disposed off FICN amounting to Rs. 50,000/- (Fifty thousand) which was in his possession through Sangneihthanga of Zuangtui, Aizawl at the rate of Rs. 80/- per Rs. 100/- of FICN.

                       

            The above act of ASI R. Kapzuala amounts to a grave misconduct, unbecoming of a Police Officer and thereby rendering himself liable for serious disciplinary action.

 

 

 

 

 

ANNEXURE –II:     STATEMENT OF IMPUTATION OF MISCONDUCT OR BEHAVIOUR IN SUPPORT OF THE ARTICLES FRAMED AGAINST ASI R. KAPZUALA (U/S) CID (SB), MIZORAM

 

ARTICLE-I:-

 

            That on the month of May this year (2009) ASI R. Kapzuala, CID (SB) who is highly interested in speculative antique and relic business went to Luimawi village with his business partners to buy alleged bullet proof stone. In connection with this ongoing business, on Dt. 7.7.09 ASI R. Kapzuala knowingly received FICN amounting to Rs. 66,000/- (Sixty six thousand only) from his colleague VL Remruata s/o Darnghaka of Durtlang Leitan which he had accepted and kept in his possession more than a month with ulterior motive and having a vested interest by not taking immediate steps which is a must for Police Officer in which he did not arrest the person who handed him the FICN and not seize the FICN at the spot for further necessary legal action.

 

ARTICLE-II:-

 

            That on Dt. 14.8.09 Bawngkawn Police arrested two suspected persons namely Sangneihthanga of Zuangtui, Aizawl and Hermanthanga of Suangpuilawn and recovered 47 nos of FICN of Rs. 1000/- denominations from their possession. Investigation revealed that ASI R. Kapzuala of CID (SB) had willfully given them the FICN amounting to Rs. 50,000/- (Fifty thousand) out of Rs. 66,000/- (Sixty six thousand) which he had received from V.L. Remruata of Durtlang Leitan and tried to illegally sell at the rate of Rs. 80/- per Rs. 100/- of FICN through the arrested accused. In this connection Bawngkawn PS C/No. 365/09 Dt. 15.8.09 u/s 498B/489C IPC was registered.

 

            The above act of ASI R. Kapzuala amounts to a grave misconduct, unbecoming of a Police Officer and thereby rendering himself liable for serious disciplinary action.

2. Statement of Sangneihthanga of Zuangtui, Aizawl:-

 

            Mualbawk khua (Mizoram) ah piangin kum riat vel lek ka nihin Burma-ah kan pema, Leipanchhawng-ah kan khawsa a, 2001 khan Mzioramah rawn kir lehin Zokhawthar-ah kum thum vel ka awma, chumi hnu chuan Aizawl-ah rawn chhuk lehin Vanthangpuii of More (Manipur) chu nupuiah neiin Chanmari-ah ka khawsa in thawmhnaw zuar in ei kan zawng ve a ni.

 

            March 2008 khan Pu Thanzuala of Bawngkawn thlanmual veng in luah in ka khawsa a, Pu Thanzuala hi Police niin CID (SB) Aizawl a awm ani a, a thian thenkhat CID (SB) mi leh sa te pawh hmelhriat ka nei ve a chung zinga pakhat chu Pu Kapzuala hi a ni

            June/08 vel khan ka in luah insawn in Zuangtui ah ka in sawn a ni. Aug/09 thlatir vel khan Pu Kapzuala hi a thian pahnih te nen rawn lengin, chiahpuam, silai leh No. 4 man tur ka hriat leh hriat loh min zawt a, pawisa a awm chuan han han zawn mai an tur a awm ka rin thuin ka chhang a. Pawisa lam chu ngaihtuah theih dan a zawn tur thu leh remchang ka hmuh phawt chuan harsatna a awm lovang tih min hrilh a. Kei chuan Thanga inti Burma mi hetiang lam chinchang hrethei awm ka hriat thu ka hrilh bawk a.

 

            Kan inbiak ni atanga ni hnihna ah chuan Thuampui kawnah kan inhmu a pawisa fun min hlan pahin cheng singnga a nih thu min hrilh a theihtawpa hma la nghal turin leh hlawhtlinpui ngei turin min chah bawk a ni. In ka thlen hnu chuan ka pawisa hawn chu kan nu nen kan en dun a, a lem ang riau a kan hriat avangin Pu Kapa chu phone in ka bia a a pawisa min pek kha a lem ni tlat a ka hriat thu ka hrilh nghal a. Ani chuan a lem anih thu leh show mai mai tur a nih avangin a pawi chuang lo tih min rawn hrilh a. Tichuan No.4 man theih dan ngaihtuah chuan hma ka la ta a ni. Thanga inti No.4 neia insawi chu ka dawr ta a, hawng khat cheng singnga leh sangnga a nih thu min hrilh a chu pawh chu Pu Kapzuala hi ka hrilh zel a ni. Kan in lei lai taka min rawn nang ching a min rawn man turin ruahmanna kan siam fel a amaerawhchu engemaw avangin Thanga hi kan intiamna hmunah a rawn kal tak loh avangin ka ruahmanna chu a tlawlh zo ta a.

Chungkua thilah hmun danga  a zin bo thut avangin lo kawl tha rih tur leh a lo haw ve leh a a rawn chin fel tur thu min hrilh a. Hetih lai hian Tlanmawia  Sihphir chuan pawisa lem singnga chu a tak a thleng na a hriat thu min hrilh a min nawr deuh ngut ngut reng bawk si a keia pawisa neilo leh tlem a zawng hlep inbeisei chuan Pu Kapzuala pawh rawn lem lo in Tlanmawia thlemna chu ka do zo ta lo a ni. A hnu a hriat leh danin Tlanmawia hi Police ho mi tirh a lo ni reng tih ka hre leh hnuhnawh a a thangah ka lo awk ta reng mai tih ka inhriat hnu chuan pawi ka ti khawp mai.
