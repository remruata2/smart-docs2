****SECRET****

-2-

Yesterday, i.e., 15.11.2020 @ around 10:00 p.m., 1 (one) Assam-Mizoram bound Truck bearing Registration No. MZ 05-A-3067, belonging to Niaz Uddin Laskar of Lailapur, Assam, transporting Sand from Bagha, Assam was illegally stopped and the Driver was forced to dumped the load (v/o Rs.36400/- approx) at the outskirt of Sairang by some members of Sairang Dinthar Drivers Union and some locals of Sairang. The act was allegedly done as the vehicle was believed to belong to Rajib Uddin Laskar @ Raju of Lailapur, Assam, against whom strong bitter feelings prevails among certain quarters due to his active involvement during the recent roadblock enforced against Mizoram at Lailapur. A case was registered at Sairang Police Station vide Case No. 24/2020 DT. 16.11.2020 u/s 341/427/506/34 IPC and a total of 15 (fifteen) accused persons were arrested in this connection and further necessary action is being undertaken.

In this regard, ZDU General Headquarters issued a Press Release today in which it condemned the above act which was done in the name of ZDU and announced their non-involvement. It further urged all its constituent Sub-Headquarters, Branch and Unit not to initiate action on their own and to await instruction of ZDU General Headquarters.

 Situation is under close watch.
