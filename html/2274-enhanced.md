****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/591

Dated 4th August, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/589 Dt. 03.08.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :** 

There was no entry of vehicle at Medili Bridge from Assam today i.e., 3.8.2021 as blockade of road backed by Assam Police is still continuing at Assam side of Medili Bridge.

115 (one hundred fifteen) vehicles (HMV-18, MMV-88, LMV-1, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-Nil, Bus-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

There was no entry of vehicle at Bairabi from Assam as blockade of road leading to Bairabi is still continuing at Ramnathpur, Hailakandi District today. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Journalists from Outlook Magazine, Kolkata and News18, Kolkata visited Vairengte to prepare necessary media coverage about the recent border incident today i.e., 4.8.2021 and they conducted media interview with residents of Vairengte and some officials. The media persons were accompanied by Dr. Irene Lalruatkimi, Department of Mass Communication, MZU and Major Carolyne Zonunmawii(Rtd), DSW&amp;RO, Kolasib.

There was no entry of vehicle from Assam today due to reported blockade of NH-306 in Cachar District of Assam.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
