****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/174

Dated 11th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/170 Dt. 10.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-7, MMV-16, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 11.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and one (1) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

As there is no internet connectivity within the vicinity of Damcherra Bridge, the vehicular movement could not be disclosed while preparing the report.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

173 (one hundred seventy three) vehicles (HMV-69, MMV-43, LMV-23, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-7, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-2) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 11.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
