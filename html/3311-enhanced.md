**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.5.2013**

**Mizoram to Manipur road remained closed******: The meeting of Langkaih and Taitesena Group YMA at Kanhmun yesterday passed a resolution saying the roads connecting Mizoram and Tripura which they have been closed will remain closed until and unless Tuikuk/Bru ask for forgiveness for alleging Mizo people and Mizoram Police in the death of Sebaram Reang s/o Sotorogan of Laithak, Manipur. Sebaram Reang was one of the accused arrested in the attack/roberry of Abdul Manaf and his family on the night of 3rd April, 2013 at Bungthuam and for raping Abdul Manaf's wife and his 9 yrs. old daughter. 

**B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 6.5.2013**

**A woman found at the point of death/unconscious**: On the night of 3rd May, 2013 (Friday) @ 11:30PM, Chhuanawmi (50) w/o Lalcheuva of Lawngtlai Bazar Veng was found unconscious near 71 RCC Pioneer Camp. Most bones of her left face were broken including her left eye, almost all her teeth fallen off with a 3 inch deep injury on her head, said her doctors. Police are investigating the case.
