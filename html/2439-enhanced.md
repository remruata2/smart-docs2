1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Mr. Sajan Ahmad Choudhury (31) S/o Qutub Uddin Choudhury, Konakpur, Silchar, Assam (truck driver) was physically assaulted by personnel on duty at Supply Check-Gate, Vairengte last evening i.e., Dt. 20.6.2021 @ 10:15 p.m. after a heated argument over entry fee. In this regard, the victim submitted complaint to Silchar Truck Drivers’ Association (STDA). Information received stated that authority of Supply Dept., Vairengte discussed the matters with leaders of STDA over telephone today and both parties amicably settled the matter, and agreed that all medical expenditure incurred by the victim should be borne by Supply Dept, Vairengte.
