B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 16.12.2013_

**Deadline for abducted – 18th December****:** Families of the 3 abducted persons were contacted on a phone by the abductors on 13th December, telling them to meet Chief Minister of Mizoram or Home Minister of Mizoram regarding their demand for ransom i.e., Rs. 5 Crore. The abductors told the victims’ families that they will call them back on 18th December to know about their meeting with Chief Minister or Home Minister of Mizoram. If their meeting has no fruitful result, they will not be responsible if anything happens to the abducted persons.

**Criminal cases registered against 145 foreigners in last 6 years**: Among the different 20 crimes recorded in Mizoram during last 6 years (2008 to October 2013) by CID; 8,657 law breakers have Indian Citizenship while 145 foreigners - Myanmarese, Bangladeshis and other foreigners were involved in criminal cases.
