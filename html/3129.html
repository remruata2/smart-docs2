SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/581

Dated: 31st July, 2021

 

  1.           Today i.e., 31st  July, 2021 @ 8:30 a.m., Assam Police constructed Sentry Duty Post using sand bags in the middle of the road (KDZKT Road) at the edge of Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border, on Assam side, blocking the whole road and thus officially cutting the supply line of Mizoram. Information received stated that senior Police Official(s) visited the area this afternoon @ 12:20 p.m. today to oversee the stated construction work and inspected Forest Department Building adjacent to the bridge reportedly for deployment of Assam Police.

 

  1.           Ashutosh Mishra, Associate Editor, India Today, Mumbai entered Mizoram via., Medili Bridge, Kanhmun today @ 12:00 noon to prepare media coverage of the recent border incident.

 

  1.           Information received  stated that DRC, Mizoram House Silchar sought proper police escort to DC, Cachar for stranded vehicles up to the inter-state border. However, DC, Cachar insisted for drivers' detail documents, viz., Vehicle documents, Driving License, Adhaar Card, etc. which appears to be an excuse and impossible to obtain on the part of DRC, Silchar in view of the present situation. At present there are 4 (four) vehicles (Oil Tanker - 2, Bolero Camper - 1, TATA Super Ace - 1) and 28 (twenty) travellers stranded at Mizoram House, Silchar.

 

  1.           Shri. Biswajit Daimary, Speaker Assam Legislative Assembly and IGP, Assam visited places where Assam Police Commando are deployed at Lailapur area today i.e., 31.7.2021 @ 2:30 p.m. under heavy armed escort. Information received stated that around 200 (two hundred) civilians gathered at Lailapur in order to block Mizoram bound vehicles as they presumed that vehicles stranded at Silchar are proceeding towards Mizoram.

 

                    Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
