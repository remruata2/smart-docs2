****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/495

Dated: 29th June, 2021

 

Information received stated that today i.e., 29.6.2021 @ 7:00 a.m. around 120 (one hundred twenty) Assam Police led by Mr. Nirmal Gosh, Dy. SP (Border), Hailakandi District visited the farm land owned by Pu Ramhluna of Vairengte Chhim Veng, located at around 5 km North-West of Vairengte, inside Mizoram. Around 40 (forty) civilians from Dolchera/Balichuri, Assam also came to the spot. Subsequently, Ms. Ramandeep Kaur, SP, Hailakandi; Mr. Jutin Moidori, SDO (Civil), Hailakandi; Mr. Montaz Ali, DFO, Hailakandi; Mr. K. Barman, Circle Officer also arrived at the spot.
On receiving information about their presence, Pu Isaac Lalrempuia, SDO (Civil), Vairengte; Pu Ramtharnghaka, SDPO Vairengte, leaders of Vairengte Thalai Pawl, Social Reconstruction Enlightment (SORE), MZP and MSU along with 14 (fourteen) Mizoram Police personnel, 7 (seven) CRPF personnel and about 30 (thirty) civilians from Vairengte immediately proceeded to the area. Mizoram officials met Assam officials at around 12:30 p.m. today and on being asked about the purpose of their visit, Assam officials informed them that they are conducting normal patrolling and intended to return after having lunch. SDO (Civil), Vairengte informed his counterpart SDO (Civil), Hailakandi the need to discuss the matter and invited him to discuss the matter at his Office Chamber. Pu Lallawmawma, Addl DC, Kolasib and Pu David J Byhnadaolai, Addl. SP, Kolasib also arrived at the spot today @ 2:00 p.m. A brief meeting was held between officials of the two states and agreed to hold DC level meeting in the coming week to resolve the matter.

Both parties are still present at the area at the time of writing the report and as per latest input, Assam Police is intending to set up makeshift camp near Pu Ramhluna’s farm land.
It was later found out today that the farm hut of Pu Chhandama, Vairengte Field Veng, located at around 1(one) km towards Vairengte from Pu Ramhluna’s land and some young betel nut trees belonging to him were allegedly destroyed by Assam Police/Civilians in between 27.6.2021 @ 4:30 p.m. and today. Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP Kolasib is currently en route to the area to take stock of the situation.
In connection with the above incident, Joint Village Council, Vairengte gave a call to youths of Vairengte to proceed to Pu Lalramhluna’s farm land this evening to construct makeshift camp for Mizoram Police personnel and to bring necessary construction tools required for the same. As per input, the response of the youths would depend on further activities of Assam Police present at the area.

Recapping earlier incident, on 27.6.2021 @ 4:30 p.m., around 10 (ten) Assam Police and Forest officials led by Mr. Nirmal Gosh, APS, Dy. SP Border, Hailakandi District and around 30 (thirty) civilians from Assam visited Vairengte PHE water source, located near Pu Vantluanga’s fish pond, along Aitlang stream, which is around 2 km West of Vairengte village, inside Mizoram. 
-2-

On receiving information about their visit, 9 (nine) leaders of Social Reconstruction Enlightment (SORE), an NGO at Vairengte led by Pu Lallawmsanga, President SORE immediately went to the area and held an interaction with Assam officials. During their interaction, Assam officials stated that they visited the area to conduct spot verification as per a complaint received from Assam Forest Department regarding encroachment of Assam Forest Reserve area. Assam officials left the area yesterday evening @ 5:00 p.m. In this connection, SDPO, Vairengte contacted SP, Hailakandi, who in turn informed him that she deployed the team to conduct spot verification in pursuance of a complaint made by Assam Forest officials on alleged encroachment of Assam Forest Reserve area.
  Situation is under close watch.
