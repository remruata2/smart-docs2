A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 14.12.2022

Minister Beichhua resigned: Mizoram Minister of State, Dr. K Beichhua tendered his resignation in compliance to an instruction received from Chief Minister, Pu Zoramthanga yesterday at around 12:45 noon. His resignation was, after consultation with the Chief Minister, approved by Mizoram Governor on yesterday evening itself. The Chief Minister had, on 12.12.2022, sent a letter to Dr. K Beichhua, requesting him to submit his resignation as he ( the Chief Minister) plans to re shuffle the portfolios of all the Ministers. Speaking to the press after his resignation, Dr. K Beichhua simply stated the possibilities of consequences for being too loyal to their superiors and implementations of their demands. He further mentioned  his allegiance to MNF party, however, could not  predict his future. Dr. K.Beichhua was elected to the state Assembly from Siaha constituency in 2018 and his portfolio during his tenure as a Minister included departments such as Social Welfare & Tribal Affairs, Excise & Narcotics and Animal Husbandry & Veterinary.

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 14.12.2022

Notice to Sunshine Overseas Pvt. Ltd. : On 14.12. 2022, Pu Lalthazuala, Director Geology and Mining Department served a notice to Sunshine Overseas Pvt. Ltd. instructing them to ceased all the illegal mining of stones and sands from  Tiau river and to explain themselves within 15 days as to why no action should be taken against them for their previous illegal activities in this connection. Previously, Youth Environment Justice, Mizoram (YEJM)  had lodged a complaint to Champhai District Magistrate against this illegal activities of Sunshine Overseas Pvt. Ltd. alleging them of polluting and damaging the river and its environment. YEJM accused the company of not possessing any environment/forest clearance and of violating Mizoram Minor Concession Rule 2000.

 

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 14.12.2022

Seizure of firecrackers:  Yesterday, Flying Squad of  Lunglei Police, which was created to implement the prohibition of firecrackers and its related products seized 25 boxes of firecrackers and the accused was also booked under appropriate sections of law at Lunglei Police Station. Senior Police officials of Lunglei police stated their intention to implement the prohibitory order strictly and that violators will be punished based on relevant sections of laws.
