THREAT PERCEPTION IN CONNECTION WITH A STRIKE TO BE ORGANISED ON _28TH & 29TH MARCH,2022_

1. Various Trade Union and business organisations such as MIMA, AMCIRDA, FOMTU, CITUM, NTUM, AMPEDA, ZDU, MTOA, MTDA, Mizoram IOCL Transporters Forum etc. have no intention of supporting the intended strike.

2.  As per input received, Financial institutions in Mizoram such as Mizoram Rural Bank, Central Bank of India, Punjab Bank, Union Bank of India, Federal Bank, UCO Bank and APEX Bank are planning to support this strike by closing all their offices during this strike period.

3.  All city branch members of Mizoram Rural Bank are planning to have a demonstration by displaying banners and placards in front of their Head Office at MINECO, Khatla, Aizawl in support of this strike. However, as per input no other activities will be carried out by them.

4.  Apart from the staffs of Mizoram Rural Bank, no other staff of other Banks mention in point no. 2 are expected to participate in this demonstration.

 According to information received, no Law &amp; Order situation that can affect the normal functioning of IOC Tankers / Workers / Establishment and even Oil depots has been perceived at the moment due to the intended strike on 28th &amp; 29t h March, 2022.
