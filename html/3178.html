A. Front page of THE AIZAWL  POST newspaper Dt.15.2.2013:

 

Bethlehem veng announced their appreciation:

            The Joint Action Committee of Bethlehem Veng make a statement appreciating the action of the police in tracking down the culprits of the incident in Bethlehem Veng on 30.1.2013 where a house and several vehicles were burnt down. The statement further mentioned their gratitude towards NGO's and churches for their efforts in assisting the victims and praised the Fire & Emergency Service Deptt. for their prompt action in preventing the fire from further escalation.  

 

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.15.2.2013:

 

Man died after assault :

            On the evening of 12th Feb. 2013, at around 4:30 PM Chawnghranga (76) S/o. Dengbana (L) of Lunglei was assaulted by his neighbor Lalrinsanga (38) S/o. Nghurnghinga (L). After the victim died the next day in Lunglei Civil Hospital, Police apprehended Lalrinsanga.

 

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM  TIMES newspaper Dt.15.2.2013:

 

PRISM Lodge FIR :

            Based on a report received from Joint Action Committee, Tuitun Lui Hariayali Project, Kolasib-V, Khamrang, the Gen. Hqrs. of PRISM yesterday lodge an FIR at ACB Mizoram to enquire about suspected misappropriation of funds in the ongoing Tuitun Lui Hariayali Project.

            According to the report, some corrupt activities were suspected in the distribution of funds to farmers under this project and that some receipt and APR shown in the government records were believed to fabricated.
