**Supplementary notes :**

1. Tute hnen atanga man nge?

 Heng ralthuamte hi mi hrang hrang licence nei lova kawltu te, dan ang ni lova hmangtute leh a tam ber hi chu hlawkna um avanga zuartu/zuar chhawngtute hnen atanga man a ni.

2. Engtinnge tihral (destroyed) a nih thin?

 Ralthuam man anih reng rengin a manna hmun(place of seizure) bialtu Police Station O/C ten case siamin a tul angin an chhuizui thin. Case peihfel anih veleh case siamtu Police Station O/C chuan ralthuam chu case kaihhnawih lehkha pawimawh zawng zawng nen Court ah a thehlut thin a. Court in case a thifel hnuah Roreltu(Magistrate) phalna in tualchher(country made) ralthuam te chu tihchhiat(destroyed) ani thin a, ralthuam tha pangngai(factory made) te chu District Magistrate Chairman-na hnuaia Board in a tihral dan tur an ngaihtuah thin.
