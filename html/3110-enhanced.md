A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 23.8.2013_

**Aizawl to Kolasib road to be restricted**: SP Aizawl and SP Kolasib issued an order that as the road from Kawnpui to Aizawl via Sairang is deteriorated due to the bad weather condition and vehicles can hardly ply, an entry routine made for LMV &amp; MMV going from Kawnpui and Aizawl via Lungdai must be followed from 24th August 2013 as below : -

**ENTRY PERIOD**

Aizawl to Kolasib – 05:00 AM to 08:00 AM &amp; 01:00 PM to 04:00 PM

Kolasib to Aizawl – 09:00 AM to 12:00 Noon &amp; 05:00 PM to 06:00 PM

 Between 09:00 PM to 05:00 AM the road will be free for any vehicle from both sides.
