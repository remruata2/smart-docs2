4.5.2022 : 6 (six) leaders of Mizoram Govt. Adhoc Aided Higher Secondary School Lecturers' Association (MIGAAHSSLA) led by Dr. David B Lalhruaitluanga, President MIGAAHSSLA met Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister of Mizoram at the latter's Office Chamber on 4.5.2022 @ 3:45 p.m. and submitted a written petition, wherein they expressed gratitude to the Chief Minister regarding elevation of their schools into Adhoc Aided School due to his efforts. It also stated that their schools have been under Adhoc Aided School status for 14 (fourteen) years and in this connection, they requested the Chief Minister to elevate the 12 (twelve) MIGAAHSSLA member schools into Provincialised school.

28.10.2022:   22nd Mizoram Secondary School Teachers' Association (MISSTA) Annual General Conference was held on 28.10.2022 at Chanmari YMA Hall. Pu Lalchhandama Ralte, Hon'ble Minister of State, School Education, etc. graced the function as Chief Guest and around 270 (two hundred seventy) members attended. The meeting approved the following agendas concerning the state government:

            (i)        State government should fill up vacant posts of teachers and LDCs in                      Govt. High School.

            (ii)       Services of teachers under Adhoc aided and Samagra should be utilized                  to meet acute shortages of teachers in Govt. High Schools.

            (iii)     State government should take necessary action for filling up of vacant                    post of headmaster in Government High School at the earliest.

            (iv)      MISSTA General Headquarters should take necessary action for                               stoppage of attach out of High school teachers to offices.

            (v)       Necessary action should be taken to change the promotion ratio of                           SDEO and High School Headmaster to DEO from 75:25 to 50:50.

            (vi)      Practical examination should be conducted prior to theory exam in                          HSLC.
