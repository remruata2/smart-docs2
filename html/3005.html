SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/848

Dated: 18th November, 2019.

 

 

                        A sit-in demonstration organised by North East Students’ Organisation (NESO) in protest of Central Government’s intention to reintroduce the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill in the Parliament was carried out in front of Vanapa Hall today, i.e. 18.11.2019 @ 2:00 p.m. The program was chaired by Peter Chhangte, Vice President, MZP and about 100 (one hundred) persons participated in the demonstration. Speeches were delivered by Ricky Lalbiakmawia, Fin. Secy., NESO and B. Vanlaltana, President, MZP, mainly denouncing the Government’s intention to reintroduce the CAB. Protesters also displayed placards containing the following slogans  –

 

            1.         We oppose Citizenship Amendment Bill.

            2.         Passing CAB 16 is injurious to North East.

      3.         MZP oppose Citizenship Amendment Bill 2016.

      4.         India for Indians, not for illegal Bangladeshis.

      5.         Down with CAB 2016.

      6.         We protest CAB 2016.

 

                        The protesters later submitted a Memorandum addressed to Hon’ble Prime Minister, with a copy to Hon’ble Union Home Minister through Hon’ble Governor of Mizoram @ 3:00 p.m, urging him not to introduce the Bill but instead grant Constitutional safeguard to the people of North East by introducing Inner Line Permit, and also implement National Register of Citizens (NRC).

           

                         Situation is under close watch.
