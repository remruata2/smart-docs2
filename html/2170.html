MIZORAM

NOTIFICATION
Dated Aizawl, the 5th September, 2020
WHEREAS, in pursuance of the Notification No.B.14015/1/2020-SEC/LC/9
issued by the State Election Commission, Mizoram on 2nd & 3rd September, 2020 under subrule (1) of Rule 3 and Rule 37 of the Mizoram Municipalities (Election to Local Councils)
Rules, 2015 read with Rule 16 of the Mizoram Municipalities (Ward Committee and Local
Councils)(Amendment) Rules, 2020, General Election to Local Councils was held for the
purpose of constituting 23 Local Councils within Aizawl Municipality on 4th September, 2020;
AND WHEREAS, the results of the elections in respect of 23 Local Councils in
the said General Elections have been declared by the Returning Officers concerned; and
NOW, THEREFORE, in pursuance of sub-rule (3) of Rule 77 of the Mizoram
Municipalities (Election to Local Councils) Rules, 2015, the State Election Commission,
Mizoram hereby notifies the names of the members elected in respect of the said Local
Councils, along with their party affiliations, in the SCHEDULE enclosed to this Notification.
Sd/- LAIMA CHOZAH
State Election Commissioner,
Mizoram.
