****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/544

Dated 12th July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/539 Dt. 09.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

****Information received stated that today i.e., 12.7.2021, an area, which is around 1 km west of Pu John Halliday’s farm, Chuhvel was cleared, which is suspected to be for road construction reportedly by Assam. In this connection, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah and Mizoram Armed Police personnel and leaders of Chuhvel village visited the area for spot verification this evening.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-10, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 12.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

19 (nineteen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-16, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha visited Mizoram Police place of deployment at Pu Thangkhawliana’s farm land, Buarchep today i.e., 12.7.2021 @ 1:50 p.m. and later he visited Mizoram Police place of deployment at Saihapui ‘V’ and took stock of the situation. He was accompanied by Pu David J. Byhnadaolai, Addl SP Kolasib, other officials and leaders of MNF party.
Leaders of Mizo Students’ Union (MSU) General Headquarters led by Pu Zodingliana, Asst General Secretary visited Mizoram Police place of deployment at Buarchep today @ 2:20 p.m.
1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

50 (fifty) vehicles (HMV-27, MMV-Nil, LMV-16, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

137 (one hundred thirty seven) vehicles (HMV-79, MMV-14, LMV-26, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-13, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
