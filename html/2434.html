  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

Addl. DC Cachar, Assam, RO Dholai Forest Range and OC Dholai PS along with 20 (twenty) Assam Police personnel crossed 22nd Bn. CRPF duty post at the inter-state border, located in between Vairengte and Lailapur today i.e., 10.6.2021 @ 11:45 a.m. CRPF personnel on duty at the border tried to stop them and informed them to wait SDPO, Vairengte so as to avoid unnecessary border tension. However, Addl. DC and party denied their request and forcefully crossed the border and proceeded upto Vairengte Auto Stand, and immediately returned towards Lailapur, Assam without getting down from their vehicles. On receiving such information, SDO (Civil) Vairengte and SDPO (Vairengte) immediately proceeded to CRPF duty post at the inter-state border and by the time they arrived Assam Govt. Officials already left the area.

163 (one hundred sixty three) vehicles (HMV-62, MMV-49, LMV-21, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
