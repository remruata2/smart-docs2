**FIR lodged**: K.Laldinthara, Mizo National Front (MNF) Party worker lodged FIR to Kulikawn Police Station on 13th July against alleged malpractice in distribution of GCI sheets under Aibawk rural development block recently. C. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 15.7.2013_

****No nomination for the upcoming VC Bye Election**: For the upcoming VC Bye Election of 15 Village Councils on 23rd July, today is the last date for submitting nomination but no nomination has been filed. Hmar Welfare Committee ordered each political party of each village to file nomination as independent candidate which made the atmosphere disorderly/confusing. State Election Commission will wait for nomination till 03:00PM today.**

**F. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES, VANGLAINI & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 15.7.2013**

****HPC(D) Team heading for Aizawl for Peace Talk****: An eight member Hmar People’s Convention (Democrats) delegation headed by its working president Thangliana is heading for Aizawl by road from Manipur for peace negotiations within Aizawl on 18th July. While four members would be from the outfit, four others would be from Aizawl including two local journalists. The state government would be represented by a six member team of officials headed by State’s Joint Secretary, Home Department, Lalbiakzama.****

**Before the commencement of Peace talk on this coming Thursday, Mizoram government requests HPC(D) to submit their Charter of Demands. Mizoram government officials want to study and examine the HPC(D) ‘s Charter of Demands before the Peace Talks that is the the reason said sources and there is no pressure from Mizoram government to HPC(D) to submit their Charter of Demands.**
