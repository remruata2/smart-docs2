Leaked-out signing of secret agreement between Bangladesh government army - RAB and Arakan Army (AA) for joint military operation against Kuki-Chin National Front (KNF) -

            On last 14/11/22, a secret meeting was held between senior officers of Arakan Army (AA) and Bangladesh Army and DGFI officials, BGB officials at Tamra on the Bangladesh-Myanmar border. Some senior officers were present on behalf of the AA Army in this secret meeting.

Decisions in the said meeting:-

1- The KNF should be dispersed from the soil of this country through the Arakan Army. As the price, the Bangladesh government will pay 10 million taka to the AA army.

2- Arakan Army and Bangladesh Army will jointly conduct operations against KNF;

3- If the campaign is successful, the Bangladesh government will provide all the necessary money to the Arakan Army;

4- Bangladesh will be allowed to use the territory until Arakan becomes independent.

            It is to be noted that on the way back to that meeting, a high-ranking officer of the DGFI was shot dead in a sudden attack by a guerilla. According to the decision of the said agreement, on 16/11/22, about 60-70 men of AA army with sophisticated weapons went to Cheikhiang neighborhood to attack the KNF. Arakanese Army 13 km by Bangladesh army car. From brings up the border.From there they came towards Cheikhiang Para. But as there were no KNF members in Cheikhiang Para, they started harassing the villagers in various ways. As soon as they came to the village, they frankly admitted that we had an agreement with the Bangladesh Army to fight with the Kuki Army KNF. Where are the KNF forces, there is panic among the villagers, then the villagers ask everyone to sit under a tree and surrender.And the KNF started looking for members. Then a KNF information stationed in the village and a KNF private on leave called to talk.

            A few innocent villagers, including unarmed KNF members, were caught and tied up. They will be handed over to the Bangladesh Army and for that AA will pay the army as per the agreement.

            On hearing the news of AA army's torture on the people, KNF army quickly moved to Cheikhiang neighborhood and three KNF army on duty on sight of AA army opened fire on the two. When KNA counterattacked, all three on duty fell on the ground. Seven more AAs were killed in the firing. The AAs retreated and finally surrendered and announced a withdrawal of hostilities.

            In both attacks and counter-attacks, the AA Army suffered about 10 casualties. Meanwhile, the Bangladesh Army and RAB forces are increasing their strength around the KNF camp, and yesterday afternoon, Bangladesh Army from Theikhiang Para, during the battle with the AA forces, targeted two or three KNFs. Bombs were thrown and several rounds were fired at the KNF forces. However, no KNF was injured.
