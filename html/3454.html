5.5.2022 :      Shri. Ram Nath Kovind, Hon'ble President of India arrived at Lengpui Airport today, on 5.5.2022 @ 11:20 a.m. on a two-day visit to the state and he was received by Dr. Hari Babu Kambhampati, Governor of Mizoram, and Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister on his arrival at Thuampui Helipad. The President graced the 16th Convocation of the Mizoram University held the same afternoon @ 4:00 p.m. at MZU as Chief Guest and delivered Convocation address. The VVIP will depart from Lengpui Airport for New Delhi on 6.5.2022 @ 10:55 a.m.

3.11.2022 :    Smt. Droupadi Murmu, Hon'ble President of India arrived at Lengpui Airport on 3.11.2022 @ 1:50 p.m. for her two day visit to the State. She attended 17th Convocation of Mizoram University (MZU) at Tanhril the same evening @ 3:05 p.m. As per scheduled, she will attend a meeting with the Chief Minister of Mizoram and Council of Ministers tomorrow morning and address the members of Mizoram Legislative Assembly at the Special Assembly, which will be held tomorrow i.e., 4.11.2022 @ 10:15 a.m.

4.11.2022 :    Smt. Droupadi Murmu, Hon'ble President of India after addressing the members of Mizoram Legislative Assembly at the Special Assembly, which was held on 4.11.2022 @10:15 am left Lengpui Airport for New Delhi the same day @12:00 Noon. No untoward incident occurred during the visit of Hon'ble President of India.
