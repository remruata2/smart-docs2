****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/700

Dated 8th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/697 Dt. 7.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 8.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – 1, MMV – 6) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 6 (six) vehicles (HMV – 2, MMV – 4) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 8.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 38 (thirty eight) vehicles (HMV-19, MMV – 14, LMV – 3, Maxi Cab- 2) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 8.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 17 (seventeen) vehicles (HMV – 9, MMV – 1, LMV –7, LPG – Nil, Maxi Cab – Nil, Oil Tanker – Nil, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 8.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

 Due to nation-wide Bharat Bandh called by various Farmers’ Union, vehicles entering into Assam from Mizoram were blocked at Lailapur, Assam and normal flow of vehicles into Mizoram was also affected. As per input, the Bandh will be called off at Lailapur at 5:00 p.m. and normal flow of vehicles will resume.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
