BRU VOTERS IN TRIPURA TRANSIT CAMPS AS ON 2.4.2019

 

During a Press Conference held on 2.4.2019 @ 2:00 p.m at NLUP Conference Hall, Pu Ashish Kundra, IAS, CEO, Mizoram appealed the NGOCC to refrain from boycotting the upcoming Parliamentary Election (Lok Sabha) citing that Postal Ballot will be counted even if NGOCC goes ahead with the boycott. The Press Conference was attended by 20 (twenty) persons from the media.
