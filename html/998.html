ABDUCTION OF THREE BENGALIS FROM TUIPUIBARI-II (RAJIVNAGAR)

            Five gunmen of NLFT (National Liberation Front of Tripura) cadres and BNCT (Borok National Council of Tripura) cadres entered Tuipuibari-II, Mamit District under West Phaileng Police Station on 25.11.2012 @ 8:00 PM and abducted the following three persons :-

1)        Sapan Kumar Roy (52) s/o Sukumar Roy of Dosda Bazar, N. Tripura.

2)        Govindo Nath (35), Driver of Dharmanagar Mission Tilla, N. Tripura.

3)        Sankar Nath (35) of Rangamati, Karimganj District, Assam.

            Two other inmates, namely - (1) Satyo Pryo Roy (50) s/o Sasinchondro Roy of Dosda and (2) Ohiram Reang (58) s/o Jeetomoni Reang of Anando Bazar managed to escape reportedly as they hid under the bed.

            The above five persons were said to be doing teak timber business since last September and were staying in a rented house belonging to Chondrodhan Chakma of Tuipuibari-II. In this connection, West Phaileng PS C/No. 36/12 dt.26.11.2012 u/s 364(A)/171/34 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act has been registered.

            Two of the abductors were wearing jungle camouflaged uniform and armed with Carbine while the rest three were in civil dress and carried sticks. The abductors also took away Teak Royalty documents/permits alongwith cash amounting to Rs. 30,000/- (Rupees thirty thousand) as they left towards Bangladesh border through the jungle.

            The three Bengali Teak (timber) traders abducted were held at NLFT camp known as “Hnahbok Camp” located inside Kasalong forest west of Zopui village in Sazek hills of Bangladesh. It is learnt that families of the abductees were contacted on 02.12.2012 through mobile phone by the abductors urging them to immediately pay up their ransom demand of Rs 30 lakhs.

                The three(3) abducted persons namely, 1) Sapan Kumar Roy (52) S/o Sukumar Roy of Dosda Bazaar, N. Tripura. 2) Govindo Nath (35) of Dharmanagar Mission Tilla, N.Tripura, 3) Shankar Nath (35) of Rangamati, Karimganj Dist. Assam were on 12.12.2012. The released hostages reached Rajiv Nagar @ 3:30pm and were taken to BSF BOP where they spent the night. Officer-in-Charge of West Phaileng PS had proceeded to Rajiv Nagar on 13.12.12@ 06.00 a.m to bring the released hostages to West Phaileng on 13.12.12 for medical check-up at PHC, West Phaileng and they were released after observing all legal formalities. Their health conditions were reportedly sound and stable.
