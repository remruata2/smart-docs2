A. Front page of VANGLAINI  newspaper Dt. 26 &27.6.2022

15,000 Liters of drinking water for Silchar by CYMA :      As a relief for flood victims in and around Silchar areas, Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) sent 15,000 liters of drinking water to Silchar.

          These consignments, which consisted of 14,196 bottles of mineral water, donated by 31 YMA branches and generous private individuals were flagged off by CYMA President, Pu R.Lalngheta yesterday and which will be handed over to D.C,Cachar.

 

 

 

B. Front Page  of VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 26& 27.6.2022

Two vehicles swept away by landslide: On the morning of 25th June,2022 (Saturday) at around 7:30 am, a massive landslide on Seling - Champhai road swept away one private car and a taxi at around half a kilometer from Seling town. Fortunately, no human lives were lost in this incident.

          These vehicles who were proceeding towards Aizawl from Saitual town were stuck in a mud when the landslide struck and swept them away. One eye witness of this incident rushed to the nearby town of Seling for help and returned with willing volunteers who conducted rescue operation for these victims. Both the vehicles were carrying one passenger each apart from the drivers.

          Apart from one lady, a passenger in the private car who was referred to Aizawl, the other three victims were taken to Thingsulthliah Hospital for treatment of their minor injuries.
