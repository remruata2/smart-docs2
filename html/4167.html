  1. BANGLADESH:

 

            Due to the military coup or change in government, there has been no development yet between KCNF/KCNA. It is learnt that all the leaders of KCNF are presently staying inside Bangladesh.

 

            The 14 Arms with 415 rounds ammunition looted by the KCNA during the Bank robberies on 2nd April and 3rd April 2024 were not surrendered  to the Bangladesh Government till date and as per latest information, recently there is no peace talk between the Bangladesh Govt. and KCNA.
