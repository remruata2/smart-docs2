REPORT ON THINGHLUN INCIDENT

 

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/582

Dated 19th October, 2020

 

            In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/580 Dt. 18.10.2020, 28 (twenty eight) members of MZP (10 members from Aizawl, 5 members from Sairang and 13 members from Mamit) led by Pu Andrew F. Lalramnghaka, Treasurer, MZP General Headquarters arrived Thinghlun today morning i.e., 19.10.2020 @ 2:30 a.m. and 34 (thirty four) members of Vengpui Branch YMA, Zawlnuam under the aegis of Langkaih Group YMA also arrived Thinghlun today at 11:00 a.m. to assist border duty at Thinghlun.

            Yesterday i.e., 18.10.2020 DC, Karimganj issued prohibitory order for assembly of 4 (four) or more persons in Chotabubirbond area of Singla Reserved Forest alleging encroachment of the said territory by miscreants from Mizoram and armed aggression by detachment of uniform personnel deployed by Government of Mizoram.       

Today i.e., 19.10.2020 @ 1:00 p.m. One Section of Assam Police, RO, Assam Forest and around 20 (twenty) villagers of Zaite Village, Assam started clearing up the proposed place for construction of Assam Police Camp at Mauhak Mual (Segontillah Hill) around 500 meters from   Pu John Zolawma’s Jhum Hut at Zaite Road. The said action was opposed by residents of Thinghlun, members of MZP and Zawlnuam YMA who are stationed at the area and rushed to the area and told them to withdraw and not to continue their work. They immediately withdrew and situation went normal.

Further, today evening i.e., 19.10.2020 @ 5:20 p.m. Shri. GD Tripathi, IAS, Home Commissioner, Assam visited Assam-Mizoram border near Thinghlun and he was accompanied by Shri. Mayank Kumar, SP, Karimganj and media persons with one platoon of Armed Assam Police escort. During their visit, Home Commissioner, Assam had a short interaction with SP, Mamit and SDO (C), Kawrthah who are present at the area. During the interaction, Home Commissioner, Assam stated that he wishes immediate restoration of peace and tranquility in the area and when asked about the proposed setting up of Assam Police Camp at Mauhak Mual (Segontillah Hill), he stated that it is the concern of DC, Karimganj and he does not have anything to say on the matter. They left the area around 5:40 p.m.

28 (twenty eight) members of MZP will continue to stay overnight at the area and 34 (thirty four) members of Zawlnuam Vengpui YMA will perform duty at the area till 12:00 midnight tonight.

            Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
