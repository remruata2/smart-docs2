A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.1.2014

Police released the arrested persons in PR Bond: Lunglei Police arrested 5 persons on 3.1.2014 (Friday) and were forwarded to court on 4.1.2014 (Saturday), but there was no magistrate duty at District Court, Lunglei, as the arrested persons cannot be detained more than 24 hours by Police without the permission of the court, they were released by Police in PR Bond on 4.1.2014 (Saturday).

 

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.1.2014

Abductors displeased at Mizoram Government: The Bru armed gang who abducted Sanglianthanga, Lalzamliana and Deep Mandal spoke to the families of the abducted on 2nd January 2014; they expressed their disappointment and anger at Mizoram Government for trying to negotiate with other group and not them.

The abductors demanded Rs. 5 Crore for ransom and 15th Jan. 2014 is the deadline they set. The Bru armed gang abducted these 3 persons on 23rd November 2013 from Dampa Forest, and has been detaining them for more than 40 days.

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.1.2014

Police intervention in the Salvation Army issues: Salvation Army Reform Movement of Kolasib accused their Commanding Officer Maj. Moses Sairengpuia of separating the congregation and stopped him from attending a church service; however Maj. Moses Sairengpuia attended the church under protection of Police.

            Salvation Army Reform Movement blocked the Commanding Officer in front of his house and posted poster, after the crowd and the Police scuffled a bit, Moses Sairengpuia somehow attended the church under Police security. The afternoon service was cancelled due to the issues.

            Kolasib DC, Jitendre Yadav already had a talk with people of Salvation Army Reform Movement and also with Salvation Army Commanding Officer Maj. Moses Sairengpuia regarding the issues in their congregation.
