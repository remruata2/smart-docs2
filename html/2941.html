SPECIAL REPORT

 

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/372

Dated: 5th June, 2019.

 

 

 

Champhai Police, who were on Anti-Drug operation at Zote crossing point on Indo-Myanmar border apprehended one ZRA (Zomi Revolutionary Army) cadre of President Thanlianpau faction, identified as S/S Pte. Thangkhandong (29 yrs) s/o Pumlampau, r/o Tuilang, Tiddim township, Myanmar, this morning (06.06.2019) @ 9:30 a.m. A .22 Pistol with 55 (fifty-five) rounds of live ammunitions were seized from his possession. The arrested ZRA cadre came in a 125 bike (Myanmar) which has also been seized and case being registered at Champhai PS.
