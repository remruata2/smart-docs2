**30.3.2022 :** In continuation of our DSI Part-X (a) Dt. 29.3.2022, a 2 (two) days long Sinlung Hills Council (SHC) Budget Session was continued on 30.3.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Tourist Lodge, Sakawrdai. Pu Malsawma Darngawn, Chairman SHC chaired the Session and 14 (fourteen) members attended. SHC Budget amounting to Rs. 601.34 lakhs was passed after deliberate discussion and the Session was adjourned sine die @ 5:10 p.m.
