10. Briefing and De-briefing :-

            CID(SB) invariably conducted meticulous briefing and de-briefing before and after every operation/deployment. These briefings are utilised to clear any doubts which may arise in connection with the intended operation. Likewise, de-briefing after the conclusion of the task are extremely helpful in finding remedy for previous mistakes as well as for future improvement.

12. Length of International Borders :-

            As per the records of CID(SB), Mizoram is having a stretch of approximately 404 Kms. international border with Myanmar and 318 Kms. international borders with Bangladesh.

13.Coordination with Border Guarding forces :-

            For safeguarding the State and the country as a whole,  sharing of intelligence with other sister agencies in the State is regularly practised. Discussion and meetings are also conducted through Lead Intelligence Agency(LIA) and Subsidiary Multi Agency Centre(SMAC)  meetings on monthly basis where representative of the Assam Rifles,  BSF, Custom officials etc. are also present. Apart from this, actual joint operations are often conducted on the ground with personnel of AR/BSF in tackling illicit items like narcotics, fake currencies, illegal arms etc. which often bore fruitful results. Being a Para military forces, these border guarding  forces often lacked the normal legal formalities which are efficiently plugged by members of the special Branch who are also the active members of these joint operations.

14.Pending Suspension Cases :-

            Presently there is no pending suspension case in respect of personnel from CID(SB). It may be treated as Nil.

15. Development activities in Units :-

            According to conveniences, in-service trainings for personnel of the unit have been organised from time to time. In these  training,  apart from normal policing functions, service matters such as leave rules, entitlements, pay, etc. have been discussed. Further, taking advantage of Government holidays, annual firing followed by entertainment programme were also organised where motivated speech were delivered along with distribution of shooting awards.
