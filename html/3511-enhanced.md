A. _Front page of VANGLAINI /THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 03.08.2022_

**Agreement between CYMA and MYC:**  An agreement for conducting training on cement masonry works for the youth was signed between Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) and Mizoram Youth Commission (MYC) at MYC conference hall, yesterday i.e 2.8.2022. According to this agreement, which was signed by Dr.Vanlaltanpuia, MLA on behalf of MYC and Pu R.Lalngheta, President, CYMA, MYC will hand over a sum of Rs.10.65 lakhs to CYMA which will be utilised for conducting the required training.
