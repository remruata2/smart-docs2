B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 9.4.2013

NIOS Principal & Students made agreement: MZP intervened and made agreement between students and principal of National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS). The problems arose as students were not allowed to appear in an exam this year after paying all the necessary fees. NIOS will pay back all the fees to students who had filled up examination form but disqualified to sit for the exam in return FIR lodged against NIOS principal at Vaivakawn Police station will be withdrawn by the students in writing to the authorities. If a student wants to sit for the exam he/she can do so by using fees he/she had already paid to NIOS.
