Sir,

                  

                   With reference to the above, point by point clarification in connection with recent influx of Rohingyas into India are as follows:

 

i.                 Action taken for:

          (a)      identification of illegal immigrants

         

          :         The office of the undersigned has sent out Foreigners Registration  Form  to every  entry  and exit point within the state of Mizoram for                    identification of illegal immigrants. Police personnel posted at the above mentioned points are also instructed to check the authenticity of travel              documents carried by foreigners while entering or exiting Mizoram.

 

          (b)      monitoring of illegal immigrants

 

          :         For monitoring  movement of illegal immigrants, security personnel posted at entry and exit points were instructed to carefully check the             authenticity of travel documents carried by every foreigners.

 

          (c)      curbing the ingress of illegal immigrants

 

          :         To curb the ingress of illegal immigrants, Police personnel posted at the             entry and exit points are instructed to check the authenticity of travel                documents carried by foreigners while entering or exiting Mizoram and              to  take  prompt  action in  this  regard. As a result of this, 8 (eight)              Rohingyas  were apprehended  on  23.04.2019 while trying to enter                 Mizoram through Vairengte Police check gate.

 

ii.                The steps taken to sensitize security personnel posted at the entry and               exit points of Mizoram

 

          :         The office of the undersigned  has sent out Foreigners Registration                     Form to security personnel posted at every entry  and exit point within              the state of  Mizoram for  identification of illegal immigrants. Police               personnel posted at the above          mentioned points are also instructed to               check the  authenticity of travel documents carried by foreigners while                 entering or exiting Mizoram.

 

                   Submitted for further necessary action.

BRIEF NOTE ON DEPORTATION PROCESS OF ROHINGYAS

APPREHENDED IN MIZORAM

 

 

 

 

 

 

                   8 (eight) Rohingyas were apprehended on 23.04.2019 at Vairengte Police check gate.

 

          1.       Toyaba D/o Soyedul Islam

          2.       Senowara D/o Sanaullah

          3.       Tismin D/o Dil Mohammed

          4.       Attoriza D/o Motiur Rohoman

          5.       Suminara D/o Hasimullah

          6.       Noor Kayes D/o Kalim Uliah

          7.       Sokina Khatun D/o Salam Ahmed

          8.       Robina D/o Nur Ahamed

 

          Initiative for their deportation is taken by the Home Department, GOM. They communicate with Ministry of Home Affairs (Foreigners Division) New Delhi and Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi. Due to late reply from Ministry of External Affairs, Govt. of Mizoram entrusted Principal Resident Commissioner, Mizoram House, New Delhi to take necessary follow-up action at the East Asia Division, Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi.

 

          Ministry of External Affairs contacted Myanmar Embassy and they received Nationality Verification Proforma and sent it to Home Department, Aizawl. CID(SB) conducted nationality verification of the above mentioned Rohingyas. Filled up nationality verification report form was already sent back to Ministry of External Affairs for further submission to the Embassy of Myanmar. After this, reply from Ministry of External Affairs is still awaited.

 

Note  :         As per Minutes of the Meeting to discuss matters relating to Foreign Nationals placed under Social Welfare Department Held in the Office Chamber of the Secretary to the Government of Mizoram, Home Department on 11th October, 20196, deportation process of the remaining 24 Rohingyas who were apprehended at Aizawl and Champhai and still lodged at Muanna In Boys Home, Mualpui, Aizawl and Protective Home at Hunthar, Aizawl is to be carried out jointly by the Home Department, Social Welfare Department and Police Headquarters.
