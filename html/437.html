Sir,

 

                    With reference to the subject cited above, I am sending herewith the verification report as under.

                    That Sir, Pu.Challianchhunga of New Vervek was contacted and stated that he had received a phone call on his mobile phone number 8974299774 in the early part of August, 2014 from one person who claimed himself to be HPC(D) cadre and told him to convey his words that Pu Vanhnuailala, Vice President, Ratu Village Council should collect donation of ` 15,000/- each from Fair Price Shop Retailers and ` 10,000/- each from Kerosine Oil Retailers of Ratu village and the collected amount inturn would be collected by HPC(D) from the hands of Pu Vanhnuailala.

                     Accordingly, Pu Challianchhunga informed Pu Vanhnuailala on 3rd August, 2014 to collect the said demand money from those retailers who inturn informed all retailers about the demand made by suspected HPC(D) cadres.

                    Pu Vanhnuailala was also contacted through telephone where he  stated that the demand made by HPC(D) is not yet collected as the authenticity and veracity of the demand is not ascertained. He further stated that if they do not receive another direct phone call from the HPC(D), they shall not collect the same.

                    The identity of the alledged suspected HPC(D) cadre who had called Pu Challianchhunga of New Vervek is not yet ascertained as he did not mention his name and rank or from which faction of HPC(D) he belonged to. Besides, the mobile number of the suspected HPC(D) was not recorded/saved by Pu Challianchhunga. The matter is closely watched.

                    This is for your kind information and further necessary action.
