****9.11.2022 :** 27th Annual General Conference of Mizoram Finance &amp; Accounts Service Association was held on 9.11.2022 @10:30 a.m. at Aijal Club, Aizawl. Dr. R. Lalthangliana, Hon'ble Minister, H&amp;FW etc. graced the occasion as Chief Guest. During the conference, the following agendas concerning the state government were passed:**

 (i) Pension/retirement scheme should be formulated for employees under  Mizoram Government, who are not under CCS(Pension) Rules, 1972  and NPS.

 (ii) Various Central Rules being enforced within the state, which are not  suitable to the State should be amended so as to suit the state needs.

 (iii) Members of MF&amp;AS should attend training at RBI.

 (iv) Re-Delegation of Financial Power Order-2019 should be reviewed in  view of the difficulties being faced in its practical implementation.

 (v) Necessary action should be taken to the state government for   implementation of Leave Travel Concession of 2 (two) times for  employees under the State Government in their entire career.
