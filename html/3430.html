29.3.2022 :    A 2 (two) days long Sinlung Hills Council (SHC) Budget Session was commenced on 29.3.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at Tourist Lodge, Sakawrdai. Pu Malsawma Darngawn, Chairman SHC chaired the Session and 14 (fourteen) members attended. During the session, SHC Budget amounting to Rs. 601.34 lakhs was presented before the House and Budget discussion will be held tomorrow, i.e.,30.3.2022.
