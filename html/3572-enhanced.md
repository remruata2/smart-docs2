A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.7.2022_

****4,809 voters for Indian Presidential Election:**  Pu H.Lalrinawma, Mizoram Legislative Assembly Commissioner &amp; Secretary and Presiding Officer for the upcoming election of President of India which isscheduled to be held on 18th July 2022, called a Press Conference yesterday i.e.12.7.2022 at Assembly House Committee room and stated that there will be 4,809 voters in this election. The Presiding Officer also highlighted the following points -**

 a) Out of 4,809 total voters ,4,033 are MLAs,543 are MPs from Lok  Sabha and 233 from MPs of Rajya Sabha.

 b) The vote of one MLA and one MP will be worth 8 and 700    respectively.

 c) The total value of votes of 4,809 members will be 10,86,431.

 d) The total value of all MLAs of Mizoram will be 320 (i.e 40x8)

 e) Votes will be casted on 18th July 2022 from 10am to 5pm.

 f) Counting of votes will be conducted on 21st July 2022 at Parliament  House, New Delhi.

A. _Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 13.7.2022_

****1,294 cases registered by Police :**Under the Chairmanship of Pu C.Lalruaia, SP Aizawl District, Crime Review Meeting of Aizawl District was held in the SP, Aizawl Conference Hall. As per record, a total of 1,294 cases were registered in the 9 Police Stations within Aizawl District during the period between January and June, 2022. Among the cases registered, 919 (71.02%) were burglary compared to 55.12% of the previous year. Out of the total cases registered, 326 were Charge Sheeted, Final Report prepared on 88 cases and 844 cases are currently under investigations.**
