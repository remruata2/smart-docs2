              With reference to the above subject and Home department letter, I am sending herewith the required information as under for further necessary action.

              A five day long training on civil society based organisation organized by Chin National Council (CNC) was held on dt.21.6.2010 - dt.25.6.2010 at Archive Hall, Babutlang, Aizawl. The main purpose of this training is to farm an NGO in the Chin State of Myanmar and some part of Myanmar plain area to act as a non-political organisation for all Chin Communities which would be functioning somewhat like YMA (NGO Non political organisation) in Mizoram state. The training was attended by selected personalities from various communities within Chin State of Myanmar and 32 delegates attended the training. This training is sponsored by New Delhi based donor Agency National Reconciliatory program:

              The following CNC leaders gave lecture to the trainers

              1. Ralhnina                -           Gen. Secy.

              2. Thanglianpianga  -           The Gen. Secy.

              3. Lalhmangaihzuali            -           The Gen. Secy. CNC

              4. Lalzenghaki          -           WLC

              5. Khera Uk Lian      -           Invite CNF

 

              During this training, they didn't have any discussion/lecture about Politics in Myanmar. However the CNC held a meeting on dt. 30.5.2010 at their office, Upper Republic, Aizawl and decided to boycott the forthcoming Myanmar election as they did not expect for free and fair election under military Junta.

              As desired by MHA, VISA Regulation and Protected Area Permit regime may be more strictly enforced and it is also suggested that Integrated Check Post may be establish along Indo - Myanmar border.
