LATEST UPDATE ON RETALIATION AGAINST BRUS BY MIZO.

 

 

 

  1. According to information received, 32 out of 35 houses of Brus in Tumpang were set ablazed around  7 PM last night.  Mizo youths of Tuidam and some members of Taitesena Group YMA were suspected to be responsible for the arson.

 

  1. 63 Bru houses in Mualthuam were raged to ground last night around 9:30 PM alongwith several livestocks.  The Bru residents were board at village Hall and School.

 

  1. Report received that Mizo youths of  Villages of Zampui  Hill Range volunteered and stand on guard against reponsible Bru retaliatory attack in their respective villages.

 

  1. About 100 Brus who fled Mizoram following widesparead attack in Bru villages have been sheltered in a School building at Kanchanpur. They are reportedly Brus fleeing from Sotapa and Mualthuam villages.

 

  1. Procession of Bru refugees numbering around 5000 from Naisingpara camp had reached Hamsapara camp at around 1:00 PM. They intended to proceed upto Dosda.

 

  1. Report received that around 15 to 20 Bru houses had been set ablazed today around 12:00 noon.

 

  1. It is reportedly learnt that leaders of MZP Gen.Hqrs. had returned from Mamit District and reached Aizawl at around  4:30 AM.

 

  1. CYMA leaders are presently having an office bearers meeting discussing about the condition of Mizo residents of Zampui Hill Range.
