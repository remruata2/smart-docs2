A.Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt.12.2.2013:

Accused of attempted rape in Jail:  A man accused of attempting to rape a 6 years old girl under the jurisdiction of Phura Police Station, 100 km away from Saiha was arrested by the police on 10th Feb. 2013. People wanted to see the accused and started surrounding the Police Station, but before anything untoward happened the accused T.Lalengzauva, who claimed to hail from Thingfal village was secretly taken to Saiha Police Station on foot by police. The accused is now detained at Saiha district jail. Saiha police reported that since there is no doctor in Phura village, medical examination has not been done on the victim.

 

 

B.Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.12.2.2013:

Bribing of voters – A crime : Distribution of money, alcohol/liquor, meal and any gift to voters is considered bribery in the course of election. The giver and the receiver can be imprisoned and fined, said Government notification.

               Flying squad and Surveillance Team will watch over this issue before and during election, said the government and asked the public to report who breach this rule to Election Expenditure Monitoring Control Room, telephone no. 0389-2312200 and 0389-2311200.  

 

 

C.Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt.12.2.2013:

Exit Poll Prohibited: On 7.2.2013 Election commission of India announced that in the upcoming by-election at Chalfilh Constituency, exit poll is not allowed before the beginning of vote casting till half an hour after vote casting is over.

               The prohibition includes print and electronic media as well as any method of spreading exit poll through any means. Election Commission of India also prohibited electronic media from publishing any opinion poll or poll survey till 48 hours after vote casting is over.
