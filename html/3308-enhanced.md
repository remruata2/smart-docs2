**A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 1.5.2013**

**Case registered regarding KMMTTP**: After preliminary investigation, ACB registered a case concerning construction of road under Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP). It has been reported that there are few people who tried to gain compensation using fake LSC and Pass which causes problem in the distribution of compensation to be handed out for construction of road under KMMTTP.

**E. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE ZOZAM TIMES & AIZAWL POST Newspapers Dt.1.5.2013**

**Abusive substance worth Rs. 3,69,32,471/- seized** : Central Customs Department seized Pseudoephedrine tablets worth Rs. 3,69,32,471/- on Sunday. It was seized from a vehicle - 207 TATA Mobile coming from Zemabawk, City Bus stand to Champhai, the substance weighs 612 kgs. and worth Rs. 3,69,32,471/- at the market rate. Tablets of Pseudoephedrine were kept in 26 bags covered with 8 bundles of tin; the driver and vehicle were also seized.

**F. Front page of NEWSLINK Newspaper Dt.1.5.2013**

****Concern against foreigners** : An executive meeting of Central YMA on April 29 at its Headquarters Office expressed concern over increasing number of foreign nationals at Rangvamual and Phunchawng localities. It warned all foreigners to leave the localities on or before May 15, 2013.**

**G. Front page of NEWSLINK & THE ZOZAM TIMES Newspapers Dt.1.5.2013**

**Forced donation** : Central YMA in its press statement on April 30 alleged that Bru militants recently served an extortion notice to Mizo families living along Hachhek hill range inside Cachar District and Bairabi area. The Central YMA asked the militants to stop the extortion.
