SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/744

Dated: 2nd October, 2019.

 

            Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) convened its Executive Committee Meeting yesterday, i.e. 1.10.2019 @ 7:00 p.m at their office. The meeting was chaired by
Pu Vanlalruata, President, CYMA and around 40 (forty) members were present. The meeting discussed about the upcoming visit of Shri. Amit Shah, Union Home Minister and BJP National President to Mizoram on 5.10.2019 and the intention of the Central Government to reintroduce Citizenship Amendment Bill -2016 (CAB-16) in the Parliament.

 

            The meeting, as a mark of protest and opposition against the Central Government’s intention to revive CAB-16, resolved that Mizo people under the banner of NGOCC will assemble at the following places in Aizawl during the visit of Shri. Amit Shah, Union Home Minister :-

  1. Thuampui Helipad Junction
  2. Bawngkawn Traffic Point
  3. Chanmari Junction
  4. Zarkawt Junction
  5. Treasury Square (infront of Raj Bhavan)

 

            It also decided that ‘Pawndum’ (Mizo traditional shawl) will be worn by all and placards condemning and opposing CAB-16 will be widely displayed. Information received indicated that placards condemning CAB-16 will be displayed in three languages, viz. Mizo, English and Hindi, on the following lines :-

  1. Mizoram Against CAB-16.
  2. CAB-16 infringe Indian Secularism.
  3. Mizoram is for Mizos, No land for foreigner.
  4. CAB-16 is for Hindus and Against Mizo Christians.
  5. CAB-16 protects Chakma Buddhist Foreigner.
  6. Mr. Amit Shah, Please don’t introduce CAB in the Parliament.
  7. CAB is crime for Mizos.
  8. Down with CAB-2016.
  9. Exclude NE from CAB-16.
  10. We oppose CAB-16.

Contd...2/-

 

-2-

            Recapping recent development, the NGO Co-ordination Committee (CYMA, MUP, MHIP, MZP & MSU) meeting held on 27.9.2019 @ 3:00 p.m at CYMA Committee Room, Aizawl resolved to protest and condemn the Central Government’s intention to revive CAB-16 during the visit of Shri. Amit Shah to Mizoram on 5.10.2019. In this connection,
the Govt. of Mizoram represented by Pu Lalnunmawia Chuaungo, IAS, Chief Secretary;
Pu S.B.K. Singh, DGP(M); 2 (two) IGsP of Mizoram Police and Home Secretary, Govt. of Mizoram  held a meeting with leaders of NGOCC led by Pu Vanlalruata, Chairman and other 11 (eleven) members on 30.9.2019 @ 8:00 a.m at Chief Minister's Conference Hall. The Chief Secretary and DGP(M) requested NGOCC leaders to cancel their intention to receive the Union Home Minister by wearing 'Pawndum' and placards condemning and opposing CAB during his visit to Mizoram.

            Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
