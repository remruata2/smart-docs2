MUP Election criteria and eligibility:

                     MUP hi kum upa lam tawh (Kum 60 chin) te tana din niin, kum upa ten an lo upat hnu pawh a khawtlang a tangkaina an neih ve theih na tura din a nih angin, he pawl hian political party/sakhuana ah hleih bik a neilo a ni. Tin, he pawl hi an hruaitu thlanna ah, pawl dang te ang lo takin an felfai in, campaign/hmingchhiatna an neih thin pawh hriat a nilo a ni.

          Tuna an Dan Bu in a sawi ang chuan, inthlan dawn kum hian Executive Committee thukhawm in, anni hian Official Candidate tur mi sawmpahnih(12) an thlang chhuak a, General Assembly ni ah Floor atangin nomination siam leh theih niin, mi panga(5) floor atangin an nominate ve leh thin a ni. Tichuan, Official Candidate te nen an zavaiin mi sawmpasarih (17) niin, heng atang hian Post by Post in Office Bearers tur mi paruk(6) an thlang chhuak ta thin a ni. Heng thlanchhuah te hian thlan an nihna post hi term hnih(2) bak an chelh thei lo a ni.

          MUP Dan Bu ah hian MUP Office Bearers turin Aizawl ami chauh thlan tur tih chuang lo mahse, Gen. Hqrs awmna hmun te a nih angin, Aizawl lam mi deuh hlir thlan an ni tlangpui a ni.
