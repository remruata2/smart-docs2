A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 25.9.2013

Total Bandh Called Off Peacefully: Total Bandh was organised on 24th September at Khawzawl and Hnahthial to demand for the function of district as both towns were announced District Headquarters a few years back. Bandh was well-responded by the public and the resolution they passed said that districts be made function before the announcement of the date of the upcoming General Election.

            Khawzawl started bandh at 5:00AM and called it off at 5:00PM while Hnahthial started at 5:00AM and called it off at 2:00PM. Nothing untoward happened during total bandh.

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 25.9.2013

Case Discharged: KL Liana, Special Judge discharged the case of former Director of AH&Vety. as ACB produced prosecution sanction issued by Commissioner/Secretary DP&AR and not the Governor as the law dictates. According to reports ACB is supposed to take prosecution sanction from Governor but it took it from Commissioner/Secretary DP&AR. The court could not accept the prosecution sanction as it was not taken from where the law demands; as such is the situation Dr. C. Sangnghina’s case regarding corruption was discharged by Special Court.

            Mizoram does not have Directorate of Prosecution but has General Registration Branch led by Inspector of Police.
