A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 9.5.2014_

**Border Dispute****:**Chief Secretary Pi L.Tochhong asked DC of Kolasib, Jitender Kumar to always have a close watch over Mizoram and Assam Border dispute and to do his best for the betterment of the situation.

 Chief Secretary held a meeting at Kolasib PWD Lodge yesterday with Kolasib DC and some important officials to discuss the situation at Buhchangphaai Ramthar Zau. Kolasib DC gave a report on their handling of the problem at Buhchangphai border. He reported that there is no enmity between dwellers of Ramthar Zau and Hailakandi and that the problem is created by a few misbehaved people. Kolasib DC will visit Ramthar Zau following the request of Chief Secretary.

 1 Company of Mizoram 1st IR Battalion stays at Ramthar Zau and no unfavourable situation has arisen.

A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 9.5.2014_

****4 Bru Armed Gang Arrested****:**Assam Rifles arrested the 4 members of Bru National Army (BNA) namely, Lalthazuala, Chief Organiser; Rajesh Chorkhy, General Secretary; Luarjoy Reang and Bailoram Reang and seized 4 rifles &amp; 122 ammunitions of AK-47, 2 nos. of 12 bore double barrel gun, 1 no. of 9mm Pistol, 4 mobile handsets and some materials from them. Assam Rifles handed over the arrested persons to Assam Police as they were arrested at Cachar District, Assam. Interrogation had also been done by the Mizoram Police at Dholai. (The detail is already reflected on this unit Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2014/510 Dated: 6th May 2014). **
