**A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.25.2.2013:**

****Caught Red-Handed****:**On the morning of last Saturday, Ramhlun Sports Complex Village Defence Party (VDP) caught David Lalhmingsanga red handed while burgling a house and handed him over to the police. David Lalhmingsanga (23) was burgling ICI D/S Quarter at Ramhlun Sports Complex; he was caught while packing TV, DVD and some other items inside the quarters. Report said he also broke steel almirah and took money. He also occasionally pretend to be an employee of P&amp;E Department and a member of HPC(D) to extort money from some people. **

**B. Second page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.25.2.2013:**

****Fake HPC(D) Commander arrested****:** Congress party reports that a man who claimed himself to be the commander of HPC(D) made a call to Village Council President (VCP) of Phuaibuang threatening to work against Dr. H.Ngurdingliana (Congress Party Candidate) so that he would not win the election. After CID(SB) investigated the case, the culprit, Lalrempianga Hrangchal who migrated from Jeribam, Assam and now lives in a rented house owned by Zoramkhuma of Bethlehem Vengthlang, was arrested on 21.2.2013 @ 11:00 PM, the Sim Card he used for making that call was also seized.**

**C. Front page of VANGLAINI & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt.25.2.2013:**

****CCTV in Aizawl****:** The State Home Department is gearing up to install CCTVs at 33 selected locations within Aizawl city as part of security measures. A two member team from Union Home Ministry had arrived Aizawl on 22nd February to inspect the proposed sites. A sub-committee has been formed to identify the sites. The four police stations within Aizawl city and Traffic office will function as sub-control rooms while Aizawl S.P office will be Control Room.**
