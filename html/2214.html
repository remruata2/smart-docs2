Brief history :

 

1.  12. 7. 2008 : Government of Mizoram proclaimed Saitual as a district by   issuing a Notification.

 

2. 21.7.2016 : Saitual District Demand Committee along with leaders of Congress Party within Chalfilh and Lengteng Assembly Constituencies had met Dr. Ngurdingliana, MLA, Chalfilh Constituency and Pu H. Rohluna, Hon'ble Minister, E&F etc./ MLA, Lengteng Constituency on 21.7.2016 seeking their support in their demand for creation of Saitual Administrative District. It was reported that both the MLAs assured them that they would give necessary support in their demand.

 

3. 27. 7. 2017 :Residents within Saitual town area hoisted white flags at their respective houses to show their demand for the implementation of Saitual District.

 

4. 28.7.2017 : Residents within Saitual town area hoisted white flags for the second consecutive day on 28.7.2017 at their respective houses in demand for the implementation of Saitual District.

 

5. 31.7.2017 : Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) took out a procession on 31.7.2017 within Saitual Town area in continuation of their demand for the implementation of Saitual District. The procession was commenced from two different junctions, i.e. Saitual Hmar Veng and Keifang. As many as 3,000 (three thousand) residents of Saitual town had participated at the procession and they assembled at the courtyard of SDO (Civil) Office, Saitual.

 

6. 1.8.2017 : Leaders of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) met Pu Lal Thanhawla, Hon'ble Chief Minister of Mizoram on 1.8.2017 and submitted a Memorandum requesting him to take initiative for the implementation of Saitual District. As suggested by the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Mizoram, leaders of SDIDC met Pu Lalmalsawma, Chief Secretary, Government of Mizoram.

 

7. 12.8.2017 : Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) held a meeting on 12.8.2017 in connection with their demand for the implementation of Saitual District and it was attended by Thirty(30) members. The meeting decided to organise Total Bandh within Saitual area ( Ruallung, Rulchawm, Sihfa, Keifang) on Independence Day. They decided to request Pu R.Lalrinawma, Dy.Speaker, Mizoram Legislative Assembly through SDO(C ), Saitual not to attend the Independence Day function at Saitual as Chief Guest. Consequent upon this, Independence Day,2017 was not celebrated and no official function was conducted in Saitual. No untoward incident was reported on the day itself.

 

8. 27.4.2018: Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) organised Total Bandh on 27.4.2018 from 5:00 am within Saitual town area to pressurise the Government in connection with their demand for a new District was called off at 4:00 pm without any untoward incident reported during the Bandh.

            In a statement issued on this day, the SDIDC justified their demand by stating that it would solve the administrative problem by downsizing the large area covered by Aizawl District, and also insisted that even the AICC President, Shri. Rahul Gandhi and Pu Lal Thanhawla, Hon'ble Chief Minister had promised them a District status earlier.

            The SDIDC urged upon the Government to take steps to improve road conditions in the area on priority basis before the onset of monsoon. They also expressed appreciation to Congress Units and Block level leaders within Tawi Constituency for their willingness to surrender/submit their seals in support of the demand for a new District.

 

9. 1.5.2018 : Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) in a statement issued on 1.5.2018 thanked the general public within the demand area for their support during the Total Bandh observed on 27.4.2018, and appealed to them to extend their support in the event of further agitation. The SDIDC leaders are also intending to meet the Hon’ble Chief Minister for which they entrusted their two advisers, namely - Pu C. Zahmuaka and Pu C. Chawngkunga to seek prior appointment.

 

10. Leaders of the SDIDC (Saitual District Implementation Demand Committee) accompanied by Pu VL Krosshnehzova, President, Saitual Youth Organisation met Pu Zodintluanga, Hon'ble UD&PA Minister and Pu P.C. Lalthanliana, Hon’ble LAD Minister at their offices on 16.5.2018. The SDIDC leaders were learnt to have requested the Ministers to include their demand in the agenda for the next Cabinet Meeting. It is learnt that the SDIDC leaders are also intending to meet the Hon'ble Chief Minister as early as possible.

 

11.  The SDIDC (Saitual District Implementation Demand Committee) leaders had met Pu R.Lalzirliana, Hon’ble Home Minister and Pu Lalsawta, Hon’ble Finance Minister on 7.6.2018 at their offices in pursuance of their demand for implementation of a District status for Saitual. It is learnt that the SDIDC leaders were disappointed with the Ministers’ response to their demand, and information received suggested further that they may resort to adopting a stronger measures in this regard.

 

12. Leaders of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) led by
Pu Rohmingliana, Chairman, SDIDC met senior leaders of Political Parties (MPCC, MNF, ZNP) on 8.8.2018 in their respective party offices at Aizawl. It has been reported that Pu Zoramthanga, President, MNF and Pu Lalduhoma, President, ZNP assured leaders of SDIDC that Saitual District will be functional if their respective party comes to power. It is also reported that leaders of SDIDC held a telephonic conversation with Prof. J.V. Hluna, President, Mizoram BJP who had also reportedly assured a fully functioning Saitual District if the party comes to power. The 5 (five) SDIDC leaders left Aizawl for Saitual in the afternoon of 8.8.2018.

 

13. An Office Bearers' Emergency Meeting of Saitual District Implementing Demand Committee (SDIDC) was held on 13.8.2018 @ 7:00 p.m at the residence of
Pu Rohmingliana, Chairman, SDIDC and it was attended by 4 (four) members. The meeting elaborated on the following points :-

           

i)   SDIDC will not allow Saitual public and Government officials (other than SDO/C) to             attend the Independence Day 2018 programme at Saitual Sub-Divisional Officer (C) Office. SDIDC stated that Saikhawpui Thalai Pawl (Youths of Saitual) will be engaged to prevent Government officials and Saitual public from participating in the     Independence Day 2018 programme.

     

ii)  SDIDC will launch road blockade at Khuangthli Kawn to prevent Pu Chalrosanga,      MLA from attending Independence Day 2018 programme at Saitual.

                                   

            It has been reported that leaders of SDIDC expressed serious concern about the presence of Police Force at Saitual on 14.8.2018 and leaders of SDIDC reportedly reiterated their decision to prevent Government officials and Saitual public from attending the Independence Day Programme on 15.8.2018.
