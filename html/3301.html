A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.18.3.2013

A Bike ran over a woman: A bike driven by Lalhnehzova (20) s/o Zonunthar ran over Vanlalsiami (30) w/o Ramdinmawia of Darlak in Mamit at 8:30AM Saturday ie, 16.3.2013. She was rushed to Mamit Hospital but succumbed to her injury at around 11:45AM on the same day. Lalhnehzova is detained at Mamit Police custody for further investigation. Besides this incident, Lalhnehzova had run over and killed an old woman in 2011 by using bike.

 

B. Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.18.3.2013

25 days past: It has been 25 days that NLFT have abducted the 5 hostages. One hostage, Vanlalruatkima @ Nelson of Hunthar Veng, Aizawl made a call to their brother-in-law Vannunthianghlima of Hunthar Veng three days back asking him about the government response regarding the kidnap, and told him that the abductors urged the govt. to take immediate action. All the hostages are alive and their health is also fine.

            One of the hostages, Lalrammawia @ Mawitea, brother of Nelson is to get married on 4th April, 2013 but as he is still in the hands of the abductors, his wedding will have to be delayed.
