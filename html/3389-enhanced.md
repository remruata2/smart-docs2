A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.4.2014_

**Bomb Exploded at SSA School****:**Assam SSA School at Zophai, Bairabi was bombed at around 4:00 am yesterday. The school is within Bairabi area of Kolasib District near 1st IR Battalion Camp; bomb exploded twice and destroyed the roofs and walls of the school building.
