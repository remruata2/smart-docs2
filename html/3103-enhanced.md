A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 13.8.2013_

****Chiahpuam Scam**: In a press conference held recently, Helena Lalsawmkimi said that regarding the Chiahpuam Scam she had met Police Investigation Team, Court, SP, DGP and Finance Dept. but no one could provide documents. So, she collected some important documents regarding Chiahpuam Scam from newspapers and Cable TV and based on these documents she called press conference on 27th July 2013.**

 Helena said that one police officer alleged her of defaming the police and told her to apoligize within one week if not defamation case will be filed. But Helena said that she has no reason to apologize and if the police wish they can explain everything in a press conference and that she has been waiting for five (5) years for the truth to be revealed. She also said that if government does not do its duty then several documents she kept will be publicized.
