A. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt. 14.2.2014_

****Crime Review Meeting****:**Mizoram Police had 2013 Crime Review meeting at Police Headquarters Arya Mess yesterday. Mr. C.Zorammuana, DIG(NR) presided over the meeting and Mr. Amulya Patnaik, DGP, Mizoram also attended the meeting. In 2013, 2,285 cases (ie., 1,728 IPC &amp; 557 non-IPC) were registered which is 302 cases less compared to the cases registered in 2013 (ie., 2,587 cases) .**

 Crime Review meeting also discussed how to empower the Police to decrease crime rate and how the Police and NGO should have better co-operation, and also discussed about crime pending cases.

 Crime Review meeting was attended by SP of each unit in Mizoram District and Commandant of each battalion.
