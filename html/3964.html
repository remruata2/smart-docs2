(ii) Infiltration/apprehension of BD nationals/Rohingyas:

 

                         

            The Indo-Bangladesh border in Mizoram Lawngtlai and Lunglei District are actively patrolled by the BSF and the activities along the border is closely monitored by the District Police.

            On January 7, 2025, at approximately 8:30 AM, a group of 28 individuals from Bandarban District, Bangladesh, entered Hruitezawl (112.7 km from Lawngtlai Town) in Lawngtlai District, Mizoram.  

 

             On 11th January, 2025, a team of Assam Rifles, BDO Office, Bungtlang South and Vasei PS pushed back 28 refugees, they were believed to proceed Myanmar via Zorinpui, Lawngtlai.

 

 

 

            On 18th January, 2025, 05 (five) members Bawm community of Bangladesh origin had reached Vathuampui, Lawngtlai from Sameng Village, Myanmar by crossing Myanmar Border .On 21st  January 2025 they were taken into Assam rifles custody. The refugees were then pushed back by the Assam Rifles on Dt. 22nd January 2025 at 10:24 am from Kakichhuah village by Boat. 

 

             On March 2, 2025, a group of 55 individuals from Bangladesh entered Bungtlang South I & II in Lawngtlai District, Mizoram and on March 7, 2025, at 12:30 pm, they were pushed backed by the Assam Rifles stationed at Tuithumhnar  from Kakichhuah village to the Myanmar border.
