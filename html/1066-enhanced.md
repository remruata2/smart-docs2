****ASSESSMENT OF SECURITY THREAT PERCEPTION OF DISTRICT/SUBORDINATE COURTS IN MIZORAM****

**1. Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench and District Court, Aizawl:**

  At present, the Gauhati High Court, Aizawl Bench security is manned by CRPF. They have 3(three) static guard consisting of 2(two) Subordinate Officers and 18(eighteen) personnel.

District Court Security is manned by 3rd MAP and it comprised of 2(two) NCOs and 4 (four) personnel.

After careful assessment of threat perception and considering the current security deployment, it is considered not necessary to enhance or revamp the existing security arrangement at these two Courts. 

**2. Lunglei District Court :**

  Security personnel from 2ndBn. MAP are being deployed for guarding the Court premises comprising of 1 (one) Havildar, 1 (one) Naik and 3 (three) constables. As there is no imminent threat, the existing security deployment is considered sufficient.

**3. Siaha District Court :**

  At present, 1(one) PSO each is provided to Addl. District &amp; Session Judge and CJM, whereas, no security personnel is deployed for guarding the District Court premises. As per assessment, security guard may be provided as and when the District court functions at their New Building complex.

**4. Champhai District Court :**

  Apart from providing PSOs to Addl. District &amp; Session Judge and CJM, the Court complex is being guarded by one section of armed personnel from 2nd IR Bn. which is considered sufficient at present.

**5. Kolasib District Court :**

  As there is no imminent threat and after careful assessment, the existing security arrangement is considered sufficient.

**6. Serchhip District Court :**

****Serchhip District Court is located at an isolated place, and there is no nearby staff quarters or civilian residence. There were instances of burglary at the District Court in the past year. Security personnel may be deployed to guard the District Court.

**7. Lawngtlai District Court:**

  Due to its location, sometime they dealt with cases involving accused from foreign country and if it is feasible, security personnel may be deployed to guard the Court premises.

**8. Mamit District Court :**

**** Security personnel from 4th IR Bn are being deployed to guard Mamit District Court and it consists of 2(two) NCOs and 4(four) constables. Considering the threat perception and having proper fencing, the existing security deployment is considered sufficient.
