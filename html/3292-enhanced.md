**A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.5.3.2013**

**A thief caught on CCTV camera at Lengpui Airport****:**A thief stole the luggage of an old man who arrived from Kolkata in Jet Airways while he bought something from the AVA (Action Vision Accountance) shop at Arrival lounge in Terminal building.

 The theft was immediately reported to Airport security, CCTV clearly captured the action of the man who committed the crime. But as the culprit already left the airport; it was reported to Vaivakawn Police Station. The Airport security pursued the thief in a vehicle and caught him at Vaivakawn.

**B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt.5.3.2013**

****Bru fast and pray for the release of the 5 kidnap hostages****:**The occupants of seven Tripura Bru Relief Camps were reported to have a fasting prayer yesterday for the release of the 5 hostages who were kidnapped from Dampa Tiger Reserve by an insurgent group, believed to be NLFT on 19th Feb. 2013. Christians prayed in Church and Hindus prayed in Mandir. Report says fasting prayer was offered from 06:00AM till 05:00PM in Naisingpara, Ashapara, Hazacherra, Naisaupara, Kaskaupara, Khakchangpara and Hamsapara.**

Meanwhile, no news has been heard about the hostages, the Government is doing its best to rescue the hostages safely.
