****12.04.2022 :** Mizoram Upa Pawl (MUP) General Headquarters held its Executive Committee Meeting on 12.04.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at Synod Conference Centre, Mission Veng, Aizawl. Pu Lalhuapzauva, President, MUP chaired the meeting and 75 (seventy five) members attended. The meeting passed the following concerning the State and Central government**

 (i) Selling of Petrol in black market should be completely banned.

 (ii) The State government should take necessary action regarding credit of  Gas subsidy into respective consumer account.

 (iii) MUP General Headquarters should take necessary action for release of  2nd Instalment Fund under Housing for All.

 (iv) The State government should take necessary action to give awareness on  various welfare schemes from the Central government.

****31.10.2022 :** Mizoram Upa Pawl (MUP) Mamit District Headquarters OB meeting held today, i.e., 31.10.2022 expressed their support of Hachhek Bial Kuhva Chingtu Pawl (HBKCP) intention to organize indefinite Total Bandh within Hachhek area on 1.11.2022 due to pending settlement of the difficulties being faced in transporting locally harvested areca nut in Hachhek constituency. The meeting urged the state government to take immediate necessary action for solving the said problem**
