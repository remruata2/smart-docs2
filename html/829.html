Subject:   3rd DG level talks between NCB, India and CCDAC, Myanmar.

 

Ref:          No. IGP(Int)/A-1/2017/241 dt.21.11.2017.

 

 

 

                  With reference to the letter no. and subject cited above, the following points are suggested to be raised during the 3rd DG level talks between NCB, India and CCDAC, Myanmar :-

 

I)     Strengthening of co-ordination between Government of Myanmar and Government of Mizoram to counter smuggling of drugs (Heroin No.4 and Methamphetamine, etc.) from Myanmar to Mizoram and smuggling of drugs (Pseudoephedrine, etc.) from Mizoram to Myanmar.

 

II)    Joint operation against drugs smuggling be conducted by Mizoram Police/Assam Rifles and Myanmar Police/Army at regular interval.

 

III) Detection and necessary legal action be taken against illicit drugs industry in Myanmar by Myanmar Police/Army.

 

                  This is for your information and further necessary action.
