A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 16.4.2013_

_Persons without ILP arrested:_ Different Police Stations had conducted drive of non-Mizos living in Aizawl city without ILP on the night of 14th April. Aizawl Police Station staff arrested 11, Bawngkawn Police Station staff 18, Kulikawn Police Station staff 12, and Vaivakawn Police Station staff 10 non-Mizos. They will be brought to the court and pushed back to their respective homeland if convicted.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAMTIMES newspapers Dt. 16.4.2013_

**_Meeting of SP and YMA_: Leaders of YMA Sub-Hqrs. had a meeting with Serchhip SP, Romil Baanya at his office yesterday. The meeting was held to discuss and find a solution to avoid many road accidents which occurred on the newly constructed NH-54. SP told them that those who drive faster than 30Km/h, who have no proper documents and who park vehicles on the roadside and congesting the road will be punished for safety measures to avoid road accidents.**
