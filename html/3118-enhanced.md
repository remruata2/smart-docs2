****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/915

Dated: 27th December, 2021

 Information received stated that on Dt. 23.12.2021, 5 (five) youths from Laisawral village, Lunglei District viz. (1) Lalhriatpuia (19) S/o Makthanga; (2) Ramdingngheta (18) S/o Biaklawmawma; (3) Remtluangpuia (16) S/o Lalduhsaka; (4) Remlaldika (18) S/o Lalchhanhima and (5) Lalremsanga (17) S/o Biaktlawmlova who went to Marpara North, Mamit District for shopping were disturbed by 3 (three) Chakma youths viz. (1) Kinon Joti Chakma (21) S/o Kristo Joti; (2) Nimol Bosu (18) S/o Mukdydur and (3) Lalenkawla (19) S/o Rajdon and one mizo youth named Lalchhandama (22) S/o HTC Chuaukunga (Teacher, Synod School, Marpara North) all from Marpara North, Mamit District and in order to escape further escalation of tension, the stated youths from Laisawral village immediately evacuated the area. However, the above mentioned youths from Marpara North, Mamit District chased them and continued to disturbed them while they were shopping at one shop at Marpara South, Lunglei District.

 Upon learning the above stated incident, OC Marpara North PS has taken immediate necessary action and summoned all youths involved at Marpara Police Station the same evening to prevent further escalation of tension and later released all the youths after both parties amicably settled the matter and taking necessary preventive action.

 Further, OC, Marpara PS held a meeting with 10 (ten) leaders of Laisawral village led by Pu C. Hmingthansanga, President YMA Laisawral and Pu Soimber, President Zonal Young Chakma Association the same evening i.e., 23.12.2021 @ 6:00 p.m. at Marpara PS as the stated incident could lead to communal tension. After discussing the matter at length, both parties amicably settled the matter the same evening. 

 However, one person named Vena of Sesawm village, Lunglei District circulated video footage of the incident on social media platforms, which later went viral. The viral video angered residents of Laisawral village and tried to react on the said incident. Upon learning development of the situation and in order to prevent escalation of communal tension the below mentioned involved youths were apprehended by Marpara PS yesterday evening i.e., 26.12.2021 and continued to detain at Marpara PS for their personal safety:

(i) Kinon Joti Chakma (21) S/o Kristo Joti of Marpara North

(ii) Nimol Bosu (18) S/o Mukdydur of Marpara North

(iii)Lalenkawla (19) S/o Rajdon of Marpara North

(iv)Lalchhandama (22) S/o HTC Chuaukunga (Teacher, Synod School, Marpara

 North) of Marpara North

 In connection with the stated incident, residents of Laisawral village intended to proceed to Marpara village this morning i.e., 27.12.2021 in protest against the stated incident. Upon learning the stated development, OC Bunghmun PS and party immediately rushed to Laisawral village to contain the situation. However, around 50 (fifty) residents of Laisawral village proceeded upto Marpara PS today i.e., 27.12.2021 @ 2:30 p.m. On their way to Marapa PS, residents of Laisawral village assaulted Ramesh (24) S/o Ajoti Bikash Chakma of Marpara North and sustained minor injury due to the assault. He was immediately evacuated to Marpara PHC for medical treatment and later released after giving First Aid.

 The situation was under control after OC Marpara PS and party rendered their best effort and the crowd dispersed this evening @ 4:30 p.m. without any untoward incident. The incident is being enquired by Marpara PS.

 Information received also stated that the above mentioned 5 (five) youths of Laisawral village intended to lodge FIR at Bunghmun PS as the PO i.e. Marpara South falls under its Jurisdiction. However, in connection with the stated incident, no FIR has been received by OC, Bunghmun PS at the time of writing the report.

 Situation is under close watch.
