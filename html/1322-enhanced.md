****2. Need to strengthen mechanism of State Police to monitor activities and Visa violations by foreigners during their visit to the region.****

(i) All the District Foreigner Registration Officers i.e Superintendent of Police (SP) of all district of Mizoram(SP Mamit, SP Lawngtlai, SP Champhai, SP Lunglei, SP Siaha, SP Serchhip &amp; SP Kolasib) except Superintendent of Police (SP) CID(SB),Mizoram (i.e., State Foreigner Registration Officer) should be made functional/operational for providing services to Foreign nationals like issue of Registration of Foreigner’s Certificate and also to enable them to monitor the movement and activities of foreign nationals visiting their respective district. All the offices of the District SPs should also be strengthen with facilities (online/internet connectivity, etc.) and trained manpower.

(ii) Foreigner Detention Centre should be set up in Mizoram to enable State authorities to house the deportees (overstaying of Foreigners, etc) and to ensure that they are kept in check until the deportation process is completed.
