A. Front page of  VANGLAIN  newspaper  Dt. 30.09.2022

Mizoram having the highest rate of Malaria : Regional Review meeting organised by National Vector Borne Disease Control Programme (NVBDCP)  and Health & Family Welfare Department of 10 states was inaugurated by Mizoram Health Minister, Dr. R. Lalthangliana  yesterday (29.9.2022) at Assembly Annexe Conference Hall, Aizawl. As per the records of this meeting, which will last for 3 days, Malaria cases were detected in Mizoram on 7,672 persons during the period between January- August, 2022.  During the year 2021, 5637 cases of Malaria were also detected where 80 % of them were from Lunglei, lawngtlai, Siaha and Mamit districts. Hence, these four districts are included in the "Intensified Control Phase (High Endemic District), which is the highest in the category. All these factors propelled Mizoram state as the state with the highest  rate of Malaria in all the states of  India.

                       

 

B.Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.30.09.2022

Seizure of Heroin and areca nuts:  Special Branch under Serchhip District Police on 27th September, 2022 recovered 63.49 grams of Heroin at New Serchhip from Thangzuala (45), his wife, Sangkhumi and their son, Lalnunthara (20)  of Champhai Electric veng. The seized items are estimated to be worth around Rs. 30 lakhs in the international market.

            Meanwhile, on the night of 28th September 2022, North Vanlaiphai police recovered  9,900 kilograms of areca nuts from four trucks which were smuggled from Champhai along with its suspected owner, F.Lalthanliana (43) s/o Zahleibila of Champhai. The seized areca nuts are estimated to be worth Rs.3,91,200 and were handed over to officials of Lunglei Custom deparatment.
