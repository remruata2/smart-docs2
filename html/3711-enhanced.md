****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/106

Dated: 11th February, 2021

 In continuation to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2020/099 Dt.9.2.2021, Bairabi All NGO Emergency Meeting was held today, i.e., 11.2.2021 @ 1:30 p.m. at Bairabi North YMA Hall. Pu Lalsanglura, President Joint YMA Bairabi presided the meeting and 30 (thirty) members attended. The meeting decided the following :-

(i) IR Battalion personnel should perform round the clock duty at Ramri Lui  (Dholachora) and the State government should construct IR Camp near Ramri  Lui. If IR Camp is not constructed before 12.2.2021 sunset, it will be constructed  on 13.2.2021 by Bairabi youths.

(ii) Ramri Lui Bridge constructed by Assam should be dismantled.

(ii) IR Camp should be shifted at Assam-Mizoram border. The state government  should construct proper IR Camp and Quarters and in this regard VC Bairabi  should take immediate necessary action with the government.

(v) State government should give compensation to injured persons.

(vi) Temporary road should be constructed beyond Zophai BOP upto Ramri Lui  under the guidance of Jt. YMA Bairabi on 13.2.2021.

Today’s afternoon at around 3:15 p.m., around 30 (thirty) members of Bairabi Thalai Pawl tried to proceed to disputed road construction site to verify whether residents of Assam gathered at the site. They also criticised presence of Assam police on the Mizoram side of Ramri Lui which they considered to be Mizoram-Assam border. However, Mizoram Police personnel deployed at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram stopped them at COVID-19 screening point, 4 (four) persons who are selected among them proceeded to disputed road construction site, but no resident of Assam was found. Upon learning this, they dispersed at around 4:30 p.m. this evening.

 Situation is under close watch.
