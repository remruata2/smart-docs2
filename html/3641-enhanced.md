**25.3.2022 :** A 4-day long LADC Budget Session commenced on 25.3.2022 @10:30 a.m. at the LADC Session Hall, Lawngtlai. The Session was chaired by Pu VL Hmuaka, LADC Chairman and 24 (twenty four) members were present. The Chairman made obituary references on the demise of 2 (two) Ex-MDC, i.e., Pu C. Sangchhunga and Pu Lalthlamuana. 2 (two) Starred Questions were asked and oral answers given in today's session and Pu V. Zirsanga, CEM LADC, presented the LADC Budget Estimates for 2022-2023 amounting to Rs. 20983.96 lakhs. 6 (six) Official Bills were also introduced in the session which was adjourned for the day at 12:40 p.m.

**28.3.2022 :** 2nd Sitting of 11th LADC Budget Session was held on 28.3.2022 @ 10:30 a.m. at LADC Session Hall, Lawngtlai.
