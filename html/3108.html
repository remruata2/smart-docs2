A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.8.2013

The  murderer’s brother not involved so far: Police PRO, Lalremsanga, Addl.SP, Aizawl told Vanglaini that the rumour of Lalremruata Ralte’s (the murderer) brother being involved in the heinous murder of K.Zohmangaihi (49) d/o Ramfangzauva of Zarkawt is not true.

      Lalremsanga said that the Police are doing their best to investigate the case in the death of K.Zohmangaihi and no one has been found to involve so far other than her husband Lalremruata Ralte.

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.8.2013

Revenue Department lodged FIR: Revenue Department lodged an FIR at Kawnpui Police Station on 19th August 2013 regarding the compensation in the Bairabi – Sairang railway track to be constructed. Meanwhile, as ACB is investigating about compensation, Police decided not to register a case.

      Revenue Department had already lodged an FIR at Kawnpui Police Station on 13th August 2013 regarding the same subject but case was not registered and they lodged it again. 13 names appeared in the previous FIR and 14 names appeared in the second FIR.

 

B. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 21.8.2013

2 houses dismantled due to landslide: Two Assam Type houses occupied by 4 families at Tuikual South were dismantled due to landslide caused by heavy rain at 12:00 midnight to 2:00 AM on 20th August 2013. The houses were owned by Lalnghinglova which was occupied by 3 families and Lalluaia which was occupied by K.Lalengmawia. Lalnghinglova and his family, and the occupants of his house carried out their belongings to safety earlier while K.Lalengmawia and his family did not do the same and landslide swept away some of their belongings.

      Meanwhile sinking of land in the area of Ramhlun Sports Complex caused 14 houses occupied by 41 families dismantled. The homeless families now reside in the Sports Council building and Church hall and some families stay at their relatives’ homes. For the time being, Mizoram Government provide ` 40/- a day for each adult and ` 30/- for each child of the victims of the sinking land and this will be carried on for 40 days, said leaders of Ramhlun Sports Complex locality.
