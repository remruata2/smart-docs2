****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/651

Dated: 2nd September, 2019.

Reference to our DSI dated 30.8.2019, Item No. E(a). Tlabung Branch YMA held its Executive Meeting on 31.8.2019 @ 7:00 a.m at YMA Run, Tlabung. The meeting was chaired by Pu Zokunga, President and 25 (twenty five) members were present. The meeting discussed the issue of alleged illegal settlement of Chakmas in Tlabung area and highlighted that despite their deadline for vacation of all illegal Chakma villages, i.e. August, 2019, only Belva and Sazekchhuah villages have complied. The meeting, paying regard to the appeal of the Government decided to stretch their timeline and to conduct spot survey of all illegal settlements during 1.9.2019 – 13.9.2019, starting with visit of Khojoisury on 1.9.2019. It also decided to convene a meeting with Pu Henry Lalrawnkima, SDO (C ), Tlabung on 6.9.2019.

In pursuance of resolution of the above meeting, 20 (twenty) Executive Members of Tlabung Branch YMA, led by Pu Zokunga, President, visited Khojoisury on 1.9.2019 @ 1:00 p.m and held a meeting with Khojoisury VC, YCA and other leaders of the community at Primary School, Lare Veng, Khojoisury and around 50 (fifty) persons attended. Tlabung Branch YMA leaders expressed their disappointment over the inaction of the latter for vacation of illegal villages and considered this as a mark of disrespect of Tlabung YMA. They further stated that if no positive action is taken by Khojoisury leaders during September, 2019, Tlabung YMA will not be held responsible in case something happens to them and this should be considered as ‘Last Warning’. The meeting ended at 3:30 p.m without any untoward incident.

 Situation under close watch.
