A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 05.12.2022

Four persons killed in road accident: On the morning of 4.12.2022,  four persons were killed after the vehicle in which they were travelling, Mahindra Pik up, rolled down  around 300 meters  from the road to around at the outskirt of Thingfal village, Lunglei district. After news of this accident was heard, volunteers under disaster Respond Team of Central YMA, Thingfal community and police personnel conduct search of the bodies of the victims. The victims ( three adults and one minor) had returned from Delhi to celebrate Christmas festival in Mizoram.

 

 

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 05.12.2022

Meeting of Brahmaputra Board: on 3rd November, 2022 (Saturday) the 78th  meeting of Brahmaputra Board members was held at Secretariat Conference hall, Aizawl and was presided over by  Pu Rajiv Yadav, their President. This meeting, which was attended by officials from various North Eastern States approved different projects such as - Springshed Management Project and  Anti Erosion Project, Survey & Investigation for Flood Protection and Mitigation Project, Khawthlangtuipui.

 

 

 

C. Second page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 05.12.2022

Around Rs 600 lakhs deposited by Geology and Mining department:  On the night of 2.12.2022, under the Chairmanship of Dr. R.Lalthangliana, Minister, Geology & Mining department Mizoram, officials of this department held a meeting on the front yard of their Directorate and announced that their department had submitted around Rs. 600 lakhs to the state treasury as a revenue. In this meeting, the Minister stressed the importance of Geology& Mining department stating that if the staffs performed their duties efficiently, it can led to the development of Mizoram state. The Minister further highlighted his previous meeting with Commerce Minister of Bangladesh where the latter mentioned the requirements of stones from the quarry of Mizoram which could be a great source of income for the state. presently, there are around 46 staffs in Mizoram Geology& Mining department including their Director.
