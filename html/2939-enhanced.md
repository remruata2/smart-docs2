****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/571

Dated: 30th July, 2019.

 Reference to our DSI dated 29.7.2019, Item No. F(a). The 2 (two) apprehended Bangladeshi nationals, namely (i) Mahmud Hassan (25) s/o Gulbarg Ali, Magura, Bangladesh and (ii) Mohamad Sayad Hussain (35) s/o Shayaib Hussain, Magura, Bangladesh alongwith the escorted parties arrived Aizawl PS today, i.e. 30.7.2019 @ 9:30 a.m. Interrogation was conducted throughout the day jointly by various Intelligence Agencies at CID (SB) office. The following, though yet to be ascertained, have been revealed during preliminary interrogation:-

1. Both had illegally crossed the Indo-Bangla border at different time and place. Mahmud Hassan had crossed the IB border in October, 2018 at Boxanagar (W.Tripura) whereas Mohd. Sayad Hussain entered via Kamlasagar, Tripura in Novemebr, 2018. Mahmud Hassan had visited Bangalore and stayed there since November, 2018. Mohd. Sayad Hossain had been staying in Ramnagar area of Agartala for some time and then went to Dharmanagar in February, 2019. During May, 2019 he again shifted to Kanchanpur. The two met at Dharmanagar Railway Station on 22 July, 2019 and entered Mizoram on 25.7.2019.
2. Mobile handset belonging to Mahmud Hassan contains some notes/messages pertaining to Islamic Jihad, as well as Explosive compositions which are being examined.
3. Six (6) Indian SIM cards seized from their possessions are being examined and CDRs being obtained for analysis.
4. The two arrested persons are in possession of fake Adhaar Card.

 Joint interrogation will be conducted again tomorrow, i.e. 31.07.2019 @ 10:00 a.m at CID(SB) Office.
