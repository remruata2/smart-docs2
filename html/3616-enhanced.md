A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.10.10.2022_

****Notice to SA to stop chiming of temple clock:** Superintendent of Police, Aizawl District issued a notice to leaders of the Salvation Army (SA) Temple Corps, Aizawl to mute the chiming of their Temple clock during the period between 10:00 pm in the evening and 6:00 am in the morning. The SP, in his notice to the SA leaders highlighted that the chiming of the Temple clock, which occurred every hour was in contravention of the Supreme Court ruling and Noise Pollution (Regulation and Control) Rules, 2000. It has been reported that, in compliance to this notice, SA, Temple Corps had completely stopped the chiming of their Temple clock.**

B. _Front page of VANGLAIN / The Aizawl Post newspapers Dt.10.10.2022_

****Police and END disposed confiscated contrabands**: Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) under Ministry of Home Affairs organised "Regional Conference on Drug Trafficking &amp; National Security" at Guwahati on 8th October 2022 which was presided over by Union Home Minister, Shri Amit Shah. To marked this occasion, different states of the North East organised incineration of confiscated contraband items in their respective state on the same day.**

 In Mizoram, Excise &amp; Narcotics department (END) incinerated their seized items whose courts cases have been settled at Bethani Multipurpose Farm, Phunchawng.
