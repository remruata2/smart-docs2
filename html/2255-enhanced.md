****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/263

Dated 6th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/246 Dt. 05.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****40 (forty) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV-24, LMV-11) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 6.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

36 (thirty six) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-20, LMV-14) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 6.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

64 (sixty four) vehicles (HMV-37, MMV-Nil, LMV-21, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 6.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

123 (one hundred twenty three) vehicles (HMV-43, MMV-38, LMV-14, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-9, Oil Tanker-15, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 6.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
