The said Azad told me that he had some consignments to be delivered to 2nd AR at Aizawl, Mizoram. Accordingly, I prepared and loaded the consignment.  On dt.25.8.2021, we left Masimpur, Silchar in the early morning and We entered inside Mizoram on dt.25.8.2021 @ 9:30 in the morning we cross vairengte and reached Aizawl at about 3:30 pm.  We entered inside the 2nd AR Camp.  Meantime, my second driver Mantu Kumar Deb received a phone call from unknown phone number 8794243098.  The caller asked my second driver that whether the consignment was to be unloaded or not the same night.  The caller told my second driver that he wanted to meet us.  Accordingly, we parked our vehicle inside the 2nd AR camp and went outside to meet the caller. Outside the 2nd AR Camp, they met the caller who is a Bengali and one Mizo male person. These two persons had arrived by Scooty (white colour) and met us at a place nearby Aizawl Mosque at around 4:30

to 5:00 pm (approx.).  We spoke with them for about 15 minutes and went inside the campus again.  The two persons requested to take some consignment to be delivered at Silchar and promised to pay Rs.20,000/- which we agreed.  They told us to meet somewhere after unloading the consignment.  We are having no knowledge that what the consignment is.  Then the two Bengali and Mizo persons left by white color Scooty.   The next day i.e. dt.26.8.2021,  our Truck was unloaded around 6:30 am the next day.  After that we received a call again from the same two persons wherein they asked us to come to a place near Bawngkawn. On reaching Bawngkawn, we waited till 9:30 am, Finally those two persons came by the same white color scooty and parked the scooty bit far away from out vehicle. These two persons then brought out two hidden packages kept in the nearby bush and loaded one consignment behind the driver seat and another consignment just the oil tank of the Truck.  They told us to call them back on their arrival at Silchar.  Then, the consignment will be taken over by some person and they will pay the transportation fee.  After loading the consignment, the two persons went away and told us to immediately move away from that place. We then started cooking food and had our lunch and started our journey around 2:00 pm for Silchar.  Then, when we are on our way to Silchar, some persons introduced themselves as CID personnel stopped us for checking our vehicle near Sairang.  On searching our vehicle, they found the consignment and told us that the consignment was strongly suspected to be contraband drugs which cost about many crores.   The suspected contraband drugs were seized from our join possession with my vehicle and my mobile handset.

 

          The Police Officer introduced before us and told us that we are strongly suspected of possessing contraband drugs and an authorization letter was issued for conducting search, seizure and arrest and read out the same before us and translated in our known language.  The Police Officer directed us to take out all our hidden articles if any. So, we took out the consignment and handed over to Police and the same was seized from our possession in presence of witneses.
