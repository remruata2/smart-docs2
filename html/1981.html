Questions &Answers :-

 

  1. How did you join Bru underground/gang?

Ans:       In the year 2014 at the end f June while I was at my uncle's home at Darlak in Mamit, BDFM Vice President Lianphunga, his friend S/S. Pvt. Laipang of NLFT, husband of Sare-i and two other men came to Darlak, they were the guests of MP Raipha near our house, I dint know the purpose of their visit but we got acquainted. Pu Lianphunga convinced me that instead of doing nothing I should join him to find a job with good pay and I did against my family's protest.
