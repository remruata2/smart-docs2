****14.12.2022 :** On 14.12.2022 @10:00 a.m., Mizoram Middle School Teachers Association held its General Conference at Synod Conference Centre, Aizawl. Pu Lalchhandama Ralte, Hon'ble Minister of State, School Education, etc. graced the Conference as Chief Guest. During the Conference, the following agenda concerning Government were passed:**

 i) To transfer Class V from Middle School to Primary School.

 ii) To take initiative for the promotion of Headmaster (who served as teacher for 16 year) to selection Grade after serving 5 years as Headmaster.

 iii) To take initiative for appointment of IV Grade in all Government Middle School.

 iv) To claim 50% of promotion to SDEO from Headmaster.
