**6.4.2022** : Information received stated that the under construction Bailey Bridge located in between Longpuighat and Vaseikai village in Lawngtlai district collapsed on the evening of6.4.2022 @ 5:45 p.m. and left four workers critically injured. The 4 (four) injured workers were immediately evacuated to LCMC &amp; Hospital Lawngtlai for medical treatment and unfortunately, Harsat Ali s/o Achar Uddin was declared 'brought dead' at the hospital. It may be mentioned that the bridge was constructed under the Border Road Organization (BRO) and the construction work is being carried out by VIJ Engineering.
