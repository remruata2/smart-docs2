12.6.2020 On 12.6.2020, CID(SB) Operation team recovered and seized 40 soap cases
containing suspected Heroin weighing 496.8 grams valued at Rs.30,00,000/-

to transport the same, Datsun (RediGo) B/R No. MZ01 R 2267 alongwith
documents was then seized from the possession of the suspected persons in
presence reliable witnesses.

40 boxes, 15 boxes have to be delivered to one person at Tuirial Airfield.
Hence, the operation team proceeded towards Tuirial Airfield and then
Vanlalnghaka informed the person who would be collecting the Heroin without
telling him his situation. Then, one person later known as Hallunga (60) s/o
KeiluaiTuirial Airfield Vengthar came out from the village and approached the
vehicle and on careful interrogation, he admitted that he came out to collect
those Heroins which was sent for him, and also admitted that he had collected
Heroin on previous consignments and then he was also apprehended.
The accused and all the seized articles along with the vehicle used for
transportation were brought and handed over to the O/C of Spl. Narcotics P/S,
Aizawl, and FIR was also lodged against the accused. In this connection, Spl.
Narcotic PS C/No. 20/2020 Dt.12.06.2020 U/s 21(c)/25/29 ND& PS Act was
registered.
