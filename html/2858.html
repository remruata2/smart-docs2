SPECIAL REPORT

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/929

Dated: 28th December, 2019.

 

            Emergency Joint meeting was held today, i.e. 28.12.2019 @ 11:00 a.m. at MZP Conference Hall under the chairmanship of Pu James C. Lalnuntluanga, President, Mizo Thalai Pawl (Mizo Youth Organization) which was attended by 30 (thirty) persons representing various organizations viz. Save Mizo Nation Society, Zoram Drivers’ Union, Mizoram Tippers’ Association, Mizoram Oil Tankers Drivers’ Association, JAC on Inner Line Reserve, Mizoram Truck Drivers Association, Mizoram Truck Owners’ Association, Ashok Leyland, MZP, MSU and other prominent citizens like Pu Clement Liankunga IAS (Rtd), Pu P.C. Lawmkunga IAS (Rtd), Pu Robert Hrangdawla IAS (Rtd).

                        The following are the agendas for the said meeting :-

  1. Opposition and agitation against CAA-2019.
  2. Illegal Chakma immigrants from Bangladesh.
  3. Bru refugees.
  4. Various issues regarding inter-state border disputes.
  5. Usage of Mizo names by non-Mizos for the purpose of running business illegally.
  6. Running of business by non-Mizos who are married to Mizo women.
  7. Administrative Reform.
  8. Issues related to registration of vehicles by non-Mizos using Mizo names and the various challenges faced by the local truck/tipper owners as a result of it.
  9. Self determination (Re-Unification of ZO/ZOFA/ZOMI/ Mizo origin).

                        Input received indicated that the meeting, in principle agreed to oppose the CAA and expressed their support towards the MZP and NESO in their opposition against the Act. However, they expressed their dissatisfaction on the actions taken up by NGOs so far in Mizoram against the CAA. The Central YMA was not invited to join the meeting. Moreover, the meeting condemned the Central YMA for keeping silent on this issue.

                        It is learnt that the other agendas are related to the interest of their respective organizations. Information has been received that representatives of SMNS are no longer interested in attending the next meeting due to the differences in interest of the organizations. The meeting discussed all the agendas but did not arrive at any substantial conclusion, resolution or plan of action. They resolved to discuss their future course of action in the next meeting the date and time for which is not yet fixed.
