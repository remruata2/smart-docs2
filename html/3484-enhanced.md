A. _Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 26.3.2014_

**Hit & Run****:**A speeding truck bearing regd. no. MZ 05 6247 heading for Silchar ran over a woman passenger of the truck, aged about 40 years between Kawnpui and Sairang at around 8:30pm on 24th March. Another local driver witnessed the incident but could not catch the driver. Kawnpui Police intercepted the said vehicle and arrested the hit and run driver Nazrul Ahmed Bharbhuya (25) of Dholai, Cachar District. The identity of the woman is yet to be ascertained. She was run over after she got down of the truck.

**B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 26.3.2014**

**Dispute Between Two Villages****: Villagers of Chilui village cultivated land in the area of N.Sabual village. Village Council (VC) of N. Sabual informed them not to burn the cultivated land but people of Chilui village burnt it and that created a rift between the villagers of N.Sabual and Chilui. The dispute between the two villages led to N. Sabual VC informing VC of Chilui to leave their village within the month of March.**
