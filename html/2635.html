ZOMI REVOLUTIONARY ARMY (ZRA) EASTERN COMMAND

CHUNGCHANG REPORT

 

 

ZRA Eastern Command SS Colonel Kham Min Thang a hote chu Eastern Command, Myanmar ram chhungah awmin an operational area chu Tidim, Tonzang leh Chikha area te a ni.

 

He an operational area chhungah hian tun hma atanga ZRA underground ni lo chu silai kenga pawl dang din an lo rem ti tawhlo thin a. Tun hnaiah Myanmar Civil Disobedient Movement (CDM) alo in tan phat atangin pawl hrang hrang sipai do turin silai kengin ram chhung mi an rawn in din ve a, chung zingah chuan ZRA operational area chhungah People Defence Force (PDF) Zoland alo ding ve a. Hei hi ngai thei lovin ZRA te chuan PDF Zoland hmalakna chu a do ta ngar ngar a, in vauna leh tharum thawh thlengin PDF mi leh sa te chungah hian an hmang ta a ni.

 

ZRA ten PDF mi leh sa te chunga vauna leh kutthlak an neih hriat theih chin chu hetiang hi a ni :-

 

  1. May Thla 2021 vel atang khan Tidim leh Tonzang Township atanga MP/MLA thlan tlinte Mizoram a lo tlan chhiate chu mobile phone hmangin an vau zui thin.

 

  1. September 2021 khan Khawzimpui khua a tlangval 5 te chu na takin thingfak hmangin kut anthlak.

 

  1. June/July 2021 bawr khan PDF mi leh sa Tiau kam Ngur village, VC ram chhunga training tum te chu hnawtdarhin an bungruate an hal chhiat sak vek.

 

  1. Dt.5.12.2021 Sunday zing dar 4 vel khan PDF Zoland member 1 Pu Mang Hen Dal, Ex Minister Personal Secretary lo ni thin Cin Sian Sum chu Tidim Township ama chennaah ZRA mi 2 ten an kap hlum.

 

Heng tarlan bakah pawh hian ZRA te hian PDF mi leh sa te hi tawngkam leh mobile phone hmang te in an vau reng thin.
