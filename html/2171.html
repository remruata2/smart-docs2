Hetih lai hian, hripui COVID-19 leng mek hripui inkai darh
hlauhawm dinhmunah an awm avanga Containment Area-a puan,
Inkharkhip/Lockdown, Contact Tracing leh test result nghah mek a nih avangin,
Ramhlun South Local Council-ah Inthlan hi neih theih a la ni lo a, State Election
Commission, Mizoram chuan an dinhmun thlir chung zelin leh Health & Family
Welfare Department rawn chungin inthlan hun tur ruahmanna thar a siam ang.
General Election to Local Councils, 2020 inthlan buaipuitu ber
Deputy Commissioner & District Municipal Election Officer, Aizawl District,
Superintendent of Police, Aizawl District, Assistant District Municipal Election
Officer & Election Officer, Aizawl District, Returning Officer & Assistant
Returning Officer, Local Level Officer, Presiding Officer, Polling Officer leh Police
te inpekna avangin inthlan thawh hnihna chu tluang taka zawh fel a ni ta a,
State Election Commission chuan an chungah lawm thu a sawi a ni.
Thu leh hla puanzar zung zung hi inthlanna hlawhtling nei tura a
serh zinga a mei ang a ni a, hemi kawngah hian Directorate of Information &
Public Relations leh Media te chuan Commission mamawh leh thu puanzar duh
tinrengah chhun leh zan pawh sawi loin a tul apiangah hma min laksak a, hun
harsa leh khirhkhan tak karah tluang tak leh felfai taka inthlanna nei thei tura
tha thawhtu pawimawh ber te zing a mi an ni a, an chungah State Election
Commission chuan lawm thu a sawi a ni.
COVID-19 laka kan him theihna tura sul sutu, Health & Family
Welfare Department-in Election Duty te leh mipui himna tura Commission in a
mamawh apiang tihsakin thurawn tha tak tak a pe a, Local Council 24-te inthlan
theihna tura pawimawh tak Zoram Medical College (ZMC) a Sample Test Result
tharlam ber nitin min hriattir bawk a, State Election Commission chuan a ngai
hlu hle a ni. Hei bakah hian, felfai taka COVID-19 Test Result Negative
Quarantine a awm te Vote thlak theih na tura hma la tu Local Level Task Force
te ruahmanna, hma lakna leh inpekna a ropui takzet a, Commission chuan lawm
thu a sawi a ni.
Hetih lai hian, hripui COVID-19 leng mek karah Local Council
inthlan a tul si avangin, State Election Commission chuan Central Young Mizo
Association (CYMA) leh Mizoram Peoples’ Forum (MPF)-te puihna lo chuan Vote
thlak ni-a Mizoram Sorkarin Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) a siam te,
Commission leh District Municipal Election Officer-in inkaihhruaina a siam te ti
hlawhtling tura hmabak hautakzia hre rengin phungbawmah a sawm a. Anni
hian tlanchhe hauh lo leh phur takin, Mizo mipui te himna tur a nih chuan tiin
tha leh zung, an hun hlu tak sengin, ruahmanna fel tak siamin veng tinah
inthlan an buaipui a, anni tawiawmna tel lo chuan tun tum inthlan hi tin chhin
ngam loh tur khawpa hautak leh huphurhawm a ni tih State Election
Commission, Mizoram chuan a hria a, an chungah lawm thu a sawi tak meuh
meuh a ni.
