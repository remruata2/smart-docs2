MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/687

Dated 9th September, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/685
Dt. 8.09.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.  

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :   

          11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-2, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge during the last 24 (twenty four) hours.

43 (forty three) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-15, LMV-27, Maxi Cab-1) entered into Mizoram from Tripura through Damcherra bridge during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saipui ‘V’/Phainuam-Buarchep,  Kolasib District:

Reference to No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/685 Dt. 8.09.2021 the road construction work undertaken by Mizoram side reached the newly set up Mizoram Police Camp at Pu Thangzachhunga’s farm land, Phainuam-Buarchep, Kolasib District today i.e., 9.9.2021.

Information received stated that the road construction work undertaken by Assam also reached their duty camp at the disputed area (Buarchep tlang). It also stated that construction of bridge connecting Pu Chalchhunga’s farm land to
Pu Lalnuntluanga’s farm land was continued today.

 

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-Nil, LMV-9, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :
  1. Road construction work which will connect newly set up Mizoram Police camp at Aitlang hnar (Aitlang stream source) was continued under Police escort today i.e., 9.9.2021 and reached upto Mizoram Police camp at Pu Lalhlimpuia (Khasi) of Vairengte farm land.

          Road construction work at Middle Aitlang was also continued today and reached upto Mizoram Police duty post at Pu Lalzova farm land at the area.

          Information received stated that 14 (fourteen) Assam Police and
20 (twenty) Assam Police Commando visited Assam Police duty post, which is located at around 100 meters from Mizoram Police duty post at Pu Lalzova’s farm land at Middle Aitlang today @ 1:30 p.m. and carefully observed the road construction work undertaken near Mizoram Police camp. Input received stated that 10 (ten) Assam Police Commando are being deployed at their duty post.

  1. Construction of Mizoram Police camp at Pu H. Lalrinawma, Ex-VCP, Vairengte farm land at Mautuizau, which is located around 3 km east of Vairengte Excise Checkgate was not continued today and 2(two) tents and
    1 (one) duty post were already erected/constructed at the area. Situation is normal.
  2. 128 (one hundred and twenty eight) vehicles (HMV-52, MMV-32, LMV-18, LPG-9, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-13, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today @ upto 3:00 p.m. and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai/Saipum, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.
