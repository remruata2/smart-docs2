A GLIMPSE AT CHIN NATIONAL FRONT/CHIN NATIONAL ARMY

 

 

 

INTRODUCTION:

                        Burma ramah sipai sorkarna (Military Junta) a piantirh kum 1962 a General Newin an sorkarna a chelh atangin Burma ram chhungah zirlai ten lungawilohna an lantir thin a, kum 1988 ah zirlai nawlpui in Gen. Newin a sorkar paihthlak tumin rampumah demonstration/agitation an huaihawt ta, hetah hian Chin State a hnam hrang hrang te pawh agitation ah hian an tel ve ani. Zirlai te lungawilohna lantir avangin kum 1988 ah chuan Gen. Newin chu paihthlak niin a aiah Gen. Than Shwe chu a lo lal leh ta a, sipai sorkarna atanga sipai sorkar bawk a lo pian zui leh tak avangin sipai sorkar te chuan zirlai hruaitu tam tak a man khawm a, a then te chu hmun hrang hrang, Thailand, India, Bangladesh ah te an tlanchhe ta chiam a ni.

 

                        Hetianga sipai leh zirlai inkara buaina leh harsatna lian tak a awm takah chuan Burma ram chhunga Chin mi te chuan Burma ram chu sipai sorkarna paihthlak a nih theih nan kum 1988 March ni 20 khan hma latu tuna Chin National Front(CNF) a lo ding ta a, an president atan Tialkhala niin underground group tur te pawh din nghal a ni. Kum 1988 October thlaah Champhai ah Burma zirlai raltlan te chu lo chhukin Champhai ah refugee camp sak a ni a, hetah hian zirlai engemawzat chu an khawsa ta a ni. Tichuan CNF dinchhuaktu Tialkhala, president leh a nau te Liannova@ Panova P/A Chhinga Veng, Aizawl te chu Burma atangin kum 1988 December thla ah Champhai ah lokal in Champhai refugee camp a awm te leh hmun dang remchang apianga mi te rawn hruai khawmin CNF chu an ti lian ta zel ani.

MIZORAM-A AN AWMDAN:

                        An hruaitu thenkhat a hmaa tarlan te bakah hian CNF/CNA mi leh sa te hi Champhai, Saiha leh Lawngtlai ah te hian englai pawhin an awm reng thin niin hriat a ni a, India khua leh tui te chungah leh sumdawng te lakah a bikin Mizoram chhungah chuan harsatna an siam hriat a ni meuh tawh lo(23.8.2010 a Lungpuk civilian te nena an intihbuaina mai lo chu). Tin, Aizawl ah hian Pu C. Vulluaia in Dawrpui Vengthar ami bakah hian awmna mumal an nei lova, kum kalta 2008/2009 atang khan an bungraw pawimawh, office bungrua te chu Pu C. Vulluaia building atang hian an lo suanchhuak tawh a, office angin engmah hmalakzuina an neih hriat a ni lo.

 

 

SYMPATHISER LEH POLITICS INVOLVEMENT:

                        Political party atang hian eng party mah support an neih hriat a ni lova, anmahni puitu politician an awm hriat a ni lo. Sympathiser chungchangah pawh hian anmahni mi leh sa Aizawl leh ramchhungah tam tak an awm a, chu bakah chuan NGO ang a ding, CNC, ABSL, NLDA(LA) etc te nen hian hrui khat vuan in an awm a, pawl angin thawhhona tha tak an nei. Tin, New Delhi lamah pawh refugee anga awm engemawzat an awm a, heng ho nen pawh hian in kalpawh reng an ni a, CNA/CNF Aizawl a an hruaitu ber General Secretary Paul Smith pawh tunah hian Delhi ah a awm mek.
