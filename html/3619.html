A. Front page of  VANGLAIN / The Aizawl Post  newspapers  Dt.13.10.2022

Bairabi - Sairang Railway line to be completed by December 2023 : Spot verification of the ongoing construction works of railway line between Bairabi and Sairang was conducted yesterday by members of Mizoram Journalist Association (MJA) led by their President Pu Lalrambuatsaiha. Receiving these journalists, consultant of  North Eastern Railways, Pu Hrangtawna, IRTS (Rtd.) told the journalist that 80% of the works have been completed and that they expected Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi to inaugurate the railways project by December of 2023.

            The foundation stone of this 51.38 kms. railway construction was laid by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi on 29th November 2014 with the initial estimated amount of Rs.2,384.34 crores. It was also scheduled to be completed by March 2020. However, due to unforeseen problems such as excessive rainfall, land acquisition problems etc. the expected completion date had been shifted to December 2023 with the estimated amount increased to Rs. 5,000 crores.

            Along this broad gauge railway line, there will be 4 stations - Hortoki, Kawnpui, Mualkhang and Sairang, 196 bridges and 23 tunnels. Passenger trains plying in this route will travel at a speed of up to 100 km/h and will take around 1 hour to cover the distance between Sairang and Bairabi while goods train will travel at a speed of around 65 km/h.

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.13.10.2022

Seven MLAs from Kerala visited Mizoram:  Committee on Local Fund Accounts (CoLFA) comprising of 7 members under Kerala Legislative Assembly reach Aizawl on 11th October 2023 as part of their ongoing study tour and have an interaction programme with their Mizoram counterparts yesterday at Assembly Committee Room, Aizawl. During the interaction, both the committee share their respective undertaking, allotments of funds to their various local bodies and their expenditures, importance of CoLFA and how to strengthened the Committee.

 

 

C. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt.13.10.2022

  Cleaning of water plant :  On 14th October 2023, Mizoram PHE department intended to clean the Water Treatment Plant of Greater Aizawl Water Supply Scheme (GAWSS) Phase -II as well as fixing its pump set delivery valve. In this connection, the concerned department requested the public to utilised their household water in the most economical way as this GAWSS will not be able pump water on this day from 8:00 am in the morning to 6:00 pm in the evening
