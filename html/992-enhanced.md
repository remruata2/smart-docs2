****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2015/86

Dated 2nd February, 2015

Today, i.e. 2.2.2015 at around 8:00 AM, a combined group of suspected National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) and Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) militants numbering around 9 (nine) and carrying 4(four) nos. of AK-47 Rifle, knife, etc stopped one Truck (Tipper) bearing Regn. No. GE-70935. (belonging to BRTF-481 RMPL) and one Motor Cycle coming from Tuipuibari, Mamit District at about 7 Kms away from Tuipuibari (i.e. about 50 Kms from West Phaileng). The militants initially taken all the occupants of the truck (i.e, 16 (sixteen) BRTF employees and 5(five) civilians) and Bike rider namely Mangala Basu, teacher (SSA), Rajiv Nagar to the jungle of Dampa Sanctuary. Later on, at about 1½ (one and half) hour, the abductors released 20 (twenty) hostages and abducted 2(two) BRTF employees namely (i) Overseer (JE), Shri Hokum Singh (51 yrs), Lowakhund village, P.O. Neuna Majra, District - Rohtak, Haryana (ii) Driver of Tipper, Pu Thangchhuaka (56yrs) @ Zoluta @ Mohamad Buizu Islam of Ramthar Veng, Aizawl.

One of the hostages namely Pu Thangchhuaka (56yrs) @ Mohamad Buizu Islam originally came from Silchar, Assam and settled in Aizawl from 1970 after he married with one local girl namely Pi Rothangi. Currently, his family is staying in a rented house of Pu Laldingliana, Ramthar Veng (Sihpuiruam), Aizawl.

The abductors were reportedly speaking in Bru and Bengali languages and all of them were dressed in camouflaged uniforms. One of the released hostages namely Mangala Basu, teacher (SSA), Rajiv Nagar narrated that due to non compliance/payment of demand money i.e. extortion money by BRTF such operation of abduction was carried out by the abductors.

Mizoram Police team under the supervision of Commandant, 5th IR, SP Mamit and SDPO West Phaileng immediately proceeded to the place of occurrence to take stock of the situation. Situation is under close watch.
