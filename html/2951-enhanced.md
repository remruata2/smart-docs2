****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/37

Dated: 16th January, 2019.

Information has been received that Lawngtlai District Land Owners’ Association (LDLOA) Gen. Hqrs. held an Executive Meeting today, i.e. 16.1.2019 @ 11:30 a.m at their office. The meeting was presided over by Pu Remmawia, President which was attended by 20 (twenty) members. The meeting resolved to uphold the resolution of the Executive Meeting of LDLOA which was held on 6.12.2018 to stay all the construction works under the Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP) indefinitely w.e.f. 20.1.2019 (Sunday), if the authority fails to issue pending compensation before end of 2018.

 Information added that LDLOA submitted a Memorandum to the Deputy Commissioner, Lawngtlai District today, i.e. 16.1.2019 and conveyed about their intention to stay all the construction works under KMMTTP indefinitely w.e.f 20.1.2019. LDLOA, in their Memorandum, alleged the authority of unnecessary pending in disbursement of compensation claims amounting to Rs. 59,20,90,515/- which was awarded vide Draft award No. 1 of 2016 under Memo No.R-12001/16/2012-DC(LTI) Dt. 6.4.2017 which was again resolved by Expert Committee vide No.B-2013/48/2016-Fc (PCCF) Dt. 4.6.2018. The letter further mentioned their decision to go through with their resolution passed on 6.12.2018 to stay all construction work w.e.f. 20.1.2019 if their demand for payment of compensation was not made before the end of 2018.

Information further indicated that there existed an expectation from LDLOA for the District Administration to take up necessary action even to the extent of arranging a meeting with higher authorities so that the impending impasse could be circumvented.
