HPC(D) DINHMUN :

 

HPC(D) Organisation hi tun dinhmun ah chuan an chau hle a, an hotute hriselna that tawk loh vang te pawh a ni pakhat a, an President H. Zosangbera hi cancer anga ngaih anih avangin Churachanpur ah a awm deuh chawt a, hmun dang a chhuak ngai meuh tawh lo. Working Chairman Lalthangliana pawh hi pumpuina (pancreatitis) nei anih avangin a chak lo hle a, Army Chief Lalropuia ber pawh hi TB natna nei leh HIV positive a rinhlelh a ni a, duh angin a che chhuak thei lova, an nau te phei chu a tlawh chhuak tawh ngai meuh lo.

 

Sum leh pai ah hian an harsa hle a, an cadre te pawhin hlawh/ lawmman an hmu ngai lova, an Commander thenkhat te nen an remchan dan angin donation an khawn a, an inngaihtuah chawp mai thin niin an sawi.

 

Tin, an cadres te zingah hian Manipur leh Mizoram mi leh sa arukin an inthliar ve hle a, Army Chief ber hi Manipur chhuak anih avangin Manipur mi te a duhsak zawk deuh thin anga sawi a ni a, langsar tak chuan an sawi lang ngamlo na in Mizoram atanga tel ve te hi silai tha pawh an kentir phal lova, operation a an kentir ve pawhin an haw veleh an dah tir leh zel thin niin an sawi. Hetiang anih avang hian an lungrual tawk lova, Mizoram sorkar a in surrender mai duh te pawh an awm reng phah a ni.
