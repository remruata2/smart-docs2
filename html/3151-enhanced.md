No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/206

Dated: 25th March, 2022

****Arrest of 6 (six) KNF Cadres by the BSF****

As per input, 6 (six) cadres of Kuki-Chin National Front (KNF) have been apprehended near Parva-II, Lawngtlai district by the BSF deployed at BOP Parva yesterday, i.e., 24.3.2022 @ around 3:00 p.m. As per preliminary enquiry conducted by the BSF, the above cadres revealed that they want separate State for them in the Chittagong Hill Track (CHT), Bangladesh, on the contention that the government is not paying adequate attention to them. For this purpose, they intend to seek shelter in south-western part of Mizoram and to carry out their fight if the Bangladesh government do not concede their demands. A letter addressed to the Commander, Parva Camp BSF was submitted by Lalsangrem, S/o Saikhuai, in which they introduced themselves as members of Kuki-Chin National Army (KNA) cadres (Armed wing of KNF) planning to seek shelter in south-western part of Mizoram and seeking help from the BSF in their settlement. They also have another letter addressed to Commander, Assam Rifle Camp at Mizoram-Myanmar border.

 The details of apprehended persons as below :-

1. Lalrammawi Bawm (45), S/o Kipbil Bawm, Baktlai Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.
2. Bawmnunsang Bawm (39), S/o Rialthang Bawm, Cheihkhiang Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.
3. Lalsangrem Bawm (34), S/o Saikhuai Bawm, Basatlang Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.
4. Lalsungkhum Bawm (32), S/o Laltinsawn Bawm, Baktlai Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.
5. Lianbawisang (30), S/o Nawndo Bawm, Fianpidung Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.
6. Ramdinglian Bawm (20), S/o Lalthangkung Bawm, Baktlai Village, Bandarban district, Bangladesh.

Some mobile phones, Sim Cards, Daos (Mizo chopper use for cutting), Indian and Bangladeshi currency notes (Rs. 21900 &amp; 80110 Taka), 01 Set of VHF Hand Set and Charger and other toiletries, including medicines, were recovered from their possessions.

It is also learnt that higher authorities of BSF, Aizawl are proceeding to Parva Camp BSF today, i.e., 25.3.2022 and the arrested persons will be handed over to Vasei Police Station.

Situation and activities of the KNF is under close watch.
