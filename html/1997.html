            Hetih hunlai (2008 kum laihawl) hian HPC (D) activities leh demand te hi Mizoram Sorkar in min ngaihsak tawk lova hriatna kan nei a. Hetih hunlai a kan Commander S/S Comdr Vanlalchhuanga (L) @ Suonga of Saipum hovin Dolakhal ah chet lak dan tur kan ngaihtuah a, chumi rual chuan Saiphai leh a chhevel khua Village Council te hnenah tukluih chhiah khawnna tur Demand Letter kan thawn darh bawk a. Kan Commander Suonga ho chuan Forest R.O. Pu K. Malsawma of Saiphai (a hma atang tawh a kan thu awih vak lo nia kan hriat) chu a Office ah kal chilh a hruai bo (abduct) kan rawt a. Chutiang tur chuan zankhat chu (a hun leh ni chiah ka hre tawh lo) Saiphai pan in kan lo chhuak a. Pu K. Malsawma, RO, Forest Deptt. Saiphai pawh chu alo awm ngei a, lungawiloh hrikthalk nan vaukhanna leh tharum thawh chu kan tan nghal a. A Quarter kawt a a Mahindra Jeep dah pawh a ke te kah keh sakin, a wind shield te pawh kan chhu keh a, amah ngei pawh chu hruaibo kan tum a amaherawhchu keimahni zingah ngaihdan in anglo a awm a. Hetih hunlai a Commander tih lovah chuan sipai senior ber ka ni a, Second Commander ang ka nih ve avngain huaisen chhuahin hruaibo kher tul ka tih loh thu kan Commander pawh ka hrilh a, silai te hmet puak a tharum kan thawh tawh avangin a duhtawk ve pawh a ni mai thei, ka thu angin hruaibo chu kan ti ta lova. Dolakhal atanga a Quarter halchhiatna tur khawnvartui 3 ltrs. vel kan ken hmang chuan RO Quarter chu kan hal chhe ta bawk a ni. Kan naute thinrim chuan Pu K. Malsawma, Forest RO, Saiphai pawh chu kut an thlak nual a, chiang taka hrilhin Mizoram Sorkarin kan thu leh hla te a ngaihsak loh bakah ama hnen ngei a kan sum Demand te min pek loh avangin kan thinrim thu kan hrilh a. Tichuan keini pawh zing dar 2 velah in sasengin a ni pawh kan chhuah ve ta nghal a khawchhung lamah riak turin a haw niin ka hria.
