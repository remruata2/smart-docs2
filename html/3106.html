B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 19.8.2013

Donation collector arrested: In the morning of Saturday ie, 17th August 2013, Bawngkawn Police arrested C.Lalsangliana (53) of Ramhlun North who collect money/donation in the name of Disabled Development Agency. C.Lalsangliana used to work for Disabled Development Agency and used his ID Card and pamphlet to continue collecting donation even after he stopped working for the agency. 

 

C. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 19.8.2013

Set fire to father in law’s house: The house of C.Lalsangzuala of Sihphir Tlangveng was set on fire by his son in law Lalhmunmawia s/o Pachhunga of Khawruhliana at around 6:30 PM last night. D. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 19.8.2013

Total bandh to demand reduction of road tax: Mizoram Auto Rickshaw Owners Association General Headquarters, Lunglei intends to organise total bandh at Lunglei from 5:00 AM to 5:00 PM tomorrow to demand reduction of road tax. Auto Rickshaw service in other places are heard to follow the bandh.
