A. _Front page of NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 6.3.2014_

****Mizoram goes to Poll on 9th April; New EVMs will be used - CEO****:**Mizoram will elect its lone representative to the Lok Sabha on 9th April, State Chief Election Officer Ashwani Kumar said yesterday. Mizoram CEO said that along with election to the State’s single Lok Sabha seat, by-poll to the Hrangturzo Assembly seat will be held.**

 Hrangturzo seat in Serchhip District was won by Chief Minister Lal Thanhawla, in addition to his tradition constituency Serchhip in the 25th November 2013 State Election Assembly polls, and left it vacant.

 Election notification will be issued on 15th March, 3 (three) days before the State Assembly Budget Session to be started from 18th March. Counting of votes will be held on16th May and results will be declared on 28th May. Last date for filing of nominations is fixed at 22nd March, while 26th March will be the last date for withdrawal of nominations.

 State CEO said that EVMs used in the last Assembly elections, manufactured by ECIL would not be reused and would be replaced by those manufactured by BHEL due to opposition party’s allegations of EVM tampering. 23 (twenty three) candidates of the largest opposition party, Mizo National Front (MNF), who lost the elections, have filed a petition in Gauhati High Court demanding probe into the EVMs. EVMs used in the 23 constituencies are being locked in the strong room and those used in the remaining 27constituencies may be used in other States, the CEO said. As many as 1,716 EVMs for the 1,126 polling stations have been ordered from BHEL in Maharashtra some of which have arrived.
