BRU REPATRIATION 2014

 

17.6.2014 :    Bru repatriation from Tripura Refugee Camps to Mizoram started on 17.6.2014 at 2 (two) Facilitation Centres, i.e. Kanhmun and Zomuantlang. As per information received, 4 families with 23 members reported at Zomuantlang Facilitation Centre and 26 families numbering 116 members reported at Kanhmun Facilitation Centre till 5:00 PM.

 

18.6.2014 :    Bru refugee continued for the 2nd day on 18.6.2014 at Kanhmun & Zomuantlang Facilitation Centres. Altogether, 56 families with 275 members were received at Kanhmun Facilitation Centre whereas 9 families with 55 members were received at Zomuantlang Facilitation Centre as on 18.6.2014.

 

19.6.2014 :    Bru refugee repatriation continued for the 3rd day on 19.6.2014 and altogether, the following nos. of families and members have so far reported at Kanhmun and Zomuantlang Facilitation Centres respectively :-

 

                        (i)        Kanhmun Facilitation Centre

                                    Nos. of families         -           108

                                    Nos. of refugees        -           547

 

                        (ii)       Zomuantlang Facilitation Centre

                                    Nos. of families         -             19

                                    Nos. of refugees        -           108

 

                                    Grand Total

                                    Nos. of families         -           127

                                    Nos. of refugees        -           615
