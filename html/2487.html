MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/665

Dated 21st November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/664
Dt. 20.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

9 (nine) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-6) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 21.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 16(sixteen) vehicles transited their goods at Medili Bridge. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

6 (six) leaders of Sinlung Hills Council led by Pu Malsawma Darngawn, Chairman, Sinlung Hills Council who visited Saihapui ‘V’ yesterday evening i.e., 20.11.2020 @ 4:30 p.m. to take stock of the situation left Saihapui ‘V’ today’s morning i.e., 21.11.2020 @ 8:38 a.m. 

5(five) Tempo Motor (Auto Rickshaw Carrier) entered Saihapui ‘V’ to ferry Betel Nut to Assam today i.e., 21.11.2020 and they returned back to Assam today itself.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

36 (thirty six) vehicles (HMV-16, MMV-Nil, LMV-13, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 21.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

         

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          190 (one hundred ninety) vehicles (HMV-78, MMV-41, LMV-42, Maxi Cab-6, Bus-nil, LPG-7, Oil-16) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 21.11.2020 @ 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

Bamboo barricade put up by residents of Karkat Village to restrict movement of vehicles and people across the border is still remained at Kachasora Bridge, towards Assam side, Mizoram-Assam boundary and there is no entry of vehicles and people across the border today i.e., 21.11.2020.

          Situation is under close watch.
