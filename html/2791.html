SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/178

Dated: 11st March, 2022

 

          15 (fifteen) Assam Police and Forest Department personnel led by
Shri. N. Lahan, OC, Ramnathpur PS, Assam, came to the farm house of
Pu Laldinpuia Ralte, Ramthar Veng, Bairabi, which is located within Bairabi Tlawngkai Forest Reserve Area (around 400 metres west of Bairabi village, across Tlawng river) today, i.e., 11.3.2022 @ 1:00 p.m. and instructed Pu Laldinpuia Ralte to dismantle his farm house. Upon learning the stated incident, Pu Vanlalmuana, SDC Bairabi and OC, Bairabi PS rushed to the area and held discussion with the Assam officials. After discussing the matter at length, Assam Officials left the area without any untoward incident.

 

          It is also learnt that Assam Police and Forest Department officials dismantled the farm house of Alta, s/o Abdul Barrik of Satmara Village, Hailakandi district, Assam, built with bamboos and tin roof and located at around 20 metres west of the said Pu Lalrinpuia Ralte’s farm house.

 

          Situation is under watch.
