A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.30.1.2013

      Blood donation by police:The 1st Bn. MAP organized a mass blood donation        programme yesterday in which 74 personnel from the battalion donated blood to Aizawl        Civil hospital.

 

      

      B. Front page of The Aizawl Post/ VANGLAINI / The Zozam Times newspaper                         Dt.30.1.2013

      Notification to deposite fire-arms:The Aizawl District Commissioner yesterday   gave a notification to all the civilian arms holders inside Chalfilh constituency to           deposit their arms to their nearest police station before 20.02.2013 as a precaution           for having a free and fair by-election.

    

    

     C. Front page of The Aizawl Post/ VANGLAINI newspapers  Dt.30 1.2013

     Seminar on child protection:In a collaboration with the Mizoram Journalist association, Centre for Peace and Development organized a seminar on the rights and       protection of children, yesterday at Aizawl Press Club. The Resource Person of this          seminar, Pi Zodinpuii highlighted the responsibilities of the parents/caregivers of children below 18 years in protecting their rights. She further mentioned that during      the past 2 years, there were as many as 333 related cases and that proposal for     creating a separate branch for these concerns in every police stations was      underway.
