****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2022/135

Dated: 25th February, 2022

 As per input, the bridge constructed across Tlawngte River, situated in between Thinghlun, Mizoram and Kalagang, Assam, and around 1 km from Mizoram Police camp at Thinghlun, Mamit District, Mizoram, was reportedly burnt last night, i.e., 24.2.2022 by some unknown miscreant(s).

 Upon seeing the smokes emanating from the fire, Mizoram Police deployed at the area immediately rushed to the spot this morning @7:00 a.m. and by the time they arrived, half of the bridge was already burnt down. It is suspected that the bridge was burnt from the Assam side by using kerosene oil as an empty kerosene can was found at the spot. As per input, the said bridge was constructed inside Mizoram territory by the villagers of Kalagang, Assam. It was also learnt that the posts of the bridge at both ends were constructed with cement concrete some 7 (seven) years back and recently connected by using wooden poles and bamboos, and it serves as a route to Ronpur bazar (market), Hailakandi District, Assam.
 Upon learning the incident, SDO (civil), Kawrthah and SDPO, Kawrthah and party rushed to the spot to enquire into the matter today and necessary enquiry is underway. No untoward incident occurred at the time of writing the report and situation is under close watch.
