SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/561

Dated: 11th October, 2020           

 

          In continuation to our Special Report vide No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/560 Dt. 10.10.2020, Information has been received that today, i.e., Dt. 11.10.20 @ around 3:30 p.m., around 15 (fifteen) Assam Police personnel came with 2 (two) LMVs  (Sumo and Gypsy) near Pu John Zolawma's Jhum Hut which was burnt down on 10.10.2020 at Mizoram-Assam border. Although they can be seen from 4th IR temporary camp set up after the incident, they did not make any verbal or physical contact with OC, Kanhmun PS or 4th IR personnel currently stationed at the area. They returned back to Assam with police siren on after taking photographs of the area where Mizoram Police set up new temporary camp due to the incident.

            Whereas there are evidences in various local and national news channels wherein District Administration authorities of Karimganj, Assam stated that the above act was done at their behest, Assam Police personnel summoned leaders of Zaite Village, Assam today and enquired about the person(s) responsible for burning of the said Jhum Hut of Pu John Zolawma and cutting down on Areca nut plants belonging to Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David-a of Thinghlun.

            Situation is under close watch.

DGP (M)
