A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 18.3.2014

3 Women Arrested: Three women namely – Khawpuimawii, (42) w/o Vanlalvena, Tuikual North; F Lalthantluangi (36) w/o VL Chhuanawma, Bawngkawn Brigade Veng and Laltanpuii (40) d/o Vanlalruata, Chaltlang Lily Veng were arrested for deceiving people by the Police on 15.3.2014.

          The women confessed to the Police that they took properties worth ` 18,55,500/- by deceiving people. The police suspected that these women stacked up money somewhere apart from what they have confessed.

          These women deceived Ramnghingliana of Maubawk Veng telling him that they can sell his 100 sack of rice and as they did not return to pay him, Ramnghingliana lodged an FIR to the Police on 15.3.2014.

          After they were arrested, other 10 people complained to the Police that these 3 women took their sack of rice and properties without paying them.
