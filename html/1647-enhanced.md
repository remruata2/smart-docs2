Sir,

 With reference to your letter no. cited above, I am furnishing the required information so far available on record under this unit as follows:

**a) Mizoramah hel pawl an awm em? An awm chuan eng pawl te nge?**

****Ans:** Mizoramah hian hel pawl an awm lo. Amaherawhchu thenawm State, Manipur hel - HPC(D) te hian Mizoram hmar lamah leh Tripura State-a Bru hel te hian Mizoram khawthlang lamah harsatna/buaina an rawn siam ve zeuh zeuh thin ani.**

**b) Hel pawl te hian sorkarah demand an nei em? An neih chuan eng nge?**

**Ans:** Nei e.

 Manipur State hel - HPC(D) te hian Mizoram hmar lam khaw thenkhatah Autonomous District Council din an rawn ngiat ve tawp a ni.

**c) Hel pawl thil demand hi sorkarin tihhlawhtlin a tum em?**

Ans:  Hel pawl thil demand hi sawrkarin tihhlawhtlin a tum lo.

**d) Kum 2012-2016 chhungin hel pawl avangin harsatna engte nge tawh anih? Mi engzatin nge hel ho kutah hian nunna chan?**

Ans:  Kum 2012-2016 chhung hian hel pawl te avangin Ambush tum 1 (khat), Extortion leh Donation khawnte a thleng ani.

 Kum 2012-2016 chhung hian Mizoram Police mi 3 (pathum) ten hel ho kut tuarin nunna an chan ani.( Heng Mizoram Police nunna chan te hi dt. 28.3.2015-a Zokhawthiang/Kani daia MLA 3 (pathum) te HPC(D) ten an lo ambush tuma nunna chan te kha an ni.)
