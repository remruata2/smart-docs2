A. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST &VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 12.4.2013

MNYF requests police for faster action on FIR: MNF party Youth (MNYF) went to Aizawl Police Station yesterday and met case IO, Aizawl South SDPO to seek explanation concerning an FIR they had filed at Aizawl Police Station last year.

            Last year, MNYF filed an FIR against Congress party accusing them of spreading lies using a press release regarding the dinner party of MNF at the residence of their Vice President Dr. R.Lalthangliana and the topic of their discussion. MNYF said that there is no development regarding the case till now. MNYF said that the Case IO told them it is difficult to deal with the case quickly but it will be looked into the matter. The MNYF demanded the Congress government to give the police a free hand in their investigations.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 12.4.2013

Police found NIOS Principal guilty - MSU: Regarding an FIR lodged against a the NIOS's Principal in Aizawl, Mizo Student Union (MSU) said that Police found out that the Principal committed malpractice and as such MSU will not withdraw the FIR filed against him. MSU said that the police, during enquiry found out the malpractice of the Principal and to have admitted his guilt. While, the police are waiting for a written withdrawal from MSU as per the agreement made by students and the Principal of NIOS,  the MSU prefers the FIR to go on and as such 2 (two) cases – Criminal Breach of Trust (Sec. 405 IPC) and Cheating (Sec. 415 IPC) were registered against the Principal.
