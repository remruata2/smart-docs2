SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/192

Dated: 12th April, 2020.   

 

                        Information has been received that Sub-Headquarters YMA, Champhai will convene its Executive Emergency Meeting tomorrow, i.e., 13.4.2020 @ 10:30 a.m at YMA Office, Tlangrawt, Champhai to discuss its standing demand for transfer of Pu S.L Naveen, IAS, DC Champhai. As per input, Sub-Headquarters YMA, Champhai has sent a letter to Chief Secretary, Govt. of Mizoram on 26.3.2020 requesting for his transfer from Champhai within the month of March, 2020 due to his alleged failure to discharge his official duties as a DC in the wake of Covid-19. Again, Sub-Headquarters YMA, Champhai sent another letter to Chief Secretary on 31.3.2020 reiterating their request for transfer of the said DC from Champhai due to his alleged inaction.

                        Amongst others, Pu S.L Naveen, IAS, DC Champhai was alleged to have self quarantined himself in his official bungalow immediately after the confirmation of Covid-19 case in Aizawl and neighbouring Myanmar and fails to take up the task of DC. He was also alleged to have ignored Sub-Headquarters YMA, Champhai proposal for establishment of Quarantine Centre prior to enforcement of Lockdown for sheltering of around 100 (one hundred) persons reportedly stranded in Myanmar. He was further alleged to be disinterested to take initiative for prevention of border crossing by Drugs peddlers and substance abusers.

                        As per input, Sub-Headquarters YMA, Champhai is resolute in its demand for transfer of Pu S.L Naveen, IAS, DC Champhai from Champhai. It is reported that it will decide to go to the extent of withdrawal of all YMA Members from any Village/Local Level Task Force activities in tomorrow's meeting if no positive action is taken up by the Government.

                        Situation under close watch.

DGP (M)
