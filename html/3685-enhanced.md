A. _Front page of VANGLAIN newspaper Dt. 23.09.2022_

****2,11,838 families having job cards**: In Mizoram, there are presently 2,11,838 families who are having job cards under Mahtma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), a scheme under Central Government. Out of this figure, 2,20,641 are considered as active by Rural Development department. The remaining 1,197 cards can be considered as invalid due to death of the holder, shifting of places, non participation etc.**

 In Mizoram, out of the 26 RD Blocks, Lunglei RD Block has the highest number of job cards (17,990) while the lowest is Thingsulthliah RD Block (3,767). The present wages of job card holders per head is Rs. 233 per day.

 These job cards are meant for the rural areas only and could not be enjoyed by residence of Aizawl city as Aizawl has been declared as municipal areas. Likewise, residence of Lunglei town can no longer hold these job cards after the establishment of Lunglei Municipal.

B._Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 23.09.2022_

**Meth seized :** Champhai police on the night of 21st September, 2022 recovered 12.584 Kgs. of suspected Methamphetamine from one truck at Tuipui village, Champhai District . The suspected owner of the contraband items - Habibur Rahman (20), Karimganj (driver of the vehicle) and Badrul Hague (40) Dharmanagar, Tripura were apprehended. The seized items were estimated to be worth around Rs. 37 crores in the international market.
