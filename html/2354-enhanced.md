****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/163

Dated 8th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/153 Dt. 4.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

33 (thirty three) vehicles (HMV-14, MMV-13, LMV-6) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 8.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

26 (twenty six) vehicles (HMV-4, MMV-17, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 8.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. 4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

81 (eighty one) vehicles (HMV-47, MMV-2, LMV-25, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-7) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 8.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

180 (one hundred eighty) vehicles (HMV-68, MMV-32, LMV-36, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-9, Oil Tanker-29, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 8.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
