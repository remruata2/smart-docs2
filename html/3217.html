A. Inside page of THE AIZAWL POST & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 26.7.2013

JAC at Rangvamual: At the behest of Central YMA, a Joint Action Committee (JAC) for Rangvamual was formed on Tuesday night ie, 23rd July, 2013 to fight against illicit liquor and other social activities.

 

B. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES & NEWSLINK newspapers Dt. 26.7.2013

Mistiris raises their wages: A one day special conference of Mizoram Mistiris Association at Aizawl Central Hall, Venghlui on 23rd July, 2013 raised their wages to ` 700/- per day for first class mistiri with effect from 1st August, 2013. Wage per hour is fixed at ` 100/-.
