Subject: Application under RTI Act, 2005.

 With reference to your letter regarding application under RTI Act 2005, the required information is furnished as under.

****1.** There had been **10(ten)** encounters done by Mizoram Police, CRPF and Assam Rifles in Mizoram since 1.1.2002 to 1.1.2014 as FIR registered in various Police Stations of the State.**

 Details in year wise of encounter:

 **2002**

 (i) **Encounter between UT Demand Army and Mizoram Police:** On 12.3.2002, an encounter took place between Mizoram Police and UT Demand Army near Chhakkhang, Mizoram in which one Ralhaia(15) S/o Tlirhnena of Niawhtlang village, a cadre of UT Demand Army was injured.

 **2003**

 (i) **Encounter between United National Liberation Front (UNLF) and CRPF:** On 11.8.2003, 20-25 armed militants of UNLF of Manipur attacked CRPF Camp at New Vervek, Mizoram and there was exchange of fire for about 15 minutes. One CRPF personnel CT. Shri Ram Gond S/o Krishna of Paharpur village, Bihar, sentry duty at the time of attack was killed and also injured Praful Kumar(Cook) on his right leg. The militants snatched away one SLR and 6 rounds of ammunitions from the slain CRPF.

 Case reference: New Vervek PS C/No.7/03 dt.11.8.03 u/s 121(A) 302/326/34 IPC r/w 25(1-A)/27(2) Arms Act.

 **2004**

 (i) **Encounter between United National Liberation Front(UNLF) and Mizoram Armed Police:** On 16.1.2004, armed militants of UNLF of Manipur attacked Vaitin MAP Post by using small weapons and 2" mortar. The MAP also promptly retaliated and there was exchange of fire for five minutes and thereafter the militants withdrew themselves immediately and left towards Manipur side by using Sumo vehicle. There was no casualty or injury on both sides.

 Case reference: New Vervek PS C/No.1/04 dt.18.1.04 u/s 121(a)/34/307 IPC r/w 25(1-A)/27 Arms Act and New Vervek PS C/No.2/04 dt.18.1.04 u/s 121(a)/379/436 IPC.

 (ii) **Encounter between Chin Integrated Army(CIA) and Mizoram Police:** On 24.10.2004, exchange of firing took place between Mizoram Police and CIA near Tiau river below Sesih village in Champhai District while searching Ngunuklian of CIA and 50 others who were reportedly collecting illegal taxes from Myanmarese traders. No casualty on both sides.

 (iii) **Encounter between Chin Integrated Army(CIA) and 25th Assam Rifles:**__On 9.12.2004, there was an encounter between CIA and 25th Assam Rifles at a jungle near Saikhumphai village in Champhai District. One Assam Rifles Captain Sanjay Arya was killed at the spot.

 Case reference: Champhai PS C/No.264/04 dt.13.12.04 u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25(1-A) Arms Act r/w 5 of IES Act.

 **2005** - NIL

 **2006**

 (i) **Encounter between Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram(BLFM) and Mizoram Police:** On 11.1.2006, armed militants of BLFM had opened indiscriminate firing towards New Kawnpui BOP of 3rd Bn. MAP under Mamit District. The Police also returned fire and repulse the militants. The militants immediately fled towards Tripura side. No casualty on both sides.

 Case reference: West Phaileng PS C/No.3/06 dt.12.1.06 u/s 307 IPC r/w 25(1-A) Arms Act.

 (ii)**Encounter between Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram(BLFM) and Mizoram Police:** On 6.2.2006, Mizoram Police Operation Party come across the BLFM cadres at the outskirts of Sihthiang village, Mamit District. When the Police party shouted at them to halt and identify themselves, the BLFM cadres suddenly opened fire of 2/3 rounds using local made gun. The Police party also instantly returned the fire and as a result of which one BLFM cadre Lalngaihawma(23) S/o Kapdula of Gosirampara Bru Refugee Camp, Tripura was shot below his left nipple while the remaining BLFM cadres could manage to escape under the cover of forest. There was no casualty on Police side.

 Case reference: Kawrthah PS C/No.4/06 dt.7.2.06 u/s 307/384 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(a)/27 Arms Act.

 (iii) **Encounter between Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram(BLFM) and Mizoram Police:** On 16.3.2006, patrolling party of Mizoram Police at Zamuang village received information that BLFM insurgents were seen and some of them carrying arms. The patrolling party rushed to the spot and found a group of 5 BLFM militants wearing camouflage uniform and ordered them to identify themselves, but instead they started to flee and one of them fired at the Police. The Police patrolling party returned back the fire and killed one of the militant Joinborai Reang@Thangkima(30) S/o Sormorai Reang who was carrying a local made gun. The rest escaped into the jungle towards Tripura.

 Case reference: Kanhmun PS C/No.3/06 dt.17.3.06 u/s 307/189/506/34 IPC r/w 25(1-B)(a)/30 Arms Act.

 (iv) **Encounter between Bru Liberation Front of Mizoram(BLFM) and Mizoram Police:** On 21.3.2006, after receiving a reliable information about the commission of extortion by 3 members of BLFM militants at Thaidawr village, Police patrolling party of New Kawnpui 3rd Bn. MAP BOP immediately rushed to the spot. As the Police cordoned the house of Chutoino, Ex-VCP of Thaidawr where the militants were reportedly hiding, the militants on seeing the Police started running. The Police party then opened fire at the fleeing militants and shot Rohila@Rohida at the right thigh. He was apprehended and brought to Kawrthah hospital for treatment. The other two BLFM militants managed to escape.
