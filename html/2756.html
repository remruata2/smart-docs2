THREAT PERCEPTION ASSESSMENT REPORT

 

 

            Discreet and careful assessment on threat perception in connection with the travel plan of Sri Shankara Bharati Swamiji along with 25 members of scholars and Shishyas was conducted and during which Sri Dinesh Hegde, P.A. to Sri Swamiji was contacted. On contrary to the tour plan received, he (the P.A) classified that Sri Swamiji and party are presently visiting Assam state and from where they will be visiting Nagaland and Manipur states, there after they will return to Assam state. His P.A. further mentioned that Sri Swamiji and Party have no intention of  visiting Mizoram State yet as he further assumed to send notification in advance if such visit will be conducted.
