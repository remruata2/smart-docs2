A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.5.2014

Selam Community Leaders in Police Custody: Selam community leaders who were arrested in connection with torching of Environment & Forest department’s truck and timber at Lengteng Wildlife Sanctuary are still detained at Champhai Police Station. Meanwhile, Selam Welfare Committee Aizawl made a demand that Selam community leaders be released as quickly as possible. After Selam community leaders were detained at Champhai Police Station for 24 hrs., they will be detained in Police custody for another 48 hrs. with the permission of the magistrate.

 

B. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 26.5.2014

Police on Duty Missing – Found Safely: SI Rochhara of 1st Bn. MAP who was sent for duty at Selam to resolve the conflict there went missing 5 kms. before reaching Selam. His friends/Police personnel and Selam villagers searched for him till dusk of Saturday but to no avail. But fortunately, he came back safely and joined his friends yesterday morning. SI Rochhara was among the Police on duty regarding the conflict caused by the burning of government’s truck at Samakzawl near Selam on 16th May.
