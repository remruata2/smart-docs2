INTERROGATION STATEMENT OF VANLALFELA

 

 

            I joined BLNF on 8.6.1997 upon invitation by the founder president, Vanlalliana and actively took part in the following refarious activities/offence of the outfit.

 

1.         I took part in the killing of Lalzawmliana on Dt.21.9.97 by 6(six) cadres and four BNLF Cadres rear Persang, Tuipuibari. One Bipota - NLFT was the person who shot and killed Lalzawmliana.

 

2.         I commanded a group of 15 Cadres who were responsible for burning of the MST bus at Chika near Tuipuibari.

 

3.         I alongwith three other Cadres namely Rema of Tuirum, Duhkima of Tuipanglui and Enga of Pathiantlang escorted a group of 34 NSCN (IM) Cadres who were planning to pass through Mizoram from Bangladesh. On the way, we came across a group of village catching Crabs at Tumpanglui near Tuidamlui, we randomly opened fire on them and returned to Bangladesh. There was no casualty.

 

4.         We kidnapped a non-local engaged in cutting bamboo around 1999-2000 for a ransom of Rs. 2 Lakhs. He was kept in captivity for 7 days and was released on payment of Rs.20000/-

 

5.         I was arrested at Patharkandi by Assam Police following the raid on BNLF Camp in Bangladesh by NLFT and was put in Karimganj District jail. After I was shifted to Aizawl, I managed to escape.

 

6.         We kidnapped a Mahout near Karimganj Medili, Assam in 2005 for a ransom of Rs.10 Lakhs. Tripura Police rescued the person. Following an encounter with CRPF between Medili and Balia where we had to retreat.

 

7.         I was arrested at Naisingpara refugee camp by Tripura Police in 2005 and kept in Custody for 11 days I was handed over to Assam where I was held for 1 ½ month, due to my involvement in various cases, I was again shifted to Central Jail, Aizawl where I served time for a period of 3 months and subsequently released.

 

            I had been staying at the DHD (Dima Halan Daogah) Harangajao NC Hillo, Assam alongwith 15 other Brus after I joined DHD in December 2006. However, I made a point of regularly visiting the Naisingpara refugee Camp where I kept my family.

            In early November 2009, Henry called me up from somewhere in the jungle area near Mehdili, Assam and told me that he was in constant touch with the MBDPF leaders. According to Henry, the MBDPF leaders felt that the ongoing negotiation with the Mizoram Govt. would not bear fruit and their demands would remain unfulfilled. In view of this, the leaders felt that it would not be wise to repatriate the Bru refugees to Mizoram at this juncture. Hence, drastic measures have to be taken up to sabotage the repatriation. Henry conveyed through phone the wish of the MBDPF leaders to me and Lalthazuala who was at the Asapara. Lalthazuala in turn asked me to come out of the DHD camp and meet him at Asapara. However, Henry warned that no good weapons(rifle, automatic weapons) must be used or else. It will create the impression of the handwork of an organized outfit endangering their future. Thus, Henry told us to do as we see fit for follow-up action self contacted/recruited some reliable youth and visited villages. When I was in Silchar, Mizoram Police arrested me at Silchar on 12.1.2010@ 0500 hrs. and seized the following articles from us :-
