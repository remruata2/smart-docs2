            Hetiang hian Three-men Commission in HPC hotute nen sawihovin HPC demand area hi an lo siam tawh niin S.Khuma, Ex-HPC Information Secretary hnen atangin hriat ani a. Hetiang zulzui hian HPC(D) te pawhin tunlai hian demand letter hi khaw tinah an sem niin hriat a ni. Chutih rual erawh chuan Bukvannei, Saihapui leh Buhchang ah te erawh chuan demand letter hi an la dawng lo niin hriat a ni.

 

            HPC leh Mizoram sorkar inrem hnu 1994 hnuah SHDC siam anih khan a chunga khaw tam zawk khi SHDC huamchhungah rin luh an ni ve ta lova, HPC(D) te hian SHDC Area hi pawm lovin a hma a demand area and khan an thilphut ADC hi an kalpui zel niin a lang.
