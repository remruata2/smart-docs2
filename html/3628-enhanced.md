****23.5.2022 :** Lai Students' Association (LSA) General Headquarters in its Press Release issued on 23.5.2022 stated that 2 (two) Doctors of Lawngtlai District Hospital viz. Dr. Paul Lalchungnunga Grade II, DMS, Lawngtlai and Dr. Andrew Kawlthangpuia Grade II, Lawngtlai have been transferred to other places without replacement in the recent Transfers and Posting Order issued by the Health &amp; Family Welfare Department, Govt. of Mizoram. It also stated that 3 (three) Doctors of Lawngtlai District Hospital had already been transferred out without replacement in the year 2020. In this connection, it expressed their disappointment and urged the state government to revoke the said transfer order or to arrange their replacements. It also informed the above stated two doctors, who are presently under order of transfer, not to join their new places of posting before the state government makes necessary replacement or arrangement.**

**2.9.2022 :** Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) held Central Executive Committee Meeting on 2.9.2022 and the meeting decided to take necessary action for delegating similar authority/power to Lai Autonomous District Council as enjoyed by other ADCs in the Northeast. In this regard, the meeting decided to constitute a 'Study Team' to undertake detail study of others ADCs in the northeast. The meeting also decided to constitute "Anti Drug Squad" under the leadership of Pu. T Rohlupuia, Financial Secretary, CYLA.
