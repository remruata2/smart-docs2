15.12.2022  : Siaha District All NGOs viz. Mara Thyutlia Py (MTP), Mara Chano Py (MCP) and Mara Students Organisation (MSO) issued a Joint Press Release on the evening of 14.12.2022 in which they expressed their severe disappointment over the alleged undue pending of Mara Autonomous District Council (MADC) no-confidence motion file by the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Mizoram. They demanded the Hon'ble Chief Minister to facilitate eligible party to from Government at MADC before the end of 15th December, 2022 and expressed their determination to take appropriate measure to safeguard the autonomy, the people and the land of Mara if their demand was not fulfilled within the given time. The Press Release also strongly condemned the Hon'ble Chief Minister over his alleged removal of Dr. K. Beichhua from the post of Minister of State on baseless ground. They held that this is an act of disregard to Mara people and other minorities.
