A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.30.7.2013_

**Highway robbers confessed**: Three persons among six persons who were apprehended by Mizoram Police in connection with the robbery of 17 NEEPCO trucks on 20th July, 2013 near Saiphai confessed to their crime. These three robbers were sent to Kolasib District Jail after their confession while the remaining three were detained for further interrogation. From these apprehended persons, cash amounting Rs.1000/- was also recovered.

1. _Front page of VANGLAINI & Second page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 30.7.2013_

**Road Blockade**: To show their demand for the speedy apprehension of the culprit by the Police in the murder of Lalnunmawia on 5th July, 2013 at Falkland Veng, members of Zuangtui community intend to barricade Zuangtui road this coming Wednesday i.e. 31.7.2013.
