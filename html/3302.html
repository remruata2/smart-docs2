A. Front page of VANGLAINI  & THE AIZAWL POST newspapers Dt.19.3.2013

Government heavily in debt of Police TA/DA: Answering the question of K.Liantlinga, ZNP member, Home minister R.Lalzirliana told the house that TA/DA owed to Mizoram Police for their transfer and posting have amounted to Rs. 1,013.97 lakhs till 1st March, 2013.

            Home Minister said that the reason for debt of transfer TA/DA is that budget provisions has been the same for the past ten years; at the same time, the formation of 3 (three) IR Battalion, making of new Border outpost in several places and the raise of TA/DA rate in 6th Pay Revision 2010 caused financial problem.

            Different members expressed places where Police Check gate and Outpost need to be made but Home Minister said that it cannot be done due to financial problem.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt.19.3.2013

Group YMA warns kidnappers: Chhawrpial Group Young Mizo Association in its Annual Conference during 12th-14th March, 2013 at Lallen Village under Mamit District, warned the militants against dire consequences if they refuse to release the 5 (five) Mizo E&F Dept. workers they had kidnapped on 18th February, 2013.
