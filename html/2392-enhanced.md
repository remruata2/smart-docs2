BORDER ISSUES WITH ASSAM Three districts of Mizoram Aizawl, Kolasib and Mamit share 164.6 km border with Assam's Cachar, Karimganj and Hailakandi districts. Residents of Mizoram claimed that 1875 notification should be the boundary between Assam and Mizoram, while Assam claimed 1933 notification as the constitutional boundary between the two states. I.
