On 26.8.2019 As per information given by MHIP Zokhawthar, 3 (three) minor girls, namely, (i) Jubaira (14) d/o Mohammed Yunus, (ii) Porbina Begam (13) d/o Sazir Ahmed and (iii) Sabhikun Nahar (14) d/o Soinunuz, all suspected to be Rohingya Muslim and victims of human trafficking, were rescued by Zokhawthar Police last evening, i.e. 26.8.2019 @ 7:00 p.m from one Tahan Hotel, Zokhawthar. They were brought to Champhai last night itself and are being remanded in Rose Children Home, Champhai. In this regard, Farook Ahmed (19) s/o Abdul Malik, Gulsora Village, Karimganj District, Assam, who has been staying at Zokhawthar since 2016 as a cement labour, was arrested due to his involvement in receiving the three girls upon their arrival at Zokhawthar from Aizawl on 25.8.2019. Champhai PS C/No.89/19 Dt. 27.8.2019 u/s 370(2)(3)(4)(5) IPC has been registered and necessary investigation is underway.
