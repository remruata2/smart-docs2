**Sir**

****Kindly refer to your letter no.E/PHQ/Attach/2019/71 dt.17.06.2025 regarding withdrawal of guards from Chakma Rest House, Lunglei, assessment has been conducted and report is produced as below.****

**As per assessment, it may be mentioned that the House Guards were initially provided due to tensions arising out of MBBS and BDS entrance quota in Mizoram escalating tensions between the Chakma and Mizo communities during the year 2017. However, even after the tensions deteriorated and situations have normalized, House Guards have been provided till date to the Chakma Rest House, Lunglei.**

**After discreet assessment, it is learnt that no more tensions or threats persist to the Chakma communities in Mizoram since recent years and no such tensions is expected in the near future.**

****Therefore, in view of the above assessment report and considering the present situation between the Mizo-Chakma communites in Mizoram, providing of House Guards to Chakma Rest house, Lunglei is found no longer necessary.****
