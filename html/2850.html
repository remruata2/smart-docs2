SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/879

Dated: 4th December, 2019.

 

 

                    Lai Students Association (LSA) and Students’ Union of Govt. Lawngtlai College continued their agitation today, i.e. 4.12.2019 in protest of recent transfer of 3 (three) Assistant Professors from Govt. Lawngtlai College without making replacements. About 150 (one hundred fifty) students had participated in picketing of DC’s Office, BDO’s Office and Govt. Lawngtlai College Office today. Offices of DC, BDO and Govt. Lawngtlai College remained close due to picketing and no untoward incident have been reported. In a statement issued today, LSA and SU of Govt. Lawngtlai College called for an indefinite picketing of all Offices within Lawngtlai town from 5.12.2019 onward as they have not received any positive response from the Government, and appealed to the public to support their agitation.
