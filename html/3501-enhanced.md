A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 21.5.2014_

**Armed Police to be Deployed****:**Mizoram Government intends to send out more than 100 armed police personnel to arrest people who torched Environment and Forest Department's truck at the outskirt of Selam village near Lengteng Sanctuary on16th May.

 Answering question regarding behaviour/conduct of Selam villagers towards Environment &amp; Forest Department staff who went to seize illegal sawn timber and arrest who are indulged in illegal timber cutting, Home Minister said that government will not tolerate such kind of behaviour and will take legal action against the wrongdoers.

 Pu Zorammawia, IGP said that the Police will remain at Selam as long as necessary to deal with illegal sawn timber and its traders. SDPO, Khawzawl and Asst. Commandant, 2nd IR Bn. will be in charge of the Police party.
