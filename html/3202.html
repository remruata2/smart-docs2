A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.7.2013

FIR lodged: According to information given by National Highway Division-II, PWD through RTI on 24.6.2013 regarding the black top work of Sakawrdai to Zohmun road, an amount of Rs. 292.33 lakh had been sanctioned and about 60% of the black top work had been completed. But when Prism visited the work site around 30 ft. of the road was blacktopped and felt the expenditure does not match the completed work, and lodged an FIR to ACB to probe into the matter.

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.7.2013

No anticipatory bail for RL Pianmawia - PRISM: Court will make decision on Anticipatory Bail request by RL Pianmawia, Mizoram government Deputy Chief Whip today. Prism is to organise a programme at 11:00AM today at Aizawl Press Club to show that they do not want the court to grant him Anticipatory Bail.

 

 

C. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.7.2013

CBI Imphal to probe AIR Aizawl: In response to FIR lodged against All India Radio Aizawl Station regarding corruption, CBI Imphal registered a case against AIR Aizawl Station and 3 (three) names were mentioned in the FIR.
