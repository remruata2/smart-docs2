Subject:          National Convention of AIKS, CITU and AIAWU

                  This is to inform you that on 5.9.2022 (Monday), All India Kisan Sabha,Centre of Indian Trade Unions and All India Agricultural Workers under  the leadership of  Hannan Mollah, General Secretary, (AIKS), Sh.Tapan Sen , General Sectary (CITU) and B.Venkat, General Secretary (AIAWU) would hold a National Convention at Talkatora stadium,New Delhi to discuss their issues and future course of action. In this connection, you are directed to collect the following information if any group/person from Mizoram is planning to attend the said convention:

 1. No. of participants.

2. Mode of Transport .

3. Any Special train to reach to Delhi

4. Name, Address and Telephone No. of the Leaders.

5. Place of stay in Delhi.

6. Any Farmer group.

7. Any political affiliation.

8. Any other information related to Law & Order.

        The information so collected may be submitted on or before 02.09.2022 (Friday) for onward submission to Special Branch, Delhi.
