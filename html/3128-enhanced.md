****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/580

Dated: 30th July, 2021

1. Information received stated that yesterday i.e., 29.7.2021 a group of Assam Police Commando arrived at Assam Temporary Duty Post, opposite to Mizoram Temporary Duty Post at Bilpadawp Tlang, Chuhvel, Mamit District. As per input, they intend to disrupt source of supply route to Mizoram Police Duty Post at the area, if they find opportune time.

1. As per input, a team of Assam Police Commando arrived at Ramnanthpur, Hailakandi District yesterday i.e., 29.7.2021.

1. Information has also been received that 1 (one) Bus carrying Assam police arrived at Bilaipur, Hailakandi District (nearby Aitlang) today i.e., 30.7.2021.

1. Input received stated that Muslim residing at Assam side opposite to Phaisen, Kolasib District instigated each other and even intend to disrupt Mizoram bound vehicles driven by Non-mizos. Even a minor scuffle occurred between residents of the area today and vehicles carrying POL and other essential items were forced to unload at Balichhora Bazar, Assam (opposite to Phaisen, Mizoram).

1. Today i.e., 30.7.2021 journalists from ‘The Print’, National News Agency arrived near Vairengte, where exchange of fire occurred between Assam Police and Mizoram Police on 26.7.2021 and prepared necessary news coverage.

1. Necessary building materials required for construction of Mizoram Police camp at Aitlang arrived at the area and progress of the construction is going smoothly.

1. Information received stated that reinforcement of several numbers of Assam Armed Police was made at Zaite, Assam today. 6 (six) Boleros and 1 (one) Armoured vehicles were reportedly seen proceeding towards Zaite village today i.e., 30.7.2021.

  Situation is under close watch.
