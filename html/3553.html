15.12.2022 :  Zoram Drivers Union held Special Assembly on 15.12.2022 @11:00 a.m., at ZDU General Hqrs. Golden Hall, Aizawl. Pu Zothansanga President ZDU, General Hqrs. presided over the first session and in the Business Session, agenda including to pressurise the Government for the formulation of Driver's Welfare Scheme, to appeal the Government to do away with late fees in collection of taxes under MV Act. As many as 70 (seventy) members attended the Special Assembly.
