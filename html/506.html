     2) HMAR PEOPLES CONVENTION (HPC)

 

    

FORMATION:

            In its Assembly held on 17-18 December. 1986 at Sakawrdai under the chairmanship of J.Laldinliana, the Mizoram Hmar Association which was formed on 3.7.1986, was dissolved and formed a new political party named Hmar Peoples Convention with the following persons as office bearers :-

                        1. President               J.Laldinliana

                        2. Vive President      Thanglianchhunga

                        3. General Secy.       Malsawma Ralchhun.

                        4. Secretary               Thangthuama.

                        5. Treasurer               Neihkunga.

 

            The following resolutions were passed in the said Assembly:-

 

     1) A Memorundum be submitted to the Prime Minister of India for amendment of North Eastern area Re-Organisation Act  1971 to protect the interest of Hmar people and also to create Hmar Autonomous District Council under sixth Schedule.

 

     2) Hmar volunteers be formed.

 

     3) National Executive Committee be formed to draft constitution for The HPC.

     OBJECTIVES:

            The main objective of HPC is to achieve Hmar Autonomous District Council. The demanded areas include five Legislative Assembly Constituencies of northern part of Mizoram comprising (1) Ngopa Constituency (2) Ratu Constituency (3) Kolasib Constituency (4) Bilkhawthlir Constituency (5) Suangpuilawn Constituency and also some portions of Cachar District of Assam and Manipur State where some Hmar communities settled.

           

            The HPC was involved in various illegal activities like extortions,   kidnappings, lootings and ambush of security forces etc. till the signing of MoS on 27.7.1994 by the Hmar Peoples Convention and Govt. of Mizoram after 14 rounds of talks which started from 31.7.1990.

Activities:

 

1) The unlawful activities of HPC started with wrongful confinement of one Mr. Kunga of Senvawn, Manipur. Mr.Kunga was coming to Mizoram for political campaign for Congress(I) party before the Legislative Assembly Election of January 1989. He was wrongfully confined by Malsawma Ralchhun, General Secretary HPC and MLA candidate on 18.1.89.

 

2) On 20.4.1989, HPC resorted to acts of sabotage in respect of Rovali Bridge located on the Aizawl-Silchar highway within Bilkhawthlir town, slightly damaging with explosives.

 

3) On 16.5.89, the Mizoram Police party consisting of SI R.Vanlalauva, H/C Ngaihdama, H/C Lalngaia, Const. Ralliana and Const. Rohnuna were ambushed by HPC volunteers led by Thangliankhuma at Bashertol near Maniarkhal Tea Estate, Cachar, Assam. During the encounter SI R.Vanlalauva was killed at the spot while H/C Ngaihdama and Const.Rohnuna sustained serious bullet injuries. Two HPC volunteers, namely Lalhuapliana S/o Sawia of Sartuinek and Lianhmingthanga S/o Phuta of Sartuinek were killed when the Police party returned the fire.

4) On 15.7.89 at 9:30pm, about 20 armed HPC volunteers raided the house of Mr. Khuma of Tinghmun village and abducted Mizoram Armed Police Constable No.467 H.Thangzela, Lalhmingthanga S/o Sapbiakthanga and Mr.F.Thanzama, President, Cong(I) Unit, Tinghmun with a view to kill them. However, Lalhmingthanga and F.Thanzama were released after severely assaulting them. Later on, Mizoram Armed Police Constable No.467 H.Thangzela was killed by HPC volunteers in Manipur.

 

5) On 3.11.1989@ 9:30 am while Police personnel of Kolasib PS were conducting patrolling at Bawktlang near Builum, 6 armed HPC volunteers opened fire upon the Police party. During the exchange of fire one HPC volunteer Lalnuntluanga S/o Tea of Mauchar was killed. The Police party recovered one .303 Rifle with 14 rds of ammns, two SBBL guns with 11 rds live cartridges and other articles from the place of encounter.

 

6) On the night of 12.12.1989@ 9:00pm, about 15 armed HPC extremists entered into Suangpuilawn and looted two .303 Rifle with 48 rds of .303 ammns from the Home Guards on duty at Suangpuilawn Rural Bank duty. Also they looted property worth of Rs.10000/- from the villagers at Suangpuilawn.

 

7) On 11.1.1990 at about 1345 hrs. while 1 MAP personnel were crossing Mauchar to Tinghmun road junction, the HPC volunteers ambushed the Police party. During the exchange of fire MAP Const. Rongheta sustained bullet injuries on his person.

 

8) On 2.2.1990@ 1:50am some unknown persons, suspected to be HPC volunteers, attacked Saipum MAP Camp. They fired 5 rounds and the Police also fired in return.

 

9) On 8.2.1990, a Police party of the 5th Camp MR Sartuinek, who tried to capture group of extremist at Sartuinek village, were fired by HPC volunteers. In self defence the Police party also returned the fire as a result of which one HPC leader, Hmingthanga S/o Sapbiakthanga of Tinghmun sustained bullet injury and succumbed to his injury on the way to Parbung Public Health Centre.

 

10) On 20.3.1990, the Mizoram Police party on operation near Daido village were fired upon by the HPC activists. The Police party also returned the fire and arrested S/S HVC Commander Siamliana s/o Kaikhawthang(L) of Phuaibuang village, Mizoram. The Police party recovered 6 nos. of SBBL Guns with 8 rounds of ammns. and 2 empty cartridge. Also 2 nos. of electric detonators with fuse were recovered by the Police from his possession.

11) On 24.4.1990 morning at about 11:00am, Mr.Lalbiakthanga,Dy.SP, 1st MAP, Wing Commander while coming by his vehicle between Pathianpakawn and Sakawrdai, ran over a mine(bomb) laid on the road by some HPC extremists. As a result of explosion, the vehicle was badly damaged and Mr.Lalbiakthanga sustained serious fracture injuries on both legs.

 

12) On 24.5.1990, 12 HPC volunteers, armed with rifle, sten gun and shot guns, and wearing green uniform entered Zohmun village. They took out Mr.Darkunga, VCP from his house and snatched away Rs.4000/- and wrist watch from him. After that they caught hold of one Chawnga from the village street while he was going home. Said Chawnga saw that the HPC volunteers kept with them C/408 Lalnunmawia of 1st MAP Zohmun Post with his hands tied at the back.

            The HPC volunteers took both Const. Lalnunmawia and Chawnga out of the village, but they released Chawnga later. The HPC volunteers fired about 10 rds and after hearing the sound of firing, Police party immediately rushed to the spot and recovered 7 empty cartridges and 1 lead cartridge of .303 rifle, 3 nos. of empty cartridges of 12 bore(shot gun) and 2 nos. of 9mm lead cartridges. On 25.5.1990, the local YMA members and Police party recovered the dead body of Const. Lalnunmawia from the road side in between Zohmun and Sakawrdai about 1 km. from Zohmun.

     3) BRU NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT (BNLF):

 

 

FORMATION:

            The BNLF was formed on 9.11.1996 at Tuirum to strengthen the repeated demands of Autonomous District Council by Bru National Union for Bru people living in western belt of Mizoram.

            The founder President of BNLF Vanlalliana went to Bangladesh in the month of November 1996 and sought the advice of NLFT leader Donu Kolai (the then Dy.Chief of Army of NLFT) and with his advice and guidance, BNLF set up, arms training and procurement of weapons have been arranged by the NLFT.

 

 

OBJECTIVES:

       A Conference convened by BNU was held at Saipuilui Village of Mamit District on 23-24 Sept 1997 and the Conference passed a resolution demanding separate Autonomous District Council for Brus in Western Belt of Mizoram. Mizos living in this belt reacted immediately against the said resolution as a result of which the situation in this area had become tense between the two communities.

       To make matter worst, Pu Lalzawmliana, Wild Life Game Watcher of Forest Department was killed by some unknown miscreants and his two companion viz. Jutendro and Ngaihdama had also been abducted on 21.10.1997. The two abducted persons resurfaced at Dampa Sanctuary on 24.10.1997 and revealed that the deceased was murdered by 10 armed Bru militant group.

       The general public of Bru community were also threatened by Bru militants to leave Mizoram and to escape the wrath of Police and Mizos due to the murder of the Forest Game Watcher. As a result, a large number of Bru people fled Mizoram in the last part of 1997 and they settled at Tripura North District as refugees.

     Numerous activities like robbing, assault of innocent civilians and endless demand letter for money have been served to the people of Mizos living in the western belt of Mizoram by the BNLF.

            Following are information that can be furnished during the BNLF insurgency till the signing of MoU in 26.4.2005:-

           

                               BNLF personnel killed        - NIL

                               Police personnel killed       - 10

                                                      Civilian killed                       - 3

 

Activities:

 

1) On 11.4.1998 at about 5:30pm, BNLF militants ambushed Mizoram Police truck(water carrier) at a place 4 kms. west of BSF Camp, Tuipuibari. The militants threw a number of hand grenades and thereafter resorted to indiscriminate firing at the truck.

 

2) On 20.4.1998, 10(ten) armed BNLF militants entered the house of Pu Soronia, Bru Village Council President of Sachan village and fired at him with an Air Gun and forcibly took away Rs. 33,110/- (Rupees thirty three thousand one hundred ten) from the him and other villagers.

 

3) On 27.7.1998, 5(five) BNLF extremists ambush Mizoram Police patrolling party in between West Bunghmun and Kawrtethawveng at 1600 hrs. In the exchange of fire 2 (two) BNLF extremists were arrested.

 

4) On 30.6.2000 at about 1530 hrs., BNLF militants ambushed Mizoram Police     Operation party consisting 19 (nineteen) personnel under the command of Hav. C.S. Lalrinzuala who were returning to Tlabung in a Truck between Bandiasora and Devasora. The militants opened fire upon the Mizoram Police and the gun fight lasted for about half an hour. 6(six) Police personnel were killed on the spot and 8(eight) Police personnel were injured of whom another succumbed to his injuries in the BRTF camp at Malsury, thus the total Police personnel killed has become 7(seven).

 

5) On 25.7.2000, one jeep belonging to Nabu of Damcherra in Tripura was stopped by unidentified militants at a place called Vawmsual while proceeding towards Damcherra from Panisagar. The militants opened fire while the vehicle was moving back killing Father Victor Christa on the spot.

 

            To end mistrust amongst different communities and militant activities of BNLF, Govt. of Mizoram initiated Peace Talk with leaders of BNLF w.e.f. 7.9.2001 and after 12 rounds of peace talks between Govt. of Mizoram and BNLF, an MoU was signed between BNLF and Govt. of Mizoram on 26.4.2005.

 

                        Arms Surrendered:   9 nos. of different types (date of surrender 25.7.2005)

                        Ammns. Surrendered:  661 nos. (date of surrender 25.7.2005)
