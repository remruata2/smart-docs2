            Madiki nu u, Hauchhungi leh ka nu Thansiami hi unau an ni a, Madiki hi ka cousin ani. Madiki hi Aizawl Saron veng a piang niin, Dinthar veng a seilian ani. Maliana of Sadaw, Myanmar (near Tahan) nen in neiin, Tahan ah tunah fa 3 leh Mabiaki nute nen an khawsa mek ani.

 

            Madiki hian Edenthar a kan in bulah Godown rawn siamin (2017-18), hetah hian tuibur vaihlo a rawn chhekkhawl thin a, he a Godown hi Pu Lalhmingliana, Edenthar  veng YMA President thin huanah ar in hlui a rawn hmang a ni. Madiki hian Vaihlo hi vawikhat ah trip 2/3 te rawn dah in semchhuah a ngaih changing mi rawn phone a, kan nufa in kan lo sem a, pawisa lak dawn ah Madiki nu emaw a mah an rawn kal thin a ni. Tin, kan thenawm huan a godown hi kumkhat a luah hnu ah ar huan lamah mi godown tawmin a insawn san a, rei pawh luah lo in a sumdawnna pawh a titawp ve ta mai ani

 

            Nikum 2018 khan Madiki hian Singapore kal duh awm ka hriat leh hriat loh min zawt in, lo zawng a hlawh te pawh nei ve turin min sawm a, kei chuan ka hriat ve loh thu leh ka chhuahvah tam loh avangin ka hriata  ka thiam ve loh tur thu in ka chhang mai a, tichuan kum 2018 chawhma lam khan amah ngeiin mizo nu pahnih(2) (pakhat phei chu kum upa tawh lam) hi kan inah a rawn hruai lut a, Singapore a inhlawh tura kal tur annih thu min hrilh ani. Heng hmeichhe pahnih(2) te hi East Lungdar lam khua niin, zankhat riakin a tukah a chhuahpui nghal ani.

 

            A chunga mite a thlah atanga thla khat(1) velah Madiki hian Mizo hmeichhe pali(4) (an khaw chhuah na ka hre lo) rawn hruai leh in kan in ah zan hnih(2) a riah tir a, hemi chhung hian Madiki hian Siaha lamah a zin san lawk ani. Siaha lam atanga a rawn kir leh hnu chuan Myanmar lam ah a chhohpui leh ta a, thlathum(3) hnu velah Madiki hi kan in ah lo thleng leh in a hruai chhoh zinga hmeichhe pakhat rawn dah thla leh ni in a in sawi(a rawn dah thlak hmeichhia hi kei chuan ka hmu ve kher chuang lo).

 

            Kumin January  thla khan Madiki hi kan inah rawn thlengin, amah  zan khat a riah hnu atuk ah Singapore kal tum nia ka rin vai (Mipa hlir pali(4)) chu Bawngkawn ah ava lam a, nilengin kan inah hahchawl in tlaiah ama ho in an chhuak veleh ta ani.

 

            February thla vel ani mai thei, Madiki chuan Phone a min rawn bia in, vai lo thleng tur an awm leh thu sawiin Greenwood Hospital thlang Sumo Stand ah kan lo va chang a. Hemi tum hian vai ho hi pariat(8) (mipa 6 hmeichhia 2) nita in ka hria, ka va hruai leh ani. Vai lo thleng  tur te hi Sumo ah ka va hmuh mai tur thu Madiki chuan sawiin, anni hian min lo nghak ani.

 

            Anni hi kan inah ka hruai thla a (zing dar ruk(6) vel) ani. Nilengin kan inah an muzal a, tlai dar 4 velah Sumo dangin an rawn lam leh ani.

 

            Zinga rawn phur thleng tu hi chu vai Sumo driver ani a, tlai lam a rawn lam tu erawh Mizo driver ani thung. An rawn lam na Sumo hi Service sumo niin ka hria.

 

            Tin, hemi hnu rei vaklo (kar hnih hnu vel) hian mi 12 vel (Hmeichhia pa 3, mipa pakua(9)) rawn thleng leh in driver Dawnga'n kan in ah rawn dah in ni 2 leh zan 1 an riah hnu in driver Dawnga vek hian a phur chho leh ta ani.

 

            March 2019 ah mi 12(hmeichhia pasarih(7) leh mipa panga(5) an rawn thleng leh a, zingkar dar 6 velah Mizo driver in rawn dah in, zan khat an riak a, a tuk tlai dar 4 vel ah driver dang (Mizo tho) in a rawn lam leh ta a ni.

 

            A hnu kar leh mai ah, group dang pakua(9) vel an rawn thleng leh a, hetah hian hmeichhia paruk (6) leh mipa pathum(3) anni. Zan 1 riak in a tuk tlaiah driver in a rawn lam leh ani.

 

            Tin, hemi hnu hian tuna mite hi niin zing dar  7-8 inkar velah lo thlengin, zan riak tura lo thleng a ni. Driver Mizo bawk in a lo thlah thla leh ani.

 

            Tin, hemi tum hian kan thenawm, Pu Biaksanga leh Nelsona lo kalin ka mikhual te lakah report a awm thu an rawn sawi a, lo enfiah tura lokal an nih thu an rawn sawi ani. Ka mikhual te an en hnu chuan Nelsona chuan vengchhung hruaitute phone nghalin vengchhung kumpuan ho, SRS, CYMA leh Police lam an rawn thleng zui ve nghal mai ani.

 

            Heng vai ho ka mikhual dawn reng reng hian min rawn hrilhre tu ber chu Madiki leh Dawng a te hi anni a, an rawn thlen hma zan leh thlen dawn zingah min  rawn phone mai thin a ni. Hetia ka mikhual turte min rawn hrilh hian hun tam zawk ah chuan a zat an rawn sawi thin ani. Dawnga hian ka mikhual te hi a chang chuan a phur chhuak ve thin bakk ani. Tin, vaiho ka mikhual dawn pawh hian Madiki hnenah pawh ka hlauhthawn leh thil dik a nih leh nih loh te pawh ka zawt a, Madiki hian hlauhthawng lo tur leh phalna nei thlapa lo lut an nih thu in min chhang a ni.

 

            Tin, heng vai ho hi zing a kan in a rawn dah tu mizo driver te hian tlai lam emaw a tuk tlai lam ah hian an rawn lam leh kher lo, a ngai in rawn lam chang chu awm bawk mahse, tin, an rawn dah na leh an rawn lam lehna motor(sumo) te hi kan in a tanga ka hmuh ve dan chuan Maxi Cab service lai ani vek in ka hria.

 

            Tin, a tum tak hi ka hre tawh lo, heng vai ho zingah hian tum hnih chu nu naupawm an rawn tel ve tawh bawk a, an nau pawm te hi kum 2 leh kum 3 bawr vel an ni.

 

            Madiki hi kum 35 vel hmeltha samsei ngil, sa inphut tawk vel, ngo lam pang ani.
