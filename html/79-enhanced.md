****STATEMENT OF C/20 ROKHUMA****

****OF CID(SB), CHAMPHAI POST****

 Champhai CID(SB) Sector ah kum 2005 atanga awm tanin tun thleng hian ka la awm a ni.

 Dt. 26.4.2013 hian Gauhati High Court in Vaphai leh Saikhumphai khua te thubuai a rel dan a tihchhuah dawn chungchangah Court hian Saikhumphai thiam a chantir dawn nia hriat anih avangin Vaphai mipuiin an dawnsawn dan leh chet lak an tum dan te thlithlai tur leh information pawimawh lakhawm turin SO CID(SB) Champhai in min tir a. Vaphai hi dt. 26.4.2013@ 4:00pm ah kan thleng a, khawtlang hruaitu VC, YMA leh mi hrang hrang kan kawm nghal a. Vaphai mipui hmalak tum dan erawh hemi ni hian kan hre theilo.

 Dt. 27.4.2013(Saturday) zingah YMA, VC, JAC ten VCP inah ro an rel a, Vaphai khaw mi Saikhumphai a leilet nei zawng zawng dt. 29.4.2013 zingkar ah kohkhawm nise an ti a. Hemi chungchang hi SO Champhai hnenah report pek nghal ani. Vaphai leh Saikhumphai buaina chungchang hi kimchang deuh a hotu sang hnena report a duh thu sawiin mobile signal a thatloh bawk avangin SO hian Champhai a kir leh a report pe turin min ko let a. Hemi ni hian Champhai ah kir lehin a tuk dt. 28.4.2013 khan report chu SO Champhai hnenah kan pe ta ani.

 Dt. 29.4.2013 zing dar 9 velah SO Champhai in phone a min rawn bia in Vaphai pan nghal turin min tih angin kan kal nghal a, mahse Vaphai kan va thlen chuan Saikhumphai khua chu Vaphai mipui chuan an lo hal hman tawh a ni.

 RO/AC  Recorded by

 C/20( ROKHUMA )  (GOVERDONA SAILO)

 Charged Official  Dy.SP, CID(SB)

 &amp;

  Enquiry Officer
