21.4.2022 : A Joint Press Release was issued on 21.4.2022 by Chin National Front
(CNF/A); Chin National Organisation (CNO/CNDF); Hualngoram People Organisation (HPO/CDF - Hualngoram); People Defence Army (PDA); People's Defence Force - Kalay (PDF - Kalay) and Zomi Federal Union (ZFU/PDF Zoland) wherein they stated the following:

            (i)        Expressed gratitude to Mizoram government, Churches, NGOs, Political Parties and             the people of Mizoram for rendering necessary assistance and support to
                        Myanmar refugees, who are taking refuge within the State.

            (ii)       It also expressed regret over reported involvement of Myanmar refugees in drugs smuggling inside Mizoram, and at the same time, it urged all Myanmar refugees to refrain from involving in such illegal activities.

            (iii)     It urged any arrested accused in drugs smuggling case to refrain from claiming themselves as Myanmar refugees, and henceforth, they intend to take necessary follow up action.

            (iv)      Appealed all Myanmar refugees to be responsible person in their respective place of shelter.
