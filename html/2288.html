MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/110

Dated 14th February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/109
Dt. 13.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

Information has been received that representatives from three Districts of Assam viz., Cachar, Hailakandi and Karimganj constituted Forum Against Mizo Aggression (FAMA) on 13.2.2021 in the wake of recent border row at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border. The Forum started collecting donations for victims of House Fire due to border conflict at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram border on 9.2.2021. The Forum intended to impose economic blockage w.e.f., today i.e., 14.2.2021. However, no report of road block at Assam side has been received at the time of writing the report and there was free flow of traffic across Assam-Mizoram border.

69 (sixty nine) vehicles (HMV-53, MMV-4, LMV-10, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-2) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 14.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

119 (one hundred nineteen) vehicles (HMV-51, MMV-42, LMV-13, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-2, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 14.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

         

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
