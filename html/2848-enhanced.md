****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/632

Dated: 24th August, 2019.

 Information has been received that 3 (three) unidentified armed personnel were sighted at Chamdurtlang, Lawngtlai District yesterday evening, i.e. 23.8.2019 @ 3:30 p.m at the outskirt of the said village (not more than 100 mtrs. from the village). The three persons were reported to be dressed with camouflaged with arms. Thereafter, it was reported that some persons were reported to have sighted 3 (three) persons entering into LIKBK school at the village at around @ 7:00 p.m. Local leaders of the village (YMA &amp; YLA) were immediately alerted and immediate pro-action were undertaken, but it was learnt that the said persons were nowhere to be found in the village or its immediate vicinity. The villagers were learnt to be on prompt vigil and deployed duty last night and today, but with no headways. In this regard, Officer-in-Charge, Vasei Police Station and party reached Chamdurtlang today at 2:00 p.m and conducted necessary enquiries into the matter. The villagers were learnt to believed that the 3 (three) suspected persons were most likely to belong to Arakan Army. The Assam Rifles based in Lawngtlai have also been alerted to keep strict vigil and the BSF stationed at Damdep village were also duly alerted.

 Situation is under close watch.
