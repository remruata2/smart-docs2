CONFIDENTIAL

OFFICE OF THE SUPERINTENDENT OF POLICE

CID(SB), MIZORAM, AIZAWL

                       

No. MSB/CB/B-26/                                                          Dated Aizawl, the               Oct., 2015          

 

To,

 

                        The Inspector General of Police,

                        Mizoram, Aizawl

           

 

Subject:          Operation report.

 

 

Sir,

 

      In the backdrop of the recent subversive and violent activities of HPC-D like deliberate firing of 2nd IR Bn Out post at Khawlian on 9.2.2015(vide Saitual Police Station Case No.7/2015 dt.6.3.2015 u/s 121 A/427 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(2) Arms Act), ambush of a convoy of three MLAs on 28.4.2015 near Zokhawthiang village killing three Mizoram Policemen (vide Sakawrdai Police Station Case No. 6/2015 dt. 29.3.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120B IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act) etc, CID (SB) has carried out an intensive human and technical surveillance against the HPC-D cadres.

 

     Through the analysis of intelligence input and technical surveillance, it was learnt that HPC-D cadres namely (i) S/S Commander Lalbiakliana S/o H. Lala, Vairengte, S/S Sgt. Chuailova (36) s/o Thuamliana, Lungthulian, Manipur and S/S Pvt. Reuben Lalromawia (19) S/o Samuela, Saipum were staying at Hmarkhawlian, Cachar District, Assam on 18.9.2015. Special Operation Team was formed under the command of Pu H.L. Thangzuala, C.O, 1st IR Bn./designate ‘Chief Commander Operation' ( vide S.P. C.I.D (SB), Mizoram order No.MSB/PB/Misc/27/ 2015 Dt.18th Sept. 2015).

 

     The team rushed to Assam on 19.9.2015 and camped at Mizoram House, Silchar as the exact location of the targets could not be immediately established. S.P CID(SB) closely supervised the whole operation  and all technical staff of CID(SB) were also working round the clock monitoring the movement of the targets. Finally, on the evening of 22nd September, 2015 at around 8:00 PM, a reliable information was received from CID(SB) source that all the three targets were hiding at the residence of  Rodingpuii W/o S/S Commander Lalbiakliana at Hmarkhawlian.

 

     Acting on this specific intelligence input, the Operation team left Silchar on 22nd September, 2015 @ 11:00 PM and conducted raid at Hmarkhawlian on the intervening night of 22nd September 2014 and on the wee hours of 23rd September 2015. It may be mentioned here that at first the Operation team raided civilian house(Non-target house) by mistake due to  wrong information furnished by source regarding exact location of the Target  house (i.e. residence of Rodingpuii W/o S/S HPC Commander Lalbiakliana). However, due to outstanding role played by the members of Operation team, the target house was immediately located and in an ensuing raid, the following persons were apprehended from the residence of Rodingpuii W/o S/S Commander Lalbiakliana, Hmarkhawlian,Assam –

 

(i)    S/S Sgt. Chuailova (36) S/o Thuamliana, Lungthulian, Manipur

 

(ii)   S/S Pvt. Reuben Lalromawia (19) S/o Samuela,Saipum, Mizoram

 

(iii) H. Lala (68) S/o D.Dala, Vairengte State Veng, Mizoram

 

      S/S Sgt. Lalchuailova was involved in the deliberate firing of 2nd IR Bn BOP at Khawlian on 9.2.2015 vide Saitual PS Case No.7/2015 Dt.6.3.2015 u/s 121 A/427 IPC r/w 25(1A)/29 (2) Arms Act and he was also involved in several extortion/intimidation cases within Mizoram using nickname L.C Hmar, Commander HPC-D.

 

      S/S Pte. Reuben Lalromawia was one of the member of HPC-D armed group who laid ambush on Mizoram Police/ entourage of 3 (three) MLA’s on 28th March, 2015 near Zokhawthiang Vide Sakawrdai Police Case No. 6/15 dt.29.03.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120 (B)/326 IPC r/w 25 (IA)/27(3) Arms Act. And he was also involved in looting of explosive materials (220 gelatin sticks, 100 No. of detonators) from Labour Camp, near Tinghmun and serving of an extortion letter to the camp leader on the intervening night of 22-23.11.2014 Vide Sakawrdai PS Case No.12/14 Dt.25.11.2015 u/s 447/506/397 IPC r/w 25(1A) Arms Act.

 

        Pu H. Lala also actively took part in recruitment of fresh cadres of HPC-D.

 

        The main target S/S Commander Lalbiakliana however managed to escape before his hiding place was raided by the Operation Team. However, 5 mobile phones belonging to Reuben Lalromawia, Lalbiakliana and some of the family members who were living in the house had been recovered from their possession.

 

        The three apprehended persons were brought to Aizawl and were handed over to Aizawl Police Station on 22nd September, 2015 @ 11:00 AM.

 

        It may also be mentioned that along with the above three HPC-D cadres, Joseph Chawngsangbera (15) S/o Lalramliana of Hebrai Veng, Hmarkhawlian, Lalramlien (38) S/o P.K. Lawma of Hebrai Veng, Hmarkhawlian and Lalhlusang (25) S/o Remliana of Hebrai Veng, Hmarkhawlian were also taken to Vairengte Police Station on 23.9.2015 and were later released as their involvements in any of the illegal activities of HPC-D could not be established.

 

        The following officers and men of CID(SB) have participated in the operation under the command of Pu H.L. Thangzuala, C.O, 1st IR Bn and designated ‘Chief Commander Operation’ :

 

 

 

  1. Dy.SP Zarzoliana    
  2. Inspr. Lalrinthanga Ralte
  3. SI Lalrosanga Colney                     
  4. SI RK. Rosangliana
  5. SI Lalduhsaka                      
  6. ASI Lalduhzuala
  7. ASI C. Vanlalruata
  8. C/53 Lalenkawla
  9. C/  Lalnuntluanga                
  10. D/SG Lalremruata   
  11. D/G-II Lalpekmawia           

 

                        Apart from CID(SB) staffs, the following officer and men of 1st IR Bn. also participated in this operation:

 

  1. SI H. Lalrammawia              
  2. Nk. Lalchhuangliana                      
  3. C/267 C. Lalmuanpuia       
  4. C/305 Lalfamkima              
  5. C/231 Lalremruata              
  6. C/244 T. Lalmalsawma       
  7. D/G-II Manoj Kumar Barman        

 

                                                                                                 

 

 

 

Yours faithfully,

 

 

 

(RODINGLIANA CHAWNGTHU)
