**I. KILLING OF IGP, DIG & SP CID (SB) ON 13.1.1975**

 On 13th January 1975, three militants of Mizo National Army(MNA), clad in Police khaki uniform arrived at Mizoram Police Headquarters, which was at that time located in Treasury Square, Aizawl. The leader of this party, donning the rank insignia of Sub-Inspector of Police straight away entered the office chamber of the then IGP of Mizoram – GS. Arya, IPS. He then sprayed the room with automatic fire using his machine pistol, instantly killing the IGP alongwith two other senior IPS officers – LB.Sewa, DIG and K. Panchapagesan, SP CID(SB) who were also inside the room. The assailants then beat a hasty retreat using a jeep which was readily parked outside the office for such purpose.

 A state funeral was held for these three martyred IPS officers on the next day at Aizawl after which their bodies were airlifted by Helicopter to their respective home state for cremation.

**II. INCIDENT NEAR VAITIN VILLAGE ON 24.3.1999**

 On 24th March, 1999, about 20 (twenty) PREPAK insurgents with arms were seen at Vaitin Village under Sakawrdai Police Outpost jurisdiction, Aizawl District. On receipt of the information, Police party under the command of ASI Zoramsanga, incharge, Sakawrdai Police Outpost immediately rushed towards Vaitin Village. However, before reaching Vaitin Village, they were ambushed by the said Insurgent group and the following Police personnel were killed in the encounter :-

 1. ASI Zoramsanga, i/c Sakawrdai Police Outpost.

 2. C/555 Lalramthara, 1st Bn. MAP

 3. C/264 Laltanpuia, 1st Bn. MAP

 4. C/121 Malsawmtluanga, 1st Bn. MAP

 5. C/360 Khualtawna, 1st Bn. MAP

 The insurgents snatched away 3 (three) AK-47 Rifles, 1 (one) 7.62 SLR, 1 (one) 9 mm Machine Carbine and 1 (one) .38 Revolver from the Police party and 1 (one) SBBL Gun belonging to a civilian.

**III. INCIDENT IN BETWEEN BANDIASORA & DEVASORA VILLAGE ON 30.6.2000**

 On 30th June, 2000, BNLF militants ambushed Mizoram Police Operation party consisting of 19 (nineteen) personnel under the command of Hav. C.S. Lalrinzuala who were returning to Tlabung from Bandiasora using a Medium Truck in between Bandiasora and Devasora. The militants killed 7 (seven) Police personnel and injuring another 7 (seven) police personnel. No arms and ammunitions were lost by Police during the encounter and its aftermath. Mizoram Police personnel killed in the encounter were :-

 1. Hav/Drv Rokunga, 1st Bn. MAP.

 2. L/Nk K. Lalrammawia, 1st Bn. MAP.

 3. C/505 Lalnunmawia, 1st Bn. MAP.

 4. C/190 P.C. Vanlalchhuana, 1st Bn. MAP.

 5. C/180 T.C. Lala, 1st Bn. MAP.

 6. L/Nk K. Lalchhuanawma 2nd MAP.

 7. C/ Thangduna Lunglei DEF.

 

**IV. INCIDENT NEAR SAIPUM VILLAGE ON 2.9.2008**

 On 2nd September, 2008, Pay Escort Party of 1st IR Bn., Mizoram and HPC(D) militants engaged in a fierce exchange of gunfire when the later ambushed the Pay Escort Party who were proceeding towards Saipum village at a place about 3 (three) Kms. from Saipum village, Kolasib District, Mizoram. This ambushed resulted in the death of the following Mizoram Police personnel :-

  1. SI R. Lalrindika, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

  2. C/808 B. Lalchunglura, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

  3. C/366 David Rualthankhuma, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

  4. C/48 Lalremthanga, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

  

**V. INCIDENT AT THE OUTSKIRT OF ZOKHAWTHIANG/KANI VILLAGE ON 28.3.2015**

 On 28th March, 2015, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance which consisted of Pu R.L. Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Pu Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Nl. Lalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) Insurgent group numbering around 12 (twelve) cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village. 3 (three)Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while 5 (five) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about 1 (one) hour and 45 (forty five) minutes. Police personnel who lost their lives in this encounter are :- 

   1. S.I. Zoramthara Khawlhring, O/C, Sakawrdai PS

  2. DG-III Lalchuailova, Aizawl DEF

  3. C/673 Hmangaihmawia, 1st Bn MAP

  The HPC(D) militants also snatched away 2 (two) AK-47 with several rounds of ammunitions, 3 (three) 9 mm Browning Pistols, 2 (two) 0.38 Revolver and 1 (one) 9 mm Glock Pistol from the possession of Mizoram Police.

 A State funeral service for the 3 (three) martyrs of Mizoram Police was held on 29.3.2015 in front of 1st Bn MAP Commandant Office, Aizawl. Chief Minister, Ministers, Parliamentary Secretaries, MsLA, Chief Secretary, DGP and senior officials paid their last respects by laying wreaths on the coffins of the three martyrs.
