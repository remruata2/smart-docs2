****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/016

Dated 12th January, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/14 Dt. 8.1.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/5 Dt. 5.1.2021 PWD, Assam has not resumed road repairing work in between Zaite Village, Assam and Assam Police Duty Post which is around 300 meters towards Assam from the place of deployment of BSF personnel w.e.f., 10.1.2021. Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/14 Dt. 8.1.2021 Kanhmun Covid-19 Screening was shifted near Chorei Bridge, Kanhmun and Covid-19 screening has been resumed w.e.f. 11.1.2021 and 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – 3, MMV – 3, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medilli Bridge today i.e., 12.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 3 (three) vehicles (LMV-3) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 1 (one) vehicle (HMV – Nil, MMV – 1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today i.3., 12.01.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 7 (seven) vehicles (HMV – Nil, MMV – 7) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 51 (fifty one) vehicles (HMV- 22, MMV – 1, LMV – 27, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-1) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 12.1.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 Reference to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/747 Dt. 27.12.2020 road widening work under Pradhan Mantri Gramin Sadhak Yojana (PMGSY) executed by Assam at Assam - Mizoram border around 2 (two) kms towards Assam side from Zophai BOP is still remain halted till today i.e., 12.1.2021.
