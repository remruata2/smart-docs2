MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/352

Dated 3rd May, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/336

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-9, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 3.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and
no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
