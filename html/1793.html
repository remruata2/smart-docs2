                   In compliance to your letter no. and subject mentioned above, I am to state that 2.609 kgs. of suspected Heroin value of Rs. 97.75 lakhs (local market rate)  and 5 nos. of .22 (AK Model, Made in China) with 10 nos. of magazine were recovered and seized by CID(SB) wef 16.8.2019 till date.

                   Further, being a Nodal Agency and with a view to reduce the time taken for Passport verification (which normally requires around 30-45 days), a new system of Passport verification was introduced by Mizoram Police which comes into effect from 1st November,2019. In this new system, the practice of dual verification conducted by both staff of CID(SB) and District Executive Forces was discontinued wherein only CID(SB) Headquarters and its sector offices located in every districts of Mizoram conducted Passport Verification. This new system enable Passport Verification to be completed within 3 days for Aizawl District and 5-7 days for outside Aizawl District.

                   This is for your kind information and further necessary action please.
