****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/560

Dated: 10th October, 2020

 In continuation of our Special Report vide No. MSB/CB/SR/2020/559 Dt. 9.10.2020, Information has been received that today, i.e., 10.10.2020 @ 10:30 a.m. MZP General Headquarters Executive Meeting was held at MZP Conference Hall, Aizawl. Pu B. Vanlaltana, President MZP chaired the meeting and around 40 (members) including 3 (three) advisers attended the meeting. After deliberate discussion, the meeting strongly condemned Assam Police and Forest officials for burning down Pu John Zolawma’s Jhum Hut at Zaite Road, Thinghlun, Mamit district and cutting down of 500-600 areca nuts at his plantation and around 500 areca nuts from the plantation of Ben David-a S/o Duhkima of Thinghlun, and resolved to reconstruct the said Jhum Hut. The meeting also considered such action as disregard and disrespect of Mizoram Government, Mizo people and Mizo youth.

 The meeting further decided to hand over compensation of Rs. 3000 each to Pu John Zolawma and Pu Ben David-a, and reconstruction of the said Jhum Hut will be undertaken by organizing mass community work during 12.10.2020-17.10.2020. During the meeting, various atrocities faced by mizo people of Mizoram at the hands of Brus and Chakmas were highlighted, and urged Mizoram Government to take stringent necessary action in this regard.

 A verification team led by Pu B. Vanlaltana, President, MZP and 5 (five) other MZP Leaders proceeded to Thinghlun, Mamit District today i.e., 10.10.2020 @ 1:50 p.m.

 Pu Vanlalhlana, MLA, Pu Andrew H Thangliana, MLA and Pu Lalchhuanthanga, MLA also proceeded to Thinghlun today i.e., 10.10.2020 in connection with the incident.

 In this connection, last evening i.e., 9.10.2020, Thinghlun All NGOs meeting was held and they decided to make the following demand when DC, Mamit visit their village today i.e., 10.10.2020 :-

1. To show them the exact state boundary between Assam and Mizoram.
2. To pay compensation to cover the lost suffered due to yesterday’s incident.

 Further, a Public Meeting was held at Community Hall, Thinghlun today i.e., 10.10.2020 @ 11:45 a.m. under the chairmanship of Dr. Lalrozama, IAS, DC, Mamit and Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, Mamit, SP, Mamit; Pu J. Malsawma, President, Langkaih Group YMA; Pu Vincent Lalpiantluanga, President, MSU, Mamit; Pu Lalthangmawia, President, Mamit Thalai Pawl; Pu Lalthlanmawia, President, MZP Headquarters, Zawlnuam and various leaders of Thinghlun NGOs were present and around 80 (eighty) persons attended

the meeting. During the meeting, the Chairman appealed the people of Thinghlun to wait for Government action and to submit FIR to Kanhmun PS and Pu Shashank Jaiswal, IPS, SP, Mamit also stated that necessary legal action will be taken. After the meeting, the place of incident was visited and consequently two makeshift tents were set up for temporary accommodation of 1 (one) Platoon of IR personnel at Zaite Road, Thinghlun and Signboard erected by Assam Forest Department yesterday i.e., 9.10.2020 was also removed. Further, residents of Thinghlun village also intended to construct temporary camp which could accommodate 1 (one) platoon of armed personnel on 12.10.2020.

 Due to the above incident, SP, Mamit immediately deployed OC, Kanhmun PS and party yesterday i.e., 9.10.2020, to monitor law and order situation at Thinghlun and two sections of armed police from Thinghlun BOP to conduct patrolling round the clock. Further, additional one platoon from 4th IR Battalion Headquarters, Mamit led by Pu Lallianmawia, IPS, CO, 4th IR Battalion proceeded to Thinghlun to reinforce the existing force in maintaining law and order situation in the area.

 Pu SBK Singh, IPS, DGP Mizoram has taken up the matter with DGP Assam expressing concern of the people and Government of Mizoram.

 Situation is under close watch.
