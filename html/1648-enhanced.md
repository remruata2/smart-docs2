****INRUKBO/KIDNAPPING CHUNGCHANG****

**CONGRESS SAWRKAR (DECEMBER, 2008 - 13TH MARCH 2017) CHHUNGA INRUKBONA THLENG CHUNGCHANG**

**a) Tun tum Sawrkarah hian Mizoram chhungah mi rukbo engzatnge awm tawh?**

Ans: Tuna Sawrkarna chelhtu (Congress Party) te term, i.e. December, 2008 atanga March ni 13, 2017 chhung hian inrukbona tum _11_ (sawmpakhat) a thleng tawh a, mihring _40_ (sawmli) ruk bo an ni tawh bawk.

Term hmasa, i.e. December, 2008 atanga November, 2013 chhung khan inrukbona tum _6_ (ruk) a thleng a, mihring _22_ (sawmhnih pahnih) ruk bo an ni.

 Tun term kal mek, i.e. December, 2013 atanga March ni 10, 2015 chhung hian inrukbona tum _5_ (nga) a thleng tawh a, mihring _18_ (sawm pariat) ruk bo an ni tawh a ni.

**b) Mi rubo tu te hi tute nge an nih?**

**Ans: Heng inrukbona thleng zawng zawng tum _10_ (sawm) zinga tum khat, i.e. dt. 6.6.2011 a Meidum atanga hnamdang mi _2_ (pahnih) Assam State hel United Democratic Liberation Army (UDLA) ten an kidnap leh tum khat dang, i.e. dt. 20.10.2014 a Bilkhawthlir bul atanga hnamdang mi _2_ (pahnih) Cachar District Local gang ten an kidnap tih loh hi chu Tripura State hel National Liberation Front of Tripura (NLFT) leh Bru hel Borok National Council of Tripura (BNCT) inti, tuna Bru Democratic Front of Mizoram (BDFM) inti te tangkawp tih vek a ni.**

**(c) Rukbote tlan chhuah nan hian Sawrkarin tlanna sum a pe em?**

Ans: Mizo mipute nawrna nasa tak avang leh Mizoram Police, a bik takin CID (Special Branch) te inpekna leh hmalakna avangte in Sawrkarin tlanna sum engmah pe lovin rukbote hi him takin robotute hian an chhuah vek a ni.

**(c) Heng inrukbona thleng fo thin ven nan hian Sawrkarin hma a la em?**

Ans: Mizoram Sawrkar, State Police leh ramri vengtu Border Security Force (BSF) te pawhin vawiduailo hetianga thil thleng thin ven nan hian meeting nei tawh in Indo-Bangla border-ah ngawrh taka duty thin a ni. Hetianga thil duhawm lo thleng thin ven nan hian hma lak mek zel a ni. Tin, Mizoram Sawrkar chuan Dampa Tiger Reserve-a Chikha khawhlui leh Zozampui, Mamit District-ah IR Bn. Outpost-a din tawh bawk a ni.
