1. BEGINNING UP TO DATE:-

 

            I had joined DHD on October, 2007 at Harangajau in North Cachar Hill on February, 2008. I was posted at Silchar and shared a room with DHD Cpl. Bima and one driver named Ganesh Badhur Sunar ar Dimasa Sanskrit Bhawan near Meghderet Cinema Hall. There was one white Bolero Vehicle belonging to DHD at our disposal here. In Silchar, there was an area Commader Biren and some other DHD members but their exact numbers is not known to me.

 

            This is the fourth time I came to Aizawl and on the first occasion we had purchase two AK-47 at Aizawl itself for Rs. 3,30,000/- a piece. On the second occasion, we had purchased three Russian made Manser Pistol but did not know the price as I was on a lookout duty. On my third visit, however, we left Aizawl doing nothing and on the fourth time here I was arrested.

 

2. WHAT MADE HIM JOINT THE DHD:-

 

            Due to offering of job by his friends after peace talk with the Government of Assam

 

3. HOW HE GOT IN TOUCH WITH DHD:-

 

            Self-styled Corporal Bima is the first person I met among DHD member who used to come to our place to meet his wife, daughter of a police Constable of 6th Bn. AP.

 

4. HOW HE FEELS INITIALLY-THEN-LATER:-

 

            I have regretted so much for joining the outfit and will definitely left at the first opportunity.

 

5. TRAINING RECENDS:-

           

            I have not yet received formal training except brief basic handling of AK-47.

7. MEETINGS:-

 

            There are about 40 Bru (Reang) and 1 (one) Naga and our chairman is in Guwahati and I do not know whether he is in touch with other outfits of the North East.

 

8. ARRANGEMENT:-

 

            All the detailments of duty are made by Zidung, the Commandant of DHD.

 

9. KNOWLEDGE OF OTHER ACTIVITIES:-

 

            Bru (reang) people always rendered help whenever needed. DHD is classified into two group’s vis. DHD (Joel Group). This is called non-ceasefire group and DHD (Nunisha Faction) which is having ceasefire with the centre. I am in the latter group and we are fighting the former DHD (J) with the help of Bru and the Punjab Regiment. The other insurgent outfit likes Amar, NSCN etc. Used to come in our area but never create problems since we do not allow doing so.

 

10. ROLE OF DHD IN ELECTION CAMPAIGN:-

 

            Since Political Parties who came to us are entertained by the higher authority, we knew nothing in this regard.

 

11. DUTY ASSIGNMENT:-

 

            The members are detailed for duty at every village in our area and the  strength is arranged in view of the size of the villages normally there are 7-10 members at a bigger villages and 2-3 members at a smaller ones.

 

12. TARGET/HIT LIST (PEOPLE WARNED BRANDED AS POLICE INFORMERS):-

 

            As far as I know, we have nothing of that kind. However, we used to apprehended those who helped DHD (J) and detained them in our own Jail. Our leaders then announced that there will be practice firing at midnight and shot them death. I do not know much they shot and who they are. Sometimes, we handed over the apprehended to the police.

 

13. DAILY ROUTINE:-

 

            We woke up at 0300AM and had fall – in Parade at 7-8 AM and detailments were announced and lunch at 12:00 Noon and roll call at 4:00 PM. At 6:00 PM we had time for worship as one’s own faith, light down at 8:00 PM and sleep.

 

14. DICIPLINE:-

 

            Discipline had been strictly maintained by our leaders and we dared not smoke or chew tobacco in their presence. Those who found undisciplined are detained in our Jail upto a month.

Cont….4/-

15. DOCUMENTS SUPPLY TO DHD:-  I knew nothing

 

16. SOURCE OF FINANCE:-

 

            Friends told me that we received funds from the State Government to some extent. All our houses are constructed by the Government and at present, one JCB and two Trippers are working at our camp and some of our leaders said that the Assam Police will also be stationed here.

 

17. SOURCE OF PROVISION OF FOOD:-

 

            Rice is given to us by the Government and we brought other items by ourselves.

 

18. SOURCE OF WEAPONS AND EXPLOSIVE:-

 

            As far as I know four AK-47 were purchase by Bru from Hmar and those purchased by us from Mizoram mentioned earlier and the rest are procured before I joined the outfit.

 

19. DUMPS/DENS OF WEAPONS, ARMS AND EXPLOSIVE:-

 

            They are kept in a large box at our Camp and the Indian Army used to inspect them. They had inspected once in my presence and I do not know any hiding peace of weapons elsewhere.

 

20. COMMUNICATION APPARATUS:-

 

            The leaders used Japan made IKON handset and while we can tune-in the signals of Police and Army, they cannot receive ours. These sets are kept by the rank of NCO only.
