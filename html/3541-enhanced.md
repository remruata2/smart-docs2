A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.12.2022_

**Dy. Speaker resigned**: Pu Lalrinawma, Dy. Speaker of Mizoram Legislative Assembly tendered his resignation at around 10 am yesterday which was promptly accepted by to Speaker, Pu Lalrinliana Sailo. Speaking to the press after submitting his resignation, Pu Lalrinawma stated that his resignation was as per instruction he received from the Chief Minister. According to information received, the former Dy. Speaker will be inducted as one of the Minister in the present Mizoram Government. As mentioned earlier, Pu K.Beichhua, who was preciously a Minister had submitted his resignation to the Chief Minister earlier this week as the CM is intending to re shuffle the port folio of his Ministers.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.12.2022_

**Voluntary blood donations:******Yesterday (15.12.2022), Youth for Kolasib and Mizo Cultural Organisation, Sub-Headquarters, Kolasib organised Communal Harmony Programme cum Blood Donation Camp at kolasib College. In this programme, 15 male and 2 female members donated 17 units of blood for Kolasib District Hospital. Meanwhile, on the same day, 12 boys and 3 girls of NSS, Siaha Higher Secondary School donated 15 units of blood for Siaha district Hospital

**C**. _Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 16.12.2022_

****Rs. 3,909.88 Lakhs worth of narcotics seized :** A meeting of Champhai District Narco Coordination Centre (NCORD) was held under the Chairmanship of Pu James Lalrinchhana, DC Champhai District yesterday at Excise &amp; narcotics department meeting hall. The meeting announced that Rs. 3,909.88 lakhs worth of narcotics have been recovered by Police and Excise &amp; Narcotics Departments (END) during the period between 1st January 2022 -15th December 2022 within Champhai District. The meeting also discussed about the importance of having reliable witnesses during seizure of illegal narcotic substances so as to have a higher convict rates of the accused/suspects. In this connection, Police, END and YMA were entrusted to spread and organise an awareness campaign programme.**
