MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/636

Dated 12th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/633
Dt. 11.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

Site preparation for proposed Assam Police Camp at Mauhak Mual around 500 meters from Mizoram Police Camp at Zaite Road, Thinghlun was started today i.e., 12.11.2020 @ 8:00 a.m. and the work is still continuing at the time of writing the report. There is no report of opposition of the said work from residents/NGOs of Thinghlun and nearby villages.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

60 (sixty) vehicles (HMV-27, MMV-33) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 12.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and there is free movement of vehicles across the border.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

47 (forty seven) vehicles (HMV-19, MMV-2, LMV-26) carrying essential commodities entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 12.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m.

MZP Sub-Headquarters, Bairabi and MSU Sub-Headquarters, Bairabi issued a joint statement today i.e., 12.11.2020 which stated that they revoked their decision not allowing non-mizos from venturing out of their home within Bairabi area as blockade of road in between Ramnathpur and Bairabi was lifted. However, all non-mizos who venture out from their home must possess ILP, added in their statement.

Zampuimanga Group YMA organized community works for repairing of Bairabi to Zamuang road today i.e., 12.11.2020 @ 10:00 a.m. and around 150 (one hundred fifty) members participated. The community work was concluded at 3:30 p.m. today’s evening.
