SPECIAL REPORT

 

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/434

Dated: 3rd June, 2021       

 

Information received stated that yesterday i.e., 2.6.2021 @ around 12:00 noon, 1 (one) Bolero believed to be carrying officials of Assam entered Chuhvel village, Mamit district and subsequently left the village after observing the area and without any verbal exchange with residents of Chuhvel village. Again, this morning, i.e., 3.6.2021 @ 10:00 a.m., 2 (two) Assam Forest officials, 9 (nine) Assam Armed Police and 4 (four) CRPF led by Circle Inspector visited the paddy field of Pu John Halliday, located at the bank of Betchhora river/Sakeilui  towards Mizoram side, in between Chuhvel and Saikhawthlir Village and around 1 (one) km from Chuhvel Village and proceeded upto the courtyard of Pu John Halliday’s Farm House. They subsequently left towards Assam side upon seeing Mizoram Police personnel
(3rd MAP personnel from Saikhawthlir BOP), who are deployed to conduct area domination patrolling at the area due to additional deployment of Assam Police personnel at Gutguti BOP Camp, Assam (around 4 km from Saikhawthlir Village) last evening i.e., 2.6.2021. Upon learning the incident, Pu Andy Lalfakzuala, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah and Pu Lalthanfala Khiangte, SDPO, Kawrthah and OC, Zawlnuam OP and party immediately rushed to the area.  In this connection, a section strength of Mizoram Police is deployed at Gutguti Junction, near Saikhawthlir Village, to conduct area domination patrolling and additional 2 (two) sections of
4th IR Battalion personnel are currently en route to the area to prevent escalation of tension at the area.

Recapping earlier developments in connection with the above, 5 (five) Assam Armed Police led by ASI and 15 (fifteen) civilians (Brus from Gutguti village, Assam) visited the paddy field of Pu John Halliday, located at the bank of Betchhora river/Sakeilui on 30.5.2021 @ 10:00 a.m. During their visit, Assam Police and civilians from Assam informed Lalrindika, JCB Operator and Lalruatfela (both employees of Pu John Halliday), who were present at the paddy field that the area falls within Assam territory, and they subsequently left the area after taking videography of the area. Information received stated that there had been no report of border dispute in and around the area before the above incident.

In connection with the above incident, Dr. Lalrozama, DC Mamit and
Pu Vineet Kumar, SP, Mamit visited Chuhvel village on 1.6.2021 @ 12:20 p.m. and held a brief interaction with leaders of Chuhvel village and Taitesena Group YMA OB at the residence of Pu Zoramsanga, VC Vice President, Chuhvel. During the interaction, leaders of Chuhvel village informed the district administration authorities about the boundary of Chuhvel Village. Further, Pu Andy Lalfakzuala, SDO (Civil), Kawrthah and Pu Lalthanfala Khiangte, SDPO, Kawrthah along with Taitesena Group YMA OB visited the incident area the same evening and took stock of the situation.

Situation is under close watch.
