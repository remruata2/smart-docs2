A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 18.10.2012

 

Mamit DRDA Cashier arrested. Anticipatory bail for APO: The Anti-Corruption Bureau(ACB) on 16.10.2012 arrested Laldinpuia, a cashier at DRDA office, Mamit district in connection with misappropriation of funds for the construction of BNRGSK building (VC House) at Bawngva village, Mamit district. Meanwhile, the Asst. Programme Officer(APO) at the same office named Lalhmingmawia evaded arrest as he had already obtained the anticipatory bail form the district Court.

 

It is reported that the arrest was made following the FIR submitted by PRISM to ACB, alleging that the sanction amount for the construction of the VC House was not properly used. Although Rs.8,70,000/-(first installment) and Rs. 95,000(second installment) were sanctioned for the construction of new VC House, the Community Hall constructed by the 4th Assam Rifles was renovated instead.

 

The PWD estimated that Rs.7,00,000/- was used for the construction of the building. Lalhmingmawia, APO gave Rs.70,000/- to Laldinpuia, cashier.

 

Laldinpuia was produced before the Court yesterday. The court allowed Police remand for interrogation.

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz. The Zozam Times.

 

 

 

B. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 18.10.2012

 

Pistol seized: The security duty at Lengpui Airport seized a .32 Revolver B/No. D4759 and 6 ammns. from one Tajvir Singh as he was about to aboard Jet Airways flight to Kolkata.

 

The interrogation confirmed that Tajvir Singh is an ex-serviceman. He told that security duty about his unawarness that carrying a pistol was an offence since he carried a licence for the pistol.

 

The security duty told him that he could send the pistol among the goods by paying Rs.5,000/-. However, as he was not unable to pay the amount, he was sent back.
