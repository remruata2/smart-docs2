16.8.2022 :    A meeting was held under the chairmanship of Pu Lawmawma Tochhawng, MLA & Vice Chairman HPC, Lunglei on the evening of 14.8.2022 @ 9:00 p.m. in connection with Chawngte Joint NGO intention to boycott Independence Day Celebration at Chawngte on 15.8.2022 as the State government has failed to fulfil their various demands within the stipulated time viz., posting of regular Doctor and Lab Technician at Chawngte CHC, posting of women Police at Chawngte PS, posting of regular staff at SDO(C) office Chawngte etc. During the meeting, the Chairman assured to take possible necessary action on their various demands and subsequently, Chawngte Joint NGO agreed to cancel their intended boycott of Independence Day Celebration at Chawngte. Consequently, Independence Day Celebration was successfully held at the courtyard of SDO(C) Office Chawngte on 15.8.2022.
