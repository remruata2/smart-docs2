****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/511

Dated: 8th July, 2019.

 Information has been received from officials of Technical Wing of Higher &amp; Technical Department, Chaltlang, Aizawl that altogether 84 (eighty four) MBBS seats, 137 (one hundred thirty seven) Engineering seats and 7 (seven) BDS seats are allotted to Mizoram under NEC. As of now, the number of seat(s) allotted to Mizoram under Central Pool is not yet known and the information is expected be available only by the month of August, 2019. Whether the number of seats allotted to Mizoram will vary as a result of setting up of Zoram Medical College (ZMC) also remains unknown.

 Selection of candidates for Medical and Engineering State Quota is to be done through Online Registration. Online registration is made available at H&amp;TE Deptt. website for those who have done well in National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET), 2019 from Mizoram wherein their choice of subjects and institution are submitted through online. On the basis of the online applications, final selection and placements will be made and the selected ones will be asked to submit necessary documents to Technical Wing, H&amp;TE Deptt., Aizawl.

 At the all India level, last date of admission for students of MBBS is fixed for 31st August, 2019 and of Engineering students is 15th August, 2019. In the meantime, date of admission varies among the Institutions.

 Officials of the said Department have further informed that the above procedure (online system) was introduced since last year, i.e. 2018 and hence Counselling of candidates will not be conducted by the Department. First phase verification of selected students will be held during 11 &amp; 12 of July, 2019 at Technical Wing, H&amp;TE Office and students are directed to bring their original documents. Second phase verification will be held during 22 – 24 July, 2019.

 In regard to the categorization of the Chakma candidates, information received indicated that unless the Department receive order or notification from the State Government regarding placement of Chakmas in Category-I or Category-II, Chakma candidates will be placed in Category-I as per the judgment of Gauhati High Court.
