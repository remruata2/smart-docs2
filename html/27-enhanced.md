**        **SPECIAL REPORT****

     Continuation and update of Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/09/946 Dt. 14.11.2009.

1. According to information received, 32 out of 35 houses of Brus in Tumpanglui were set ablazed around 7 PM last night. Mizo youths of Tuidam and some members of Taitesena Group YMA were suspected to be responsible for the arson.

2. 20 Bru houses in Mualthuam and 43 others were razed to ground last night around 9:30 PM alongwith several livestocks. The Bru residents were boarded at the village Hall and School.

3.  The CYMA Press Release Dt. 14.11.09 issued late last night condemned the murder of Zarzokima (18) s/o Lalngilneia, Bungthuam by BRU demanding immediate action for arrest of the culprits and urged YMA members to exercise restraint.

4.  Bru Student Association (BSA) also issued a Press Release Dt. 14.11.09 in which they condemned the brutal killing of Zarzokima by suspected BSA militants. The BSA in its PR expressed fear that the incident could hamper the on-going process of repatriation of Bru refugees.

5.  Bru Co-ordination Committee meeting was held at Naisingpara Refugee Camp this morning @ 7:00 AM.The Meeting decided to organise a peaceful procession from Naisingpara to Dosda in protest against the widespread burning of Bru villages in the aftermath of the killing of Zarzokima on 13.11.09. The BCC in its meeting held that the Mizoram Police, instead of protecting the minority Brus were hand in glove with the YMA who had unleashed acts of violence on Bru Community. They also expressed suspicion as to whether any Bru are in involved in the killing of Zarzokima, and insisted that there was no evidence to implicate any Bru in that incident.

 Report received stated that the above mentioned procession concluded peacefully @ 1600 hrs. at Dosda. They numbered around 4000-5000. The procession displayed placards like ‘No solution no rest’, ‘We are Indian and we have Right’ etc. and shouted slogans accusing the Mizoram Home Minister of failure in maintaining law and order . Elvis Chorkhy, Gen. Secretary BCC and Laldawngliana, Organisation Secretary BCC addressed the procession condemnig the Mizo people for widespread violence against the Bru minorities which violated Human Rights. The BCC leaders also conducted Press Interview before the Tripura Media after the procession.

 It is also learnt that they intended to submit a memorandum to the Tripura State Government.

Cont......2/- 6. About 100 Brus who fled Mizoram following widesparead attack in Bru villages have been sheltered in a School building at Kanchanpur. They are, reportedly, Brus fleeing from Sotapa and Mualthuam villages.
7. Report received stated that around 42 Bru houses in K. Sarali had been set ablaze today around 12:00 noon.

8.  Information received also stated that Bru Women &amp; Children fleeing from New Eden had reached Behliangchhip this afternoon @ 1500 hrs. with the intention of reaching Bru Refugee Camps where they were provided food by the local Mizos. The Behliangchhip YMA is having apprehension about possible attack with no sighting of male Bru from New Eden.

 Report received further stated that Mizo youths of Villages of Zampui Hill Range volunteered and stand on guard in their respective villages against possible Bru retaliatory attack.

9.  An emergent meeting of the Central Executive Committee of the CYMA was held today i.e. 15.11.09 at 12:0 noon-at their office which was attended by 53 members and presided over by Pu Lalchungnunga President CYMA. The meeting discussed about the killing of Zarzokima of Bungthuam by Bru militants on 13.1109 and the escalating law and order situation in the aftermath of the incident. The meeting had decied to submit a 5 points appeal to the Hon,ble Chief Minister which include, among other things, taking preventive steps to avoid possible retaliation on the Mizos living in Jampui Hills by Brus and grant of compensation to the bereaaved family of Zarzokima.

10. It is reportedly learnt that leaders of MZP Gen.Hqrs. had returned from Mamit District and reached Aizawl at around 4:30 AM this morning.      As reflected on DSI No. MSB/PB/2009(196)/932 Dt. 10.11.2009 MZP, CYMA, MHIP and MUP set a meeting for tomorrow i.e. 16.11.2009 @ 1400 hrs. at MZP Pisa to discuss about the Bru repatriaion and formation of NGO Co-ordination Committee. With the unfolding of the present L &amp; O situation at Mamit District, the meeting is strongly expected to deliberate on the present law and order situation in Mamit District.

 Situation under constant watch.
