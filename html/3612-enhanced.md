A. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 03.10.2022_

**Public requested the Police to fulfil their faith in them**: On 1st October 2022, in connection with the sexual assault of a minor child of their locality, MHIP Zonuam Branch organised a peaceful procession, which was supported by women and children, Local Council, YMA and people from other communities who sympathised the victim. In this event, leaders of Zonuam veng community expressed their faith upon the police in finding out the culprit of this crime and requested the police not to disappoint their faith. The community leaders also requested the government to give the police a free hand for the immediate arrest of the culprit and to avoid necessary action to be taken by the community.

B._Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt.03.10.2022_

****Mizoram state among the tops in voluntary blood donations:** Mizoram state is among the top seven state/UT in the mass blood donation programme organised in connection with National Voluntary Blood Donation Day. To observed National Voluntary Blood Donation Day which fall on 1st October , the Honourable Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi had inaugurated a mass donation programme , Raktdaan Amrit Mahotsav on 17th September 2022 at New Delhi. In pursuance of this, Mizoram also organised various blood donation camps where 346 units of blood were received from voluntary donors. In the biggest events, organised to mark the Voluntary Blood Donation Day at New Delhi, Dr. Hmingthankimi, H&amp;FW OSD received an award from the Honourable Union Health Minister on behalf of Mizoram state.**
