                        Rajya  Sabha  Unstarred  Question  Dy.No. U3598

 

 

a) The number of insurgent groups operating in the North-East, State-wise;

 

 

-           NIL  .

 

 

b) The number of extremists who have surrendered to the forces in the North-East and have come into the mainstream; and

 

-           During the last 5 (five) years, 114  HPC(D) cadres have come into the             mainstream.

 

 

c) The step taken by Government to rehabilitate former militants from the North-East?

 

 

-           Rehabilitation package amounting to Rs. 2,80,000/-  was distributed to           114 (one hundred and fourteen) HPC(D) cadres at function held on          13.06.2018 at PHQ, Khatla as per the Memorandum of Settlement Signed         between Government of Mizoram and Hmar People's Convention (Democrats) on 2.4.2018 (The total rehabilitation package was Rs.       3,00,000/- and as Rs. 20,000/- advance was already distributed to the      cadres, the remaining i.e., Rs. 2,80,000 was distributed on that stated    date).
