2.9.2022:       Information received stated that clashes between Arakan Army and Myanmar Army near the Indo-Myanmar border continued till 2.9.2022. As per information received, the clashes resulted in the deaths of 7 (seven) Arakan Army and 37 (thirty seven) Myanmar Army. Due to the stated incident, as many as 541 (five hundred forty one) refugees are taking shelter within the state as follow:

            (i)        Hmawngbuchhuah, Lawngtlai District : 204 (two hundred and four)                               persons (47 families).

            (ii)       Zochachhuah, Lawngtlai District : 81 (eighty one) persons (22 families).

            (iii)     Laitlang, Lawngtlai District : 172 (one hundred seventy two) persons                (37 families)

            (iv)      Dumzau Tlang, Lawngtlai District : 84 (eighty four) persons (22                                    families).

            Information received also stated that the said refugees are accommodated in vacant Community Halls and buildings/houses. Leaders of Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) led by Pu R. Rampana, Treasurer CYLA distributed reliefs to the refugees today. It may be mentioned that no report of any injured Arakan Army entering into the state has been received.

8.9.2022:       Information received stated that due to the recent clashes between Myanmar Army and Arakan Army near the Indo-Myanmar Border, as many as 529 (five hundred twenty nine) refugees have taken shelter in Lawngtlai District, Mizoram as follows:

(i)        Hmawngbuchhuah village - 210 (two hundred ten) persons (49Families)

(ii)       Laitlang village           - 127 (One hundred twenty seven) persons (37 Families)

(iii)     Zochachhuah village - 124 (one hundred twenty four) persons (30 families)

(iv)      Dumzautlang village - 68 (sixty eight) persons (21 families).

 

                                    Information received further stated that concerned VCs have taken necessary action for provision of their accommodation and DC, Lawngtlai handed over relief materials to the refugees on 7.9.2022. It may be mentioned that no report of entry of Arakan Army or Myanmar Army personnel into Mizoram has been received.
