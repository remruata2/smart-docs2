**III NOS. OF ARMED CADRE:-** Approximately there are 1000 (One thousand) Armed Cadres.

 The brief note on PCJSS is as under :-

Shanti Bahini (Peace Corps) is the name of armed wing of the Parbatya Chattagram Jana Samhati Samiti (PCJSS) meaning United People's Party of Chittagong Hill Tracts. It is considered an insurgent group in Bangladesh. The Shanti Bahini is made out of mostly members from the Chakma tribe. The Chittgong Hill Tracts Peace Accord was signed between Bangladesh Government and PCJSS on 2nd Dec. 1997.

 The PCJSS accused Bangladesh Government that the peace accord agreement are yet to be implemented despite more than 20 years had lapsed since the signing of the Peace Accord. Hence, their armed wing is still in operation and some of their armed wing are operating from Mizoram.
