1.       (a) All out efforts is being made to monitor and watch the activities of Shanti Bahini.

          (b) Information received from reliable sources stated that Shanti Bahini did not have the patronage of ISI but are having close link with the BSF (Border Security Force).

          (c) Input of information received indicated that LeT (Lashker-e-Toiba) and Huji etc. activities are not notice across the border in Bangladesh though the presence of Huji couldnot be ruled out.

                   Further efforts is being made to ascertain the presence of Huji and their activities if any across the border.
