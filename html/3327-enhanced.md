A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 27.5.2013_

**FICN seized at Kolasib - 3 men arrested******: Kolasib police on Saturday i.e., 25.5.2013 seized ` 1,72,500/- of Fake Indian Currency Notes (FICN) to be deposited at Kolasib SBI, and apprehended 3 (three) persons involved in the case.

 1 lakh rupees was tried to be deposited in the account of Kamlalmawia of Khuangpuilam by Khualzangaua of Kolasib Khuangpuilam at Kolasib SBI. It was found out by the bank staff that out of all the ` 500/- denomination of ` 1 lakh, half the amount i.e., ` 50,000/- were fake, reported it to the police and Khualzangaua was apprehended.

 According to police investigation, Khualzangaua was hired with a pressurise of ` 2,000/- as reward by Ginlala of Tuithiang, Manipur who resides at Kolasib Khuangpuilam to deposit the cash. Ginlala was arrested by the police and seized ` 22,500/- from his trousers' pocket. Ginlala let Thangvela of Rengtekawn keep ` 1 lakh which was also seized by the police. Ginlala said that he bought fake currency notes from one man at Silchar, he bought ` 15,000/- for ` 10,000/- and he had already deposited ` 25,000/- in a Bank.

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & NEWSLINK newspaper Dt. 27.5.2013_

**NGOs hunger strike******: NGOs co-ordination committee of Tuipuiral is to start an indefinite hunger strike from today at Khawbung Centenary Auditorium. The NGO co-ordination committee submitted a three point demand such as road maintenance, posting of more teachers and introduction of police station in the region to the authorities but to no avail till today.
