A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 07.08 & 08.08.2022_

****CYMA to fight against Narcotic substances:** Central Young Mizo Association (CYMA) organised a meeting of all the Office Bearers of YMA Branches within Aizawl city on 5th August 2022 (Friday) at Vanapa Hall, Aizawl to discuss on two agendas - The functioning of YMA and a fight against drugs &amp; its related substances. This meeting. which was presided over by CYMA President, Pu R.Lalngheta was attended by 440 leaders of different YMA branches of Aizawl.**

 Deliberate discussion on the two topics was conducted where several members expressed their suggestions for improvements in the functioning of YMA in Mizoram. As suggested by the majority of the members in the meeting, it was decided to create a cell/group as a counter measures against drugs menace in Mizoram, the details of which will be pursued by CYMA.

B. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 07.08 & 08.08. 2022_

**Surprise Checking for SUP:******Mizoram Pollution Control Board (MPCB) is intending to conduct random surprise checking for the selling and usage of Single Use Plastic (SUP) which had been prohibited by the Government.

 Mizoram Pollution Control Board, as per instruction received from Central Government, Mizoram Pollution Control Board had prohibited the import, selling and usage of SUP from 1st July 2022 and created SUP Enforcement Squad for this issue. MPCB further requested the public and businessmen to refrain from dealing with SUP as violators will be booked under appropriate sections of law if they are apprehended by SUP Enforcement Squad.
