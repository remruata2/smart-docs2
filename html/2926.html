SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/130

Dated: 20th February, 2019.

 

 

In continuation to our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/128 Dt.19.2.2019, an informal meeting between Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib and Shri. Mohneesh Mishra, IPS, SP, Hailakandi was held yesterday, i.e. 19.2.2019 @ 12:00 noon at Police Guest House, Hailakandi. SP, Kolasib was accompanied by Pu Lalrinpuia Varte, Addl. SP, Kolasib and
Pu Lalmalsawma, SDPO, Kolasib and Shri. Varun, IPS, Addl. SP, Hailakandi and Shri. Nayan Mani Barman, APS, Dy.SP (Hqrs.) were also present from Hailakandi District. The meeting deliberately discussed about issues relating to Inter-State Police Cooperation and Zophai Border area. The meeting also proposed DC Level talks between DC, Kolasib and DC, Hailakandi. The 2 (two) SsP underlined the importance of resolving any issues with mutual respect and understanding.
