3. Criteria/eligibility for O.B election-

            Criteria for election of MSU Gen. Hqrs. O.B according to chapter14 of MSU Gen. Hqrs. Constitution :

            1) To be leaders of Gen. Hqrs., he/she must be a member of (Station) Gen.Hqrs.

2) To be an Elected OBs, he/she must not be a government servant with no political party affiliations.

3) No members can be nominated as O.Bs of Gen.Hqrs who are not pure Mizos in blood.

 

4.    Sources of income of MSU –

 

            1) Building/floor/house rent

            MSU Gen. Hqrs. is yet to have a building of their own. As such, they themselves rented private building for their office with no revenue from house/building rentals.

 

            3) Other source of income per month/year.

                      Unlike MZP, MSU Gen. Hqrs. has no revenue from budgets submitted from Hqrs./Sub-Hqrs./Branches etc.

                        The three main sources of income of MSU Gen. Hqrs are -

                        1) Monthly donations collected from Non-mizo traders within Aizawl city. This can be considered as their main source of income. Headquarters/Sub-Hqrs. and Branches are also collecting the same within their respective jurisdiction.

                        2) Another source is collection of donations from different Courier services of Mizoram.

                        3) For organising big events, MSU General Headquarters often seeks assistance from concerned Minister in Charge which normally resulted in the form of sponsorship from suitable Government Department.

                        From the various sources of income mentioned above, it can be roughly estimated that MSU General Headquarters can earn around Rs.1,30,000 - Rs.1,50,000 per month.
