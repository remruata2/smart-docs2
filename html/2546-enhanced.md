****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/504

Dated 2nd July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/498 Dt. 1.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

******Yesterday i.e., 1.7.2021 @ 2:30 p.m. Shri. Khageswar Pegu, DC, Karimganj, Assam; SP, Karimganj; Forest Officials and around 60 (sixty) Assam Police visited Medili Bridge, inter-state border and asked Post Commander, 4th IR Battalion, who are deployed at the area about the nature of their duty and when they started functioning their duty post at Medili bridge, and they left the area the same day @ 2:50 p.m.**

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-7, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 2.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

15 (fifteen) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-11, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

46 (forty six) vehicles (HMV-34, MMV-2, LMV-7, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Information received stated that in pursuance of the report received regarding the visit of Pu Sangluaia farm land, located at the edge of Aitlang area, which is around 5 Km north-west of Vairengte, inside Mizoram by security force from Assam today, i.e., 2.7.2021 @ 1:00 p.m., one platoon of Mizoram Armed Police led by Pu Bruce K. Vabeirokhai, Deputy Commandant, 1st IR Battalion rushed to the area. Upon reaching the area, they met CRPF personnel, who were on their way back to Assam, and on being asked, CRPF personnel stated that they visited the area for normal patrolling.
