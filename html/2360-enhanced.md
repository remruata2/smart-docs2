****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/186

Dated 16th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/182 Dt. 15.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-12, LMV-10) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 16.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

28 (twenty eight) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-19, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 16.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 6 (six) vehicles (MMV-6, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

84 (eighty four) vehicles (HMV-45, MMV-Nil, LMV-30, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-9) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 16.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

Police personnel from 3rd Battalion MAP, 2nd IR and 5th IR Battalion deployed at Zophai in connection with last year border dispute were closed at their respective Battalion Headquarters today.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

138 (one hundred thirty eight) vehicles (HMV-47, MMV-42, LMV-22, LPG-1, Maxi Cab-14, Oil Tanker-12, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 16.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
