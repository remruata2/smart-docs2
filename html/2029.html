BRIEF HISTORY:

 

            A chunga tarlan hi ka pian leh murna leh ka particulars tlangpui chu a ni e.

 

            Kum 1977-1985 thleng hi ka nu leh pate bulah awmin Tuipuibari ah Class-IV thleng ka zir a, 1986 ah hian Salem Boarding School, Damparengpui ah Class-V ka zir a, 1987 atanga 1990 thleng hian ka u Lalrinthanga, Inspr. of Legal Metrology Deptt., Zemabawk ami hnenah awmin Class-X thleng ka zir a, 1991 atanga 1996 thleng hian Pu K.Lalzawmliana, Forester, chutih hun laia Tuipuibari post ami chenna, Dawrpui ah khawsain B.Sc-III yr. thleng Pachhunga University College, Aizawl ah ka zir a, lehkha hi zir chhunzawm tawh lovin 1996 atanga 1999 chhung hian kan thiante nen Zampui Hills, Tripura atanga thingzai te phur chhuakin Aizawl ah kan hralh thin a ni.

 

            Kum 2000 kum hian TIDAL Company hnuaiah Medical Representative in ka inhlawh ve zawk a, sales a thatloh avangin lawmman/hlawh a tha hleithei lova, company lam pawh an in withdraw tak avangin ka thawk chhunzawm ta lo ani. Kum 2001-2003 chhung hian hnathawh bik em em pawh nei lovin Pu Zawma of Dawrpui hnenah ka awm a, 2003-2008 chhung hian kan khua Tuipuibari ah haw thla lehin lo te neiin ka khawsa a ni.

 

            February 2009 atang hian Vanlalfela of Tuirum sawmna angin Dima Halam Daogah(DHD) cadre zawm turin Harangajao, NC Hills ah ka chhukthla a, Vanlalfela hi chuan 2006 atang khan DHD cadre hi alo zawm tawh a, physical training te zawm ve nghalin thla thum kan training a, hetih chhung hian Joot Drill deuh chauh kan training a, weapons handling leh arms firing kan nei ngai lova, DHD leh Indian Army te hian Suspension of Operation an neih mek avangin weapons training leh firing lo neih hi a remchanglo niin a lang. DHD cadre hi mi 1000 vel kan ni a, instructor leh a kaihruaitu zawng zawng pawh Dimasa hnam vek an ni a, kan Chairman pawh Dilip Nunisa a ni.

 

            September 2009 khan Rajmoni Meska, South Tripura lam mi chuan tawng a thiam tawk loh avangin ZRA camp, Songtal, Manipur ah hruai turin min ngen a, a hmun kan han thlen chuan Bru zing ami ve bawk Thomas, Max leh Jora te an lo awm tawh sa a, haw lehna mai tura pawisa ka neihloh avangin thla khat leh a chanve zet ka awm a, hetih chhung hian Joot Drill te pawh ka training ve nghal bawk. Rajmoni leh Thomas hi chu a hma pawha an lo kal tawh avangin an tlangnel sa hle a ni. Thla khat leh a chanve ka awm hnu chuan Thomas an Rs.3000/- kal man senso atan min pe a, DHD Camp, Harangajao ka pan leh ta a ni.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

PAWL DIN CHUNGCHANG:

 

            Thomas hian nikum 2010 Christmas kha DHD camp, Harangajao ah a rawn hmang a, Vanlalfela'n alo mikhual a, hemi tum hian khawlaia kan titi ho naah pawl din tha kan tih thu leh pawl hming turte kan rawt khawm a, chung zingah chuan United Liberation Army(ULA), People Federal Front(PFF), Bru Federal Army(BFA) leh United Liberation Front of Bru(ULFB) tihte kan rawt chhuak a, amaherawhchu thutlukna engmah siam lovin Thomas hian midang rawn zau deuh a, thutlukna chu ama kuta awmtir a duh a, tun hnua ka hriat leh danin Peoples Federal Front(PFF) tih hi an lo thlang ta niin ka hria. Vanlalfela leh kei hi chuan DHD cadres kan nih anga kan rank neihlai kan chhawm zel mai turah kan ngai a, Vanlalfela ai hian Thomas hi ranking/designation ah hniam zawk mahse thuneitu sang zawk chu ala ni zui zel ta tho ani.
