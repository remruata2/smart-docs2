****ATR on recommendations of 27th Conference of DGsP/IGsP and Heads of CPOs held at Agartala (November 22-23, 2022)****

**III. Radicalization/Terrorist Activities in North Eastern States**

****a) " A Systematic approach by State Government to regularize and monitor Mosques/Madrassas and fix accountability of respective Madrassa Boards".****

- _There has been no report of any Madrassas run by the Muslim community in Mizoram. However, Mosque and Markaz (Tablighi Jamaat Centre) set up within the state have been regularly monitored and visited randomly. It may be mentioned the following:_

_I. The Aizawl Mosque located at Dawrpui is the main religious centre of the Muslim community in Aizawl. There are four other Mosques in Mizoram which are located at Lunglei, Siaha, Kolasib and Sairang, all are affiliated under Aizawl Mosque._

_Leaders of Aizawl Mosque Committee and Aizawl Muslim Welfare Society were briefed to work in close co-ordination with the law enforcement agencies concerning identification any suspicious elements that could have bearing on national as well as security of the state._

_II. A separate file dealing with Tablighi Jamaat has been opened and maintained. Dedicated officers and staff are entrusted to monitor the movement and activities of Tablighi Jamaat devotees. These personnel were meticulously trained and briefed to enable them to have an in-depth knowledge of Tablighi Jamaat movement and their philosophy. In close co-ordination with their Imam Md. Oliullah Barbhuiya (38) S/o Abdul Rehman, Khelma Part-VI, PS Katigora, Cachar and leader Prof. Dr. Jamal Hussain, HOD Mathematics, Mizoram University, visitors of Tablighi Jamaat devotees from other states are maintained and closely monitored._

_Recently, Tablighi Jamaat was shifted from Chanmari Markaz, which is the only Markaz from Lalchhunga Building, Chaltlang Road, Chanmari, Aizawl to Pu Vanlalhluna Building, H/No. 69, Chaltlang Road, Chanmari, Aizawl._

**V. Improving operational and intelligence coordination: MAC/SMAC**

****e) "All agencies and State Police to prepare data base of Major/Minor trans-border crimes, identify the conduits, their hideous etc. for sharing it on MAC/SMAC forums".****

-  _Intelligence pertaining to activities of insurgent groups and smuggling of arms and contraband items has been regularly shared via SMAC platform to concerned sister agencies for further necessary follow up action. Further, steps have been taken for preparation of data pertaining to trans-border crimes, sector officers of CID (SB) posted at various strategic locations having jurisdiction with international border are instructed to closely monitor trans-border crime and to prepare such data on weekly basis for further sharing it via MAC/SMAC platform._
