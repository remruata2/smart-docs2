****OFFICE OF THE SUPERINTENDENT OF POLICE****

****O R D E R****

****Information is received from Special Bureau, Govt. of India that a consignment of around 4-5 Rocket Propelled Grenade launchers (RPGL) is likely to be smuggled apparently by Chin National Army (CNF) cadres and delivered to members of United Progressive Democratic Front (UDPF) a Bangladesh insurgent group in Bangladesh via Mizoram by the end of October, 2016. The likely route to be used by the arms traffickers is Falam (Myanmar) - Farkawn (Champhai District, Mizoram) - Keitum (Serchhip District, Mizoram) - Aizawl. From Aizawl, the consignment is expected to be taken to any of the three places close to Indo - Bangladesh border in Mamit District Viz. Tuipuibari, Damchera or Phuldungsei.****
