A. Front page of  VANGLAINI  newspaper  Dt. 28.6.2022

Purchase of grapes for Champhai and Hnahlan Winery : Champhai Grape Growers Society (CGGS) and Hnahlan Grape Growers & Processing Cooperative Society (HGGPCS) are intending to buy grapes harvested by farmers to be processed in their winery.

          After obtaining No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the opposition political parties for the processing of wine, CGGS expected the state government  to grant them license for manufacturing of wine under the MLPC Act.

          According to CGGS, for the first phase, they decided to purchase grapes w.e.f 13th July 2022 at the rate of Rs.60 per kilogram. Hnahlan Grape Growers & Processing Cooperative Society will on the other hand intended to start purchasing of grapes from this week at the same rate as CGGS.

 

 

 

B. Front Page  of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper  Dt.  28.6.2022

Convocation of NIT Mizoram: The 5th Convocation of National Institute of Technology, Mizoram was held at Aijal Club yesterday. In this programme, various degrees were awarded to successful candidates of 2020-2021 academic session, 2016-2017 & 2017-2018 under graduate batch,2019-2020 post graduate and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D).

          Delivering a speech in this function, Pu Zoramthanga, Chief Minister congratulated all the successful candidates and appeal to them to utilised their knowledge for the benefit of the country and its people . He also expressed his expectation to students of NIT to utilise their technological expertise in transforming their campus as environment friendly "Green NIT" as an example for other states.
