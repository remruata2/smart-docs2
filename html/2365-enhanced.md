****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/210

Dated 23rd March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/204 Dt. 22.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

29 (twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-12, MMV-12, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 23.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

14 (fourteen) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-11, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 23.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 9 (nine) vehicles (MMV-9, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

73 (seventy three) vehicles (HMV-55, MMV-Nil, LMV-14, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 23.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

139 (one hundred thirty nine) vehicles (HMV-81, MMV-12, LMV-19, LPG-4, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-23, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 23.03.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
