b)       Rubotute min hrilh thei em?

 

Ans:  i)           Dt. 6.6.2011 khan United Democratic Liberation Front ten ABCI                   hnathawk mi pahnih te chu an camp Meidum, Kolasib District                        atangin an ru bo.

 

            ii)        Dt 25.3.2012 khan National Liberation Front of Tripura(NLFT) ten                          ABCI hnathawk mi paruk te chu an camp, New Sachan leh Mauzam                         inkar,Lunglei Distict atangin an ru bo.

 

          iii)        Dt. 25.11.2013 khan Teak-a sumdawng, Bengali mi pathum chu                    NLFT  leh  BNCT ( Borok National Council Of Tripura )-ten                       Tuipuibari - II (Rajivnagar), Mamit District atangin an ru bo.

            iv)       Dt.19.2.2013 zingah NLFT/WNDLFM (Western National                                Development Liberation Front of Mizoram) inti ten Forest Deptt.                    hnathawk mi pangate chu an camp Tuipuibari leh Damparengpui                     inkar Poaching Camp, Mamit Dist. atangin an ru bo.

 

c)       Rukbo chhan leh an thil phut enge ni?

 

Ans:  i)           Dt.6.6.2011-a ABCI hnathawk rubo tute hian an rukbote tlanchhuah                        nan  pawisa cheng vaibelchhe nga (Rs.5crores) an phut a.                             Amaherawhchu, Mizoram police operation team ten Dt. 16.6.2011               ah Assam ram chhung atangin rukbo te hi an chhanchhuak.

 

            ii)        Dt.25.3.2012 a ABCI hnathawk rubo tute hian tlancchuahna pawisa                         vaibelchhe khat leh maktaduai hnih (Rs. 1.2 crores) an phut.

 

            iii)       Dt.25.11.2012 a Bengali sumdawng pathum rubo tute hian tlanna                  pawisa cheng nuai sawmthum (Rs. 30 lakhs) an phut.

 

            iv)       Dt.19.2.2013 a Forest Deptt. hnathawk rubo tute hian tlanna pawisa                         cheng  vaibelchhe khat (Rs. 1 crore) an phut a.
