A. Front page of  VANGLAINI newspaper  Dt. 20.7.2022

Buying of hervested grapes for winery: In anticipation of receiving licence for the processing and selling of grape wine, Champhai Grape Growers Society (CGGS) and Hnahlan Grape Growers & Processing Cooperative Society (HGGPCS) have started buying harvested grapes for their winery. Commenting about this, leaders of HGGPCS expressed that they strongly believed that the state government will issued them licence for fermentation and selling of grape wine in the near future. So far, 827 quintals of grapes have been bought by HGGPCS and around 60 quintals by CGGS at the rate of Rs.60 per kilogram.

         

 

B. Front page of  VANGLAINI/ THE AIZAWL POST  newspapers 

Dt.20.7. 2022

Rally for environmental protection in Aizawl: Youth for Environment Justice, Mizoram (YEJM) organised a mass rally yesterday i.e.19.7.2022 in Aizawl. 'Development without damaging the environment' was the main theme of this rally. The rally, in which around 1,500 members participated, was started at 11:00 am from Vanapa Hall and proceeded towards the office of Principal Chief  Conservator of Forest (PCCF) at Tuikhuahtlang and the office of National highways Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) where shouting of slogans were also done.
