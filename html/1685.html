Sl No.50   Develop Skills  of the State Police in its role as first responders:

 

State Disaster Response Force (SDRF):

High wind/cyclonic storm always strike the State of Mizoram  by damaging dwelling houses and crops. Due to excess rainfall, landslides are very serious hazard for the State of Mizoram. Due to absence of proper regulation in land use and buildings in the past, Aizawl city and other towns in the State are more vulnerable to earthquake and other calamities. Roads are narrow and will be difficult to bring heavy equipments for rescue work. Keeping this in mind, Four Units of “State Disaster Response Force” (SDRF) was  established with a strength of 1(one) Company each in 4 Battalions of the Mizoram Armed Police and the Indian Reserved Police Company as given below. Its main duty is Search & Rescue, Evacuation works, Medical First Responder, etc. at the disaster site. The area of operation shall be as follows:

51. Formulate Standard guidelines for granting expeditious sanction for the visit of Investigating Officers abroad:

            The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI ), functioning under Deptt. of Personnel, Ministry of Personnel, Pension & Public Grievances, Government of India, is the premier investigating police agency in India. It is the nodal police agency in India which coordinates investigation on behalf of Interpol Member countries. Mizoram Police have been following the Comprehensive guidelines issued by Internal Security Division, Ministry of Home Affairs,  Government of India  on 31.12.2007 regarding investigation abroad,etc

 It is suggested that Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India may issued a new Comprehensive guidelines for granting expeditious sanction for the visit of Investigating Officer abroad.

 

52.Augment Intelligence apparatus, including State Special Branches, inculcate professionalism in the police force and separate law and order from investigation to improve prosecution rate.

Mizoram Police is having CID (Special Branch) which is the intelligence agency of the state  and  is playing an important role in the collection and dissemination of intelligence pertaining to the movement of insurgents, illegal trafficking of arms and other anti-social elements bearing security of the state on law and order problem. The Special Branch is equipped to cope up with growing menace of Cyber crime and provided with lawful interception equipments.

55 In view of the limitations of Sniffer dogs and technical equipments like metal detectors and explosive detectors, there is need to find newer devices which are capable of detecting mines buried more than 3-4 ft in the ground.

            Stressing the need for employing the latest technology to tackle IED menace, the Union Home Ministry has, in its action plan (2014) to fight Left-wing extremism, proposed mine detector AN/PSS 14, developed by US-based L3 Cyterra for the counter-Naxal grid.

It has recommended that CRPF's Institute of IED Management at Pune be asked to study the system and make suggestions. The AN/PSS 14 mine detector revolutionizes landmine detection by combining ground penetrating radar (GPR) and highly sensitive metal detector (MD) technology using advanced data fusion algorithms.

59. Specific strategies like creation of websites, etc to counter the adverse publicity on social media.

            The official websites of Mizoram Police i.e police.mizoram.gov.in has been regularly updated. Mizoram Police Newsletters has also been published and regularly uploaded on the website.

 

60.Make State Special Branches more effective ,so as to strengthen MAC functioning

The Subsidiary Multi Agency Centre (SMAC) is established in the office of CID ( Special Branch) Aizawl as one of the Centre amongst various Intelligence Agencies under Ministry of Home Affairs for effective means of sharing intelligence report between the state Agencies.

 

61. Move towards people friendly Police Station (PS) and a customer-service provider relationship.

 

            Mizoram Police Act-2011 had been enacted as a part of the initiative of Mizoram Police to ensure people friendly practices and a customer-service provider relationship in Mizoram.

62. Improve working conditions at Police Stations and basic amenities, etc

 

A lot of emphasis has been given on creating infrastructure in Mizoram police Department. New Police Station buildings equipped with modern amenities like TV, Computer, etc have been constructed in different part of Mizoram to improve working conditions and to provide better police services to people.

63. Well-equipped ‘work station’ for every Investigation Officer (IO)

 

             A  lot of emphasis has been given to provide workstation for Investigation Officer in all the newly constructed Police Station in Mizoram.

90.The Group should standardize training in inter-agency interface modules and synergy

95 &96. Assess usage of IVFRT data & State Police to speed up the installation of IVFRT Project        

Mizoram Police under the unit of SP CID (Special Branch) is having Foreigner Registration Office (FRO) at Aizawl. The FRO deals with registering of Foreigners entering into Mizoram and subsequent movement inside Mizoram. The FRO is also having a direct link with Foreigners Division, Ministry of Home Affairs, Delhi for “Immigration, Visa and Foreigners Registration & Tracking (IVFRT) project and  steps are being taken for full implementation of the  IVFRT project in Mizoram.
