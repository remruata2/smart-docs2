MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/416

Dated 1st June, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/411
Dt. 31.05.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

Dr. Lalrozama, DC Mamit and Pu Vineet Kumar, SP, Mamit visited Chuhvel village today i.e., 1.6.2021 @ 12:20 p.m. and held a short interaction with leaders of Chuhvel village and Taitesena Group YMA OB at the residence of Pu Zoramsanga, VC Vice President, Chuhvel. During the interaction, leaders of Chuhvel village informed the boundary of Chuhvel village to the District Administration Authorities, and DC, Mamit and SP, Mamit left Chuhvel @ 2:30 p.m. todays’s afternoon.

Further, Pu Andy Lalfakzuala, SDO(Civil), Kawrthah and
Pu Lalthanfala Khiangte, SDPO, Kawrthah along with Taitesena Group YMA OB visited Sakeilui paddy field/Betchhora zau today’s evening @ 3:15 p.m. and took stock of the situation. Situation is normal.

 

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-3, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 1.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

8 (eight) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-7, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 1.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :
