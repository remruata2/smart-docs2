Remarks

1

SI. Vanlalauva

DSB, Aizawl

16.5.1989

Sonai PS C/No. 81/89

u/s 147/148/149/326/302 IPC

The Mizoram Police party consisting of 1) SI R.Vanlalauva 2) HC Ngaihdama 3) HC Lalngaia 4) Const. Ralliana 5) Const. Rohnuna were ambushed by HPC volunteers led by Thangliankhuma at Bashertol near Moniarkhal Tea Estate, Cachar, Assam. During the encounter, SI R.Vanlalauva was killed at the spot while HC Ngaihdama and Const. Rohnuna sustained serious bullet injuries. Two HPC volunteers namely; Lalhuapliana, S/o Sawia of Sakawrdai and Lianhmingthanga S/o Phuta of Sartuinek were also killed at the spot.

 

2

C/467 Thangzela

1st Bn. MAP

17.7.1989

i) New Vervek PS C/No. 20/89

 u/s 448/365/341 IPC

ii) Parbung PS C/No. 78/89

u/s 302/34 IPC

On 15.7.1989@9:30pm, about 20 armed HPC volunteers raided the house of Mr.Khuma of Tinghmun village and abducted C/467 Thangzela of 1st Bn. MAP, Lalhmingthanga S/o Sapbiakthanga and F.Thanzama, President Cong(I) Unit Tinghmun with a view to kill them. However, Lalhmingthanga and F.Thanzama were released after severely assaulting them. Later on, C/467 Thangzela of 1st Bn. MAP was killed by HPC volunteers in Manipur.

1

C/87 Lalhriata

1st Bn. MAP

20.3.1991

Ngopa PS C/No. 7/91

u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25 (1B)(a) A. Act

On 20.3.1991, a Police party went for patrolling in the outskirt of Daido village and when they reached near Vawngkawt (Mizoram-Manipur border), 11 nos. of HPC extremist ambushed and sp fire on the Police party and shot dead one C/87 Lalhriata of 1st Bn. MAP. It is not known whether there is any casualty on HPC side.

 

2

C/681 Lalngaihthanga

1st Bn. MAP

15.5.1991

 

Ngopa PS C/No. 12/91

u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25 (1B)(a)/27 A. Act.

On 15.5.1991, patrolling party of 10(ten) Police personnel under the command of SI. Tlanglawma proceed towards Daido village and while passing Tlangnuam village about 1km. towards Daido, the Police party were ambushed by the HPC extremists, as a result C/681 Lalngaihthanga was shot dead at the spot.

3

Hav. SC Nath

Vairengte PS

28.9.1991

Vairengte PS C/No. 53/91

u/s 302/307/326/324/435/120B IPC r/w 3(2) TADA Act r/w 3 of ES Act.

On the morning of 28.9.1991@ 8:00am, explosion took place in MST bus (Silchar-Aizawl) at Vairengte. 4(four) persons including one Police personnel viz. Hav. SC Nath of Vairengte PS died at the spot and 25 persons sustained injury. It is suspected that HPC extremists have planted the explosive in the bus.

 

4

C/605 Rokima

1st Bn. MAP

18.12.1991

Ngopa PS C/No. 28/91

u/s 302/34 IPC r/w 25 (1B)(a)/27 A. Act

On receipt of the information about the movements of HPC volunteers in Khawkawn village, MAP patrolling party chased them towards Tuivai river, bordering Manipur on 18.12.1991. The HPC volunteers laid an ambush near Tuivai river towards Mizoram. During the encounter, one C/605 Rokima of 1st Bn. MAP was killed and two other HPC volunteers namely Lalkaltluanga, S/o Hrangvunga of Khawlian and Chawngneiha, S/o Aikhama, Khawlian were also killed in the encounter.
