****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/226

Dated: 1st April, 2019.

 Reference to our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2019/222, dated 30.3.2019. Information has been received that NGO Coordination Committee (NGOCC) meeting was held today, i.e. 1.4.2019 @ 11:00 a.m at CYMA Office which was presided over by Pu Vanlalruata, Chairman and attended by 20 (twenty) members from its organisations except representatives of Mizo Students’ Union (MSU) Gen. Hqrs. The meeting deliberated on their future course of action if authority takes any arrangement of Special Polling Booths for Bru voters in Tripura Transit Camps to cast their votes in the upcoming General Election of MP (Lok Sabha), 2019. The meeting resolved the following and issued a Press Release to this effect today itself :-

1. The NGOCC reiterated their demand of making Bru voters in Tripura Transit Camps to cast their votes only at their native villages where they are enrolled in the Electoral Roll and opposed the ongoing preparation of setting up of Special Polling Booths for Bru voters at Kanhmun by the Election officials and the Government. The meeting further resolved to organise State-wide indefinite Total Bandh w.e.f. 8.4.2019 to press the authorities for the implementation of their demand.
2. The NGOCC further requested commercial and private vehicles already engaged for transporting security personnel for the election duty not to continue their service and further requested the vehicle owners not to let their vehicles in connection with any election matters.
3. The NGOCC requested the people of Mizoram to support their intention to organise the Total Bandh.

 Consequently, Central YMA convened a meeting today @ 1:00 p.m at their office which was presided over by Pu Vanlalruata, President and attended by about 50 (fifty) members including CYMA OBs, leaders of Sub-Hqrs. and Groups which are under direct control of CYMA. The meeting expressed their support of the NGOCC’s decision of organising State-wide indefinite Total Bandh w.e.f. 8.4.2019 and further requested its Sub-Hqrs., Groups and Branches to coordinate with other NGOs at their respective places and chalk out their course of action. A Press Release to this effect was also issued today. Information received indicated that the following Sub-Hqrs. YMA leaders were absent in today’s meeting :-

1. Lunglei Sub-Hqrs. ii) Champhai Sub-Hqrs. iii) Mamit Sub-Hqrs.

 Information received further indicated that Pu Peter Chhakchhuak, Asst. Secretary, CYMA will proceed to Lawngtlai and Siaha tomorrow, i.e. 2.4.2019 to meet leaders of Central Young Lai Association (CYLA) and Mara Thyutlia Py (MTP) in connection with the proposed Total Bandh.
