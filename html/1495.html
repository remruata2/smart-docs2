Action taken report:

 

4.4   Agenda Item No. 4: Human Trafficking in the North East.

 

              Contents posted in various social media platforms are constantly monitored by staff of CID (SB) in order to detect any crime related contents such as sexual related offences, human trafficking, any other intelligence inputs etc.

 

 

4.5 Agenda Item No. 5 : Indo Myanmar Border Issues :-

 

  1. There has been no report of movement of International Insurgent Groups (IIGs) across Mizoram-Myanmar border since 2020. However, Sector officers of this Unit posted in Mizoram-Myanmar border areas have been informed to keep vigil for any movement of IIGs within their respective areas.

 

  1. Regarding informal trade across the Indo-Myanmar border, Sector officers of CID (SB) posted in Mizoram-Myanmar border have been sensitized and alerted.
