SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/666

Dated: 6th September, 2019.

 

 

 

            Joint Monitoring Group (JMG) meeting was held today, i.e. 6.9.2019 at New Delhi under the chairmanship of Pu A.P. Maheshwari, Spl. Secretary (Internal Security) and attended by about 10 (ten) representatives from Tripura Government, Mizoram Government, Central YMA, MBDPF besides Central Government. Information has been received that the meeting resolved to commence the repatriation process latest by 1.10.2019. Representatives from Bru NGOs reiterated about their various demands submitted to the Hon’ble Chief Minister and the Home Minister of Mizoram but Central Government regretted. Information received indicated that Pu Vanlalruata, President, Central YMA also highlighted about the previous attempts of the repatriations and the Mizo civil society’s welcoming of the Brus. He further stated that the Mizoram NGOs will not welcome any who do not want to be repatriated in the scheduled time.
