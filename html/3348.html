A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 24.9.2013

DGP(M) Farewell: A farewell parade was held at 3rd Bn. MAP ground, Mualpui yesterday by Mizoram Police to bid farewell to Mr. Alok Kumar Verma, IPS, Director General of Police, Mizoram who has been recently transferred to Delhi. A farewell dinner was also held for Mr. Alok Kumar Verma  by Police officers at Arya Mess.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 24.9.2013

Election Training: Police officers above the ranks of inspector under Aizawl District underwent election training programme at the Community room of Aizawl DC on 23rd September. K.Lalremsanga, Addl SP, Aizawl gave necessary training on duties, responsible, Do’s and Don’ts for Police officers during the height of political campaign in Mizoram.

 

C. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 24.9.2013

ACB Registered a Case: Regarding the misappropriation of ` 18,84,815/- professional tax of 2nd IR Bn. Headquarters, Khawzawl, Anti Corruption Bureau (ACB) registered a case recently. After probing into the FIR on misappropriation of professional tax, ACB found there was something wrong and registered a case. Ministerial staff Sub-Inspector retired R. Vanlaltana s/o R.Romawia (L) is the prime suspect in the case.
