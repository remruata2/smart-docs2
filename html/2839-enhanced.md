****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/235

Dated: 3rd April, 2019.

 Reference to our Special Report No. MSB/CB/SR/2019/207 dated 25.3.2019. Information has been received that today, i.e. 3.4.2019, Rulkual Land Owners’ Association has withdrawn their Stay Order which was imposed to the Contractors of the ongoing construction works of Kaladan Multi Modal Transit Transport Project (KMMTTP) at Lawngtlai District w.e.f. 22.3.2019, unconditionally. Further information received indicated that 2 (two) leaders of Rulkual Land Owners’ Association had been apprehended by Lawngtlai Police yesterday, i.e. 2.4.2019 and were released on PR Bond. Consequently, the Land Owners’ Association revoked their Stay Order today.
