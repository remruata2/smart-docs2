****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/671

Dated 24th November, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/668 Dt. 23.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report** **:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 11(eleven) vehicles (HMV-5, LMV-1, MMV-5) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via., Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 24.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 18 (eighteen) vehicles (MMV-18) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 7(seven) vehicle (Auto Rickshaw - 7) entered Saihapui ‘V’ from Assam today i.e., 24.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

 40(forty) vehicles (HMV-13, LMV-19, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 24.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.
