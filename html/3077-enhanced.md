A. _Middle page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 2.4.2013_

**_Heroin Seized_:****A joint team of Special Narcotic Staff PS, CID (Crime) &amp; Central Anti-Drug Squad (CADS) seized 24 grams of Heroin near Millenium Centre, Dawrpui, Aizawl worth ` 1.3 lakh in the local market rate. The alleged owners Lalramliana (34) of Tahan, Myanmar and Liannawn (37) of Kalei District, Myanmar were arrested.**

B. _Middle page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 2.4.2013_

_2261 Tourists in Mizoram_:****There were 2216 foreigners, who visited Mizoram in between 2010 to 13th March, 2013. Answering the question at Assembly Session, Pu R. Lalzirliana, Home Minister said that there were 712 foreigners/tourists in 2010, 657 in 2011, 744 in 2012 and 148 till 13th March in 2013. Each District Superintendent of Police except Aizawl District SP is District Foreigners Registration Officer.
