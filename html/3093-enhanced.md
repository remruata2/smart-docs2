A. _Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 26.4.2013_

_FIR lodged to ACB._

 Prism lodged an FIR to ACB yesterday as it feels there is misappropriation in the development works of Minor Irrigation Project. According to the FIR, an amount of ` 48,38,778-/ was sanctioned for construction of site camp in 12 places under Champhai Sub-Division Minor Irrigation Project. Out of these, site camp is built only in 5 places and 7 places still remain to be taken up. The expense of money did not match the quality of the work said Prism.

B. _Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 26.4.2013_

_Ford Figo seized :_

 Vairengte Police on duty in Saiphai seized Ford Figo bearing registration No.MZ-05/6632 on the night of 23rd April. The Car was driven by Ryon Ali Barbhruia Maharpur and owned by Jhumur Dutta of Silchar. Sources said, the car used VIP light and ran around in the city of Aizawl with suspicious passenger, the owner alleged its driver of stealing the car. The car and its driver were handed over to Aizawl Traffic Police.

C. _Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 26.4.2013_

_53 years old woman raped_ :

 Lalruatmawia (26) allegedly raped a widow of 53 yrs old at Chalrang village in Champhai District on Tuesday. The widow went to sow rice at a farmland a short distance from her village, Lalruatmawia grabbed the woman whicle she was sowing rice and raped her. The widow fought back so hard that she hurt her hips and could not even walk. The victim was taken to Khawzawl Hospital, the rapist is being searched by the Police.

_D. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper Dt. 26.4.2013_

_Contractors under Multi Modal Transport Project given stay order_ :

 Yesterday, Land Owners Association, Lawngtlai who have not received compensation gave stay order to the contractors - RDS and ARSS Company in their on-going construction of road-connecting India and Myanmar i.e. Multi Modal Transport Project.

 Company authority said that even if the Government told them to resume the work, they did not have the courage to continue. Due to the incident, Lunglei District DC ordered Magistrates on duty on different places and a number of MAP personnel were sent to Lawngtlai to prevent any law &amp; order problems.

_E. Front page of Vanglaini, The Zozam Times & Aizawl Post newspapers Dt. 26.4.2013_

_Intention to Boycott General Election :_

 Nausel Joint Action Committee made a notice saying if the road of Sihphir Venghlun to Nausel (northern suburb of Aizawl city) is not black topped before the upcoming Assembly Election at the end of this year, they will boycott the General Election.

 Nausel JAC said their village existed since 1967 and through it is short distance from Aizawl, their roads are in a dilapidate condition; they also said the Government has funds for the same.
