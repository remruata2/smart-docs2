****2.2.2022 :**  Ref. to DSI Part-IV Dt. 28.1.2022 All Mizoram Accredited Social Health Activist (ASHA) continued their strike and did not perform their duties in demand for payment of their pending incentives on 2.2.2022. The strike was started w.e.f. 1.2.2022 and information received stated that they intend to continue their strike until their incentives is paid by the State government.**

****28.1.2022 :** All Mizoram Accredited Social Health Activist (ASHA) in its Press Release issued on 28.1.2022 stated that 1091 (one thousand ninety one) ASHA intended not to perform their duty w.e.f. 1.2.2022 (Tuesday) in demand for payment of their long pending incentives. It also urged the state government to immediately pay all pending incentives. It further stated that they will not resume their duty before full payment of all pending incentives.**

****11.3.2022 :** NHM Employee Union Mizoram (NEUM) in its Press Release issued on 11.3.2022 stated that all NEUM members should wear black badge from 14-17.3.2022 as no favorable action has been taken on their various demands by the State government. It also stated that if no positive action is taken by the State government during the above mentioned period, they intend to avail Mass Casual leave from 21-23.3.2022.**

****14.3.2022:** Ref. to our DSI Part-VI Dt. 11.3.2022, members of NHM Employee Union Mizoram (NEUM) across the State wore black badge on 14.3.2022 in demand for immediate fulfilment of their standing demands by the State government. They intend to wear black badge till 17.3.2022.**

**15.12.2022 :** The 2nd General Conference of National Health Mission Employees Union Mizoram (NEUM) was held on 15.12.2022 @10:30 a.m., at Vanapa Hall, Aizawl. Dr. R. Lalthangliana, Hon'ble Minister, Health &amp; Family Welfare, etc. graced the Conference as Chief Guest. Pu Malsawmtluanga Hauhnar, President NEUM presided over the General Conference and about 306 (three hundred and six) members were present. The following agenda concerning Government were deliberated and passed:

 (i) To take initiative for the entitlement/claiming of New Pension Scheme for NHM Staff.

 (ii) To take initiative for the implementation of Pay Rationalisation in impartial and transparent manner.

 (iii) To initiate amendment of Recruitment Rules for regularisation of NHM staff.

 (iv) To take initiative for placing of NHM Fund under direct funding.
