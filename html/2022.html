INTERROGATION STATEMENT OF PROBESH CHAKMA

 

 

 

          I joined JSS in October 2002 after persuasion by Major. Asish Chakma that Bangladesh Gov't failed to honour the peace accord. Hence I joined the movement to pressurize the Bangladesh Gov't to fulfil her promises.

 

          I was arrested by BSF party at Chhotapansury on 18.7.09 @ 2:00 pm (near Borapansury India/Mizoram) who seized our arms and ammunition and others items on 19.7.09 we were handed over to police of Borapansury.
