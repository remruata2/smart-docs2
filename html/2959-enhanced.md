****SPECIAL REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/57

Dated: 25th January, 2019.

In continuation of our Special Report No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/54, Dt. 24.01.2019: A meeting between the Government and NGOCC was held today, i.e. 25.01.2019 @ 10:30 a.m at Secretariat Conference Hall which was chaired by Pu Zoramthanga, Hon’ble Chief Minister and attended by Pu Lalrinliana Sailo, Hon’ble Speaker; Pu Lalchamliana, Hon’ble Home Minister; Pu Lalruatkima, Hon’ble MOS, Rural Development; Pu Lalchhandama Ralte, Hon’ble MOS, School Education; Pu T.J. Lalnuntluanga, Hon’ble MOS Law &amp; Judicial Department; Pu Robert Romawia Royte, Hon’ble MOS, Sports Department; top officials from Civil and Police administration and 21 (twenty one) leaders of the NGOCC headed by Pu Vanlalruata, Chairman, NGOCC and President, CYMA.
The meeting had a comprehensive discussion on the intended boycott and picketing of Republic Day celebration, 2019 by NGOCC. After due deliberation and further closed-door consultation amongst themselves by NGOCC, the meeting resumed which decided that the State Republic Day celebration will go ahead as scheduled. However, the top level of Government, viz. Council of Ministers, Speaker &amp; Dy. Speaker, MsLA, MsP, Secretaries and above, Police Officers of the rank of SP and above, award recipients, DC, Aizawl and staff and SP, Aizawl and staff will attend the ceremonial parade. Armed contingents and band parties will participate in the ceremonial parade. The Hon’ble Governor of Mizoram, Pu Kummanam Rajasekharan will unfurl the National Flag at 8:00 a.m as previously scheduled. There will be no participation of un-armed and school contingents in this year’s ceremonial parade. At the District Headquarters, the National Flag will be unfurled by respective Deputy Commissioners without any ceremonial parade.

At the same time, the NGOCC will gather outside the three gates of A.R Lammual from 6:00 a.m onwards, but will not picket the above mentioned dignitaries and officials of the Government. All NGOCC, Mizoram issued a Notification to all branches in which they highlighted the meeting held today with Mizoram Government as a result of negotiation with MKHC and an agreement reached with the Government regarding the celebration of 70thRepublic Day celebration as follows :-

i) Celebration function at Aizawl will be attended by few Government officials. Civilians, students and others, not included in the agreement, will not attend the function.

ii) At District level, DC may unfurl the National Flag at DC Office courtyard but participation of others will not be permitted.

iii) Sub-Division/Block level are allowed to unfurl National Flag but not allowed to organise celebration programme.

iv) Celebration function at Central Institutions and Central Government offices is objected.
