A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 07.12.2022_

****Schools to be opened till 16 December**: According to the calendar of Mizoram School Education Department, schools are to remain open till 21st December 2022 and will be re-open from 5th January 2022. However, for conveniences, the timing are altered slightly by the meeting of the concerned department with its Minister to December 16, 2022 and 10 January 2023 respectively. In this connection, Pu Lalchhandama Ralte, Minister of School Education requested students to considered the school closing period as study leave and to prepare themselves properly as they are about to face examinations in the coming next year.**

**B**. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 07.12.2022_

****Home Department clarified how to register Non-Mizos:** In connection with the ongoing process undertaken by various Deputy Commissioner of different districts to conduct census for non Mizos staying in the state, Mizoram Home Department issued a clarification on how to register these non Mizos. In their statement, Home Department explained that this is not an ordinary census and are conducted as part of the amendment process of the Constitution Scheduled Tribe Order, 1950. Under the Constitution Scheduled Tribe Order, 1950 Part - XVII, all the Mizo tribes within Mizoram are not included while at the same time, other tribes outside Mizoram are also included, who are not supposed to be included. To amend these defects, Mizoram Home Department has been making lots of efforts for a long time and advised the public to understand it and not to panic.**

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 07.12.2022_

**Funds for salaries released :******Mizoram Government started to release funds for salaries of its employees from yesterday (6.12.2022). These funds, which are meant for the month of November, 2022 also included the pensions of retired government personnel. Previously, funds for provisional employee, Muster Roll etc. have been released along with salaries for regular government employees who are registered under IFMIS.
