A. Front page of VANGLAINI, THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.22.2.2013:

 

Two Myanmar nationals arrested at Lengpui Airport:  Yesterday, Lengpui Airport Security duty arrested two Myanmar nationals, viz. Malsawmdawnga, S/o Lalzuimawia of Tahan-B, and Vanlalnunsanga, S/o Vanlalhmangaiha of Tahan-G for using fake Identity Card (fake EPIC and fake Driving License) to board the plane from Aizawl to Delhi. They were apprehended at the Check-In Counter and handed over to Sairang Police Station.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt.22.2.2013:

 

Life convict escape from custody:  A 27 years old convicted in rape-murder case, escaped while being brought to Aizawl Civil Hospital with his hands cuffed yesterday. Zothanpuia who claimed to be a native of Zohmun village in north eastern Mizoram was accused of killing a 66 years old woman at Baktawng village, Aizawl district on 5th December 2011.

           

C. Second page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.22.2.2013:

D. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt.22.2.2013:

 

Prisoners strike:  Prisoners of Lunglei Dist. Jail organized a strike on 20th Feb. 2013, (Wednesday) night, they shouted in unison about their need for better water supply, and they also demand Court of Visitors Chairman, Lunglei DC to visit District Jail.

As Lunglei DC was out of station, Lunglei SDO (Sadar) visited District Jail on his behalf; he met with representatives of the prisoners, and promised them better water supply and that Lunglei DC will visit them when he returns to the station.
