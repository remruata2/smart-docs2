B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 17.4.2014

Land Verification: Regarding the report submitted by ACB to the Government on Mualkhang VC area where Bairabi to Sairang railway will be passed through, re-verification was done on 15&16.4.2014 by government Officials.

No fraud is found so far in the land verification done for 2 days, it will be continued on 21.4.2014, and land re-verification was conducted by Revenue Department officials, ACB and North Frontier Railway representatives. 149 people are to receive Compensation in Mualkhang area, out of which 49 were found to be needed re-verification by ACB.
