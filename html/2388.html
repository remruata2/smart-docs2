MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/409

Dated 28th May, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/402
Dt. 27.05.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

11 (eleven) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-10, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 28.5.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
