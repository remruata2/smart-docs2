A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt.27 & 28.1.2013

 

1.Police personnel rewarded on Republic Day : On the celebration of Republic Day at Aizawl, the Governor of Mizoram Vakkom Purushothaman gave away Governor's Gold medal to Inspr. Laltanpuia Sailo CID (SB), Police medal for meritorious service to Brajesh Kumar Singh S.P. Champhai, Inspr. K. Malsawma 4th I.R. Bn. and S.I. P. Vanlalruata CID(SB). At the same time, Indian Police medal was also awarded to O.N. Bhashkar, IPS Dy. Dir. SIB.

 

2.Republic Day Hockey tournament result: In a Hockey Tournament which was organized as part of Republic Day celebration, the 1st Bn. MAP emerges as the champion who were followed by the team of 1st I.R. Bn., Mualvum as the 1st runner up.   

 

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt.28.1.2013

 

25 Bru's gave themselves up in Zawlnuam police out post  : 25 Bru's of Kolalian village, who were responsible for ransacking the 'Betel-leaves' gardens of two Thinghmun villagers gave themselves up at Zawlnuam police out post. Previously, tension was running high between YMA group of these areas and Kolalian village. However, after this, the leaders of these YMA group expressed their appreciation and requested the enforcement agencies to track down any other culprits who are left out.
