I. KILLING OF IGP, DIG & SP CID (SB) ON 13.1.1975

 

          On the morning of 13th January 1975, three members of Mizo National Army(MNA) militant, clad in Police khaki uniform arrived at Mizoram Police Headquarters, which was at that time located in Treasury Square, Aizawl. The leader of this party, donning the rank insignia of Sub-Inspector of Police straight away entered the office chamber of the then IGP of Mizoram – GS. Arya, IPS. He then sprayed the room with automatic fire using his machine pistol, instantly killing the IGP alongwith two other senior IPS officers – LB.Sewa, DIG and K. Panchapagesan, SP CID(SB) who were also inside the room. The assailant there beat a hasty retreat using a jeep which was readily parked outside the office for such purpose.

 

            A state funeral was held for these three martyred IPS officers on the next day at Aizawl after which their bodies were airlifted using helicopter to their respective home state for cremation.

 

II. INCIDENT NEAR VAITIN VILLAGE ON 24.3.1999

 

                                    On 24.3.1999, about 20 (twenty) PREPAK insurgents with arms were seen at Vaitin Village under Sakawrdai Police Outpost jurisdiction, Aizawl District. On receipt of the information, Police party under the command of ASI Zoramsanga, incharge, Sakawrdai Police Outpost immediately rushed towards Vaitin Village. However, before reaching Vaitin Village, they were ambushed by the said insurgent group and the following Police personnel were killed in the encounter :-

 

                                    1. ASI Zoramsanga, i/c Sakawrdai Police Outpost.

            2. C/555 Lalramthara, 1st Bn. MAP

            3. C/264 Laltanpuia, 1st Bn. MAP

            4. C/121 Malsawmtluanga, 1st Bn. MAP

            5. C/360 Khualtawna, 1st Bn. MAP

 

            The insurgents snatched away 3 (three) AK-47 Rifles, 1 (one) 7.62 SLR, 1 (one) 9 mm Machine Carbine and 1 (one) .38 Revolver from the Police party and 1 (one) SBBL Gun belonging to a civilian.

III. INCIDENT IN BETWEEN BANDIASORA & DEVASORA VILLAGE ON 30.6.2000

 

                                    On 30.6.2000, BNLF militants ambushed Mizoram Police Operation party consisting of 19 (nineteen) personnel under the command of Hav. C.S. Lalrinzuala who were returning to Tlabung in a Medium Truck about 1 KM after leaving Bandiasora between Bandiasora and Devasora. The militants killed 7 (seven) Police personnel and injuring another 7 (seven) police personnel. No arms and ammunitions were lost by Police during the encounter and its aftermath. Mizoram Police personnel killed in the encounter were :-

 

                                    1. Hav/Drv Rokunga, 1st Bn. MAP.

            2. L/Nk K. Lalremmawia, 1st Bn. MAP.

            3. C/505 Lalnunmawia, 1st Bn. MAP.

            4. C/190 P.C. Vanlalchhuana, 1st Bn. MAP.

            5. C/180 T.C. Lala, 1st Bn. MAP.

            6.  L/Nk K. Lalchhuanawma 2nd MAP.

            7. C/ Thangduna Lunglei DEF.      

 

IV. INCIDENT NEAR SAIPUM VILLAGE ON 2.9.2008     

 

                                    On 2.9.2008, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram Pay Escort Party commanded by Inspr. Mohendra Rai and HPC(D) militant engaged in a fierce encounter when the later ambushed the Pay Escort Party proceeding towards Saipum village, Kolasib District, Mizoram. The Place of occurrence was about 3 (three) Kms. from Saipum village, Kolasib District, Mizoram. 4 (four) Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while 3 (three) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter. As per report received, the following Officer and men were killed at the ambush site:-

 

                                    1. SI R. Lalrindika, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

                                    2. C/808 B. Lalchunglura, 1st  IR Bn., Mizoram.

                                    3. C/366 David Rualthankhuma, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

                                    4. C/48 Lalremthanga, 1st IR Bn., Mizoram.

                  

V. INCIDENT AT THE OUTSKIRT OF ZOKHAWTHIANG/KANI VILLAGE ON 28.3.2015           

 

                                    On 28.3.2015, an entourage party of Committee on Government Assurance members such as Pu R.L. Pianmawia, Govt. Dy. Chief Whip, Pu Lalthanliana, MLA (Aizawl North – II) and Nl. Lalawmpuii Chawngthu, MLA (Hrangturzo) was ambushed by HPC(D) Insurgent group numbering around 12 (twelve) cadres at the outskirt of Zokhawthiang/Kani village. 3 (three)Mizoram Police personnel were shot dead by the HPC(D) outfit while 5 (five) persons sustained injuries due to the encounter which lasted for about 1 (one) hour and 45 (forty five) minutes. In this connection Sakawrdai Police Station Case No. 6/15 dt. 29.3.2015 u/s 302/307/396/397/120B/326 IPC r/w 25(1A)/27(3) Arms Act has been registered.

 

                                    The HPC(D) outfit targeted the pilot vehicle which went ahead of the other vehicles. 3 (three) Police personnel who lost their lives in this encounter are :-   

 

                                    1. S.I. Zoramthara Khawlhring, O/C, Sakawrdai PS

                                    2. DG-III Lalchuailova, Aizawl DEF

                                    3. C/673 Hmangaihmawia, 1st Bn MAP

           

                                    The HPC(D) militants also snatched away 2 (two) AK-47 with several rounds of ammunitions,  3 (three) 9 mm Browning Pistols, 2 (two) 0.38 Revolver and 1 (one) 9 mm Glock Pistol from the possession of Mizoram Police.
