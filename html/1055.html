                        This is to inform you that a meeting to review security of protected persons in Mizoram held on 23rd March,2020 had downsized the number of PSOs allotted to the Hon'ble Speaker of Mizoram, Pu Lalrinliana Sailoand Hon'ble Minister of State, Pu Lalchhandama Ralte.

 

                        However, both the P.S of the two dignitaries have submitted a request mentioning that since the Hon'ble Speaker and the Hon'ble Minister of State are both elected from constituencies falling within the Demand Areas (Chalfilh A/C & Tuivawl A/C respectively) they may be allow to retain their existing 3(three) PSOs.

 

                        In this regard, you are hereby directed to conduct assessment of threat perception of the said Hon'ble Speaker and Hon'ble Minister of State, Mizoram Legislative Assembly and submit your report at the earlier.
