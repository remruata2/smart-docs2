A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 02.09.2022_

**50th anniversary of Fire and Emergency Services** : Fire and Emergency Services (FES) on yesterday (1.9.2022) celebrated their Golden Jubilee at their Headquarters in Hunthar veng, Aizawl. Gracing the function as Chief Guest, Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister, Mizoram delivered a speech wherein he mentioned that Rs. 4,725 lakhs worth of projects have been submitted to Ministry of Home Affairs for upgrading FES of Mizoram. A souvenir prepared to marked this 50th anniversary was also released by the Home Minister.

 FES was established in the year 1972 with 183 personnel manning 14 Fire Stations within 9 districts of the State. Proposal has also been made for the establishment of Fire Stations in each of the new three districts. According to the records of FES, there were 129 burning incidents in the year 2019, 214 incidents in 2020, 318 in the year 2021 and 105 cases till July of 2022 in Mizoram.

B. _Second page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 02.09.2022_

**Arms seized** : Vaivakawn police on 30th August 2022 (Tuesday) seized three .22 pistols (country made) and another dismantled pistol with a magazine near Lalboi Filling Station, Hunthar veng, Aizawl. Two persons suspected to be the owners of these illegal arms - Ramdinthara (26) s/o H.Lalthlengliana, Dam veng and Lalremruati (26) d/o Tlangsawiruala, Tuikual South were also arrested.

C. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 02.09.2022_

****Violators of law through Youtube summoned by MSCPR :** Mizoram State Commission for Protection of Child Rights (MSCPCR) on yesterday (1.9.2022) summoned in their office two Youtubers who, in their Youtube channels, revealed the names and identities of children who were the victims of recently committed crimes against children. Authorities of MSCPRC reprimanded these youtube channels operators and instructed them not to commit the same mistake again while at the same time gave them awareness on Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 (JJ Act) Section 74 along with its punishments.**
