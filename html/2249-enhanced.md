****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/291

Dated 13th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/285 Dt. 12.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****39 (thirty nine) vehicles (HMV-9, MMV-23, LMV-7) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 13.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 3 (three) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-2, LMV-1) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

36 (thirty six) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-22, LMV-6) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 13.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 7 (seven) vehicles (MMV-6, LMV-1) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

67 (sixty seven) vehicles (HMV-43, MMV-2, LMV-16, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 13.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

146 (one hundred forty six) vehicles (HMV-56, MMV-11, LMV-27, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-11, Oil Tanker-37, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 13.04.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
