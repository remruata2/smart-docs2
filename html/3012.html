SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/750

Dated: 3rd October, 2019.

 

            The 9th & Final Phase of the repatriation process of Brus living in Tripura Relief Camps commenced today, i.e. 3.10.2019. All necessary civil and security arrangements were provided by Tripura and Mizoram Governments. Besides the officials from Mizoram, officials from Tripura including District Magistrate, Tripura North District; Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Kanchanpur Sub-Division; SDPO, Kanchanpur Sub-Division were stationed at the Relief Camps today. There was no report of any untoward incident.

According to the movement plan, Mamit District officials will continue repatriation of inmates from Kaiskau Camp tomorrow, i.e. 4.10.2019.

 

The repatriated families from Lunglei District will halt tonight at Mamit. 6 (six) families repatriated from Mamit District safely reached Borai village this evening which is the village selected for their resettlement. Movement of the repatriated people was done under proper Police escort.

 

Officials from Kolasib District deployed to receive the repatriated persons withdrew themselves today @ 2:30 p.m since no person from the targeted camps turned out for repatriation.

 

Information has been received that Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples Forum (MBDPF) and Mizoram Bru Indigenous Democratic Movement (MBIDM) intended to organize public meeting tonight, i.e. 3.10.2019 @ 6:00 p.m at Hazacherra and Naisingpara Camps. Pu A. Sawibunga, President of MBDPF will address the gathering at Hazacherra and Pu G.B. Herbert, President, MBIDM and Pu Phillip Apeto, Gen. Secretary, MBIDM will address the public at Naisingpara Camp. Leaders are expected to apprise the people about the non-implementation of their various demands by the Government.

 

            Situation is under close watch.
