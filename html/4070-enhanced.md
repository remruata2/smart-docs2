_CNF_

 After the disbanding of the locally elected government of Chin state by Myanmar military Junta after their military coup of 2021and the subsequent conflict between the military and the volunteer forces, there has been no proper civil administrations within Chin state ever since. To meet these short comings,17 various factions of the civil defence forces came together and formed Chinland Joint Defense Committee (CJDC). After three rounds of meeting at camp Victoria, the camp of CNF, to decide future interim government of Chinland, CJDF decided to organise a conference comprising of representatives of all the 17 various civil defence forces of the region to prepare a Constitutional Draft.

 In pursuance of CJDC decision, 'Future Chinland Constitutional Planning Workshop' was held during 7th -8th September 2023 at Central YMA Hall, Aizawl, Mizoram. The programme, which was presided over by Mr. Zing Cung, CNF Chairman, was attended by representatives of various 16 factions, created a Constitutional Drafting Committee comprising of 31 members. The Drafting Committee with Mr.Ramkul Cung ,CNF Assistant General Secretary, Mr.Tial Ling, MP and Salai Paul, CDF Paletwa as its Convener consisted of 5 members from CNF,9 Members of Parliament, and 1 representatives each from CDF/PDF.

 The Final Draft of the Constitution, drafted by the Constitution Drafting Committee was approved by the Working Committee for Chinland Council at a conference held at Camp Victoria between 27.11.2023 to 01.12.2023.  The Working Committee for Chinland Council conference also decided to hold another conference at Camp Victoria between 30.01.2024 to 03.02.2024 to chalk out the formation of Chinland Government. The New Chinland Government will be headed by a Chief Minister who will be assited by 14 Ministers. As per scheduled, the Working Committee for Chinland Council held its conference starting from 30.01.2023 at Camp Victoria and was expected to be attended by 110 members.
