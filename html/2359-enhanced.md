****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/182

Dated 15th March, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/177 Dt. 12.03.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

33 (thirty three) vehicles (HMV-13, MMV-12, LMV-8) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 15.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

44 (forty four) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-27, LMV-9) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 15.03.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 4 (four) vehicles (MMV-4, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
