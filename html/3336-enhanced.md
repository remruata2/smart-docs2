A. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 6.9.2013_

**Son of Lianengi apprehended for interrogation****:** C.Lalramzauva, the son of the murdered Lianengi whom she stayed within their house was apprehended by the Police for interrogation to probe into the murder of three family members on Tuesday morning. On the night of the murder C.Lalramzauva slept somewhere else. He was apprehended by Lunglei Police for interrogation. No new information has been gathered in the murder case, but C.Lalramzauva will be in the Police custody for 72 hours for further interrogation as allowed by the Magistrate.

B. _Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 6.9.2013_

**Houses unlocked at Mualkhang****:** Kolasib District Magistrate ordered residential houses locked in Nazareth Veng of Mualkhang by JAC, Mualkhang to be opened yesterday. The Police opened these residential houses as ordered. A discussion was held with JAC Mualkhang and the decision of the government was unanimously agreed.
