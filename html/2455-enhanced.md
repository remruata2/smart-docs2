****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2020/712

Dated 13th December, 2020

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/711 Dt. 12.12.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

 No vehicles entered Mizoram from Assam via Meidili and Damcherra Bridges today i.e., 13.12.2020.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

57 (fifty seven) vehicles (HMV-28, MMV – 6, LMV – 18, Maxi Cab- 5) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 13.12.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

 176 (one hundred and seventy six) vehicles (HMV – 70, MMV – 53, LMV –29, LPG – 18, Maxi Cab – 2, Oil Tanker – 3, Bus - 1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 13.12.2020 between 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
