ACHIEVEMENT OF CID(SB) WEF 16.8.2018 – till date (ie, 18.6.2019)

 

 

21.8.2018 :    On the evening of dt. 21.08.2018, acting upon specific information gathered through technical surveillance pertaining to illicit drugs consignment, CID (SB) operation team intercepted suspected illicit drugs traffickers at Bawngkawn, Aizawl near Greenwood Hospital. After attaining legal formalities, the suspected persons viz. Lalruatfela (28) s/o Zaneihhranga of Tuichirh, Falam District, Myanmar and Lalrinkimi (23) d/o Lalrokhuma of Vengthlang, Champhai were searched alongwith their belongings and motor- cycle B/R No. MZ-02 B/0887 used for transportation. As a result, 17 nos. of soap cases, hidden inside a separate compartment (meant for concealing/ smuggling) of the motor-cycle gas tank and 3 nos. of soap cases, hidden among laphetauh (edible nuts) kept inside backpack, which was a total of 20 soap cases, each containing suspected Heroin, weighing a total of 307.1 grams and value of Rs. 10 lakhs (local market rate) were seized from the joint possession of the accused persons, in the presence of independent civilian witnesses and woman constable. The accused persons alongwith the seized articles were handed over to O/C, Special Narcotic PS, who then registered Special Narcotic PS Case No. 18/18 Dt. 21.08.2018 u/s 21 (c)/29/25 ND & PS Act.
