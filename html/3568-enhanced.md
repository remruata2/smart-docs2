A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 7.7.2022_

**NLFT President apprehended by Mizoram police and Assam Rifles:**  A combine team of Mizoram Police and Assam Rifles apprehended Jacob Hrangkhawl (55), Tripura, President of National Liberation of Tripura (NLFT) at Vaivakawn, Aizawl yesterday. No arms and ammunitions were seized from him.

 Jacob Hrangchal is presently being detained at Vaivakawn Police Station for further interrogation. However, since he has not committed any crimes inside Mizoram, he is expected to be handed over to Tripura Police without booking any cases against him by Mizoram Police.

****Union Minister visited Mamit District:******Shri V.Muraleedharan, Union Minister of State for External Affairs and Parliamentary Affairs visited Mamit District yesterday and had a discussion on various projects undertaken under CSS with District Government officials, community leaders and leaders of NGOs.
