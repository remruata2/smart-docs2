A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 5.7.2022_

****Selling of Wine for Religious Rituals Prohibited - END :** Excise &amp; Narcotics Department (END), Mizoram stated that processing of wine even for Religious Rituals is prohibited as per The Mizoram Liquor Prohibition (MLP) Act, and at the same time, there has been no intention of strict enforcement of the said act in connection with use of wine for sacrament by some denominations. The END officials further stated that the State Government had not issued any exemption order to any individuals for manufacturing of wine and any act of violation of the law will be dealt as per the law.**

B. _Second Page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5. 7. 2022_

**56 piglets culled in Pang village:******Due to the outbreak of****African Swine Fever (ASF), 56 (fifty six) piglets were culled in Pang village, Lawngtlai District yesterday i.e., 4.7.2022. A team led by Veterinary Doctor of Sangau town, Dr. Lalrinkimi and OC, Sangau Police Station, Vanlalhlimpuia supervised the stated culling of 56 piglets, all of them were having symptoms of ASF. Samuel Zodinsanga, SDO(Civil) Sangau had earlier issued an order on 29 June 2022 which prohibit import of piglets and its allied products from outside Sangau town and its adjacent villages - Pang, Thaltlang, Sentetfiang and Cheural villages as a precautionary measures due outbreak of ASF.

**Inspection of Serchhip Court by High Court Judge :** Justice Michael Zothankhuma, Judge Gauhati High Court Aizawl Bench and Portfolio &amp; Station Judge conducted an inspection of Serchhip District Court on Monday. He was accompanied by Pu Lalbiakzama Aizawl District &amp; Session Judge and Pi Gracy L. Bawihtlung, Joint Registrar, Gauhati High Court Aizawl Bench. The inspecting team had an interaction with Serchhip District Court officials, Police Department officers and officials from other concerned departments.
