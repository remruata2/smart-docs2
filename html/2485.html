MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/661

Dated 19th November, 2020

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/657
Dt. 18.11.2020, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

          12 (twelve) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-9) carrying essential commodities entered Mizoram via Medili Bridge, Assam-Mizoram border today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. and 16(sixteen) vehicles transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

          Mizoram Police and Assam Police today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. inspected Pu Vanlalrova, Member Bairabi North VC farm hut, which is under construction at his paddy field around 2(two) kms towards north from Zophai BOP. The inspection was conducted as per instruction of SP, Kolasib and Hailakandi. Assam Police told Pu Vanlalrova to halt construction of his farm hut temporarily citing they received various complaints regarding his construction. After inspection, Pu Vanlalrova was allowed to continue his construction work. Upon hearing this, and reportedly unhappy with the presence of Assam Police in the area, around 10(ten) Bairabi youths assembled at Zophai Check gate at 4:00 p.m. and they dispersed after apprising the matter at 4:30 p.m. under peaceful atmosphere.

         

          45(forty five) vehicles (HMV-17, MMV-1, LMV-27) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 4:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

          Today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 11:00 a.m. 5(five) leaders of MZP Sub-Headquarters Vairengte led by Pu Lalropuia, President, MZP Sub-Headquarters Vairengte visited Aitlang and its surrounding areas due to the visit of the said area by Smt. Keerthi Jalli, DC, Cachar on 17.11.2020. They served a notice to all non-Mizo paddy field caretakers in the area to possess ILP within this year and stated that they will not be held responsible, if anything happen to them due to non-possession of ILP. There are 7(seven) Mizo paddy fields beyond Aitlang Bridge (Dholai Bridge) towards Assam.

         

          329(three hundred twenty nine) vehicles (HMV-130, MMV-101, LMV-38, Maxi Cab-5, LPG-16, Oil-39) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 19.11.2020 @ 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

 

          Situation is under close watch.
