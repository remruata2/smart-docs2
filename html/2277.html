MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/67

Dated 1st February, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/63
Dt. 29.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :

20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV – 6, MMV – 13, LMV - 1) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 1.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2) transited their good at Medili Bridge.

21 (twenty one) vehicles (HMV – 6, MMV – 13, LMV - 2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 1.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 6 (six) vehicles (MMV –6) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

            99 (ninety nine) vehicles (HMV- 63, MMV – 2, LMV –30, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 1.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

On 29.1.2021 Assam Forest officials visited the on-going construction work of ponds by residents of Mizoram at Aitlang Sihpuizau, Assam-Mizoram border and left the area without any communication. As per report, officials of Assam Forest Department intended to visit the area today, i.e., 1.2.2021, but no officials from Assam forest has turn up at the area today. Owners of paddy fields at the area stated that Assam Forest officials might visit the area due to the complaint lodged against them to Assam officials by one labourer of Assam engaged at the area, who was sacked recently due to his bad performance.

134 (one hundred thirty four) vehicles (HMV – 78, MMV – 6, LMV –12, LPG – 3, Maxi Cab – 5, Oil Tanker – 30, Bus - Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 1.02.2021during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

           

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

            No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

           

            Situation is under close watch.

.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police
