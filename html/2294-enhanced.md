****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/126

Dated 23rd February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/124 Dt. 22.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

37 (thirty seven) vehicles (HMV-16, MMV-18, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 23.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

10 (ten) vehicles (HMV-8, MMV-19, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today i.e., 23.02.2021 @ 3:30pm. 3 (three) vehicles (MMV-3, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.
1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

74 (seventy four) vehicles (HMV-54, MMV-Nil, LMV-17, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-3) entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 23.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

184 (one hundred eighty four) vehicles (HMV-70, MMV-42, LMV-37, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-12, Oil Tanker-19, Bus-1) entered Vairengte from Assam today i.e., 23.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
