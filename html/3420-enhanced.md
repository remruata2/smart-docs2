A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 22.1.2014_

**Zokhawthar Police Seized Geletine****:** In the evening of Friday at around 4:00pm, Zokhawthar Police seized 200 sticks of geletine, 10 coils of fuse, 4,000 mtrs. of cortex and nitrogen from the possession of Zosangpuia (24) s/o Tharlawmsiama and Raldosanga (37) s/o Lalrothanga of Zokhawmawi, Myanmar.

These two men were on the verge of passing through to Myanmar with their bomb beside Tiau River Bridge between Mizoram and Myanmar; they said they were paid to bring these weapons to Myanmar. Both men were brought to court yesterday and are now in Champhai District Jail.

 _All the local dailies highlighted the release of the two Mizo hostages with photographs in their front pages._
