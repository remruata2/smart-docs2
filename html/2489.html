  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :

            Saihapui ‘V’ Branch MHIP held General Body Meeting last evening i.e.,         Dt. 22.10.2020 @ 7:00pm at Pi Lalthansangi’s residence. The meeting was chaired by Pi Lalzirpuii, President MHIP Saihapui ‘V’ and attended by 20 (twenty) members. During the meeting, a discussion was made regarding blockade of approach road to their cultivation site by Assam Police personnel in the aftermath of border dispute between Mizoram and Assam and resolved that the matter be brought to the notice of Deputy Commissioner, Kolasib through the SDO (Civil) Vairengte with a request to solve their problems immediately.

            Information has been received that a bomb blast occurred at SSA Lower Primary School last evening i.e., 22.10.2020 @ 10:15 p.m and the blast was believed to have been caused by using gelatin dynamite. The said school is run by Assam Government and is located at around 300 meters from Saihapui ‘V’ Village and around 400 meters from Mizoram Police Camp at Assam-Mizoram border towards Mizoram. The said blast completely destroyed 2(two) main doors of the school building and 6 (six) windows, blown away GCI roof and made a hole (around 2 (two) feet in diameters) at the southern corner of the wall. In this regard, Pu Vanlalfaka Ralte, SP, Kolasib immediately rushed to the spot and deployed security personnel to cordon the place of occurrence.

            In this connection, SI C. Lalhrezuala of Kolasib DEF conducted an enquiry and basing on his finding, Vairengte PS C/No-31/20 Dt. 23.10.2020 u/s 436/448 IPC r/w u/s 3(1) of The Prevention of Damage to Public Property Act, 1984 was registered and investigation is underway to detect the unknown perpetrator(s) of the bomb blast.        Pu Vanlalzawna, VCP Saihapui 'V' has also submitted a separate FIR in this connection.

            Today i.e., 23.10.2020 @ 2:00 p.m. Shri. Gaurav Kumar Agarwal, Addl. SP, Cachar District, Assam and party visited Assam-Mizoram border with an intention to visit PO of the said incident but Mizoram Police personnel on duty told them not to visit the PO considering the current prevailing situation at the area and they returned to Assam without visiting the PO and without any objection.

            4 (four) leaders of Presbyterian Church of India (Mizoram Synod) paid a visit to Saihapui V today i.e., 23.10.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. The members include Rev. Lalramliana Pachuau and Rev. Dr. H. Vanlalauva and they handed over Rs. 5000/- to VLTF Saihapui V.

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

            Vairengte Joint NGO meeting was held last evening i.e., dt. 22.10.2020 @ 8:30 p.m. at Forest Inspection Bungalow, Vairengte. The meeting was presided over by       Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte, MLA and attended by representatives of all NGOs and also by members of MZP General Headquarters. The meeting reviewed the prevailing situation on the border disputes between Mizoram and Assam. After careful deliberation, the meeting resolved that Mizoram Police Duty Post should be constructed on                   dt. 23.10.2020 at a particular hillock beyond Vairengte Auto Rickshaw Stand (approximately 60 mtrs towards Lailapur, Assam, and approximately 10 mtrs away from the main road on the eastern side) where levelling work has already been done. However, it was planned that the construction works is to be carried out covertly by selected members representing each of the NGOs’ besides MZP and MSU in order to avoid public attention.

            A community work under the aegis of Joint VC, Vairengte was organized today i.e., 23.20.2020 to construct IR Camp near Vairengte Auto Stand, Assam-Mizoram border and around 30 (thirty) members participated. As per report, members of MZP who are available at Vairengte (20 (twenty) from Aizawl, 7 (seven) from Kolasib and   8 (eight) from Bilkhawthlir) extended their support to Joint VC, Vairengte and All NGOs Vairengte initiative for construction of IR Camp near Vairengte Auto Stand, Mizoram-Assam border. The said camp was constructed 16ft x 10ft length with bamboo thatched walls and Silpaulin roof. Pu K. Vanlalvena, MP Rajya Sabha and     Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte, MLA visited the area at 6:40 p.m. Construction work was completed at 6:50 p.m. this evening and dispersed peacefully afterwards. Some leaders of NGOs who participated at the said community work stated that the said camp will be occupied by Mizoram Police deployed at Vairengte but there is no official confirmation regarding occupation of the said camp by Mizoram Police. It has been reported that Assam Police are conducting surveillance of the said construction work. There is free vehicular movement on either side.

Situation is under close watch.

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
