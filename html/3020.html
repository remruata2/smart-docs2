SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/771

Dated: 12th October, 2019.

 

Information has been received that yesterday, i.e. 11.10.2019 at around 3:30 p.m, 2 (two) non-Mizo persons namely, i) Debu Pal (60) s/o Debendra Pal of Singua Fultali Village, PS-Ratabari, Karimganj District, Assam and ii) Doya Moy Nath @ Joy (35) s/o Hemanta Nath of Lala Village, Hailakandi, Assam were produced at Bairabi Police Station by some civilians from Suarhliap village on suspicious grounds.

Information received indicated that the two arrested persons, Debu Pal and Doya Moy Nath came to Saikhawthlir, Mamit District on 11.10.2019 forenoon and some of the non-Mizo residents of Saikhawthlir (who were employed in the paddy fields in Suarhliap area, Mamit District by Mizo residents of Saikhawthlir village) accused them of being child abductors and apprehended them. Upon hearing the incident, Pu H. Daniala, one of the land owners and some of the non-Mizo paddy field workers brought the 2 (two) accused persons to 4th IR Bn. Camp at Saikhawthlir BOP. Consequently, they were produced before Bairabi Police Check Gate after which they were taken to Bairabi Police Station yesterday @ 3:30 p.m.

Further information received indicated that the two persons met each other at Bairabi Railway platform on the night of 10.10.2019. On 11.10.2019 early in the morning, the two of them proceeded towards Saikhawthlir village, Mamit District in search of menial work. The two apprehended persons do not possess any valid Inner Line Permit (ILP). They had passed Bairabi Police Check Gate by travelling in the forested area on foot. On checking their belongings, no weapon, instrument or any other types of article which could suggest that they are intending to kidnap children were found. Some cigarette butts, empty cigarette packets, lighter, worn sheets of paper, torn clothes, etc. were the only items found in their possession.

Input received indicated that one of the apprehended persons, i.e. Debu Pal is having mental problem and used to beg and that he is a vagabond in and around his village. His prior involvement in any kind of criminal activity is not known. The other person, Doya Moy Nath used to stay at Saikhawthlir village in the past two years. He was working under ABCI Company and as a teak transporter in Bairabi side as well. He is known to be a habitual criminal and often cheated others. It may not be ruled out that he was accused to be child kidnapper by some of the people (non-Mizos) he had cheated in the past in Saikhawthlir area. The two apprehended persons suffered some minor external injuries at the hands of their captors. They were brought to Kolasib District Hospital for necessary medical check-up last night and were medically examined after which they were brought back to Bairabi PS on the night itself.

Bairabi PS Case No.3/19, Dt.12.1.2019 u/s 3 of EBFR Act was registered since their involvement in any other crime is not found except for violation of ILP.
