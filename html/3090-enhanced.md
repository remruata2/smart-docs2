A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 19.4.2013_

_One kidnapper arrested_: A joint team of Mizoram police and Tripura police arrested one Lochiram Reang (38) s/o Norgojoy Reang of Naisingpara Relief Camp who was alleged to be among the kidnappers of 5 workers at Dampa Tiger Reserve forest in Mamit District on 18.2.2013 from old Chikha village, and sent to Central Jail yesterday ie, 18.4.2013.

 Lochiram Reang (38) s/o Norgojoy Reang is from Naisingpara Relief Camp in North Tripura, he was arrested on Tuesday ie, 16.4.2013 @ 4:00PM at Asapara Relief Camp by a joint team of Kanhmun Police and Tripura police, he was brought to Mamit Police Station on Wednesday ie, 17.4.2013 and sent to Central jail yesterday ie, 18.4.2013.

 Lochiram Reang said that the kidnappers who kidnapped 5 workers from old Chikha village were Bru gang under NLFT with 14 members, out of which 12 were involved in the kidnapping. **(W.PLG. PS C/No. 4/13 dt. 20.2.2013 u/s 364(A)/34 IPC r/w 25 (1A) Arms Act).**

B. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, VANGLAINI & THE ZOZAM TIMES newspapers Dt. 19.4.2013_

_Arms seized_: On 17.4.2013 @ 4:00PM, a joint team of West Phaileng police and BSF posted at Phuldungsei seized one .22 pistol (made in USA) with its empty magazine and 1 magazine of SLR; 3 ammunitions of .22; 2 ammunitions of SLR; 6 ammunitions of AK-47; 21 ammunitions of 5.56 INSAS Rifle and 40 ammunitions of 9mm at 9Kms. away from Phuldungsei to Pukzing and also arrested the alleged owner, Lalbiakhluna (27) s/o Lalrothanga of Bethlehem Veng, West Phaileng while his accomplice Lalramhluna fled.
