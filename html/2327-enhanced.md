****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/571

Dated 27th July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/564 Dt. 26.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit; Pu Shashank Jaiswal, SP Mamit; Pu KL Vulmawia, Commandant, 4th IR and other concern officials visited Thinghlun BOP today i.e., 27.7.2021 @ 12:40 p.m. to take stock of the situation.
Bunker was constructed at Thinghlun border today by engaging JCB, the JCB was sponsored by Pu Lalrindika, constituency MLA. Around 100 (one hundred) residents of Thinghlun led by Pu Vanneihchhunga, VCP Thinghlun participated in the said work.

Reinforcement of additional 1 (one) platoon of 4th IR Battalion was made today at Thinghlun. At the same time, Assam also made reinforcement of 2 (two) platoon of Assam Armed Police at their side.

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit; Pu Shashank Jaiswal, SP Mamit; Pu KL Vulmawia, Commandant, 4th IR and other concern officials visited Medili border today i.e., 27.7.2021 @ 11:30 a.m. to take stock of the situation.
Information received stated that Assam Police carried out blockade of road at Assam side w.e.f., today @ 12:00 noon and Mizoram bound vehicles were directed to return back. The blockade is still continuing at the time of writing the report.

7 (seven) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-5) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 Mizoram Police deployed at Buarchep paddy field were shifted at hillock near Phainuam LP School yesterday evening due to its strategic location. Mizoram Police makeshift camps are being constructed at the area today. 

 Pu Lalruatkima, Minister; Lalchhandama Ralte, Minister and Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte visited Saihapui V and Buarchep this morning @ 7:30 a.m. and took stock of the situation. They observed that there was shortage of force at Saihapui ‘V’ and informed the matter to Home Minister, and accordingly, reinforcement of 2 (two) platoon of IR were made at the area.
1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

3 (three) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-1, LMV-1, Maxi Cab-Nil) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Pu Lalchamliana, Home Minister; Pu Robert Romawia Royte, Sport Minister; Pu K. Lalrinliana, Supply Minister; Pu Lalrinsanga Ralte, MLA; Er. Lalzirliana, MLA and Pu John Neihlaia, IGP (Hqrs) visited IR Camp at Vairengte Auto Stand today i.e., 27.7.2021 @ 12:15 p.m. The Home Minister encouraged Mizoram Police deployed at the area and held a brief meeting with Mr. Sarbitt Singh, IGP, CRPF. During the meeting, Pu John Neihlaia, IGP (Hqrs) highlighted his surprise regarding crossing of CRPF duty post at the border by around 200 (two hundred) Assam Police yesterday and expressed his desire that such incident do not occur again in future.
There was no entry of vehicle from Assam at Vairengte today. Situation at the inter-state border is tense but under control.
