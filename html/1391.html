Demands of Joint NGOs of  Brus as contained in their memorandum submitted to The Union Home Minister on 15.12. 2007

 

  1. Immediate release of cash dole of Rs.5/- per day per adult and Rs.2.5/- per day per minor, withheld since July,2017 notwithstanding any requirement as to opening of bank accounts, and continue to pay the same until further orders.

 

  1. Provide regular ration i.e. 600 gms per head per day for adult member and 300 gms rice per head per day for minor member at the 7 relief camps in Tripura without any delay whatsoever to avoid starvation.

 

  1. Enhance the assistance to Brus at relief camps as shown by the Union of India in the Affidavit dated 25.2.2015 in ‘Akhil Bhartiya Vanvasi Kalyan ashram V. State of Mizoram & Ors. WP(C) 1/2015, as under:

 

  1. double the cash dole to Rs.10/- from of Rs.5/- per adult per day, and Rs.5/- from Rs.2.5/- per day per minor.
  2. distribute clothes twice as instead of one:
  3. double of cash for clothes given on Durga Puja to Rs.1264/- from Rs.632.22/- per adult per year, and to Rs.930/- from Rs.465.85/- per minor per year.
  4. provide once every 6 months instead of once every 3 years.
  5. provide bathing soap once every 3 months per family in place of once every year per family.
  6. construct dwelling houses for each family at 7 Relief Camps in Tripura.
  7. provide Drinking water, educational facility, health care,etc. at 7 Relief Camps in Tripura.

 

  1. Immediately provide all the above assistance, including cash dole, to the Brus who had migrated to Tripura post 2009 violence in Mizoram.

 

  1. Allow the MBDPF leaders to participate in all the meeting conducted by Union of India, State of Tripura and State of Mizoram on the matter of repatriation of Brus from Tripura to Mizoram and rehabilitation in Mizoram after repatriation.
