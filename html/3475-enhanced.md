A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 11.3.2014_

**Meth Seized****:**DSB of Aizawl District Police seized 41,000 tablets of Methamphetamine on Sunday at around 4:45pm from the rented house of Lalngaizuala (25) s/o Ngaihtama at Dinthar Veng, Aizawl. The alleged owner Liana (38) s/o Chindothanga of Tahan 'A' Group and Lalngaizuala (25) s/o Ngaihtama of Tahan 'E' Group were also arrested. The seized Meth is worth ` 61,50,000/- in local market rate.

B. _Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 11.3.2014_

****MNYF Guard EVM****:**The meeting of Mizo National Youth Front (MNYF) with its President L.Thangmawia at Hnam Run yesterday announced that they object to an attempt to send EVM used in Mizoram Assembly General Election to Arunachal Pradesh; and they started guarding EVM kept at DC Office Strong Room from last night. MNYF announced that they object to move EVM made by ECIL until and unless the issue is settled by the court. **
