****REPORT ON SEIZURE OF****EXPLOSIVES****

No. MSB/CB/A-20/2022/53

Dated: 22nd January, 2022

Information has been received that on 20.1.2022 @ around 11:30 p.m. huge cache of explosives and other related illegal items were recovered by Assam Rifles at the outskirt of Zawngling village, Siaha District from 1 (one) vehicle (Tripper/909), B/R/No. MZ-07 7936, driven by MC Lallungrualpuia (41) s/o MC Laldingliana, Lawngtlai-III, with Lalthlengliana (30) s/o Nunnema, Lawngtlai-I, as the handleman of the said vehicle. On being informed about the recovery, O/C Tipa PS and party immediately rushed to the spot for necessary legal actions. The owner of the explosives, who allegedly hired the vehicle and boarded the vehicle, was reported to be Sangkima (29) s/o Lalsanga, Lawngkai, Paletwa Dist., Myanmar and the following items were seized by the Police from the said vehicle:

i) Belox 83 mm Dia x 2.08 kgs (Total : 97 boxes, i.e., 1164 numbers of   explosives).
ii) Indra ED, Aluminium detonator – 20 bundles, i.e., 1000 numbers of   detonators.
iii ) Detonating Fuse (Codex) – 12 bundles, i.e., 4500 metres.

iv) Rs.7,3500/- Indian Currency Notes of Rs.500 denomination.

v) 9,35,500 Kyats of Myanmar Currency (5000 kyat x 184 = 9,20,000; 1000   kyat x 12 = 12000; 500 kyat x 7 = 3500)
In this regard, a Case vide No.1/2022, dated 21.1.2022, u/s 5 of Explosive Substances Act, r/w 14A Foreigners Act was registered at Tipa Police Station on the strength of an FIR submitted by NB Sub Khusraj Shrestha, ‘F’ Coy, 17 AR, and the above 3 persons were arrested and they were produced at Siaha Police Station on the same day @7:20 p.m.

It is believed that the seized explosives items were intended to supply to Chin Defence Force (CDF), Mindat, Myanmar, and expected that further investigation will reveal the intended final destination.

After conducting thorough interrogation, the arrested accused persons viz., Sangkima (29), s/o Lalsanga, Lawngkai, Paletwa Dist., Myanmar and Lalthlengliana (30), s/o Nunnema, Lawngtlai-I were remanded into judicial custody on 21.1.2022 and the other arrested person named MC Lallungrualpuia (41), s/o MC Laldingliana, Lawngtlai-III is under Police Remand for further interrogation.

Investigation of the case is being underway and the case investigation is taken up by SI David H. Lallianhluna, OC, Tipa PS.
