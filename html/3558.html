17.2.2022 :    Mizoram Civil Pensioners Association General Headquarters in its Statement issued on 17.2.2022 informed all their members to wear black badge during
21-25.2.2022 in support of the Federation of Mizoram Government Employees & Workers (FMGE&W) ongoing demand for increase of Dearness Allowance (DA),
timely payment of monthly salary and pension benefits.

25.4.2022 :    Mizoram Civil Pensioners' Association (MCPA) held its 46th General Assembly cum Election (for the term 2022-2024) on 25.4.2022 @ 11:00 a.m. at Synod Conference Centre, Aizawl. Pu K. Lalthansanga, President, MCPA presided the conference and 180 (one hundred eighty) members attended. During the conference, various agendas concerning their association were discussed, which was followed by Election for MCPA OB 2022-2024 and Pu Tlanglianruma of Chaltlang Lily veng was elected as President, MCPA.

29.6.2022      Mizo Civil Pensioners' Association (MCPA) in its Press Release issued on  28.6.2022 expressed their support of the black badge agitation resorted to by members of Federation of Mizoram Government Employees & Workers (FMGE&W) in demand of hike in rate of DA to 34 percent. In this regard, as a way of showing their support, around 15,000 members of MCPA wore black badge w.e.f., 28.6.2022 at their respective places.
