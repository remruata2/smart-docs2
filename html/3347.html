A. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 23.9.2013

DGP(M) transfer: Union Home Ministry has recently transferred Mr. Alok Kumar Verma, IPS, Director General of Police, Mizoram to New Delhi. He served in the State for about one and a half year.

 

B. Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 23.9.2013

District Total Bandh: Khawzawl District Implementation Demand Committee and Hnahthiah District Function Demand Committee intend to organise 12 hour total bandh in their respective villages on 24th September, 2013 to urge the government to function Hnahthial and Khawzawl as Districts, which the Government announced in 2008.

 

C. Inside page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 23.9.2013

Solomon Lalremruata died in the hands of assailant: Solomon Lalremruata (30) s/o K.Thanruala of Khatla, Aizawl who resided at Lungpuizawl near Lunglei died in the hands of an assailant. According to reports, Solomon Lalremruata was in a live-in relationship with Roneihthangi (55) of Lungpuizawl who lives with her son Lalrintluanga (23). On Friday night ie, 20.9.2013, Solomon and Roneihthangi had a row and she left the house leaving Solomon and her son together, it seemed that the two had a fight afterwards, Solomon was found suffering from serious injuries by his neighbours in the morning of Saturday ie, 21.9.2013 @ 2:00AM and took him to Lunglei Civil Hospital, and he died at 12:55PM the same day. Lalrintluanga, the assailant was arrested by the Police, he admitted to the crime but as he is mentally challenged he could not be interrogated properly.
