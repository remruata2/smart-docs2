A. _Front page of THE AIZAWL POST, THE ZOZAM TIMES & VANGLAINI newspapers Dt. 14.8.2013_

****Intention to boycott I-Day**: Tlabung area Village Council Association intends to boycott Independence Day celebration if their demand for posting SDO(C) and Supply Inspector at Tlabung is not fulfilled by the State Government before Independence Day.**

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 14.8.2013_

**Houses dismantled****:** 8 houses occupied by 17 families had been dismantled at Ramhlun Sports Complex due to the land sinking. 2 houses were dismantled yesterday and 5 more houses will have to be dismantled said Local Council leaders of Ramhlun Sports Complex. 16 houses were dismantled last year due to the land sinking at this area.
