SPECIAL REPORT

 

No.MSB/CB/SR/2019/193

Dated: 15th March, 2019.

 

 

 

            Information received indicated that Mizoram Bru Displaced Peoples’ Forum (MBDPF) submitted a Memorandum dated 14.3.2019 to Shri. Rajiv Gauba, Union Home Secretary, Govt. of India today, i.e. 15.3.2019 through the Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Kanchanpur, Tripura North. The Memorandum appealed for resumption of relief rations, cash dole and other relief facilities which will be discontinued w.e.f. 31.3.2019 vide MHA Letter No.F.7/32/97-NE-II dated 11.1.2019 and continue the same till the displaced Brus are fully repatriated to Mizoram.

            The Memorandum further requested the official to approach the Election Commission of India to set up Polling Booths inside relief camps for Bru refugees to enable all voters to exercise their universal adult franchise in the upcoming General Election of MP (Lok Sabha), 2019.
