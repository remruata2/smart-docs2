A. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.3.2014_

**K.Chhawnthuama Appeared before Court; Next Court Date – 14th March****:**Pu K.Chhawnthuama (Mizo Phantom) appeared before court yesterday in connection with the text message he sent to Chief Minister of Mizoram. But Chief Judicial Magistrate post was newly taken up by Sylvie Z. Ralte, Pu K.Chhawnthuama and his lawyer waited for Chief Judicial Magistrate till 1:30pm. After Pu K.Chhawnthuama signed his name, they went home. APP told them that the next court date is 14th March.

 APP said that the case IO, Addl.SP CID (Crime) requested for more time which was agreed by the court and so 14.3.2014 is fixed for the next court date. 

B. _Front page of THE ZOZAM TIMES newspaper Dt. 5.3.2014_

**Magistrate’s Car Blocked; Offenders Arrested****:**The Maruti-800 car parked by VCP K.Lalmawia’s son and his friend blocked the car of BT Lala, Magistrate of Champhai near Champhai Vengthar’s Church on Sunday night at around 8:00pm and they had an argument. The two offenders were arrested by Champhai Police and charged were made under sections 334,506 &amp; 341 IPC.

C. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 5.3.2014_

**Laltanpuia Succumbed to his Injury****:**Laltanpuia s/o K.Hranglinga of Lawngtlai Vengpui succumbed to his injuries on the night of Monday at Civil Hospital, Aizawl. Laltanpuia was assaulted by Laltanpuia and Lalfakzuala both from Lawngtlai Bazar Veng on 28.2.2014. His body was sent for post-mortem yesterday and the body was brought home before midnight. His eyes were donated to Civil Hospital Eye Bank as he wished.
