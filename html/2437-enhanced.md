****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/454

Dated 14th June, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/452 Dt. 12.06.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

Ref. to our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/452 Dt. 12.06.2021 the mass community work for verification of inter-state boundary intended to be held today by Chuhvel Joint NGOs was kept in abeyance as per the request of DC, Mamit.

5 (five) leaders of MZP Headquarters, Zawlnuam led by Pu Lalthlanawma Jongte visited inter-state border near Chuhvel and Saikhawthlir villages today i.e., 14.6.2021 @ 11:00 a.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****23 (twenty three) vehicles (HMV-6, MMV-12, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 14.6.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

31 (thirty one) vehicles (HMV-5, MMV-24, LMV-2) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

52 (fifty two) vehicles (HMV-25, MMV-1, LMV-18, Maxi Cab-8) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

135 (one hundred thirty five) vehicles (HMV-42, MMV-33, LMV-29, LPG-6, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-21, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
