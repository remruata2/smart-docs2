A. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 17.10.2012

 

The corpse of Lalnunsiama was found: The corpse of Lalnunsiama, Driver, Maxi Cab who was swept down and killed in a landslide on 11.10.2012 at Aizawl to Mamit road alongwith JCB and two persons was found yesterday at Tut river. It was found by a man from Phaizau, 18kms. away from the end of Tut river at a spot called 'Samuela's Crossing'. The corpse was found in a bad condition, it was not identified at the first sight but was later identified that it was Lalnunsiama's body. Meanwhile the body of the JCB operator Mujib Uddin S/o Basail, Karimganj is still not found.

 

The same topic is also highlighted by other daily local newspaper viz., The Aizawl Post, The Zozam Times, Tawrhbawm.

 

 

 

 

B. Front page of Vanglaini newspaper. Dt. 17.10.2012

 

Election to 15 Village Councils under SHDC area may be discussed in Cabinet Meeting: Pu R.Lalzirliana, Home Minister said that as no nomination was filed for the VC election in 15 villages till the last date, the situation might have to be brought before the Cabinet Meeting.

 

As no nomination was filed for the VC election to be held in 15 villages under SHDC, the State Election Commissioner C.Ropianga said that the Govt. is to decide what to do next. The Home Minister said that he has not yet officially received intimation about non filling of nomination for the VC election. As such he has not much to say.

 

Home Minister said that the authority would take necessary action regarding VC election. He also said that as no one filed nomination, these 15 villages might have to be looked after by Circle Assistant. Meanwhile State Election Commission informed Govt. that VC election in 15 villages under SHDC was cancelled as no one filed nomination.

 

 

 

 

C. Front page of The Zozam Times newspaper. Dt. 17.10.2012

 

On 15.10.2012, Lalrammawia of Keifang Mel Veng slashed/chopped his elder brother Lalremmawia who lives in Rawpuichhip. Lalremmawia is now being hospitalised at Aizawl Civil Hospital.

 

Lalrammawia visited his elder brother at Rawpuichhip on his way home from West Phaileng, his elder brother Lalremmawia and his wife were staying in their jhum hut at Rawpuichhip. The siblings quarreled while having dinner, then Lalrammawia chopped his brother Lalremmawia, he cut off his brother's left knee-cap and also cut his right leg just below the knee, the bone of his shoulder also was broken and also hit him with the butt of a gun. Lalrammawia also tried to harass his brother's wife but she escaped. Lalrammawia is now in the custody of Sairang PS.
