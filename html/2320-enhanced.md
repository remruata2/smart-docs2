1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

Pu K. Lalrinliana, Supply Minister visited Chuhvel, Mamit District today i.e., 8.7.2021 @ 8:00 a.m. and attended a meeting held with leaders of Chuhvel village at Chuhvel Community Hall. The meeting was presided by Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit District. The meeting was attended by Addl. SP, Mamit, SDPO, Kawrthah and leaders of various Chuhvel NGOs. During the meeting, the minister stated that necessary steps are being taken by the state government regarding inter-state border issues, and appealed leaders of Chuhvel village to cooperate the state government in this regard. Chuhvel VC and Joint NGO also submitted a written petition to the Minister, wherein they stated the following:
1. To set up CRPF Camp at Hnanglui (disputed area with Assam).
2. To improve road connecting Hnanglui (around 1.7 km from Chuhvel to Bairabi road).
3. To provide electricity at Hnanglui and to construct bridge at Hnanglui river.
4. To set up Food &amp; Civil Supply Sub-Godown at Chuhvel village.

Further, Chuhvel VC and Joint NGO also submitted a written petition to Chief Minister, which requested creation of Police Outpost at Chuhvel village due to its location.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**
