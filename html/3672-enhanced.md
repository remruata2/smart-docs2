A. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 06.09.2022_

**Aizawl Municipal Corporation requested the public to follow Rules and Regulations** : The Enforcement Committee under Aizawl Municipal Corporation (AMC) held a Press Conference at AMC Auditorium yesterday (5.9.2022) wherein they requested the public to abide by the Building Regulations and other Rules under the AMC. Speaking in this press conference, Chairman of the Enforcement Committee, Pu John Zosangliana expressed that they have received 15 suggestions so far through the AMC Suggestion/Complaint box which was installed on 13th June 2022. 13 out of the 15 suggestions which were related to building constructions were approved by the Municipal council while the remaining 2 suggestions were left for further examinations. In every building constructions, drawing engineer will supervised the construction till the end and failure to comply with the drawing of engineer by the owner of the building may result in issuing of stay order by the concerned Local Council, the Chairman added.

B. _Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 06.09.2022_

**Closing Function of Camping at 2nd Bn. MAP :** Closing function for the Detoxification &amp; Rehabilitation Camp organised at the headquarters of 2nd Battalion MAP, Luangmual, Lunglei was held on yesterday (5.9.2022). The function, which was presided over by Dy. Commandant Pu K.Beihmotaosa was graced by Pu Lallianmawia , DIG (Southern Range) who delivered a motivational speech. All together, 44 police personnel attended this Detoxification &amp; Rehabilitation camping.

C. _Front page of The Aizawl Post newspaper Dt. 06.09.2022_

**Mizoram Legislative Assembly Session:** The 10th Session of 8th Mizoram Legislative Assembly will commenced from today i.e 6.9.2022 and will last till 8.9.2022. In this session, there will be two Bills -The Mizoram Goods and Services Tax (Amendment) Bill 2022 and The Mizoram Land Revenue (Amendmend) Bill 2022 for discussion while there are 188 Starred Questions, 104 Unstarred Questions, 6 Reports and 11 Papers to be laid on the table of the Assembly House.
