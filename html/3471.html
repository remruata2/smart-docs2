A. Inside page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 4.3.2014

Scuffle broke out between Lawngtlai Police & People: People of Lawngtlai Vengpui gathered at Lawngtlai District Jail on 2.3.2014 at 11:00pm and intended to see Laltanpuia and Lalfakzuala of Lawngtlai Bazar veng who reportedly assaulted Laltanpuia s/o K.Hranglinga on 28.2.2014. Laltanpuia (the victim) was on his way home from Lawngtlai Hospital when these two lads attacked and left him unconcious.

            Due to the incident, Lawngtlai people went to Lawngtlai District Jail,broke open the jail gate and entered into the jail compound. After a lot of scuffle, the police had to open fire more than 70 times in the air to disperse the crowd.

            The crowd dispersed only after Lawngtlai DC, SP, Central YLA leaders, VC of Lawngtlai vengpui and YLA had a talk with Laltanpuia’s family who requested the crowd to back down. Government will take stern action against the assailants of Laltanpuia.

            Laltanpuia is being treated at ICU in Civil Hospital, Aizawl and he remains unconcious till now, said his family.  

           

 

B. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 4.3.2014

Heroin seized: Special Narcotic Police Station Staff under CID(Crime) seized 17 grams of Heroin near Millenium Centre, Aizawl yesterday, the alleged owners – Vanlalrawna (37) s/o Salkunga, who claimed himself to be from Dawrpui locality and Lalthansiama (29) s/o Maliana, who claimed himself to be from Zemabawk locality were also arrested. The seized heroin is worth ` 60,000/- in local market rate.
