****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/273

Dated 8th April, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/269 Dt. 07.04.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under :-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

****5 (five) vehicles (HMV-2, MMV-2, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today i.e., 8.04.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 1 (one) vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-1, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.
