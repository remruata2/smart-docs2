****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/94

Dated 8th February, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/92 Dt. 5.02.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun,Mamit District Report :**

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV - 6, MMV - 9, LMV - 7) entered Mizoram from Assam via Medili Bridge today, i.e., 8.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and 2 (two) vehicles (MMV-2) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

20 (twenty) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-14, LMV-3) entered Mizoram from Tripura via Damcherra bridge today, i.e., 8.02.2021 @ 3:30 pm. 11 (eleven) vehicles (MMV-11, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

77 (seventy seven) vehicles (HMV-39, MMV-1, LMV-33, LPG-Nil, Maxi Cab-4) entered Bairabi from Assam today, i.e., 8.02.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

141 (one hundred forty one) vehicles (HMV-84, MMV-8, LMV-20, LPG-5, Maxi Cab-5, Oil Tanker-19, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today, i.e., 8.02.2021 during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
