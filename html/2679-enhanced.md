****REPORT ON RECENT INCIDENT ALONG INDO-MYANMAR BORDER****

 Information have been received that on 12.1.2018 @ around 2:00 pm at Myanmar-India border near Sabualtlang, Myanmar Army personnel shot Monoto (63) S/o Pataiha of Sabualtlang, Lawngtlai District. The shooting incident took place within Myanmar territory. The victim, Monoto along with his wife had gone to the forest to gather wild vegetables (Anchiri) and was mistaken to be a member of militant outfit by the Myanmar Army. The victim was evacuated by Myanmar Army personnel and was handed over to 1st Assam Rifles camped at Tuithumhnar, Lawngtlai District. He was shifted to District Hospital, Lawngtlai today, i.e., 13.1.2018 morning at around 9:00 am. The victim sustained snapped bones injury below right knee caused by bullet projectile and another wound at his left thigh. The Myanmar Army personnel are reportedly expediting around 15 rounds of firearm ammunitions.
