A. Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 30.8.2013

FIR will not be registered: Regarding distribution of compensation for the land used in the construction of Barabi to Sairang railway track, the Police will not register a case for the FIR lodged by Land Revenue and Settlement Department as ACB is carrying out an inquiry on the case.

 

B. Front page of THE AIZAWL POST newspaper Dt. 30.8.2013

31 families evacuated: 31 families living in 26 buildings in Champhai Vengthlang IB Veng in Champhai were evacuated till date due to a large earth crack that endangered their houses. The houses will be dismantled from today by Champhai vengthlang community.

            The officials of DM&R Department, H.Rochungnunga, Secy., Lalpeksanga, Director, and Lalsangluaia Dy. Director will visit this place and will reach Champhai today. Champhai DC, Lalthangpuia Sailo, Chairman, Champhai District Level Disaster Committee and his team recently visited the earth cracked place.
