B ._Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 25& 26.09.2022_

****5,05,000 tablets seized:** A joint operational team of Mizoram Police and Assam Rifles on 23.9.2022 recovered 5,05,000 tablets of suspected Methamphetamine (55.80 Kgs.) at Melbuk village, Champhai District which is the highest record for Meth seizure in the state in one incident. The total seized items are estimated to be worth around Rs.167.86 crores in the international market. The suspected owner of the contraband, one female person was also apprehended and later handed over to Zokhawthar Police Station on 24.9.2022 where a case was registered.**

C ._Front page of VANGLAINI newspaper Dt. 25& 26.09.2022_

**Refugee camps visited :**Mizoram Home Secretary, Pu H.Lalengmawia on 23.9.2022 visited Champhai and Zokhawthar and have an interaction with government officials. The Home Secretary inspected the problems and plights of Myanmar refugees of the areas and instructed the concerned government officials to find ways to provide electricity to the refugee camps located on the banks of Tiau river and Lipuichhuah. Presently, there are around 1,000 Myanmar refugees in Zokhawthar village.
