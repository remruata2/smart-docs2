****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/557

Dated 19th July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/555 Dt. 16.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

 Information received stated that Assam government intended to upgrade/develop Assam Police Camp at Zaite, Kamrimganj District, Assam and in this regard, DC, Karimganj, Assam Police and Forest officials conducted spot verification today i.e., 19.7.2021. Information received also stated that Assam intended to replace Assam Police deployed at the area by Central Armed Police (Border Security Force).

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

****

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

Pi Lalbiaksangi, Home Secretary visited Mizoram Police deployed at Medili Bridge, Kanhmun today i.e., 19.7.2021 @ 2:25 p.m. and took stock of the situation. The Home Secretary was accompanied by Addl SP, Mamit and Dy CO, 4th IR Battalion and left the area @ 3:00 p.m.

33 (thirty three) vehicles (HMV-10, MMV-22, LMV-1) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 19.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV-3, MMV-9, LMV-4) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

 No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

27 (twenty seven) vehicles (HMV-15, MMV-1, LMV-5, Maxi Cab-6) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.
