****SPECIAL REPORT****

No. MSB/CB/SR/2021/587

Dated: 2nd August, 2021

**1.** Information received stated that road blockade was organized by civilians of Assam at Tripura-Assam border at Loirpowa, Karimganj district, Assam today i.e., 2.8.2021 w.e.f., 8:00 a.m. due to apprehension that essential commodities are being transported to Mizoram from Tripura. However, the blockade was lifted this afternoon @ 1:30 p.m. after holding local level meeting which reportedly resolved that the road blockade will be lifted with the condition that any vehicles carrying essential commodities will not be allowed to enter into Mizoram from Tripura.

 Information received also stated that civilians from Assam near Assam-Tripura border threatened to organize blockade of vehicles carrying essential commodities to Tripura from Assam via Choraibari if Tripura Government did not block vehicles carrying goods and essential commodities from entering into Mizoram via Damcherra Bridge, Kanhmun, Tripura-Mizoram border.

**2.** Assam Police continued construction of bunkers near Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border, on Assam side, adjacent to Assam Forest Department building. Road blockade by Assam Police is still continuing at Assam side of Medili Bridge.
