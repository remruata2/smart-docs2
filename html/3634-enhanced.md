****16.11.2022 :** Pu Lalrinenga Sailo, AMC Mayor held a Press Conference on 16.11.2022 @ 2:00 p.m. at Aizawl Press Club, Treasury Square with regard to hike of Property Tax by AMC. 15 (fifteen) media persons attended the Press Conference. Pu Lalrinenga Sailo briefed media persons on the alleged bogus report on the hike of property tax in Aizawl. He stated that out of the 4500 building surveyed by AMC, 60% are levied tax @ Rs.500 or below, 12% are levied @ Rs.500-Rs.1000, 22% are levied @ Rs.1000-Rs.5000 and only 6% are levied @ Rs.5000 or more. He said the property tax levied by AMC is lesser compare to other cities. He also said that Property Tax has been increased as per the recommendation of the 15th Finance Commission.**
