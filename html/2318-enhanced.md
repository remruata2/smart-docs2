****MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT****

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/521

Dated 5th July, 2021

 In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/504 Dt. 02.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-
1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:**

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :**

16 (sixteen) vehicles (HMV-Nil, MMV-11, LMV-5) entered Mizoram from Assam via., Medili Bridge today, i.e., 5.7.2021 @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (HMV-Nil, MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Medili Bridge.

22 (twenty two) vehicles (HMV-1, MMV-21, LMV-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District :**

Information received stated 5 (five) Assam Police and Forest Department officials performed patrolling at Buarchep upto Upper Phainuam LP, School (where bomb blast occurred on 22.10.2020) yesterday i.e., Dt. 4.7.2021. Assam officials informed the locals near the area that the purpose of their visit is to conduct survey as Assam government intended to construct road upto the said school. Situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :**

39 (thirty nine) vehicles (HMV-13, MMV-2, LMV-19, Maxi Cab-5) entered Bairabi from Assam today @ 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :**

****No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :**

Information received stated that Assam side started construction of motorable road in between Balichhora, Assam to Aitlang lui Hnar (Pu Ramhluna broom field) by engaging excavator (JCB) yesterday, and they constructed the road upto an area, around 100 meters away from Assam duty post.

138 (one hundred thirty eight) vehicles (HMV-57, MMV-30, LMV-28, LPG-2, Maxi Cab-4, Oil Tanker-17, Bus-Nil) entered Vairengte from Assam today during 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Saiphai, Kolasib District Report**:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 Situation is under close watch.
