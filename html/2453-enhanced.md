****REPORT ON MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER****

No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/593

Dated 22nd October, 2020

1. **Thinghlun, Mamit District Report**:

In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2020/588 Dt. 21.10.2020, information has been received that Villagers of Zaite Village were instructed by Karimganj District Officials to construct Assam Police Camp at Mauhak Mual (Segontillah Hill), located at around 500 metres from Mizoram Police Camp constructed at Zaite Road, Assam-Mizoram border, but the work is not yet started as no one turn up at the area. 5 (five) Assam Police personnel visited Zaite village today i.e., 22.10.2020 and return back without performing any activity. Langkaih Group YMA withdraws deployment of their members for border duty at Thinghlun w.e.f., today i.e., 22.10.2020 due to restoration of normalcy at the area. Situation is normal.

1. **Kanhmun, Mamit District Report–**

Around 30 (thirty) vehicles carrying essential commodities arrived today i.e., 22.10.2020 at Medili Bridge near Kanhmun, Assam-Mizoram border and all goods were transited at Medili bridge (due to COVID-19 pandemic, vehicles were not allowed to enter Mizoram by nearby Villagers). Road block was conducted at Rangamati w.e.f., 17.10.2020 @ 12:00 noon but no report of road block is received from either side since yesterday i.e. 21.10.2020, and situation is normal.

1. **Saihapui ‘V’, Kolasib District –**

Tension was escalated at Assam -Mizoram border near Saihapui V due to dismantling of Saihapui ‘V’ VLTF Covid-19 duty post manned by 1st IR personnel on 12.10.2020 @ 12:00 noon by civilians form Hawaithang, Assam and Assam Police. Since then, 55 (fifty five) Mizoram Armed Police were deployed at Assam-Mizoram, at around 500 metres from Saihapui ‘V’ to maintain law and order at the area and one section of Assam Police was also deployed at the Assam Side, at around 150 metres from Mizoram Police Camp. Situation is normal.

1. **Bairabi, Kolasib District Report –**

There was no report of road block or any initiative taken which may disturb peace and tranquillity in the Assam-Mizoram border near Bairabi from either side. 1 (one) Platoon of 5th IR Battalion was deployed to reinforce the existing Mizoram Armed Police at Zophai, Assam-Mizoram Border. 30 (thirty) vehicles carrying essential commodities entered Bairabi from Assam today i.e., 22.10.2020 @ 3:00 p.m. Situation is normal.

1. **Phaisen, Kolasib District Report-**

On 20.10.2020 @ 10:30 a.m., residents of Dolchhora Village, Assam dug up the road connecting Phaisen, Mizoram to Dolchhora, Assam, at around 15 (fifteen) metres away from inter-state boundary pillar at Assam side and blocked the road by constructing bamboo wall on the road. Today i.e., 22.10.2020, Leaders of CYMA led by Dr. Malsawmliana, Asst. Secretary CYMA had a short interaction with around 50 (fifty) Congress party workers led by one Candidate of Hailakandi Constituency and both parties stated their desire for restoration of normalcy in the area.

1. **Vairengte, Kolasib District Report –**

On 17.10.2020 @ 6:30 p.m., there was physical clash between residents of Vairengte, Mizoram and Lailapur, Assam due to stone pelting of IR Camp at Assam-Mizoram border allegedly by residents of Lailapur. Due to the said incident, residents of Lailapur organised economic blockade w.e.f., 17.10.2020 evening. Tension along the border eased down after a joint meeting was conducted between Officials of Mizoram Government and Assam Government yesterday i.e., 21.10.2020 at CIJW, Lailapur. There was no report of road block on either side. 201 (two Hundred one) vehicles carrying essential commodities entered Vairengte from Assam till 5:30 p.m. today. Mizoram Police personnel is re-deployed at their previous place of deployment i.e., Vairengte Auto Stand, Assam-Mizoram border.

Situation is under close watch.
