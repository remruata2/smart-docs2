MIZORAM-ASSAM BORDER SITUATION REPORT

No.MSB/CB/B-4/2021/586

Dated 2nd August, 2021

 

          In continuation of our report vide No. MSB/CB/B-4/2021/579
Dt. 30.07.2021, Situation Report on Mizoram-Assam Border is as under:-

 

  1. Thinghlun, Mamit District Report:

          Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit and Pu Shashank Jaiswal, SP Mamit visited Thinghlun BOP today and took stock of the situation. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Saikhawthlir, Mamit District Report:

          No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Kanhmun, Mamit District Report :  

Assam Police continued construction of bunkers near Medili Bridge, Mizoram-Assam border, on Assam side adjacent to Assam Forest Department building. Blockade of road back by Assam Police is still continuing at Assam side of Medili Bridge. Dr. Lalrozama, DC, Mamit and Pu Shashank Jaiswal, SP Mamit visited Medili Bridge today and took stock of the situation

110 (one hundred ten) vehicles (HMV-24, MMV-78, LMV-3, LPG-3, Maxi Cab-2, Oil Tanker-Nil, Bus-Nil) entered Mizoram from Tripura via., Damcherra bridge today @ 3:30 p.m. and no vehicle (MMV-Nil, LMV-Nil) transited their goods at Damcherra Bridge.

 

  1. Saihapui ‘V’,  Kolasib District:

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Bairabi, Kolasib District Report :

There was no entry of vehicle at Bairabi from Assam as blockade of road leading to Bairabi is still continuing at Ramnathpur, Hailakandi District today. Situation is normal.

 

  1. Phaisen, Kolasib District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

 

  1. Vairengte, Kolasib District Report :

There was no entry of vehicle from Assam today due to the tense inter-state border situation.

 

  1. Saiphai, Kolasib District Report :

No new development has been reported during the last 24 (twenty four) hours and situation is normal.

         

          Situation is under close watch.

 

(K. LALREMSANGA)

Sr. Superintendent of Police

CID (SB) : Mizoram : Aizawl.
