
# ECONOMICS
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives
- Understand basic concepts and principles of economics.
- Develop analytical skills to interpret economic issues.
- Understand Indian economic development.
- Relate economic theory to real-life situations.
