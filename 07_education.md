## Objectives

- Understand the multifaceted nature of education.
- Analyze constitutional provisions related to education in India.
- Evaluate contemporary educational issues and initiatives.
- Understand aims of education and educational philosophies.
- Gain knowledge of educational psychology concepts.
- Appreciate guidance and counselling for holistic development.
