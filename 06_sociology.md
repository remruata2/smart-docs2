
# SOCIOLOGY
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives

- Enable learners to relate classroom learning to their social environment.
- Introduce basic concepts of sociology for observing and interpreting social life.
- Develop awareness of the complexity of social processes.
- Appreciate diversity in Indian and global societies.
- Build capacity to analyze change in contemporary Indian society.
