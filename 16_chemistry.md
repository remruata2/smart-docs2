
# CHEMISTRY
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives

The general objectives of teaching Chemistry at the Higher Secondary stage are to enable learners to:

- understand basic concepts, theories and principles of chemistry.
- develop experimental skills and scientific attitude.
- apply chemical knowledge to daily life situations.
- appreciate the role of chemistry in industry, agriculture, medicine and environment.
- develop problem-solving ability and analytical thinking.
- prepare students for higher studies and professional courses.
