
# POLITICAL SCIENCE
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives

- Enable students to understand the key features of the Indian Constitution.
- Familiarize learners with political processes and institutions.
- Develop political reasoning and argumentation skills.
- Introduce political theory and major political thinkers.
- Encourage informed participation in democratic life.
- Help students understand India’s position in the contemporary world.
