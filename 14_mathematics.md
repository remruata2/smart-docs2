## Objectives

The general objectives of teaching Mathematics at the Higher Secondary stage are to enable learners to:

- develop logical thinking and mathematical reasoning.
- acquire problem-solving skills.
- understand mathematical concepts and structures.
- apply mathematics in daily life and other disciplines.
- prepare students for higher studies in science, engineering and commerce.
