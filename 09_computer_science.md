
# COMPUTER SCIENCE
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives

The general objectives at this stage are to enable the learners to:

- understand and apply basic computational thinking.
- understand data types and data structures and apply them in different situations.
- appreciate the notion of algorithms and how they handle corner cases.
- develop understanding of computer systems, operating systems, mobile and cloud computing.
- work responsibly in the cyber world with awareness of cyber ethics, safety and cybercrime.
- understand the role of technology in society, including gender and disability issues.
- gain awareness of technology behind biometric identification systems.
