
# PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION
## Higher Secondary School Syllabus (w.e.f. 2024–2025)
**Mizoram Board of School Education (MBSE)**

---

## Objectives
- Understand the meaning and scope of public administration.
- Analyze administrative structures and processes.
- Appreciate the role of administration in development.
- Understand Indian administrative system.
